INDIVIDUAL HERBS

NAME: DING SHU XIU (DI DAN TOU) - 丁蜀秀 - HERBA ELEPHANTOPUS SCABER

Pharmaceutical Latin: Herba Elephantopus Scaber
Common English: Elephantopi

CATEGORY

Herbs that Drain Dampness (Diuretics)

Herbs that Clear Heat

PROPERTIES

ACTIONS AND INDICATIONS

Actions Indications/Syndromes

Promotes urination and reduces swelling

Edema, edema of the face and limbs, leg Qi, swelling due to nephritis of hydronephrosis

Hepatitis, bronchitis, fever and cough with pneumonia

Painful swelling

Clears Heat, purges Fire, eliminates Toxins

Intestinal bleeding from the rectum during or before a bowel movement, bleeding hemorrhoids

Relieves pain

Headache or migraine headache

Wind-Damp Bi

CONTRAINDICATIONS, INCOMPATIBILITIES AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • This herb may weaken the Middle Jiao and should be taken with spleen tonics.

NOTES

  1. This herb decreases urinary albumin.
  2. This herb is also said to calm the Liver and keep the Lungs pure.
  3. This herb inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
  4. This herb has been used to treat stomatitis and pharyngolaryngitis.
  5. The whole herb has an inhibitive action on a variety of bacteria.
