INDIVIDUAL HERBS

NAME: ZI SHI YING - 紫石英 - FLUORITUM - FLUORITE

Pharmaceutical Latin: Fluoritum
Common English: Fluorite
Amethyst

CATEGORY

Substances that Calm the Spirit: Substances that Anchor, Settle and Calm the Spirit

PROPERTIES

Taste Temperature Entering Meridians Dosage
Sweet
Warm
Heart
Liver
9-16g

ACTIONS AND INDICATIONS

Actions Indications/Syndromes

Sedates the Heart and settles tremors and palpitations

Disorientation, insomnia, palpitations with anxiety or convulsions due to Heart Blood Deficiency or Liver Yang Rising

Warms the Lungs and descends Qi

Cough or wheezing due to Deficiency Cold of the Lungs

Used for wheezing with copious sputum

Warms the Womb and warms and unblocks the Chong and Ren channels

Excessive menstruation, uterine bleeding or infertility due to Cold from Deficiency of the Womb

CONTRAINDICATIONS, INCOMPATIBILITIES AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with Yin Deficiency Fire.
  • Contraindicated for those with severe Blood Dryness.

Palpitations with anxiety, disorientation and insomnia due to Heart Blood Deficiency with Liver Yang Rising.

Fright, pain and convulsions due to Phlegm-Heat in the Heart channel.

Rebellious Qi in the Lungs with cough and wheezing with copious sputum.

NOTES

  1. This herb calms the Spirit and settles the mind while augmenting the Liver Blood in the Lower Jiao.
  2. It moistens and eliminates Dryness.
  3. Traveling through the Chong and Ren Channels, it warms the Womb.
  4. Calcined Fluorite Duan Zi Shi Ying is easier to pulverize, more easily warms the Lungs, descends Qi, dispels Cold and warms the womb. This form of the herb is rarely used.
