INDIVIDUAL HERBS

NAME: YI TIAO GEN - 一條根 - RADIX MOGHANIAE

Pharmaceutical Latin: Radix Moghaniae
Common English: Moghania

CATEGORY

Herbs that Dispel Wind-Dampness (Antirheumatics)

PROPERTIES

Taste Temperature Entering Meridians Dosage
Acrid
Warm
Liver
Kidney
3-30g

ACTIONS AND INDICATIONS

Actions Indications/Syndromes

Dispels Wind and Dampness and opens the channels and collaterals

Bi syndrome with musculoskeletal pain, stiffness and soreness

External and Internal trauma

Discharges Stagnancy and removes toxic factors

Toxic Heat, carbuncles, tonsillitis, hepatitis, chronic nephritis

CONTRAINDICATIONS, INCOMPATIBILITIES AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Use caution for those with weak digestive systems.
