ACTIONS AND INDICATIONS

Actions Indications/Syndromes

Clears Heat, drains Dampness, stops diarrhea and reduces inflammation

Large Intestine Damp-Heat

Urinary Bladder Damp-Heat

Damp-Heat in the Spleen

Diarrhea, difficult urination

Cools the Blood and eliminates toxins and stops bleeding

Toxic Heat in the Blood that causes bleeding and cancer

Dysentery, hemafecia, epistaxis, hemorrhoidal bleeding

CONTRAINDICATIONS, INCOMPATIBILITIES AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Do not use long term on elderly patients.
  • Contraindicated during pregnancy.
  • Contraindicated for those with cold or recurrent dysentery.

NOTES

  1. This herb treats gonorrhea, hepatitis, intestinal parasites and urodynia.
  2. It can be used topically for eczema, nettle rash, boils and injuries (sprains and strains).
  3. This herb is known as "the holy herb for retropharyngeal abscess.
  4. This herb has been shown to inhibit the growth of cancer cells.
  5. To stop bleeding and generate new flesh, the juice from this herb is mixed with wine and may be ingested or applied topically.
  6. The powder treats pain due to Qi Stagnation.
