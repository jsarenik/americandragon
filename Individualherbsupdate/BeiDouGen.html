INDIVIDUAL HERBS

NAME: BEI DOU GEN - 北豆根 - RHIZOMA MENISPERMI

Pharmaceutical Latin: Rhizoma Menispermi
Common English: Asiatic Moonseed Rhizome
Sinomenium

CATEGORY

Herbs That Clear Heat and Purge Fire: Herbs That Clear Heat and Detoxify; Target Warm and Heat Diseases

PROPERTIES

Taste Temperature Entering Meridians Dosage
Bitter
Cold
Lung
Stomach
Large Intestine
(Urinary Bladder)
(Heart )
3-12g
Tincture: 1-3ml

ACTIONS AND INDICATIONS

Actions Indications/Syndromes

Clears Heat and resolves toxicity

Wind-Heat toxin affecting the Lungs and Stomach such as sore throat, cough and mumps

Snakebite

Dispels Wind and stops pain

Wind-Damp Bi

Resolves Dampness and reduces swelling

Damp-Heat

CONTRAINDICATIONS, INCOMPATIBILITIES AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with Spleen and Stomach Deficiency Cold.
  • Contraindicated for those with diarrhea.
  • Overdose can cause epigastric pain and fullness, acid regurgitation, vertigo, extensive sweating, chest oppression, tachypnea, agitation, nausea, vomiting, low blood pressure.
  • There is some cumulative toxicity, do not use continuously.

NOTES

  1. This is a modern addition to the pharmacopia.
  2. It is used for a wide variety of Heat-toxin related problems, especially swelling.
  3. it is used for upper digestive tract cancers and irregular heartbeat.
  4. It is used in surgical anesthesia.
  5. It is sometimes used as a substitute for Rx. Sophorae Tonkinensis Shan Dou Gen, in Northern China.
