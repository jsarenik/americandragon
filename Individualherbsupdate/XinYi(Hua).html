INDIVIDUAL HERBS

NAME: XIN YI HUA - 辛夷花 - FLOS MAGNOLIAE - MAGNOLIA FLOWER

Pharmaceutical Latin: Flos Magnoliae
Common English: Magnolia Flower
Yulan Magnolia Bud
Biond Magnolia Flower Bud
Sprenger Magnolia Flower Bud

CATEGORY

Herbs that Release the Exterior: Warm Acrid Herbs that Release the Exterior (Wind-Cold Diaphoretics)

PROPERTIES

Taste Temperature Entering Meridians Dosage
Acrid
(Slightly Astringent)
Warm
(Slightly Warm)
Lung
Stomach
2-10g
Tincture: 2-4ml

ACTIONS AND INDICATIONS

Actions Indications/Syndromes

Expels Wind-Cold and unblocks the nasal passages

Nasal obstruction or congestion, pain, discharge, lack of smell sinus problems and related headache due to Wind-Cold

With appropriate herbs for any nasal or sinus condition

CONTRAINDICATIONS, INCOMPATIBILITIES AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with Yin Deficiency with Heat signs.
  • Contraindicated for those with Liver Yang Rising.
  • Contraindicated for those with nasal congestion due to Wind-Cold invasion who are Qi Deficient.
  • Contraindicated for those with headache from Blood Deficiency with accumulated Fire.
  • Contraindicated for those with toothache from Stomach Fire.
  • Overdose may cause dizziness or red eyes.
  • Avoid prolonged use.
INCOMPATIBILITIES
  • Incompatible with Rz. Acori Tatarinowii Shi Chang Pu.

NOTES

  1. This is the primary herb for treating sinus problems.
  2. It is the furry unopened flower bud.
  3. It is usually combined with Pueraria Ge Gen and other Wind expelling herbs.
  4. The essential oil can be inhaled for quick relief of sinus congestion.
  5. This herb has a quite strong anti-fungal action on the skin and can be used as a facial cream for chloasma.
  6. Daoists say this herb adds years to one's life span.
  7. Dry-fried Magnolia Flower Chao Xin Yi has the hairs which irritate the throat removed.
