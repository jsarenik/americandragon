NAME: TIAN MEN DONG - 天门冬 - RADIX ASPARAGI - ASPARAGUS ROOT

Pharmaceutical Latin: Radix Asparagi
Common English: Asparagus Tuber
Shiny Asparagus Tuber

CATEGORY

Herbs that Tonify the Yin

PROPERTIES

Taste Temperature Entering Meridians Dosage
Sweet
Bitter

Kidney

ACTIONS AND INDICATIONS

Actions Indications/Syndromes

Nourishes Kidney Yin, clears Lung Heat and sedates Fire

Lung Yin Deficiency with Heat signs in the upper Jiao such as Dryness of the mouth,

Dryness of the Lung with dry mouth and a dry, non-productive cough or a productive cough with scanty, viscous, Blood-streaked sputum

Kidney Yin Deficiency

Jing Deficiency due to Kidney Yin Deficiency with leg atrophy

Moistens the Lungs, nourishes the Kidneys,
generates Fluids and resolves Phlegm

Lung and Kidney Yin Deficiency, especially wasting and thirsting disorder(upper, middle and lower)and consumption with low-grade afternoon fever

Constipation due to dry Intestines

*CONTRAINDICATIONS, INCOMPATIBILITIES AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with diarrhea and anorexia due to Spleen Deficiency Cold.
  • Contraindicated for those with cough due to Wind-Cold.

NOTES

  1. It is often used with Tub. Ophiopogonis Mai Men Dong as a syrup.
  2. It is used in combination with other Yin tonics to treat feverishness in the upper body.
  3. It can be used alone for a mild dry cough.
  4. Some sources say that this herb has diuretic properties.
  5. Some sources say that taking this herb with meat increases the production of milk for nursing mothers.
  6. One source says that it breaks up Blood Stagnation.
  7. This herb has been shown to inhibit the growth of cancer cells.
  8. One source says that this herb cures pyogenic Lung cancer.
  9. This herb is said to cure pyogenic sores, abdominal masses and carbuncles.
  10. For swelling and carbuncles, wash and grind 30-150g of Tian Men Dong, filter with wine and take orally.
  11. For chronic stomatitis, use equal portions of Tian Men Dong, Rx. Ophiopogonis Mai Men Dong and Rx. Scrophulariae Xuan Shen, make into honey-coated pills and suck on one pill a day.
  12. This herb is said to eliminate foul body odor.
  13. Rx. Trichosanthis Tian Hua Fen, Tub. Ophiopogonis Japonici Mai Men Dong, and Tub. Asparagi Tian Men Dong all clear Heat and moisten the Lungs. The first is best for Lung Heat from Stomach Heat, the second is best for Lung Heat from Heart Fire and the last is best for Lung Dryness from Kidney Yin Deficiency.
  14. Both Tian Men Dong and Rx. Ophiopogonis Mai Men Dong nourish Lung Yin, moisten Dryness and stop coughs and are used together for dry coughs with Blood tinged sputum, thirst due to injured Yin and constipation from Intestinal Dryness. Mai Men Dong is slightly cold and less Yin-nourishing but is less greasy and cloying than Tian Men Dong, and can eliminate irritability which Tian Men Dong cannot do. Tian Men Dong is very cold and more strongly clears Heat and moistens Dryness. It also enriches Kidney Yin which Mai Men Dong cannot do.
  15. Fresh Asparagus Tuber Xian Tian Dong are colder, rich, moistening and very nourishing to the Yin and fluids and are used for insatiable thirst that nothing else will quench.
  16. Succus Asparagi, Raw Asparagus Juice Sheng Tian Men Dong Zhi is very cold, sweet and tasty with a strong ability to nourish Yin and generate fluids. It also cools the Blood and stops bleeding especially bleeding related to Lung or Stomach Heat. It is especially suited to those who cannot eat and are vomiting Blood due to Stomach Heat.
  17. Dry-fried Asparagus Tuber Chao Tian Dong has a reduced greasy, cloying effect and facilitates digestion.
  18. Honey-prepared Asparagus Tuber Mi Zhi Tian Dong is better able to moisten the Lungs and stop coughs due to Lung Yin Deficiency.
  19. Cinnabar Asparagus Tuber Zhu Tian Dong has a Spirit-calming action that is most appropriate to treat insomnia, irritability or emotional disturbance associated with Lung and Kidney Yin Deficiency.
