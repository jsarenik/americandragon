INDIVIDUAL HERBS

NAME: HEI ZHI MA - 黑芝麻 - (HU MA REN) (胡麻仁) - SEMEN SESAME NIGRUM - BLACK SESAME SEEDS

Pharmaceutical Latin: Semen Sesame Nigrum
Semen Sesame Indici
Common English: Black Sesame Seed

CATEGORY

Herbs that tonify Yin

PROPERTIES

Taste Temperature Entering Meridians Dosage

ACTIONS AND INDICATIONS

Actions Indications/Syndromes

Nourishes and fortifies Liver and Kidney Yin

Kidney and Liver Yin Deficiency with blurred vision, tinnitus and dizziness

Aids in recovery from severe illness

Increases the quantity of breast milk

Nourishes Jing and Blood and extinguishes Wind

Headaches, dizziness and numbness due to Blood Deficiency or Yin Deficiency

Especially for dizziness that worsens with activity

Moistens and lubricates the Intestines and Lungs

Constipation due to Dry intestines or Blood Deficiency

CONTRAINDICATIONS, INCOMPATIBILITIES AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated in those with diarrhea due to Spleen Deficiency.
  • Use with caution for those with toothache from Rising Fire.

NOTES

  1. It moistens dry tissues for dry coughs and dry Intestines.
  2. The oil is applied topically to the head for migraines and vertigo.
  3. It treats sores and insect bites when crushed and applied topically.
  4. Sesame oil, when applied topically treats acute burns.
  5. This herb brightens the eyes, blackens the hair, moistens the skin and lubricates the Intestines.
  6. Sesame Oil (Oleum Sesame) Xiang Yu is used as a base for herbal powders.
  7. Both Hei Zhi Ma and Fr. Mori Sang Shen serve as food as well as medicinals, both tonify the Liver and Kidneys, enrich Yin, augment the Blood, moisten Dryness and lubricate the Intestines. Hei Zhi Ma is neutral, enters the Kidneys to replenish the essence and augment the marrow. Sang Shen is cool, enters the Blood Level and secondarily tonifies the Kidneys.
  8. Dry-fried Sesame Seeds Chao Hei Zhi Ma have an enhanced ability ability to augment the Liver and Kidneys, replenish essence and Blood, lubricate the Intestines and treat constipation.
