INDIVIDUAL HERBS

NAME: LUO SHI TENG - 络石藤 - CAULIS TRACHELOSPERMI

Pharmaceutical Latin: Caulis Trachelospermi
Common English: Star Jasmine Stem
Confederate Jasmine Stem

CATEGORY

Herbs that Dispel Wind-Dampness

PROPERTIES

Taste Temperature Entering Meridians Dosage
Bitter
Slightly Cold
(Cool)
Liver
(Heart)
(Kidney)
6-16g
Tincture: 2-4ml

ACTIONS AND INDICATIONS

Actions Indications/Syndromes

Dispels Wind-Dampness and unblocks the channels and collaterals

Wind-Damp Bi and spasms of the sinews (especially Hot Bi)

Cools the Blood and reduces swellings

Red, hot painful abscesses and other toxic sores

Throat Bi

CONTRAINDICATIONS, INCOMPATIBILITIES AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with diarrhea.
  • Contraindicated during pregnancy.
  • Large doses may lead to convulsions and respiratory arrest.
INCOMPATIBILITIES
  • Counteracts Bul. Fritillariae Cirrhosae Chuan Bei Mu and Bul. Fritillariae Thunbergii Zhe Bei Mu.
  • Counteracts Rz. Acori Gramenei Shi Chang Pu.
  • Antagonizes Frusta Ferri Sheng Tie Luo.

NOTES

  1. This herb is non-toxic and can be used for a long time.
  2. This herb can be used for both upper and lower body problems.
  3. This is a mild herb with Wind expelling and Heat clearing properties and can be used for Bi pain in children and adolescents. Members of this age group usually do not suffer from Deficiency but rather are more apt to suffer from Wind-Dampness invasion with a short duration.
  4. This herb governs lumbar and sacral pain.
  5. This herb should be soaked in wine to use.
  6. It can be used alone as a tea for sore throat.
  7. This herb is enhanced by Cx. Eucommiae Du Zhong and Cx. Moutan Mu Dan Pi.
  8. Both Luo Shi Teng and Caulis Piperis Kadsurae Hai Feng Teng dispel Wind and promote the flow of Qi in the collaterals and are often used to treat restricted joint movement due to Wind-Dampness, cramps and spasms. Hai Feng Teng is slightly warm and a better choice when these disorders are not associated with Heat. Luo Shi Teng is cooling and preferred when Heat is a factor. It also cools the Blood and reduces swellings and is used for swollen, toxic sores and trauma leading to swelling and hematoma.
