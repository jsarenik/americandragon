INDIVIDUAL HERBS

NAME: JIAO GU LAN - 绞股蓝 - RHIZOMA SEU HERBA GYNOSTEMMATIS

Pharmaceutical Latin: Rhizoma seu Herba Gynostemmatis
Common English: Gynostemma

CATEGORY

Herbs that Tonify Qi

PROPERTIES

Taste Temperature Entering Meridians Dosage
Slightly Bitter
Cold
Lung
Heart
5-12g
Powder: 0.75g-1g

ACTIONS AND INDICATIONS

Actions Indications/Syndromes

Tonifies Qi, moistens the Lungs, generates Body fluids and dispels Phlegm

Chronic disorders such as asthma, migraines, neuralgia impaired respiratory function and impaired gastrointestinal function characterized by Deficiency with nausea, vomiting, shortness of breath and chest congestion

Clears Heat, eliminates toxins and reduces swelling and inflammation and alleviates pain

Gastric and duodenal ulcers, hyperlipidemia, nodules, cystitis, and herpes zoster

Cancers

Lowers Blood pressure and cholesterol

High blood pressure

High cholesterol

CONTRAINDICATIONS, INCOMPATIBILITIES AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • This herb has a mild sedative effect. Use caution when driving.
INCOMPATIBILITIES
HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS
  • This herb has sedative, hypnotic and analgesic effects. It potentiates the sedative effect of barbiturates and reverses the effect of mescaline23,24,25.
