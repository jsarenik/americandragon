INDIVIDUAL HERBS

NAME: LOU LU - 漏蘆 - RADIX RHAPONTICI/ECHINOPSIS

Pharmaceutical Latin: Radix Rhapontici/Echinopsis
Common English: Rhaponticum Root
Echinops
Broad Leaf Globethistle Root

CATEGORY

Herbs that Clear Heat and Purge Fire: Herbs that Clear Heat and Relieve Toxicity

PROPERTIES

Taste Temperature Entering Meridians Dosage
Bitter
Salty
Cold
Large Intestine
Stomach
3-14g
Tincture: 1-4ml

ACTIONS AND INDICATIONS

Actions Indications/Syndromes

Clears Heat, resolves toxicity, drains abscesses and swellings and expels pus

Early stages of sores and abscesses when they are red, swollen and painful

Especially for swelling and pain in the breast area

Causes milk to descend (promotes lactation)

Encourages lactation, especially when there are signs of Heat

CONTRAINDICATIONS, INCOMPATIBILITIES AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated during pregnancy.
  • Do not use for insufficient lactation due to Deficiency.

NOTES

  • This herb has an anti fungal effect.
  • This herb is used primarily for Heat toxin in the skin, noxious sores, boils, hemorrhoids, Damp Bi and descending milk.
  • It is especially good for breast abscesses due to Qi and Blood Stagnation.
  • Although it is Cold, it still has unblocking and facilitating actions.
  • It can be used alone for dermatological disorders.
  • This herb has been shown to inhibit the growth of cancer cells.
  • It promotes the movement of Qi, improves vision and prolongs life if taken over a long period of time.
  • One source says that it soothes the Small Intestine, cures emission, hematuria, hematochezia, infantile high fever, breast abscess and scrofula.
  • It stops bleeding, draws out pus and supplements the Blood to nourish the muscles.
  • It is also said to kill roundworms
