ACTIONS AND INDICATIONS

Actions Indications/Syndromes

Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, opens the channels, unblocks menstruation and alleviates pain

Blood Stasis, especially in women for gynecological problems such as abdominal pain, amenorrhea, postpartum dizziness, retained lochia and abdominal masses

Trauma, non suppurative sores, carbuncles and dark, purplish erythema

Incomplete expression of rash of measles

Chest Bi due to Blood Stasis

CONTRAINDICATIONS, INCOMPATIBILITIES AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated during pregnancy.
  • Contraindicated for those with any bleeding disorder, such as hemorrhagic disease, menorrhagia or peptic ulcer.
  • Overdose can cause continuous bleeding.
  • Caution, this herb may cause weight loss.
INCOMPATIBILITIES
HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS
  • Use with caution for patients taking anticoagulants such as heparin, warfarin (Coumadin) and enoxaparin (Lovenox) or antiplatelet drugs such as aspirin, dipyridamole (Persantine) and clopidogrel (Plavix) as concurrent use may have an additive or synergistic effect.

NOTES

  1. Large doses dispel Blood Stasis. Small doses harmonize the Blood.
  2. Stigma Crocus Sativa (Tibetan saffron) Fan Hong Hua or Xi Hong Hua is stronger and more expensive than this herb. It is used for severe cases of Blood Stasis with chest pain.
  3. The dried flower of this herb is the raw material for rouge.
  4. This herb is a powerful Blood activator.
  5. It is most often used for gynecological problems.
  6. Other good saffron comes from Spain and Iran.
  7. Some sources say that this herb induces labor.
  8. This herb has been shown to inhibit the growth of cancer cells.
  9. It moistens Dryness and harmonizes the Blood to sooth Liver constraint. In large doses, it breaks up the Blood and in small doses, it gently moves and nourishes the Blood.
  10. For ceruminitis with unceasing fetid pus, pulverize 0.3g of Hong Hua and 30g of Alumen Ming Fan and fill the ear.
  11. It is also said that decocted in water, this herb nourishes the Blood and decocted in wine, removes Blood Stasis.
  12. Both Hong Hua and Hb. Leonuri Yi Mu Cao invigorate the Blood and enter the Blood Levels of the Heart and Liver channels. Hong Hua is warm and more strongly invigorates the Blood, reduces swellings and, in large doses, strongly breaks up Blood Stasis . Yi Mu Cao is slightly cold and gently cools Blood Heat when Heat is associated with Stagnation. It also facilitates urination.
  13. Both Hong Hua and Lignum Sappan Su Mu are similar in their actions. Hong Hua is slightly warm and harmonious while Su Mu is slightly cool.
  14. Flos Crocus Sativa Fan Hong Hua is sweet, cold, enters the Heart and Liver channels. It has similar functions to Hong Hua, but has a much stronger ability to invigorate and cool the Blood, eliminate Blood Stasis and eliminate Heat toxin. It is commonly used when Heat is Attacking the Ying and Xue levels with maculae and high fever. It is expensive and seldom used. The dosage is 1.5-3g.
