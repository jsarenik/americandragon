NAME:E JIAO - 阿膠 - COLLA CORII ASINI - ASS-HIDE GLUE

Pharmaceutical Latin: Colla Corii Asini
Gelatinum Corii Asini
Equus
Common English: Ass-Hide Glue
Donkey-Hide Gelatin
Gelatin

CATEGORY

Herbs that Tonify Blood

PROPERTIES

Taste Temperature Entering Meridians Dosage
Sweet
Neutral
Kidney
Liver
Lung
3-16g
Tincture: 2-4ml

ACTIONS AND INDICATIONS

Actions Indications/Syndromes

Tonifies the Blood

Blood Deficiency with dizziness, sallow or pale complexion and palpitations

Nourishes the Blood and stops bleeding

Any kind of bleeding, especially consumptive disorders with hemoptysis, hemafecia, menorrhagia or metrorrhagia

Nourishes and moistens Yin

Yin Deficiency with irritability and insomnia in the aftermath of a Warm febrile disease

Moistens the Lungs and Large Intestine

Dry Lung coughs due to Yin Deficiency or consumption

CONTRAINDICATIONS, INCOMPATIBILITIES AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with Exterior disorders.
  • Contraindicated for those with Spleen and Stomach Deficiency.
  • Contraindicated for those with focal distention in the epigastrium.
  • Contraindicated for those with diarrhea.
  • Contraindicated for those with nausea and vomiting.
  • Contraindicated for those with retention of Dampness, Cold-Phlegm or thin mucus.
INCOMPATIBILITIES
  • Counteracts Rx. et Rz. Rhei Da Huang.

NOTES

  1. This herb can be used as a stand alone herb or melted in warm rice or millet wine.
  2. It is used when there is bleeding due to Deficiency of Blood and Yin with Heat damaging the vessels.
  3. For bleeding during pregnancy, dry-fry and use as a powder.
  4. It is generally used with Qi and Blood tonics to enhance their effects.
  5. Both E Jiao and Rx. Rehmanniae Shu Di Huang enter the Liver and Kidney channels, nourish Blood, enrich Kidney Yin and treat Blood Deficiency and gynecological disorders such as irregular menstruation or pregnancy or postpartum disorders. Shu Di Huang generates Jing and augments marrow, while E Jiao enriches Lung Yin and stops bleeding. Both substances are greasy and can interfere with digestion.
  6. E Jiao nourishes the Blood, moistens Dryness and expels Heat. Rx Polygoni Multiflori He Shou Wu enters the Liver, tonifying Blood to expel, Wind and blackens the hair and beard. but is unable to moisten Dryness and settle wheezing. Colla Cornus Cervi Lu Jiao Jiao warms the Du channel and the Chong channel to augment Blood, but cannot clear Lung channel Heat and stop coughing. Colla Plastri Testudinis Gui Ban powerfully tonifies Jue Yin, fills the Ren channel to reduce Heat and expel steaming bone disorder, but cannot tonify Yang within Yin.
  7. Ass-Hide Glue Pearls E Jiao Zhu or clam-powder fried Ass-Hide Glue Ge Fen Chao E Jiao or fried Ass-Hide Glue Chao E Jiao better nourishes the Lung Yin to moisten the Lungs, stops cough and transforms Phlegm. It is frequently used for dry cough and wheezing due to Lung Dryness or consumptive cough when Yin has been injured and Fire is over-abundant, particularly if there is coughing up of Blood.
  8. Pollen Typhae Fried Ass-Hide Glue Pu Huang Chao E Jiao nourishes the Blood and moistens Dryness. it also invigorates the Blood, transforms Stasis, stops bleeding and harmonizes the collaterals. It tonifies without being cloying, and stops bleeding without Stagnation. It is used for gynecological disorders such as threatened miscarriage or continuous uterine bleeding.
