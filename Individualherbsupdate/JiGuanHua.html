INDIVIDUAL HERBS

NAME: JI GUAN HUA - 雞冠花 - FLOS CELOSIAE CRISTATAE

Pharmaceutical Latin: Flos Celosiae Cristatae
Common English: Coxcomb Flower
Celosia Flower

CATEGORY

Herbs that Stabilize and Bind (Astringents)

PROPERTIES

Taste Temperature Entering Meridians Dosage
Sweet
Astringent
Cool
Liver
Large Intestine
(Kidney)
(Bladder)
4.5-15g
Tincture: 2-5ml

ACTIONS AND INDICATIONS

Actions Indications/Syndromes

Restrains and holds to stop bleeding and vaginal discharge

A variety of bleeding and other discharges from the Lower Jiao

Clears Heat, cools the Blood, binds the Intestines and stops bleeding

Dysenteric disorders, especially chronic cases or those marked by Blood

Consolidates Jing and stops diarrhea

Stops diarrhea, dysentery and vaginal discharges

CONTRAINDICATIONS, INCOMPATIBILITIES AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with uterine bleeding due to Blood Stasis.
  • Contraindicated for those with early stages of dysenteric disorders with Exterior signs.
  • Contraindicated for those with Damp-Heat.

NOTES

  1. This herb can be used as a stand-alone herb in powder form dissolved in wine for incessant menstruation.
  2. This herb treats bleeding hemorrhoids, red and white dysentery, hematemesis, hemoptysis, Blood Lin, metrorrhagia and red and white vaginal discharge.
  3. The flowers can be decocted and used as a wash for vaginal pruritis and discharge.
  4. Charred Flos Celosiae Ji Guan Hua Tan is less Cool but better able to astringe bleeding and vaginal discharge.
  5. This herb has been shown to eliminate trichomonas vaginitis in ten minutes.
  6. It has also been shown to kill parasites.
