INDIVIDUAL HERBS

NAME: ZAO XIN TU - 竈心土 - TERRA FLAVA USTA - IGNITED YELLOW EARTH

Pharmaceutical Latin: Terra Flava Usta (Fu Long Gan)
Common English: Oven Earth
Ignited Yellow Earth
Stove Earth

CATEGORY

Herbs that Regulate the Blood: Herbs that Stop Bleeding (Hemostatics)

PROPERTIES

Taste Temperature Entering Meridians Dosage
Acrid
Warm
(Slightly Warm)
Spleen
Stomach
15-90g Place separately in a cheesecloth bag and decoct first
Large dose: 90-120g

ACTIONS AND INDICATIONS

Actions Indications/Syndromes

Warms the Blood and stops bleeding

Bleeding due to Stomach and/or Intestinal Deficiency Cold

Warms and harmonizes the Stomach (Middle Jiao) and stops vomiting

Vomiting due to Stomach Cold

Morning sickness

Stops diarrhea

Spleen Deficient diarrhea

*CONTRAINDICATIONS, INCOMPATIBILITIES AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with bleeding due to Yin Deficiency with Heat signs.
  • Contraindicated for those with nausea and vomiting with Heat signs.
  • Do not use alone for those with swollen sores with overabundant toxicity that is hard to eliminate.

NOTES

  1. This herb comes from ashes at the bottom of a stove where Rx. Lithospermi/Arnebiae Zi Cao has been burned.
  2. One source indicates that it is old clay from the clay bed inside a stove that has burned dry grass or wood for a long time.
  3. On source say that this herb has astringent properties.
  4. For swollen sores, it is mixed with garlic into a paste and applied to the area.
  5. For moxa blisters, boil and pour the liquid over it.
  6. It can be used alone for general bleeding.
  7. It can be used alone for nausea and vomiting during pregnancy.
