INDIVIDUAL HERBS

NAME: HE YE - 荷葉 - FOLIUM NELUMBINIS - LOTUS LEAF

Pharmaceutical Latin: Folium Nelumbinis
Common English: Lotus Leaf

CATEGORY

Herbs that Clear Heat and Purge Fire: Herbs that Clear Heat and Relieve Toxicity; Herbs that Clear Heat and Relieve Summerheat

PROPERTIES

Taste Temperature Entering Meridians Dosage

Slightly Sweet

Neutral

Spleen

ACTIONS AND INDICATIONS

Actions Indications/Syndromes

Relieves Summerheat

Summerheat and Dampness with fever, irritability, excessive sweating, scanty urine and especially diarrhea

Raises and clears Spleen Yang

Spleen Yang Deficiency diarrhea especially in the aftermath of Summerheat

Removes Stagnation and stops bleeding

Bleeding in the Lower Jiao due to Heat or Stagnation

Hematemesis (adjunctive)

CONTRAINDICATIONS, INCOMPATIBILITIES AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with bleeding due to Cold or Deficiency.
INCOMPATIBILITIES
  • Counteracts Poria Fu Ling.

NOTES

  1. Lotus Stem, Caul. Nelumbinis Lian Geng is similar to the leaf plus it opens and moves Qi in the chest. It is used for Summerheat-Dampness with a stifling sensation in the chest and diarrhea and Summerheat with vaginal discharge or painful urinary dysfunction. It can unblock the Qi, relax the sinews, raise Yang fluids, alleviate thirst, cool Heat, alleviate night sweats, mobilize water and cure irregular uterine bleeding and painful urinary dysfunction.
  2. One source says that He Ye reduces Food Stagnation and astringes Jing.
  3. Another source says that He Ye disperses Blood and raises Yang.
  4. Fresh Lotus Leaf Xian He Ye or Raw Lotus Leaf Sheng He Ye is stronger at cooling Summerheat and stops bleeding from Heat in the Blood. It is specific for epistaxis and hematemesis.
  5. Charred Lotus Leaf He Ye Tan is stronger at stopping bleeding and is combined with other herbs for irregular menstrual bleeding or pus and Blood in dysenteric disorders. It is also applied externally for sores which exude yellowish fluid and for moist eczema.
  6. Calyx Nelumbinis He Ye Di raises Yang Qi, strengthens the Spleen's transportive and transformative functions, dispels Dampness and harmonizes the Stomach.
