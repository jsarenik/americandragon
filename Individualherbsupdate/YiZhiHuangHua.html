INDIVIDUAL HERBS

NAME: YI ZHI HUANG HUA - 一枝黄花 - (LIU ZHI HUANG - 柳枝黃) - HERBA SOLIDAGINIS

Pharmaceutical Latin: Herba Solidaginis
Common English: Solidago
Common Goldenrod

CATEGORY

Herbs that Dispel Wind-Dampness and Unblock the Channels (Antirheumatics)

PROPERTIES

Taste Temperature Entering Meridians Dosage

Sweet
Cold

ACTIONS AND INDICATIONS

Actions Indications/Syndromes

Relieves pain, reduces inflammation and opens the channels and collaterals

Pain, inflammation, traumatic injury

Reduces swelling and eliminates toxins

Toxic swelling, sore throat, jaundice, cold headache, whooping cough, infantile convulsion, fungal infection of the hand, carbuncle and swelling on the back

Athlete's foot, topically

CONTRAINDICATIONS, INCOMPATIBILITIES AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated during pregnancy.
  • Consuming by livestock can lead to paralysis and movement disorders and other symptoms of poisoning.

NOTES

  1. One source says that Caulis Hyptis Capitatae Mo Gu Xiao and Caulis Vanieriae Huang Jin Gui should always be used together for their synergistic action to reduce inflammation and relieve spinal pain.
  2. Prolonged storage of the herb can render it ineffective.
    One source says that this herb invigorates the Blood.
  3. This herb has been shown to inhibit the growth of cancer cells.
  4. It has been shown to be effective for thyroid cancer and cancer of the tongue and larynx.
  5. For lumbodorsal carbuncle, acute mastitis and bubo, simmer 21-30g of this herb in wine, drink and grind the dregs into a paste to be applied to the lesion.
  6. It is said that washing with a decoction of this herb can cure pathogenic infections.
  7. One source that this herb removes Blood Stasis and obstruction and is effective for traumatic injuries, skin itching and herpes zoster.
  8. Do not decoct for a long time as it can cause vomiting.
