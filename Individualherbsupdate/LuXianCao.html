NAME: LU XIAN CAO - 鹿啣草 - HERBA PYROLAE

Pharmaceutical Latin: Herba Pyrolae
Common English: Pyrola
Asian Wintergreen Root and Herb
Lu Han Cao

CATEGORY

Herbs that Dispel Wind-Dampness

PROPERTIES

ACTIONS AND INDICATIONS

Actions Indications/Syndromes

Dispels Wind-Dampness and strengthens the sinews and bones

Wind-Damp Bi and lack of strength in the back and legs (can be used alone)

Stops bleeding

A variety of bleeding disorders including hemoptysis, epistaxis, irregular uterine bleeding and bleeding from trauma

Augments the Kidneys and aids them to grasp Qi (Tonifies the Lungs and Kidneys)

Coughing and wheezing from chronic Lung Qi Deficiency or failure of the Kidneys to grasp Qi

Invigorates Blood circulation and regulates menstruation

Leukorrhea

Nourishes the Heart and stops perspiration

Palpitations and night sweating associated with Heart Yin Deficiency

NOTES

  1. This herb has a contraceptive action and may cause atrophy of the uterus and ovaries.
  2. It lowers blood pressure.
  3. In Japan, it is used to treat beriberi and promote diuresis.
  4. The juice of the raw herb treats toxic insect bites and incised wounds.
  5. This herb can be applied topically as a powder or crushed as a fresh herb for bleeding due to trauma.
  6. This herb is particularly good for older patients as it tonifies the Kidneys.
  7. It also moistens the Lungs.
  8. It also nourishes the Heart and astringes sweating and is used to treat palpitations and nightsweats.
  9. It is also said to treat symptoms of Phlegm-Fire.
