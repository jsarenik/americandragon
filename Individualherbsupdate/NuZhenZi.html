INDIVIDUAL HERBS

NAME: NU ZHEN ZI - 女真子 - FRUCTIS LIGUSTRI LUCIDI

Pharmaceutical Latin: Fructus Ligustri Lucidi
Common English: Ligustrum
Privet Fruit
Glossy Privet Berry

CATEGORY

Herbs that Tonify the Yin

PROPERTIES

Taste Temperature Entering Meridians Dosage
Bitter
Sweet
Cool
(Neutral)
Kidney
Liver
4.5-18g
Tincture: 2-4ml

ACTIONS AND INDICATIONS

Actions Indications/Syndromes

Nourishes and tonifies Liver and Kidney Yin

Kidney and Liver Yin Deficiencies with dizziness, spots before the eyes, lower back soreness, premature graying of the hair and tinnitus

Clears Deficiency Heat

Yin Deficiency with Empty Fire Rising

Improves vision

Diminished visual acuity in those with Liver and Kidney Deficiency

CONTRAINDICATIONS, INCOMPATIBILITIES AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with Yang Deficiency.
  • Contraindicated for those with diarrhea due to Spleen Deficiency Cold.
INCOMPATIBILITIES
HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS
  • Use caution for those taking insulin, sulfonylureas and other antidiabetics such as tolbutamide (Orinase), glipizide (Glucotrol) and glyburide (DiaBeta/Micronase) as there may be a synergistic effect leading to hypoglycemia.

NOTES

  1. This herb is an ancient Taoist longevity tonic.
  2. Large doses may cause diarrhea.
  3. It is common in treatments for immune Deficiency.
  4. Some sources say that this herb releases the bowels and lubricates the Intestines.
  5. This herb is used in treating various allergic and immune complex conditions.
  6. It is used to treat tuberculosis of the Lungs or lymph in patients with signs of Yin Deficiency.
  7. This herb is similar to Fr. Lycii Gou Qi Zi and Fr. Mori Sang Shen. Nu Zhen Zi nourishes Kidney and Liver Yin Deficiency only. Gou Qi Zi treats Yin and Yang Deficiencies and Sang Shen treats Liver and Kidney Yin and Blood Deficiencies.
  8. Both Nu Zhen Zi and Hb. Ecliptae Mo Han Lian tonify Liver and Kidney Yin and treat symptoms of dizziness, tinnitus, vertigo premature graying of the hair and sore and weak low back and knees. Nu Zhen Zi also treats hot flashes and steaming bones from Deficiency Heat, while mo Han Lian more strongly cools the Blood to stop various kinds of bleeding.
