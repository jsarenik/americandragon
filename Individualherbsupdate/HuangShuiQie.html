NOTES

  • In Taiwan the whole plant (Fructus et Folium Solani Indici) is used. It is Cold and Bitter. It Expels Wind and relieves headaches. It is used for toothaches and sinusitis. it has antiphlogistic, expectorant, hyperglycemic and antitussive properties. It has also been shown to inhibit the growth of cancer cells.
  • It is said that ulcers can be cured by using the powdered leaves.
  • For Food Stagnation or water in the Stomach, the herb is washed, dried, calcined yellow, ground into a powder, blended with sugar and mixed with 15g of boiling water. It is then taken on an empty Stomach.
  • It removes Stagnation, and removes swelling, inflammation and pain. It is indicated for pharyngitis, tonsillitis, lymphatic tuberculosis and gastralgia.
  • For gastralgia, take 9-15g in decoction.
