INDIVIDUAL HERBS

NAME: WU YI - 芜荑 - FRUCTUS ULMI MACROCARPAE

Pharmaceutical Latin: Fructus Ulmi Macrocarpae
Common English: Elm Cake  (paste made from the fruit of the Stinking Elm)
Ulmus

CATEGORY

Herbs that Expel Parasites

PROPERTIES

Taste Temperature Entering Meridians Dosage
Bitter
Acrid
Warm
(Neutral)
Spleen
Stomach
3-10g

ACTIONS AND INDICATIONS

Actions Indications/Syndromes

Kills parasites

Roundworms and tapeworms

Childhood nutritional impairment

Abdominal pain and accumulation due to parasites

Treats tinea infections (topically)

Tinea infections, scabies

CONTRAINDICATIONS, INCOMPATIBILITIES AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with Spleen or Stomach Deficiency.
  • Contraindicated for those with Dry Heat in the Spleen and Lungs.
  • Do not take long-term.

NOTES

  1. This herb also treats Intestinal Wind with bleeding hemorrhoids, noxious sores, scabies and tinea.
  2. It governs pathogenic Qi in the five Zang organs, disperses perverse Dampness in the skin and bony joints, and transforms Food Stagnation.
  3. One source says that it governs accumulated Cold Qi, pain in the Heart and abdomen due to fixed abdominal masses and eliminates the sensation of crawling insects due to Wind Attack entering the muscles, skin and joints.
  4. It can be used alone for Intestinal parasites manifesting as a pale or sallow face and abdominal pain when eating.
