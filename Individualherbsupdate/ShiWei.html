Common English: Pyrrosia Leaf

NOTES

  1. This herb has a very strong stone removing action.
  2. It can be used alone to stop bleeding.
  3. This herb has shown to be effective in treating cancer patients who have low white blood cell counts after receiving chemotherapy or radiation. It helps increase the white blood cell count.
  4. Both Shi Wei and Talcum Hua Shi are cold and unblock painful urinary dysfunction. Hua Shi is slippery and facilitates Dampness and is best used for for Damp-Heat Lin and Stone Lin. It also cools Summerheat and alleviates thirst. Shi Wei cools the Blood and stops bleeding and is best for Blood Lin. It also cools the Lungs, stops cough.
  5. Both Shi Wei and Spora Lygodii Hai Jin Sha clear Damp-Heat, unblock painful urinary dysfunction and are used to treat Hot painful urinary dysfunction (Lin Syndrome) or hematuria. Shi Wei is especially good for bloody painful urinary dysfunction and Damp-Heat painful urinary dysfunction. it also treats cough and wheezing by clearing Lung Heat and irregular uterine bleeding by cooling Blood and stopping bleeding in the Lower Jiao. Hai Jin Sha is particularly effective at treating stony painful urinary dysfunction.
