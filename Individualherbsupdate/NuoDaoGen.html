NOTES

  1. It may be used singly with sugar added or with other herbs.
  2. It is said to tonify Qi, transform Phlegm, enrich the Yin of the Stomach, expel Wind-Dampness, treat Cold in the genitals, quiet the fetus, harmonize the Blood and remedy frostbite and wounds from metal.
  3. It is an effective treatment for night sweats.
  4. It also works for fever, spontaneous perspiration, hepatitis and filariasis.
  5. Chyluria can be treated with decoction of 120g of Nuo Dao Gen, twice a day for 20 days as a treatment course.
  6. For spontaneous perspiration caused by Qi Deficiency, Nuo Dao Gen can be used alone.
