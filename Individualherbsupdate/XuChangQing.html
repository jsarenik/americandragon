NAME: XU CHANG QING - 徐長卿 - RADIX CYNANCHI PANICULATI

Pharmaceutical Latin: Radix Cynanchi Paniculati
Herba Pycnostelmae
Common English: Paniculate Cynanchum
Paniculate Swallow-Wort

CATEGORY

Resolves toxins and stops itching

Wind rashes (topical and internal)

Toxic sores (Chuang Yung)

Skin itching including eczema and snakebite (can be used as a stand-alone herb)

NOTES

  1. Some sources say that this herb promotes Qi flow and disperses swelling.
  2. Other parts of the plant may be used using a dosage of 15-30g.
  3. This herb can also be used to treat epigastric pain and toothache.
  4. This herb treats epidemic disorders, noxious pathogenic Qi and warm malarial disorders.
  5. It treats abdominal distention in children with bluish-green veins and bites from rabid dogs.
  6. It treats continuous uterine bleeding and vaginal discharge.
  7. It is effective for severe pain.
  8. This herb has been shown to inhibit the growth of cancer cells.
  9. It promotes urination, reinforces the Kidneys, tranquilizes the mind and expels Cold.
  10. It is said to stop cough, promotes drainage and removes toxins.
  11. It is said that toothache can be cured by gargling wine in which the seedling of this herb has been steeped.
  12. For malignant celialgia caused by visceral disorder, powder this herb and Benzoinum An Xi Xian, steep in wine, screen, simmer into a paste, make into pills and take ten pills at a time with vinegar as frequently as needed.
