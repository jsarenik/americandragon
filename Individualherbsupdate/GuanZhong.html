NAME: (MIAN MA) GUAN ZHONG - 貫眾 - RHIZOMA DRYOPTERIS

Pharmaceutical Latin: Rhizoma Dryopteris
Common English:

Dryopteris Root
Shield-Fern
Wood-Fern

ACTIONS AND INDICATIONS

Actions Indications/Syndromes

Kills parasites

Intestinal parasites including tapeworms, roundworms, pinworms (mild effect), liver fluke, threadworms and hookworms

Clears Heat and relieves Fire toxicity

Warm-Heat toxic diseases with Fire Blazing (regardless of the level), measles, encephalitis, pneumonia

Cools the Blood and stops bleeding

Blood Heat bleeding, all types of bleeding, especially uterine bleeding

*CONTRAINDICATIONS, INCOMPATIBILITIES AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated during pregnancy
  • Contraindicated for weak patients.
  • Contraindicated for those with a recent history of ulcers.
  • Contraindicated for children.
  • Contraindicated in patients with Cold from Deficiency and no Excess Heat.
  • Contraindicated in those with Ying Qi Deficiency.
  • Contraindicated for desiccated Blood.
  • Contraindicated with Liver Fire.
  • Contraindicated with Kidney Yin Deficiency with Fire Rising.
  • Contraindicated in those with cough due to Yin Deficiency.
  • Do not use with oily or fatty foods.

NOTES

  1. Habitual use causes a diminution in taste sensation, an increase in appetite, periodontal disease, and in some cases chronic diarrhea, in addition to the discoloration of the mouth and lips. (It has a narcotic effect and is chewed with an alkaline substance).
  2. This herb is useful for gastrointestinal problems that are associated with bloating and edema.
  3. This herb is especially effective against tapeworms.
  4. It can be used for Wind-Heat common cold and epistaxis.
  5. It breaks up fixed and mobile masses, eliminates head wind and treats wounds from metal.
  6. It treats Intestinal Damp-Heat with burning, difficulty passing stool, hemafecia, abdominal pain and thirst.
  7. It contracts the female organs after pregnancy.
  8. Charred Dryopteris Guan Zhong Tan stops bleeding.
  9. Postpartum bleeding by using 6g of vinegar-treated Guan Zhong served with rice wine.
