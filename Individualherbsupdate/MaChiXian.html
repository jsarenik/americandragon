NAME: MA CHI XIAN - 馬齒莧 - HERBA PORTULACAE

Pharmaceutical Latin: Herba Portulacae
Common English: Purslane
Portulaca

CATEGORY

Actions Indications/Syndromes

Clears Damp-Heat and treats sores

Fire toxin carbuncles or sores Chuang Yung (internal and topical)

Antidote for the pain and swelling of snakebite and wasp stings

Snakebite and wasp stings

Stops bleeding, disperses Blood Stagnation and unblocks painful urinary dysfunction

Bloody painful urinary dysfunction (Blood Lin)

Uterine bleeding, especially postpartum

Hot. painful urinary dysfunction

NOTES

  1. It is especially for treating good for treating dysentery where colicky tenesmus persists after bowel movements.
  2. This herb can stimulate abortion.
  3. The fresh juice is used alone to treat bacterial diarrhea or dysentery.
  4. It can be used topically for Heat and toxins in the skin.
  5. Made into a paste and applied topically it is used for sores, lesions, hemorrhoids, ulcerations and eczema.
  6. It is used for gastrointestinal tract infections, leukorrhea, skin eruptions, insect bites and snake bites.
