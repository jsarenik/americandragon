ACTIONS AND INDICATIONS

Actions Indications/Syndromes

Clears Heat and relieves Fire toxicity

Chuang Yung (hot swellings), especially red, hot, swollen eyes; swollen, painful throat and ears; mumps and snakebite, breasts, intestines

Clears hot sores (Chuang Yung) internally and externally

Sores and abscesses, especially of the head and back

CONTRAINDICATIONS, INCOMPATIBILITIES AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with slow-growing swellings that have not yet come to a head.
  • Do not use for Deficiency Cold.
  • Caution for those with Yin type sores.

NOTES

  1. Violet is an old folk remedy that has become standard in many modern Chinese herb formulas to treat Heat and toxins.
  2. Apply externally to drain abscesses.
  3. This herb is said to treat tuberculosis and cancer.
  4. Both Zi Hua Di Ding and Hb. Taraxaci Pu Gong Ying are bitter, cold, resolve toxicity and treat Yang type sores and toxic swellings. Zi Hua Di Ding cools the Blood and excels at resolving toxicity and is more effective in treating deep-set toxic sores and carbuncles. It also assists in then treatment of snakebite. Pu Gong Ying excels at descending and dispersing stagnant Qi and is particularly effective at treating breast abscesses.
