INDIVIDUAL HERBS

NAME: HU TAO REN - 胡桃仁 - SEMEN JUGLANDIS

Pharmaceutical Latin: Semen Juglandis
Common English: Walnut
English Walnut
Persian Walnut

CATEGORY

Herbs that Tonify the Yang

PROPERTIES

Taste Temperature Entering Meridians Dosage
Sweet
Warm

ACTIONS AND INDICATIONS

Actions Indications/Syndromes

Tonifies the Kidneys, replenishes Jing and
strengthens the back and knees

Kidney Yang Deficiency with a Cold and painful back and knees and urinary frequency

Kidney Jing Deficiency

Warms and astringes the Lungs, resolves cold Phlegm, arrests wheezing and helps the Kidneys grasp Qi

Lung and Kidney Deficiencies with chronic cough and wheezing that worsens with any exertion

Kidney unable to grasp Lung Qi

Moistens the Intestines and unblocks the bowels

Constipation from Deficient Fluids and Blood

Constipation in the elderly

Constipation due to injured Fluids following a febrile disorder

CONTRAINDICATIONS, INCOMPATIBILITIES AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with Phlegm Fire.
  • Contraindicated for those with Hot cough.
  • Contraindicated for those with Yin Deficiency with Heat signs.
  • Contraindicated for those with watery stool.
  • Do not take with strong tea.
INCOMPATIBILITIES
HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS
  • Hu Tao Ren has anticoagulant and thrombolytic effects and should be used with caution in patients who take anticoagulant or antiplatelet medication such as warfarin (Coumadin), heparin, enoxaparin (Lovenox), aspirin, dipyridamole (Persantine) and clopidogrel (Plavix)

NOTES

  1. This herb is prescribed when both Kidneys and Lung are involved.
  2. This herb is gentle and generally serves as an assistant.
  3. One sources says that Walnuts can tonify the Qi, nourish the Blood, moisten Dryness, transform Phlegm, augment the Ming Men, facilitate the San Jiao, warm the Lungs, and moisten the Intestines to treat coughs due to Deficiency Cold and heavy pain in the low back and feet.
  4. One source says that this herb enriches and tonifies the Liver and Kidneys and strengthens sinews and bones. It stabilizes the teeth and blackens the hair.
