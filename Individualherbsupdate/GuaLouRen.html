INDIVIDUAL HERBS

NAME: GUA LOU REN - (GUA LOU ZI) - 栝蔞仁 - SEMEN TRICHOSANTHIS

Pharmaceutical Latin: Semen Trichosanthis
Common English: Trichosanthis Seed
Snakegourd Seed

CATEGORY

ACTIONS AND INDICATIONS

Actions Indications/Syndromes

Clears Heat and transforms Hot Phlegm (cools and moistens the Lungs)

Phlegm-Heat cough (especially with thick, difficult to expectorate sputum)

Expands the chest

Phlegm accumulation in the chest with a stifling sensation, pain, or diaphragmatic pressure

Moistens the Intestines and promotes bowel movements

Dry constipation (especially with Lung/Large Intestine Heat)(dry mouth, thirst, irritability)

Promotes healing of sores (dissipates nodules and eliminates pus)

Adjunctively for unsuppurated sores, breast abscess and swelling

Promotes urination

 Edema

CONTRAINDICATIONS, INCOMPATIBILITIES AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with weak, deficient Spleen with either nausea or loose stools.
  • Contraindicated for those with diarrhea due to Spleen and Stomach Deficiency Cold.
INCOMPATIBILITIES
  • Incompatible with Rx. Aconiti Wu Tou.
  • Antagonizes Rz. Zingiberis Gan Jiang.
  • Counteracts Rx. Achyranthis Bidentate Niu Xi.

NOTES

  1. The earlier texts do not differentiate between this herb (the fruit), the seed Gua Lou or the husk, Gua Lou Pi.
  2. This herb both cools and moistens to treat coughing with chest pain and thick, difficult to expectorate sputum.
  3. This herb, Sm. Persicae Tao Ren and Sm. Cannabis Huo Ma Ren treat dry constipation. Sm. Persicae Tao Ren treats constipation due to Blood Stasis and Heat. This herb treats constipation due to Lung Heat with thirst and Sm. Cannabis Huo Ma Ren treats constipation due to severe prominent Heat.
  4. This herb is common in Lung Heat formulas, especially with Yin Deficiency.
  5. This herb has a unique combination of sweet, bitter and sour tastes.
  6. Dry-fried Trichosanthis Seeds Chao Gua Lou Ren is the usual form of this herb because its raw taste can illicit nausea and vomiting.
  7. Defatted Trichosanthis Seeds Lou Ren Schuang is less moistening of the Intestines and is used for Spleen Deficient patients with with cough and Phlegm.
