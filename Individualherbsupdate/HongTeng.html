INDIVIDUAL HERBS

NAME: HONG TENG - 紅藤 - CAULIS SARGENTODOXAE

Pharmaceutical Latin: Caulis Sargentodoxae
Common English: Sargentodoxa Vine

CATEGORY

Herbs that Clear Heat and Purge Fire: Herbs that Clear Heat and Detoxify; Target Warm and Heat Diseases

PROPERTIES

Taste Temperature Entering Meridians Dosage
Bitter
Neutral
Large Intestine
Liver
(Stomach)
9-30g
Tincture: 2-4ml

ACTIONS AND INDICATIONS

Actions Indications/Syndromes

Clears Heat, resolves toxicity, reduces abscesses and stops pain

Fire toxicity with Intestinal abscess and skin lesions with Heat, swelling and pain (Chuang Yung) (internal and topical)

Invigorates the Blood and disperses Stasis

Blood Stagnation from trauma

Dysmenorrhea and joint pain

NOTES

  1. According to some sources, this herb is not often used .
  2. It is mostly used for gastrointestinal problems, especially worms and cancer and for rheumatism.
  3. This herb is a non-toxic anthelmintic.
  4. It is said to be useful for abdominal bacterial infection.
  5. Some sources say that this herb dispels Wind and opens the channels.
  6. Hong Teng, Hb. Houttuyniae Yu Xing Cao and Hb. Patriniae Bai Jiang Cao all are cooling, resolve toxicity, strongly disperse Blood and cool Blood Heat, and are all specific for treating Intestinal abscess. Hong Teng focuses on both the Intestines and breasts, Yu Xing Cao focuses on the Lungs and Bai Jiang Cao on the Intestines. They are often used together to treat gynecological abdominal pain from Blood Stasis.
  7. Both Hong Teng and Caulis Spatholobi Ji Xue Teng are frequently used together for abdominal pain due to Blood Stasis. They both invigorate the Blood, however Ji Xue Teng also supplements and has a relatively gentle pain-relieving action. Hong Teng is mores dispersing, cooling and able to resolve toxicity. It is an essential herb for abdominal pain due to pelvic inflammation.
  8. Both Hong Teng and Caulis Lonicerae Ren Dong Teng resolve toxicity, reduce sores, clear Heat, and treat Bi Syndrome and abdominal pain. Ren Dong Teng clears Wind-Heat from the collaterals to stop pain while Hong Teng resolves toxicity, invigorates Blood, stops pain and treats breast and abdominal abscesses.
