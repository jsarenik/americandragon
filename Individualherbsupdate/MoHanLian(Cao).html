NAME: MO HAN LIAN - 墨旱莲 - (HAN LIAN CAO) - (旱莲草) - HERBA ECLIPTAE

Pharmaceutical Latin: Herba Ecliptae
Common English: Eclipta
Field Lotus Herb

CATEGORY

Herbs that Tonify the Yin

ACTIONS AND INDICATIONS

Actions Indications/Syndromes

Nourishes and tonifies Liver and Kidney Yin

Kidney and Liver Yin Deficiencies with dizziness, blurred vision, vertigo, and premature graying of the hair

Cools the Blood and stops bleeding

Yin Deficiency with Empty Fire Rising and bleeding due to Heat in the Blood with hematemesis, hemoptysis, epistaxis, hemafecia, uterine bleeding and especially hematuria

CONTRAINDICATIONS, INCOMPATIBILITIES AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with Kidney and Spleen Deficiency Cold.
  • Contraindicated for those with diarrhea or loose stools due to Cold from Kidney Qi Deficiency.
  • Use caution for Spleen Qi Deficient patients who have Food Stagnation.

NOTES

  1. This herb can be applied externally to assist blackening of the hair and to stop bleeding.
  2. This herb has recently been used to treat Damp-related disorders of the perineum, such as perineal itching and vaginal itching. it is generally used as an external wash.
  3. This herb has been shown to inhibit the growth of cancer cells.
  4. It is said that violent hematemesis can be cured with a decoction of this herb, young boy's urine, pounded ink stick and Nodus Nelumbinis Rhizomatis Ou Jie.
  5. For hematochezia, calcine, pound and powder this herb, then take 6g at a time.
  6. For migraine and other types of headache drop the juice of this herb into the nose.
  7. Both Mo Han Lian and Fr. Ligustri Lucidi Nu Zhen Zi enrich Kidney Yin and augment the Liver. Mo Han Lian strongly tonifies the Yin, blackens the hair and cools the blood to stop bleeding. Nu Zhen Zi is less tonifying but cools Heat due to Yin Deficiency and brightens eyes.
  8. Charred Eclipta Han Lian Tan is better able to stop bleeding, but is less able to tonify Yin and cool.
  9. This herb is considered the main Yin tonic without an oily quality.
  10. This herb has a reputation for slowing down the signs of aging.
  11. The juice of this herb treats diphtheria and dysentery when ingested and treats headaches when applied topically to the nasal cavity.
  12. The fresh herb has stronger Heat clearing and Blood cooling functions than the dried herb.
  13. Hb. Hypericum Ascyron Hong Han Lian is is sometimes used as a substitute for Mo Han Lian. It is stronger at cooling the Blood, invigorating the Blood and healing sores and ulcers. Mo Ha Lian stops bleeding and is more effective at tonifying the Kidney Yin.
