NOTES

  1. Prevention of common cold: Soak 100g each of fresh ginger and garlic in 500ml of vinegar in a tightly sealed jar for 30 days or more. Then drink 10ml of the resulting liquid after each meal.
  2. Indigestion from overeating: Take 15ml of vinegar, and 1-3 grams of fine green tea. Put the vinegar and tea leaves in a teacup and pour 300ml of boiling water over it. Allow to steep for five minutes. Then divided into three doses and drink one dose at a time as needed.
  3. Viral hepatitis and/or enteritis: Eat or drink 30ml of vinegar each time, three times per day.
  4. Bacterial dysentery: Wash a daikon radish in water and slice thinly. Then add a suitable amount of vinegar and sugar to taste. Eat two times per day.
  5. High blood pressure:
    a. Dry stir-fry 500g of soybeans till fragrant. Then allow to cool and soak in 1000ml of vinegar for 10 days. Afterwards, eat the soybeans as one wants.
    b. Soak unroasted peanuts in vinegar for seven days. Do not remove the shells. Then eat 10 peanuts each morning and night. This formula can also be used for arteriosclerosis and coronary artery disease.
  6. Arteriosclerosis: Each day, drink one tablespoon of vinegar.
  7. Angina pectoris: Eat vinegar and garlic on a daily basis.
  8. Diabetes: Wash 100g of soybeans in water. Then soak in 110ml of vinegar for eight days. Each three beans each time, 3-6 times per day. Constant eating gets the effect.
  9. Thyroid swelling: Soak 60g of dried seaweed in a suitable amount of vinegar and eat regularly.
  10. Obesity: Eat (or drink) 15-40ml of vinegar per day.
  11. Headache: Boil some vinegar and inhale the fumes.
  12. Edema: 1. Cook 60g of Fresh Ginger Sheng Jiang into soup with 100ml of vinegar and wash the affected limb with the resulting liquid once each day.
  13. Powder a suitable amount of Semen Sinapis Bai Jie Zi and mix with vinegar. Apply to the affected area.
  14. Insomnia: Drink a bowl of hot water mixed with vinegar.
  15. Night sweats: Each night before sleep, wash the chest and upper back with vinegar.
  16. Mouth sores (including thrush): Stir-fry 15g of Fructus Evodiae Rutaecarpae Wu Zhu Yu. Then powder and mix into a paste with vinegar. Apply this paste once each evening before going to bed on the bottoms of the feet at KI-1.
  17. Gingivitis: Mix 50ml of vinegar with 50ml of hot water and gargle with the resulting liquid two times per day for 14 days.
  18. Uterine bleeding: Cook 150g of tofu with 100ml of vinegar. Eat once per day for 7-10 days.
  19. Vaginal candidiasis and itching: Douche with 100ml of vinegar in 200ml of water once per day for 10 days.
  20. Nausea and vomiting during pregnancy: Drink one teaspoon of vinegar.
  21. Boils and other inflamed sores: Mix mashed garlic and vinegar and apply to the affected area.
  22. Traumatic injuries: Grind some Sm. Phaseoli Radiati Lu Dou into powder and mix with vinegar. Apply to the affected area. this can also be used for nonhealing lower limb sores, such as diabetic ulcers.
  23. Nosebleed: Soak some sterile cotton in vinegar and insert into the nose.
  24. Flat warts: Boil 200ml of vinegar down to 100ml and apply the resulting liquid to the warts three times per day.
  25. Common warts: Mash a suitable amount of Fresh Ginger Sheng Jiang to obtain the juice. Then mix with a suitable amount of vinegar and apply several times per day to the warts.
  26. Palmar eczema: Soak the hands for in warm vinegar for 10 minutes each time, two times per day.
  27. Nail fungus: With a knife or scissors, cut off as much of the dead nail as possible. Then soak the affected nail in vinegar for 30 minutes once per day.
  28. Corns: Soak one Fructus Pruni Mume Wu Mei in a suitable amount of vinegar. Then apply to the corn once per day.
  29. Itching: Mix some soy sauce and vinegar and apply to the affected area.
  30. Hives:
    a 1. Boil 60g of brown sugar and 30g of Fresh Ginger Sheng Jiang in 200ml of vinegar for five minutes. Then apply 20-30ml of the resulting liquid mixed with warm water 2-3 times per day
    b. Mix two parts vinegar to one part alcohol and apply to the affected area. 
  31. Psoriasis: Apply strong vinegar 3-4 times per day.
  32. Herpes zoster: Mix vinegar and Realgar Xiong Huang together and apply externally to the herpes lesion once the blister has ruptured and there is a wet, glistening, slow-healing sore.
  33. Hair loss: Mix 130ml of vinegar with 200ml of hot water and rinse the hair with this mixture, massaging it into the scalp, once per day on a regular basis.
  34. Body odor: Soak five grams of Fr. Foeniculi Xiao Hui Xiang in 50ml of vinegar. Then apply the resulting liquid to the underarms two times per day.
  35. Sweaty feet: Boil 30g of Radix Sophorae Flavescentis Ku Shen and 20g of Fructus Zanthoxyli Bungeana Chuan Jiao in 50ml of aged vinegar. Apply the resulting liquid once each evening before going to bed. One should see an effect in 2-3 days and the full result in seven days.
  36. Foot odor: Mix 15-20ml of vinegar in warm water and soak the feet for 15-20 minutes two times per day. Do continuously for 7-10 days and the foot odor can be eliminated.
