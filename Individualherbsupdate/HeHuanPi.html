NOTES

  1. It nourishes the Heart and Spleen, generates flesh and has a marked Yin tonic effect.
  2. It focuses the mind and makes people happy.
  3. Long term use lightens the body, brightens the eyes and allows people to achieve their goals.
  4. It promotes family affection and harmony.
  5. It has a moderate effect and requires large doses over time.
  6. Both He Huan Pi and the flower, Flos Albizziae He Huan Hua, are sweet and neutral, augment the Heart and Spleen, remove constraint and calm the Spirit. He Huan Pi has a slightly greater ability to invigorate the Blood, stop pain and reduce swellings, while He Huan Hua moves Liver Qi, and is better for soothing the Liver, unbinding the Stomach, promoting memory, promoting sleep and brightening the eyes.
  7. It is used primarily for insomnia, irritability and poor memory especially when accompanied by epigastric pain and pressure in the chest.
  8. Both He Huan Pi and Caul. Polygoni Multiflori Ye Jiao Teng calm the Spirit. He Huan Pi soothes the Heart and Spleen Qi , invigorates the Blood, stops pain and reconnects sinews and bones. Ye Jiao Teng nourishes the Heart and Liver Blood and expels Wind in the collaterals to stop itch and treat skin disorders.
