INDIVIDUAL HERBS

NAME: MAN JING ZI - 蔓荊子 - FRUCTUS VITICIS

Pharmaceutical Latin: Fructus Viticis
Common English: Vitex Fruit
Chastetree Fruit
Three-leaf Chastetree Fruit

CATEGORY

Herbs that Release the Exterior: Cool Acrid Hears that Release the Exterior (Wind-Heat Diaphoretics)

PROPERTIES

Taste Temperature Entering Meridians Dosage
Bitter
Acrid
Cool

Maximum dosage: 30g

ACTIONS AND INDICATIONS

Actions Indications/Syndromes

Disperses Wind and clears Heat

Wind-Heat with headache or eye pain

Dispels Wind-Heat in the Liver channel and clears and benefits the head and eyes

Liver Channel Wind-Heat with excessive tearing, red, painful or swollen eyes or spots in front of the eyes

Liver Yang Rising

Drains Dampness, expels Wind and relieves pain

Wind-Dampness in the limbs (auxiliary) with stiffness, numbness, cramping or heaviness

CONTRAINDICATIONS, INCOMPATIBILITIES AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Use caution with headaches or eye problems due to Yin or Blood Deficiency.
  • Use caution for those with Spleen and Stomach Qi Deficiencies.
  • Use with caution for those with dizziness or headache due to Blood deficiency.
INCOMPATIBILITIES
  • Antagonizes Rx. Aconiti Wu Tou (family).
  • Antagonizes Gypsum Shi Gao.

NOTES

  1. If it is given to Qi Deficient patient, it creates paranoia and agoraphobia (bladder energy imbalanced).
  2. It has a similar effect as aspirin.
  3. Usually used for upper body pains.
  4. It is particularly for Wind-Heat in the face and eyes but also useful for stiff painful joints from Wind-Damp Bi.
  5. This herb can also be used for swollen,painful gums and teeth.
  6. It is said to treat some parasites.
  7. Besides treating Wind-Damp Bi, it also treats cold bulging disorders and leg Qi.
  8. Many consider it the best herb for tinnitis and deafness.
  9. Man Jing Zi and Rz. Gastrodiae Tian Ma both treat headaches. Man Jing Zi treats headaches due to Wind-Heat, while Tian Ma treats headaches due to Internal Liver Wind.
  10. Unprepared Vitex Fruit Sheng Man Jing Zi has a stronger dispersing action and is indicated for expelling Wind-Heat, dispersing Wind and stopping pain.
  11. Dry-fried Vitex Fruit Chao Man Jing Zi is less acrid and dispersing but is better at clearing the head and eyes. It is used to treat headaches around the eyes, watery eyes or blurred vision. This is the form that is generally used.
