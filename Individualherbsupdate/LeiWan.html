INDIVIDUAL HERBS

NAME: LEI WAN - 雷丸 - OMPHALIA

Pharmaceutical Latin: Omphalia
Common English: Omphalia Fruiting Body
Mylitta
Thunderball Fungus   

CATEGORY

Herbs that Expel Parasites

PROPERTIES

Taste Temperature Entering Meridians Dosage
Bitter
Cold
Slightly Toxic
Large Intestine
Stomach
Powders and Pills: 4-60g Do not decoct

ACTIONS AND INDICATIONS

Actions Indications/Syndromes

Kills Parasites

Roundworms and tapeworms

Childhood nutritional impairment or abdominal pain due to parasites

Topically for tinea infections

 

*CONTRAINDICATIONS, INCOMPATIBILITIES AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Use caution for those with parasitic infestations along with Spleen or Stomach Deficiency.
  • Excessive use can lead to infertility.
  • Long-term use injures Yin in both men and women.
INCOMPATIBILITIES
  • Antagonizes Rx. Puerariae Ge Gen.
