INDIVIDUAL HERBS

NAME: HU YAO HUANG - 虎咬癀 - HERBA LEUCUS MOLLISSIMAE

Pharmaceutical Latin: Herba Leucus Mollissimae
Common English: Leucus

CATEGORY

Herbs that Clear Heat and Eliminate Toxins

PROPERTIES

Taste Temperature Entering Meridians Dosage
Bitter
Cool
Large Intestine
Small Intestine
Lung
6-15g

ACTIONS AND INDICATIONS

Actions Indications/Syndromes

Clears Heat and eliminates Toxins

Infection and inflammation

Appendicitis, enteritis, metritis

Invigorates the blood and promotes urination

Uterine infections and nephritis

CONTRAINDICATIONS, INCOMPATIBILITIES AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated during pregnancy.
