Pharmaceutical Latin: Herba Euphorbiae Lunulatae
Common English: Crescent-shaped Euphorbia

Actions Indications/Syndromes

Resolves Phlegm, resolves toxicity and promotes urination

Phlegm, cough and asthma, edema, scrofula, cancer, swelling of unknown origin

NOTES

  1. It is said that this herb governs cervical lymph nodes.
  2. It treats tuberculosis.
  3. This herb has been shown to inhibit the growth of cancer cells.
  4. For treatment of chronic bronchitis, grind Mao Yan Cao, Sm. Lepidii/Descurainiae Ting Li Zi and Rx. Adenophorae Nan Sha Shen into a powder. Make the powder into 0.25g tablets. Take 6 tablets 3 times a day for a course of 20 days.
  5. This herb is said to have the same functions as Rx. Euphorbiae Pekinensis Hong/Jing Da Ji, but is somewhat milder.
