INDIVIDUAL HERBS

NAME: CI SHI - 磁石 - MAGNETITUM - MAGNETITE

Pharmaceutical Latin: Magnetitum
Common English: Magnetite
Lodestone

CATEGORY

Substances that Calm the Spirit: Substances that Anchor, Settle and Calm the Spirit

PROPERTIES

ACTIONS AND INDICATIONS

Actions Indications/Syndromes

Anchors Liver Yang and calms the Spirit

Agitated Heart and Spirit with restlessness, palpitations, insomnia, tremors, dizziness and vertigo in patients with Yin Deficiency with Yang Rising, especially Kidney Rising, especially when Kidney Yin Deficiency leads to Liver Fire that deranges and disturbs the Spirit

Convulsions in children caused by shock or fear

Benefits Yin, nourishes the Kidneys, augments the Liver and improves hearing and vision

Kidney and Liver Yin Deficiency with impaired hearing or deafness, tinnitus or visual disturbances

Aids the Kidneys in grasping Qi

Chronic asthma due to Kidneys Unable to Grasp Qi

CONTRAINDICATIONS, INCOMPATIBILITIES AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Use caution for those with a weak Spleen or Stomach.
  • Do not use with iron.
  • Excessive or prolonged use in pill or powder form is contraindicated.
INCOMPATIBILITIES
  • Antagonizes Cx. Moutan Mu Dan Pi.

NOTES

  1. All mineral substances are toxic except for Ci Shi which approaches harmony.
  2. This herb protects the True Yin.
  3. This herb often serves as the Chief herb because of its ability to descend to the lower body, nourish the Kidneys, augment Jing and Blood and suppress and contain floating Yang.
  4. Both Ci Shi and Haematitum Dai Zhe Shi are heavy substances that anchor the Yang, sedate fright and quiet the Spirit. Ci Shi protects the True Yin and is best used when the True Yin is deficient in the lower body, allowing Yang to float up. Dai Zhe Shi suppresses and descends Qi to clear and descend Liver Fire. It is used for treating Liver Yang rising, with dizziness, headaches and tinnitus, to sedate fright and to stop nausea, vomiting and wheezing. It also cools the Liver Blood to stop bleeding.
  5. Calcined Magnetite Duan Ci Shi is easier to pulverize and is stronger at tonifying the Kidneys, grasping Qi and improving hearing and vision. Its sedating qualities are less. It treats tinnitus, deafness, blurred vision, cataracts, wheezing from Kidney Deficiency and spermatorrhea.
  6. Floating Magnetite Fei Ci Shi has any amounts of arsenic removed.
