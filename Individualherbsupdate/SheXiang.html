INDIVIDUAL HERBS

NAME: SHE XIANG - 麝香 - MOSCHUS

Pharmaceutical Latin: Moschus
Common English: Musk

CATEGORY

Aromatic Substances that Open the Orifices

ACTIONS AND INDICATIONS

Actions Indications/Syndromes

Strongly opens the Orifices and Luo vessels, revives the Spirit and unblocks closed disorders

Ying Stage Heat Attacks the Pericardium due to a Warm pathogen disease with convulsions, delirium, stupor and fainting

Closed disorders (Yang Obstruction, Yin Obstruction, Phlegm Obstruction)

Tetanic collapse

Seizures

Invigorates the Blood, dissipates masses, reduces swellings, detoxifies, opens the channels and alleviates pain

Severe problems due to Blood Stasis including fixed palpable masses, toxic sores and carbuncles

Obstructions of the channels and collaterals due to traumatic injury or painful obstruction

Coronary artery disease

Hastens delivery and facilitates the passage of stillborns (induces abortion)

Labor

Dead fetus or placenta fail to descend

CONTRAINDICATIONS, INCOMPATIBILITIES AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated during pregnancy.
  • Contraindicated for those with Yin Deficiency with Heat signs.
  • Use with caution for those with hypertension.
  • Use with caution for those with allergies.
  • Use with caution for those with reduced Kidney function.
  • Use with caution for those with cerebral hyperactivity.
  • Use with caution for those with cerebral congestion.
INCOMPATIBILITIES
  • Do not use with garlic.

NOTES

  1. Due to this animal’s endangered status and the great expense of this herb, almost all of the commercially available product is either from raised deer, or synthesized muscone.
  2. Because of its intensely aromatic, penetrating nature, this substance is used to treat a wide variety of problems that impair consciousness.
  3. This herb also treats paralysis of the Intestines.
  4. This herb may be used alone for immobile palpable masses or organomegaly.
  5. It is highly valued and used in many patent medicines for congestion of Blood and Phlegm.
  6. It is used to “remove filth”, and stimulate circulation.
  7. One source says that it is used to treat gastritis.
  8. It has been shown to inhibit the growth of cancer cells.
  9. It is said to cure all sorts of diseases and eliminate all sorts of foul smells.
  10. It is also said that this herb activates all senses and channels and cures all sorts of retention and abdominal masses.
  11. One source says that stomatoma can be cured in three days by taking 3g of the ground herb with water.
  12. This is the most intensely aromatic and penetrating herb in the Chinese materia medica.
  13. It can be used internally or topically.
  14. It is said that this herb should never be boiled.
  15. Because of its ability to invigorate Blood, reduce swelling, unblock Stagnation, stop pain, prevent necrosis and reduce inflammation, it is very effective at treating toxic swollen sores, abscesses, traumatic injuries, swelling and pain from stagnant Blood mobile and fixed abdominal masses and amenorrhea.
  16. Both She Xiang and Borneolum Bing Pian are acrid, mobile, piercing, open the Orifices and awaken the Spirit. They are often combined to treat loss of consciousness, delirium associated with febrile diseases, fainting due to Summerheat, Wind-Stroke and swollen sores due to Heat toxin. She Xiang more powerfully opens the Orifices, awakens the Spirit invigorates the Blood,unblocks the collaterals and reduces and disperses Qi and Blood Stagnation. It treats hematomas, traumatic injury and abdominal masses or amenorrhea due to Blood Stasis. Bing Pian dissipates Fire constraint, to reduce swelling and stop pain. It treats sore red eyes, mouth ulcers and toothache.
  17. Both She Xiang and Styrax Su He Xiang are acrid, warm, aromatic, open the Orifices and awaken the Spirit. She Xiang is stronger at opening the Orifices, awakening the Spirit and unblocking the channels and collaterals to treat Qi and Blood Stagnation with swelling and pain. Su He Xiang more strongly clears away filth and expels Phlegm to treat turbid, filthy Qi and Wind-Stroke with overabundant Phlegm causing loss of consciousness.
