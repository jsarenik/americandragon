INDIVIDUAL HERBS

NAME: LONG KUI - 龍葵 - HERBA SOLANI NIGRI

Pharmaceutical Latin: Herba Solani Nigri
Common English: Black Nightshade
Winter Cherry Herb
Solanum

CATEGORY

Herbs that Clear Heat and Purge Fire: Herbs that Cleat and Relieve Toxicity

ACTIONS AND INDICATIONS

Actions Indications/Syndromes

Clears Heat, resolves toxicity, invigorates the Blood and reduces swellings

Sores and abscess, pruritic sores and rashes, erysipelas swollen and painful throat and cancer

Treats erysipelas and furuncle topically

Promotes urination, and unblocks painful urinary dysfunction

Hot, painful urinary dysfunction or edema with urinary dysfunction

CONTRAINDICATIONS, INCOMPATIBILITIES AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated during pregnancy and breastfeeding.
  • Do not use continuously due to accumulated toxicity.

NOTES

  1. This herb also expels Phlegm, stops cough and alleviates itching.
  2. The leaves are used topically for pemphigus and other toxic swellings. They reduce Heat, disperse Blood, suppress toxic effects of minerals and metals. It also expels corrupted Blood in women and treats bronchitis along with a wide variety of cancers.
  3. It has been shown to have an inhibitory effect on the growth of cancer cells.
  4. The fruit restores the sinews and reduces deep-rooted swellings.
  5. The seed cure furuncles.
  6. It is said to clear fever and treat Heat and swelling.
  7. The root facilitates urination, especially with Caulis Akebiae Mu Tong.
  8. It also treats traumatic injury.
  9. It is said to treat illnesses caused by Wind.
  10. It treats Yuan Qi Deficiency in men and lochioschesis in women.
