ACTIONS AND INDICATIONS

Actions Indications/Syndromes

Clears Heat, cools the Blood, invigorates Blood circulation, relieves Fire toxins and encourages rashes

Xue Stage Heat, Blood Heat, Toxic Heat and dark purple rashes (eczema, measles, chicken pox)

Moistens the Intestines, unblocks the bowels and promotes urination

Blood Heat constipation and rashes accompanied by constipation and/or urinary dysfunction

Clears Damp-Heat from the skin (aids the healing of tissues)

Damp-Heat skin lesions or vaginal itching topically

Burns and wounds topically

CONTRAINDICATIONS, INCOMPATIBILITIES AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with Spleen and Stomach Deficiency with diarrhea or loose stools.
  • Contraindicated for those with Spleen Deficient pox or sores.
  • Contraindicated for those with measles if rash has already appeared.
  • Do not mix or store near urine, horse manure or smoke.
  • Long-term administration of high doses may cause liver cancer.

NOTES

  1. Arnebia is soft and its root is called “soft purple herb”.
  2. Lithospermi is harder and is called “hard purple herb”.
  3. Most sources say Lithospermi is the proper herb for Zi Cao.
  4. This herb has been shown to inhibit the growth of cancer cells.
  5. It is used in the treatment of skin cancer and breast tumors.
  6. It is used as a preventative for measles - 91% effective.
  7. Treats most skin diseases and cuts and burns.
  8. It is said to cure malignant sores, eruptions, smallpox and tinea.
  9. This herb is reported to cure jaundice with flaming fever in the morning. It is not effective for those with red or black flecks on the body.
  10. For those with red or black flecks on the body, use this herb to fumigate the arches of the feet, palms and points DU-20 and LI-8.
  11. For smallpox, take a decoction of 3g Zi Cao, 1.5g Per. Citri Reticulatae Chen Pi and 4g Bul. Allii Macrostemi Xie Bai.
  12. Western relatives are borage for fevers and comfrey as a “cure all” including cancer.
  13. Facilitates the flow of urine.
  14. Both Zi Cao and Fr. Arctii Niu Bang Zi resolve toxicity, vent rashes, moisten the bowels and unblock the Intestines. Zi Cao is best for clearing and regulating Blood Heat with a lesser ability to eliminate Damp-Heat. It treats unexpressed rashes due to Blood Heat toxin. Niu Bang Zi mobilizes the Qi level, releases the Exterior, disperses Wind-Heat, benefits the throat, disperses clumps, drains the In Interior, descends Qi and resolves Heat toxin. It treats rashes due to Wind-Heat confined in the Exterior.
