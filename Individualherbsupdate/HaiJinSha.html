INDIVIDUAL HERBS

NAME: HAI JIN SHA - 海金沙 - SPORA LYGODII

Pharmaceutical Latin: Spora Lygodii
Common English: Lygodium Spore
Japanese Fern

CATEGORY

Herbs that Drain Dampness (Diuretics)

PROPERTIES

Taste Temperature Entering Meridians Dosage
Sweet

ACTIONS AND INDICATIONS

Actions Indications/Syndromes

Clears Heat, promotes urination, unblocks painful urinary dysfunction and expels stones

Damp-Heat Painful Urinary Dysfunction of any type, especially Stone Lin or Blood Lin

Can be used for biliary stones

Eliminates Damp-Heat, reduces edema and decreases jaundice

Swelling and fullness due to Spleen Dampness and Damp-Heat jaundice

CONTRAINDICATIONS, INCOMPATIBILITIES AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with urinary disturbance due to Yin Deficiency.
  • Overdose can cause numb tongue, nausea, dizziness, aversion to Cold and urinary frequency.

NOTES

  1. This herb excels in draining Damp-Heat lurking at the Blood level of the Small Intestine and Bladder channels while unblocking urinary painful urinary dysfunction and resolving toxicity.
  2. Many sources consider this herb to be in the “Clear Heat and Relieve Toxicity” category.
  3. This is an essential herb for treating the pain and discomfort associated with all types of painful urinary dysfunction.
  4. Both Hai Jin Sha and Fol. Pyrrosiae Shi Wei clear Damp-Heat, unblock painful urinary dysfunction and are used to treat Hot painful urinary dysfunction (Lin Syndrome) or hematuria. Shi Wei is especially good for bloody painful urinary dysfunction and Damp-Heat painful urinary dysfunction. it also treats cough and wheezing by clearing Lung Heat and irregular uterine bleeding by cooling Blood and stopping bleeding in the Lower Jiao. Hai Jin Sha is particularly effective at treating stony painful urinary dysfunction.
  5. Both Hai Jin Sha and Hb. Lysimachiae Jin Qian Cao facilitate urination, unblock painful urinary dysfunction and are commonly used together to treat Stone Lin leading to rough, painful urination. Hai Jin Sha clears Heat and unblocks Lin Syndrome, but also enters the Blood level to cool the Blood to treat Bloody Lin. Jin Qian Cao excels at facilitating the removal of Dampness and relieving jaundice and is often used to treat Damp-Heat jaundice and gallstones.
  6. Hb. Lygodii Jin Sha Teng clears Toxic Heat, promotes urination and treats pneumonia, hemoptysis, toothache, urodynia, dysuria, lumbar sprain and hepatitis. Its dosage is 15-60g.
