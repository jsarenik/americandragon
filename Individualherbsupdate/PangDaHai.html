INDIVIDUAL HERBS

NAME: PANG DA HAI - 胖大海 - SEMEN STERCULIAE LYCHNOPHORAE

Pharmaceutical Latin: Semen Sterculiae Lychnophorae
Common English: Sterculia Seed
Boat Sterculia Seed
Boat-fringed Sterculia Seed

CATEGORY

Herbs that Transform Phlegm and Stop Coughing: Herbs that Cool and Transform Phlegm-Heat

PROPERTIES

Taste Temperature Entering Meridians Dosage
Sweet
(Slightly Sweet)
Cold
Large Intestine
Lung
(Stomach)

ACTIONS AND INDICATIONS

Actions Indications/Syndromes

Clears and disseminates Lung Qi

Sore throat, hoarseness, Phlegm-Heat cough and constrained Lung Qi

Used alone as a tea for hoarseness

Moistens the Intestines and unblocks the bowels

Mild to moderate constipation due to Heat clumping in the Intestines, often with concurrent headache, red eyes and fever

Can be used alone for mild cases

Encourages the expression of rashes

Adjunctively, as an external wash, for incomplete expression of rashes

NOTES

  1. Some sources say that this herb eliminates Heat and toxin, moistens the Lung and clears the throat to treat toothache, dry cough with no sputum, throat inflammation with hoarseness, tonsillitis and constipation.
  2. This herb excels at disseminating Lung Qi while simultaneously in blocking and draining the Intestines.
  3. Both Pang Da Hai and Rx. Platycodi Jie Geng disseminate Lung Qi, cool the Lungs, resolve toxicity and benefit the throat. Jie Geng is more intense. It raises Lung Qi, releases the Exterior, benefits the throat by eliminating Phlegm and pus and directs the actions of the other herbs to the Upper Jiao. It also treats dysenteric disorder and abdominal pain. Pang Da Hai is cooling, moistening, drains constrained Fire and is used specifically used for throat disorders. it also moistens the Intestines and treats mild cases of constipation and blood in the stool.
  4. Both Pang Da Hai and Fr. Momordicae Luo Han Guo cool Lung Heat, lubricate the Intestines, are gentle and are often used as stand-alone herbs for sore throats and coughs. Pang Da Hai disseminates and disperses and excels at opening and spreading the Lung Qi and cooling and draining Fire from constraint. It is primarily indicated for hoarseness, loss of voice, sore throat and Phlegm-Heat cough due to blocked Lung Qi from constraint. Luo Han Guo moistens and cools to threat Phlegm-Fire cough and constipation. It is stronger at moistening Intestines than Pang Da Hai.
  5. Pang Da Hai and Sm. Oroxyli Mu Hu Die are two of the best herbs to restore loss of voice. They can be taken as tea throughout the day for those who need to speak or sing for a prolonged period of time.
