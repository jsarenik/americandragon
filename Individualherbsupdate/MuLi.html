INDIVIDUAL HERBS

NAME: MU LI - 牡蠣 - CONCHA OSTREAE - OYSTER SHELL

Pharmaceutical Latin: Concha Ostreae
Common English: Oyster Shell

CATEGORY

Substances that Calm the Spirit: Substances that Anchor, Settle and Calm the Spirit (Substances that Calm the Liver and Extinguish Wind)

PROPERTIES

Taste Temperature Entering Meridians Dosage
Salty
Astringent
Cool
Liver
Kidney
(Gallbladder)
(Heart)
9-30g cook first when used in decoction

ACTIONS AND INDICATIONS

Actions Indications/Syndromes

Heavily settles and calms the Spirit

Agitated Heart and Spirit with palpitations with anxiety, restlessness and insomnia

Calms the Liver, benefits Yin and anchors Floating Yang

Liver Yin Deficiency with Liver Yang Rising or Yin Deficiency with internal Heat with restlessness, irritability, palpitations and spontaneous sweating

Prevents the leakage of fluids

Continuous sweat after febrile disease or in patients with steaming bone disorder

Loss of fluids due to Deficiency with night sweats, spontaneous sweating, nocturnal emissions, spermatorrhea or vaginal discharge and uterine bleeding

Softens hardness and dissipates nodules

Various kinds of lumps particularly neck lumps such as goiter or scrofula

Absorbs acidity and alleviates pain (calcined)

Excessive Stomach pain with a sour taste in the mouth

CONTRAINDICATIONS, INCOMPATIBILITIES AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with high fever due to Excess with an absence of sweat.
  • Contraindicated for those with Deficiency disorders with Cold.
  • Contraindicated for those with Kidney Deficiency without Fire.
  • Contraindicated for those with spermatorrhea.
  • Contraindicated for those with Damp-Heat.
  • Overdose may lead to indigestion and constipation.
INCOMPATIBILITIES
  • It antagonizes Fr. Evodiae Wu Zhu Yu.
  • It antagonizes Hb. Asari Xi Xin.
  • It antagonizes Hb. Ephedrae Ma Huang.

NOTES

  1. This herb works synergistically with Bul. Fritillariae Chuan Bei Mu or Zhe Bei Mu, Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao, Rx. Achyranthis Bidentatae Niu Xi and Rx. Polygalae Yuan Zhi.
  2. This herb is better a dispersing Phlegm than Fossilia Ossis Mastodi Long Gu.
  3. It is combined with Fossilia Ossis Mastodi Long Gu to treat insomnia, neurosis, epilepsy and other disorders requiring stronger sedative agents.
  4. This herb is produced by an animal, but the material is basically a mineral. It must be crushed for use in decoction.
  5. This herb has been shown to inhibit the growth of cancer cells.
  6. It is said that it eliminates the sense of fullness and rigidity of the hypochondrium, scrofula and all types of furuncles.
  7. It relieves Heart and Spleen pain due to Qi Stagnation, leukorrhea with a reddish discharge, goiter and tuberculosis.
  8. For ulcerous paronychia and phalangeal polyps with pus, grind the thick head into a powder and take 6g each time with an alcohol decoction of Flos Carthami Hong Hua. Take three times a day orally and apply to the lesion topically.
  9. To draw out pus from a painful swelling without festering, blend the powder in water and apply to the affected area frequently.
  10. For carbuncle, apply the powder to the area and put egg white around it. Do this frequently.
  11. It is said that the cooked meat of this herb regulates the Middle Jiao, cures consumptive and inflammatory diseases and menoxenia. When taken raw with ginger, it can cure inflammatory diseases and relieve thirst and irritation caused by drinking.
  12. The cooked meat treats consumptive diseases, inflammatory diseases, menoxenia and regulates the Middle Jiao
  13. The meat taken raw with ginger and vinegar treats inflammatory diseases and relieves thirst and irritation caused by drinking.
  14. One source says that this herb softens hard lumps, dispels Phlegm, eliminates scrofula, relieves hiccups, restores sperm and cures metrorrhagia and leukorrhagia.
  15. Both Long Gu and Concha Ostreae Mu Li are similar in many of their qualities and are often used together. Long Gu is sweet, astringent enters the Heart and Liver channels and is better at stopping abandonment, suppressing fright calming the Spirit. It does not soften hardness or disperse clumping and has much less Yin-nourishing ability so is less used when Yin Deficiency causes night sweats. Mu Li is salty, astringent, enters the Kidneys to soften hardness, transform Phlegm and cool Heat.
  16. Calcined Oyster Shell Duan Mu Li has an enhanced ability to bind and an added ability to dry so that it astringes sweating and Jing and stops vaginal discharge. It secures the Lower Jiao, expels turbid Dampness and inhibits Deficiency sweating.
