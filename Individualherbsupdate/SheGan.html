INDIVIDUAL HERBS

NAME: SHE GAN - 射干 - RHIZOMA BELAMCANDE

Pharmaceutical Latin: Rhizoma Belamcandae
Common English: Belamcanda Rhizome
Blackberry Lily Rhizome

CATEGORY

Herbs that Clear Heat and Purge Fire: Herbs that Clear Heat and Detoxify; Target Warm and Heat Diseases

PROPERTIES

Taste Temperature Entering Meridians Dosage

ACTIONS AND INDICATIONS

Actions Indications/Syndromes

Clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity and improves the throat

Swollen, painful throat due to Fire Excess, Fire toxin or Phlegm-Heat obstruction

Can be used alone for sore throat

Transforms Phlegm, clears the Lungs and eliminates wheezing

Cough and wheezing with Phlegm obstruction, Lung-Heat asthma

Combined with warming herbs for Phlegm Obstruction due to Cold-Phlegm

CONTRAINDICATIONS, INCOMPATIBILITIES AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with loose stools or diarrhea due to Spleen Deficiency Cold.
  • Do not use for an extended period of time.
  • Contraindicated during pregnancy.

NOTES

  1. Some sources say that this herb descends Qi and disperses Blood.
  2. It is used externally as a wash with a little salt to treat field dermatitis.
  3. Both She Gan and Lasiosphere/Calvatia Ma Bo clear Heat, benefit the throat, resolve toxicity, disperse clumped heat and are frequently used for swelling and pain of the throat. She Gan is bitter, cold, descends pathogenic Heat and transforms Phlegm. Ma Bo is lightweight and dispersing, acrid neutral, focuses on the Lungs and the Blood to treat epistaxis and treats swelling and pain of the throat due to Wind-Heat.
  4. Dry-fried Belamcanda Chao She Gan is less cold and bitter and is appropriate for deficient patients who have a sore, swollen throat.
  5. She Gan and Rx. Sophorae Tonkinensis Shan Dou Gen have similar actions of clearing Heat. She Gan is stronger at dispelling Phlegm and dispersing Stagnation, while Shan Dou Gen is stronger at sedating Fire.
