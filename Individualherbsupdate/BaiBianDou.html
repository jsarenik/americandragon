NAME: BAI BIAN DOU - 白扁豆 - SEMEN LABLAB ALBUM - HYACINTH BEAN

Pharmaceutical Latin: Semen Lablab Album
Semen Dolichorus Lablab
Common English: Hyacinth Bean
Dolichos

CATEGORY

Herbs that Clean Heat and Purge Fire: Herbs that Clean and Relieve Summerheat

PROPERTIES

ACTIONS AND INDICATIONS

Actions Indications/Syndromes

Clears Summerheat and resolves Dampness

Summerheat with pronounced or chronic diarrhea, dysentery, nausea, vomiting and loud borborygmus

Strengthens the Spleen

Spleen Deficiency

CONTRAINDICATIONS, INCOMPATIBILITIES AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS:

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with intermittent fevers and chills.
  • Contraindicated for those Cold disorders.
  • The prepared form is preferred as the raw form is considered toxic.

NOTES

  1. Its main use is for digestive ailments related to ingestion of contaminated food and drink (Summerheat), summer colds and alcoholic intoxication.
  2. It may help treat diabetes.
  3. This herb can be used alone to treat poultry poisoning, calomel poisoning or powdered for arsenic poisoning.
  4. It is said that snakebites can be cured with External application of the smashed leaves of this herb.
  5. To treat spasms that occur after vomiting or diarrhea, add a little vinegar to a handful of smashed leaves of this herb, squeeze out the juice and apply it to the affected area.
  6. It is said that this herb cures Spleen and Stomach Deficiency with nausea, regurgitation, chronic diarrhea, dyspepsia, abdominal masses and infantile malnutrition.
  7. This herb has been shown to inhibit the growth of cancer cells.
  8. It is said that Stomach cancer and other tumors can be with the juice extracted from 750g of the leaves of this herb. Take frequently without dosage restriction.
  9. Toast to treat Spleen Deficiency.
  10. Lablab Testa Bian Dou Yi is the dried capsule of the hyacinth bean. It regulates the function of the Stomach and Spleen when diarrhea occur in the summer. Its dosage is 6-12g.
  11. Flos Lablab Bian Dou Hua is the dried flower of the hyacinth bean. It relieves Summerheat, removes Dampness and controls bleeding and is used to treat dysentery, diarrhea and leukorrhea. Its dosage is 6-12g.
