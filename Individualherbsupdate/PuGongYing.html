NAME: PU GONG YING - 蒲公英 - HERBA TARAXACI - DANDELION

Pharmaceutical Latin: Herba Taraxaci
Common English: Dandelion
Taraxacum
Mongolian Dandelion

CATEGORY

Herbs That Clear Heat and Purge Fire: Herbs That Clear Heat and Detoxify; Target Warm and Heat Diseases

ACTIONS AND INDICATIONS

Actions Indications/Syndromes

Reduces abscesses and dissipates nodules

Internal abscesses and external sores particularly if they are firm and hard, especially breast and intestinal abscess, Chuang Yung (internally and topically)

Clears the Liver and clears the eyes

Eye redness and swelling (alone as a steam)

Liver Heat, cirrhosis

Clears Heat, resolves Dampness and unblocks painful urinary dysfunction

Damp-Heat painful urinary dysfunction

Damp-Heat jaundice

Relieves Fire toxicity

Fire toxicity , swollen, painful throat, upper respiratory tract infection

Promotes lactation

Insufficient lactation due to Heat

Clears Heat in the Blood

Congested Fire of the Spleen and Stomach

CONTRAINDICATIONS, INCOMPATIBILITIES AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Overdose can cause mild diarrhea.
  • Contraindicated for those with Yin sore or carbuncles where there is no redness or pain.
  • May cause allergic reactions such as pruritis, sensations of Heat, urticaria, abdominal discomfort, nausea and vomiting.
  • Contraindicated in Middle Jiao Deficiency.
  • Contraindicated for Cold Blood.
  • There may be heartburn on oral administration.
INCOMPATIBILITIES
HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS
  • Concurrent use with quinolone antibiotics such as Ciprofloxacin may influence the bioavailability and disposition of the quinolone antibiotics.

NOTES

  1. This her may be used singly or with other herbs in decoction.
  2. Local dandelion can be used and should be at least two years old.
  3. This herb excels at draining, directing downward and dispersing Qi Stagnation and clumping.
  4. It is traditionally used for breast abscesses and other sores – both internally and topically.
  5. For women's breast abscess and edema, boil into a juice to both drink and apply. It shows immediate results.
  6. It is really wonderful for eye diseases. It is quite effective in treating dysphagia.
  7. This herb has shown to inhibit the growth of cancer cells.
  8. It is a key herb for eliminating pathogenic Heat and cooling the Blood.
  9. It is said to cure female breast abscess and breast cancer.
  10. Fresh Taraxacum Xian Pu Gong Ying has a stronger cooling and detoxification function than the dried herb. It has a wider scope of action and is most effective when drunk and applied topically.
  11. Smaller doses are more stimulating and restoring, while larger doses are more Heat-clearing and draining.
  12. Both Pu Gong Ying and Hb. Violae Zi Hua Di Ding cool Heat, resolve toxicity, reduce swelling and disperse clumping. Pu Gong Ying is better at dredging constraint and dispersing clumping (focusing on the Liver channel), while Zi Hua Di Ding is better at cooling the Blood and resolving toxicity (focusing on the Heart and Liver channels).
  13. Both Pu Gong Ying and Hb. Patriniae Bai Jiang Cao cool Heat, resolve toxicity and reduce swollen abscess, better at dredging constraint and dispersing clumping (focusing on the Liver and Stomach channels), while Bai Jiang Cao is better at eliminating Stasis and expelling pus.
  14. Both Pu Gong Ying and Rz. Paridis Chong Lou resolve toxicity, reduce swelling and alleviate pain. Pu gong Ying also promotes urination, unblocks painful urinary dysfunction and treats jaundice, while Chong Lou calms fright while cooling the Liver. It also resolves snakebite toxins and treats many types of cancers.
  15. Both Pu Gong Ying and Flos Lonicerae Jin Yin Hua are effective in reducing swellings and ulcerations. Pu Gong Ying only enters the Liver and Stomach while Jin Yin Hua enters every channel but cannot exert its full effect without being combined with Pu Gong Ying.
