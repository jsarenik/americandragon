INDIVIDUAL HERBS

NAME: YE JIAO TENG - 首乌藤 - (SHOU WU TENG) - (何首藤) - CAULIS POLYGONI MULTIFLORI

Pharmaceutical Latin: Caulis Polygoni Multiflori
Common English: Polygonum Vine
Fleeceflower Caulis
Flowery Knotwood Stem

CATEGORY

Substances that Calm the Spirit: Herbs that Nourish the Heart and Calm the Spirit

PROPERTIES

Taste Temperature Entering Meridians Dosage
Sweet

ACTIONS AND INDICATIONS

Actions Indications/Syndromes

Nourishes the Heart Yin and Blood and calms the Spirit

Heart Yin Deficiency or Heart Blood Deficiency with insomnia and irritability

Especially for dream-disturbed sleep

Unblocks channels and dispels Wind-Dampness

General weakness, soreness, pain and numbness due to Blood Deficiency

Backache and aching limbs due to Wind-Damp Bi

Alleviates itching (topical)

Itching and skin rashes (topically as a wash)

Astringes sweat

Profuse perspiration from Deficiency

NOTES

  1. It calms mental hyperactivity by strengthening the Heart and consolidating Qi.
  2. It is used as an external wash to treat Wind sores and scabies.
  3. The root of the same plant is Rx. Polygoni Multiflori He Shou Wu which Nourishes Blood.
  4. Both Ye Jiao Teng and Cx. Albizziae He Huan Pi calm the Spirit. Ye Jiao Teng nourishes the Heart and Liver Blood and expels Wind in the collaterals to stop itch and treat skin disorders. He Huan Pi soothes the Heart and Spleen Qi , invigorates the Blood, stops pain and reconnects sinews and bones.
  5. Ye Jiao Teng, Flos Albizziae He Huan Hua and Sm. Platycladi Bai Zi Ren all treat insomnia. Ye Jiao Teng treats insomnia due to Blood Deficiency, He Huan Hua treats insomnia due to Liver Qi Stagnation and Bai Zi Ren treats insomnia due to Heart Deficiency.
