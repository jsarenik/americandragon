ACTIONS AND INDICATIONS

Actions Indications/Syndromes

Clears Liver Fire and brightens the eyes

Liver Fire with red, painful or swollen eyes or headache and dizziness, double vision, watery eyes

Eye pain which is worse in the evening due to Liver Yin Deficiency, especially when the eyes are neither red nor swollen

Clears Hot Phlegm and dissipates nodules

Any neck lumps or nodules due to Phlegm-Fire such as scrofula, lipoma, carbuncle, swollen glands or goiter thyroid or enlarged lymph glands

Treats hypertension

Hypertension due to Liver Fire or Liver Yang Rising, headaches, Dizziness

NOTES

  1. Typically used when Liver Fire and Stagnation combine to produce hot swelling.
  2. This herb is used in Western herbology as an astringent and tonic - the whole top is used.
  3. Its hypotensive action is the subject of much research at the present time.
  4. This herb is good at removing neurological symptoms in the head due to heavy metal poisoning.
  5. This herb has been shown to inhibit the growth of cancer cells.
  6. It is used to treat breast abscess and breast cancer.
  7. This herb is said to treat circulatory disorders, tuberculosis, hepatic inflammation, throat and mouth inflammation, thrush and Internal bleeding.
  8. Long-term consumption of Xia Ku Cao as a syrup has a reliable therapeutic effect of scrofula.
  9. Fresh Prunella Xian Xia Ku Cao has stronger Heat-clearing and Fire-draining properties and is often used for swelling and pain in the eyes. It can be taken internally and applied externally.
  10. Prunella Dew Xia Ku Cao Lu is the aromatic distillate of the fresh spikes and has the same effects as Xia Ku Cao . It is a popular beverage for summertime Heat. It can be given to children to prevent Summerheat or summertime rash and can be used as a drink for scrofula or thyroid conditions. It moderates the cold effect of Xia Ku Cao on the Stomach and can be taken for a long time.
