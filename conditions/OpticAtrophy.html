OPTIC ATROPHY(OPTIC NERVE ATROPHY)

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • Progressive decrease in vision leading to blindness
  • Abnormal visual field and color sensations
  • Light color of optic papillae
Treatment Principle
Herb Formulas
Points
  • DU-14, UB-1, UB-18, UB-20, UB-23
  • GB-20
  • GB-37, LI-4, ST-36, UB-1
  • GB-20, LI-4, ST-1, ST-36, UB-18, UB-23
  • GB-20, GB-37, Jianming #1, Jianming #2, Shangming, ST-36
  • Inflammation: GB-20, GB-37, Qiuhou, SI-6, UB-1
  • Green blindness:
    1. GB-1, GB-3, LI-1, ST-3, UB-6, UB-8
    2. 7 cones of moxa on SI-6, UB-18, UB-19, UB-23, 5 cones of moxa on GB-37, LI-1
  • Sudden blindness: DU-20, DU-21, DU-22, DU-23, DU-24, UB-2, Prick to bleed UB-40

SPLEEN QI DEFICIENCY

Clinical Manifestations
  • Dizziness
  • Tired vision
  • Sallow complexion
  • Fatigue
  • Anorexia
  • Four limbs feel tired and heavy
  • Abdominal and epigastric distention after eating
  • Maybe acid regurgitation or vomiting
  • Loose stools
  • Shortness of breath
  • T: Pale and flabby - may have teethmarks
  • C: White
  • P: Even, forceless and deficient
Treatment Principle
  • Strengthen the Spleen
  • Regulate the Stomach
  • Benefit Qi
  • Ascend Yang
Herb Formulas
  • Bu Zhong Yi Qi Tang
  • Yi Qi Cong Ming Tang
  • Xiang Sha Liu Jun Zi Tang
  • Shen Ling Bai Zhu San
Points
  • LIV-13, REN-12, SP-3, SP-5, SP-6, ST-36, UB-20 + general points

SPLEEN AND KIDNEY YANG DEFICIENCY

Clinical Manifestations
  • Fear
  • Body cold
  • Limbs icy
  • Complexion white and shiny
  • Low back, knees and abdomen feel cold and painful
  • Lower abdomen feels cold and painful
  • Loose stools containing undigested food
  • “Cock-crow” diarrhea (occurs at about 5 am)
  • Edema of face and limbs
  • Dysuria
  • Severe:
    Abdominal ascites
  • T: Pale, moist and swollen
  • C: Thin and white or White and greasy
  • P: Deep, thready, weak and maybe slow
Treatment Principle
  • Warm and Reinforce the Spleen and Kidneys
  • Warm Yang
Herb Formulas
  • Jin Gui Shen Qi Wan
  • You Gui Wan
  • With Phlegm Stagnation: Ling Gui Zhu Gan Tang
Points
  • Moxa or moxa needle REN-4, REN-12, SP-6, SP-9, ST-36, UB-20, UB-23

HEART YANG NOT VIGOROUS: WATER QI INSULTS THE HEART WITH LUNG AND SPLEEN QI DEICIENCY

Clinical Manifestations
  • Palpitations
  • Shortness of breath
  • Dizziness
  • Epigastric fullness and contravection (reflux vomiting)
  • Sensation of Qi rushing upward to the chest (Running Piglet)
  • Suffocating sensation in chest
  • Weak cough
  • Expectorating watery white Phlegm
  • T: Pale
  • C: White
  • P: Deep and wiry
Treatment Principle
  • Clear Yang
  • Transform Harmful Fluid
Herb Formulas
  • Ling Gui Zhu Gan Tang
Points
  • Moxa or moxa needle LU-9, REN-12, REN-17, ST-36, ST-40, UB-13, UB-20, UB-43

LIVER WIND STIRRING INTERNALLY: LIVER FIRE (YANG) GENERATES WIND

Clinical Manifestations
  • Commonly seen in children
  • Both eyes affected
  • Deviation of the eyes and mouth
  • enlarged pupils
  • Anxiety
  • Rigidity
  • Dizziness and vertigo to the point of falling over
  • Headache with a drawing sensation
  • Numbness and tremor of limbs
  • Twitching and involuntary writhing of hands and feet
  • Dysphasia
  • Unsteady gait
  • With Wind-Stroke:
    Sudden loss of consciousness
    Tongue stiffness with aphasia
    Facial paralysis
    Hemiplegia
  • Maybe coma
  • Sometimes death
  • T: Red
  • C: Little
  • P: Strong and wiry
Treatment Principle
  • Clear Heat
  • Detoxify
  • Pacify the Liver
  • Subdue Wind
Herb Formulas
  • Gou Ten Xi Feng Yin
  • Zi Jin Ding
  • If necessary: An Gong Niu Huang Wan
Points
  • Bleed the Jing-Well points of Six Hand Channels, needle DU-20, DU-26, GB-20, GB-34, KI-1, KI-3, LIV-2, LIV-3, PC-8, UB-18, UB-23
