ALLERGIC RHINITIS

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • Itchy nose, mouth, eyes, throat, skin, or any area
  • Problems with smell
  • Runny nose
  • Sneezing
  • Tearing eyes
  • Tends to occur at the same time each year

Symptoms that may develop later include:

  • Stuffy nose (nasal congestion)
  • Coughing
  • Clogged ears and decreased sense of smell
  • Sore throat
  • Dark circles under the eyes
  • Puffiness under the eyes
  • Fatigue and irritability
  • Headache
  • Memory problems and slowed thinking
Treatment Principle
  • Dispel Wind
  • Unblock the nose
Herb Formulas
  • Qing Bi Tang
  • For perennial allergic rhinitis:
    Xin Yi San + Xiao Qing Long Tang + Xiang Sha Liu Jun Zi Tang
  • Topical: Medicated Oil #1
Points
  • UB-7, UB-3, DU-24, Yintang, LI-20 through to Bitong, LI-4, LU-7, GB-20
    For severe sinus congestion: UB-2
    For sore throat: PC-6, ST-9
