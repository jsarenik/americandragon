GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • Premature ovarian failure, a.k.a. premature menopause, refers to signs and symptoms of estrogen deficiency in women below 40 years of age. There may be any of several immune disturbances.

KIDNEY YIN DEFICIENCY WITH SPLEEN QI AND BLOOD DEFICIENCY

Clinical Manifestations
  • A dark, gloomy or sallow facial complexion
  • Dizziness
  • Tinnitus
  • Soreness and weakness of the low back and knees
  • Possibly a dry mouth and throat
  • Tidal fever
  • Night sweats
  • Both cheeks tidally red
  • Five sole Heat
  • Dry skin
  • Dry, astringent eyelids
  • Constipation
  • Scanty, reddish urination
  • Heart palpitations
  • Fatigue
  • Anorexia
  • Four limbs feel tired and heavy
  • Abdominal and epigastric distention after eating
  • Maybe acid regurgitation or vomiting
  • Loose stools
  • Shortness of breath
  • T: Red or Pale or Pale and flabby - may have teethmarks
  • C: Scanty or White
  • P: Even, forceless and Deficient or Deep and wiry or Deep and thready or Possibly floating at the inch and/or cubit positions if Yin fails to control Yang or Rapid if there is Heat
Treatment Principle
  • Nourish Yin
  • Nourish the Kidneys
  • Nourish the Blood
  • Tonify the Spleen
  • Augment Qi
Herb Formulas
  • Liu Wei Di Huang Wan + Gui Pi Tang
  • Chinese Patent Medicine: Fu Ke Yang Rong Wan
Points
  • DU-20, KI-3, PC-6, REN-6, SP-6, SP-10, ST-36, UB-20, UB-23, Zigong

BLOOD STAGNATIN IN THE UTERUS

Clinical Manifestations
  • Palpable masses that may or may not be painful
  • Lower abdominal pain without palpable masses
  • Lower abdominal distention
  • Lower back pain
  • Lower abdominal distention during menstruation
  • Frequent menstruation (3-5 timer per month)
  • Spotting
  • Dark purple menstrual Blood (usually with clots)
  • Abnormal uterine bleeding with abdominal soreness and pain
  • Aversion to Cold
  • Depression
  • Cold extremities
  • White vaginal discharge
  • T: Pale
  • C: White
  • P: Choppy
Treatment Principle
  • Invigorate the Blood
  • Promote the movement of Qi
  • Dispel Blood Stasis
  • Warm and regulate menstruation
  • Unblock the collaterals
  • Alleviate pain
Herb Formulas
  • Xue Fu Zhu Yu Tang
  • Shao Fu Zhu YU Tang
  • Tao Hong Si Wu Wan
  • Gui Zhi Fu Ling Wan
Points
  • LI-4, REN-2, SP-8, SP-10, ST-25, ST-29
