GASTROPTOSIS (GASTRIC/STOMACH PROLAPSE)

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • Gastroptosis is the abnormal downward displacement of the stomach. It is not a life-threatening condition. The condition frequently causes digestive symptoms and constipation and is much more prominent in women than men.
Treatment Principle
Herb Formulas
Points
  • ST-21, ST-22, UB-10, UB-11, UB17, UB-18, UB-20, UB-22

YANG MING HAN

Clinical Manifestations
  • History of Yang Ming Jing or Fu

SHAO YANG JING

TAI YIN EXCESS

Clinical Manifestations
  • Nausea
  • Clear, frequent urination
  • Lack of thirst
  • Anorexia
  • Vomiting of clear fluid
  • Abdominal distension and pain (sharp)
  • Diarrhea or loose stool
  • Prefers warmth
  • T: Pale or pink
  • C: White
  • P: Slow and tight or Soft
Treatment Principle

  • Warm the Middle Jiao
  • Disperse Cold
  • Relieve pain
Herb Formulas
  • Gui Zhi Jia Shao Yao Tang
  • Li Zhong Tang
  • With Excess Cold: Fu Zi Li Zhong Tang
  • With a need to purge: Fu Gui Li Zhong Tang
Points
  • For pain and Cold: moxa the abdomen
  • To strengthen the Spleen and Stomach: LIV-13, REN-10, ST-36, UB-20, UB-21 (moxa if severe)
  • For abdominal pain and nausea: PC-6, SP-4, ST-25
  • For frequent, clear urination: moxa REN-4, REN-6, UB-23
  • For diarrhea: moxa REN-8
  • Use few needles with deep insertion and moxa

  • Easily satiated
  • Fatigue
  • Weak, tired limbs
  • Loose stools or
  • Diarrhea or
  • A tendency toward sluggish bowel movements
  • Puffiness around the eyes or in the fingers
  • Rapid weight fluctuations
  • A pale, sallow face and lips
  • Shortness of breath
  • Reluctance to speak
