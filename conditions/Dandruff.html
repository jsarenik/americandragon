DANDRUFF, FLAKING HAIR OR SCALP

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
Treatment Principle
Herb Formulas
  • Topical: Bai Ai Tang
Points

WIND-DAMPNESS

Clinical Manifestations
  • Dandruff
  • Mild generalized body aches
  • Slight aversion to Wind
  • Chronic respiratory complaints
  • Fever that worsens in the afternoon
  • Dry skin
Treatment Principle
  • Release the Exterior
  • Dispel Wind-Dampness
  • Resolve Phlegm
Herb Formulas
  • Ma Xing Yi Gan Tang
Points

LIVER WIND STIRRING INTERNALLY: LIVER BLOOD DEFICIENCY GENERATES WIND

Clinical Manifestations
  • Dandruff
  • Pale complexion
  • Pale lips
  • Blurred or failing vision
  • Spots before eyes
  • Dizziness
  • Dry eyes
  • Pale nail beds
  • Irritability
  • Frightens easily
  • Insomnia with much dreaming
  • Tinnitus
  • Numbness of limbs
  • Tremor, spasms or involuntary movements (chorea)
  • Involuntary shaking of head
  • Light menses or amenorrhea
  • Late menses
  • Tic or tremor of hands and feet
  • Maybe trismus
  • T: Pale
  • C: Little
  • P: Thready and wiry
Treatment Principle
  • Nourish the Blood
  • Subdue Wind
Herb Formulas
  • Si Wu Tang
  • Bu Gan Tang
Points
  • DU-16, DU-20, GB-20, LIV-3, REN-4, SP-6, ST-36, UB-17, UB-18, UB-20

YIN AND BLOOD DEFICIENCY

Clinical Manifestations
  • Alopecia occurs over the vertex or at the lateral frontal areas
  • The hair loss is insidious
  • Often affects people in their twenties and thirties
  • The scalp is frequently greasy or itchy with dandruff
  • Soreness of the lower back
  • Abdominal pain
  • Emaciation
  • Achy and uncomfortable flesh and bones of the hands and feet
  • Five-Sole-Heat
  • Anxiety
  • Dizziness
  • Light headedness
  • Tinnitus
  • Tidal fever
  • Fever with exhaustion
  • Recurrent fever with night sweats or hemoptysis
  • General disquietude
  • Gradually worsening afternoon fevers
  • T: Red
  • C: Scanty
  • P: Thready and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Nourish Yin and Blood
  • Clear Deficient Heat
Herb Formulas
  • Lu Shi Ban Tu Tang
Points

BLOOD DEFICIENCY

Clinical Manifestations
  • Dandruff
  • Dizziness
  • Palpitations
  • Insomnia
  • Dry skin
  • Dry hair
  • Poor memory
  • Paleness
  • Fatigue
  • Scanty periods
  • T: Pale
  • C: Thin, white and dry
  • P: Thready and rapid or Weak
Treatment Principle
  • Nourish Blood
  • Tonify the Spleen
Herb Formulas
  • Si Wu Tang
Points
  • LIV 3, SP-6, ST-36, UB 11, UB-17

BLOOD DEFICIENCY DUE TO WIND-DRYNESS

Clinical Manifestations
  • Dandruff with oily, bright hair and plenty of scales
  • Hair loss, typically starting from the vertex or temples
  • Itching of the head
  • Headache
  • Dizziness
  • Vertigo
  • Tinnitus
  • Blurred vision
  • Sensation of Heat rushing to the head
  • Insomnia
  • Dream disturbed sleep
  • Numbness
  • Twitching and spasms in the extremities
  • Trembling tongue
  • Light-headedness
  • Pallor
  • Broken skin
  • Dry skin
  • Sometimes bleeding and pain
  • Worse in Cold weather
  • Better during the summer
  • T: Pale or pink
  • C: Thin and white
  • P: Thin or Weak and taut or Deep or Wiry and rapid or Wiry, tight and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Enrich the Blood
  • Moisten Dryness
  • Expel Wind
  • Regulate Blood flow
Herb Formulas
  • Shen Ying Yang Zheng Dan
Points
  • DU-20, KI-3, LIV-8, SP-6, ST-8
    With Wind predominant (itching): + LU-7, SP-10
