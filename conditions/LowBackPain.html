WIND-COLD

Clinical Manifestations
  • Acute lower back ache, unrelieved by changing position or stretching
  • The pain is often accompanied by hypertonicity and a cold sensation in the low back
  • Wandering pain in the back and leg
  • Soreness or itching of the throat
  • Aversion to Wind and Cold
  • Tight sensation and tender points in the back area
  • Limitation of movement
  • When turning from side to side the pain may radiate to the upper back and make turning difficult
  • Stiffness and spasm of the back muscles
  • Symptoms are relieved by warmth and exacerbated by Wind-Cold
  • Fever and chills
  • Shivering
  • Stiff neck
  • Occipital headache
  • Cough with expectoration of thin, watery or frothy white sputum
  • Anhidrosis
  • No thirst
  • Aversion to Wind
  • Blocked and / or runny nose with clear, watery nasal discharge
  • Sneezing
  • General body aches
  • Maybe asthma
  • T: Pink
  • C: Thin, white and moist
  • P: Floating and tight or Floating and soggy

  • Expel Wind
  • Dispel Cold
  • Ventilate the Lungs
  • Resolve Phlegm
