LOWER ABDOMINAL MASSES

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • Masses in the lower abdomen may be accompanied by pain in the lower abdomen or low back, distension, bloating, infertility, irregular vaginal bleeding, irregular menses, leukorrhea and miscarriage.  They may be fibroids, tumors or cancer. 
Treatment Principle
Herb Formulas
Points

LIVER QI STAGNATION

Clinical Manifestations
  • A mass that moves from time to time
  • Distention and pain in the sides or flanks
  • Suffocating sensation in the chest
  • Tendency to sigh
  • Melancholy
  • Depression
  • Irritability
  • Inappropriate anger
  • Irregular menses
  • Dysmenorrhea
  • Breast distention and pain before and during menses
  • T: Pink
  • C: Thin and white
  • P: Wiry
Treatment Principle
  • Soothe the Liver
  • Clear Stagnation
Herb Formulas
  • Xiang Leng Wan
Points

BLOOD STAGNATION

Clinical Manifestations
  • A very hard mass in the lower abdomen that is fixed and painful
  • Pain is worse with pressure
  • Dark complexion
  • Bleeding with clots
  • Purple lips
  • Purple nails
  • Dry skin
  • Blue, dark or purplish discoloration of skin in small or large areas
  • Varicose and spider veins
  • Cold hands and feet
  • Tendency to hemorrhage dark blood or clots
  • Dark clotted, delayed, heavy menses
  • T: Purple, possible purple or red spots
    Red purple if there is Heat
    Blue purple if there is Cold
  • C: White
  • P: Choppy or Wiry and choppy
Treatment Principle
  • Invigorate the Blood
  • Break up Stagnation
Herb Formulas
  • Gui Zhi Fu Ling Wan
  • Da Huang Zhe Chong Wan
  • Dai Di Dang Wan
  • Zhe Chong Yin
  • With Heat: Qian Gen San
Points

BLOOD AND PHLEGM STAGNATION

Clinical Manifestations
  • Mild, persistent uterine bleeding of purple and black blood during pregnancy
  • Abdominal pain that is worse with pressure
  • Abdominal spasms and tension
  • Immobile masses in the lower abdomen with pain and tenderness
  • Amenorrhea with distention and pain
  • Dysmenorrhea
  • Headache
  • Sinus congestion
  • Constipation
  • Excess weight in the pelvis
  • Lower back ache
  • Irregular menstruation
  • Nervousness
  • Fatigue
  • Fetal instability
  • Skin eruptions
  • Scanty menstrual flow
  • Scaly, dry skin
  • Retention of lochia
  • Headache
  • Shoulder stiffness
  • Vertigo
  • Cold low body and feet
  • Flushed face
  • Protruding abdomen
  • T: Dark purple or with echymoses or petechiae or pale with purplish spots
  • C: White
  • P: Choppy or Tense, deep and slow
Treatment Principle
  • Invigorate the Blood
  • Transform Blood Stasis
  • Soften lumps
  • Reduce fixed abdominal pain
Herb Formulas
  • Gui Ling Xiang Liu Wan
Points

STAGNATION OF PHLEGM AND DAMPNESS

Clinical Manifestations
  • A mass that is soft and fluid filled or hard
  • Large amounts of leukorrhea that is sticky white
  • Cold body
  • Poor appetite
  • Uterine fibroma
  • Pain in the abdomen or low back
  • Infertility
  • Irregular vaginal bleeding
  • Irregular menses
  • Miscarriage
  • May be tumor, or cancer
  • Dizziness
  • Obesity
  • T: Purple
  • C: Greasy and white
  • P: Slippery and Deep
Treatment Principle
Herb Formulas
  • Kai Yu Er Chen Tang
  • Da Huang Mu Dan Tang
