NASAL OBSTRUCTION

WIND ATTACKS THE LUNGS

Clinical Manifestations
  • Nasal discharge
  • Sneezing
  • Itching eyes and nose
  • Red, swollen conjunctiva
  • Edema and vasodiation of the nasal mucus membrane
  • Maybe nasal obstruction
  • Nasal congestion
Treatment Principle
  • Release the exterior
Herb Formulas

  • Chuan Xiong Cha Tiao San
Points

  • Yintang, LI-4, LI-20, Bitong

  • GB-20, UB-10, DU-14, DU-16, DU-20, UB-12, LI-4, LI-19, LI-20, PC-8, SI-2
  • DU-23, LI-4, LIV-3
  • UB-7, DU-23, Yintang, LI-4

      1. GB-20, DU-23, Yintang, LI-4, LI-20

  • Ear: Internal nose, Adrenal, Endocrine

TAI YANG ZHONG FENG

Clinical Manifestations
  • Aversion to Wind
  • Mild, or severe chills
  • Fever which may be persistent
  • Uneasiness from fever
  • Spontaneous perspiration
  • Headache
  • Nausea
  • Nasal irritation
  • Nasal obstruction
  • Tendency to snore
  • Itchy throat
  • T:  Normal
  • C:  Thin and white
  • P:  Superficial and slow or Moderate and maybe weak
Treatment Principle
  • Create sweat to relieve the Exterior and expel Wind
  • Harmonize Ying and Wei to protect digestion and nourish Wei Qi
Herb Formulas
  • Gui Zhi Tang
  • San Niu Tang
  • With stiff neck and shoulders: Gui Zhi Jia Ge Gen Tang
  • With Dampness: Ren Shen Bai Du San
  • With dyspnea, asthma and sputum:
    Gui Zhi Jia Hou Po Xing Ren Tang
  • With slightly severe or severe chills after diaphoresis:
    Gui Zhi Qu Shao Yao Jia Fu Zi Tang
  • If, after diaphoresis, there is gasping without severe fever:
    Ma Xing Shi Gan Tang
Points
  • Bitong, DU-16, GB-20, KI-7, LI-4, LI-20, LU-7, SJ-5, UB-12, UB-13, Yintang
    With stiff neck and shoulders: + GB-21
    With Dampness: + SP-6, SP-9
    With dyspnea, asthma and sputum: Dingchuan, REN-22
    With nocturnal emission, excess dreams, Cold lower extremities, dizziness and hair loss: + DU-4, KI-3, UB-23
    With continuous perspiration, muscle spasms of the limbs with difficult flexion and extension and difficult urination: + REN-4
    With sustained fever of long duration accompanied by severe chills: + DU-14
