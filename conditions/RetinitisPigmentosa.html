RETINITIS PIGMENTOSA

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • Retinal degeneration, caused by the loss of light receptor nerve cell function
  • Genetically related, occurring most often in the offspring of carrier patients
  • Night blindness
  • Narrowed visual field (tunnel vision)
  • Vascular stenosis
Treatment Principle
Herb Formulas
  • Retinitis Pigmentosa #1
Points
  • ST-1, Jianming, Jianming #5, GB-20, UB-18, UB-20, UB-23
  • ST-2, Yintang, Taiyang, GB-20, Yuyao, GB-37, SI-6, LIV-3, triple needle DU-24
    For Heat with inflammation: LI-11
    For Blood Stagnation: SP-10
  • Local points: Taiyang, UB-1, UB-2, ST-1, GB-1, GB-14, GB-20, SJ-23, Qiuhou, Yuyao
  • Distal points: UB-18, UB-23, GB-37, LI-4, ST-36, SP-6, LIV-8, KI-7, KI-10
  • For congenital Deficiency and Deficiency of Ming Men Fire: REN-6, DU-4, UB-23
  • For Liver Blood and Kidney Jing Deficiency: UB-17, UB-26, REN-4, KI-3
  • For Spleen and Stomach Deficiency with Yuan Qi Deficiency: UB-21, REN-12, ST-36

LIVER AND KIDNEY YIN DEFICIENCY WITH LIVER YANG RISING

Clinical Manifestations
  • Dizziness
  • Impaired vision
  • Night blindness
  • Tinnitus
  • Irascibility
  • Throbbing headache with distended feeling
  • Flushed face
  • Blurred vision
  • Red, dry eyes with feeling of grit in eye
  • Dry mouth and throat with bitter taste in mouth
  • Palpitations
  • Headache
  • Nervousness
  • Insomnia with much dreaming
  • Dry throat
  • Thirst
  • Five Sole Heat (palms, soles and center of chest)
  • Night sweats
  • Light or late menses or
  • Amenorrhea
  • T: Red and dry
  • C: Little or None
  • P: Thready and wiry or Wiry and rapid (when Yang is Rising and Excess, pulse is forceful) or Thready and rapid when Yang is not prominent
Treatment Principle
  • Nourish Yin
  • Pacify the Liver
  • Subdue Yang
Herb Formulas
  • Retinitis Pigmentosa #1
Points
  • DU-20, GB-20, GB-34, GB-43, KI-3, LIV-2, LIV-3, UB-18, UB-23
