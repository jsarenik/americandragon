WIND-COLD DAMPNESS WITH BLOOD AND PHLEGM STAGNATION

Clinical Manifestations
  • Chronic pain, weakness and numbness (especially in the lower extremities) due to Windstroke
  • Muscle spasms
  • Cramps
  • Heaviness and pain in the lower back and legs
  • Fixed or migrating pain in the bones in the bones and joints with reduced range of motion
  • Long-term numbness or paralysis in the hands and feet
  • T: Normal
  • C: White and moist
Treatment Principle
  • Dispel Wind
  • Eliminate Dampness
  • Transform Phlegm
  • Invigorate the Blood
  • Unblock the collaterals
  • Alleviate pain
  • Warm the channels and collaterals
Herb Formulas
  • Wu Yao Shun Qi San
Points

LIVER YANG RISING

Clinical Manifestations
  • Insidious onset
  • Memory loss without inattentiveness and depression (early stage)
  • Loss of computational ability
  • Word-find and concentration problems
  • Difficulty with ordinary activities such as dressing, cooking, balancing the checkbook
  • Severe memory loss (later stage)
  • Complete disorientation (late stage)
  • Social withdrawal (late stage)
Treatment Principle
  • Calm Liver Yang
Herb Formulas
  • Zhi Bai Di Huang Wan
Points
  • Group 1: DU-15, PC-8, ST-36, UB-23
  • Group 2: DU-14, REN-15, SP-6, KI-1
  • Group 3: DU-15, Shixuan, LI-10, KI-3
  • Use the three groups alternately day for 1 minutes for three days. Rest 5-7 days. Repeat
  • Moxibustion: Use ginger and 3-5 moxa cones every other day on the above points for ten treatments. Rest 5 days and repeat as needed
