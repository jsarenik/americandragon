CHYLURIA (CLOUDY URINATION)

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • Chyluria, also called chylous urine, is a medical condition involving the presence of chyle in the urine stream, which results in urine appearing milky white.
Treatment Principle
Herb Formulas
Points

DAMP-HEAT IN THE LOWER JIAO

Clinical Manifestations
  • Urinary frequency
  • Urinary urgency
  • Painful, burning urination or
  • Continuous dripping of urine or
  • Sudden interruption to the urinary stream
  • Turbid urine or
  • Pus and blood in the urine or
  • Sand and stones in the urine
  • Lower abdominal distention
  • In Damp-Heat Pouring Downward there may also be:
    Fever
    Thirst
    Dry mouth
    Low back pain
  • In Damp-Heat Crystallizes in Urinary Bladder there may also be:
    Colicky pain in the low back, possibly radiating to the groin
  • T: Red
  • C: Yellow
  • P: Slippery and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Clear Heat
  • Eliminate Dampness by diuresis
Herb Formulas
  • Ba Zheng San
  • Xiao Ji Yin Zi
  • Zhi Zhuo Gu Ben Wan
  • Wu Lin San
  • Jia Wei Er Miao Wan
  • Cheng Shi Fen Xing Yin
  • Bei Xie Shen Shi Tang
Points
  • KI-7, LIV-2, REN-3, SP-6, SP-9, UB-23, UB-28, UB-39
  • REN-3, SP-6, SP-9, UB-23, UB-28
  • HT-8, REN-3, SP-6, SP-10, UB-17, UB-28
  • To disperse Heat: LI-4, LI-11
    To eliminate Dampness: REN-9, SP-6, SP-9
  • For retention of urine:
    1. REN-3, SP-6, SP-9, UB-28
    2. REN-3, SP-6, SP-9, UB-39

CENTRAL QI SINKING

Clinical Manifestations
  • Prolapse of one or more organs
  • Sallow complexion
  • Anorexia
  • Four limbs feel tired and heavy
  • Abdominal and epigastric distention after eating
  • Maybe acid regurgitation or vomiting
  • Loose stools
  • Shortness of breath
  • T: Pale and flabby - may have teethmarks
  • C: White
  • P: Even or Soft and thready
Treatment Principle
  • Strengthen the Spleen
  • Benefit Qi
  • Reinforce the Center
  • Invigorate Yang
Herb Formulas
  • Bu Zhong Yi Qi Tang
Points
  • DU-4, DU-20, KI-3, LIV-13, LU-7, REN-6, SP-6, UB-20, UB-23

HEART FIRE TRANSFERRING TO THE SMALL INTESTINE

Clinical Manifestations
  • Stabbing pain in the Bladder
  • Ulceration, erosion, and pain of mouth and tongue
  • Thirst with a desire for cold drinks
  • Irritability
  • Irascibility
  • Feeling of Heat and disturbance in the Heart
  • Insomnia
  • Flushed face
  • Bitter taste in the mouth
  • T: Red tip with ulcers especially on the tip
  • P: Excess and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Clear Heat
  • Purge Fire
  • Calm the Shen
Herb Formulas
  • Cheng Shi Fen Xing Yin
Points
  • HT-7, HT-8, PC-7, PC-8, UB-15
  • HT-8, KI-6, PC-6, REN-3, SP-6, SP-9, ST-28, Yintang

JUE YIN STAGE: COLD AND HEAT

Clinical Manifestations
  • Delirium
  • Heat in upper body
  • Cold in lower body
  • Sensation of Qi rushing to the chest
  • Vomits worms
  • Persistent thirst
  • Feverishness
  • Anorexia (hungry but can't eat)
  • Chronic diarrhea
  • Cold extremities
  • Maybe edema
  • Vertex headache
  • Vomits food
  • Cardiac Heat with pain
  • T: Red or pink or pale and flabby
  • C: Thick and greasy or thin
  • P: Deep, thready and weak or wiry
Treatment Principle
  • Clear worms
  • Clear Heat (Do not purge, create diaphoresis, or create emesis)
  • Revive Yang
Herb Formulas
  • Wu Mei Wan
Points
  • Moxa needle REN-4, REN-6, REN- 8, ST-25, ST-36, needle DU-14, LI-4, LI-11, LIV-3, REN-17

TURBID (MILKY) (LAO) LIN

Clinical Manifestations
  • Frequent, urgent, painful urination
  • Turbid milky urine that soon sediments with catkin substance at the bottom (chyluria)
  • A fatty, oily substance at the surface of the urine container with coagulation
  • Bloody difficult urinary discharge with feverish pain
  • Soreness in the lumbar region and knees
  • Dizziness
  • Lassitude
  • Suprapubic distention and discomfort
  • fullness in the chest and abdmen
  • Poor appetite
  • Bitter taste in the mouth
  • Thirst with no desire to drink
  • A tendancy toward constipation or
  • Alternating constipation and diarrhea
  • T: Red tongue proper
  • C: Slightly white and greasy
  • P: Soft and rapid or Rapid and thready
Treatment Principle
  • Warm the Kidneys
  • Drain Dampness
  • Separate the turbid from the clear
Herb Formulas
  • Bei Xie Fen Qing Yin
  • Qing Xin Lian Zi Yin
  • Ba Zheng San
  • Sang Piao Xiao San
  • Cheng Shi Bei Xie Fen Qing Yin
  • Chinese Patent Medicines:
    Te Xiao Pai Shi Wan
    Long Dan Xie Gan Wan
    Qian Lie Xian Wan
    Chuan Xin Lian Kang Yan Pian
Points
  • DU-20, KI-3, LIV-2, REN-3, REN-6, SP-9, UB-23, UB-28
  • KI-3, KI-6, LIV-2, REN-3, SP-9, UB-23, UB-28
  • REN-4, REN-6, SP-6, SP-9, UB-22, UB-27, UB-28
  • GB-41, KI-7, LIV-5, REN-3, SJ-5, SP-4, SP-6, SP-9, UB-22, UB-28
  • Ear: Urinary Bladder, Kidney, Sympathetic, Occiput, Adrenal, Endocrine

KIDNEY YIN DEFICIENCY WITH FIRE FLARING

Clinical Manifestations
  • Dizziness
  • Tinnitus or deafness
  • Poor memory
  • Aching, soreness or weakness of the low back
  • Spermatorrhea or
  • Premature ejaculation
  • Aching in the bones
  • Aching in the leg and heel
  • Dry mouth
  • Thirst
  • Night sweats
  • Five Sole Heat
  • Malar flush
  • Low grade fever in the afternoon
  • Dry throat, maybe recurrent sore throat
  • Dry stools and/or
  • Constipation
  • Urine short and red
  • Heightened libido
  • If Heart Fire also Flares:
    Insomnia
    Frequent dreams
    Palpitations
  • If Liver Yang also Rises:
    Dizziness
    Blurred vision
  • T: Red
  • C: Little or None
  • P: Thready and rapid

  • Nourish Yin
  • Subdue Fire
Herb Formulas

  • Chinese Paent Medicines:
    Zhi Bai Ba Wei Wan

KIDNEY YANG DEFICIENCY

Clinical Manifestations
  • Chronic, or recurrent cloudy or opaque urination that is frequent and copious
  • Lumbago: low back feels cold, weak and aching
  • Feeling chilled
  • Aversion to Cold
  • Cold limbs
  • General debility
  • Waxy, pale complexion with dark rings under the eyes
  • Oliguria with edema
  • Edema below the waist and in the ankles
  • Cold extremities
  • Maybe nocturia
  • Lassitude
  • Listlessness
  • Lethargy
  • Withdrawn, subdued behavior
  • Knees feel cold and aching
  • Dizziness
  • Tinnitus
  • Difficult urination
  • Impotence
  • Spermatorrhea
  • Premature ejaculation
  • Low libido
  • Female infertility ("uterus cold and infertile")
  • Maybe anorexia
  • Maybe loose stools
  • Maybe chronic diarrhea or "cock crow" diarrhea
  • T: Pale and swollen
  • C: Thin and white
  • P: Deep and thready - maybe slow
Treatment Principle
  • Warm and Reinforce Kidney Yang
Herb Formulas
  • Jin Gui Shen Qi Wan
  • Lu Rong Bu Se Wan
  • For frequent urination: Sang Piao Xiao San modified
  • With turbid urine: Bei Xie Fen Qing Yin
  • With less or no Cold and evidence of Heart involvement (frequent and cloudy urination, forgetfulness and disorientation: Sang Piao Xiao San
  • Chinese Patent medicines:
    Jin Gui Shen Qi Wan
    Ren Shen Lu Rong Wan
    Ge Jie Bu Shen Wan
    Jin Suo Gu Jing Wan
    *     The last formula is usually taken with one of the first three.
Points
  • DU-26, KI-7, REN-3, SP-6, moxa or moxa needle DU-4, REN-6, REN-9, UB-23, UB-53
  • DU-26, KI-3, KI-11, SI-3, SP-6, moxa or moxa needle DU-4, REN-4, UB-23
  • Moxa or moxa needle DU-4, KI-3, REN-4, UB-23
    For chronic diarrhea, rectal prolapse and Vanquished Yang: + moxa DU-20, REN-8
  • For Kidney Yang Deficiency impotence: moxa or moxa needle DU-4, DU-20, KI-3, REN-4, UB-23
  • For Kidney Yang Deficiency (Cock Crow) Diarrhea: moxa or moxa needle DU-20, KI-3, REN-4, UB-23
