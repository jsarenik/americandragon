(BENIGN) PROSTATIC HYPERTROPHY, BPH, PROSTOMEGALY

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • Hesitating and straining to urinate
  • Reduced urinary stream
  • Enlarged prostate
  • Acute urinary retention
  • Uremia following prolonged obstruction
  • Frequent nocturnal urination
  • Increased thirst
  • Continued urge to urinate
Treatment Principle
Nutrition
  • Zinc - 50mg
  • Linseed oil - 1 tablespoon twice a day
  • Avoid:
    Dairy
    Grease
    Fat
    Rich food
    Alcohol
    Coffee
    Smoking
  • Eat:
    Pumpkin seeds
    Tangerines
    Cherries
    Figs
    Mangoes
    Sunflower seeds
    Seaweeds

Points
