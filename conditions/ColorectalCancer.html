COLON CANCER

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
Treatment Principle
Herb Formulas
  • Cancer #1
  • Decoct 60g Hb. Scutellariae Barbatae Ban Zhi Lian and 60g Hb. Hedyotis Diffusae Bai Hua She She Cao and take daily.
  • Take a decoction of 120g Hb. Hedyotis Diffusae Bai Hua She She Cao and 120g Rz. Curculiginis Xian Mao daily.
  • Take a liquid extract of Hb. Erodii/Geranii Lao Guan Cao daily.
  • Use 25 ml of a retention enema once daily consisting of 200g Hb. et Sm. Sophorae Alopecuroides Ku Dou Zi and 10g of starch mixed with 1000ml saline solution.
  • With continuous bleeding: Parch Bul. Lilii Bai He with wine and grind into a powder for oral administration with water.
  • Decoct 20g of dried Fr. Fici Bi Li and drink or eat five fruits after meals every day.
  • Make Fol. et Ram. Sarcandrae Zhong Jie Feng into 0.3g tablets and take 4-6 tablets three times per day.
  • Take 1 gram of powdered Hirudo Shui Zhi with water or wine.
  • For hematemesis:
    1. Grind 30g Bombyx Batryticatus Jiang Can and 30g Fr. Mume Wu Mei into a powder, blend with rice and make small boluses. Take 100 boluses three times daily with water before meals.
    2. Soak 5 kg Fr. Chebulae Mu Gua in wine, wrap in a piece of cloth, simmer and crush. Mix with 30g Rx. Angelicae Dahuricae Bai Zhi, 30g Rx. Saposhnikoviae Fang Feng and 30g Rx. Gentianae Macrophyllae Qin Jiao, parch, pulverize and blend with rice to make small pills. Take 9g morning and evening.
  • For constipation: Finely crush 21g Aloe Lu Hui  and 15g Cinnabaris Zhu Sha,  blend with rice and wine to make boluses. Take 9g with wine. 
  • Topical:
    1. Cancer #8
    2. Grind Aloe Lu Hui into a powder in white wine and mix with a small amount of Borneolum Bing Pian for external application.
    3. Powder 10g Rz. Zingiberis Recens Sheng Jiang and 0.6g Realgar Xiong Huang, spread on a piece of general plaster and apply to the sacrococcygeal area to stop pain.

BLOOD STAGNATION

Clinical Manifestations
  • Chronic diarrhea with a feeling of incomplete evacuation or tenesmus
  • Purple or black stools which are sticky or tarry
  • Fixed or localized sharp or stabbing abdominal pain (usually in the lower left quadrant) which is worse with pressure
  • A dark complexion
  • Dark rings around the eyes
  • Purple nails
  • Spider naevi or vesicular abnormalities over the abdomen, face and legs (inner knee and ankle)
  • T: Purple or Brown or Purple stasis spots, maybe dark, distended sublingual veins
  • C: White
  • P: Wiry or Choppy or Thready
Treatment Principle
  • Transform and eliminate Blood Stagnation from the Intestines
Herb Formulas
  • Shao Fu Zhu Yu Tang
  • With Toxic Heat: Colon Cancer #2
  • With Toxic Heat and Phlegm: Yan Zhong Wan Yi Hao
  • Chinese Patent Medicines:
    Xue Fu Zhu Yu Wan
    Fu Ke Wu Jin Wan
    With bleeding: add Sheng Tian Qi Pian to either of the above formulas
Points
  • Ahshi points over the abdomen, Ahshi points along the Stomach channel between ST-36 to ST-39, LI-4, LIV-3, REN-6, SP-6, SP-8, SP-10, ST-25, UB-17, UB-25
    With Bleeding: + Ahshi points over the Ba Liao on the sacrum
    With Cold: + Moxa
  • Ear: Large Intestine, Distal Segment Rectum, Abdomen, Sanjiao, Shenmen, Adrenal
