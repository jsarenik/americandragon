MENORRHAGIA (HEAVY MENSTRUATION, LONG MENSTRUATION)

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • The amount of menstrual bleeding is significantly increased during menstruation, to 100 ml/day more or less. (normal 30-80ml).
  • There here may be a regular, early, or late cycle.
  • the color of the Blood is pale, dark, or purple red.
  • The consistency of the Blood is thick and sticky or, thin, or clotted.
  • Sometimes there may be pain.
Treatment Principle
Herb Formulas
Points
  • SP-1, SP-6
  • PC-6, SP-4

SPLEEN QI DEFICIENCY

Clinical Manifestations
  • Pale-red to normal Blood color
  • Thin and watery Blood with clots
  • Irregular menstruation
  • Sallow complexion
  • Fatigue
  • Anorexia
  • Four limbs feel tired and heavy
  • Abdominal and epigastric distention after eating
  • Maybe acid regurgitation or vomiting
  • Loose stools
  • Shortness of breath
  • May be much Blood due to Spleen Not Governing Blood
  • May be little Blood due to Spleen Qi Sinking
  • The Blood is pale-red in color and watery
  • An empty feeling in the lower abdomen
  • Spleen Not Governing Blood:
    Shortness of breath
    Dizziness
    Palpitations
    Functional Uterine Bleeding
    Purpura
    Epistaxis
    Hemafecia
    Hematuria
    Menorrhagia
    Metrorrhagia
  • Spleen Qi Sinking:
    Prolapse of one or more organs
  • T: Pale and flabby - may have teethmarks
  • C: White
  • P: Even, forceless and deficient
Treatment Principle
  • Nourish the Middle Jiao Qi
  • Keep the Blood in the Chong and Ren channels
Herb Formulas

  • Ju Yuan Jian modified
  • Bu Zhong Yi Qi Tang
  • Si Jun Zi Tang
  • Menorrhagia #2
  • With inconsolidation of the Chong and Ren channels: Gu Chong Tang
  • Chinese Patent Medicine: Gui Pi Wan

  • Unregulated intake of drink and food
  • Taxation damaging the body
  • Heart Qi insufficiency.
Clinical Manifestations

    Blood mixed with mucus
    Sallow or yellowish complexion
    Slightly puffy face
    Heavy head
    Dizziness
    Listlessness
    Stuffy chest and epigastrium
    Sticky sensation in the mouth
    T: Normal
    C: Thin whitish and sticky
    P: Slippery and Choppy

    Profuse purplish vaginal
    Hemorrhage
    Repulsive odor
    Flushing or Heat sensations
    Spontaneous sweating
    Bitter taste in mouth
    Thirst

    Constipation or diarrhea

  • Lumbar and sacral pain which are worse following menstruation or when tired
  • Profuse, clear, watery leukorrhea
  • Maybe infertility
  • Maybe dark rings around the eyes
  • Palpitations
  • Shortness of breath
  • Faint, weak respiration
  • Drenched with perspiration
  • Dizziness
  • Vertigo

  • Four limbs cold and sore

  • Loss of taste
  • Fatigue
  • Upset stomach
