GENITAL WARTS (HPV)

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • Latency period: 2-3 months
  • Affected Areas: Genital warts are commonly found at the boundaries of the skin and mucous membranes where it is damp and moist.
    Males: Mostly on the penile shaft, the ditch below the head of the penis, the glans penis, at the urethral opening, etc.
    Females: Mostly on the labia majora and minora, the area where the labia are tied together, the cervix, the vaginal orifice, on the perineum, and surrounding the anus.
    Age:
    Males: Older
    Females: Younger
    Lesions: Genital warts may occur singly, scattered about, or collected in a cluster.
    Grayish white or light red in color
    The tumorous hyperplasia may be papilliform, cauliflower shaped, or coxcomb shaped
    The main body of the wart may be large but the base may be narrow when seen from the outside
    Its surface may be damp and moist and is soft and pliable Because genital warts typically are found in secret places where they are constantly soaked, their form and color are filthy and dirty.
Treatment Principle
Herb Formulas
  • Bei Xie Shen Shi Tang Jia Jian #1
  • Genital Warts #1
  • Topical:
    1. Pound Fructus Bruceae Ya Dan Zi into a mash and apply this paste to the affected area. Use adhesive tape to keep it firmly in place. Change this herbal medicine once every three days.
    2. Use Folium Artemisiae Argyi Ai Ye to burn as moxa directly on the wart. Each day do one time. Stop when the wart sheds or drops off.
    3. HPV #1
    4: If the external shape of the lesion obviously appears to have a stem, it is possible to tie a fine silk thread or hair around the wart's stem. After some days, the wart may spontaneously fall off or slough.
    5. Rub the wart once a day with Ji Nei Jin(chicken gizzard), after it has been soaked in water.
    6. Take a little Xiong Huang, blended with white wine, applied to the wart surface for 1 day - 5 days.
    5. Zi Cao Gao modified
    6. Ma Qing Shui Xi Ji
    7. Ya Dan Zi You
    8. Qing Re Jie Du Xi Ji
    9. Bai Hua She She Cao Xi Ji
    10. Decoct 30g Rx Isatidis Ban Lan Gen, 30g Hb. Equisti Mu Zei and 30g Rz. Cyperi Xiang Fuand wash the affected part.
