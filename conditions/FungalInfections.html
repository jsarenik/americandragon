VAGINAL FUNGUS

Clinical Manifestations
  • Spleen Deficiency with Liver Qi Stagnation, Dampness and turbidity in the Lower Jiao:
    Profuse vaginal discharge that is white or pale-yellow
    The discharge is thin in consistency, not foul smelling and usually continuous
    Fatigue
    Lethargy
    A shiny, pale complexion
    Loose stools
    Diarrhea
    T: Pale
    C: White
    P: Soggy and frail or Moderate
Treatment Principle
  • Tonify the Spleen
  • Tonify the Middle Jiao
  • Disperse Liver Qi Stagnation
  • Transform Dampness
  • Stop vaginal discharge
Herb Formulas
  • Wan Dai Tang
  • Due to Toxic Heat: Zhi Mei Jing Yin Sai Ji
