PARKINSON'S DISEASE

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • Trembling feet, hands and neck
  • Tremor worse with emotional disturbances
  • Tremors disappear during sleep
  • Pill rolling tremor of the thumb and index finger or wrist
  • Maybe to and fro tremor of the head
  • Fixed facial expression like crying or smiling
  • Staring, unblinking eyes
  • Mouth slightly open often with drooling
  • Greasy facial skin
  • Seborrheic scaling
  • Muscular rigidity
  • Impaired movement
  • Stooping posture
  • Maybe oculogyric crisis
  • Gout
  • Speech difficulties
Treatment Principle
Herb Formulas

  • DU-9, LU-6, REN-6, REN-13, UB-10, UB-11, UB-62

SHAO YANG FU WITH UNDERLYING LIVER QI STAGNATION

  • Constipation
