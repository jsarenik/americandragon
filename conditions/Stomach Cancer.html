STOMACH CANCER - GASTRIC CANCER

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • Early stage:
    Often no symptoms
    Upper abdominal pain
    Discomfort in the upper abdomen
  • Middle stage:
    Anorexia
    Progressive dysphagia
    Nausea
    Weight loss
    Melaena
  • Late stage:
    Vomiting after eating
    Edema
    Ascites
    Anemia
    Hepatomegaly
    Abdominal tumor
    The tumor is firm and irregular
