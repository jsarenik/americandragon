ESOPHAGEAL CANCER

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • Progressive dysphagia
  • Vomiting after eating
  • Upper portion of the esophagus: choking sensation on swallowing
  • Lower portion of the esophagus: Vomiting after eating
Treatment Principle
Herb Formulas
  • Stew 500g Fr. Fici Bi Li with 100g of lean meat for one-half hour then consume both the meat and broth.
  • Grind 30-60g of the shell or kernel of Fr. Trapae Bispinosae Ling Jiao into a powder. Take 6g raw or with honey at a time.
  • Wash and decoct 120g of Pseudobulbus Cremastrae/Pleiones Shan Ci Gu then mix with120g white honey and make a paste. Take 9-15g orally twice a day.
  • Steep Sm. Impatientis Ji Xing Zi in wine for three days, grind it into a powder and roll the powder in wine to form mung bean size pills. Take eight pills each time with warm boiled water. if there is dysphagia, decoct six pills into a broth and drink. Discontinue use once the effect has been gained.
  • Decoct 30g Sm. Impatientis Ji Xing Zi and 30g Hb. Salviae Chinensis Shi Jian Chuan and then blend with 2-3g Salmiacum Xi Sha for sipping.
  • Grind Rx. Sophorae Tonkinensis Shan Dou Gen with vinegar and suck.
  • Roast 15g of Rx. Kansui Gan Sui with wheat flour and pulverize wit 3g of Rx. Aucklandiae Mu Xiang. Mix with wine for ingestion. Patients with strong constitutions should take 3g at a time. Patients with weak constitutions should take 1.5g at a time.
  • Powder Bombyx Batryticatus Jiang Can and Sm. Strychni Ma Qian Zi and take orally.
  • Combine Bombyx Batryticatus Jiang Can with Caulis Bambusae in Taeniam Zhu Ru and take orally.
  • Make Fol. et Ram. Sarcandrae Zhong Jie Feng into 0.3g tablets and take 4-6 tablets three times per day.
  • Decoct 9g Rz. Dysosmae Pleianthae Ba Jiao Lian and take as a tea daily.
  • Take equal amounts of the juices of Rx. Ginseng, Ren Shen, Arillus Longan Long Yan Rou, Rz. Phragmitis Lu Gen, sugar cane, pear and milk and stew with a small amount of ginger juice into a soft extract for taking slowly.
  • Pulverize 0.5kg Rx. Semiaquilegiae Tian Kui Zi and immerse in 5kg sorghum or cereal wine for 7 days. Take 50ml three times a day.
  • Powder 1 g Rx. Ophiopogonis Mai Men Dong and 0.3g Salmiacum Pi Shuang mix with water and apply topically.
  • Take 3g Fol. Hibisci Fu Rong Ye twice daily.
  • Take one dose a day of 6-12g Hb. Sedi Chui Pen Cao  combined with 9-16g Hb. Capsellae Ji Cai.
