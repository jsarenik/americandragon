EYELID TWITCH - EYELID TIC - EYELID SPASM

EXTERNAL WIND INVASION

Clinical Manifestations
  • Twitching eyelid
  • Headache in any part of the head
  • Fever and chills
  • Dizziness
  • Nasal congestion
  • Stuffy nose
  • Clear or white nasal discharge
  • Sneezing
  • Body aches
  • Nasal obstruction
  • Vertigo
  • Aversion to Wind
  • Copious sputum
  • T: Normal
  • C: Thin and white
  • P: Floating
Treatment Principle
  • Dispel Wind
  • Alleviate spasms
Herb Formulas
  • Gui Zhi Gan Cao Long Gu Mu Li Tang
Points
  • DU-16, GB-20, LU-5, LU-7 + LI-4, KI-7, UB-12, UB-13
  • GB-4, GB-5, GB-20, GB-34, SJ-5, UB-40
  • DU-16, DU-23, LI-4, LI-20, SJ-5, ST-8, UB-12

HEART BLOOD AND SPLEEN QI DEFICIENCY

Clinical Manifestations
  • Eyelid flickering
  • Blurred vision
  • Spots before the eyes
  • Forgetfulness
  • Absent-mindedness
  • Lack of concentration
  • Menorrhagia
  • Palpitations with or without anxiety
  • Insomnia (difficulty falling asleep)
  • Nightmares
  • Reduced appetite
  • Pale, wan complexion
  • Pale lips and nails
  • Early menstruation with copious pale blood
  • Continuous flow with little blood (spotting)
  • Excess perspiration
  • Night sweats
  • Irregular menstruation
  • Leukorrhea
  • Dizziness (mild and postural, worse with sitting or rising or lying down or when fatigued)
  • Memory loss
  • Low spirits
  • Depression
  • Bruising easily
  • Dream disturbed sleep
  • Short attention span
  • Anxiety
  • Phobias
  • Panic attacks
  • Fear
  • Low grade fever
  • Withdrawal
  • Fatigue and lethargy
  • Weakness
  • Genital itch
  • Impotence
  • Nocturnal emissions
  • Weak abdomen
  • Abdominal distention after eating
  • Premenstrual spotting
  • Dry skin and hair
  • T: Pale, maybe swollen
  • C: White
  • P: Thready and weak or Thready and moderate
Treatment Principle
  • Augment Qi
  • Nourish the Blood
  • Strengthen the Spleen
  • Nourish the Heart
Herb Formulas
  • Gui Pi Tang
  • Shi Quan Da Bu Tang
Points
  • HT-7, LIV-13, PC-6, REN-4, REN-12, REN-14, SP-1, SP-3, SP-5, SP-6, ST-36, UB-15, UB-17, UB-20
  • HT-7, SP-6, Yintang, DU-20, KI-1, KI-16

LIVER WIND

Clinical Manifestations
  • Twitching and spasms of the eyelid
  • Headache
  • Dizziness
  • Vertigo
  • Tinnitus
  • Blurred vision
  • Sensation of Heat rushing to the head
  • Insomnia
  • Dream disturbed sleep
  • Numbness
  • Twitching and spasms in the extremities
  • Hemiplegia
  • Paralysis
  • Convulsions
  • Trembling tongue
  • Deviation of mouth and eyes
  • Coma
  • T: Red
  • P: Wiry and rapid or Wiry, tight and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Calm the Liver
  • Extinguish Wind
  • Clear Heat
  • Invigorate the Blood
  • Tonify the Liver and Kidneys
Herb Formulas
  • Tian Ma Gou Teng Yin
Points
  • DU-20, DU-26, GB-20, GB-34, KI-1, KI-3, LIV-2, LIV-3, PC-8, UB-18, UB-23
