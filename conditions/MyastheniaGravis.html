MYASTHENIA GRAVIS

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • Myasthenia gravis is a chronic neuromuscular transmission disturbance disorder
  • It is considered an autoimmune disorder
  • Easy fatigue of affected striated muscles, relieved after rest
  • Ptosis of the eyelids
  • Difficulty chewing and swallowing
  • Flaccidity of the muscles and limbs
  • Severe cases: Death
Treatment Principle
  • Treat Wei Syndrome
Herb Formulas
  • For abdominal and calf spasms: Shao Yao Gan Cao Tang
Points
  • Local Points:
    For upper arm weakness: DU-14, Huatoujiaji(T-1-T-8) (select 2 or 3 points)
    For forearm weakness: LI-4, LI-10, LI-11, LI-14, LI-15, SJ-5 (select 2 or 3 points)
    For weakness of the buttocks and thighs: DU-3, Huatoujiaji(T-10-L-5), UB-32, UB-34 (select 2 or 3 points)
    For weakness of the lower legs: ST-31, ST-32, ST-34, ST-36,  ST-37, ST-41 (select 2 or 3 points)
  • Symptomatic Points:
    For drooping eyelids: SJ-23, UB-2, Yuyao or Yuyao through to SJ-23 or SJ-4 through to Yuyao
    For drooping eyelids and double vision: GB-14 through to Yuyao, LI-4, SI-6, SJ-23, ST-36, UB-2
    For difficulty swallowing, an inability to speak clearly with a strong nasal sound and an inability to chew: GB-20, HT-5, LI-18, REN-22, ST-6, ST-7, ST-36
    For weakness of the wrist: LI-5, SJ-4
    For weakness of the hands: LI-4 through to SJ-3
    For weakness or numbness of the fingers: Baxie
    For weakness of the knees: LIV-8, UB-40
    For weakness of the feet or numbness of the toes: Bafeng
    For talipes equinus due to weakness of the sinew vessels of the Foot Yang Ming, foot Shao Yang and Foot Shao Yin: GB-34, GB-40, LIV-4, ST-37, ST-41
    For talipes equinus due to weakness of the sinew vessels of the Foot Tai Yang and foot Shao Yang: GB-39, GB-40, UB-60, UB-62
    Foot Tai Yin and foot Shao Yin: KI-3, KI-6, SP-4, SP-6

WIND-COLD-DAMP WITH DAMP PREDOMINANT

Clinical Manifestations
  • Chronic fever with swelling of the extremities
  • Numbness and heaviness of the extremities
  • Soreness and fixed pain of joints
  • Stiffness or numbness of the muscles or skin
  • Pain worse on cloudy or rainy days
  • Maybe peripheral or systemic edema
  • Pain is alleviated by pressure and warmth
  • Pain lingers and is recalcitrant to treatment
  • Worse during cloudy or wet weather
  • Limited movement
  • Edema
  • Maybe a stifling sensation in chest
  • Maybe anorexia or indigestion
  • Maybe abdominal distention
  • Maybe loose or semi-liquid stools
  • T: Pink or Pale
  • C: Greasy and white
  • P: Slow, moderate and soft
Treatment Principle
  • Expel Damp
  • Clear the channels
Herb Formulas
  • Yi Yi Ren Tang
Points
  • SP-6, SP-9
  • To eliminate Dampness and ease sinews:
    1. SJ-6, GB-34, SP-5, SP-6, SP-9, LI-10, ST-36, ST-40
    2. UB-20, SP-9
  • When affected by weather changes:
    1. SJ-5, UB-35, ST-36, SP-5
    2. SP-5, ST-38, ST-36
    3. LI-13, ST-32, UB-38, GB-30, GB-34, SJ-8, DU-1
  • Lower back pain: moxa DU-2
  • Legs: GB-29, GB-30, UB-40
