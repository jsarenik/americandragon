ARTHRALGIA, JOINT PAIN

GENERAL

Etiology
  • Invasion of Wind, Cold and Damp or Wind, Heat and Damp in the Meridians
  • Weakness of the body due to Yang Deficiency
  • Qi and Blood Deficiencies due to childbirth or long term illness causing Wei Qi Deficiency
  • Overstraining, hence weakening the body
  • A Yang excess constitution, a Yin Deficient constitution or long standing Wind-Cold-Damp, stagnation may turn into Heat Bi
Clinical Manifestations
  • With a strong constitution, the etiologic factors may include:
    Living for a long period of time in Cold areas
    Lacking adequate protection against Cold weather
    Prolonged exposure to Cold at work (air conditioning)
    Sleeping in areas exposed to Wind, rain, snow or fog
    Living in Damp climates
    Exposure to Wind and rain
    Working in water
    Exposure to Wind, Cold or Damp after physical exertion with sweating
    Entering the water while sweating
    Wearing wet clothes
    Chronic Bi can lead to Qi and Blood Stagnation with Phlegm Stasis. This generates swelling and joint deformation. This is much more difficult to treat.
  • Special characteristics:
    Recalcitrant to treatment
    Joint swelling and pain
    Swelling is hard
    Pain is excruciating
    Limbs are numb
  • Blood Stasis:
    T: Purple spots
    C: White
    P: Thin and choppy
  • Phlegm Stagnation:
    T: Normal
    C: White and greasy
    P: Soggy and even
  • It usually starts gradually. In the initial stages, treatment is aimed at relieving the Excess condition.
  • Secondary phase - Excess type:
    The pathogen has not been cleared and moves deeper into the tissues and organs.
  • Chest Bi - Heart blockage:
    Uncomfortable feeling in the chest
    Palpitations
    Asthmatic panting
    Irritability
Treatment Principle
  • Eliminate Wind
  • Warm Cold (Clear Heat)
  • Expel Damp
  • Clear the Channels
Herb Formulas
  • Advanced cases: Feng Shi Han Tang
  • Shoulder pain so severe, it feels like it is about to break off:
    Chai Hu Gui Zhi Tang
