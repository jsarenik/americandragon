SKIN CANCER

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
Treatment Principle
Herb Formulas
  • For dermatoma:
    Take 15-45g Rz. Arisaematis Preparata Zhi Tian Nan Xing a day as a tea for three months.
    Medicinal bag: Wash 9g Rz. Arisaematis Preparata Zhi Tian Nan Xing, add 0.5ml of 75% ethanol, pound into a paste, wrap with gauze, and apply to the lesion or insert into the vagina. Change once a day.
  • Topical:
    1. Dry fresh Hb. et Rx. Ranunculi Mao Gen at low temperature, pulverized into a powder and mixed with ox oil to make an ointment for external use.
    2. Distill the volital oil from the root of Hb. et Rx. Ranunculi Mao Gen, mix with three equal portions of the whole herb decoction and take orally. Start with a small dose and gradually increase.
    3. Distill the volital oil from the root of Hb. et Rx. Ranunculi Mao Gen, mix with two equal portions of the powder of the whole herb and make into a solid plaster with lard to be applied topically.
    4. Apply a 10%-20% solution of Rz. Dysosmae Pleianthae Ba Jiao Lian directly to the lesion.
    5. Decoct 1500g of the fresh leaves of Rx. Wikstroemiae Pu Yin and then boil into a paste and mix with 100g sebum and 200g petroleum jelly. Apply to the lesion.
    6. Pound Pseudobulbus Cremastrae/Pleiones Shan Ci Gu, mix with vinegar and apply to the lesion.
    7. Apply a paste of decocted Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao around the pen-shaped tumor. Then apply a powder of equal parts Flos Genkwa Yuan Hua, Rx. Euphorbiae Pekinensis Hong/Jing Da Ji and Rx. Kansui Gan Sui blended with vinegar, on top of the tumor. The mass will contract and become crisp spontaneously after several applications.
    For squamous epithelial carcinoma: Decoct and make a plaster of Hb. Hydrocotyli Sibthorpioiditis Tian Hu Sui, and apply it to the lesions.
