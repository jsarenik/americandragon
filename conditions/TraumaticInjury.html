Points

QI AND BLOOD STAGNATION

Clinical Manifestations
  • Pain that is fixed, stabbing and /or severe or
  • Pains that move around
  • Distention
  • Depression
  • Irritability
  • Pan is worse with pressure
  • Dark complexion
  • Fixed, palpable masses
  • Bleeding with clots
  • Purple lips
  • Purple nails
  • Dry skin
  • Blue, dark or purplish discoloration of skin in small or large areas,
  • Varicose and spider veins
  • Cold hands and feet
  • Tendency to hemorrhage dark blood or clots
  • T: Purple, possible purple or red spots
    Red purple if there is Heat
    Blue purple if there is Cold
  • C: White
  • P: Choppy or Wiry and choppy
Treatment Principle
  • Zheng Gu Zi Jin Dan
  • Ribs: Shu Gan Tang
Herb Formulas
  • Chinese Patent Medicines:
    Yunnan Baiyao
    San Qi Powder
Points
  • GB-40, GB-41, LIV-3, LIV-14, PC-6, SJ-6, ,SP-6, SP-10, ST-36, UB-17, UB-18, UB-40

HEAD: BLOOD STAGNATION

Clinical Manifestations
  • Persistent tinnitus or
  • Hearing loss
  • There may be black or dark discharge from the ear or
  • Dark matter mixed in with the ear wax
  • Maybe an earache or pain
  • Chronic headaches
  • Spider navii on the face, neck and trunk
  • Vertigo
  • Chronic tinnitus
  • Hair loss
  • Dark purple complexion or
  • Blue or green facial complexion with or without rosacea or vitiligo
  • Darkness around the eyes
  • Brandy nose
  • Progressive emaciation
  • Abdominal distention
  • Purplish discoloration of the sinews
  • Tidal fever
  • Dizziness
  • Deafness
  • Pale face
  • Cyanosis
  • Phthisis
  • Muscle weakness
  • Sudden loss of vision
  • Pain that is fixed, stabbing and /or severe
  • Pan is worse with pressure
  • Dark complexion
  • Fixed, palpable masses
  • Bleeding with clots
  • Purple lips
  • Purple nails
  • Dry skin
  • Varicose and spider veins
  • Cold hands and feet
  • Tendency to hemorrhage dark blood or clots
  • T: Purple, possible purple or red spots
    Red purple if there is Heat
    Blue purple if there is Cold
  • C: White
  • P: Choppy or Wiry and choppy
Treatment Principle
  • Activate the Blood
  • Break up Stagnation
  • Clear and open the ears
Herb Formulas
  • Tong Qiao Huo Xue Tang
  • Xue Fu Zhu Yu Tang modified
  • Fu Yuan Huo Xue Tang
  • Huo Xue Zhi Tong Tang
  • For forgetfulness due to head trauma: Xue Fu Zhu Yu Tang modified
  • Postpartum: Qing Hun San  modified
  • Chinese Patent Medicines:
    Fu Fang Dan Shen Pian
    Sheng Tian Qi Pian
Points
  • SP-6, SP-10, ST-36, UB-17, UB-40
  • Ahshi points on the head, LI-4, LIV-3, LIV-4, UB-17
  • Choose two or three of the following: GB-2, GB-20, GB-39, KI-3, SI-19, SJ-17, SJ-21, SP-6, SP-10, UB-17

LOW BACK: BLOOD STAGNATION

Clinical Manifestations
  • Lower back pain that is fixed, stabbing and /or severe
  • Pan is worse with pressure
  • Dark complexion
  • In severe cases, movement is difficult and very painful with restricted range of motion
  • The pain may be worse in the evening and is unaffected by weather or temperature
  • Purple lips
  • Purple nails
  • Dry skin
  • Blue, dark or purplish discoloration of skin in small or large areas
  • Varicose and spider veins
  • Cold hands and feet
  • Tendency to hemorrhage dark blood or clots
  • T: Purple, possible purple or red spots
    Red purple if there is Heat
    Blue purple if there is Cold
  • C: White
  • P: Choppy or Wiry and choppy
Treatment Principle
  • Activate the Blood
  • Break up Stagnation
Herb Formulas
  • Huo Luo Xiao Ling Dan modified
  • Gu An Fang
  • With Heat: Xia Yu Xue Tang
  • Chinese Patent Medicines:
    Jin Gui Shen Qi Wan
    Qi Li San
    Gu Zhe Cuo Shang San
    Zheng Gu Shui Lineament
    Die Da Zhi Tong Gao
Points
  • General points + SP-6, SP-10, UB-17
  • SP-6, SP-10, ST-36, UB-17, UB-40
