POLYCYSTIC OVARY SYNDROME (PCOS)

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • Menstrual abnormalities:
    Irregular menstruation
    Early menstruation
    Late menstruation
    Scanty menstruation
    Metrorrhagia
    Amenorrhea
  • Development of male sex characteristics:
    Elevated levels of male hormones
    Androgens may result in physical signs, such as
    Decreased breast size
    Deepening of the voice
    Enlargement of the clitoris
    Hirsutism (excess facial and body hair)
    Adult acne or
    Severe adolescent acne
  • Obesity:About 50% of women with PCOS are overweight or obese.
  • Prediabetes or type 2 diabetes: Many women with PCOS have
    high blood glucose level and type 2 diabetes due to significantly high insulin resistant.
  • Acanthosis nigricans: This is the medical term for dark or thick skin markings and creases around the armpits, groin, neck, and breasts due to insulin sensitivity.
Treatment Principle
Herb Formulas
  • PCOS #6
  • With anovulation: Chai Ling Tang
  • With galactorrhea-amenorrhea syndrome: Ding Gan Bu Shen Tang
Points
  • DU-20, ST-25, ST-29, REN-3, Moxa REN-4, REN-6, REN-8, Electro-acupuncture UB-23, UB-32. UB-52
  • For strengthening Spleen Qi: ST-36, SP-10, ST-40, SP-9, SP-6, UB-20
  • For warming Kidney Yang: UB-23, UB-25, DU-4, DU-3, KI-7, KI-6
