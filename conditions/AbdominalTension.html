ABDOMINAL TENSION

WIND-HEAT

Clinical Manifestations
  • Redness, pain and swelling of the eyes
  • Dizziness
  • Right hypochondriac pain and tension
  • Headache
  • Abdominal tension
  • P: Rapid and floating
Treatment Principle
  • Dispel Wind
  • Clear Heat
  • Release pathogens from the Exterior
  • Reduce swelling and pain
Herb Formulas
  • Xi Gan Ming Mu Tang
Points
  • SJ-5 + LI-4, GB-20, DU-14, DU-16, LU-11, LU-10, LU-5, UB-13, UB-12

LARGE INTESTINE EXCESS HEAT

Clinical Manifestations
  • Knotted constipation or
  • Watery, thin diarrhea (Heat knots or flows)
  • Burning hot sensation in the anus
  • Abdominal distention and pain (dislikes pressure)
  • High fever or infantile tidal fever
  • Hands and feet dripping with perspiration
  • Vomiting
  • Irritating thirst
  • Delirium
  • T: Pink, maybe prickles (swollen papillae on the tongue that look like thorns and are very sensitive to the touch) or Red
  • C: Thick, yellow and dry
  • P: Deep and Shi
Treatment Principle
  • Clear Excess Heat with bitter, cold, strong purgative herbs
Herb Formulas
  • Da Cheng Qi Tang
Points
  • UB-25, ST-25, ST-37, ST-44, SJ-6, LI-11
