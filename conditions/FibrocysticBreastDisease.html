FIBROCYSTIC BREAST DISEASE (FIBROIDS) (BREAST LUMPS)

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • Lump(s) in the breast(s) that are of different sizes, or shapes
  • Can have pain or no pain
  • Can change in size with menstrual cycle and emotions
  • Luteal phase = larger, follicular phase = smaller
  • Patient should get mammogram, if lump is malignant it will be hard, fixed and feel like cauliflower and there may be an inverted nipple (significantly inverted- including areola), the edge of areola may not be distinct (fades to pale orangey), pink fluids may leak, check lymph nodes (axillary, 52-53 nodes).
