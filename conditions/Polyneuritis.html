POLYNEURITIS, MULTIPLE PERIPHERAL NEURITIIS

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • A non-traumatic generalized disorder of the peripheral nerves, affecting the distal fibers most severely, with proximal shading (for example, the feet are affected sooner or more severely than the hands), and typically symmetrically; most often affects motor and sensory fibers almost equally, but can involve either one, either solely or very disproportionately; classified as axon degenerating (axonal), or demyelinating; many causes, particularly metabolic and toxic; familial or sporadic.
  • Symptoms:
    Hand paresthesia
    Hand numbness
    Hand pain
    Hand tingling
    Pain of the fingers
    A sensation that one is wearing a glove
    Wrist drop
    Foot paresthesia
    Foot numbness
    Foot pain
    Foot tingling
    Toe pain
    A sensation that one is wearing slippers
    Abnormal gait or deformity
    Loss of muscular force
    Muscular atrophy
    cold limbs
    Dry, cracked skin
    Heart symptoms
    Severe:
    Quadriplegia
    Urinary retention
    Urinary incontinence
    Breathing difficulty
    Difficulty speaking
    Difficulty swallowing
Treatment Principle
Herb Formulas
Points
  • Ahshi points, DU-9, DU-12, DU-14, GB-34, LI-11, UB-14, UB-16, UB-60
  • LI-4, LIV-3
  • Hand: LI-4, PC-7, SI-3, SJ-5 + knuckles
  • Feet: KI-6, ST-41, UB-60 + toe knuckles

SPLEEN AND STOMACH QI DEFICIENCY

  • Gradual loss of muscle mass
  • Sallow complexion
  • Facial puffiness
  • Fatigue
  • Lassitude
  • Anorexia
  • No desire for food and drink
  • Frequent belching
  • Borborygmus
  • Loss of sensation of taste
  • Four limbs feel tired and heavy
  • Abdominal and epigastric distention after eating
  • Maybe acid regurgitation or vomiting
  • Loose stools
  • Shortness of breath

  • May or may not have clots
  • Menstrual cycle is shortened (early menstruation)
  • Palpitations
  • Dizziness
  • Blurred vision
  • Pale, lusterless complexion
  • Pale nails
  • Abdominal pain
  • Irregular menstruation with little flow
  • Fixed lower abdominal pain
  • Vertigo
  • Tinnitus
  • Pale lips
  • May be amenorrhea
  • Insomnia
  • Menstruation may also be accompanied by distention and pain
  • T: Pale
  • C: Normal
  • P: Thin and weak or Thin and choppy

  • Tonify and activate the Blood

  • Break up Blood Stagnation
  • Regulate menstruation
  • Alleviate pain

  • Dry skin
  • Restlessness
  • Irritability

  • Cough
  • A dry throat
  • Scanty sputum
  • Scanty, yellow urine
  • Dry stool
  • T: Red
  • C: Yellow and scanty
  • P: Thready and rapid
