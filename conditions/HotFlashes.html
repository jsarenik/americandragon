HOT FLASHES

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • Hot flashes are sudden feelings of warmth, which are usually most intense over the face, neck and chest. The skin might redden, as if blushing. Hot flashes can also cause sweating, and if too much body heat is lost, there might be a chilled feeling afterward.
