BLADDER CANCER (CYSTOMYOMA,CYSTIC CARCINOMA)

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • Hematuria - in the beginning, a small amount of Blood appears at the end of urination. As it progresses, the volume of Blood increases.
  • Blood clots causing pain on urination
  • Pain in the pubic and perineal areas
  • Pain at the end of the penis
  • Anal pain
  • Low back and waist pain and aching
  • Polyuria
  • Oliguria
  • Frequent urination
Treatment Principle
Herb Formulas
  • Decoct 30g Fr. Fici Bi Li and Caulis Aristolochiae Manshuriensis Guan Mu Tong
  • Gui Zhi Fu Ling Wan
  • For papilloma: Decoct into a pate 60g Rz. Smilacis Glabrae Tu Fu Ling and 30g Petiolus Trachycarpi Zong Lu Pi, then make into 0.3g tablets. Take 5 tablets three times per day.
  • For hematuria:
    Pound into a juice 120g Hb. Hydrocotyli Sibthorpioiditis Tian Hu Sui and 120g Hb. Polygoni Avicularis Bian Xu and take orally with sugar.
    Hematuria #1
  • For epithelial tumor of the Bladder: Decoct 30g Hb. Solani Nigri Long Kui and 30g Hb. Solani Lyrati Shu Yang Quan. Take daily.
  • Topical:
    Cancer #8

KIDNEY YIN DEFICIENCY WITH EMPTY FIRE RISING

Clinical Manifestations
  • Episodic hematuria with fresh pale or bright red Blood
  • The bleeding may be mild or recurrent
  • Mild urinary pain
  • The urine may be concentrated
  • Dizziness
  • Tinnitus or deafness
  • Poor memory
  • Aching, soreness or weakness of the low back and knees
  • Heel pain
  • Spermatorrhea or
  • Premature ejaculation
  • Aching in the bones
  • Aching in the leg and heel
  • Dry mouth
  • Irritability
  • Restlessness
  • Thirst
  • Night sweats
  • Five sole Heat
  • Malar flush
  • Facial flushing
  • Low grade fever in the afternoon
  • Dry mouth and throat, maybe recurrent sore throat
  • Bone steaming fever
  • Dry stools and/or
  • Constipation
  • Heightened libido
  • Symptoms are worse in the afternoon
  • If Heart Fire also Flares:
    Insomnia
    Frequent dreams
    Palpitations
  • If Liver Yang also Rises:
    Dizziness
    Blurred vision
  • T: Red
  • C: Little or None
  • P: Thready and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Nourish Kidney Yin
  • Subdue Fire
  • Cool the Blood
  • Stop bleeding
Herb Formulas
  • Zhi Bai Di Huang Wan modified
  • Chinese patent medicines:
    Zhi Bai Di Huang Wan
    Da Bu Yin Wan
    Zhi Bai Ba Wei Wan
    Yun Nan Bai Yao (take this formula along with either of the above formulas)
Points
  • KI-1, KI-2, KI-3, KI-6, KI-9, KI-10, REN-4, SP-6, UB-23
  • KI-2, KI-3, KI-6, LU-7, REN-4, UB-23, UB-28
