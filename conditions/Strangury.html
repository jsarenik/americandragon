STRANGURY (STRANGURIA)

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • Difficulty in micturition in which the urine is passed only drop by drop with pain and tenesmus.
Treatment Principle
Herb Formulas
  • For dribbling urination that starts and stops: Wu Bi Shan Yao Wan
Points
  • REN-3, SP-6, SP-9, UB-28

SPLEEN QI DEFICIENCY WITH DAMPNESS

Clinical Manifestations
  • Dribbling urination which occurs due to overwork and fatigue
  • Sallow complexion
  • Anorexia
  • Four limbs feel tired and heavy
  • Abdominal and epigastric distention after eating
  • Maybe acid regurgitation or vomiting
  • Loose stools
  • Shortness of breath
  • Edema in limbs, especially lower limbs
  • Maybe anasarca
  • Maybe abdominal distention
  • Physical and mental fatigue
  • T: Pale and flabby - may have teethmarks
  • C: White and moist
  • P: Soft and even
Treatment Principle
  • Warm Yang
  • Strengthen the Spleen
  • Benefit Qi
  • Move water
Herb Formulas
  • Shen Ling Bai Zhu San
Points
  • REN-6, REN-9, SP-6, SP-9, ST-28, ST-36, UB-20, UB-22, UB-23

CENTRAL QI SINKING

Clinical Manifestations
  • Dribbling urination which occurs due to overwork and fatigue
  • Prolapse of one or more organs
  • A sallow or bright white complexion
  • Lassitude
  • Anorexia
  • Four limbs feel tired and heavy
  • Abdominal and epigastric distention after eating
  • Lower abdominal sagging and distention
  • Maybe acid regurgitation or vomiting
  • Loose stools
  • Shortness of breath
  • T: Pale and flabby - may have teethmarks
  • C: White
  • P: Even or Soft and thready or Thready and weak
Treatment Principle
  • Strengthen the Spleen
  • Benefit Qi
  • Reinforce the Center
  • Invigorate Yang
Herb Formulas
  • Bu Zhong Yi Qi Tang  modified
Points
  • DU-20, REN-4, REN-6, ST-36
    With cold limbs and diarrhea: + moxa REN-8
    With gastric prolapse: + SP-16 through to REN-8
    With rectal prolapse: + DU-1
    With uterine prolapse: + KI-11, Zigong

SPLEEN AND STOMACH DEFICIENCY COLD

Clinical Manifestations
  • Dribbling urination which occurs due to overwork and fatigue
  • Mild constant epigastric pain; relieved by warmth and pressure
  • Anorexia
  • Abdominal pain and fullness after meals
  • Nausea
  • Vomiting
  • Loose, unformed stools
  • Excessive salivation
  • Cold limbs
  • Lassitude
  • Weakness
  • Sallow or pale, dull complexion
  • Maybe edema
  • Maybe scanty urination
  • Maybe clear, watery, copious vaginal discharge
  • T: Pale
  • C: White and greasy
  • P: Deep, thready and forceless
Treatment Principle
  • Strengthen the Spleen
  • Warm Yang
  • Warm the Center
  • Dispel Cold
Herb Formulas
  • Fu Zi Li Zhong Wan  modified
Points
  • Moxa or moxa needle LIV-13, REN-12, SP-3, SP-6, ST-36, UB-20, UB-21

URINARY BLADDER DAMP-HEAT

Clinical Manifestations
  • Urinary frequency
  • Urinary urgency
  • Painful urination or
  • Continuous dripping of urine or
  • Sudden interruption to the urinary stream
  • Turbid urine or
  • Pus and blood in the urine or
  • Sand and stones in the urine
  • In Damp-Heat Pouring Downward there may also be:
    Fever
    Thirst
    Dry mouth
    Low back pain
  • In Damp-Heat Crystallizes in Urinary Bladder there may also be:
    Colicky pain in the low back, possibly radiating to the groin
  • T: Red
  • C: Yellow
  • P: Slippery and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Clear Heat
  • Eliminate Dampness by diuresis
Herb Formulas
  • Ba Zheng San
  • With Blood Stagnation: Interstitial Cystitis #1
Points
  • KI-7, LIV-2, REN-3, SP-6, SP-9, UB-23, UB-28, UB-39
  • REN-3, SP-6, SP-9, ST-28, UB-28
    For lower abdominal distention and oppression: + UB-27
    For a fever: + bleed UB-40
    For low back pain: + UB-26
  • For retention of urine:
    1. REN-3, SP-6, SP-9, UB-28
    2. REN-3, SP-6, SP-9, UB-39

HEAT (RE) LIN

Clinical Manifestations
  • Rapid onset of urinary dripping
  • Frequent, urgent, painful urination
  • Dark yellow urine with feverish and painful sensations
  • Hasty urinary discharge
  • Pain involving the umbilicus
  • Fever with aversion to Cold
  • A bitter taste in the mouth
  • T: Red
  • C: Yellow and greasy
  • P: Rapid and soft
Treatment Principle
  • Clear Heat
  • Purge Fire
  • Promote urination
  • Unblock painful urinary dysfunction
Herb Formulas
  • Ba Zheng San
Points
  • KI-3, LI-4, LIV-2, REN-3, SJ-5, SP-9, UB-28
  • Ear: Urinary Bladder, Kidney, Sympathetic, Occiput, Adrenal, Endocrine

STONE (SHI) LIN

Clinical Manifestations
  • Frequent, urgent, painful urination
  • Difficult urination with occasional sandy substance in the urine
  • Dysuria with pain involving the lower abdomen difficult to tolerate or
  • On and off urinary discharge with stabbing lumbar pain that involves both the lower abdomen and external genitalia
  • Occasional bloody urine
  • T: Red
  • C: Thin and white or Thin and yellow
  • P: Wiry or Wiry and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Clear Heat
  • Purge Fire
  • Promote urination
  • Unblock painful urinary dysfunction
Herb Formulas
  • Shi Wei San
  • Ba Zheng San
  • With Kidney Deficiency: Liu Wei Di Huang Wan modified
  • For sand strangury with severe, cutting distention and pain stretching to the low back, urethra or perineum which is worse during urination, sand in the urine and maybe hematuria: Shi Wei San  modified
Points
  • KI-2, KI-3, LIV-2, REN-3, SP-9, UB-28, UB-39
  • KI-1, LIV-1, REN-4, UB-23, UB-28, UB-40
  • KI-6, REN-3, SP-6, SP-9, UB-28, UB-39
  • Ear: Urinary Bladder, Kidney, Sympathetic, Occiput, Adrenal, Endocrine

  • Soreness in the lumbar region and knees
  • Dizziness
