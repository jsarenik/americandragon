THYROID CANCER

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • Rapid growth of the tumor in a short time
  • A hard irregular mass involving the entire thyroid tissue and fixed to the adjacent tissues
  • Lymph node enlargement
  • Hoarseness
  • Metastasis (Lungs, bones)
Treatment Principle
Herb Formulas
  • Make Fr. Tritici Levis Fu Xiao Mai and Fol. Artemisiae Argyi Ai Ye into a steamed cake and take daily.
  • Grind one Rz. Arisaematis Tian Nan Xing rhizome into powder mix with 5-7 drops of vinegar and apply to the lesion.
  • Decoct and drink 30g of Fr. Polygoni Orientalis Shui Hong Huang Zi once daily. Some may be pulverized into a paste for external application.
  • Take 3-6 0.5g pills of Fol. Artemisiae Argyi Ai Ye three times daily.
  • Blend Fol. Artemisiae Argyi Ai Ye with wheat flour to make cakes. Eat frequently.
  • For tumors on the neck without pain: Grind 30g Cx. Phellodendri Huang Bai and 30g Sargassum Hai Zaointo a fine powder. Pour 1.5g on the palm and lick it with the tongue. The tumor should disappear in one day.
