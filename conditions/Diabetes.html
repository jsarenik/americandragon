DIABETES (XIAO KE), DIABETES MELLITUS, DIABETES INSIPIDUS

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • Diabetes mellitus: A group of metabolic diseases in which a person has high blood sugar, either because the pancreas does not produce enough insulin, or because cells do not respond to the insulin that is produced.
  • Diabetes insipidus: A condition in which the Kidneys are unable to conserve water.
  • Insulin dependent (type 1): Severe - absolute deficiency of insulin
    Occurs mostly in children but may occur in adults, especially the non-obese or elderly when hyperglycemia first appears
    Polyuria
    Polydipsia
    Rapid weight loss
    Hyperglycemia
    Ketonemia
    Ketonuria
  • Non-insulin dependent (type 2):
    Most patients are over 40 years of age
    Most patients are obese
    Candida vaginitis in women
    Hypertension
    Hyperlipidemia
    Arteriosclerosis
    Many patients have few or no symptoms
