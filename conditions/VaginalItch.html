VAGINAL ITCH, PRURITIS VULVA

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • Persistent itching of the vulva and / or vagina
  • May be excessive vaginal discharge
Treatment Principle
Herb Formulas
  • For vulvar itch with ulceration: Vulvar Itch #1
  • Topical:
    1. She Chuang Zi San
    2. Grind 3g Pulvis Concha Meretricis/Cyclinae Ge Qiao into a fine powder. Apply to the external genitalia. Use 1-2 times per day for 10 days.
    3. Decoct 1 bulb of garlic and use as a fumigant and wash for 10 days.
    For pruritis vulva due to trichomonas vaginitis: Decoct 10g Sm. Cnidii She Chuang Zi, 10 grams Cx. Phellodendri Huang Bai and 6g Alumen Ming Fan and use as a wash.
    For pruritis vulva due to haemophilus vaginitis: Decoct 15g Cx. Pseudolaricis Tu Jing Pi, 15g Rx. Sophorae Flavescentis Ku Shen and 2g Borneolum Bing Pian and use as a wash
Points
  • For general itching, aching, pain fatigue, tired limbs and dribbling urination: LIV-1, REN-3, REN-4, SP-10, UB-31, UB-32, UB-33, UB-34, UB-40, UB-67
    For Heat vexation with extreme itching: + HT-8, LIV-2
    For Damp-Heat: + Baichongwo, SP-9
  • For itching of the external genitalia:
    1. LIV-5, LIV-11, REN-3, SP-9, SP-10
    2. LIV-8, SP-6, UB-67
    3. LIV-11, REN-1, REN-2, SP-6, SP-9
