HEART FIRE

Clinical Manifestations

  • Restlessness
  • Agitation
  • Anxiety
  • Difficulty falling asleep
  • Dream disturbed sleep
  • Frequent waking with nightmares
  • Palpitations
  • Ulceration, erosion, and pain of mouth and tongue
  • Thirst with a desire for cold drinks
  • Restlessness
  • Irritability
  • Irascibility
  • Feeling of Heat and disturbance in the Heart
  • Flushed face
  • Bitter taste in the mouth
  • Scanty, dark urine
  • A red complexion
  • Maybe painful urination
  • Maybe hematuria
  • T: Red tip, ulcers especially on tip or Red with a redder tip
  • C: Normal or Yellow
  • P: Excess and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Clear Heat
  • Purge Fire
  • Calm the Shen
Herb Formulas
  • Huang Lian Jie Du Tang

  • For Heart Yin Deficiency following a Shao Yin Heat condition: Huang Lian E Jiao Tang

  • With severe palpitations and anxiety: Zhu Sha An Shen Wan
  • With Blood and Yin Deficiency: Zhu Sha An Shen Wan

    Niu Huang Shang Qing Wan
    Niu Huang Jie Du Pian
    Dao Chi Pian
    Xiao Er Qi Xing Cha

  • HT-7, HT-8, PC-7, PC-8, UB-15
