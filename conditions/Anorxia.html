ANOREXIA, LACK OF APPETITE (POOR APPETITE)

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • Strictly speaking, anorexia means lack of appetite. Lack of appetite is an extension of a poor appetite or reduced food intake and is treated in the same way. Therefore anorexia, poor appetite and reduced food intake are included under this heading.
