VAGINITIS

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • Vaginitis is an inflammation of the vagina that can result in discharge, itching and pain. The cause is usually a change in the normal balance of vaginal bacteria or an infection. Reduced estrogen levels after menopause and some skin disorders can also cause vaginitis.
  • Symptoms:
    Change in color, odor or amount of discharge from your vagina
    Vaginal itching or irritation
    Pain during intercourse
    Painful urination
    Light vaginal bleeding or spotting
Treatment Principle
Herb Formulas
  • Herbal douche: Decoct equal amounts of HB. Epimedii Yin Yang Huo, Cx. Dictamni Xu Duan and Rx. Sophorae Flavescentis Ku Shen and use as a douche 3 times daily.
  • For vaginal flatulence: Shi Quan Da Bu Tang
  • For vaginal bleeding due to intercourse: Gui Pi Tang  modified
  • Fumigant and wash for a swollen vagina: Qi Ai Fang Feng Da Ji Ao Tang
