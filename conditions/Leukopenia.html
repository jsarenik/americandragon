LEUKOPENIA

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • Leukopenia is a low white blood cell count, a decrease in leukocytes in the Blood.
Treatment Principle
Herb Formulas
  • Due to chemotherapy and radiotherapy: Steam 15-30g Cx. Acanthopanacis Wu Jia Pi and take orally.
Points
  • DU-14, LI-11, SP-6, ST-36, UB-20
  • Moxa or moxa needle Pirexue, UB-17, UB-20, UB-23, Yishu
  • REN-4, ST-36, UB-23
  • REN-4, REN-6, REN-20, SP-6, ST-36

LUNG QI AND YIN DEFICIENCY

Clinical Manifestations
  • Soft, weak cough with clear thin sputum
  • Physical and mental exhaustion
  • Lassitude
  • Weakness
  • Soft, weak voice
  • Reluctance to speak
  • Shortness of breath
  • Pale face
  • Severe, dry hacking cough
  • No sputum expectorated
  • Small amounts of difficult to expectorate sputum
  • Blood tinged sputum (when Yin Deficiency Fire is present)
  • Afternoon fever
  • Night sweats
  • Malar flush (especially in afternoon)
  • Five-Sole-Heat
  • Dry mouth and throat
  • Itching in throat
  • Hoarse voice, low voice or loss of voice
  • Emaciation
  • Dysphoria
  • Dizziness
  • Blurred vision
  • T: Pale red and dry
  • C: None
  • P: Weak or Thready and rapid or Thready and weak
Treatment Principle
  • Reinforce and moisten the Lungs
  • Strengthen Qi
  • Nourish Yin
Herb Formulas
  • Sheng Mai San   modified
  • Si Jun Zi Tang + Qi Wei Du Qi Wan
Points
  • KI-3, KI-6, LU-1, LU-5, LU-6, LU-7, LU-9, LU-10, REN-17, ST-36, UB-13, UB-17, UB-23, UB-43

SPLEEN AND KIDNEY YANG DEFICIENCY

Clinical Manifestations
  • Body cold
  • Limbs icy
  • Complexion white and shiny
  • Low back, knees and abdomen feel cold and painful
  • Lower abdomen feels cold and painful
  • Poor appetite
  • Cold limbs
  • Loose stools containing undigested food
  • “Cock-crow” diarrhea (occurs at about 5 am)
  • Edema of face and limbs
  • Dysuria
  • Dizziness
  • Tinnitus
  • Fatigue
  • Disinclination to talk
  • Severe: Abdominal ascites
  • T: Pale, moist and swollen
  • C: Thin and white or White and greasy
  • P: Deep, thready, weak and maybe slow
Treatment Principle
  • Warm and Reinforce the Spleen and Kidneys
Herb Formulas
  • Fu Zi Li Zhong Wan  modified
  • Huang Qi Jian Zhong Tang  + You Gui Wan
Points
  • Moxa or moxa needle REN-4, REN-12, SP-6, SP-9, ST-36, UB-20, UB-23
