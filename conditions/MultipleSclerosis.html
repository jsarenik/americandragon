MULTIPLE SCLEROSIS

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • May show demylenation in the brain, spinal cord and optic nerves.
  • Varied and individual symptomatology - including:
    Stress
    Depression
    Anxiety
    Weakness
    Tingling
    Numbness
    Spasms
    Tremors
    Gait disturbance
    Speech problems
    Visual problems
    Digestive disturbance
    Incontinence of urine
Treatment Principle
Herb Formulas
Points
  • Scalp Acupuncture:
    For motor problems (paralysis, weakness of limbs, tingling, numbness, pain in limbs, urine and bowel problems): Sensory and Foot motor areas unilaterally or bilaterally
    For dizziness: Dizziness area
    For balance problems: Balance area
    For limb spasms: Tremor area
    Rotate 200 times/minute for three minutes, rest 10 minutes and repeat 3 to 4 times.
  • Lower Limbs: Electro-acupuncture or rotate 200 times/minute and moxa GB-34, LI-3, KI-3, Bafeng
    Upper Limbs: Electro-acupuncture or rotate 200 times/minute and moxa LI-11, LI-4, SJ-5, Baxie
    For older and weak patients: Moxa ST-36, SP-6, REN-4, KI-1, UB-23

SPLEEN AND STOMACH DAMP-HEAT

Clinical Manifestations
  • Flaccid or slightly swollen legs
  • Legs a little hot to the touch
  • Legs may sometimes feel cold
  • Legs may be numb and swollen
  • Feet feel hot with a desire to stretch them out
  • General heaviness
  • Sensation of fullness in the chest and epigastric region
  • Painful joints, worse with Heat
  • Maybe low grade fever
  • Frequent, urgent, yellow urination
  • Painful urination
  • Hot, brownish urine
  • T: Pink or Red
  • C: Sticky and yellow or Greasy and yellow
  • P: Soft and rapid or Slippery and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Eliminate Damp-Heat
  • Clear the channels

Clinical Manifestations

  • Unsteady standing and movement

  • Pale, dull complexion
  • Pale lips
  • Blurred or failing vision which is worse when tired
  • Muscle stiffness
  • Spastic cramping
  • Weak eyesight
  • Spots before the eyes
  • Dizziness
  • Dry eyes
  • Pale nail beds
  • Irritability
  • Frightens easily
  • Insomnia with much dreaming
  • Tinnitus
  • Numbness of the limbs

  • Amenorrhea

  • General debility
  • Pale face
  • Low libido
  • Urinary frequency with abundant, clear urine or
  • Female infertility ("uterus cold and infertile")
  • Oliguria with edema
  • Maybe nocturia
  • Maybe anorexia
  • Lassitude
  • Maybe chronic diarrhea or "cock crow" diarrhea
  • Dizziness
  • Maybe loose stools

  • Failure to thrive
