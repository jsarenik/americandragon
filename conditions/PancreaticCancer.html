PANCREATIC CANCER

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
Treatment Principle
Herb Formulas
  • Xian Zhong Pian
Points

TOXIC DAMP-HEAT

Clinical Manifestations
  • Pain and fullness in ribs on both sides
  • Bitter taste in mouth
  • Nausea
  • Vomiting sour and bitter fluids
  • Anorexia
  • Maybe alternating chills and fever
  • Concentrated, dark urine
  • Local redness, swelling and pain
  • Maybe fever and chills
  • Abdominal distention and discomfort
  • Suffocating sensation in the chest
  • Dislike of oily foods
  • Lassitude
  • Maybe thirst or thirst with no desire to drink
  • Diarrhea with foul smell or
  • Constipation
  • Fever
  • Sweet sticky taste or bitter taste in mouth
  • Dizziness
  • Tinnitus or deafness
  • Yellow vaginal discharge with foul smell
  • T: Pink
  • C: Yellow and greasy or sticky
  • P: Wiry and rapid or Slippery and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Clear Heat
  • Relieve Dampness
  • Resolve toxicity
  • Inhibit the growth of cancer cells
Herb Formulas
  • Pancreatic Cancer #1
  • With Liver cancer and jaundice: Liver Cancer #9
Points

BLOOD STAGNATION

Clinical Manifestations
  • Stabbing pain in the epigastrium which is worse with pressure
  • Substantial or insubstantial abdominal mass which is painful on palpation
  • Vomiting food mixed with Blood
  • Dry, black stools
  • Melaena
  • Dry skin
  • T: Purple or Purple spots
  • C: White
  • P: Deep and thready or Choppy
Treatment Principle
  • Invigorate the Blood
  • Eliminate Stasis
Herb Formulas
  • Pancreatic Cancer #2
Points

QI, BLOOD AND PHLEGM STAGNATION

Clinical Manifestations
  • Facial color dark and dull
  • Emaciation
  • Sides and ribs distended on both sides (maybe with sharp pain)
  • Enlargement of the Liver and Spleen
  • Suffocating sensation in chest
  • Tendency to sigh
  • Melancholy
  • Depression
  • Irritability
  • Inappropriate anger
  • Irregular menses
  • Dysmenorrhea
  • Breast distention and pain before and during menses
  • Anorexia
  • Edema
  • Abdominal distention
  • Chest and hypochondriac distention
  • Dizziness
  • Nausea
  • Vomiting
  • Thirst with no desire to drink
  • Maybe no thirst
  • Palpitations
  • Shortness of Breath
  • T: Varies from Normal to Dark purple (maybe purple spots on edge of tongue)
  • C: Thick and slippery
  • P: Wiry, choppy and slow or Slippery, full and maybe wiry
Treatment Principle
  • Circulate Qi
  • Invigorate the Blood
  • Break up Stasis
  • Eliminate Phlegm
Herb Formulas
  • Pancreatic Cancer #3
Points

TOXIC HEAT WITH BLOOD STAGNATION

Clinical Manifestations
  • Hypochondriac pain
  • A lump under the ribs
  • High fever
  • Thirst
  • Epistaxis
  • Spots on the skin
  • Constipation
  • Scanty, red urine
  • Hemorrhaging
  • Loss of consciousness
  • May be ascites (late stage)
  • Weakness
  • Bleeds easily
  • Hypochondriac pain
  • Chest distention
  • Abdominal pain
  • Poor appetite
  • Fatigue
  • Pain that is fixed, stabbing and /or sever
  • Pain is worse with pressure
  • Dark complexion
  • Fixed, palpable masses
  • Bleeding with clots
  • Purple lips
  • Purple nails
  • Dry skin
  • Blue, dark or purplish discoloration of skin in small or large areas,
  • Varicose and spider veins
  • Cold hands and feet
  • Tendency to hemorrhage dark blood or clots
  • Maybe jaundice
  • T: Purple, possible purple or red spots
    Red purple if there is Heat
  • C: Yellow
  • P: Choppy or Wiry and choppy or Thready and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Disperse Heat
  • Relieve toxicity
Herb Formulas
  • For sharp pain due to Blood Stagnation: Cancer #35
