RENAL TUBERCULOSIS, NEPHROTUBERCULOSIS (TUBERCULOSIS OF THE KIDNEYS)

TAI YIN EXCESS

Clinical Manifestations
  • Nausea
  • Clear, frequent urination
  • Lack of thirst
  • Anorexia
  • Vomiting of clear fluid
  • Abdominal distension and pain (sharp)
  • Diarrhea or loose stool
  • Prefers warmth
  • T: Pale or pink
  • C: White
  • P: Slow and tight or Soft
Treatment Principle
  • Regulate the middle
  • Warm the Middle Jiao
  • Disperse Cold
  • Relieve pain
Herb Formulas
  • Li Zhong Wan
Points
  • For pain and Cold: moxa the abdomen
  • To strengthen the Spleen and Stomach: LIV-13, REN-10, ST-36, UB-20, UB-21 (moxa if severe)
  • For abdominal pain and nausea: PC-6, SP-4, ST-25
  • For frequent, clear urination: moxa REN-4, REN-6, UB-23
  • For diarrhea: moxa REN-8
  • Use few needles with deep insertion and moxa

KIDNEY YANG DEFICIENCY

Clinical Manifestations
  • Lumbago: low back feels cold, weak and aching
  • Feeling chilled
  • Aversion to Cold
  • Cold limbs
  • General debility
  • Pale face
  • Urinary frequency with abundant, clear urine or
  • Oliguria with edema
  • Maybe nocturia
  • Lassitude
  • Withdrawn, subdued behavior
  • Knees feel cold and aching
  • Dizziness
  • Tinnitus
  • Impotence
  • Spermatorrhea
  • Premature ejaculation
  • Low libido
  • Female infertility ("uterus cold and infertile")
  • Maybe anorexia
  • Maybe loose stools
  • Maybe chronic diarrhea or "cock crow" diarrhea
  • T: Pale and swollen
  • C: Thin and white
  • P: Deep and thready - maybe slow
Treatment Principle
  • Warm and Reinforce Kidney Yang
Herb Formulas
  • Jin Gui Shen Qi Wan (Ba Wei Di Huang Wan)
  • Niu Che Shen Qi Wan
Points
  • Moxa or moxa needle DU-4, KI-3, REN-4, UB-23
    For chronic diarrhea, rectal prolapse and Vanquished Yang: + moxa DU-20, REN-8
  • For Kidney Yang Deficiency impotence: moxa or moxa needle DU-4, DU-20, KI-3, REN-4, UB-23
  • For Kidney Yang Deficiency (Cock Crow) Diarrhea: moxa or moxa needle DU-20, KI-3, REN-4, UB-23

DAMP-HEAT IN THE URINARY BLADDER

Clinical Manifestations
  • Urinary frequency
  • Urinary urgency
  • Painful urination or
  • Continuous dripping of urine or
  • Sudden interruption to the urinary stream
  • Turbid urine or
  • Pus and blood in the urine or
  • Sand and stones in the urine
  • In Damp-Heat Pouring Downward there may also be:
    Fever
    Thirst
    Dry mouth
    Low back pain
  • In Damp-Heat Crystallizes in Urinary Bladder there may also be:
    Colicky pain in the low back, possibly radiating to the groin
  • T: Red
  • C: Yellow
  • P: Slippery and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Clear Heat
  • Eliminate Dampness by diuresis
Herb Formulas
  • Xiao Ji Yin Zi
Points
  • KI-7, LIV-2, REN-3, SP-6, SP-9, UB-23, UB-28, UB-39
  • For retention of urine:
    1. REN-3, SP-6, SP-9, UB-28
    2. REN-3, SP-6, SP-9, UB-39
  • For Blood Lin:
    1. KI-3, LIV-2, REN-3, SP-6, SP-9, SP-10, UB-28
    2. KI-1, KI-7, LIV-3, REN-3, SP-10, UB-17, UB-23, UB-27
  • For Stone Lin:
    1. KI-2, KI-3, LIV-2, REN-3, SP-9, UB-28, UB-39
    2. KI-1, LIV-1, REN-4, UB-23, UB-28, UB-40
  • For Milky Lin:
    1. KI-3, KI-6, LIV-2, REN-3, SP-9, UB-23, UB-28
    2. REN-4, REN-6, SP-6, SP-9, UB-22, UB-27, UB-28
