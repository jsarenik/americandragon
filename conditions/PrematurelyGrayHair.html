PREMATURELY GRAY HAIR

LIVER QI STAGNATION

Clinical Manifestations
  • Gray hair
  • Pain and distention in the chest and lateral costal regions
  • Vexation
  • Irritability
  • Anorexia
  • Irregular menstruation
  • Breast distention and pain either before or during menstruation
  • Symptoms are worse with motional disturbance
  • T: Red tip and edges
  • C: Thin and white or Thin and yellow
  • P: Wiry and Rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Regulate the Liver
  • Resolve depression
  • Clear Heat
  • Blacken the hair
Herb Formulas
  • Jia Wei Xiao Yao San  modified  
Points
  • LIV-3, LIV-14, SP-6, ST-44

KIDNEY YANG DEFICIENCY

Clinical Manifestations
  • Prematurely gray hair
  • Lumbago: low back feels cold, weak and aching
  • Feeling chilled
  • Aversion to Cold
  • Cold limbs
  • Edema of the lower extremities
  • General debility
  • Pale face
  • Urinary frequency with abundant, clear urine or
  • Oliguria with edema
  • Maybe nocturia
  • Lassitude
  • Withdrawn, subdued behavior
  • Knees feel cold and aching
  • Dizziness
  • Tinnitus
  • Muddleheadedness or
  • Slow to learn
  • Impotence
  • Spermatorrhea
  • Premature ejaculation
  • Low libido
  • Female infertility ("uterus cold and infertile")
  • Maybe anorexia
  • Maybe loose stools
  • Maybe chronic diarrhea or "cock crow" diarrhea
  • T: Pale and swollen
  • C: Thin and white
  • P: Deep and thready - maybe slow
Treatment Principle
  • Warm and tonify Kidney Yang
  • Replenish Jing
  • Tonify the Blood
Herb Formulas
  • You Gui Wan
  • Ba Wei Di Huang Wan
  • Chinese Patent Medicines:
    Jin Kui Shen Qi Wan

    Ge Jie Bu Shen Wan
