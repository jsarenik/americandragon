BONE CANCER, OSTEOSARCOMA

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
Treatment Principle
Herb Formulas
  • For osteocytoma: Bone Cancer #3
  • For giant cell tumor of the bone: Take 4-8 pills of Ping Xiao Dan  (Chinese Patent Medicine) three times daily
  • For bone metastasis: Yang He Tang  modified
  • For cancer of maxillary antrum: Take 60g Rx. Actinidiae Chinensis Teng Li Gen stewed with meat for one month consecutively. It should relieve swelling, pain and nasal discharge and reduce the lump. Radiotherapy should be combined.

TOXIC DAMP-HEAT

Clinical Manifestations
  • Local redness, swelling and pain
  • Maybe fever and chills
  • Anorexia
  • Abdominal distention and discomfort
  • Suffocating sensation in the chest
  • Dislike of oily foods
  • Nausea
  • Vomiting
  • Lassitude
  • Scanty, dark urine
  • Maybe thirst or thirst with no desire to drink
  • Diarrhea with foul smell or
  • Constipation
  • Fever
  • Sweet sticky taste or bitter taste in mouth
  • Dizziness
  • Tinnitus or deafness
  • Poor memory
  • Aching, soreness or weakness of low back
  • Spermatorrhea or premature ejaculation
  • Aching in bones
  • Aching in leg and heel
  • Lumbago: low back feels cold, weak and aching
  • Feeling chilled
  • Aversion to Cold
Treatment Principle
  • Promote urination
  • Dispel Dampness
  • Clear Heat
  • Relieve Fire toxins
  • Tonify the Liver and Kidneys
  • Strengthen the sinews and bones
Herb Formulas
  • Bone Cancer #1
  • Cancer #24
  • With Kidney Yang Deficiency and Wind-Dampness: Bone Cancer #2

KIDNEY YANG DEFICIENCY WITH INTERNAL COLD

Clinical Manifestations
  • Localized painful swellings without a head that bleed into the surrounding tissues and do not affect the color or texture of the skin
  • The swellings are not hot to the touch
  • No thirst
  • Swollen and painful joints
  • Wasting of the muscles of the thigh and calf
  • T: Very pale or Pale-pink
  • C: White
  • P: Thready and forceless or Deep and slow
Treatment Principle
  • Warm Yang
  • Tonify the Blood
  • Disperse Cold
  • Eliminate Stagnation
Herb Formulas
