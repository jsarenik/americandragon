TAI YANG SHANG HAN

  • Dislike of Cold

  • Diarrhea

  • Headache

  • T: Normal
  • C: Thin and white
  • P: Floating and tight
Treatment Principle
  • Expel Wind
  • Dispel Cold
  • Ventilate the Lungs
  • Resolve Phlegm
Herb Formulas
  • Ge Gen Tang
Points
  • DU-16, GB-20, LI-4 + LU-7 or LI-4 + KI-7, LU-5, UB-12, UB-13

TAI YANG - YANG MING

Clinical Manifestations
  • Acute or recurrent, frequent, urgent, foul smelling, hot diarrhea
  • A burning sensation in the anus and/or
  • Tenesmus
  • Fever or
  • Afternoon fever
  • Unproductive sweating which tends to come in waves and is worse in the afternoon
  • Thirst
  • Restlessness
  • Irritability
  • Gasping
  • Palpitations
  • Tenesmus
  • Cramping abdominal pain
  • Maybe red tinged urine
  • Irritability and Heat in the chest and epigastrium
  • Stiffness and spasms at the back of the neck
  • Dry mouth
  • Dyspnea with perspiration
  • Maybe wheezing
  • Concentrated scanty urine
  • T: Red
  • C: Yellow and greasy
  • P: Rapid or Slippery and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Release the Exterior
  • Clear Internal Yang Ming Heat
  • Raise Yang to stop diarrhea
Herb Formulas
  • Ge Gen Huang Qin Huang Lian Tang
  • Chinese patent medicines:
    Huang Lian Jie Du Wan
    Chuan Xin Lian Kang Yan Pian
    Bao Ji Wan
    Huang Lian Su Pian
Points
  • LI-11, SP-9, ST-25, ST-37, ST-44, Zhixie
    With high fever: + DU-14, LI-4
    With nausea: + PC-6
    With sudden, severe diarrhea, vomiting and fever: + bleed or needle PC-3, UB-40
  • Ear: Large Intestine, Small Intestine, Spleen, Abdomen, Distal Segment Rectum, Sympathetic
