UTERINE CANCER

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • Uterine cancer is the abnormal growth of any cells that comprise uterine tissue. The buildup of cancer cells may form a mass. Non-cancer cells that form a mass are termed benign tumors.
Treatment Principle
Herb Formulas
  • Grind 30-60g of the shell or kernel of Fr. Trapae Bispinosae Ling Jiao into a powder. Take 6g raw or with honey at a time.
  • Decoct 24g Rz. et Rx. Veratri Nigri Li Lu with 18g Sm. Iridis Ma Lin Zi and take once a day.
  • Uterine neck: Apply a 10%-20% solution of Rz. Dysosmae Pleianthae Ba Jiao Lian directly to the lesion.
  • With breast cancer: Uterine Cancer #7
