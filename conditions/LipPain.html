LIP PAIN

WIND-COLD

Clinical Manifestations
  • Sudden onset of lip pain after catching a cold
  • A cold feeling on the face and lips
  • A purplish color around the lips
  • Aggravation of lip pain on exposure to Cold
  • Aversion to Cold
  • A slight fever
  • Headache
  • Generalized body pain
  • Lack of thirst
  • Coughing with expectoration of white Phlegm
  • T: normal
  • C: Thin and white
  • P:Floating and tight
Treatment Principle
  • Dispel wind
  • Eliminate Cold
  • Remove toxins
Herb Formulas
  • Jing Fang Bai Du San  modified
  • Chinese Patent Medicines:
    Jing Fang Bai Du Pian
    Chuan Xiong Cha Tiao Pian
Points
  • LI-4, LU-7, REN-24, SJ-5, ST-4, UB-12
    With severe lip pain: + SP-8, ST-34
    With swollen lips: + ST-40
    With insect bites: + DU-10, SP-6
    With aggravation of the pain on exposure to Cold: + moxa or moxa needle LI-4, REN-6, SJ-5
    With a headache: + GB-20
    With a cough and expectoration of white Phlegm: + LU-5, UB-13

WIND-HEAT

Clinical Manifestations

  • Yellow blisters on the lips
  • Red, swollen lips
  • Fever
  • A slight aversion to Cold
  • Headache
  • Thirst
  • Coughing
  • T: Red tip
  • C: Thin and yellow
  • P: Floating and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Dispel wind
  • Clear Heat
  • Remove toxins
Herb Formulas
  • Yin Qiao San  modified
  • Chinese Patent Medicines:
    Yin Qiao Pian
    Yin Qiao Jie Du Pian
Points
  • DU-24, DU-28, LI-2, LI-4, LU-7, LU-10, SJ-3, ST-4
    For many blisters with a yellow discharge : + GB-43, ST-44

    With insect bites: + DU-10, SP-6

WIND-HEAT (FIRE) WITH SPLEEN AND STOMACH DAMP-HEAT

Clinical Manifestations
  • Chronic or recurring burning pain, redness, swelling, dryness, cracking, bleeding, blistering, erosion, exfoliation and crusting of the lips
  • Dry mouth
  • A bitter taste in the mouth
  • A sticky or cloying sensation in the mouth
  • Thirst with no desire to drink
  • A red complexion
  • Greasy skin
  • Possible strong body odor
  • Anorexia
  • Morning nausea
  • Fullness and stuffiness in the chest
  • Restlessness
  • Irritability
  • Maybe a low-grade or afternoon fever
  • A heavy sensation of the body
  • Lethargy
  • Loose stools or
  • A tendency toward sluggish stools or
  • Constipation
  • Concentrated urine
  • T: Reddish
  • C: Dry or greasy and yellow
  • P: Slippery or Soft and rapid or Thready and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Dispel Wind and Heat
  • Clear Damp-Heat
  • Nourish and cool the Blood
  • Moisten Yin and Dryness
Herb Formulas
  • Si Wu Xiao Feng San
  • Topical: Huang Lian Gao
  • Chinese Patent Medicine:
    Xiao Feng Wan
    Xi Gua Shuang
Points
  • DU-26, DU-27, LI-4, LI-11, LIV-8, REN-12, REN-24, ST-4, SP-9, ST-4, ST-36

WIND-DAMP-HEAT (FIRE)

Clinical Manifestations
  • Burning lip pain, itchiness or tingling in the early stages
  • Cracked or swollen lips
  • Blisters or erosion appear over time
  • Maybe a serous exudate
  • The lips may quiver or tremble
  • Maybe bleeding, dryness, gradual exfoliation and scabbing
  • A thin, yellow membrane may appear over the mucous membrane of the inner lip
  • Fever or
  • Fever and chills or
  • Alternating fever and chills
  • Thirst with a desire for cool drinks
  • Halitosis
  • Dry stools
  • Constipation
  • Concentrated urine
  • Frontal headache
  • Y: Red o0r
    Red tip
  • C: Yellow
  • P: Floating in the early stage, becoming slippery or wiry and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Dispel Wind-Damp-Heat
  • Clear Toxic Heat
Herb Formulas
  • Shuang Jie Tong Sheng San
  • Topical: Huang Lian Gao
  • Chinese Patent Medicine:
    Fang Feng Tong Sheng Wan
    Huang Lian Shang Qing Pian
    Niu Huang Jie Du Pian
    Xi Gua Shuang
Points
  • DU-14, DU-26, DU-27, LI-4, LI-11, REN-24, ST-4, ST-36, ST-44
    With constipation: + SJ-6, ST-25
    With severe Heat, redness and swelling: + ST-45
  • Ear: Mouth, Lips, Spleen, Shenmen

SPLEEN AND STOMACH EXCESS HEAT

Clinical Manifestations
  • Lip pain with redness and swelling
  • Cracked lips
  • Ulcerations on the lips or tongue
  • Thirst with a preference for cold drinks
  • Hungers easily
  • Toothache
  • Bad breath
  • Constipation
  • Stomach pain
  • Maybe gum bleeding
  • T: Red
  • C: Yellow and dry
  • P: Forceful and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Clear Heat
  • Reduce Fire
  • Stop pain
Herb Formulas
  • Qing Wei San  modified
  • Xie Huang San
  • Chinese Patent Medicine: Wei Te Ling
Points
  • DU-28, LI-4, LI-11, SP-2, ST-3, ST-4, ST-42, ST-44, ST-45
    With an ulcerated tongue and lips and bad breath: + SP-6, ST-40
    With bleeding gums: + SP-10
    With Stomach pain: + REN-12, UB-21
    With thirst and dry lips and mouth: + SP-5
    With constipation: + ST-25

STOMACH FIRE BLAZING

Clinical Manifestations
  • Lip pain which is better with Cold
  • Burning Heat and pain in the epigastrium
  • Irritating thirst: drinks copious fluids and prefers cold drinks
  • Excessive appetite: hungry again quickly after eating (as if food vanishes)
  • Swelling, pain, ulceration and bleeding of gums
  • Foul breath
  • Toothache
  • Maybe vomiting right after eating
  • Vomiting acid or undigested food
  • Constipation
  • Maybe scanty, red urine
  • T: Red
  • C: Thick, yellow and dry
  • P: Slippery, big, Excess and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Clear and Purge Stomach Fire
Herb Formulas
  • Qing Wei San modified
  • Mai Men Dong Tang modified
Points
  • LI-4, LI-11, PC-6, PC-8, ST-6, ST-34, ST-36, ST-43, ST-44, ST-45, UB-21

  • Outbreaks are initiated by stress
  • Frontal or temporal headaches
  • Shoulder and neck tension
  • Tooth grinding at night
  • Red eyes
  • Facial flushing
  • Depression
  • Irritability
  • Moodiness
  • Angers easily
  • Hypochondriac tension or discomfort

  • A bitter taste in the mouth
  • Irregular menstruation
  • Premenstrual syndrome

  • C: Thick and yellow
