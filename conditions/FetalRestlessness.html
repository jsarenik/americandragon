FETAL RESTLESSNESS

SPLEEN DEFICIENCY

Clinical Manifestations
  • Fetal restlessness
  • Increasing bearing down sensation
  • Lumbago
  • Abdominal distention
  • Watery stools and/or
  • Abdominal pain and distention with severe vomiting and diarrhea
  • T: Pale
  • C: Thin and sticky or White
  • P: Thready and slippery
Treatment Principle
  • Tonify the Spleen
  • Replenish Qi
  • Calm the fetus
Herb Formulas
  • Xiang Sha Liu Jun Zi Tang
  • With Food Stagnation: Jian Pi Wan

LIVER QI STAGNATION INVADES THE SPLEEN

Clinical Manifestations
  • Fetal restlessness
  • Continuous cramping pain in the abdomen
  • Pain is not severe
  • Pain is relieved by pressure
  • Edema of the feet
  • Urinary difficulty
  • Slight edema (primarily of the lower limbs)
  • Symptoms generally occur during pregnancy
  • Pale face
  • Fatigue
  • Heaviness of head
  • Lassitude
  • Lower abdominal pain
  • Dizziness
  • Tinnitus
  • Lumbago
  • Edema
  • Abdominal pain with edema during pregnancy due to Blood Deficiency and abnormal water circulation
  • Oliguria
  • Palpitations
  • Cold feet
  • Extreme coldness of the hands and feet
  • Vertigo
  • Weakness and numbness of lower back and legs during pregnancy or postpartum
  • Mal position of the fetus
  • Dysmenorrhea
  • Irregular menstruation
  • Right upper quadrant pain
  • Hepatomegaly
  • Anemia
  • Lack of energy
  • Sore back
  • Thirst with no desire to drink
  • T: Pale or pink maybe petechiae on margin
  • C: Thin or Greasy white
  • P: Deep and weak or Soft, floating, small and slow or Wiry and small or Wiry and rough
Treatment Principle
  • Nourish Liver Blood
  • Soften the Liver
  • Spread Liver Qi
  • Strengthen the Spleen
  • Resolve Dampness
  • Reinforce the Spleen and Kidney
Herb Formulas
  • Dang Gui Shao Yao San

DEFICIENCY COLD OF THE UTERUS

  • Sallow complexion or
  • Dark and gloomy complexion
  • Pain of lower limbs
  • Lassitude
  • Lack of vitality
  • Weakness
  • Irregular circulation of channels
  • Failure to conceive for a long time
  • Intolerance of Cold
  • Cold limbs
  • Soreness of waist
  • Pain in the lower back
  • Long-term or heavy menstrual bleeding
  • Painful menstruation which gets increasing worse
  • Pelvic pain during intercourse
  • Fullness and stuffiness in the hypochondriac region
  • Dizziness
  • Tinnitus
  • Shortness of breath
  • Difficult speech
  • Delayed menstrual cycle
  • Abdominal pain during menstruation
  • Abdominal Cold pain which can be relieved by warmth
  • Scanty menstruation
  • Dark in color
  • Blood masses or
  • Amenorrhea
  • Distending pain in the lower abdomen prior to or during menstruation which can be relieved by warmth
  • T: Pale
  • C: White or White and greasy fur
  • P: Weak or Deep and tense pulse or Thready and weak or Deep and slow

  • Reinforce the Kidney and Liver
  • Support Yang
  • Eliminate Cold
  • Warm the uterus
  • Nourish the Blood
  • Invigorate the Blood
