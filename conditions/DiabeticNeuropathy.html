DISHARMONY BETWEEN THE LIVER AND SPLEEN

Clinical Manifestations
  • Abdominal pain
  • Irritability
  • Slight chills
  • Spasms of the calf muscles
  • Cramps in the hands
  • Weak legs
  • Hypertonicity of the extremities characterized by difficult flexion and extension
  • Wheezing
  • Coughing
  • Hiccup
  • Constipation
  • Vomiting
  • Diarrhea
  • Sciatic pain
  • Severe and painful muscle spasms in the Stomach, bronchi, Gallbladder and uterus. Intestines
  • Shoulder pain
  • Pain in the head, body or limbs due to arterial spasms characterized by a severe pulling pain
  • T: Normal or Reddish
  • C: None or Yellowish
  • P: Wiry and rapid or Thready and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Soften the Liver
  • Nourish the Blood
  • Nourish Yin
  • Moderate painful spasms
  • Relieve pain
  • Harmonize the Middle Jiao
  • Replenishes Body Fluids
Herb Formulas
  • Shao Yao Gan Cao Tang

KIDNEY YANG DEFICIENCY WITH WATER FLOODING

Clinical Manifestations
  • Edema of whole body particularly at and below the waist
  • Pitting edema
  • Low back pain, aching and heaviness
  • Abdominal distention with full sensations
  • Oliguria
  • Cold limbs
  • Face gray and congested or pale
  • Feeling nervous and cold with mental weariness
  • In severe cases:
    Ascites
    Edema of scrotum
  • T: Pale and swollen
  • C: Thin, white or Thick, white and sticky
  • P: Deep and thready
Treatment Principle
  • Warm Kidneys
  • Promote diuresis
Herb Formulas
  • Ji Sheng Shen Qi Wan
  • Jia Wei Shen Qi Wan
Points
  • KI-7, REN-6, REN-9, SP-6, SP-9, ST-28, ST-36, UB-20, UB-22, UB-23, UB-39

BLOOD STAGNATION

Clinical Manifestations
  • Palpitations
  • Dizziness
  • Blurred vision
  • Pale, lusterless complexion
  • Pale nails
  • Abdominal pain
  • Irregular menstruation with little flow
  • Fixed lower abdominal pain
  • Vertigo
  • Muscle ache of the neck, back, legs and shoulder
  • Whole body pain
  • Shoulder pain
  • Elbow pain
  • Pain at the waist
  • Arm pain
  • Low back pain
  • Leg pain
  • Tinnitus
  • Pale lips
  • May be amenorrhea
  • Insomnia
  • Menstruation may also be accompanied by distention and pain
  • T: Pale or Purple
  • C: Normal
  • P: Thin and weak or Thin and choppy
Treatment Principle
  • Invigorate the Blood
  • Dispel Blood Stasis
  • Drain Dampness
Herb Formulas
  • Zhu Yu Tang
Points

BLOOD STAGNATION DUE TO A MILD ATTACK OF WIND COLD WITH QI DEFICIENCY

Clinical Manifestations
  • Relatively superficial numbness without pain
  • Paresthesia and numbness of the skin and flesh
  • Paresthesia of the shoulders
  • Neck and shoulder stiffness and pain
  • Abdominal pain alleviated by warmth and pressure
  • Pale complexion
  • Hemiplegia
  • Stiff tongue
  • Flaccidity if the extremities
  • Migratory pain
  • Pain, weakness, tightness or spasms of the extremities
  • Spontaneous sweating
  • Shortness of breath
  • Aphasia
  • Excess salivation
  • Aversion to Wind
  • T: Normal or Thick
  • C: White or Smooth and white or White and greasy
  • P: Faint, choppy and tight or Slightly rough and tight or Large and weak or Slow and large
Treatment Principle
  • Tonify Qi and Blood
  • Warm and harmonize the channels and collaterals
  • Harmonize the Ying and Wei
  • Unblock painful obstruction
  • Warm the Middle Jiao
  • Dispel Cold
  • Alleviate pain
  • Harmonize Yin and Yang
Herb Formulas
  • Huang Qi Gui Zhi Wu Wu Tang
Points

DAMP-HEAT

Clinical Manifestations
  • Soreness, heaviness and lack of strength of the four extremities or of the two lower extremities
  • Possible progressive paralysis or atrophy
  • Insensitivity, numbness or tingling of the four extremities
  • Limb aching and pain
  • Burning Heat which is ameliorated by Cold
  • Generalized fever which is not easily alleviated
  • Possible deviation of the face, mouth and eyes
  • Poor appetite
  • Thirst with no desire to drink
  • Nausea/vomiting
  • Abnormal vaginal discharge
  • Red, hot, rough, painful urination
  • Loose stools
  • T: Red
  • C Slimy and yellow
  • P: Rapid or Slippery and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Clear Heat
  • Eliminate Dampness
Herb Formulas
  • Qing Xin Lian Zi Yin
