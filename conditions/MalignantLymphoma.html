MALIGNANT LYMPHOMA, LYMPHOSARCOMA, LYMPHOCARCINOMA

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • Malignant lymphoma is a malignant cancer in the body’s lymphatic system.
  • The most common symptom is swollen glands.
Treatment Principle
Herb Formulas
  • Decoct 30-60g Rz. Dysosmae Pleianthae Ba Jiao Lian with millet wine in water and take daily.
  • Decoct 30g Rx. Wikstroemiae Pu Yin for four hours to remove toxicity and take orally.
  • Decoct 15-60g Rz. Smilacis Glabrae Tu Fu Ling in water and drink daily.
  • Take a quantity of Fr. Polygoni Orientalis Shui Hong Huang Zi and bake one half of it. Grind the unprocessed and baked halves together into a fine powder and take 6g with wine three times a day.
  • Take a decoction of 1.5g powdered Bombyx Batryticatus Jiang Can in hot water twice daily.
Points
  • Calcine and grind Fr. Lagenariae Ku Hu Lu into a powder and blend with sesame oil to be applied as a paste to the lesion.
  • Xian Zhong Pian
  • Take 1.5-3g Hb. et Rx. Gei Lu Bian Qing in pill form, prepared with honey every day all year long.
  • Immerse 30g of Hb. et Rx. Gei Lu Bian Qing in 400ml in white wine for some time, then take a teaspoonful of wine 3- four times a day.
  • Decoct 45g Hb. et Rx. Gei Lu Bian Qing in 500ml water down to 250ml and take 1 teaspoonfull every 1/2 hour.
  • Decoct 90g Hb. Cirsii Xiao Ji with thin pork and drink.
  • Pulverize 120g Rz. Pinelliae Preparatum Zhi Ban Xia and 180 g Bul. Fritillariae Cirrhosae Chuan Bei Mu, blend Succus Zingiberis Jiang Zhi  and make into boluses for oral administration. Take 3.6g twice a day.

TOXIC DAMP-HEAT

Clinical Manifestations
  • Local redness, swelling and pain
  • Maybe fever and chills
  • Anorexia
  • Abdominal distention and discomfort
  • Suffocating sensation in the chest
  • Dislike of oily foods
  • Nausea
  • Vomiting
  • Lassitude
  • Scanty, dark urine
  • Maybe thirst or thirst with no desire to drink
  • Diarrhea with foul smell or
  • Constipation
  • Fever
  • Sweet sticky taste or bitter taste in mouth
  • T: Pink
  • C: Thin white or yellow or Thick, yellow and greasy (maybe sticky)
  • P: Rapid and forceful or Soft and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Clear Heat
  • Cool and invigorate the Blood
  • Resolve toxicity
  • Promote urination
  • Dissolve accumulations
  • Inhibit the growth of cancer cells
