ABDOMINAL BLOCKAGE

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • Blockage generally results from Stagnation of Liver Qi or improper diet preventing Qi from its normal dispersal. Blockage is generally located in the Qi Level, so that the principal factor is strong pathogenic evil. Because of its relatively rapid development and short duration its course does not show stages.

FOOD STAGNATION WITH PHLEGM BLOCKAGE

Clinical Manifestations
  • Epigastric fullness, distention and pain with dislike of pressure
  • Belching with rotten food smell
  • Acid regurgitation
  • Detests food or
  • Vomits after eating
  • Distention is reduced after vomiting
  • Unformed stools or
  • Knotted constipation
  • T: Pink
  • C: Thick and greasy
  • P: Slippery
Treatment Principle
  • Regulate Qi
  • dissipate Phlegm
  • unblock the Intestines
  • Release retention
Herb Formulas
  • Liu Mo Tang  modified
Points
  • REN-6, REN-12, ST-25, ST-36, ST-40, UB-25
  • LIV-13, LIV-14, PC-6, REN-11, REN-12, SP-4, ST-21, ST-36, ST-44
  • Epigastric pain due to Food Stagnation in the Stomach:
    1. LIV-13, PC-6, REN-12, ST-36, ST-44
    2. LIV-13, ST-21, ST-44
    3. PC-6, REN-11, ST-36
    4. PC-6, REN-10, REN-12, SP-4, ST-21, ST-36
  • Vomiting due to Food Stagnation in the Stomach:
    PC-6, REN-12, SP-4, ST-25, ST-36

LIVER QI STAGNATION

Clinical Manifestations
  • Distention and pain in the sides or flanks
  • Suffocating sensation in the chest
  • Tendency to sigh
  • Melancholy
  • Depression
  • Irritability
  • Inappropriate anger
  • Irregular menses
  • Dysmenorrhea
  • Breast distention and pain before and during menses
  • T: Pink
  • C: Thin and white
  • P: Wiry
Treatment Principle
  • Soothe the Liver
  • Clear Stagnation
Herb Formulas
  • Mu Xiang Shun Qi Wan  modified
Points
  • LIV-1, LIV-13, REN-6, REN-13, UB-18
  • GB-40, GB-41, LIV-2, LIV-3, LIV-4, LIV-5, LIV-13, LIV-14, PC-6, SJ-6, SP-6, UB-18
