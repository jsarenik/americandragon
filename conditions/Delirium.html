DELIRIUM

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • Rapid onset
  • Inattention
  • Rambling
  • Irrelevant or incoherent speech
  • Difficulty staying awake during exam
  • Perceptual disturbances (misinterpretations, illusions or hallucinations)
  • Insomnia
  • Daytime sleepiness
  • Increased or decreased psychomotor activity
  • Disorientation (time, place or person)
  • Memory impairment (inability to learn new material or remember past events)
  • Evidence of specific organic factors
  • Etiologically related to the disturbance
  • or
  • Ruling out of any non-organic mental disorder
  • Fluctuation in the course of the disease
  • Most commonly occurs in those over eighty with pre-existing cognitive impairment

TAI YANG SHANG HAN WITH INTERNAL HEAT

Clinical Manifestations
  • Fever and chills
  • Maybe fever only
  • Achiness
  • Anhidrosis
  • Occipital headache
  • Loud cough
  • Sticky, yellow mucus
  • Sore throat
  • Generalized muscle aches
  • Nasal obstruction
  • Asthma
  • Aversion to Cold
  • Edema
  • Thirst with a desire for cold liquids
  • Restlessness
  • Irritability
  • Fidgeting
  • Constipation or
  • Dry stools
  • Sometimes dry achy eyes
  • T:  Normal or red or Red tip
  • C:  Yellow or white
  • P:  Floating and tight or Floating and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Expel Wind
  • Disperse Cold
  • Cool Heat
Herb Formulas
  • Shi Gao Tang
Points
  • DU-14, DU-16, GB-20, LI-4, LI-11, ST-44, UB-13, sedate LU-7, LU-9, LU-10, SJ-5, UB-12, tonify SP-1, SP-2
  • Dingchuan, DU-14, LI-4, LI-11, LU-5, REN-17, SJ-5, UB-12
    For a severe, swollen, sore throat: + LU-11, SI-17
    With Excess Internal Heat: + LU-10, SJ-2

WEN BING: YING STAGE HEAT ATTACKS THE PERICARDIUM

Clinical Manifestations
  • High fever worse at night
  • Thirst
  • Insomnia
  • Major mental problems
  • Dramatic change of personality
  • Delirium
  • Speech difficulty
  • Aphasia
  • Poor memory
  • Dizziness
  • Maybe fainting
  • Maybe coma
  • Feverish extremities
  • T: Deep red
  • P: Thready and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Clear Heat in Pericardium
Herb Formulas
  • An Gong Niu Huang Wan
  • Zi Xue Dan
  • Niu Huang Qing Xin Wan
Points
  • To Clear Heat from the Heart: HT-3, PC-8, Sifeng, bleed Shixuan
    For delirium: PC-7
    To detoxify: LI-11
    To Pacify Heart and calm the Shen: HT-5, HT-6, HT-7
    To Nourish Yin: KI-3, SP-6

PHLEGM OBSTRUCTS THE HEART ORIFICE: TENSE TYPE YANG OBSTRUCTION

Clinical Manifestations
  • Sudden collapse with loss of consciousness
  • Lockjaw (trismus)
  • Eyes open
  • Both fists clenched
  • Retention of urine
  • Constipation
  • Stiffness and spasm of limbs
  • Red face
  • Body feels hot
  • Coarse breathing
  • Bad breath
  • Restlessness
  • Involuntary movements
  • Usual sequelae of C.V.A. such as:
    Hemiplegia
    Aphasia
    Facial paralysis
  • T: Pink
  • C: Yellow and greasy
  • P: Slippery, wiry and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Clear Phlegm
  • Open the Orifices
Herb Formulas
  • (Ju Fang) Zhi Bao Dan
  • An Gong Niu Huang Wan
  • Ling (Yang) Jiao Gou Teng Tang modified
Points
  • Bleed the Jing-Well points of the Six Hand channels, needle DU-20, DU-26, KI-1, LIV-3, ST-40

LIVER FIRE

Clinical Manifestations
  • Severe, splitting headaches with dizziness
  • Sudden tinnitus or
  • Sudden deafness
  • Flushed face
  • Red, swollen, painful eyes
  • Irascibility with violent angry outbursts
  • Dry mouth
  • Bitter taste in mouth
  • Thirst
  • Vomiting sour or bitter liquid
  • Insomnia
  • Restlessness
  • Constipation or dry stools
  • Maybe rib pain
  • In severe cases:
    Mania
    Hemoptysis
    Hematemesis
    Epistaxis
  • Liver Fire Attacks the Lungs:
    Hemoptysis
    Rib pain which is severe when coughing
  • Liver Fire Attacks the Stomach:
    Hematemesis
  • T: Red tip and edges
  • C: Thick, yellow, dry and rough
  • P: Wiry, Excess and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Clear the Liver
  • Purge Fire
Herb Formulas
  • Long Dan Xie Gan Tang
Points
  • GB-12, GB-34, GB-37, LIV-2, UB-18, UB-19

TOXIC HEAT WITH PHLEGM RETENTION

Clinical Manifestations
  • Distending full painful sensation in the Stomach and Intestines
  • Vomiting
  • Diarrhea

  • Inability to swallow even fluid
  • Pinkish vomit
  • Dull complexion
  • Weight loss
  • Dry skin
  • Dry mouth and throat
  • Constipation
  • Stools like pebbles
  • Violent cramping abdominal pain
  • Nausea
  • Tenesmus
  • Restlessness
  • Mental confusion bordering on delirium
Treatment Principle
  • Transform Phlegm
  • Open the Orifices
  • Clear Toxicity
Herb Formulas
  • Yu Shu Dan
Points
