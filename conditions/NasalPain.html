NASAL PAIN

WIND-COLD

Clinical Manifestations
  • Acute onset of nasal pain
  • Blocked and/or runny nose with clear, watery nasal discharge
  • A diminished or complete disappearance of the sense of smell
  • Itching or tickling in the throat
  • Cough with expectoration of thin, watery or frothy white sputum
  • Chills with low grade fever
  • Aversion to Cold
  • Anhidrosis
  • No thirst
  • Headache
  • Aversion to Wind
  • Sneezing
  • Chest pain
  • General body aches
  • Maybe asthma
  • T: Pink
  • C: Thin, white and moist
  • P: Floating and tight
Treatment Principle
  • Expel Wind
  • Dispel Cold
  • Ventilate the Lungs
  • Resolve Phlegm
  • relieve blockage in the nose
Herb Formulas
  • Ma Huang Tang  modified
  • Chinese Patent Medicine: Chuan Xiong Cha Tiao Wan
Points
  • LI-4, LI-20, LU-6, LU-7, UB-12, UB-13, UB-64
    With aversion to Cold: + moxa or moxa needle LI-4, LI-20, LU-7, UB-12, UB-13
    With a severe cough and expectoration of white Phlegm: + LU-1, LU-5
    With expectoration of profuse Phlegm: + SP-6, ST-40
    With diminished or a lack of the sense of smell: + SI-18, ST-36
    With chest pain: + REN-17

WIND-HEAT

Clinical Manifestations
  • Acute nasal pain
  • Burning pain in the nostrils
  • Redness and swelling in the nose
  • Runny nose with turbid mucous
  • Cough with thick, sticky, yellow sputum
  • Sore, red, swollen throat
  • Thirst with a desire to drink cold liquids
  • Runny or blocked nose with thick, yellow discharge
  • Fever with perspiration
  • Slight chills
  • Aversion to Wind
  • Headache
  • Maybe dyspnea with flared nostrils
  • Maybe chest pain
  • T: Red tip
  • C: Thin and yellow
  • P: Superficial and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Expel Wind
  • Clear Heat
  • Ventilate the Lungs
Herb Formulas
  • Sang Ju Yin  modified
  • Chinese Patent Medicines:
    Yin Qiao Pian
    Yin Qiao Jie Du Pian
Points
  • LI-4, LI-20, LU-6, LU-7, LU-10, UB-2, UB-12, UB-13, UB-64
    With a fever: + DU-14, LI-11
    With a cough and expectoration of yellow Phlegm: + LU-5, ST-40
    With thirst: + LU-8
    With a headache: +GB-20
  • DU-14, DU-16, GB-20, LI-4 + LU-7 or LI-4 + SJ-5, LU-5, LU-10, LU-11, UB-12, UB-13
  • LI-4, LI-20, LU-1, LU-7, UB-13
    With a high fever: + DU-14

WIND-COLD-DAMPNESS

Clinical Manifestations
  • Slight pain in the nostrils
  • Nasal congestion
  • Runny nose
  • Clear mucous
  • Slight aversion to Wind and Cold
  • Fever
  • Fullness in the Stomach
  • Poor appetite
  • Abdominal distention
  • Loose stools
  • TP: Normal
  • C: Slimy and white
  • P: Floating and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Dispel Wind
  • Transform Dampness
  • Scatter Cold
Herb Formulas
  • Huo Xiang Zheng Qi Tang  modified
Points
  • LI-4, LI-20, LU-5, LU-7

LUNG AND STOMACH HEAT

Clinical Manifestations
  • Gradual onset of nasal pain with a burning sensation
  • Pain often in the lower part of the nostrils or the septum
  • Pai exacerbated by pressure
  • Epistaxis
  • Tenderness adjacent to the nose
  • Chest pain
  • Thirst
  • Dry throat
  • Hungers easily
  • A foul smell in the mouth
  • Constipation
  • Dark colored urine
  • T: Red
  • C: Yellow
  • P: Wiry and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Clear Heat in Lungs
  • Strengthen when weak or elderly
Herb Formulas
  • Bai Hu Tang modified
  • Chinese Patent Medicine: Qing Fei Yi Huo Pian
Points
  • LI-4, LI-11, LI-20, LU-5, LU-10, ST-3, ST-40, ST-44
    With a cough and expectoration of yellow Phlegm: + LU-1, UB-13
    With a dry nose: + LU-8, SP-6
    With constipation: + ST-25, ST-37
    With a tendency to hunger easily: + REN-12
  • KI-7, LI-19, LI-20, LU-5, LU-11, ST-44

LUNG AND KIDNEY QI AND YIN DEFICIENCY

Clinical Manifestations
  • Minor nasal pain
  • Diminished or loss of the sense of smell
  • Catches cold easily
  • Frequent white nasal discharge
  • Coughing
  • Aversion to Cold
  • Shortness of breath on exertion
  • Low back pain
  • Poor memory
  • Fatigue
  • Five-Sole-Heat
  • Thirst
  • T: Red
  • C: Thin and white
  • P: Deep, thready and weak
Treatment Principle
  • Tonify Qi
  • Nourish Yin
  • Benefit the nose
Herb Formulas
  • Liu Wei Di Huang Wan  modified
  • Qing Wei San   modified
  • Chinese Patent Medicine: Liu Wei Di Huang Wan
Points
  • KI-3, LI-20, LU-5, LU-8, LU-9, SI-18, SP-6, ST-3, ST-36
    With a tendency to catch cold easily: + LU-7, UB-13
    With a diminished or no sense of smell: + DU-24, UB-2
    With general fatigue, shortness of breath on exertion and low back pain: + KI-10, UB-23
    With a dry nose and Five-Sole-Heat: + KI-2, KI-7

DAMP-HEAT IN THE SPLEEN AND STOMACH

Clinical Manifestations
  • Chronic onset of nasal pain
  • Redness of the nasal mucosa
  • Slight redness and swelling of the nose
  • A loss of the sense of smell
  • Itching in the nose
  • Yellow nasal discharge
  • Anorexia
  • Abdominal distention and discomfort
  • Suffocating sensation in the chest
  • Epigastric mass
  • Maybe abdominal pain on palpation
  • Yang jaundice (sclera and skin are bright yellow)
  • Dislike of oily foods
  • Nausea
  • Vomiting
  • Lassitude
  • Scanty, dark urine
  • Maybe thirst or thirst with no desire to drink
  • Diarrhea with foul smell or
  • Constipation
  • Fever
  • Sweet sticky taste or bitter taste in mouth
  • T: Pink or Red
  • C: Thick, yellow and greasy (maybe sticky)
  • P: Soft and rapid or Slippery and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Clear Heat
  • Benefit Dampness
  • Activate the Spleen and Stomach
Herb Formulas
  • San Ren Tang modified
  • Chinese Patent Medicine: Xiang Sha Yang Wei Wan
Points
  • LI-4, LI-20, SP-6, SP-9, ST-3, ST-34, ST-40, ST-44
    With yellow nasal discharge: + LU-5, LU-10
    With no sense of smell: + SI-18, UB-2
    With a red, swollen nose: + LI-2, LI-11
    With fullness of the abdomen, a poor appetite and loose stools: + SP-3, SP-4

LIVER/GALLBLADDER FIRE

Clinical Manifestations
  • Gradual onset of nasal pain with a burning sensation
  • Discharge of yellow, sticky Phlegm from the nose with a foul smell
  • Diminished sense of smell
  • Headache
  • A sense of distension in the head
  • Nervousness
  • Red eyes
  • A bitter taste in the mouth
  • Tinnitus
  • T: Red
  • C: Yellow
  • P: Wiry and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Clear Hear
  • Drain fire
  • Stop pain
Herb Formulas
  • Long Dan Xie Gan Tang modified
  • Chinese Patent Medicine: Long Dan Xie Gan Wan
Points
  • GB-15, GB-16, GB-20, LI-4, LI-11, LI-20, LIV-2
    With a stuffy nose: + Bitong, UB-2
    With a red, swollen nose: + SI-18, ST-3
    With a headache and nervousness: + DU-20
    With discharge of yellow Phlegm: + LU-5

BLOOD STAGNATION

Clinical Manifestations
  • Stabbing pain in the nose
    Pain worse at night
  • A purplish color to the nose
  • Swollen nose
  • Headache
  • Insomnia
  • Chest pain
  • Depression
  • T: Purplish
  • C: thin and white
  • P: Wiry or Choppy
Treatment Principle
  • Smooth the Liver
  • Circulate Qi
  • Invigorate the Blood
  • Eliminate Stasis
Herb Formulas
  • Tao Hong Si Wu Tang  modified
  • Chinese Patent Medicine: Xiao Huo Luo Dan
Points
  • LI-4, LI-20, LIV-3, LU-5, LU-7, SI-18, SP-6, UB-2, UB-17
    With a headache: + GB-20
    With restlessness and insomnia: + HT-3, UB-15
    With chest pain and depression: + LIV-14, PC-6, REN-17

TOXIC HEAT

Clinical Manifestations
  • Acute onset of nasal pain
  • Redness, Heat and swelling in the nose
  • A yellow discharge
  • Fever
  • A slight aversion to Cold
  • Headache
  • Thirst
  • Cough with expectoration of yellow Phlegm
  • Redness and pain in the throat
  • T: Red, especially the tip
  • C: Thin and yellow
  • P: Floating and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Clear Heat
  • Remove toxins
  • Stop pain
Herb Formulas
  • Wu Wei Xiao Du Yin  modified
  • Chinese Patent Medicine: Niu Huang Jie Du Pian
Points
  • DU-10, LI-4, LI-11, LI-20, LU-5, LU-7, LU-10, SP-6, ST-3, ST-44
    With a fever: + DU-14
    With a headache: + SJ-2, Taiyang
    With restlessness: + HT-3, HT-7
    For a cough with yellow Phlegm: + LU-1, ST-40
