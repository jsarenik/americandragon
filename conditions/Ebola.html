EBOLA VIRUS

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • In general sunlight kills ebola.
Treatment Principle
Herb Formulas
Points

XUE STAGE: BLOOD HEAT CAUSES BLEEDING

Clinical Manifestations
  • High fever
  • Confusion of mind
  • Delirium (mania)
  • Dark yellow urine
  • Excessive skin rashes all over body
  • Hemorrhage
    Hematemesis
    Hemoptysis
    Epistaxis
    Hemafecia
    Hematuria
  • T: Deep red, dry or Purple red
    C: None
    P: Rapid or Thready and rapid or Thready and feeble
Treatment Principle
  • Cool the Blood
  • Dispel Heat and Toxins from the Xue Stage
  • Promote Blood Circulation
Herb Formulas
  • Xi Jiao Di Huang Tang modified
Points
  • To clear Heat and Toxins: Bleed the Jing Well points, DU-14, LI-11, ST-44
  • To promote Blood circulation: SP-10, UB-17
  • To treat hemafecia: SP-1, ST-25, ST-37
  • To restore consciousness: DU-26, K-1
  • To nourish Yin: KI-3, SP-6
  • To calm the Shen: HT-7

XUE STAGE: BLOOD HEAT GENERATES WIND

Clinical Manifestations
  • High fever
  • Confusion of mind
  • Delirium
  • Mania
  • Dark yellow urine
  • Excessive skin rashes all over body
  • Hemorrhage:
    Hematemesis
    Hemoptysis
    Epistaxis
    Hemafecia
    Hematuria
  • Stiffness
  • Convulsions:  
    Clenched jaws
    Clenched fists
    Shaking
  • Opisthotonis
  • Stiff neck
  • Semi-consciousness
  • T: Deep red and dry
  • C: Yellow
  • P: Wiry and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Subdue Wind
  • Clear Heat
  • Nourish Yin
  • Stop convulsions
Herb Formulas
  • Ling Jiao Gou Teng Tang modified
Points
  • To clear Heat and Toxins: Bleed the Jing Well points, DU-14, LI-11, ST-44
  • To promote Blood circulation: SP-10, UB-17
  • To treat hemafecia: SP-1, ST-25, ST-37
  • To restore consciousness: DU-26, K-1
  • To nourish Yin: KI-3, SP-6
  • To calm the Shen: HT-7
