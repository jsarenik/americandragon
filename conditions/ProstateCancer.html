PROSTATE CANCER

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • Usually asymptomatic until later stages
  • Urinary outflow obstruction
  • Elevated prostate-specific antigen (PSA)
Treatment Principle
Herb Formulas
  • Take a liquid extract of Hb. Erodii/Geranii Lao Guan Cao daily.
Points
  • KI-3, KI-7, REN-2, REN-3, REN-4, ST-28, ST-29, ST-30, UB-25, UB-28, UB-63

KIDNEY YIN DEFICIENCY WITH EMPTY FIRE RISING

Clinical Manifestations
  • Episodic hematuria with fresh pale or bright red Blood
  • The bleeding may be mild or recurrent
  • Mild urinary pain
  • The urine may be concentrated
  • Dizziness
  • Tinnitus or deafness
  • Poor memory
  • Aching, soreness or weakness of the low back and knees
  • Heel pain
  • Spermatorrhea or
  • Premature ejaculation
  • Aching in the bones
  • Aching in the leg and heel
  • Dry mouth
  • Irritability
  • Restlessness
  • Thirst
  • Night sweats
  • Five sole Heat
  • Malar flush
  • Facial flushing
  • Low grade fever in the afternoon
  • Dry mouth and throat, maybe recurrent sore throat
  • Bone steaming fever
  • Dry stools and/or
  • Constipation
  • Heightened libido
  • Symptoms are worse in the afternoon
  • If Heart Fire also Flares:
    Insomnia
    Frequent dreams
    Palpitations
  • If Liver Yang also Rises:
    Dizziness
    Blurred vision
  • T: Red
  • C: Little or None
  • P: Thready and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Nourish Kidney Yin
  • Subdue Fire
  • Cool the Blood
  • Stop bleeding
Herb Formulas
  • Zhi Bai Di Huang Wan modified
  • Chinese patent medicines:
    Zhi Bai Di Huang Wan
    Da Bu Yin Wan
    Zhi Bai Ba Wei Wan
    Yun Nan Bai Yao (take this formula along with either of the above formulas)
Points
  • KI-1, KI-2, KI-3, KI-6, KI-9, KI-10, REN-4, SP-6, UB-23
  • KI-2, KI-3, KI-6, LU-7, REN-4, UB-23, UB-28
  • KI-6, KI-10, LIV-1, REN-3, REN-4, SP-1, SP-6, UB-23

KIDNEY QI DEFICIENCY

Clinical Manifestations
  • Difficult urination
  • No power in urination
  • Dribbling urine
  • Frequent nocturnal urination
  • Pale face
  • Lack of spirit
  • Lower back pain
  • Knee pain
  • Maybe a dull pain on urination
  • Urge to urinate when tired or after exertion
  • T: Pale
  • C: Little or none
  • P: Deep and rapid
  • For pronounced Kidney Yang Deficiency:
    Pallor
    Pale urine
    Chilliness
    T: Pale and maybe swollen
  • For pronounced Kidney Yin Deficiency:
    Scant, yellow urine
    Urgent urination
    Maybe hematuria
    Night sweats
    T: Red
    C: Little or None
    P: Thready and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Warm Yang
  • Nourish Yin
  • Nourish the Kidneys
  • Promote urination
Herb Formulas
  • Jing Gui Shen Qi Wan modified
  • Liu Wei Di Huang Wan modified
Points
  • KI-3, KI-7, REN-4, REN-6, UB-25, UB-28
    For pronounced Kidney Yang Deficiency: + moxa or moxa needle
    For pronounced Kidney Yin Deficiency: + KI-2, KI-6

QI STAGNATION

Clinical Manifestations
  • Urinary retention
  • Discomfort before urination
  • Distention and pain in the lower abdomen, relieved by urination and made worse by stress
  • Urine color is normal
  • T: Normal
  • C: White
  • P: Wiry and deep
Treatment Principle
  • Circulate Qi
Herb Formulas
Points
  • GB-34, LI-4, LIV-3, LIV-5, REN-3, REN-6, ST-36

COLD-DAMP RETENTION

Clinical Manifestations
  • Turbid, pale urine
  • Urination stops and starts
  • Terminal dribbling
  • Symptoms are better with Heat and worse with Cold
  • Dragging down sensation in the lower abdomen
  • Lower abdominal ache
  • Fatigue
  • T: Normal
  • C: Sticky or Greasy at the root
  • P: Slippery
Treatment Principle
  • Warm Cold
  • Eliminate Dampness
Herb Formulas
Points
  • Moxa or moxa needle REN-3, REN-12, SP-6, SP-9, ST-36, UB-20, UB-22, UB-25, UB-28

Clinical Manifestations
  • Low back pain
  • Lower abdominal fullness
  • Difficult urination
  • Bitter taste in the mouth
  • Thirst, does not like to drink
  • Constipation
  • T: Red
  • C: Yellow and greasy
  • P: Rolling and rapid

  • Promote urination
Herb Formulas

  • Prostate Cancer #4

Points

BLOOD STAGNATION

Clinical Manifestations
  • Sharp, stabbing rough pain during urination without burning
  • Decreased urination or
  • Interrupted urination or
  • Constant urination or
  • Anuria
  • Lower abdominal fullness
  • Fixed, stabbing lower abdominal pain which may radiate to the genitals
  • Maybe hematuria
  • Palpable masses which may or may not be painful
  • Purple urine with blood clots
  • Dark or purplish spider naevi or broken vessels on the trunk , around the inner ankle and inner knee
  • Dark, ashen, sallow or purplish facial complexion
  • Dark or purplish lips and conjunctiva
  • Dark rings under the eyes
  • T: Dark purple or with brown or purplish echymosis spots and/or dark and distended sublingual veins
  • C: Thin
  • P: Slow and choppy or Thin or Deep and choppy or Thready and choppy
Treatment Principle
  • Relieve Stasis
  • Shrink the tumor
  • Promote urination
  • Warm Yang
Herb Formulas
  • Shao Fu Zhu Yu Tang modified
  • With Damp-Heat in the Lower Jiao: Prostate Cancer #1
  • For mild cases with Yin Deficiency, Heat and Phlegm: Qian Gen San modified
  • For the elderly and other Qi deficient patients: Bu Zhong Yi Qi Tang modified
  • Chinese Patent Medicines:
    Yun Nan Bai Yao to be taken along with Qi Li San
    Yun Nan Bai Yao to be taken along with Qi Li San
    Sheng Tian Qi Pian
    Yan Hu Suo Zhi Tong Pian
    Fu Ke Wan Jin Wan
Points
  • REN-3, REN-4, SP-1, SP-6, SP-10, ST-29, UB-17, UB-28
  • LIV-3, SP-6, SP-10
