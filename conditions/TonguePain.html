TONGUE PAIN

WIND COLD

Clinical Manifestations
  • Tongue pain
  • A red, swollen tongue
  • Erosions on the tongue
  • Sore, red, swollen throat
  • Thirst with a desire to drink cold liquids
  • Itching or tickling in the throat
  • Cough with expectoration of thin, watery or frothy white sputum
  • Chills with low grade fever
  • Anhidrosis
  • No thirst
  • Headache
  • Aversion to Wind
  • Blocked and / or runny nose with clear, watery nasal discharge
  • Sneezing
  • General body aches
  • Maybe asthma
  • T: Pink
  • C: Thin, white and moist
  • P: Floating and tight
Treatment Principle
  • Expel Wind
  • Dispel Cold
  • Ventilate the Lungs
  • Resolve Phlegm
  • Alleviate tongue pain
Herb Formulas
  • Jing Fang Bai Du San  modified
Points
  • DU-15, GB-20, LI-4, LU-7 , REN-23, SJ-5
    With a red tongue and throat, and a burning sensation on the tongue: + LI-2, LI-11
    With a high fever: + DU-14
    With thirst and a dry throat and tongue: + LU-8, SP-6
    With tongue pain and spasms due to Wind-Cold: + ST-6, ST-40
  • DU-16, GB-20, LI-4 + LU-7 or LI-4 + KI-7, LU-5, UB-12, UB-13

WIND-HEAT

Clinical Manifestations
  • Tongue pain
  • A red, swollen tongue
  • Erosions on the tongue
  • Sore, red, swollen throat
  • Thirst with a desire to drink cold liquids
  • Runny or blocked nose with thick, yellow discharge
  • Fever with perspiration
  • Slight chills
  • Aversion to Wind
  • Headache
  • Maybe dyspnea with flared nostrils
  • Maybe chest pain
  • T: Red
  • C: Thin and yellow
  • P: Superficial and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Expel Wind
  • Clear Heat
  • Relieve tongue pain
Herb Formulas
  • Yin Qiao San  
  • Chinese Patent Medicine: Jing Fang Bai Du San
Points
  • DU-15, GB-20, LI-4, LU-7 , REN-23, SJ-5

PERVERSE DRYNESS

  • Tongue pain
  • A red, swollen tongue
  • Erosions on the tongue
  • Sore, red, swollen throat
  • Thirst with a desire to drink cold liquids
  • Dry cough with little or no sputum
  • Sputum is difficult to expectorate
  • Maybe hemoptysis
  • Cough may cause chest pain
  • Dry or sore throat
  • Hoarse voice
  • Dry lips and mouth
  • Thirst
  • Dryness in nose
  • Epistaxis
  • Chills and fever
  • Headache
  • Anhidrosis
  • Dry skin and hair
  • Constipation
  • T: Red tip
  • C: Thin, yellow and dry
  • P: Superficial, maybe rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Clear the Lungs
  • Moisten Dryness
  • Alleviate tongue pain
Herb Formulas
  • Sang Xing Tang
Points

LUNG HEAT

Clinical Manifestations
  • Tongue pain, especially at the tip with a burning, pricking sensation and red spots
  • A dry cough
  • Yellow sputum
  • Wheezing
  • Fever
  • Skin that feels hot to the touch
  • Symptoms worse in the late afternoon
  • Dry mouth
  • Dry nose
  • Epistaxis
  • Thirst
  • Little or difficult to expectorate sputum
  • Burning pain in the chest and hypochondrium
  • Hemoptysis
  • A warm steaming sensation from the skin that is most prominent in the late afternoon
  • Maybe constipation
  • Impatience
  • A dry, painful throat
  • Hurried respiration leading to dyspnea
  • Irritability
  • Dyspnea
  • Afternoon fever
  • Anhidrosis
  • Dizziness
  • Conjunctival congestion
  • Dry, bitter taste in the mouth
  • Dry stools
  • T: Red
  • C: Yellow
  • P: Superficial and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Clear Heat
  • Disperse Lung Qi
Herb Formulas
  • Xie Bai San
  • Chinese Patent Medicine: Qing Fei Yi Huo Pian
Points
  • LI-4, LI-11, LU-5, LU-6, LU-10, REN-23
    With epistaxis: + LI-20, LU-11
    With a cough: + LU-1, UB-13
    With expectoration of yellow Phlegm: + ST-40

LUNG YIN DEFICIENCY FIRE

Clinical Manifestations
  • Tongue pain which is worse at night
  • Thirst
  • Severe, dry hacking cough
  • No sputum expectorated
  • Small amounts of difficult to expectorate sputum
  • Blood tinged sputum (when Yin Deficiency Fire is present)
  • Sometimes epistaxis
  • Afternoon fever
  • Night sweats
  • Malar flush (especially in afternoon)
  • Five Sole Heat
  • Dry mouth and throat
  • Throat pain
  • Chest pain
  • Itching in throat
  • Hoarse voice, low voice or loss of voice
  • Emaciation
  • Dry stools
  • Restlessness
  • T: Red tip
  • C: None or Peeled
  • P: Thready and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Nourish Yin
  • Moisten and cool the Lungs
Herb Formulas

  • Chinese Patent Medicine: Liu Wei Di Huang Wan
Points
  • LU-5, LU-8. LU-10, REN-23, SP-6, UB-13

    With constipation: + LI-4

STOMACH FIRE

Clinical Manifestations
  • Whole tongue pain or
  • Pain in the middle of the tongue
  • Burning Heat and pain in the epigastrium
  • Irritating thirst: drinks copious fluids and prefers cold drinks
  • A dry mouth
  • Abdominal pain
  • Restlessness
  • Excessive appetite: hungry again quickly after eating (as if food vanishes)
  • Swelling, pain, ulceration and bleeding of gums
  • Foul breath
  • Toothache
  • Maybe vomiting right after eating
  • Vomiting acid or undigested food
  • Constipation
  • Maybe scanty, red urine
  • T: Red
  • C: Thick, yellow and dry
  • P: Slippery, big, Excess and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Clear and Purge Stomach Fire
Herb Formulas
  • Qing Wei San modified
  • Chinese Patent Medicine: Wei Te Ling
Points
  • LI-4, LI-11, ST-40, ST-42, ST-44, ST-45
    With Stomach pain: + REN-12, UB-21
    With nausea and vomiting: + PC-6, SP-4
    With constipation: + ST-25
  • LI-4, LI-11, PC-6, PC-8, ST-6, ST-34, ST-36, ST-43, ST-44, ST-45, UB-21

LIVER FIRE

Clinical Manifestations
  • Tongue pain, especially on the edges
  • Severe, splitting headaches with dizziness
  • Sudden tinnitus or
  • Sudden deafness
  • Flushed face
  • Red, swollen, painful eyes
  • Irritability
  • Nervousness
  • Irascibility with violent angry outbursts
  • Dry mouth
  • Bitter taste in mouth
  • Thirst
  • Vomiting sour or bitter liquid
  • Insomnia
  • Restlessness
  • Constipation or dry stools
  • Maybe rib pain
  • In severe cases:
    Mania
    Hemoptysis
    Hematemesis
    Epistaxis
  • T: Red tip and edges
  • C: Thick, yellow, dry and rough
  • P: Wiry, Excess and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Clear the Liver
  • Purge Fire
Herb Formulas

  • Chinese Patent Medicine: Long Dan Xie Gan Wan
Points
  • GB-40, GB-41, GB-43, LI-4, LIV-2, LIV-8
