VIRAL CARDITIS

HEART QI AND BLOOD STAGNATION

Clinical Manifestations
  • Palpitations
  • Stabbing pain in the anterior region over the Heart
  • Suffocating sensation in the chest
  • Maybe drawing pain down the internal aspect of arm (especially the left arm)
  • Pain (in chest and/or arm) tends to be severe and intermittent
  • Severe cases:
    Cold limbs
    Cyanosis of lips and nails
  • T: Dark red with purple spots or Purple body
  • C: Little
  • P: Minute (scarcely perceptible) and thready or Knotted
Treatment Principle
  • Clear Yang
  • Invigorate the Blood
  • Transform Stagnation
Herb Formulas
  • Xue Fu Zhu Yu Tang
Points
  • HT-5, HT-6, HT-7, PC-3, PC-4, PC-5, PC-6, PC-7, REN-14, REN-17, UB-15, UB-17

HEART YIN AND YANG DEFICIENCIES WITH BLOOD STAGNATION

Clinical Manifestations
  • Palpitations
  • Shortness of breath (especially on exertion)
  • Excessive perspiration (in daytime)
  • Symptoms worse after exertion or when tired
  • Facial pallor
  • Fatigue, weakness
  • Suffocating sensation in chest
  • Irregular Heart beat
  • Cold limbs
  • Maybe chills
  • Cyanosis
  • Irritability
  • Easily startled
  • Insomnia
  • Much dreaming
  • Poor memory
  • Five sole heat
  • Low grade fever
  • Night sweats
  • Restlessness
  • Dry mouth
  • Thirst
  • Dry throat
  • Malar flush
  • Suffocating sensation in chest
  • Depression
  • Facial pallor
  • Maybe stabbing pain in the anterior region over heart
  • Maybe drawing pain down internal aspect of arm (especially left arm)
  • Maybe pain (in chest and/or arm)
  • T: Pale and swollen or Red, and dry
  • C: White or Little or None
  • P: Weak or Big and weak or Intermittent or Thready and weak or Thready and rapid or Knotted
Treatment Principle
  • Tonify and nourish Heart Yin and Yang
  • Invigorate the Blood
  • Dispel Stagnation
Herb Formulas
  • Yi Qi Huo Xue Fa
Points

HEART FIRE WITH BLOOD AND YIN DEFICIENCY

Clinical Manifestations
  • Insomnia
  • Continuous palpitations
  • A sensation of Heat and irritability in the chest
  • A desire to vomit without result
  • Dream-disturbed sleep
  • Anxiety
  • Tongue ulcers
  • Fidgeting
  • Palpitations
  • Maybe a rancorous disposition
  • Irritability
  • Fear and fright
  • Night sweats
  • Shortness of breath
  • Nausea
  • T: Red or Red tip
  • C: Normal
  • P: Thin and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Sedate the Heart
  • Calm the Shen
  • Drain Fire
  • Nourish Yin
  • Relieve palpitations
Herb Formulas
  • Zhu Sha An Shen Wan
