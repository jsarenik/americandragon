SICK SINUS SYNDROME

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • Sick sinus syndrome ( SSS ), also known as sinus node disease or sinus node dysfunction, is a relatively uncommon heart rhythm disorder. SSS is not a specific disease, but rather a group of signs or symptoms that indicate the sinus node, the heart's natural pacemaker, is not functioning properly.
  • When they occur, sick sinus syndrome signs and symptoms might include:
    • Slower than normal pulse (bradycardia)
    • Fatigue
    • Dizziness or Light-headedness
    • Fainting or near fainting
    • Shortness of breath
    • Chest pains
    • Confusion
    • A sensation of rapid, fluttering heartbeats

  • Vomits worms
  • Persistent thirst

HEART QI DEFICIENCY

Clinical Manifestations
  • Palpitations
  • Shortness of breath (especially on exertion)
  • Disinclination to talk
  • Spontaneous perspiration
  • Excessive perspiration (in daytime)
  • Symptoms worse after exertion or when tired
  • Facial pallor
  • Fatigue
  • General debility
  • Weakness
  • Suffocating sensation in chest
  • T: Pale and swollen
  • C: White
  • P: Weak or Big and weak or Intermittent or Deep, slow and weak
  • With Blood Stagnation:
    Stabbing pain in the chest
    T: Dark red with echymosis
    P: Deep, slow and hesitant
Treatment Principle
  • Reinforce and Strengthen Heart Qi
  • Warm Yang to invigorate the Blood

    Bai Zi Yang Xin Wan

BLOOD AND YANG DEFICIENCY WITH COLD

Clinical Manifestations
  • Localized painful swellings without a head that bleed into the surrounding tissues and do not affect the color or texture of the skin
  • The swellings are not hot to the touch
  • No thirst
  • Swollen and painful joints
  • Wasting of the muscles of the thigh and calf
  • T: Very pale or Pale-pink
  • C: White
  • P: Thready and forceless or Deep and slow
Treatment Principle
  • Warm the Yang
  • Tonify the Blood
  • Disperse Cold
  • Eliminate Stagnation
Herb Formulas
