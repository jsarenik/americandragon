CANCER

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
Treatment Principle
Herb Formulas
  • For various cancers:
    1. Take 15g Hb. Cirsii Xiao Ji in decoction every day.
    2. Decoct Fr. Arctii Niu Bang Zi in 1 liter of water, divide into 3 parts and take in the morning, evening and at night.
    3. Make a jam of the sarcocarp of Fr. Mume Wu Me and take a small dose every day for a long time.
    4. Decoct 30g Hb. Salviae Chinensis Shi Jian Chuan and 30g Hb. Scutellariae Barbatae Ban Zhi Lian and drink frequently.
    5. Soak 6-9g Hb. Elephantopus Scaber Ding Shu Xiu  in water and take 3 times daily.
    6. Decoct equal portions of Flos Trifolii San Xiao Cao, Hb. Violae Zi Hua Di Ding and Rx. Rumicis Yang Ti and take daily.
    7. Decoct 9g Fr. Xanthii Cang Er Zi (or the leaf or stalk) three times in water and take 3 times a day.
  • For various cancers with severe pain: Cancer #14
  • For cancer of the abdominal cavity:
    1. Decoct 5kg of Rz. Sparganii San Leng then further boil the broth into a thick syrup. Take 4g twice daily.

LIVER QI STAGNATION

Clinical Manifestations
  • Local distention and pain
  • Soft masses
  • Hypochondriac pain and distention
  • Pain worse with emotional upset and great stress
  • Headache
  • Distention and pain in the sides or flanks
  • Suffocating sensation in the chest
  • Tendency to sigh
  • Melancholy
  • Depression
  • Irritability
  • Nervousness
  • Inappropriate anger
  • Irregular menses
  • Dysmenorrhea
  • Breast distention and pain before and during menses
  • T: Pink
  • C: Thin and white or thin and yellow
  • P: Wiry or Wiry and tight
Treatment Principle
  • Soothe the Liver
  • Regulate Qi
  • Clear Stagnation
  • Sedate pain
Herb Formulas
  • For pain: Xiao Yao San  modified
  • Chinese Patent Medicines for pain:
    Xiao Yao Wan
    Shu Gan Wan
Points
  • GB-20, LIV-3, LIV-5, LIV-14, PC-6, REN-17
    With Qi Stagnation in the Stomach and esophagus: + REN-12, ST-21
    With abdominal pain: + ST-25, ST-28
    With coughing and chest pain: + LU-5, UB-13
    With pain in the groin or perineum: + LIV-11, LIV-12
    With irritability and nervousness: + DU-20, LIV-2
    With insomnia and restlessness: + HT-3, HT-8
    With a poor appetite, belching and nausea: + SP-4, ST-36, ST-40
    Chinese Patent Medicines: LI-4, SJ-6
  • GB-40, GB-41, LIV-2, LIV-3, LIV-4, LIV-5, LIV-13, LIV-14, PC-6, SJ-6, SP-6, UB-18

KIDNEY YIN AND YANG DEFICIENCY

Clinical Manifestations
  • Lumbago: low back feels cold, weak and aching
  • Feeling chilled
  • Aversion to Cold
  • Cold limbs
  • General debility
  • Pale face
  • Urinary frequency with abundant, clear urine or
  • Oliguria with edema
  • Maybe nocturia
  • Lassitude
  • Withdrawn, subdued behavior
  • Knees feel cold and aching
  • Dizziness
  • Tinnitus
  • Impotence
  • Spermatorrhea
  • Premature ejaculation
  • Low libido
  • Female infertility ("uterus cold and infertile")
  • Maybe anorexia
  • Maybe loose stools
  • Maybe chronic diarrhea or "cock crow" diarrhea
  • T: Pale and swollen
  • C: Thin and white
  • P: Deep and thready - maybe slow
Treatment Principle
  • Warm and Reinforce Kidney Yang
Herb Formulas
  • He Che Da Zao Wan
Points
  • Moxa or moxa needle DU-4, KI-3, REN-4, UB-23
    For chronic diarrhea, rectal prolapse and Vanquished Yang: + moxa DU-20, REN-8
  • For Kidney Yang Deficiency impotence: moxa or moxa needle DU-4, DU-20, KI-3, REN-4, UB-23
  • For Kidney Yang Deficiency (Cock Crow) Diarrhea: moxa or moxa needle DU-20, KI-3, REN-4, UB-23

BLOOD STAGNATION

Clinical Manifestations
  • Pain that is fixed, stabbing and /or severe
  • Pain is worse with pressure
  • Pain is worse at night
  • Pain is worse with immobility
  • Pain is better with activity
  • Dark complexion
  • Fixed, palpable masses
  • Bleeding with clots
  • Bleeding with purple Blood
  • Purple lips
  • Purple nails
  • A dark complexion
  • Dry skin
  • Blue, dark or purplish discoloration of skin in small or large areas
  • Varicose and spider veins
  • Cold hands and feet
  • Tendency to hemorrhage dark blood or clots
  • T: Purple, possible purple or red spots
    Red purple if there is Heat
    Blue purple if there is Cold
  • C: White
  • P: Choppy or Wiry and choppy
Treatment Principle
  • Invigorate the Blood
  • Break up Stagnation
Herb Formulas
  • For pain:
    Upper Jiao: Xue Fu Zhu Yu Tang
    Middle Jiao: Ge Xia Zhu Yu Tang
    Lower Jiao: Shao Fu Zhu Yu Tang
  • Chinese Patent Medicine for pain: Bie Jia Jian Wan
Points
  • LI-4, LIV-3, LIV-5, PC-6, SP-6, SP-10, UB-17
    With severe pain: + LIV-6

    With constipation: + ST-25, ST-40

TOXIC FIRE

Clinical Manifestations
  • Acute or subacute pain in the body
  • Pain is usually fixed at one place but can also be throughout the whole body
  • Masses formed on the skin or
  • Internal masses
  • Erosion and necrosis of the masses with a foul smell
  • Discharge of sticky, yellow liquid from the masses
  • High fever
  • Restlessness
  • Thirst
  • A bitter taste in the mouth
  • Deep yellow urine
  • Constipation
  • T: Red
  • C: Yellow
  • P: Wiry, slippery and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Cool Heat
  • Remove toxicity
  • Reduce Fire
  • Sedate the pain
Herb Formulas
  • For pain: Gan Lu Xiao Du Dan  modified
  • Chinese Patent Medicine for pain: Gan Lu Xiao Du Dan
