LUNG CANCER, PULMONARY CARCINOMA

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • Coughing
  • Bloody sputum
  • Chest pain
  • A choking sensation over the chest
  • May be signs of infection
  • If the tumor invades the pleura: Dyspnea
  • If the tumor invades the phrenic nerve:
    Horner's syndrome (ptosis miosis)
    Enopthalmos
    Ipsolateral decreased sweating on the involved side
  • If the tumor compresses the superior vena cava:
    Swelling of the face, neck and upper chest
    Engorgement of the vein
Treatment Principle
Herb Formulas
  • Take 1.5g powdered Rz. Dysosmae Pleianthae Ba Jiao Lian daily.
  • Take 60ml water solution of Rx. Wikstroemiae Pu Yin daily.
  • Take 10-30ml garlic juice Da Suan twice daily.
  • Take a decoction of 3g Rx. Scutellariae Barbatae Ban Zhi Lian and Hb. Solani Lyrati Shu Yang Quan
  • Take 15g Hb. Hedyotis Diffusae Bai Hua She She Cao and 15g Hb. Scutellariae Barbatae Ban Zhi Lian as a tea.
  • Decoct 60g Hb. Scutellariae Barbatae Ban Zhi Lian and 60g Hb. Hedyotis Diffusae Bai Hua She She Cao and take daily.
  • Take a liquid extract of Hb. Erodii/Geranii Lao Guan Cao daily.
  • Take a decoction of 60g Hb. Selanginellae Juan Bai and 30g Hb. Hedyotis Diffusae Bai Hua She She Cao  once daily.
  • For the prevention and treatment of tumors:
    Lung Cancer #46
    Lung Cancer #47
  • Initial stage:
    To dissipate limbs and dissolve Phlegm: Jie Geng Tang
    For dyspnea and mild fever: Jia Wei Xiao Yao San
    For a constricted esophagus and obstruction of food in the throat: Kai Guan Li Ge Fang
  • For epistaxis: Cancer #2
  • For hemoptysis: Grind equal portions of charred Fr. Zizyphi Jujube Da Zao and tea-fermented Galla Chinensis Wu Bei Zi into a powder and take 10g each time with rice porridge.
  • For malignant hydatidiform mole metastasized to the Lung: Hong Sheng Dan
  • For toxic Wind-Damp-Heat with edema of the neck: Si Shun Qing Lian Yin
  • For pulmonary edema or ascites: Decoct 120g dried Hb. Solani Nigri Long Kui in water and take once daily.
  • For tension and resistance under the ribs, trypanosomiasis with severe chest pain and muscular hardness from the ribs to the umbilicus: Chai Hu Shu Gan Tang
  • For malignant tumors: Zi Gen Mu Li Tang
  • For a secondary pleural tumor: Decoct 50g Hb. Scutellariae Barbatae Ban Zhi Lian and 50g Rz. Typhonii Preparatum Zhi Bai Fu Zi in water and take daily as a tea.
