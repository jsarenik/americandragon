BLOOD DEFICIENCY HEAT

Clinical Manifestations
  • Threatened miscarriage early in pregnancy
  • Mild menorrhagia that suddenly transforms into copious menorrhagia
  • Clear, light red, and thick blood or
  • The blood is bright red,
  • A general feeling of heat
  • Thirst
  • Evening fever
  • Hot palms and soles
  • Restlessness
  • Insomnia
  • Dark yellow urine
  • T: Red
  • C: Yellow
  • P: Rapid and overflowing or Thready and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Cool the Blood
  • Clear Heat
  • Astringe Yin
  • Nourish Yin
  • Strengthen the Kidneys
  • Regulate menstruation
Herb Formulas
  • Bao Yin Jian

BLOOD STAGTNATION

Clinical Manifestations
  • Firmness and distention in the lower abdomen which is painful and gives resistance on pressure
  • Cold pain in the lower abdomen
  • Smooth urination
  • Manic behavior
  • Forgetfulness
  • Black stools that are easy to expel
  • Lumps in the lower abdomen which refuse pressure
  • Cold appearance
  • Vomiting
  • Dyspepsia
  • Delayed menstruation
  • Amenorrhea
  • Irregular menstruation
  • Coagulated Blood
  • Thirst
  • Dry mouth
  • Polyuria
  • Liver spots indicating stagnant blood found on the face, lips, tongue, limbs and finger nails
  • T: Purplish
  • C: Normal
  • P: Deep, slow and irregular
Treatment Principle
  • Break up and dispel Blood Stasis
Herb Formulas
  • Di Dang Tang
  • Sheng Hua Tang
  • Gui Zhi Fu Ling Wan
  • Shi Xiao San
  • With Qi Stagnation: Hua Ren Shi Tang (no information)
Points
  • LIV-3, REN-4, SP-6, SP-10, ST-28, UB-34

  • C: White
  • P: Deep, tight and slow

  • Expel Cold
  • Invigorate the Blood
Herb Formulas
  • Qi Zhen San
