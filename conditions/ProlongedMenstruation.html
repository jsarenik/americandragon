SPLEEN QI DEFICIENCY - SPLEEN NOT GOVERNING BLOOD

Clinical Manifestations
  • Sallow or pale complexion
  • Anorexia
  • Four limbs feel tired and heavy
  • Physical and mental exhaustion
  • Abdominal and epigastric distention after eating
  • Maybe acid regurgitation or vomiting
  • Loose or unformed stools
  • Shortness of breath
  • Dizziness
  • Palpitations
  • Sallow complexion
  • Fatigue
  • Shortness of breath
  • T: Pale and flabby - may have teethmarks
  • C: White
  • P: Even, forceless and Deficiency
Treatment Principle
  • Nourish Qi
  • Stop Bleeding
Herb Formulas
  • Gui Pi Tang
  • Bu Zhong Yi Qi Tang modified
  • Gui Pi Tang + Jiao Ai Tang
  • Ju Yuan Jian  modified
Points
  • DU-20, LI-11, REN-4, SP-1, SP-3, SP-4, SP-10, ST-36, UB-1, UB-20
  • DU-20, KI-3, REN-6, SP-1, SP-6, SP-8, ST-36, Tituo, UB-20

YIN DEFICIENCY HEAT IN THE BLOOD

Clinical Manifestations
  • Early menstrual period (short cycle)
  • Discharge of a small amount of thick, bright red Blood
  • Heavy flow
  • Mid cycle bleeding (spotting)
  • Bloody vaginal discharge
  • Discharge begins with scant light-colored Blood
  • Dizziness
  • Blurred vision
  • Hectic fever
  • Flushed cheeks
  • Sore throat
  • Tinnitus
  • Heartburn
  • Thirst
  • Five sole Heat
  • Night sweats
  • Lack of skin luster
  • Weak and sore lower back and knees
  • Weakness and atrophy of the legs
  • Dry, parched mouth and throat
  • Dizziness
  • Blurred vision
  • Insomnia
  • Dream disturbed sleep
  • Prematurely gray hair
  • Premature hair loss
  • A bitter taste in the mouth
  • Hypermenorrhea
  • T: Red or Crimson or Red and dry
  • C: Little or None
  • P: Thready and rapid or Deep, small and weak
Treatment Principle
  • Nourish Yin
  • Clear Heat
  • Stop bleeding
Herb Formulas
  • Liang Di Tang + Er Zhi Wan
Points
  • KI-3, KI- 6, REN-3, SP-8, SP-10
    With hot flashes: + HT-6, KI-7
    WIth Dampness: + SP-9, ST-40

BLOOD STAGNATION WITH BLOOD DEFICIENCY

Clinical Manifestations
  • Copious bleeding of dark purple blood
  • The blood is sticky during menstruation
  • May or may not have clots
  • Menstrual cycle is shortened (early menstruation)
  • Palpitations
  • Dizziness
  • Blurred vision
  • Pale, lusterless complexion
  • Pale nails
  • Abdominal pain
  • Irregular menstruation with little flow
  • Fixed lower abdominal pain
  • Vertigo
  • Tinnitus
  • Pale lips
  • May be amenorrhea
  • Insomnia
  • Menstruation may also be accompanied by distention and pain
  • T: Pale
  • C: Normal
  • P: Thin and weak or Thin and choppy
Treatment Principle
  • Tonify and Invigorate the Blood
  • Regulate Blood Circulation
  • Break up Stagnation
  • Eliminate Blood Stagnation
  • Regulate menstruation
  • Alleviate pain
Herb Formulas
  • Tao Hong Si Wu Tang
  • Zong Pu San
  • WIth abdominal pain: + Shi Xiao San
Points
  • LIV-3, LIV-13, LIV-14, SP-6, UB-17
  • REN-3, SP-6, SP-10, ST-29, Zigong

BLOOD HEAT DUE TO HEAT IN THE CHONG AND REN

Clinical Manifestations
  • Continuous menstruation or
  • Uterine bleeding that alternates between spotting and gushing
  • The Blood is very red, thick and sticky and may contain dark-purple clots
  • A sensation of Heat and irritability in the chest
  • Menstruation may be delayed
  • Dry mouth
  • Leukorrhea
  • Dysuria
  • Abdominal pain
  • Dark urine
  • Red urine
  • Red face
  • Difficulty defecating
  • T: Red
  • C: Normal or Yellow
  • P: Wiry or Wiry and rapid or Tight and rapid or Slippery and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Enrich Yin
  • Clear Heat
  • Clear Deficiency Heat
  • Stop bleeding
  • Stabilize the menses
Herb Formulas
  • Gu Jing Wan
Points

DAMP-HEAT WITH HEAT IN THE BLOOD

Clinical Manifestations
  • Pain in low back and knees
  • Weakness or atrophy of lower extremities
  • Scanty yellow urine
  • Red, hot, swollen and painful feet or knees
  • Edema in lower legs
  • Eczema
  • Painful and swollen feet
  • Pain and swelling of the joints
  • Leukorrhea (thick, yellow and foul-smelling)
  • Sores on lower extremities due to Damp
  • Severe numbness and weakness in lower extremities
  • T: Normal
  • C: Yellow and greasy
  • P: Soft and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Eliminates Heat
  • Resolves Dampness
  • Cool the Blood
Herb Formulas
  • Si Miao Wan modified
Points
  • KI-3, KI- 6, REN-3, SP-8, SP-9, SP-10, ST-40
    With hot flashes: + HT-6, KI-7
