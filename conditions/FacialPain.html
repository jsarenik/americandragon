WIND-COLD

Clinical Manifestations
  • A sharp pain on the face with spasming
  • Pain worse on exposure to Cold
  • Pain is alleviated by warmth
  • Purplish or slightly blue facial complexion
  • Itching or tickling in the throat
  • Cough with expectoration of thin, watery or frothy white sputum
  • Chills with low grade fever
  • Anhidrosis
  • No thirst
  • Headache
  • Aversion to Wind
  • Blocked and / or runny nose with clear, watery nasal discharge
  • Sneezing
  • General body aches
  • Maybe asthma
  • T: Pink
  • C: Thin, white and moist
  • P: Floating and tight
Treatment Principle
  • Expel Wind
  • Dispel Cold
  • Unblock the channels
  • Sedate pain
Herb Formulas
  • Chuan Xiong Cha Tiao San  modified
  • Chinese Patent Medicine: Chuan Xiong Cha Tiao Wan
Points
  • Moxa or moxa needle GB-20, LI-4, LU-7, SI-18, SJ-5, ST-7
    With a runny nose with white discharge and sneezing: +UB-12, UB-13
    With a headache: + DU-16
    With a an aversion to Cold and cold extremities: + ST-36
    With facial spasms: + LIV-3, SP-3
  • DU-16, GB-20, LI-4 + LU-7 or LI-4 + KI-7, LU-5, UB-12, UB-13

WIND-COLD WITH PHLEGM

Clinical Manifestations

  • Pain worse on exposure to Cold
  • Pain is alleviated by warmth
  • Pallor when the pain occurs
  • Facial swelling
  • A sensation of something tight wrapped around the head
  • Heavy headedness or head distention
  • T: Swollen and pale
  • C: Thick ans greasy
  • P: Soft and slippery
Treatment Principle

  • Unblock the channels
  • Resolve Phlegm
  • Sedate pain

    With pain at the temples: + GB-3, Taiyang
    With pain in the nose: + Bitong, DU-25, LI-20
    With cheek pain: + SI-18, ST-3, ST-4, ST-6
    With pain around the ear: + GB-2, SI-19. SJ-17, SJ-20, SJ-21
    With pain around the lips: + DU-24, DU-26, ST-4
    With chin pain: + REN-23, REN-24, ST-5

WIND-HEAT

Clinical Manifestations
  • Facial pain with a burning or prickling sensation or a sharp pain
  • Constant pain
  • Red face
  • Pain is aggravated by exposure to warmth
  • Pain is alleviated by cold
  • Cough with thick, sticky, yellow sputum
  • Sore, red, swollen throat
  • Thirst with a desire to drink cold liquids
  • Runny or blocked nose with thick, yellow discharge
  • Fever with perspiration
  • Slight chills
  • Aversion to Wind
  • Headache
  • Maybe dyspnea with flared nostrils
  • Maybe chest pain
  • Deep, yellow urine
  • T: Red tip
  • C: Thin and yellow
  • P: Superficial and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Expel Wind
  • Clear Heat
  • Sedate the pain
Herb Formulas
  • Xiong Zhi Shi Gao Tang  modified
  • Qing Shang Juan Tong Tang
  • Chinese Patent Medicine: Yin Qiao Pian
Points
  • GB-20, LI-4, LI-11, SJ-5, ST-6, ST-7

LIVER FIRE

Clinical Manifestations
  • A painful face with a burning feeling
  • Pain aggravated by emotional upset and nervousness
  • Pain aggravated by Heat
  • Severe, splitting headaches with dizziness
  • Sudden tinnitus or
  • Sudden deafness
  • Flushed face
  • Red, swollen, painful eyes
  • Irascibility with violent angry outbursts
  • Dry mouth and throat
  • Bitter taste in mouth
  • Thirst
  • Fullness of the chest
  • Vomiting sour or bitter liquid
  • Insomnia
  • Restlessness
  • Irritability
  • Constipation or dry stools
  • Lateral costal distention
  • Frequent sighing
  • Maybe rib pain
  • Maybe Five-Sole Heat
  • Dark colored urine
  • In severe cases:
    Mania
    Hemoptysis
    Hematemesis
  • T: Red tip and edges or Red
  • C: Yellow or Thick, yellow, dry and rough
  • P: Wiry, Excess and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Clear the Liver
  • Purge Fire
Herb Formulas
  • Long Dan Xie Gan Tang  modified
  • Mian Fang Tang San Hao
  • Chinese Patent Medicine: Long Dan Xie Gan Wan
Points

    With restlessness, palpitations and insomnia: + HT-3, PC-8
    With a bitter taste in the mouth with vomiting: + GB-34, PC-6

WIND-PHLEGM IN THE CHANNELS

Clinical Manifestations
  • Intermittent facial pain
  • Spasms of the facial muscles
  • Mostly on one side of the face
  • Facial tics
  • Pain is aggravated by emotions, especially anger
  • Numbness of the face
  • T: Normal
  • C: Thin, white and greasy
  • P: Wiry and slippery
Treatment Principle
  • Suppress Wind
  • Resolve Phlegm
  • Sedate the pain
Herb Formulas
  • Qian Zheng San  modified
Points
  • GB-3, GB-20, LI-4, LIV-3, LIV-5, LIV-6, SP-6, ST-40
    With a tic at the corner of the mouth: + ST-3, ST-4
    With a tic at the corner of the eye: + GB-14
    With aversion to Cold on the face: + moxa the face, moxa or moxa needle ST-36
    With depression, nervousness, irritability, and emotional instability: + UB-15, UB-17, UB-18
