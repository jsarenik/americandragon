DYSURIA DURING PREGNANCY

KIDNEY YANG DEFICIENCY

Clinical Manifestations
  • Painful or difficult urination
  • Anuria or
  • Frequent scanty urination
  • Pallor
  • Palpitations
  • Shortness of breath
  • Dizziness
  • Pain and weakness of the back and legs
  • Loose stools
  • T: Pale
  • C: Thin and white
  • P: Deep and slow or Slippery and feeble
  • General malaise
  • T: Pale
  • C: Thin and white
  • P: Weak, thready and slippery
Treatment Principle
  • Tonify the Kidneys
  • Warm Yang
  • Promote circulation of Qi
Herb Formulas
  • Jin Gui Shen Qi Wan
Points
  • KI-3, KI-7, UB-22, UB-23, UB-25

QI STAGNATION

Clinical Manifestations
  • Sudden dysuria upon conception through the last 7-8 months
  • Distention and acute pain in the lower abdomen
  • Heartburn
  • A stuffy sensation in the chest
  • T: Dark
  • C: Thin and sticky
  • P: Deep and wiry
Treatment Principle
  • Remove Stagnation
  • Move Qi
Herb Formulas
  • Fen Qi Yin (No Information)
Points
  • LIV-3, LIV-5, SP-9, UB-28, UB-32

QI DEFICIENCY

Clinical Manifestations
  • Painful or difficult urination
  • Anuria or
  • Frequent scanty urination
  • Pallor
  • Palpitations
  • Shortness of breath
  • Dizziness
  • General malaise
  • T: Pale
  • C: Thin and white
  • P: Weak, thready and slippery
Treatment Principle
  • Tonify Qi
  • Raise prolapse
Herb Formulas
  • Bu Zhong Yi Qi Tang
  • With Blood Deficiency:
    Ba Zhen Tang
Points
  • UB-13, UB-20, UB-21, UB-23, UB-28

DAMP-HEAT

Clinical Manifestations
  • Painful, dark, short urination
  • Abdominal distention
  • Heartburn
  • Dizziness
  • Heaviness of the head
  • A bitter taste in the mouth
  • Constipation or
  • Obstructed diarrhea
  • T: Red
  • C: Thin, yellow and sticky
  • P: Slippery
Treatment Principle
  • Clear Heat
  • Eliminate Dampness
Herb Formulas
  • Dang Gui Bei Mu Ku Shen Wan
  • Wu Lin San modified
  • Kui Zi San (No Information)
  • Quan Shen Fu Ling San (No Information)
Points
  • KI-7, LIV-2, LU-7, SP-9, UB-28, UB-32
