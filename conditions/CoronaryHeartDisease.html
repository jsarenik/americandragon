CORONARY HEART (ARTERY) DISEASE

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • It may feel heavy or like someone is squeezing the heart. It is felt under the breast bone (sternum), but also in the neck, arms, stomach, or upper back
  • The pain usually occurs with activity or emotion, and goes away with rest or a medicine called nitroglycerin
  • Other symptoms include shortness of breath and fatigue with activity (exertion)
  • Women, elderly people, and people with diabetes are more likely to have symptoms other than chest pain, such as:
  • Fatigue
  • Shortness of breath
  • Weakness
Treatment Principle
Herb Formulas
  • Ma Zi Ren Wan
  • For abdominal pain and cramping and muscle spasms in the calves: Shao Yao Gan Cao Tang
  • For the elderly with Qi, Blood, Yin, and Yang Deficiency with Blood stagnation: Jin Shen Tang
