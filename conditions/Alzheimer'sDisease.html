ALZHEIMER'S DISEASE

LUNG AND KIDNEY QI DEFICIENCY

Clinical Manifestations
  • Chronic cough with sparse sputum
  • Sputum difficult to expectorate
  • Shortness of breath
  • Spontaneous perspiration
  • Dry mouth and tongue
  • Palpitations with a stifling sensation in chest
  • Fatigue
  • Irritability
  • Thirst
  • Dry throat
  • Insomnia
  • Listless speech
  • T: Red and dry or Pink and dry
  • C: Thin and dry
  • P: Deficient and rapid or Irregular and slow or Thready and deficient or Consistently irregular
Treatment Principle
  • Augment Qi
  • Clear Heat
Herb Formulas
  • Sheng Mai San
Points

SPLEEN DEFICIENCY WITH PHLEGM STASIS

Clinical Manifestations
  • Memory loss
  • Sallow complexion
  • Fatigue
  • Anorexia
  • Four limbs feel tired and heavy
  • Abdominal and epigastric distention after eating
  • Maybe acid regurgitation or vomiting
  • Loose stools
  • Shortness of breath
  • T: Pale and flabby - may have teethmarks
  • C: White
  • P: Even, forceless and Deficient
Treatment Principle
  • Nourish Qi
  • Strengthen the Spleen
  • Resolve Phlegm
  • Induce resuscitation
Herb Formulas
  • Ban Xia Xie Xin Tang modified
Points
  • Group 1: DU-15, PC-8, ST-36, UB-23
  • Group 2: DU-14, REN-15, SP-6, KI-1
  • Group 3: DU-15,, Shixuan, LI-10, KI-3
    Use the three groups alternately day for 1 minutes for three days. Rest 5-7 days. Repeat
    Use ginger and 3-5 moxa cones every other day on the above points for ten treatments. Rest 5 days and repeat as needed.
  • Moxibustion: Use ginger and 3-5 moxa cones every other day on the above points for ten treatments. Rest 5 days and repeat as needed.

SPLEEN AND KIDNEY DEFICIENCY

Clinical Manifestations
  • Memory loss
  • Body cold
  • Limbs icy
  • Complexion white and shiny
  • Low back, knees and abdomen feel cold and painful
