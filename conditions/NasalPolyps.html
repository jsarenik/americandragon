NASAL POLYPS

WIND-ATTACKS THE LUNGS

Clinical Manifestations
  • Headache due to Wind attack
  • Headache in any part of the head
  • Fever and chills
  • Dizziness
  • Nasal congestion
  • Stuffy nose
  • Clear or white nasal discharge
  • Sneezing
  • Body aches
  • Nasal obstruction
  • Vertigo
  • Aversion to Cold
  • Copious sputum
  • T: Normal
  • C: Thin and white
  • P: Superficial
Treatment Principle
  • Dispel Wind
  • Alleviate pain
  • Eliminate polyps
Herb Formulas
  • Chuan Xiong Cha Tiao San modified
Points
  • DU-23, Yintang, LI-20, ST-36
  • LI-19, LI-20, KI-3, LU-7, DU-14, DU-15, DU-16, DU-24, DU-25, DU-26, DU-28
  • LI-19, LI-20 through to Bitong, DU-22

WIND-DAMP INVADES THE LUNGS WITH SPLEEN QI DEFICIENCY

Clinical Manifestations
  • Aversion to Wind and Cold
  • Facial edema moving to generalized edema
  • Slight fever or fever and chills
  • Thirst
  • Slight but continuous perspiration
  • Sore, heavy limbs
  • Decreased urination
  • Headache
  • Edematous orbits
  • A red, swollen sore throat
  • Weak abdomen
  • Belching
  • Cough
  • Asthma
  • Dyspnea
  • Bulging eyes
  • Severe cough
  • Weak, aching feet
  • Skin problems
  • T: Normal
  • C: White or Thin white
  • P: Floating or Floating and big
Treatment Principle
  • Promote perspiration
  • Disseminate Lung Qi
  • Clear Heat
  • Move water
  • Suppress asthma
Herb Formulas
  • Yue Bi Jia Zhu Tang

PHLEGM-HEAT ATTACKS THE LUNGS

Clinical Manifestations
  • Down-hanging growths in the nose
  • Purple in color
  • Slightly hard on palpation
  • Difficulty breathing
  • The root of the nose gradually enlarges
  • Thin yellow nasal discharge
  • Cough with expectoration of copious thick, purulent, yellow-green sputum or blood-flecked, foul-smelling pus
  • Dyspnea
  • Asthma
  • Pain in the ribs
  • Maybe chills and fever
  • T: Scarlet
  • C: Yellow and smooth
  • P: Slippery and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Clear Heat
  • Counteract Toxin
  • Transform Stasis
  • Drain Pus
  • Resolve Phlegm. (The use of Clear Heat / Counteract Toxin herbs and the principle of "Draining Pus" are only relevant when foul-smelling pus is being expectorated such as in pulmonary abscess).
  • Soothe asthma in case of asthma
  • Eliminate polyps
Herb Formulas
  • Xin Yi Qing Fei Yin
Points
  • DU-23, Yintang, LI-20, ST-36
  • LI-19, LI-20, KI-3, LU-7, DU-14, DU-15, DU-16, DU-24, DU-25, DU-26, DU-28
  • LI-19, LI-20 through to Bitong, DU-22
  • For Phlegm-Heat: LU-1, LU-5, LU-6, LU-10, UB-13, ST-40, REN-17, REN-22

LUNG AND SPLEEN QI DEFICIENCY

Clinical Manifestations
  • Recurrent episodes of relatively severe nasal congestion or
  • Persistent runny nose with thin watery or sticky white sputum
  • Nasal mucosa swollen and pale or ashen
  • Nasal polyps
  • Children may have upturned noses from frequent wiping upwards with the palm of the hand
  • Nasal itch
  • Sneezing
  • Reduction or loss of sense of smell
  • Fullness and heaviness in the head
  • Wooly headaches
  • Fatigue
  • Listlessness
  • Aversion to Cold
  • Tired limbs
  • Poor appetite
  • Picky eater
  • Loose stools or diarrhea
  • T: pale or Pale and swollen with tooth marks
  • C: White
  • P: Soft and weak
Treatment Principle
  • Strengthen the Spleen
  • Tonify Qi
  • Tonify the Lungs to consolidate Qi
Herb Formulas
  •  Liu Jun Zi Tang modified
  • For children with perennial rhinitis: Shen Ling Bai Zhu San modified
  • Patent Medicines:
      Xiang Sha Liu Jun Zi Wan
      Bu Zhong Yi Qi Wan
      Bi Min Gan Wan (Pe Min Kan Wan)
Points
  • ST-40, SP-3, LU-7, UB-13, UB-20, GB-20, LI-4, LI-19, LI-20, DU-23
  • For patients with atopic asthma: + UB-23, DU-4 or REN-4
  • For chronic cases moxa three or four of the following: Yintang, REN-4, REN-6, REN-12, DU-4, DU-12, DU-23, SP-6, ST-36, UB-23

BLOOD STAGNATION

Clinical Manifestations
  • Chronic, continuous nasal congestion or
  • Post nasal drip with sticky yellow or white
  • Nasal mucous membranes appear swollen, hard and or dark purple
  • Reduction or loss of sense of smell
  • T: Dark red or Purple with brown or purple spots
  • C: Normal
  • P: Wiry and thready or Choppy
Treatment Principle
  • Harmonize Qi and Blood
  • Move Stagnation
  • Eliminate Blood Stasis
Herb Formulas
  •  Dang Gui Shao Yao San modified
Points
  • LI-20, LI-4, DU-20, DU-23, Yintang, Bitong, UB-2, UB-17, GB-20, SP-6
  • Moxa Yintang
