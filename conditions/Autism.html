AUTISM(AUTISM SPECTRUM DISORDERS, ASD, ADD, ADHD, ASPERGER'S SYNDROME)

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • ** At this point, Chinese Medicine is used to treat the symptoms of Autism Spectrum Disorders and does not offer a cure.
  • Autism is a complex neurological disorder of  largely unknown cause characterized by impairments in social interaction and communication  and by restricted, repetitive and stereotyped patterns of behavior. It is often accompanied  by extreme behavioral challenges.
  • Autism is characterized by a profound disturbance in the emergence of social relations, apparent as early as the first months of life and almost always by age three years.
  • There is no known cure. Not everyone with autism has the same symptoms.
  • The symptoms include but are not restricted to:
    Anxiety
    Depression
    Mood swings (bipolar disorder)
    Obsessions
    Compulsions
    Inattention
    Hyperactivity
    Many autistic children show allergy symptoms  such as:
    Eczema
    Allergic rhinitis
    Asthma
    Chronic infection or inflammation
  • The signs of autism in Traditional  Chinese Medicine are:
    Severe bronchitis
    Constant gastritis
    Recurrent hepatitis
    Distension of biceps
    Dizziness
    Nausea
    Peptic ulcer
    Copious Phlegm
    Anxiety
    Unhealthy taste
    Shortage of consciousness
    Shyness
    Minor thirst
    Violet tongue
    Evasive-abrupt pulse
    Rapid-wiry pulse
    Spleen and Kidney Deficiency

PHLEGM CONFUSES THE HEART ORIFICE (DIAN)

Clinical Manifestations
  • Taciturn and uncommunicative behavior
  • Dementia
  • Lack of sympathy
  • Apathy
  • Soliloquy
  • Mental confusion
  • Clouding of consciousness
  • Talking to oneself
  • Acting as if no one else is present
  • Dull expression with blank stare
  • Odd behavior
  • Maybe loss of consciousness
  • Maybe falling to the ground
  • Sound of phlegm in throat with gurgling
  • T: Pink
  • C: White and greasy
  • P: Deep and slippery; maybe wiry
Treatment Principle
  • Clear Phlegm
  • Open the Orifices
Herb Formulas
  • Autism #3
Points
  • DU-16, DU-26, HT-7, PC-5, ST-40, UB-15, UB-18, UB-20, UB-62

HEART BLOOD DEFICIENCY

Clinical Manifestations
  • Shyness
  • Inattention
  • Depression
  • Mood swings
  • Obsessions
  • Palpitations
  • Irritability
  • Anxiety
  • Easily startled
  • Insomnia
  • Much dreaming
  • Poor memory
  • Dizziness
  • Pale face and lips
  • T: Pale
  • C: White
  • P: Thready and weak
Treatment Principle
  • Nourish Heart Blood
  • Calm the Heart and Shen
Herb Formulas
  • Autism #1
  • For hyperkinetic syndrome in patients who display changeable interests, divided attention, amnesia, dreaminess, stuttering or problems in formation of phrases and sentences: Autism #2
Points
  • HT-7, PC-6, REN-4, REN-14, SP-6, ST-36, UB-15, UB-17, UB-20, Rice-grain moxa SP-1

LIVER QI STAGNATION: PHLEGM AND QI STAGNANT AND BLOCKED

Clinical Manifestations
  • Apathy
  • Dementia
  • Muttering to oneself
  • A feeling of obstruction in throat as if a slice of roast meat were stuck there which cannot be coughed up nor swallowed down. This sensation becomes more prominent during emotional upset and subsides when relaxed
  • Distention and pain in the sides or flanks
  • Suffocating sensation in the chest
  • Tendency to sigh
  • Melancholy
  • Depression
  • Irritability
  • Inappropriate anger
  • Irregular menses
  • Dysmenorrhea
  • Breast distention and pain before and during menses
  • T: Pink
  • C: Thin and white
  • P: Slippery and wiry (slippery due to Phlegm)
Treatment Principle
  • Spread Qi
  • Resolve Phlegm
Herb Formulas
  • Autism #5
Points
  • LI-18, LIV-3, PC-5, SI-17, SJ-6
