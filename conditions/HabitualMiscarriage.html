SHAO YANG FU WITH CONGESTED FLUIDS

Clinical Manifestations
  • Alternating chills and fever (chills predominate) or
  • Chills without fever
  • Sensation of irritability in the chest
  • Sweating from the head
  • Fatigue
  • Gastrointestinal weakness
  • Heavy head
  • Neuralgia
  • Epigastric distension
  • Lower abdominal pain
  • Tension around pubic bone
  • Periumbilical pain
  • Urinary difficulty
  • T: Moist
  • C: White and slippery
  • P: Superficial and wiry
Treatment Principle
  • Disperse Clumping
  • Harmonize the Shao Yang
  • Warm the Interior
  • Dispel Cold
Herb Formulas
  • Chai Ling Tang
Points
  • GB-41, SJ-5, moxa or moxa needle REN-4, REN-6, REN-8, REN-12, SJ-6, ST-25, ST-36

SPLEEN QI DEFICIENCY

Clinical Manifestations
  • Habitual miscarriage
  • Pale-red to normal Blood color
  • Thin and watery Blood with clots
  • Irregular menstruation
  • Sallow complexion
  • Fatigue
  • Anorexia
  • Four limbs feel tired and heavy
  • Abdominal and epigastric distention after eating
  • Maybe acid regurgitation or vomiting
  • Loose stools
  • Shortness of breath
  • May be much Blood due to Spleen Not Governing Blood
  • May be little Blood due to Spleen Qi Sinking
  • The Blood is pale-red in color and watery
  • An empty feeling in the lower abdomen
  • Spleen Not Governing Blood:
    Shortness of breath
    Dizziness
    Palpitations
    Functional Uterine Bleeding
    Purpura
    Epistaxis
    Hemafecia
    Hematuria
    Menorrhagia
    Metrorrhagia
  • Spleen Qi Sinking:
    Prolapse of one or more organs
  • T: Pale and flabby - may have teethmarks
  • C: White
  • P: Even, forceless and deficient
Treatment Principle
  • Nourish the Middle Jiao Qi
  • Keep the Blood in the Chong and Ren channels
Herb Formulas
  • Bu Zhong Yi Qi Tang
Points

SPLEEN NOT GOVERNING THE BLOOD

Clinical Manifestations
  • Abdominal pain
  • Uterine bleeding (metrorrhagia)
  • Excessive menstruation
  • Menstruation with constant spotting
  • Postpartum bleeding
  • Bleeding during pregnancy
  • The Blood is pale and thin without clots
  • Weakness
  • Sore lower back
  • Post abortion bleeding
  • Dull complexion
  • Weakness and pain of the legs
  • Light red, unelevated and indistinct eruptions
  • T: Pale
  • C: Thin and white or Whitish and moist
  • P: Thready and weak
Treatment Principle
  • Nourish and warm the Blood
  • Stop bleeding
  • Regulate menstruation
  • Calm the fetus
  • Prevent miscarriage
Herb Formulas
  • Jiao Ai Tang
  • Xiong Gui Jiao Ai Tang
Points
  • SP-1, SP-3, SP-10, UB-17, UB-20, ST-36, other points are selected according to signs and symptoms

  • Lassitude

  • Dizziness
  • Tinnitus
  • Lumbago
  • Palpitations
  • Cold feet
  • Vertigo
  • Weakness and numbness of lower back and legs during pregnancy or postpartum
  • Thirst with no desire to drink
  • Malposition of the fetus
  • Dysmenorrhea
  • Right upper quadrant pain
  • Irregular menstruation
  • Hepatomegaly
  • T: Pale or pink maybe petechiae on the edges
  • C: Thin or Greasy white
