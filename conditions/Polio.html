KIDNEY YIN DEFICIENCY WITH YANG RISING

Clinical Manifestations
  • Dizziness
  • Tinnitus or deafness
  • Poor memory
  • Aching, soreness or weakness of the low back
  • Spermatorrhea or
  • Premature ejaculation
  • Aching in the bones
  • Aching in the leg and heel
  • Dry mouth
  • Thirst
  • Night sweats
  • Five Sole Heat
  • Malar flush
  • Low grade fever in the afternoon
  • Dry throat, maybe recurrent sore throat
  • Dry stools and/or
  • Constipation
  • Urine short and red
  • Heightened libido
  • If Heart Fire also Flares:
    Insomnia
    Frequent dreams
    Palpitations
  • If Liver Yang also Rises:
    Dizziness
    Blurred vision
  • T: Red
  • C: Little or None
  • P: Thready and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Nourish Yin
  • Subdue Fire
Herb Formulas
  • Di Huang Yin Zi
Points
  • KI-1, KI-2, KI-3, KI-6, KI-9, KI-10, REN-4, SP-6, UB-23
  • If Heart Fire also Flares:
  • HT-7, PC-7, UB-15
  • If Liver Yang also Rises:
  • LIV-3, UB-18

PHLEGM OBSTRUCTS THE HEART ORIFICE: EXHAUSTION SYNDROME

Clinical Manifestations
  • Sudden collapse with loss of consciousness
  • Mouth open and hands relaxed
  • Eyes closed
  • Limbs cold
  • Snoring
  • Perspiration ("flows like oil")
  • Incontinence of urine and feces
  • T: Not described
  • P: Minute and thready - about to collapse or Superficial and big but with no root (with a red face)
Treatment Principle
  • Support Zheng Qi
  • Consolidate Exhaustion
  • Strengthen Qi
  • Return Yang
Herb Formulas
  • Di Huang Yin Zi
Points
  • Moxa REN-4, REN-6, REN-8, ST-36

  • T: Red
  • C: Yellow (Greasy)
