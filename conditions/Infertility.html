INFERTILITY

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • The inability to conceive in women who have at least two years normal sexual life and whose partner has normal reproductive functioning.
  • Habitual miscarriage
  • May bear one child and fail to conceive after that
  • Menstrual Phase:
    Endometrial lining is shed
    Menstrual bleeding
    Progesterone drops
    The Chong and Ren gradually empty
    Kidney Yin and Liver Blood are at their lowest levels
    Problems during menses are generally related to Qi and Blood flow
    Problems at the end of menstruation are usually related to Blood Deficiency
  • Follicular Phase:
    New follicles containing eggs begin their growth
    The menstrual Blood flow is over
    FSH (follicle stimulating hormone) is released
    The ovarian follicles mature
    The dominant follicle releases its eggs on ovulation
    The regeneration of the endometrium takes place including tissue repair of the uterus and the development of estrogen and progesterone receptors
    Estrogen levels rise
    proliferation of the endometrium ensues
    Cervical fluid is produced
    The Chong and Ren are filling with Yin and Blood
  • Ovulatory Phase:
    Leuteinizing hormone is released
    Ovulation occurs
    Basal temperature increases during the second half of the cycle (switching from Yin dominance to Yang dominance)
  • Luteal Phase:
    Egg fertilization occurs
    Egg implants in the endometrial lining
    Corpus luteum is formed
    Progesterone is secreted
Treatment Principle
  • Menstrual Phase:
    Clear Stagnation
    Move Qi and Blood
  • Follicular Phase:
    Nourish Yin and Blood
    Clear Heat
    Nourish the Chong and Ren
    Nourish the Heart and Liver
  • Ovulatory Phase:
    Strengthen Heart Qi and Blood
    Nourish Liver Blood
    Invigorate Qi and Blood
  • Luteal Phase:
    Tonify Qi and yang
    Nourish Yin and Blood
Herb Formulas
  • Menstrual Phase: Tao Hong Si Wu Tang modified
  • Follicular Phase: Gui Shao Di Huang Wan modified
  • Ovulatory Phase: Fu Ren Bu Yin Pian
  • Luteal Phase: Jia Wei Jian Gu Tang
  • For abdominal pain and cramping: Shao Yao Gan Cao Tang
  • For frigidity or lack of sexual desire due to Cold: Ba Wei Di Huang Wan
    Topical: Finely powder equal amounts of Rx. Polygalae Yuan Zhi, Rz. Zingiberis Gan Jiang. Fr. Cnidii She Chuang Zi and Fr. Evodiae Wu Zhu Yu. Wrap the mixture in fine silk and insert in the vagina. Cure should occur within two days.
  • Patent Medicines:
    Nuan Gong Yun Zi Wan
    Wu Chi Pain Feng Wan
Points

  • KI-4, KI-6, LU-7, REN-3, SP-5, ST-30
