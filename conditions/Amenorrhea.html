AMENORRHEA

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • Primary amenorrhea: After 18 years old, the patient still does not have menstruation
  • Secondary amenorrhea: Menstruation stops for more than three months before normal menopause
  • Normal times for amenorrhea:
    Pregnancy
    During puberty when period is not yet regulated
    During breast-feeding
