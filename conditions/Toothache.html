WIND-COLD ATTACKS THE LUNGS

Clinical Manifestations
  • Acute onset of a toothache with a contracting feeling
  • Aggravation of pain when eating cold food or drinks
  • Pain alleviated from eating warm food and drinks
  • Aversion to Cold
  • Itching or tickling in the throat
  • Cough with expectoration of thin, watery or frothy white sputum
  • Chills with low grade fever
  • Anhidrosis
  • No thirst
  • Headache
  • Aversion to Wind
  • Blocked and / or runny nose with clear, watery nasal discharge
  • Sneezing
  • General body aches
  • Maybe asthma
  • T: Pink
  • C: Thin, white and moist
  • P: Superficial and tight
Treatment Principle
  • Expel Wind
  • Dispel Cold
  • Ventilate the Lungs
  • Resolve Phlegm
  • Stop pain
Herb Formulas
  • Ma Huang Tang
  • Jing Fang Bai Du San  modified
  • Jin Fei Cao San
  • Chinese Patent Medicines:
    Jing Fang Bai Du Pian
Points
  • LI-4, LI-20, LU-7, ST-6, ST-7, ST-40

    For a headache: + DU-16, GB-20
    For a sore throat: + REN-23
    For coughing with expectoration of white Phlegm: + UB-12, UB-13
  • ST-5
