ACUTE HEPATITIS (HEPATITIS A AND E)

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • Primary Stage: (This is mostly applicable to hepatitis A, in the early stage of the disease.)
    Fatigue
    Occasional jaundice (Damp-Heat Stage affecting the Blood)
  • If the disease is cured at this stage, there will be no further manifestations.
  • If the disease is not cured at this stage: (This stage is not seen in hepatitis C.)
    Fever and chills
    Nausea
    Gas
Treatment Principle
Herb Formulas
Points
  • For acute hepatitis: LIV-3, ST-36, UB-19
  • For chronic hepatitis: SP-6, ST-36, UB-18
  • With jaundice: + LI-4, SI-3
  • With abdominal distention: + REN-4
  • With upper abdominal distention: + REN-12
  • With lower abdominal distention: + ST-25
  • With hypochondriac pain: + Dannangxue, GB-34
