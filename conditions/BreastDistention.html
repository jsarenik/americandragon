BREAST DISTENTION AND PAIN, PREMENSTRUAL BREAST DISTENTION

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • Breast distention may occur premenstrually or during menstruation.
Treatment Principle
Herb Formulas
Points
  • LIV-14, REN-17, ST-16, ST-18, ST-34
    With nipple pain    : + LIV-2
    With Liver Qi Stagnation: + LIV-3, PC-6
    With Liver Fire: + LIV-2
    With Kidney Deficiency: + KI-3, UB-23
    With Deficiency Cold of the Chong and Ren: + moxa or moxa needle REN-4
    With Fire Flaring: + PC-8, UB-15
    For Blood stagnation in the breast with a pricking pain through to the back: + UB-14, UB-17
    With Phlegm Obstructing the Jing-Luo: + ST-36, ST-40

LIVER AND STOMACH FIRE

Clinical Manifestations
  • Premenstrual breast distention
  • Irritating pain in the breast during menstruation
  • An itching or tickling sensation or a mucoid discharge from the nipple
  • A sensation of Heat in the area of the breast
  • Dizziness
  • distention or constriction of the chest and flanks
  • Severe, splitting headaches with dizziness
  • Sudden tinnitus or
  • Sudden deafness
  • Flushed face
  • Red, swollen, painful eyes
  • Irascibility with violent angry outbursts
  • Dry mouth
  • Bitter taste in mouth
  • Thirst
  • Vomiting sour or bitter liquid
  • Insomnia
  • Restlessness
  • Constipation or dry stools
  • Maybe rib pain
  • Hematemesis
  • Burning Heat and pain in the epigastrium
  • Irritating thirst: drinks copious fluids and prefers cold drinks
  • Excessive appetite: hungry again quickly after eating (as if food vanishes)
  • Swelling, pain, ulceration and bleeding of gums
  • Foul breath
  • Toothache
  • Maybe vomiting right after eating
  • Vomiting acid or undigested food
  • Constipation
  • Maybe scanty, red urine
  • Maybe infertility
  • Maybe excessive leukorrhea
  • T: Red or Red tip and edges or Scarlet
  • C: Thick, yellow and dry and rough
  • P: Slippery, big, excess and rapid or Wiry, excess and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Clear Heat and Fire
Herb Formulas
  • Jia Wei Xiao Yao San  modified
Points
  • LIV-2, LIV-14, PC-7, REN-17, ST-18, ST-34
