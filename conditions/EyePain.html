WIND-COLD

Clinical Manifestations
  • Acute onset of eye pain
  • Aggravation of the pain on exposure to Wind and Cold
  • No redness or swelling of the eyelid
  • Slight fever
  • Aversion to Wind and Cold
  • Headache with a contracting sensation which refers to the neck and back
  • No thirst
  • Nasal congestion
  • Nasal discharge
  • T: Normal
  • C: Thin and white
  • P: Floating and tight
Treatment Principle
  • Dispel wind
  • Eliminate Cold
Herb Formulas
  • Chuan Xiong Cha Tiao Wan
  • Ming Mu Xi Xin Tang Jia Jian
  • Chinese Patent Medicine: Chuan Xiong Cha Tiao Wan
Points
  • GB-20, LI-4, LU-7, SJ-5, ST-2, ST-3, UB-2
    With pain at the orbit: + GB-14, Yuyao
    With pain at the external corner of the eye: + SJ-23, Taiyang
    With pain at the inner corner of the eye: + UB-1, Yintang
    With a headache: + DU-23, GB-8
    With aversion to Cold and general body pain: + UB-58, UB-63
    With a runny nose, cough and expectoration of white Phlegm: + LU-5, UB-13
    With a fever: + DU-14
    With a blocked nose: + LI-20
