EMOTIONAL STRESS

TAI YANG - YANG MING

Clinical Manifestations
  • Severe fever and chills without sweat
  • Intolerance of Cold
  • Irritability
  • Fidgeting
  • Headache
  • Cough
  • Wheezing (asthma)
  • Muscle aches
  • General body aches
  • Thirst
  • Heaviness and aching of the entire body
  • Shortness of breath
  • Floating edema (severe)
  • Restlessness
  • Anhidrosis
  • Fidgeting
  • May be high fever
  • Edema
  • Dyspnea
  • T: Normal
  • C:Thin white or Yellow
  • P: Floating and tight or Rapid or Tight and rapid or wiry and tight
Treatment Principle
  • Promote diaphoresis
  • Release the Exterior
  • Clear Interior Heat
  • Relieve irritability
Herb Formulas
  • Da Qing Long Tang
Points

  • The tinnitus is usually high pitched
  • There may be ear pain and discharge
  • Flushed face
  • Red, swollen, painful eyes
  • Irascibility with violent angry outbursts
  • Dry mouth and throat
  • Bitter taste in mouth
  • Thirst

  • Insomnia
  • Restlessness
  • Hypochondriac pain
  • Constipation or dry stools
  • Maybe rib pain
  • Constipation
  • Concentrated urine

    Mania
    Hemoptysis
    Hematemesis
    Epistaxis

    Hematemesis
