UPPER AND MIDDLE BACK PAIN, STIFFNESS AND TENSION

TAI YANG ZHONG FENG

Clinical Manifestations
  • Neck pain and stiffness
  • Aversion to Wind
  • Mild, or severe chills
  • Mild fever which may be persistent
  • Uneasiness from fever
  • Spontaneous perspiration
  • Headache
  • Nausea
  • Nasal irritation
  • Nasal obstruction
  • Tendency to snore
  • Itchy throat
  • T: Normal
  • C: Thin and white
  • P: Superficial and slow or Moderate and maybe weak
Treatment Principle
  • Create sweat to relieve the Exterior and expel Wind
  • Harmonize Ying and Wei to protect digestion and nourish Wei Qi
Herb Formulas
  • Gui Zhi Jia Ge Gen Tang
Points
  • Bitong, DU-16, GB-20, GB-21, KI-7, LI-4, LI-20, LU-7, SJ-5, UB-12, UB-13, Yintang
