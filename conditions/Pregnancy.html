BLOOD DEFICIENCY WITH WIND-COLD DURING PREGNANCY

Clinical Manifestations
  • Acute headache
  • Fever
  • Mild chills
  • Little or no sweating
  • A lusterless, sallow complexion
  • Pale nails and lips
  • Fatigue
  • Palpitations
  • Dizziness
  • T: Pale
  • C: White
  • P:Thready and floating or Thready and weak
Treatment Principle
  • Nourish the blood
  • Clear the Exterior
Herb Formulas
  • Cong Bai Qi Wei Yin  modified
  • Chinese Patent Medicines:
    Gan Mao Ling
Points
  • DU-14, GB-20, LU-7, ST-36, UB-17

EXCESSIVE AMNIOTIC FLUID

Clinical Manifestations
  • Usually manifests between the fourth and sixth month of pregnancy
  • Abnormal enlargement of the abdomen
  • Fullness and oppression of the chest and diaphragm
  • Forced, urgent breathing
  • Dyspnea which will not be quieted and in severe cases prohibits lying flat
  • Edematous swelling of the lower extremities and external genitalia
  • Maybe generalized edema
  • T: Pale
  • C: Slimy
  • P: Deep, slippery and forceful
Treatment Principle
  • Warm and transform Phlegm and congested Fluids
  • Strengthen the Spleen
  • Resolve Dampness
  • Improve metabolic function
Herb Formulas
  • Ling Gui Zhu Gan Tang  modified

  • Prolonged labor
  • Weak uterine contractions
  • Slow dilation of the cervix
  • Maybe intrauterine fetal hypoxia
  • Lassitude
  • Drowsiness
  • Easily fatigued
  • Pale face
  • T: Pale and dry
  • C: White
  • P Superficial and empty
Treatment Principle
  • Tonify Qi and Blood

DIS-COORDINATED LABOR (DYSTOCIA)

Clinical Manifestations
  • Labor is too active or uncoordinated
  • Restlessness
  • Labor pain excessively strong
  • Insufficient relaxation of the uterus between contractions
  • Tenderness in the lower part of the uterus
  • The cervix is somewhat hard
  • Evidence of pronounced fetal suffering
  • T: Red
  • P: Rapid and tense
Treatment Principle
  • Regulate Qi and Blood
