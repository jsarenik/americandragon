HEART YANG DEFICIENCY

Clinical Manifestations
  • Blurred vision when trying to see distant objects
  • Can see near objects relatively clearly
  • Squinting in order to see distant objects
  • Maybe flashing sensations
  • Floaters
  • Low vision in a dim environment
  • Visual fatigue
  • Maybe blurred vision for both far and near objects
  • Palpitations
  • Mental fatigue
  • Forgetfulness
  • Pale complexion
  • T: Pale
  • C: Thin and white
  • P: Thready and weak
Treatment Principle
  • Tonify and nourish the Heart
  • Replenish Qi
  • Calm the Shen
  • Stabilize the Mind
Herb Formulas
  • Myopia #1 *
Points
  • Ten Day Course:
    Use one combination each day (five day week), alternating each week.
    Use tonification method.
    Take one week off in between courses (may need up to four courses)
    Check improvement with an eye chart after each course.
    Day 1: ST-1, Yiming(EX-HN-13)
    Day 2: ST-2, SI-15
    Day 3: ST-8, Qiuhou(EX-HN-8)
    Day 4: UB-1, GB-37
    Day 5: SJ-23, LIV-3, KI-6
  • Ear Acupuncture: Shenmen, Liver, Spleen, Kidney and Ear Apex
    One treatment is fourteen days.
  • Tui Na Massage: Special point 1/3 cun below UB-2 (this is the main myopia point), UB-2, Yuyao, SJ-23, ST-2, UB-1
    Press and knead the main myopia point first using the index finger for one to two minutes, then massage the other points for one to two minutes each.
  • Massage three to five times for one treatment.

HEART AND GALL BLADDER QI DEFICIENCY

Clinical Manifestations
  • Insomnia or frequent waking, often early in the morning
  • Unable to fall back to sleep
  • Dream disturbed sleep
  • Easily frightened or startled
  • Tends to wake up in fright
  • Easily unsettled by seeming trivial events
  • Anxiety
  • Timidity
  • Palpitations
  • Shortness of breath
  • Lethargy
  • Fatigue
  • Depression
  • Spontaneous sweating
  • T: Normal or Pale (chronic or congenital cases may show a deep narrow crack to the tip
  • C: Thin and white
  • P: wiry and thready
Treatment Principle
  • Strengthen Qi
  • Calm the Shen
  • Alleviate fearfulness
Herb Formulas
  • Ding Zhi Wan
Points
  • GB-40, HT-7, LIV-3, LIV-5, PC-7, REN-15
