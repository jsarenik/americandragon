ECTOPIC PREGNANCY

QI AND BLOOD STAGNATION

Clinical Manifestations
  • Pain that is fixed, stabbing and /or severe or
  • Pains that move around
  • Distention
  • Depression
  • Irritability
  • Pan is worse with pressure
  • Dark complexion
  • Fixed, palpable masses
  • Bleeding with clots
  • Purple lips
  • Purple nails
  • Dry skin
  • Blue, dark or purplish discoloration of skin in small or large areas,
  • Varicose and spider veins
  • Cold hands and feet
  • Tendency to hemorrhage dark blood or clots
  • T: Purple, possible red spots
  • C: White
  • P: Choppy or Wiry and choppy
Treatment Principle
  • Tonify Qi
  • Invigorate the Blood
Herb Formulas
  • Ectopic Pregnancy #1 (when urine pregnancy tests turn negative or serological markers lower on two occasions, switch to Ectopic Pregnancy #2)
  • Ectopic Pregnancy #2
  • Huo Luo Xiao Ling Dan
  • Huo Luo Xiao Ling Dan Jia Wei
  • With Qi and Blood Deficiency: Yi Qi Huo Xue Tang

QI AND YANG DEFICIENCY (RUPTURED , SHOCK TYPE)

Clinical Manifestations
  • Abrupt pain in the lower abdomen
  • Tenderness
  • Pallor
  • Cold limbs
  • Profuse cold sweat
  • Irritability
  • Low Blood pressure
  • T: Pale
  • C: White
  • P: Faint or None or Thready or Rapid and weak
Treatment Principle
  • Invigorate the Blood
  • Remove Blood Stasis
  • Tonify Yang
  • Tonify Qi
  • Restore from collapse
Herb Formulas
  • Ectopic Pregnancy #5

BLOOD STAGNATION

Clinical Manifestations
  • Mild, persistent uterine bleeding of purple and black blood during pregnancy
  • Abdominal pain that is worse with pressure
  • Abdominal spasms and tension
  • Immobile masses in the lower abdomen with pain and tenderness
  • Amenorrhea with distention and pain
  • Dysmenorrhea
  • Headache
  • Sinus congestion
  • Constipation
  • Excess weight in the pelvis
  • Lower back ache
  • Irregular menstruation
  • Nervousness
  • Fatigue
  • Fetal instability
  • Skin eruptions
  • Scanty menstrual flow
  • Scaly, dry skin
  • Retention of lochia
  • Shoulder stiffness
  • Vertigo
  • Cold low body and feet
  • Flushed face
  • Protruding abdomen
  • T: Dark purple or with echymoses or petechiae or pale with purplish spots
  • C: White
  • P: Choppy or Tense, deep and slow
Treatment Principle
  • Invigorate the Blood
  • Transform Blood Stasis
  • Soften lumps
  • Reduce fixed abdominal pain
Herb Formulas
  • Gui Zhi Fu Ling Wan
  • Sheng Hua Tang
  • Shi Xiao San
  • Gong Wai Yun Fang
  • Ge Xia Zhu Yu Tang
  • Shao Fu Zhu Yu Tang
  • Ectopic Pregnancy #3
  • Due to Blood Deficiency:
    Si Wu Tang
    Si Wu Tang Jia Wei
Points

BLOOD STAGNATION (UNRUPTURED)

Clinical Manifestations
  • Unruptured ectopic pregnancy
  • Morning sickness
  • Vague pain
  • Distention and a dragging sensation on one side of the lower abdomen
  • Slight tubal dilation or
  • A soft, tender mass on one side of the oviduct
  • Urine pregnancy test positive
  • T: Purple, possible red spots
  • C: White
  • P: Choppy or Wiry and choppy
Treatment Principle
  • Invigorate the Blood
  • Remove Blood Stasis
    Eliminate the mass
  • Destroy the embryo
Herb Formulas
  • Ectopic Pregnancy #4

BLOOD STAGNATION WITH BLOOD HEAT (RUPTURED)

Clinical Manifestations
  • Acute lower abdominal pain, which is worse with pressure
  • Fever which is worse art night
  • Constipation or
  • Black, tarry stools
  • Manic behavior in severe cases
  • Delirium in severe cases
  • T: Deep red with purple spots
  • C: Yellow
  • P: Deep and full
Treatment Principle
  • Clear Heat
  • Break up Blood Stagnation
Herb Formulas
  • Tao He Cheng Qi Tang
Points
  • LIV-2, PC-9, SP-4, SP-6, SP-8, SP-10, ST-40, Zigong
