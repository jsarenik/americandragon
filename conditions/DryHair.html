DRY HAIR

PERVERSE DRYNESS ATTACKS THE LUNGS

Clinical Manifestations
  • Dry hair
  • Dry cough with little or no sputum
  • Sputum is difficult to expectorate
  • Maybe hemoptysis
  • Cough may cause chest pain
  • Dry or sore throat
  • Hoarse voice
  • Dry lips and mouth
  • Thirst
  • Dryness in nose
  • Epistaxis
  • Chills and fever
  • Headache
  • Anhidrosis
  • Dry skin and hair
  • Constipation
  • T: Red tip
  • C: Thin, yellow and dry
  • P: Superficial, maybe rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Clear the Lungs
  • Moisten Dryness
Herb Formulas
  • Qing Zao Jiu Fei Tang
  • If symptoms persist for a long time: Yang Yin Qing Fei Tang
Points
  • KI-3, LU-1, LU-6, SP-6, UB-13
