VAGINAL PAIN

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • Pain in vaginal area possibly radiating upwards towards uterus, abdomen, or even to the breasts
Treatment Principle
Herb Formulas
Points

WIND-COLD

Clinical Manifestations
  • Postpartum vaginal pain
  • It can be very severe
  • Swollen pain
  • No inflammation or redness or burning
  • Cough with expectoration of thin, watery or frothy white sputum
  • Chills with low grade fever
  • Anhidrosis
  • No thirst
  • Headache
  • Aversion to Wind
  • Blocked and / or runny nose with clear, watery nasal discharge
  • Sneezing
  • General body aches
  • Maybe asthma
  • T: Pink
  • C: Thin, white and moist
  • P: Superficial and tight
Treatment Principle
  •  Expel Wind and Cold to harmonize Luo
  • Relieve pain
Herb Formulas
  • Qu Feng Ding Tong Tang
Points
  • LI-4, LIV-3, SP-8, KI-10, REN-2, REN-3, UB-18, ST-29
    For lower abdominal pain: + moxa REN-8, ST-25

SPLEEN QI SINKING

Clinical Manifestations
  • local heavy pain pulling downward
  • thin clear vaginal discharge
  • Prolapse of one or more organs
  • Sallow complexion
  • Anorexia
  • Four limbs feel tired and heavy
  • Abdominal and epigastric distention after eating
  • Maybe acid regurgitation or vomiting
  • Loose stools
  • Shortness of breath
  • T: Pale and flabby - may have teethmarks
  • C: White
  • P: Even or Soft and thready
Treatment Principle
  • Strengthen the Spleen
  • Reinforce the Center,
  • Invigorate Yang
  • Benefit Qi
Herb Formulas
  • Bu Zhong Yi Qi Tang
  • Ju Yuan Jian
Points
  • DU-20, LIV-13, REN-12, SP-3, SP-5, SP-6, ST-36, UB-20

LIVER FIRE

Clinical Manifestations
  • Vaginal pain that radiates to the abdomen and possibly nipple
  • Severe, splitting headaches with dizziness
  • Sudden tinnitus or deafness
  • Flushed face
  • Red, swollen, painful eyes
  • Irascibility with violent angry outbursts
  • Dry mouth
  • Bitter taste in mouth
  • Thirst
  • Vomiting sour or bitter liquid
  • Insomnia
  • Restlessness
  • Constipation or dry stools
  • Maybe rib pain
  • T: Red tip and edges
  • C: Thick, yellow, dry and rough
  • P: Wiry, Excess and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Clear the Liver
  • Purge Fire
Herb Formulas
  • Jia Wei Xiao Yao San
Points
  • GB-12, GB-34, GB-37, LIV-2, UB-18, UB-19

LIVER AND KIDNEY YIN DEFICIENCY

Clinical Manifestations
  • Older women
  • Dryness with burning pain
  • Local area may be shrunken or shriveled
  • Night sweats
  • Dry mouth and tongue
  • Lower back and knee pain
  • Urinary difficulty
  • Weakness of the lower back, knees and extremities
  • Steaming bones
  • Light-headedness
  • Vertigo
  • Tinnitus
  • Diminished hearing
  • Five sole heat
  • Chronic dry, sore throat
  • Toothache
  • Wasting and thirsting disorder (Xiao Ke)
  • Dizziness
  • Muscular weakness
  • Irritability
  • Dry skin
  • Deafness
  • Spontaneous and nocturnal emissions
  • Tidal fevers
  • Loose teeth
  • Dripping urine
  • Thirst
  • Swollen gums, painful and light red in color
  • Numbness and weakness at the waist and knees
  • Heel pain
  • Urinary incontinence
  • Pain at midline and in lower back
  • Sore throat
  • T: Red
  • C: Little or None
  • P: Thready and rapid (large at the Kidney positions)
Treatment Principle
  • Enrich Yin
  • Nourish the Essence of the Liver and Kidneys
  • Reduce Deficiency Fire
Herb Formulas
  • Zhi Bai Di Huang Wan
Points
  • KI-3, LIV-3, SP-6, UB-18, UB-23

DAMP-HEAT

Clinical Manifestations
  • Swollen pain
  • Yellow thick smelly leukorrhea
  • Local ulcerations or inflammation
  • Hypochondriac pain
  • Headache
  • Dizziness
  • Red sore eyes
  • Hearing loss
  • Swelling in the ears
  • Bitter taste in the mouth
  • Irritability
  • Short temper
  • Difficult and painful urination with a heat sensation in the urethra
  • Swollen and itchy genitalia
  • Short menstrual cycle with reddish-purple blood
  • Ear discharge
  • Yellow urine
  • Chest pain
  • Constipation
  • Turbid urine
  • Dry throat
  • Hematuria
  • Pain in the armpits
  • Sweating in the groin
  • T: Red or Red edges
  • C: Yellow or Yellow and greasy
  • P: Wiry, Excess and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Clear Heat
  • Resolve Dampness
Herb Formulas
  • Long Dan Xie Gan Tang
  • Yi Huang Tang
  • Xiao Yao San  modified
Points
  • REN-3, SP-6, SP-10, UB-17, UB-28, HT-8
  • UB-23, UB-28, REN-4, REN-3, SP-6 with medium to strong stimulation every day or every other day. Ten to fifteen treatments equals one course.
  • REN-4, REN-6, SP-6, LIV-4
