MORNING SICKNESS - GRAVID HYPEREMESIS- PERNICIOUS VOMITING

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • Vomiting
  • Nausea
  • Dizziness
  • Anorexia
  • Aversion to food
  • Fatigue
  • Prompt vomiting after eating
  • All symptoms occur during pregnancy especially in the early stage
Treatment Principle
Herb Formulas
  • If the patient does not want to eat: soup from Nonglutinous rice Geng Mi.

  • PC-6, REN-12, ST-36
  • PC-3, ST-36, moxa REN-12, SJ-4

  • LU-5, PC-5, PC-6, PC-7, ST-36, UB-19
    First five months: + KI-21, REN-11, REN-12
  • Severe: PC-6 through to SJ-5
    With a poor appetite: + ST-36
  • Cutaneous: Huatuojiaji from T-5 to T-9, L-1 to L-4, LIV-3, PC-6, REN-12, REN-13, REN-17, SP-4, ST-34, ST-36, ST-44, Taiyang, UB-13, UB-18, UB-20, Yintang
