Clinical Manifestations
  • Transitory, recurrent episodes of paroxysmal sneezing, nasal itch with copious clear watery nasal discharge or congestion
  • The symptoms are triggered or aggravated by exposure to Wind or Cold air
  • Easily catches colds
  • Reduction or loss of sense of smell
  • Chronic nasal congestion
  • Frequent colds
  • Soft, low voice
  • Shortness of breath
  • Spontaneous sweating
  • Waxy, pale complexion
  • In atopic individuals, there may be a history or concurrent eczema or asthma
  • T: Pale
  • C: Thin white
  • P: Deficient and weak
