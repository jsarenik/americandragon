CHRONIC DACRYSTITIS, INFLAMMATION OF THE TEAR DUCTS

WIND-COLD ATTACKS THE LUNGS

Clinical Manifestations
  • Cough with a heavy sound
  • Inflammation of the tear ducts
  • Choking cough
  • Stuffy and runny nose
  • Thin white sputum
  • Chills and fever
  • Headache
  • Nasal obstruction
  • Itching larynx
  • Aching of limbs
  • Muscle soreness
  • Chilliness
  • Anhidrosis
  • T: Normal
  • C:Thin and white
  • P: Floating or Floating and tense
Treatment Principle
  • Disperse Wind and Cold
  • Release Lung Qi
Herb Formulas
  • Xiao Qing Long Tang
Points
  • LI-4, LU-9, UB-11, UB-12, UB-13
  • GB-20, LU-8, SJ-5, UB-12, UB-13
  • DU-16, GB-20, KI-7, LI-4, LU-5, LU-7, UB-12, UB-13

TAI YANG BIAO FU

Clinical Manifestations
  • Chills and fever
  • Runny nose
  • Achiness
  • Stiff neck and shoulders
  • Anhidrosis
  • Headache
  • Stabbing joint pain
  • Itchy throat
  • Cough
  • Asthma
  • Aversion to Cold
  • Maybe frozen shoulder
  • The Tai Yang symptoms remain for 2-3 days
  • Thirst with no desire to drink
  • May have vomiting immediately after drinking
  • Difficult urination
  • May have upper body edema
  • TC: White
  • P: Floating and slippery
Treatment Principle
  • Promote urination
  • Dispel Dampness
  • Expel Wind
Herb Formulas
  • Wu Ling San
Points
  • UB-13, ST-28, REN-3, SP-6, SP-9, KI-3, LI-4, LI-20, LU-7

TAI YANG - YANG MING (ACUTE)

Clinical Manifestations
  • Inflammation of the tear ducts
  • Fever
  • Sweating
  • Thirst
  • Dysenteric diarrhea
  • Foul-smelling stools
  • A burning sensation around the anus
  • Gasping
  • Ephidrosis
  • Palpitations
  • Tenesmus
  • Maybe red tinged urine
  • Irritability and Heat in the chest and epigastrium
  • Stiffness and spasms at the back of the neck
  • Dry mouth
  • Dyspnea with perspiration
  • Maybe wheezing
  • T: Red
  • C: Yellow (Greasy)
  • P: Rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Release the Exterior
  • Clear Internal Yang Ming Heat
Herb Formulas
  • Ge Gen Huang Qin Huang Lian Tang
Points

DAMP-HEAT INJURES THE SPLEEN

Clinical Manifestations
  • Anorexia
  • Abdominal distention and discomfort
  • Suffocating sensation in the chest
  • Epigastric mass
  • Maybe abdominal pain on palpation
  • Yang jaundice (sclera and skin are bright yellow)
  • Dislike of oily foods
  • Nausea
  • Vomiting
  • Lassitude
  • Scanty, dark urine
  • Maybe thirst or thirst with no desire to drink
  • Diarrhea with foul smell or
  • Constipation
  • Fever
  • Sweet sticky taste or bitter taste in mouth
  • T: Pink
  • C: Thick, yellow and greasy (maybe sticky)
  • P: Soft and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Clear Heat
  • Benefit Dampness
Herb Formulas
  • Yin Chen Wu Ling San
Points
  • LI-11, REN-12, SP-9, ST-36, ST-44, UB-20

SPLEEN DEFICIENCY EDEMA

Clinical Manifestations
  • Edema in limbs, especially lower limbs
  • Maybe anasarca
  • Maybe abdominal distention
  • Loose stools
  • Physical and mental fatigue
  • T: Pale
  • C: White and moist
  • P: Soft and even
Treatment Principle
  • Warm Yang
  • Strengthen the Spleen
  • Benefit Qi
  • Move water
Herb Formulas
  • Si Ling San
Points
  • REN-6, REN-9, SP-6, SP-9, ST-28, ST-36, UB-20, UB-22, UB-23

HEAT

Clinical Manifestations
  • Lachrymal sac infection
  • Blockage of nasolacrymal duct
  • Pain
  • Redness
  • Swelling
  • Watery, oozing pus
  • Possible fever
Treatment Principle
  • Clear Heat
Herb Formulas
  • Wu Wei Xiao Du Yin + Huang Lian Jie Du Tang
  • With Deficiency: Ren Shen Yang Ying Tang
