STOMACH AND LIVER HEAT

Clinical Manifestations
  • Recurrent or relentless acid reflux and heartburn, aggravated by coffee, chocolate and alcohol or stress
  • The patient may wake in the middle of the night (1-2 am) with discomfort and reflux
  • Sour or bitter belching
  • Epigastric discomfort or burning sensation
  • Indeterminate, gnawing hunger
  • Foul stools
  • Dry mouth
  • Thirst
  • Irritability
  • Quick temper
  • T: Red with red edges
  • C: Yellow
  • P: Wiry and/or slippery, maybe rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Clear Heat from the Stomach and Liver
  • Harmonize the Stomach
  • Descend Stomach Qi
Herb Formulas
  • Hua Gan Jian Jia Wei
  • Severe cases: Zuo Jin Wan modified
  • When Stomach Heat is primary: Qing Wei San modified
  • Chinese Patent Medicines:
    Jia Wei Xiao Yao San
    Wen Dan Wan
    Wei Te Ling
    Xiang Lian Wan
Points
  • GB-34, LI-11, LIV-2, PC-8, REN-12, REN-13, REN-17, ST-36, ST-44
    With severe Heat: + SJ-2, ST-45
    With constipation: + SJ-6, ST-25
    With Lung and Stomach Heat: + LU-5
    With nausea: + PC-6

  • Dry mouth and throat

  • Constipation or dry stools
