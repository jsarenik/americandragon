HEART AND LIVER BLOOD DEFICIENCY

Clinical Manifestations
  • Palpitations
  • Insomnia with much dreaming
  • Easily startled
  • Eye dizziness
  • Both eyes dry and rough
  • Twitching of the eyes
  • Pain in the ribs and flanks
  • Numbness of the limbs
  • Stiffness and spasms of the tendons
  • Low grade fever
  • Night sweats
  • Irregular, scanty menses
  • T: Pale and thin
  • C: Little
  • P: Thready, choppy and forceless
Treatment Principle
  • Nourish Yin
  • Reinforce the Blood
  • Conceal Yang
  • Calm the Shen
Herb Formulas
  • Dang Gui Bu Xue Tang
Points
  • HT-7, LIV-3, REN-4, SP-6, ST-36, UB-15, UB-17, UB-18

LIVER QI STAGNATION

Clinical Manifestations
  • Absent or scanty lactation
  • Obstructed lactation
  • Breast distention
  • Hardness of the breasts
  • Painful breasts
  • Hypochondriac distension
  • Irritability
  • Epigastric distension
  • Pain and distention along the flanks
  • Chest oppression
  • Eructation
  • Nausea
  • Distention in the head
  • Headaches
  • T: Red edges
  • P: Wiry
Treatment Principle
  • Nourish and Invigorate the Blood
  • Regulate Qi
Herb Formulas
  • Xia Ru Yong Quan San
  • Tong Gan Xia Ru Tang
  • Tong Cao San
  • With breast distention and pain or swelling with palpable lumps:
    Lou Lu San
    Yong Quan San
Points
  • GB-21, LIV-14, PC-1, PC-6, REN-17, SI-1

QI AND BLOOD DEFICIENCY

Clinical Manifestations
  • Insufficient or absent lactation after childbirth
  • Watery milk
  • The breasts are overly soft and never get engorged
  • Absence of breast distention or pain
  • No discharge of milk or
  • Only a few drops when the breast is pressed
  • Pallor
  • Fatigue
  • Vertigo
  • Blurred vision
  • Sallow complexion
  • Dry skin
  • Poor appetite
  • Tiredness
  • May have large amounts of lochia discharge postpartum
  • T: Pale
  • C: White
  • P: Thready and weak
Treatment Principle
  • Tonify Qi and Blood
Herb Formulas
  • Tong Ru Dan
  • Zhu Ti Tang
  • Ba Zhen Tang
  • Dang Gui Bu Xue Tang
  • Si Wu Tang  modified (Boil this decoction with a soup made from ham hocks. Wash the nipples with onion soup prior to ingesting th decoction)
  • Insufficient Lactation #1
  • With Blood Stagnation: Tong Ru Tang
Points
  • REN-17, SI-1, ST-36, UB-13, UB-20, UB-23
