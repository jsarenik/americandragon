NEURASTHENIA

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • An ill-defined medical condition characterized by lassitude, fatigue, headache and irritability, associated chiefly with emotional disturbance.
Treatment Principle
Herb Formulas
  • Fry 12-15 seeds of Sm. Zizyphi Spinosae Suan Zao Ren to half-cooked, pulverize and grind. Take before sleep.
  • Decoct and drink 30g Sm. Zizyphi Spinosae Suan Zao Ren and 12g Rx. Salviae Miltiorrhizae Dan Shen.
  • Decoct and drink 4.5-9g Fr. Schisandrae Wu Wei Zi.
  • Chinese Patent Medicine:
    An Shen Bu Xin Wan
Points
  • DU-14, HT-7, PC-6, SP-6, ST-36, Yiming
  • Ear: Shenmen, Subcortex, Heart, Kidney, Endocrine, Sympathetic

TAI YANG ZHONG FENG

Clinical Manifestations
  • Aversion to Wind
  • Mild, or severe chills
  • Fever which may be persistent
  • Uneasiness from fever
  • Spontaneous perspiration
  • Headache

  • Nasal obstruction
  • Tendency to snore
  • Itchy throat

  • C: Thin and white
  • P: Superficial and slow or Moderate and maybe weak

  • Harmonize Ying and Wei to protect digestion and nourish Wei Qi
Herb Formulas
  • Gui Zhi Tang

  • With dyspnea, asthma and sputum: Gui Zhi Jia Hou Po Xing Ren Tang
Points

    With stiff neck and shoulders: + GB-21
    With Dampness: + SP-6, SP-9
    With dyspnea, asthma and sputum: Dingchuan, REN-22
    With nocturnal emission, excess dreams, Cold lower extremities, dizziness and hair loss: + DU-4, KI-3, UB-23
    With continuous perspiration, muscle spasms of the limbs with difficult flexion and extension and difficult urination: + REN-4
    With sustained fever of long duration accompanied by severe chills: + DU-14
