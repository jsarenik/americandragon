  • Reduced sense of smell or
  • Permanent loss of sense of smell
  • Dull or thick headache with dizziness
  • Shortness of breath
  • Soft voice or
  • Reluctance to speak
  • Fatigue
  • Spontaneous sweating
  • Aversion to wind
  • Waxy, pale complexion
  • Weak cough with thin white sputum
  • All these symptoms are worse with Cold
  • T: Pale
  • C: Thin and white

  • Tonify and warm Lung Qi

  • Open the nose
