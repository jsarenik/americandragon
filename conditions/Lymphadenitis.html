LYMPHADENITIS

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • Lymphadenitis is the medical term for enlargement in one or more lymph nodes, usually due to infection. Lymph nodes are filled with white blood cells that help the body fight infections. When lymph nodes become infected, it's usually because an infection started somewhere else in the body.
Treatment Principle
Herb Formulas
  • For toxic swellings in patients with Cold and Deficient constitutions: Qian Jin Nei Tuo San
Points

TAI YANG SHANG HAN

Clinical Manifestations
  • Cervical lymphadenitis
  • Neck pain
  • Upper back pain
  • Itching or tickling in the throat
  • Cough with expectoration of thin, watery or frothy white sputum
  • Chills with low grade fever
  • Anhidrosis
  • No thirst
  • Headache
  • Aversion to Wind
  • Blocked and / or runny nose with clear, watery nasal discharge
  • Sneezing
  • General body aches
  • Maybe asthma
  • T: Pink
  • C: Thin, white and moist
  • P: Floating and tight
Treatment Principle
  • Expel Wind
  • Dispel Cold
  • Ventilate the Lungs
  • Resolve Phlegm
Herb Formulas
  • Ge Gen Tang
Points
  • DU-16, GB-20, LI-4 + LU-7 or LI-4 + KI-7, LU-5, UB-12, UB-13
