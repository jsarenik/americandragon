ACNE VULGARIS

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • Open and closed pustules and cysts
  • Worse in males
  • Mild soreness, pain and itching
  • The lesions occur mainly on the face, neck, upper chest upper back and shoulders
  • Comedones are common
  • Open comedones are known as blackheads
  • Closed comedones are called whiteheads
  • Over time the papules may become inflamed
  • Severe cases may present deeply inflamed nodules and pus-filled cysts
  • Preteens often present with comedones as the first lesions
  • Younger teens may have inflammatory lesions in the middle of the face, extending outward as the patient ages
  • Women in their thirties and forties often present with papular lesions around the chin and mouth
Treatment Principle
Herb Formulas
  • Gui Shen Wan
  • Topical:
    1. Dian Dao San
    2. Jin Huang Gao
    3. Qu Ban Gao
    4. Rosacea Paste #1
    5. Decoct 30g each, Galla Rhois Chinensis Wu Bei Zi, Fr. Chebulae He Zi  and Cx. Phellodendri Huang Bai in 4 cups of water until the water boils. Strain the decocted herbs, and wash the affected area two times a day.
    6. Grind 30g Sm. Armeniacae Xing Ren  into a powder and mix with egg whites to form a paste. Apply to the affected area before bedtime.
  • Chinese Patent Medicines:
    San She Jie Yang Wan
    Huang Lian Jie Du Pian
