POSTPARTUM FAINTING

QI DEFICIENCY

Clinical Manifestations
  • Listlessness
  • Sudden fainting
  • Pallor
  • Cold extremities
  • Cold interior of the mouth
  • Sputum choking the throat
  • Sweating on the forehead
  • T: Pale
  • C: Thin and white
  • P: Thready and feeble
Treatment Principle
  • Tonify Qi
  • Replenish the Blood
Herb Formulas
  • Bu Qi Jie Yun Tang (no information)
  • Qing Hun San
  • For Qi and Blood Deficiency: Shi Quan Da Bu Tang
Points
  • DU-20, REN-4, REN-6, REN-12, ST-25, ST-36, UB-20, UB-21

BLOOD STAGNATION

Clinical Manifestations
  • Less lochia than Blood Deficiency
  • Reddish-purple face and lips
  • Dizziness
  • Blurred vision
  • Inability to sit straight
  • Loss of consciousness
  • Aphasia
  • Distending pain in the lower abdomen which is worse with pressure
  • T: Purple or Dark
  • C: Thin and white
  • P: Deep and choppy
Treatment Principle
  • Invigorate the blood
  • Remove Stagnation
Herb Formulas
  • Shi Xiao San
  • Duo Ning San (no information)
  • With a headache due to fever from Wind, a greenish tongue, with a white coat and a floating, choppy pulse: Jing Jie San (no information)
    With shortness of breath: + Xing Ren, Zhi Gan Cao
  • For a Cold Invasion with a grayish face, cold limbs and abdomen, loose stools, and a deep, tight, choppy pulse: He Shen San (no information)
  • With Qi Stagnation: Yu Jin San (no information)
  • With Heat:
    Tong Yu Yin (no information)
    Hong Hua San     (no information)
Points
  • LIV-3, REN-4, SP-6, SP-10, ST-29, UB-25

BLOOD DEFICIENCY

Clinical Manifestations
  • Dizziness
  • Blurred vision
  • Inability to sit up straight
  • Suffocating sensation
  • Loss of consciousness
  • Aphasia
  • Pale face and lips
  • Sweat on the forehead
  • T: Pale
  • C: Thin and white
  • P: Weak
Treatment Principle
  • Nourish the Blood
  • Restore consciousness
Herb Formulas
  • Xiong Gui Tiao Xue Yin modified
Points
  • KI-3, SP-6, ST-36, UB-17, UB-20, UB-23
