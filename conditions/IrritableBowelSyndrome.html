IRRITABLE BOWEL SYNDROME (SPASTIC COLON)

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • Abdominal pain (triggered by eating, relieved by defecation)
  • Constipation or
  • Diarrhea
  • Bloating
  • Flatulence
  • Nausea
  • Mucus in the stool
  • A feeling of incomplete defecation
  • Pain in the anus or rectum
  • Headache
  • Fatigue
  • Lassitude
  • Depression
  • Anxiety
  • Poor concentration
Treatment Principle
Herb Formulas
  • IBS-C:
    Tao Ren Cong Rong Wan + Xiao Chai Hu Tang
    Tao Ren Cong Rong Wan + Xiao Yao San
    Tao Ren Cong Rong Wan + Si Ni San
Points
  • ST-25, ST-37, UB-25

  • Urine short and red
  • Sustained fever despite profuse perspiration or
  • Raging fever in the afternoon

    High fever and coma

  • Body heavy
  • Limbs tired
  • Fullness in chest

  • Vomiting
