LOWER ABDOMINAL PAIN

INTESTINAL ABSCESS

Clinical Manifestations
  • This can also be called Blood Stagnation in the Large Intestine, Blood and Heat Stagnation in the Large Intestine or Toxic Heat in the Large Intestine
  • Pain in the lower right abdomen; refuses pressure
  • Pain may be severe, boring, and in a fixed position or without fixed location
  • Constipation or
  • Mild diarrhea
  • Maybe fever
  • Maybe nausea and vomiting
  • Anorexia
  • T: Red
  • C:Thick, yellow and greasy or sticky
  • P: Rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Clear Heat
  • Transform Dampness
  • Invigorate Blood circulation
  • Conduct Stagnation.
Herb Formulas
  • Da Huang Mu Dan Pi Tang
  • Da Cheng Qi Tang
Points
  • Lanweixue, ST-37, ST-36
    For high fever: + LI-11, LI-4
    For abdominal pain: + ST-25
    For nausea and vomiting: PC-6, SP-4

SPLEEN AND KIDNEY YANG DEFICIENCY

Clinical Manifestations
  • Body cold
  • Limbs icy
  • Complexion white and shiny
  • Low back, knees and abdomen feel cold and painful
  • Lower abdomen feels cold and painful
  • Loose stools containing undigested food
  • “Cock-crow” diarrhea (occurs at about 5 am)
  • Edema of face and limbs
  • Dysuria
  • T: Pale, moist and swollen
  • C: Thin and white or White and greasy
  • P: Deep, thready, weak and maybe slow
Treatment Principle
  • Warm and Reinforce the Spleen and Kidneys
Herb Formulas
  • Zhen Wu Tang
  • Fu Zi Li Zhong Wan
Points
  • Moxa or moxa needle UB-20, UB-23, ST-36, SP-6, SP-9, REN-4, REN-12

SMALL INTESTINE DEFICIENCY COLD

  • Borborygmus

  • Chronic, incessant diarrhea
  • Urinary frequency

  • C: Thin and white
  • P: Deep and even

  • Warm and Clear the Small Intestine
Herb Formulas
  • Li Zhong Tang
Points
  • Moxa needle LIV-13, REN-4, REN-12, SP-3, ST-25, ST-36, ST-37, ST-39, UB-20, UB-27

LIVER QI AND BLOOD STAGNATION

KIDNEY YANG DEFICIENCY

Clinical Manifestations
  • Aversion to Cold
  • Watery stools with undigested food
  • Lower back pain
  • Weakness of the lower extremities
  • A cold sensation in the lower half of the body
  • Tenseness in the lower abdomen
  • Pain and cramps in the lower abdomen
  • Panting and irritability to the point of having difficulty lying down
  • Will breathe most comfortably while leaning against something
  • Nocturia
  • May be numbness in the lower abdomen
  • Urinary difficulty with edema or Spasmodic pain in the lower abdomen
  • Excessive urination
  • If severe, may be incontinence of urine
  • May have persistent diarrhea
  • Dysuria
  • May have flushed face (Yang floating upward)
  • Thirst with frequent, copious urination (Xiao Ke)
  • Fatigue (severe)
  • May have severe perspiration (Yang floating upward)
  • Constipation
  • Lassitude
  • Pale face
  • Edema of the face and feet
  • Severe edema of the legs (may prevent walking)
  • Maybe cough
  • Maybe asthma
  • T: Pale, may be swollen
  • C: Thin, white and moist
  • P: Deep, Deep and thready and/or Weak and slow
Treatment Principle
  • Warm and Tonify Kidney Yang
Herb Formulas
  • Jin Gui Shen Qi Wan
Points
  • UB-23, REN-4, KI-3, DU-4

TOXIC-HEAT OBSTRUCTING THE UTERUS

Clinical Manifestations
  • Acute lower abdominal pain or
  • Acute suprapubic pain
  • The lower abdomen feels warm or Hot
  • Worse with pressure
  • High fever
  • Chills
  • Rigors
  • Thirst
  • Copious malodorous vaginal discharge with Blood and/or pus
  • Constipation
  • Concentrated urine
  • T: Red
  • C: Thick, greasy and yellow
  • P: Slippery and rapid or Wiry and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Clear Toxic Heat
  • Activate the Blood
  • Dispel Blood Stasis
Herb Formulas
  • Yin Qiao Hong Jiang Jie Du Tang
  • With chronic Damp-Heat and Blood Stagnation following unresolved Toxic-Heat: Qing Re Jie Du Hua Yu Tang

    Huang Lian Jie Du Wan
    Yu Dai Wan
    Long Dan Xie Gan Wan
    Chuan Xi Lian Kang Yan Pian

    With nausea : + PC-6
