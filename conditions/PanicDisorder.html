HEART AND GALLBLADDER QI DEFICIENCY DUE TO SHOCK OR FRIGHT

Clinical Manifestations
  • Insomnia or frequent waking, often early in the morning
  • Unable to fall back to sleep
  • Nightmares
  • Waking terrified
  • Easily frightened or startled
  • Easily unsettled by seeming trivial events
  • Apprehension
  • Restlessness
  • Fearfulness
  • Inappropriate worry
  • Timidity
  • Phobias
  • Anxiety (frequent and severe)
  • Frequent panic attacks
  • Palpitations
  • Shortness of breath
  • Lethargy
  • Fatigue
  • Depression
  • Postnatal depression
  • Spontaneous sweating
  • T: Normal or Pale (chronic or congenital cases may show a deep narrow crack to the tip
  • C: Thin and white
  • P: Wiry and thready or Weak, thready and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Strengthen Qi
  • Calm the Shen
  • Alleviate fearfulness
  • Calm the mind
  • Nourish and tranquilize the Heart
Herb Formulas
  • An Shen Ding Zhi Wan
  • Ding Zhi Wan
  • Gui Zhi Jia Long Gu Mu Li Tang
  • With Yin Deficiency: Tian Wang Bu Xin Dan
  • Chinese Patent Medicines:
    Bai Zi Yang Xin Wan
    Ding Xin Wan
    Yang Xin Ning Shen Wan
    Bu Nan Wan
    Jian Nao Wan
Points
  • Anmian, DU-20, PC-7, HT-7, GB-40, UB-7, UB-23, UB-47, UB-52, ST-36, Yintang
  • GB-39, SJ-5, UB-15, PC-6, HT-7, HT-9, REN-14, DU-19, DU-24
  • DU-19, DU-20, DU-24, GB-40, HT-5, HT-7, HT-9, PC-6, REN-14, UB-7, UB-15
    Ear: Liver, Gallbladder, Heart, Subcortex, Sympathetic

HEART FIRE WITH BLOOD AND YIN DEFICIENCY

Clinical Manifestations
  • Insomnia
  • Continuous palpitations
  • A sensation of Heat and irritability in the chest
  • A desire to vomit without result
  • Dream-disturbed sleep
  • Anxiety
  • Tongue ulcers
  • Fidgeting
  • Maybe a rancorous disposition
  • Irritability
  • Fear and fright
  • Night sweats
  • Shortness of breath
  • Nausea
  • T: Red or Red tip
  • C: Normal
  • P: Thin and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Sedate the Heart
  • Calm the Shen
  • Drain Fire

LIVER QI STAGNATION PHLEGM AND QI STAGNANT AND BLOCKED

Clinical Manifestations
  • A feeling of obstruction in throat as if a slice of roast meat were stuck there which cannot be coughed up nor swallowed down. This sensation becomes more prominent during emotional upset and subsides when relaxed.
  • Distention and pain in the sides or flanks
  • Suffocating sensation in the chest
  • Tendency to sigh
  • Melancholy
  • Depression
  • Irritability
  • Inappropriate anger
  • Women:
    Irregular menses
    Dysmenorrhea
    Breast distention and pain before and during menses
  • T: Pink
  • C: Thin and white
  • P: Slippery and wiry (slippery due to Phlegm)
Treatment Principle
  • Spread Qi
  • Resolve Phlegm.
Herb Formulas
  • Ban Xia Hou Po Tang
Points
  • PC-5, LIV-3, SJ-6, LI-18, SI-17

HEART FIRE

Clinical Manifestations
  • Insomnia
  • Restlessness
  • Agitation
  • Anxiety
  • Difficulty falling asleep
  • Dream disturbed sleep
  • Frequent waking with nightmares
  • Palpitations
  • Ulceration, erosion, and pain of mouth and tongue
  • Thirst with a desire for cold drinks
  • Restlessness
  • Irritability
  • Irascibility
  • Feeling of Heat and disturbance in the Heart
  • Flushed face
  • Bitter taste in the mouth
  • Scanty, dark urine
  • A red complexion
  • Maybe painful urination
  • Maybe hematuria
  • T: Red tip, ulcers especially on tip or Red with a redder tip
  • C: Normal or Yellow
  • P: Excess and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Clear Heat
  • Purge Fire
  • Calm the Shen
Herb Formulas
  • Huang Lian Jie Du Tang
  • For a chronic condition with Yin Deficiency or dysuria: Dao Chi San
  • For Heart Yin Deficiency following a Shao Yin Heat condition: Huang Lian E Jiao Tang
  • For residual Heat following a Summerheat condition: Zhu Ye Shi Gao Tang
  • With severe palpittions and anxiety: Zhu Sha An Shen Wan
  • With Blood and Yin Deficiency: Zhu Sha An Shen Wan
  • Chinese Patent Medicines:
    Niu Huang Shang Qing Wan
    Niu Huang Jie Du Pian
    Dao Chi Pian
    Xiao Er Qi Xing Cha
Points
  • HT-7, HT-8, PC-7, PC-8, UB-15
