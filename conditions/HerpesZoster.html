HERPES ZOSTER (SHINGLES)

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
  • Prior to onset there may be:
    Slight fever
    Lymph node enlargement
    Local skin sensitivity and pain
  • This is an acute infectious skin disease
  • Usually along one side of the body
  • Papular eruptions
  • Lymph node enlargement
  • Nerve pain which may be very severe and may continue after disappearance of the papules
  • Occurs along the nerve routes, usually of the neck, intercostals, abdomen, lower back or extremities
    Ramsey-Hunt Syndrome:
    Facial paralysis
    Ear pain
    External ear papular eruption
  • Several days later they become scabs
  • Then they become light red
  • There may be pain without eruption
  • There may only be erythema
  • The virus may distribute through the blood system (systemic)
  • If Damp predominates: the lesions are primarily located on the lower part of the body with more fluid discharge
  • If Heat predominates: there is more pain, heat and redness
  • If Wind predominates: the lesions appear more often in the upper body with more itching
  • If Dampness and Heat combine: there will be painful, red blisters with discharge
  • If Wind and Heat combine: there will be painful, red blisters in the upper body
  • Channels:
    Liver/Gall Bladder: lesions and pain in the flanks and intercostal regions
    Yang Ming and Shao Yang: lesions and pain on the head and face
Treatment Principle
Herb Formulas
  • Herpes Zoster #2
  • Acute: Da Yan Tang
  • With Yin Deficiency: + Liu Wei Di Huang Wan
  • With Yin Deficiency and Fire Rising: + Zhi Bai Di Huang Wan
  • With Yin and Yang Deficiency: + Er Xian Tang
  • After emotional stress causing Toxic-Heat: Jie Du Zhi Tong Tang
  • Topical: Jin Huang San (Gao)
    Before the lesions have ruptured:
    1. Mash together 1 fresh Aloe Leaf Lu Hui about 1 gram in length, 0.3-1g Borneolum Bing Pian and a pinch or Margarita Zhen Zhu and apply as a paste.
    2. Use Fol. Hibisci Fu Rong Ye in an ointment base.
    After the lesions have ruptured: Apply equal parts Realgar Xiong Huang and Alumen Ming Fan mixed with water directly to the open sore, 1-2 times per day. Do not apply to the surrounding tissue and do not use if there are no wet, open, glistening sores.
    For erosive lesions: Decoct 30g each of Rx. Sanguisorbae Di Yu and Hb. Portulacae Ma Chi Xian and apply as a wet compress.
    After the erosive lesions have dried and scabs have formed: Apply Jin Huang Gao
