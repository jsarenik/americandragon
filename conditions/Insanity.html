PHLEGM-FIRE DISTURBS THE HEART

Clinical Manifestations
  • Irritability
  • Palpitations
  • Bitter taste in mouth
  • Insomnia
  • Much dreaming
  • Dizziness
  • Easily startled
  • Restlessness
  • A stuffy feeling in the chest
  • T: Red tip
  • C: Yellow and greasy
  • P: Slippery, Excess, rapid and maybe wiry
Treatment Principle
  • Clear the Heart
  • Break through Phlegm
Herb Formulas
  • Wen Dan Tang
  • Gun Tan Wan
  • Dang Tan Tang
Points
  • Bleed the Jing-Well points of the six Hand channels, Anmian, Dingshen, DU-14, DU-16, DU-20, DU-26, HT-7, PC-5, PC-6, PC-7, PC-8, REN-14, SP-1, ST-40, UB-15

HEART AND GALLBLADDER QI DEFICIENCY

Clinical Manifestations
  • Fear
  • Paranoia
  • There are often no physical symptoms at all
  • Insomnia or frequent waking, often early in the morning
  • Unable to fall back to sleep
  • Nightmares
  • Waking terrified
  • Easily frightened or startled
  • Easily unsettled by seeming trivial events
  • Apprehension
  • Restlessness
  • Inappropriate worry
  • Timidity
  • Phobias
  • Anxiety (frequent and severe)
  • Feeling disheartened
  • Forgetfulness
  • Frequent panic attacks
  • Palpitations
  • Shortness of breath
  • Incessant laughter and glee
  • Lethargy
  • Fatigue
  • Depression
  • Postnatal depression
  • Spontaneous sweating
  • Worry
  • Dizziness
  • T: Normal or Pale (chronic or congenital cases may show a deep narrow crack to the tip
  • C: Thin and white
  • P: Wiry and thready or Weak, thready and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Calm the Shen
  • Tonify the Heart Qi
  • Pacify timidity
Herb Formulas
  • Ding Zhi Wan
  • With Yin Deficiency: Tian Wang Bu Xin Dan
Points

HEART AND KIDNEY YIN DEFICIENCY WITH HEART BLOOD DEFICIENCY

  • Irritability

  • Fatigue

  • Very restless sleep
  • Inability to think or concentrate even for short periods
  • Nocturnal emissions
  • Forgetfulness
  • Dry stools
  • Mouth sores
  • Tongue sores
  • Low grade afternoon fever
  • Dream disturbed sleep
  • Night sweats
  • Constipation

  • C: Little
  • P: Rapid or Thready and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Enrich the Yin
  • Nourish the Blood
  • Tonifythe Heart and Kidneys
  • Calm the Shen
  • Clear Heat
Herb Formulas
  • Tian Wang Bu Xin Dan
