HEEL PAIN(CALCANEUS PAIN)

GENERAL

Clinical Manifestations
Treatment Principle
Herb Formulas
Points

WIND-COLD-DAMP

Clinical Manifestations
  • Heel pain with a cold, heavy sensation
  • Pain is worse on exposure to Cold and alleviated by warmth
  • Swelling of the joints
  • Slight fever
  • Aversion to Cold
  • Numb or frigid toes
  • Cold limbs
  • Poor appetite
  • No thirst
  • Clear urine
  • T: Pale
  • C: White and moist
  • P: Superficial and tense
Treatment Principle
  • Dispel wind-Cold-Damp
  • Regulate Qi
  • Invigorate the Blood
  • alleviate pain
Herb Formulas
  • Juan Bi Tang Er Hao  modified
  • Chinese Patent Medicines:
    Feng Shi Pian
    Jing Fang Bai Du Pian
    Tian Ma Fu Qu Feng Bu Pian
Points
  • KI-3, LI-4, SI-4, SP-6, UB-58, UB-60, UB-63, UB-64
    With a cold sensation in the heel: + moxa or moxa needle KI-3, UB-60
    With swelling of the heel: + SP-9
    With numbness of the heel: + plum-blossum the heel
    With heel pain along the Kidney channel: + KI-4, KI-5
    With heel pain along the Bladder channel: + UB-59, UB-62
    With painful toes: + Bafeng
    With a headache: + GB-20
    With a fever: + DU-14

TOXIC FIRE

Clinical Manifestations
  • Acute heel pain which is worse with warmth and better with Cold
  • Heat and burning in the heel
  • Rednesws and swelling of the heel
  • Formation of ulcers on the heel, maybe with pus
  • Fever
  • Aversion to Cold
  • Thirst
  • Restlessness
  • Insomnia
  • Yellow urine
  • Constipation
  • T: Red
  • C: Yellow and dry
  • P: Rapid and forceful
Treatment Principle
  • Clear Fire
  • Eliminate toxicity
  • Invigorate the Blood
  • Relieve pain
Herb Formulas
  • Pu Ji Xiao Du Yin  modified
  • Chinese Patent Medicines:
    Feng Shi Xiao Tong Wan
    Niu Huang Jie Du Pian
Points
  • KI-2, LI-4, LI-11, SI-4, SJ-6, SP-6, SP-10, ST-44, UB-60, UB-63
    With redness and swelling of the foot: + GB-43
    With heel pain along the Kidney channel: + KI-4, KI-5
    With itching of the feet: + LIV-2
    With a stabbing pain at the heel: + KI-1
    With a wound on the heel: + Bafeng
    With a fever: + DU-14
    With nervousness: + GB-20
    With throat pain and swelling: + LU-10
    With coughing and expectoration of yellow Phlegm: + LU-5
    With restlessness and insomnia: + HT-8

DAMP-HEAT

Clinical Manifestations
  • Heel pain with a feverish, heavy sensation
  • Redness and swelling of the heel
  • Symptoms worse with warmth and better with Cold
  • Bitter taste in the mouth
  • Poor appetite
  • Fever
  • A feeling of heaviness in the legs
  • Painful urination
  • Loose stools
  • Abdominal pain and distention
  • Deep yellow urine
  • T: Red
  • C: Yellow and greasy at the root
  • P: Slippery and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Eliminate Dampness
  • Clear Heat
  • Harmonize the collaterals
  • Relieve pain
Herb Formulas
  • Si Miao Wan  modified
  • Chinese Patent Medicine: Si Miao Wan
Points
  • GB-34, KI-2, SI-3, SI-4, SJ-6, SP-6, SP-9, UB-63, UB-64
    With a sharp paint: + LI-4, LIV-3
    With a heavy sensation in the feet: + KI-6
    With heel pain along the Kidney channel: + KI-3, KI-4, KI-5
    With heel pain along the Bladder channel: + UB-59, UB-60, UB-62, UB-65
    With redness on the feet: + SP-10
    With ulcers on the feet: + ST-44
    With itching on the heel: + Bafeng
    With a fever: + DU-14, LI-11
    With a poor appetite: + REN-12, SP-4
    With leukorrhea and itching in the genital area: + GB-41, LIV-5

QI AND BLOOD STAGNATION

Clinical Manifestations
  • Trauma to the heel with swollen muscles or tendons
  • Long duration of heel pain
  • A history of injury or surgery on the heel
  • Stabbing pain with a fixed location
  • Pain worsens at night
  • Rigidity of the ankle joints
  • A purplish color on the skin of the heel
  • T: Purplish
  • C: Thin
  • P: Wiry and choppy
Treatment Principle
  • Promote the circulation of Qi and Blood
  • Eliminate Blood Stasis
  • Relieve pain
Herb Formulas
  • Fu Yuan Huo Xue Tang
  • Chinese Patent Medicines:
    Jin Gu Die Shang Wan
    Xiao Huo Luo Dan
Points
  • GB-34, LI-4, LIV-3, SI-3, SI-4, SP-6, UB-60
    With heel pain along the Kidney channel: + KI-3, KI-4, KI-5
    With heel pain along the Bladder channel: + UB-59, UB-60, UB-62, UB-63, UB-64
    C     Bafeng
    With with a purplish color to the feet: + UB-67
    With a bone fracture in the foot: + GB-39, KI-3
    With red spots on the feet: + SP-10
    With depression: + LIV-14, REN-17
    With a cold foot or aversion to Cold: + ST-36, KI-3
    With restlessness and insomnia: + HT-3, PC-6

QI AND BLOOD DEFICIENCY

Clinical Manifestations
  • Heel pain with a dull sensation
  • Symotoms worse with fatigue and exertion and alleviated with rest
  • A slight, diffused tenderness on the heel
  • Lower back pain
  • Fatigue
  • Lassitude
  • Loose stools
  • Poor appetite
  • Dizziness
  • Pale face
  • Weak voice
  • Shortness of breath
  • Reluctance to speak
  • Sweating with little or no exertion
  • Palpitations
  • Insomnia
  • Dry skin
  • Dry hair
  • Poor memory
  • Paleness
  • Scanty periods
  • Nycturia
  • T: Pale or Pale, thin and dry
  • C: Thin and white
  • P: Weak or Thin and choppy
Treatment Principle
  • Tonify Qi
  • Nourish the Blood
  • Nourish the muscles
  • Alleviate pain
Herb Formulas
  • Ba Zhen Tang  modified
  • Chinese Patent Medicine: Shi Quan Da Bu Wan
Points
  • GB-34, GB-39, SI-3, SI-4, SP-6, ST-36, UB-63, UB-64
    With heel pain along the Kidney channel: + KI-3, KI-4, KI-5
    With a poor appetite: + REN-12, SP-3
    With dizziness : + DU-20, GB-20
    With diarrhea : + SP-9

lIVER AND KIDNEY YIN DEFICIENCY

Clinical Manifestations
  • Heel pain with difficulty in ratation, flexion and extension
  • Symptoms aggravated by overstrain or standing too long
  • Symptoms relieved by rest
  • Weakness of the knees and ankles
  • Rigidity in the morning
  • Joint deformity
  • Muscular atrophy
  • Fatigue
  • Aversion to Cold
  • Facial complexion pale and sallow
  • Both cheeks slightly red
  • Dizziness
  • Dry eyes
  • Blurred vision
  • Spots before the eyes
  • Numbness of limbs
  • Flaccid paralysis (Wei syndrome), weakness
  • Tinnitus
  • Deafness
  • Forgetfulness
  • Low back and knees sore and painful
  • Throat dry and painful
  • Five Sole Heat
  • Low-grade fever
  • Night sweats
  • Irritability
  • Insomnia
  • Stools difficult to pass
  • Constipation
  • Men:
    Spermatorrhea
  • Women:
    Irregular menses or
    Scanty menses or
    Amenorrhea
    Vaginal discharge
  • T: Red
  • C: None
  • P: Thready and rapid
Treatment Principle
  • Nourish and Reinforce the Liver and Kidneys
  • Strengthe the tendons and bones
  • Unblock the channels
  • Alleviate pain
Herb Formulas
  • Liu Wei Di Huang Wan  modified
  • Chinese Patent Medicines:
    Liu Wei Di Huang Wan
    Hai Ma Bu Shen Wan
    Kang Gu Zhong Sheng Pian
Points
  • GB-34, GB-39, KI-3, KI-6, KI-7, LIV-3, SI-3, SP-6, ST-36, UB-63
    With heel pain along the Kidney channel: + KI-4, KI-5
    With heel pain that is occasionally influenced by weather changes: + SJ-5
    With an aversion to exposing the heel to Cold: + moxa or moxa needle KI-3, REN-6
    With weakness of the low back, knees and legs: + UB-23
    With impotence: + REN-4
    For abdominal pain with a cold sensation: + ST-25
    With frequent nocturnal urination: + KI-10
    With fatigue and shortness of breath due to Kidney Deficiency: + DU-20
