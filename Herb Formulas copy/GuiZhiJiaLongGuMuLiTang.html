HERB FORMULAS

GUI ZHI JIA LONG GU MU LI TANG - 桂枝加龍骨牡蠣湯 - CINNAMON TWIG DECOCTION PLUS DRAGON BONE AND OYSTER SHELL

English: Cinnamon Twig Decoction Plus Dragon Bone and Oyster Shell
Also Known As: Cinnamon and Dragon Bone Combination
Cinnamon and DB Combination

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Ram. Cinnamomi Gui Zhi 4.5-9g Releases the muscle layer, assists the Heart Yang, warms and facilitates the flow of Yang Qi in the chest, assists the Yang, adjusts the Ying and Wei and warms and facilitates the flow of Qi through the channels and collaterals and Blood through the vessels.
With Bai Shao, warms and tonifies the Middle Jiao.
With Long Gu and Mu Li, for irritability and insomnia due to floating Yang in the Upper Jiao and injured Yin in the Lower Jiao due to Heart Yang Deficiency.
Rx. Paeoniae Alba Bai Shao 4.5-9g Astringes Yin, adjusts the Ying and Wei, softens the Liver and relieves pain . With Gan Cao, regulates the relationship between the Liver and Spleen, nourishes sinews and treats abdominal pain due to Disharmony between the Liver and Stomach and epigastric, hypochondriac or flank pain.
With Gui Zhi, Gan Cao and Mu Li, for Yang Deficiency with unstable exterior and muscle layers.
With Long Gu and Mu Li, for spontaneous sweating and night sweats due to Yin Deficiency.
With Gui Zhi, Sheng Jiang and Da Zao, for Wind-Cold with Deficiency and perspiration.
Fossilia Ossis Mastodi Long Gu 4.5-30g Settles anxiety, calms the Spirit, calms the Liver, prevents leakage of fluids and anchors and preserves Floating Yang.
With Gui Zhi and Gan Cao, for spermatorrhea, diarrhea, irritability vaginal discharge, and excessive uterine bleeding due to Kidney and Spleen Yang Deficiencies.
Concha Ostreae Mu Li 4.5-30g Heavily settles and calms the Spirit, calms the Liver, prevents leakage of fluids benefits the Yin and anchors Floating Yang.
With Gui Zhi and Long Gu, for restlessness, irritability, palpitations and spontaneous sweating due to Floating Yang.
With Long Gu and Bai Shao, for anxiety, palpitations, insomnia, dream disturbed sleep, headache, dizziness and tinnitus and other symptoms associated with Yin Deficiency with Yang Rising.
Rz. Zingiberis Recens Sheng Jiang 4.5-9g Adjusts the Ying and Wei - normalizes the flow of Qi at the center.
With Da Zao, protects the Stomach Qi and reduces irritation of the gastrointestinal. tract by other herbs and adjusts the Ying and Wei.
With Gui Zhi and Da Zao, harmonizes the Ying and Wei to drive out pathogens.
Fr. Jujube Da Zao 4.5g Nourishes the Blood, calms the Spirit and moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 3-6g Tonifies the Spleen and augments Qi and moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs.
