HERB FORMULAS

ZUO GUI YIN - 左归片 - RESTORE THE LEFT (KIDNEY) DRINK

English: Restore the Left (Kidney) Drink
Also Known As: Replenish the Yin-Fluid Decoction

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Rehmanniae Preparata Shu Di Huang 6-60g Tonifies the Kidneys and nourishes Jing, Yin and Blood.
With Shan Zhu Yu, nourishes Kidney Yin and secures Jing, while restraining urination and sweating.
Rx. Dioscoreae Shan Yao 3-15g Strengthens the Spleen , stabilizes the Kidneys and astringes Jing.
With Shu Di Huang and Shan Zhu Yu, for Kidney Deficiency with spermatorrhea, dizziness, tinnitus, lower back pain, impotence and night sweats.
Fr. Lycii Gou Qi Zi 6-15g Nourishes and tonifies Liver and Kidney Blood and Yin.
With Shu Di Huang, for Liver and Kidney Deficiency.
Poria Fu Ling 4.5-12g Strengthens the Spleen and drains Dampness.
Fr. Corni Shan Zhu Yu 3-9g Nourishes Kidney Yin and Yang, astringes Jing and Yin and stops perspiration.
With Shan Yao, tonifies the Liver and Spleen.
With Shu Di Huang and Shan Yao, for urinary frequency (day or night), dizziness, tinnitus and lower back pain due to Kidney Deficiency.
With Shu Di Huang, Gou Qi Zi and Shan Yao, for true Yin Deficiency.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Preparata Zhi Gan Cao 3-6g Moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Nourishes Yin
  • Tonifies the Kidneys

SYNDROMES

  • Kidney Yin and Jing Deficiency
  • Atrophy disorder (Wei Syndrome) due to Liver and Kidney Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Lower back soreness
  • Spontaneous emissions
  • Spermatorrhea
  • Night sweats
  • A dry mouth and throat
  • Thirst with a desire to drink
  • T: Red
  • C: Peeled and shiny
  • P: Thready and rapid

TREATS

  • Menopause syndrome
  • Diabetes
  • Renal tuberculosis
  • Lacrimation
  • Myasthenia gravis
  • Multiple sclerosis
  • Polyneuritis
  • Guillain-Barre disease
  • Acute myelitis (inflammation of the spinal cord)
  • Progressive muscular dystrophy
  • The sequelae of poliomyelitis
  • Night sweats
  • Spermatorrhea
  • Constipation
  • Atrophy (Wei) Syndrome
  • Lumbago
  • Addison's disease
  • Amenorrhea
  • Alopecia
  • Progressive myodystrophy
  • Progressive myodystrophia
  • Hysterical paralysis
  • Periodic paralysis
  • Facial paralysis
  • Acute infectious multiple neuritis
  • Impotence
  • Osteoporosis
  • Nocturnal emissions
  • Post-menopausal uterine bleeding
