HERB FORMULAS

BA ZHENG SAN - 八正散 - EIGHT HERB POWDER FOR RECTIFICATION

English: Eight Herb Powder for Rectification
Also Known As: Dianthus Formula
Powder of Eight Ingredients
Powder for Dispersing Heat and Promoting Urination

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Caul. Akebiae Mu Tong 3-10g Promotes urination, unblocks painful urinary dysfunction, clears Damp obstruction and drains Heart Heat through the Small Intestine.
With Hua Shi, Che Qian Zi and Zhi Zi, for painful urinary dribbling due to Damp-Heat in the Bladder.
With Che Qian Zi, for Heart Fire Transferring to the Small Intestine channel causing edema and painful urinary dysfunction.
Talcum Hua Shi 3-30g Drains Heat from the Bladder, aids expulsion of stones, expels Damp-Heat and stops bleeding due to Heat.
With Mu Tong, for Heat Lin.
With Zhi Zi and Mu Tong, for Lin Syndrome with hot, painful urinary dribbling and scanty, dark, painful urination.
With Che Qian Zi, Qu Mai and Bian Xu, clears Damp-Heat by promoting urination.
With Mu Tong, Che Qian Zi and Zhi Zi, for Damp-Heat dysuria with pain and cloudy urine.
Sm. Plantaginis Che Qian Zi 1.5-15g Promotes urination and clears Heat.
With Mu Tong and Hua Shi, for Lin Syndrome.
Hb. Dianthi Qu Mai 3-12g Promotes urination, drains Damp-Heat from the Bladder and unblocks painful urinary dysfunction.
With Hua Shi and Mu Tong, for Lin Syndrome.
With Zhi Zi, for Lower Jiao Damp-Heat with burning, painful, dribbling urination.
With Che Qian Zi and Hua Shi, for Heat dysuria (Re Lin) with burning and pain during urination.
Hb. Polygoni Avicularis Bian Xu 3-12g Promotes urination, drains Damp-Heat from the Bladder and eliminates painful urination.
With Qu Mai and Che Qian Zi, for Damp-Heat in Lower Jiao or Stone Lin with urinary discomfort.
With Qu Mai, for painful urinary dysfunction.
With Mu Tong, Che Qian Zi, Zhi Zi and Hua Shi, for Damp-Heat dysuria (Shi-Re Lin).
Fr. Gardeniae Zhi Zi 3-10g Drains Heat from the three Jiaos through the urine and stops bleeding.
With Hua Shi, for painful urinary dysfunction due to Damp-Heat in the Bladder.
With Hua Shi, Bian Xu and Che Qian Zi, for urinary tract infection.
Rx. and Rz. Rhei Preparata Zhi Da Huang 1-10g Drains Damp-Heat and clears Heat through the stool.
With Che Qian Zi and Bian Xu, for hot, painful urinary dysfunction.
With Che Qian Zi, Mu Tong and Zhi Zi, for hot, painful urinary dysfunction.
With Zhi Zi, for constipation.
Medulla Junci Deng Xin Cao 1-9g Promotes urination, leaches out Dampness, unblocks painful urinary dysfunction, clears Heat from the Heart channel by directing Fire downward and calms the Spirit.
With Hua Shi, for Damp-Heat in the Heart, Lung and Bladder channels causing incomplete, painful urination.
With Mu Tong, for difficult, scanty and burning associated with Heart channel Heat shifting to the Large Intestine or Heat clumping in the Bladder or Damp-Heat lodging in the Lower Jiao.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Preparata Zhi Gan Cao 1.5-9g Harmonizes, relieves abdominal pain, clears Heat and relieves Fire toxicity.
With Hua Shi, relieves irritability.
(Rx. Glycyrrhizae tips) (Gan Cao Shao) (1.5-9g) (Strengthens the diuretic action of the formula.)

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Heat
  • Purges Fire
  • Promotes urination
  • Unblocks painful urinary dysfunction

SYNDROMES

  • Bladder Damp-Heat
  • Stone Lin
  • Blood Lin
  • Heat in Heart Channel Transferring to the Small Intestine Channel

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Dark, turbid, scanty, difficult urination or
  • Frequent, urgent and painful
  • Urination may be red
  • Maybe fever
  • Sores in mouth, on tongue or throat
  • Dry mouth and throat
  • Maybe urinary retention (severe)
  • Maybe lower abdominal distention and pain (severe)
  • Thirst
  • Maybe total obstruction
  • Parched throat
  • Severe sore throat
  • Maybe anuria (severe)
  • Dribbling urination
  • May be profuse whitish, yellow vaginal discharge
  • Maybe be urinary calculi
  • Constipation
  • Eye redness and pain
  • Epistaxis
  • Distending lower back pain
  • Irritability
  • Scorched lips
  • T: Red
  • C: Thick, yellow and greasy
  • P: Slippery and rapid or Smooth and rapid

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with chronic Lin Syndrome
  • Contraindicated during pregnancy
  • Long term use may produce weakness, palpitations, light headedness and loss of appetite
  • Contraindicated for those with chronic disease with weakness.
  • Contraindicated for those with Deficiency Cold.
