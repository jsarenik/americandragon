HERB FORMULAS

QING WEN BAI DU YIN - 清瘟敗毒飲 - CLEAR EPIDEMICS AND OVERCOME TOXIN DECOCTION

English: Clear Epidemics and Overcome Toxin Decoction
Also Known As: Decoction for Clearing Away Pestilent Factors and Detoxification
Antipyretic and Antitoxic Decoction, Antiphlogistic Decoction for Epidemic Diseases

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Gypsum Fibrosum Shi Gao 20-240g Clears Heat in the Qi Stage and drains Fire.
 With Zhi Mu, for high fever from Wind-Heat or Qi Stage Heat or Yang Ming Heat with irritability and extreme thirst.
With Sheng Di Huang, for excessive Fire with injured fluids.
With Xuan Shen and Shui Niu Jiao, for Heat at the Qi and Blood Stages with skin eruptions and echymosis.
With Shui Niu Jiao, for epidemic Damp-Heat toxin with high fever, loss of consciousness, epistaxis, and rash.
With Sheng Di Huang and Zhi Mu, for Stomach Fire.
With Sheng Di Huang, Mu Dan Pi and Xuan Shen, for excessive Heat in the Qi and Xue Levels with continuous high fever and maculae.
With Huang Lian, Zhi Zi, Mu Dan Pi, Chi Shao and Zhi Mu, for influenza, epidemic poliomyelitis and epidemic encephalitis B with high fever, headache irritability dry mouth insomnia and in severe cases, bleeding
Rx. Anemarrhenae Zhi Mu 3-18g Clears Heat from the Qi Stage.
With Xuan Shen and Sheng Di Huang, for ulcers of the oral cavity.
With Huang Lian, for Blazing Stomach Fire with intense hunger, dry stools, dry, yellow tongue coat and a slippery rapid pulse.
With Gan Cao, regulates Cold and Heat, harmonizes the Exterior and Interior and treats malarial diseases with Tai Yin turbid Dampness fuming upward (irritability, Heat, profuse sweating).
With Huang Qin, for cough with Excess Lung Heat.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 1.5-12g Clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity and moderates and harmonizes the properties of other herbs.
With Jie Geng, for pain and swelling of the throat.
Hb. Lophatheri Dan Zhu Ye 1.5-12g Clears Heat and Eliminates irritability.
 With Shi Gao, for lingering fever from febrile disease with insomnia, irritability and a scanty dry tongue coat, cough with fever in children and Heart and Stomach Heat with mouth ulcers and swollen gums
With Shi Gao and Zhi Mu, for febrile disease with irritability and thirst.
Cornu Bubali Shui Niu Jiao 30-120g Relieves Fire toxicity and cools the Blood.
With Sheng Di Huang, for loss of consciousness and delirium, with epistaxis, hematemesis, or purpura due to Blood Heat.
With Huang Lian and Xuan Shen, for high fever, loss of consciousness, delirium, hematemesis, epistaxis, and purpura associated with warm febrile disease.
With Shi Gao and Xuan Shen, for high fever which worsens at night, dark-red skin blotches, thirst, and a rapid pulse due to Fire in the Qi and Blood levels.
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 6-30g Clears Heat and cools the Blood.
 With Shui Niu Jiao, Mu Dan Pi and Chi Shao, for Toxic Heat in the Blood.
With Shui Niu Jiao and Mu Dan Pi, for rashes due to Blood Heat.
With Shui Niu Jiao and Xuan Shen, for Heat in the Ying Stage.
With Mu Dan Pi, for Ying and Xue Stage Heat with a high fever, scarlet tongue, thirst, a rash all over the body, epistaxis and hemoptysis, residual Warm pathogen which has injured the Yin and Kidney Yin Deficiency with steaming bones.
With Xuan Shen, for Excess or Deficiency Heat patterns, late-stage febrile disease with irritability, thirst and constipation and Kidney Yin Deficiency with Fire Blazing with soreness and swelling in the throat, dry mouth and a parched tongue.
With Mu Dan Pi and Chi Shao, for purpura or bleeding with maculae and blotches on the skin.
Cx. Moutan Mu Dan Pi 3-18g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, drains pus and reduces swelling.
 With Chi Shao, cools the Blood and disperses Stasis, for Ying Stage Heat with epistaxis, hemoptysis, hemafecia, hematuria, menorrhagia, purpuric rash, hematemesis, and Heat injuring the Ying and Xue levels, Blood Stasis disorders (amenorrhea, menstrual pain, traumatic injury) and irregular menstruation due to Heat and obstruction from Blood Deficiency.
With Chi Shao and Sheng Di Huang, for hematemesis and epistaxis due to Heat in the Xue level.
With Zhi Mu and Sheng Di Huang, for menopause with Yin Deficiency and Heat.
Rx. Paeoniae Rubra Chi Shao 3-18g Clears Heat and cools the Blood.
Rx. Scrophulariae Xuan Shen 3-18g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, drains Fire and relieves toxicity.
 With Mu Dan Pi, for erysipelas and purplish rashes.
With Shi Gao, Shui Niu Jiao and Zhi Mu, for Excess Toxic Heat with maculae and eruptions.
With Sheng Di Huang, Shui Niu Jiao and Lian Qiao, for Ying Stage Heat.
With Shi Gao, Shui Niu Jiao and Zhi Mu, for Excess Toxic Heat with maculae and eruptions.
Rz. Coptidis Huang Lian 3-18g Drains Fire and Relieves toxicity.
 With Huang Qin and Zhi Zi, for high fever, irritability, dry mouth and throat, and dark urine due to Fire Toxin in the three burners.
With Sheng Di Huang, for Insomnia and delirium due to unremitting Heat injuring the Ying level.
With Zhi Zi and Shi Gao, for Ying Stage Heat with delirium and loss of consciousness.
With Huang Qin, drains pathogenic Heat from the Upper and Middle Jiaos for high fever, redness, swelling and pain of the head and face, dry mouth, parched throat, ulcerations of the mouth and tongue, epistaxis, rashes and irritability in warm-febrile disease, explosive diarrhea and dysentery with burning pain in the anus or fitted, filthy stools and abdominal pain and nausea in pregnancy with Heat disturbing the fetus.
Rx. Scutellariae Huang Qin 1.5-12g Drains Fire and Detoxifies.
with Zhi Zi, powerfully clears and drains lurking Heat from the Lungs with fever, fullness in the chest, cough with yellow sputum and epistaxis or hematemesis, treats Damp-Heat and jaundice, sores due to Heat toxin and irregular vaginal bleeding due to Liver Fire.
With Shi Gao, Zhi Mu and Mu Dan Pi, for Excess Heat with persistent high fever and irritability.
Fr. Gardeniae Zhi Zi 3-18g Clears Heat, reduces Fire, eliminates irritability, cools the Blood and relieves toxicity.
With Lian Qiao, for Heat entering the Pericardium, and Heat remaining in the Heart channel with sores in the mouth or on the tongue, dark, scanty urine or urinary discomfort
With Huang Lian, Huang Qin and Lian Qiao, for boils and carbuncles.
With Mu Dan Pi, resolves Qi Level constraint leading to Heat and Heat in the Blood, treats irregular menstruation, headache, red eyes, and thirst due to Liver Qi Heat from constraint and dysmenorrhea, headache, dry, scratchy eyes and intercostal pain due to Liver Blood Deficiency.
Fr. Forsythiae Lian Qiao 3-18g Clears Heat, relieves toxicity, clears Blood Heat and directs the formula to the upper body.
With Shi Gao, Sheng Di Huang and Xuan Shen, for Heart Fire.
Rx. Platycodi Jie Geng 1.5-12g Expels pus, benefits the throat and directs the effects of other herbs to the upper body.
With Gan Cao, disseminates Lung Qi, expels Phlegm, resolves toxicity, and expels pus for productive cough with hoarseness, pain and swelling of the throat due to Wind-Heat, Lung abscess with expectoration of pus and pain in the chest and flanks and Excess disorders in the Upper Jiao with no disorder in the Lower Jiao.
With Huang Qin and Gan Cao, for hoarseness, pain and swelling of the throat due to Wind-Heat (stronger action).

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Heat
  • Relieves toxicity
  • Cools the Blood
  • Drains Fire
  • Stops bleeding

SYNDROMES

  • Severe Fire in the Qi and Blood (Xue) stages

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Intense fever
  • Strong thirst
  • Dry heaves
  • Severe, stabbing headache
  • Extreme irritability
  • Delirious speech (severe)
  • Rash (severe)
  • Epistaxis (severe)
  • Maculae
  • Mania
  • Convulsions
  • Lips chapped, dark and scorched
  • Coma
  • Hematemesis
  • Extreme coldness of the extremities
  • Restlessness
  • Spasms or convulsions
  • Blurred vision
  • T: Dark red
  • C: Dry or prickly
  • P: Deep, thin and rapid or Floating, large and rapid

  • Behcet's syndrome
  • Nasal Furuncles
  • Influenza
  • Oliguria
  • Epidemic hemorrhagic fever
  • Hematemesis
  • Perspiration
  • Eclampsia
  • Rheumatic fever
  • Acute leukemia
  • Uremia
  • Leptospirosis
  • Necrotizing enterocolitis
  • Facial erysipelas
  • Measles
  • Epidemic hemorrhagic fever
  • Viral pneumonia
  • Nephritis
  • Dermatological disorders
  • Epistaxis
  • Kidney failure

  • Systemic lupus erythematosus

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with Yang Deficiency.
  • Contraindicated for those with weakness of the Spleen and Stomach.
