HERB FORMULAS

XIAO HE SAN - 小合散 - REDUCE MASSES POWDER

English: Reduce Masses Powder
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Bupleuri Chai Hu 10g Spreads Liver Qi, relieves Stagnation and resolves Phlegm and congestion.
With Bai Shao, for vertigo, dizziness, thoracic and costal pain, due to Liver Qi Stagnation.
Rx. Paeoniae Rubra Chi Shao 10g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis,clears Heat, cools the Blood and relieves pain.
With Bai Shao, for spasms and cramping pain associated with Blood Deficiency and Blood Stagnation.
Rx. Paeoniae Alba Bai Shao 15g Nourishes the Blood, regulates menstruation, astringes Yin, adjusts the Ying and Wei, softens the Liver and relieves pain.
With Gan Cao, for epigastric, hypochondriac or flank pain.
Rx. Salviae Miltiorrhizae Dan Shen 15g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, cools the Blood, reduces abscesses, nourishes the Blood and calms the Spirit.
Per. Citri Reticulatae Viride Qing Pi 10g Spreads Liver Qi, breaks up Stagnant Qi, dissipates accumulations, dries Dampness and transforms Phlegm.
With Bai Jie Zi, for cough with chest and flank pain due to congested fluids.
With Chai Hu, restores the Qi circulation between the Upper and Lower Jiaos to treat long-term Qi Stagnation moving to Blood Stasis with fixed pain and occasional palpable lumps.
Rx. Linderae Wu Yao 10g Promotes the movement of Qi and alleviates pain.
Sm. Sinapis Bai Jie Zi 15g Promotes Qi movement, dissipates nodules, unblocks the collaterals and alleviates pain.
Hb. Scutellariae Barbatae Ban Zhi Lian 15g Clears Heat, resolves Fire toxins, reduces swellings, invigorates the Blood, mildly dispels Blood Stasis and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Bai Hua She She Cao and Shan Ci Gu, for cancer.
Pseudobulbus Cremastrae/Pleiones Shan Ci Gu 15g Clears Heat, resolves toxicity, reduces abscesses, reduces swelling, dissipates nodules and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Concha Ostreae Mu Li 20g Calms the Liver, benefits Yin, anchors Floating Yang, softens hardness, dissipates nodules and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Hb. Hedyotis Diffusae Bai Hua She She Cao 20g Clears Heat, strongly relieves Fire toxins, reduces abscesses and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 5g Resolves Phlegm, moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs and guides the herbs to all twelve channels.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Moves Qi
  • Breaks up Stagnation

SYNDROMES

  • Qi and Blood Stagnation

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • A sensation of oppression in the chest
  • A feeling of distention in the hypochondrium
  • The chest pain is worse just prior to and after menstruation
  • The pain is worse with emotional upset
  • Breast masses that are moderately hard and painful on palpation
  • Pale red or Blood-tinged secretions from the nipple
  • Pain that is fixed, stabbing and/or severe or
  • Pains that move around
  • Distention
  • Depression
  • Irritability
  • Pan is worse with pressure
  • Dark complexion
  • Fixed, palpable masses
  • Bleeding with clots
  • Purple lips
  • Purple nails
  • Dry skin
  • Blue, dark or purplish discoloration of skin in small or large areas,
  • Varicose and spider veins
  • Cold hands and feet
  • Tendency to hemorrhage dark blood or clots
  • T: Purple, possible purple or red spots
  • C: White
  • P: Choppy or Wiry and choppy

TREATS

  • Fibrocystic breast disease
