HERB FORMULAS

GAN LU XIAO DU DAN - 甘露消毒丹 - SWEET DEW SPECIAL PILL TO ELIMINATE TOXIN

English: Sweet Dew Special Pill to Eliminate Toxin
Also Known As: Antiphlogistic Pills of Dew
Pill Like Manna for Detoxification
Pu Ji Jie Du Dan Universal Benefit Special Pill to Resolve Toxicity

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Fr. Forsythia Lian Qiao 5-15g Clears Heat, especially in the Upper Jiao and relieves toxicity.
Rx. Scutellariae Huang Qin 5-15g Clears Lung Heat, dries Dampness, drains Fire and detoxifies.
Hb. Menthae Haplocalycis Bo He 3-9g Disperses Wind-Heat, cools and clears the head and eyes, benefits the throat and expels turbid filth.
With Lian Qiao, Huo Xiang and Bai Dou Kou, for vomiting and diarrhea due to Summerheat and Dampness.
Rz. Belamcandae She Gan 5-12g Clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity, improves the throat, transforms Phlegm and clears the Lungs.
With Huang Qin, for sore throat and cough due to Heat in the Lungs.
Bul. Fritillariae Cirrhosae Chuan Bei Mu 6-10g Clears Heat, transforms Phlegm, stops cough and nourishes and moistens the Lung.
With Lian Qiao, releases constrained toxins.
Talcum Hua Shi 15-30g Promotes urination and drains Heat from the Bladder. expels Damp-Heat and releases Summerheat.
With Mu Tong, for Heat Lin.
Caulis Akebiae Mu Tong 5-12g Promotes urination.
Hb. Artemisiae Scopariae Yin Chen Hao 10-30g Clears Damp-Heat from the Liver and Gallbladder, relieves jaundice and clears and releases Dampness in the the exterior.
With Hua Shi and Huang Qin, for Summerheat or Warm febrile disease causing urinary dysfunction and jaundice.
Hb. Agastaches/ Pogostemonis Huo Xiang 5-12g Aromatically transforms Dampness, releases the exterior, dispels Summerheat, transforms turbidity, harmonizes the Middle Jiao and stops vomiting.
With Shi Chang Pu and Bai Dou Kou, eliminates turbidity and revives the Spleen.
With Huang Qin, Yin Chen Hao and Hua Shi, for early stage Damp-Warm febrile disease with fever, achy limbs, lethargy, abdominal distention and dark, scanty urine and constipation and Summerheat with Dampness.
Rz. Acori Tatarinowii Shi Chuang Pu 4-10g Harmonizes the Middle Jiao and transforms turbid Dampness.
Fr. Amomi Rotundus Bai Dou Kou 5-12g Aromatically transforms Dampness, warms the Middle Jiao, descends Qi and stops vomiting.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Resolves Dampness
  • Transforms turbidity
  • Clears Heat
  • Relieves toxicity

SYNDROMES

  • Qi Stage Damp-Heat (Spleen/Stomach)
  • Summer Heat with Damp
  • San Jiao - Middle Jiao Phlegm-Heat
  • San Jiao - Middle Jiao Damp Stagnation
  • San Jiao - Middle Jiao Damp-Heat
