HERB FORMULAS

GU YIN JIAN - 股荫煎 - STABILIZE THE YIN DECOCTION

English: Stabilize the Yin Decoction
Also Known As: Decoction for Keeping Yin

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Ginseng Ren Shen 3-10g Tonifies Qi.
With Shu Di Huang, for Qi and Yin Deficiency.
(Rx. Codonopsis) (Dang Shen) (3-20g) (Substitutes for Ren Shen.)
Rx. Rehmanniae Preparata Shu Di Huang 9-20g Nourishes the Blood, nourishes Liver and Kidney Yin, nourishes Jing and Blood and fills the Marrow.
With Shan Zhu Yu, nourishes Kidney Yin and secures Jing, while restraining urination and sweating
With Shan Zhu Yu and Shan Yao, for dizziness, weak and sore back, impotence, sperma-torrhea and night sweats, due to Kidney and Liver Yin Deficiency.
With Ren Shen, for Qi and Yin Deficiency.
Rz. Dioscoreae Shan Yao 6-12g Tonifies Kidney Yin and astringes Jing.
With Shu Di Huang and Shan Zhu Yu, for Kidney Deficiency with spermatorrhea and night sweats.
With Shan Zhu Yu and Tu Si Zi, for leukorrhea due to Spleen and Kidney Deficiency.
Fr. Corni Shan Zhu Yu 4.5-10g Stabilizes the Kidneys, astringes Jing and Body Fluids, tonifies Liver and Kidney Yin, strengthens Kidney Yang, stabilizes the menses and stops bleeding.
Rx. Polygalae Yuan Zhi 2.1-3g Calms the Spirit, sedates the Heart and clears the channel joining the Heart and Kidney Expels Phlegm, opens the Orifices.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Preparata Zhi Gan Cao 3-6g Tonifies the Spleen and augments Qi and moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs.
With Ren Shen, relieves palpitations and anxiety.
Fr. Schisandrae Wu Wei Zi 3-6g Tonifies the Kidneys, astringes Jing, stops diarrhea and spermatorrhea, tonifies Qi and quiets the Spirit and calms and contains Heart Qi.
With Shu Di Huang, Shan Zhu Yu and Shan Yao, for Lung and Kidney Deficiency with cough and wheezing.
Sm. Cuscutae Tu Si Zi 6-10g Strengthens Yang, nourishes Yin, astringes Jing and urine, benefits the marrow, tonifies the Kidneys and Liver and calms the fetus.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Enriches and tonifies the Kidneys
  • Nourishes the Liver
  • Regulates the Chong and Ren channels
  • Regulates menstruation
  • Strengthens the Spleen

SYNDROMES

  • Early or Late menstruation due to Kidney Deficiency
  • Amenorrhea due to Kidney and Spleen Yang Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Early menstruation
  • Late menstruation
  • Scanty menses
  • Light-colored menses
  • Menses may be mixed with leukorrhea
  • Low back pain that can be severe
  • Soft stools
  • Distention in the lower abdomen
  • A sense of prolapse in the lower abdomen
  • Anorexia
  • Fatigue
  • Light headedness
  • Dizziness
  • Frequent erotic dreams
  • Tinnitus
  • Urinary frequency at night
  • Weakness in the lower limbs
  • Vertigo
  • T: Pale
  • C: Thin
  • P: Deep and frail

TREATS

  • Early menstruation
  • Late menstruation
  • Chronic cervicitis
  • Chronic pelvic cellulitis
  • Irregular menstruation
  • Leukorrhagia
  • Neurasthenia
  • Metrorrhagia
  • Amenorrhea
