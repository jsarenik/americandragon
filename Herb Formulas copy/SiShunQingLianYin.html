HERB FORMULAS

SI SHUN QING LIAN YIN - 四顺淸敛饮 - FOUR FAVORABLE INGREDIENTS HEAT CLEARING DRINK

English: Four Favorable Ingredients Heat Clearing Drink
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Saposhnikoviae Fang Feng 9g Releases the Exterior, expels Wind-Dampness and alleviates pain.
With Qiang Huo, for Wind-Dampness.
With Qiang Huo and Dang Gui, for Wind-Cold-Damp Bi.
Fr. Gardeniae Zhi Zi 9g Clears Heat, reduces Fire, eliminates irritability, drains Damp-Heat, cools the Blood, relieves toxicity, invigorates the Blood, reduces swelling and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Da Huang, for constipation.
Fr. Forsythiae Lian Qiao 12g Clears Heat especially in the Upper Jiao, relieves toxicity, reduces abscesses, dissipates clumps, clears Blood Heat and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. et Rz. Notopterygii Qing Hao 6g Clears Damp-Heat, cools the Blood, stops bleeding and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. et Rz. Rhei Da Huang 6g Drains Heat, purges accumulations, transforms Dampness, promotes urination, cools and invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, eliminates Phlegm and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Dang Gui, for Blood Stasis.
Rx. Paeoniae Rubra Chi Shao 10g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, relieves pain, clears Heat, cools the Blood, reduces swelling from sores and abscesses and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 12g Tonifies, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood, reduces swelling, expels pus, generates flesh and alleviates pain.
With Jin Yin Hua, reduces obstruction and toxicity for swollen, painful sores and necrosis of surrounding tissue.
Flos Lonicerae Jin Yin Hua 30g Clears Heat, resolves Fire toxicity, vents and disperses External Wind-Heat and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Lian Qiao, for various Heat disorders.
Fr. Trichosanthis Gua Lou 15g Clears Lung Heat, transforms Hot Phlegm, regulates Qi (unbinds the chest), reduces abscesses, dissipates nodules and eliminates pus.
Medulla Junci Deng Xin Cao 3g Clears Lung Heat, promotes urination, leaches out Dampness, cools the Blood and stops bleeding.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 3g Clears Heat and relieves Fire toxicity, inhibits the growth of cancer cells, moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs and guides the herbs to all twelve channels

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Releases the Exterior
  • Expels Wind-Dampness
  • Alleviates pain
  • Clears Lung Heat
  • Clears Damp-Heat
  • Cools the Blood
  • Resolves Fire toxicity

SYNDROMES

  • Toxic Wind-Damp-Heat

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Lung cancer
  • Edema in the neck
  • Fever
  • Dyspnea
  • P: Rapid

TREATS

  • Lung cancer with edema in the neck
