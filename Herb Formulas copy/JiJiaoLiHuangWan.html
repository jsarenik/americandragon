HERB FORMULAS

JI JIAO LI HUANG WAN - 己椒藶簧囊 - STEPHANIA, ZANTHOXYLUM, TINGLI AND RHUBARB PILL

English: Stephania, Zanthoxylum, Tingli and Rhubarb Pill
Stephania, Zanthoxylum, Descurainia and Rhubarb Pill
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Stephaniae Tetrandrae Han Fang Ji 30g Promotes urination and reduces edema especially in the lower body.
With Da Huang, Jiao Mu and Ting Li Zi, for dysuria and constipation due to water accumulation.
Sm. Zanthoxyli Bungeani Jiao Mu 30g Promotes diuresis, regulates urination, resolves swelling, eliminates Phlegm and relieves dyspnea.
Sm. Lepidii/Descurainiae Ting Li Zi 30g Drains the Lungs, reduces Phlegm, calms wheezing, circulates water and reduces edema.
With Han Fang Ji, Jiao Mu and Da Huang, for facial edema and fluid accumulation in the chest and abdomen.
Rx. et Rz. Rhei Da Huang 30g Drains Heat, purges accumulations, transforms Dampness, promotes urination, activates the Blood and dispels Blood Stasis.
With Han Fang Ji and Ting Li Zi, for water Accumulation with edema and abdominal fullness.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Dries Dampness
  • Scours out Thin Mucus
  • Moves Qi
  • Reduces distention

SYNDROMES

  • Accumulation of Water in the Stomach and Intestines with Qi Stagnation

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Abdominal distention
  • Borborygmus
  • A dry mouth and tongue
  • Constipation
  • Reduced urination
  • Diarrhea
  • T: Normal
  • C: Yellow and greasy
  • P: Wiry and slippery or Deep, full and slightly rapid

TREATS

  • Pleural effusions
  • Acute bronchial asthma
  • Edema from cardiopulmonary disease
  • Ascites
  • Constipation
  • Pyloric obstruction
  • Rheumatic Heart disease
  • Functional gastrointestinal disorders
  • Diarrhea
  • Cirrhosis of the Liver

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with Spleen Deficiency
