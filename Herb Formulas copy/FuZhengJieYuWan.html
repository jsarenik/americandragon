HERB FORMULAS

FU ZHENG JIE YU WAN

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Ginseng Ren Shen 1-9g Powerfully tonifies Yuan Qi and tonifies Spleen, Stomach, Heart and Lung Qi.
With Huang Qi and Chai Hu, for Qi Deficiency with various types of prolapse due to Sinking of the Clear Yang.
Rx. Astragali Huang Qi 9-30g Tonifies Qi and Blood, strengthens the Spleen, raises the Yang Qi of the Spleen and Stomach Tonifies Wei Qi, tonifies the Lungs, promotes urination, reduces edema, promotes the discharge of pus, generates flesh, expels toxins and relieves numbness and pain.
With Ren Shen, for Qi Deficiency with general debility, anorexia, fatigue, tired extremities and spontaneous sweating.
Rx. Bupleuri Chai Hu 3-12g Spreads Liver Qi, relieves Stagnation and raises Yang Qi.
With Zhi Ke, for a stifling sensation in the chest, abdominal pain, poor appetite and irregular bowel movements.
Rz. Pinelliae Preparatum Zhi Ban Xia 3-15g Dries Dampness,d transforms Phlegm, descends Rebellious Qi and stops vomiting.
With Sheng Jiang, helps eliminate toxins.
With Ren Shen, for vomiting due to Stomach Deficiency.
Rz. Cyperi Xiang Fu 6-14g Spreads and regulates Liver Qi, regulates menstruation and alleviates pain.
With Chai Hu, for Qi Stagnation and menstrual irregularity.
Fr. Aurantii Zhi Ke   Promotes the movement of Qi and reduces distention and pressure.
Rx. Scutellariae Huang Bai 3-12g Drains Damp-Heat especially from the Lower Jiao, drains Fire and relieves Fire toxicity.
Rz. Zingiberis Recens Sheng Jiang 1-10g Warms the Middle, stops vomiting, adjusts the Ying and Wei, normalizes the flow of Qi at the center and reduces the toxicity of other herbs.
With Da Zao, alleviates epigastric pain, nausea and vomiting by strengthening the Spleen, protects Stomach Qi, reduces irritation of the gastrointestinal tract by other herbs, adjusts the Ying and Wei, prevents tonics from generating Dampness in the Middle Jiao, prevents Phlegm accumulation, nourishes the Blood, prevents injury from harsh Blood movers and assists the movement of Qi.
With Zhi Ban Xia, for vomiting due to many causes including Stomach Cold.
Fr. Jujube Da Zao 10-30g Tonifies the Spleen and Stomach, augments Qi, nourishes the Blood, calms the Spirit and moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Preparata Zhi Gan Cao 3-6g Tonifies the Spleen, augments Qi, moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs and guides the herbs to all twelve channels.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Soothes the Liver
  • Clears Stagnation
  • Strengthens the Spleen
  • Tonifies Qi
  • Eliminates Dampness

SYNDROMES

  • Liver Qi Stagnation with Spleen Dampness

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Distention and pain in the sides or flanks
  • Suffocating sensation in the chest
  • Tendency to sigh
  • Melancholy
  • Depression
  • Irritability
  • Inappropriate anger
  • fright
  • Loneliness
  • Irregular menses
  • Dysmenorrhea
  • Breast distention and pain before and during menses
  • Fatigue
  • Abdominal pain
  • Stomach ache
  • Reduced appetite
  • Subcostal bloating
  • Tendency to diarrhea
  • Nervousness
  • Nausea
  • Vomiting
  • T: Pink
  • C: Thin and white or moist
  • P: Wiry or Wiry and soft

TREATS

  • AIDS/HIV
