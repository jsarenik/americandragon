HERB FORMULAS

ZENG YE TANG - 增液湯 - INCREASE THE FLUIDS DECOCTION

English: Increase the Fluids Decoction
Also Known As: Scrophularia and Ophiopogon Combination

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Scrophulariae Xuan Shen 3-30g Nourishes Yin, generates Fluids, clears Heat, cools the Blood, drains Fire and moistens Dryness.
With Sheng Di Huang, for Excess or Deficiency Heat patterns, late-stage febrile disease with irritability, thirst and constipation and Kidney Yin Deficiency with Fire Blazing with soreness and swelling in the throat, dry mouth and a parched tongue
With Mai Men Dong and Sheng Di Huang, for constipation due to Dryness and sore, dry throat due to Deficiency Fire.
Rx. Ophiopogonis Mai Men Dong 3-24g Moistens the Lungs, nourishes the Yin, stops cough, nourishes Stomach Yin, generates fluids and moistens the Intestines.
With Xuan Shen and Sheng Di Huang, for thirst, constipation, irritability and low-grade fever due to Stomach Yin Deficiency in the aftermath of warm febrile disease where to the fluids have been injured and thirst, constipation, irritability and a low-grade fever due to severely injured fluids as in the later stages of a Warm pathogen disease.
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 3-24g Nourishes Yin, clears Heat, cools the Blood, increases saliva and generates Fluids.
With Xuan Shen and Mai Men Dong, for dry throat, irritability, a red tongue, and a fast thready pulse due to Devastated Yin with Blazing Fire, excessive thirst, irritability, and a scarlet tongue due to Ying Stage Heat, swelling of the throat and lymph nodes and constipation due to Excess Heat with Yin Deficiency.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Generates Fluids
  • Moistens Dryness
  • Unblocks the bowels
  • Nourishes Yin
  • Clears Heat

SYNDROMES

  • Deficiency of Fluid in Colon (Yang Ming Xu)
  • Constipation due to Yin Deficiency
  • Ying Stage Heat with Middle Jiao Dryness

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Thirst
  • Constipation
  • Dry throat
  • Dry nose
  • Dizziness
  • T: Red, dry and thin
  • C: None or Yellow and dry
  • P: Thready and slightly rapid or Weak and forceless or Deep and forceless

TREATS

  • Habitual constipation
  • Sequelae of infectious diseases
  • Hemorrhoids
  • Irritable bowel syndrome
  • Chronic pharyngitis
  • Cheilitis
  • Stomatitis
  • Post-surgical recovery
  • Viral infection
  • Erysipelas
  • Dizziness
  • Fever
  • Nausea/Vomiting
  • Tinnitus
  • Dysphagia
  • Reflux esophagitis
  • Bad breath
  • Aphthous ulcers
  • Hyperthyroid conditions
  • Oral side effects of radiation
  • Chronic pancreatitis
  • Diabetes
  • Atrophic rhinitis
  • Morning sickness
  • Varicose veins
  • Tuberculosis of the Large Intestine
  • Vertigo
  • Bi-polar disorder
  • Constipation
  • Mania
  • Weakness
  • Esophageal cancer
  • Drug-induced dermatitis
  • Osteoarthritis

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Use only for constipation caused by Heat and Dryness severely damaging Yin and Body Fluids.
