HERB FORMULAS

JIAN LING TANG - 建瓴汤 - CONSTRUCT ROOF TILES DECOCTION

English: Construct Roof Tiles Decoction
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Achyranthis Bidentatae Niu Xi 30g Invigorates the Blood, expels Blood Stasis, and nourishes Liver and Kidney Yin.
With Sheng Di Huang, powerfully clears Deficiency Fire Blazing Upward.
Haematitum Dai Zhe Shi 24g Calms the Liver, anchors Floating Yang, clears Liver Fire and strongly descends rebellious Qi.
With Long Gu, Mu Li and Niu Xi, for dizziness, vertigo, headache and tinnitus due to Liver Yang Rising.
With Long Gu, Mu Li, Bai Shao and Niu Xi, for hypertension with irritability, dizziness and constipation.
Fossilia Ossis Mastodi Long Gu 18g Settles anxiety, calms the Spirit, calms the Liver, anchors and preserves Floating Yang and prevents leakage of fluids.
With Mu Li, Niu Xi and Dai Zhe Shi, for insomnia, dizziness, irritability, and blurred vision associated with Liver Yin Deficiency with Liver Yang Rising.
Concha Ostreae Mu Li 18g Heavily settles and calms the Spirit, calms the Liver, benefits Yin, anchors Floating Yang and prevents leakage of fluids.
With Long Gu and Bai Shao, for anxiety, palpitations, insomnia, dream disturbed sleep, headache, dizziness and tinnitus and other symptoms associated with Yin Deficiency with Yang Rising.
Rz. Dioscoreae Shan Yao 30g Tonifies the Spleen, nourishes Stomach Yin, tonifies Lung Qi, nourishes Lung Yin, tonifies Kidney Yin and astringes Jing.
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 18g Nourishes Yin, generates fluids and cools Heart Fire.
With Bai Shao, for Blood Deficiency Heat.
Rx. Paeoniae Alba Bai Shao 12g Nourishes the Blood, regulates menstruation, astringes Yin, adjusts the Ying and Wei, calms Liver Yang and Liver Wind and alleviates pain.
With Long Gu and Mu Li, for spontaneous sweating and night sweats due to Yin Deficiency.
With Sheng Di Huang, Niu Xi, Dai Zhe Shi and Mu Li, for Liver Yang Rising.
Sm. Platycladi Bai Zi Ren 12g Nourishes the Heart, calms the Spirit, moistens the Intestines, unblocks the bowels and astringes Yin Deficiency sweat.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Sedates the Liver
  • Extinguishes Liver Wind
  • Nourishes Yin
  • Calms the Shen

SYNDROMES

  • Liver Yang Rising

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Dizziness
  • Vertigo
  • Impaired vision
  • Night blindness
  • Tinnitus
  • Irascibility
  • Throbbing headache with distended feeling
  • Flushed face
  • Blurred vision
  • Red, dry eyes with feeling of grit in eye
  • Dry mouth and throat with bitter taste in mouth
  • Palpitations
  • Headache
  • Nervousness
  • Forgetfulness
  • Insomnia with much dreaming
  • Swelling or a distended sensation of the ears
  • Dry throat
  • Irritability
  • Restlessness
  • Thirst
  • Five sole Heat
  • Night sweats
  • Light or late menses or
  • Amenorrhea
  • T: Red and dry
  • C: Little or None
  • P: Thready and wiry or Wiry and rapid or Wiry, hard and long (when Yang is Rising and Excess, pulse is forceful) or Thready and rapid when Yang is not prominent

TREATS

  • Trigeminal neuralgia
  • Hypertension
  • Headache
  • Palpitations
  • Dizziness
  • Amenorrhea
  • Late menstruation
  • Forgetfulness
  • Tinnitus
  • Aphasia
  • Apraxia
  • Vertigo
  • Insomnia
  • Scanty menstruation
  • Chronic glomerulonephritis
