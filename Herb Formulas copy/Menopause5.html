HERB FORMULAS

MENOPAUSE #5

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Hb. Epimedii Yin Yang Huo 4.5g Tonifies the Kidneys, strengthens Yang and increases the libido.
Rz. Curculiginis Xian Mao 4.5g Tonifies the Kidneys and strengthens Yang.
With Yin Yang Huo, for menopausal hypertension.
Rz. Atractylodis Macrocephalae Bai Zhu 6g Tonifies the Spleen, augments Qi, dries Dampness and promotes water metabolism.
Rx. codonopsis Dang Shen 6g Tonifies the Middle Jiao , augments Qi, nourishes the Blood and promotes the generation of Body Fluids.
With Fu Ling, for Spleen and Stomach Deficiency with fatigue, a sensation of fullness in the abdomen, dizziness, lethargy, shortness of breath, anorexia and loose stools or diarrhea.
With Bai Zhu, for Spleen Qi Deficiency with anorexia, loose stools and vomiting.
Rx. Aucklandiae Mu Xiang 3g Promotes the movement of Qi, alleviates pain, strengthens the Spleen and prevents Stagnation.
With Bai Zhu, for anorexia with epigastric and abdominal pain and distention.
Ram. cum Uncis Uncariae Gou Teng 6g Drains Liver Heat and pacifies Liver Yang.
Poria Fu Ling 6g Promotes urination, leaches out Dampness and strengthens the Spleen.
With Bai Zhu, strengthens the Spleen and resolves Dampness.
With Mu Xiang, for diarrhea and borborygmus due to Dampness.
Poria Pararadicis Fu Shen 6g Quiets the Heart, calms the Spirit and soothes the nerves.
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 6g Nourishes Yin, generates Fluids and increases saliva.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Nourishes and balances Kidney Yin and Yang
  • Strengthens the Spleen
  • Tonifies Qi
  • Resolves Dampness

SYNDROMES

  • Kidney Yin and Yang Deficiency with Yang Deficiency primary

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Aversion to Cold
  • Cold limbs
  • Frequent hot flashes with sweating
  • Headache
  • Dizziness
  • Vertigo
  • Tinnitus
  • Dry mouth and throat
  • Aches in the waist
  • Weakness
  • Five Sole Heat
  • Malar flush
  • Irregular menstruation
  • Metrorrhagia
  • Alternating Hot and Cold sensations
  • T: Pale or Pink or Pale-pink
  • C: Thin or None
  • P: Deep and thready or Deep and weak or Deep, thready and wiry

TREATS

  • Menopause syndrome
