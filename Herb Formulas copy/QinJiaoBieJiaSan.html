HERB FORMULAS

QIN JIAO BIE JIA SAN - 秦艽鳖甲散 - LARGE GENTIAN AND SOFT-SHELLED TURTLE POWDER

English: Large Gentian and Soft-Shelled Turtle Powder
Also Known As: Gentiana Macrophyllae and Soft-Shelled Powder
Gentiana Macrophyllae and T. S. Formula

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Gentianae Macrophyllae Qin Jiao 5-15g Expels Wind-Dampness and clears Deficiency Heat.
With Bie Jia, vents the muscle layer and cools tidal fevers associated with consumption, especially Wind consumption with steaming bones.
With Zhi Mu, Bie Jia and Di Gu Pi, for steaming bone sensation with tidal fever and night sweats caused by Yin Deficiency and Heat.
Rx. Bupleuri Chai Hu 9-30g Harmonizes the Exterior and Interior, disperses Wind-Heat and resolves Phlegm and congestion.
Carapax Trionycis Bie Jia 9-30g Nourishes Yin, anchors Yang and invigorates the Blood
Rz. Anemarrhenae Zhi Mu 5-15g Clears Heat and drains Fire from the Lungs and Stomach, clears Deficiency Fire, nourishes Yin, moistens Dryness, generates Fluids and quenches thirst.
With Bie Jia, for steaming bone disorder.
Cx. Lycii Di Gu Pi 9-30g Cools the Blood, drains Yin Deficiency Fire, cools the Blood, eliminates lurking Heat, clears Heat from the bones and drains Heat in the Lung.
With Zhi Mu and Bie Jia, for Blood Heat with Yin Deficiency and infantile malnutrition with Heat sensations.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 5-15g Tonifies, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood, stops cough and treats dyspnea.
(Fr. Mume) (Wu Mei) (2-15g) (Astringes leakage of Lung Qi, stops leakage of sweat, generates fluids, alleviates thirst and stops coughs. Included in some versions.)
(Hb. Artemisiae Annuae) (Qing Hao) (1.5-15g) (Clears Summerheat or Damp-Heat, Clears Deficiency fevers and Cools the Blood. Included in some versions.)
(With Bie Jia, for the final stages of a Warm febrile disease when the Yin Fluids have already been damaged and the pathogen still lurks deep within the Yin level.)

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Nourishes Yin and Blood
  • Clears Deficiency Heat
  • Reduces steaming bones sensation
  • Dispels Wind-Dampness

SYNDROMES

  • Wind consumption (unsuccessful treatment of a patient with a Wind Invasion with underlying Yin Deficiency)
  • Yin Deficiency Heat

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Steaming bones sensation
  • Night sweats
  • Tidal fever
  • Warm sensation in the afternoon
  • Red lips
  • Red cheeks
  • Cough with sticky yellow or blood tinged sputum
  • Exhaustion
  • Brittle hair
  • Insomnia
  • Lethargy
  • Alternating chills and fever
  • Five-Sole-Heat
  • Dry mouth
  • Dry throat
  • Emaciation
  • Weak limbs
  • T: Red
  • C: Little or None
  • P: Faint and rapid

TREATS

  • Recovery from a chronic respiratory infection
  • Tuberculosis (recovery-stage)
  • Pneumonia (recovery-stage)
  • High fever with night sweats
  • AIDS/HIV
  • Lupus erythematosis
  • Night sweats
  • Chronic bronchitis
  • COPD
  • Fever of unknown origin
  • Cough
  • Aplastic anemia
  • Insomnia

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with Wind Obstructing the Exterior.
  • Contraindicated for those with seriously exhausted Yin Fluids.
