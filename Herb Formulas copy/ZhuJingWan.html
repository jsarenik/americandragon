HERB FORMULAS

ZHU JING WAN - 助景囊 - PRESERVE VISTAS PILL

English: Preserve Vistas Pill
Also Known As: Long Vistas Pill

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Sm. Cuscutae Tu Si Zi 15-20g Strengthens Yang, nourishes Yin, astringes Jing and urine, benefits the marrow and tonifies the Kidneys and Liver.
With Che Qian Zi and Shu Di Huang, for blurred vision and spots before the eyes due to Kidney and Liver Deficiencies.
Rx. Rehmanniae Preparata Shu Di Huang 10-12g Nourishes the Blood, nourishes Liver and Kidney Yin, nourishes Jing and Blood and fills the Marrow.
Sm. Plantaginis Che Qian Zi 6-10g Promotes urination, clears Damp-Heat and clears Liver Heat.
With Shu Di Huang and Tu Si Zi, for Liver and Kidney Deficiencies causing cataracts with diminished vision.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Nourishes the Liver and Kidneys
  • Supplements the Jing
  • Nourishes the Blood

SYNDROMES

  • Liver Blood and Kidney Jing Deficiencies

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Blurred vision when trying to see distant objects
  • Can see near objects relatively clearly
  • Squinting in order to see distant objects
  • Maybe flashing sensations
  • Floaters
  • Low vision in a dim environment
  • Visual fatigue
  • Maybe blurred vision for both far and near objects
  • Dizziness
  • Tinnitus
  • Insomnia with intense dreaming
  • Weakness and aching of the low back and knees
  • Dribbling urination
  • T: Pale
  • C: thin and white
  • P: Thin or Thin and wiry

TREATS

  • Myopia
  • Strangury
  • Lacrimation
