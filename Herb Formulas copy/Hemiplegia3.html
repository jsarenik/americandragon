HERB FORMULAS

HEMIPLEGIA #3

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Ginseng Ren Shen 75g Tonifies Qi.
Rz. Chuanxiong Chuan Xiong 75g Invigorates the Blood, promotes the movement of Qi, expels Wind and alleviates pain.
Placenta Hominis Zi He Che 75g Tonifies the Liver and Kidneys, strengthens Jing, tonifies Qi and nourishes the Blood.
Gecko Ge Jie 5 pairs Tonifies the Kidneys, strengthens Yang, Jing and Blood, controls and stabilizes Kidney Qi and stabilizes Ming Men Fire.
With Ren Shen, for disorders of Kidney Qi such as impotence and premature ejaculation.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Tonifies Qi
  • Tonifies the Kidneys
  • Strengthens and Warms Yang

SYNDROMES

  • Qi and Yang Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Hemiplegia
  • Weakness of the limbs
  • Low back and knee weakness and pain
  • Retention of Urine or
  • Urinary incontinence
  • Fecal incontinence
  • Fatigue
  • Loose stools
  • Anorexia
  • Dizziness
  • Pale face
  • Weak voice
  • Reluctance to speak
  • Sweating with little or no exertion
  • Chills
  • Cold limbs
  • Inability to get warm
  • Profuse clear urine
  • Spontaneous sweating
  • Lassitude
  • T: Pale
  • C: White
  • P: Slow, deep and weak

TREATS

  • Hemiplegia
  • Post-stroke paralysis

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

NOTES

  • Grind the ingredients into a powder and divide into twenty parts. Each part is one dose. Take one dose twice daily. Take one course (10 days), rest and repeat.

MODIFICATIONS

For mouth and face awry: For a rigid tongue and difficulty speaking:
+ 10-15g Rz. Typhonii Preparatum Zhi Bai Fu Zi + 10-15g Rx. Acori Tatarinowii Shi Chang Pu
+ 10-15g Bombyx Batryticatus Jiang Can + 6-10g Rx. Polygalae Yuan Zhi
For constipation:
+ 6-10g Scolopendra Wu Gong + 15-30g Sm. Cannabis Huo Ma Ren
+ 6-10g Periostracum Cicadae Chan Tui + 10-15g Sm. Pruni Yu Li Ren
+ 10-15g Sm. Persicae Tao Ren
+ 6-10g Rx. Saposhnikoviae Fang Feng + 3g Fol. Sennae Fan Xie Ye
For urinary retention:
+ 30-60g Hb. Dendrobii Shi Hu + 30-60g Cx. Mori Sang Bai Pi
For urinary incontinence: For cold limbs:
+ 10-15g Fr. Alpiniae Oxyphyllae Yi Zhi Ren + 15-30g Ram. Cinnamomi Gui Zhi
