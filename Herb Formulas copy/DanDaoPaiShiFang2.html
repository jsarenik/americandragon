HERB FORMULAS

DAN DAO PAI SHI FANG #2- 胆道排石汤 - GALLSTONE EXPELLING FORMULA #2

English: Gallstone Expelling Formula #2
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Fr. Aurantii Zhi Ke 15g Promotes the movement of Qi, reduces distention and pressure, resolves hardenings and removes stagnated food.
Rx. Aucklandiae Mu Xiang 15g Promotes the movement of Qi, adjusts and regulates stagnant Qi in the Intestines, dispels Damp-Heat, harmonizes the Liver and Spleen and alleviates pain.
With Zhi Ke and Yan Hu Suo, for epigastric and abdominal pain and distention due to Qi Stagnation.
Rz. Corydalis Yan Hu Suo 25g Invigorates the Blood, circulates Qi, stops pain and disperses extravasation.
Fr. Gardeniae Zhi Zi 20g Clears Heat, reduces Fire, eliminates irritability and drains Damp-Heat.
With Da Huang, for constipation.
Rz. Polygoni Cuspidati Hu Zhang 50g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, unblocks the channels, clears Heat, resolves Dampness and stops pain.
With Mu Xiang and Zhi Ke, for biliary or urinary tract stones.
With Jin Qian Cao, for biliary or urinary tract stones.
Hb. Lysimachiae Jin Qian Cao 50g Clears Liver and Gallbladder Damp-Heat and expels gallstones.
Rx. et Rz. Rhei Da Huang 10g Drains Heat, purges accumulations, invigorates the Blood and dispels Blood Stasis.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Heat
  • Resolves Dampness
  • Promotes the movement of Qi
  • Benefits the Gallbladder
  • Removes stones
  • Stops pain

SYNDROMES

  • Liver and Gallbladder Damp-Heat
