HERB FORMULAS

KANG AI PIAN - 抗癌片 - ANTI-TUMOR PILL

English: Anti-Tumor Pill
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Gold Pill* Jin Dan 300g Clears Heat, drains Fire, relieves toxicity, dries Dampness, resolves Phlegm, dispels Accumulation, restrains and inhibits sores, generates flesh, transforms putrefication, promotes the healing of sores, nourishes the Blood, sedates the Heart and calms the Spirit.
Rx. Notoginseng San Qi 600g Invigorates the Blood, transforms Blood Stasis, reduces swelling and alleviates pain.
 With Bai Ji, for hematemesis, hemoptysis, epistaxis and hematuria.
Calculus Bovis Niu Huang 80g Drains Heat,resolves Fire toxicity and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rz. Coptidis Huang Lian 60g Drains Fire, resolves Fire toxicity and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Succinum Hu Po 60g Invigorates the Blood, dissipates Stasis, reduces swelling and promotes healing.
With San Qi, for Blood Stagnation.
Pseudobulbus Cremastrae/Pleiones Shan Ci Gu 300g Clears Heat, resolves toxicity, reduces swelling, reduces abscesses, dissipates nodules and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Fr. Mori Sang Shen 90g Tonifies the Blood and enriches Yin.
Rz. Dioscoreae Shan Yao 300g Tonifies the Spleen, nourishes Stomach and Lung Yin and tonifies Lung Qi.
Rx. Curcumae Yu Jin 60g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, regulates Qi flow, clears Heat, cools the Blood,
speeds healing of chronic sores and alleviates pain.
With Ming Fan, for seizures due to Phlegm Obstruction.
Flos Lonicerae Jin Yin Hua 90g Clears Heat, resolves Fire toxicity and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Gan Cao, for toxic swellings and sores.
Rx. Astragali Huang Qi 90g Tonifies Qi and Blood, promotes the discharge of pus, generates flesh, expels toxins and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Agkistrodon Bai Hua She 90g Powerfully unblocks the channels, extinguishes Wind and has an antagonistic effect on cancer cells.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 90g Clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity, tonifies the Spleen, augments Qi, moistens the Lungs,
resolves Phlegm, stops cough, inhibits the growth of cancer cells, alleviates pain, moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs and guides the herbs to all twelve channels.
Rz. Bletillae Bai Ji 300g Reduces swelling, generates flesh, resolves Phlegm, nourishes the Lungs and eliminates ulcers.

NOTES

  • Grind the above ingredients into a fine powder. Make into 1000 tablets, each of which contains 0.03125g Gold Pill Jin Dan. Take five tablets twice daily.
  • *Preparation of Gold Pill Jin Dan: Mix Alumen Ming Fan 60g, Natrii Sulfas Mang Xiao 60g, Calomelas Qing Fen 60g, Melanteritum Zao Fan 30g, Cinnabaris Zhu Sha 15g and stir-fry alternately over a soft fire and then a strong fire for three hours. Cool and make into pills.
