HERB FORMULAS

BAI HE DI HUANG TANG - 百合地黃汤 - LILY BULB AND REHMANNIA DECOCTION

English: Lily Bulb and Rehmannia Decoction
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Bul. Lilii Bai He 15-30g Nourishes Yin, moistens the Lungs, clears Heat, clears the Heart and calms the Spirit.
With Sheng Di Huang, cools the Heart and treats both Qi and Blood to restore harmony to the Ying and Wei after a warm febrile disease.
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 15-30g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, nourishes Yin, generates fluids and cools Heart Fire.
Fr. Jujube Da Zao 30g Tonifies the Spleen and Stomach, augments Qi, nourishes the Blood and calms the Spirit.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Moistens the Lungs and Heart
  • Nourishes Fluids
  • Nourishes Yin
  • Clears Deficiency Heat
  • Cools the Blood

SYNDROMES

  • Late-stage or aftermath of Ying or Xue Stage Heat
  • Bai He Bing (Lily Syndrome)

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Mental disorientation
  • Irritability
  • Insomnia
  • Palpitations
  • Depression
  • Restlessness
  • Hopelessness
  • Sudden mood swings
  • Erratic behavior
  • Feels cold then not cold
  • Feels hot then not hot
  • Aversion to life
  • Dry mouth
  • Dry stools
  • Dry throat
  • Fatigue
  • Irregular stools
  • Lethargy
  • Mental instability
  • Obsessions
  • Craves food then hates food
  • Does not like to talk
  • Does not know what he/she wants
  • Dark urine
  • A bitter taste in the mouth
  • Dizziness
  • Muddled thinking
  • T: Red
  • C: Little or None
  • P: Slightly rapid or Weak and rapid or Thready and rapid

TREATS

  • Perimenopausal syndrome
  • Neurosis
  • Autonomic dystonia
  • Sleep walking
  • Hysteria
  • Lily syndrome
  • Depression
  • Mental confusion
  • Nervous breakdown
  • Neurasthenia
  • Palpitations

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with Excess Fire in the Lungs and Heart.
  • Contraindicated for those with Yin Deficiency as the main pathology.
