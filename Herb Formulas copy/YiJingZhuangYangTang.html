HERB FORMULAS

YI JING ZHUANG YANG TANG - 医精壮阳汤 - STOP NOCTURNAL EMISSIONS AND STRENGTHEN KIDNEY YANG DECOCTION

English: Stop Nocturnal Emissions and Strengthen Kidney Yang Decoction
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Dioscoreae Shan Yao 20g Tonifies Kidney Yin and astringes Jing.
With Shu Di Huang and Shan Zhu Yu, for Kidney Deficiency with spermatorrhea and night sweats.
Rx. Rehmanniae Preparata Shu Di Huang 20g Nourishes Liver and Kidney Yin, nourishes Jing and Blood and fills the Marrow.
With Shan Yao, nourishes Kidney Yin and secures Jing, while restraining urination and sweating.
Fr. Lycii Gou Qi Zi 20g Nourishes and tonifies Liver and Kidney Blood and Yin, benefits Jing and brightens the eyes.
With Shu Di Huang, for Liver and Kidney Deficiency.
Fr. Corni Shan Zhu Yu 20g Stabilizes the Kidneys, astringes Jing and Body Fluids, tonifies Liver and Kidney Yin and strengthens Kidney Yang.
With Shu Di Huang and Shan Yao, for urinary frequency (day or night), dizziness, tinnitus and lower back pain due to Kidney Deficiency.
Poria Fu Ling 15g Promotes urination, leaches out Dampness and strengthens the Spleen.
With Gan Cao, for Spleen and Heart Deficiency causing facial edema, palpitations and shortness of breath.
Hb. Cistanches Rou Cong Rong 15g Tonifies the Kidneys, strengthens the Yang
and benefits Jing and marrow.
With Suo Yang, for impotence, Cold and discomfort in the low back.
Hb. Cynomorii Suo Yang 15g Tonifies the Kidneys, benefits Jing and marrow, strengthens Yang, nourishes Liver Yin and Blood and strengthens sinews and bones.
Rx. Morindae Officinalis Ba Ji Tian 15g Tonifies the Kidneys and strengthens Yang.
With Rou Cong Rong and Tu Si Zi, for Impotence, spermatorrhea and premature ejaculation due to Kidney Yang Deficiency.
With Ren Shen and Shan Yao, for impotence or infertility.
With Rou Cong Rong, tonifies Yang without injuring Yin.
Rx. Ginseng Ren Shen 15g Powerfully tonifies Yuan Qi Tonifies Lung, Heart, Spleen and Stomach Qi, generates Body Fluids, stops thirst and treats impotence.
With Shu Di Huang and Tian Men Dong, for Qi and Yin Deficiency.
Sm. Zizyphi Spinosae Preparatum Zhi Zao Ren 15g Nourishes Heart Yin, tonifies Liver Blood, calms the Spirit, astringes sweat and generates fluids.
Sm. Cuscutae Tu Si Zi 15g Strengthens Yang, nourishes Yin, astringes Jing and urine, benefits the marrow, tonifies the Kidneys and Liver and improves vision.
Rx. Asparagi Tian Men Dong 10g Moistens the Lungs, nourishes the Kidneys,
generates fluids and resolves Phlegm.
With Ren Shen and Shu Di Huang, for debility and low grade fever due to Yin Deficiency.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 10g Tonifies the Spleen, augments Qi, moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs and guides the herbs to all twelve channels.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Tonifies Kidney Yang
  • Nourishes the Liver and Kidneys
  • Nourishes Yin
  • Relieves Empty Fire due to Deficiency of Yin

SYNDROMES

  • Kidney Yin and Yang Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Impotence
  • Failure to maintain an erection
  • Dizziness
  • Tinnitus
  • Weak legs
  • Lower back ache
  • Low-grade fever in the afternoon
  • Cold hands and feet
  • Fatigue
  • Red cheeks
  • Hot flushed face
  • Spermatorrhea due to Deficient Yin
  • Nocturnal emissions
  • Fever with thirst
  • Burning soles of feet
  • Hot flashes
  • Night sweating
  • Spontaneous sweat
  • Dry cough
  • Headache
  • Restlessness
  • Early periods
  • Pale dilute menstrual Blood
  • Nocturia
  • General feeling of being Cold
  • Restless insomnia
  • Vertigo,
  • T: Pale and swollen or Red or a Red tip
  • C: Thin and white or Little or None
  • P: Deep and weak or Thready and rapid

TREATS

  • Impotence
  • Nocturnal emissions
  • Spermatorrhea
