HERB FORMULAS

DA BU YUAN JIAN - 大補元煎 - GREAT TONIFY THE PRIMAL DECOCTION

English: Great Tonify the Primal Decoction
Also Known As: Tonic Decoction for Debility

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Ginseng Ren Shen 3-10g Powerfully tonifies Yuan Qi and tonifies Spleen and Stomach Qi.
With Shu Di Huang, for severe Qi and Blood Deficiencies due to loss of Blood and Qi and Yin Deficiencies.
Rz. Dioscoreae Shan Yao 6-18g Tonifies the Spleen, nourishes Stomach Yin, stops diarrhea, tonifies Kidney Yin and astringes Jing.
Rx. Rehmanniae Preparata Shu Di Huang 9-90g Nourishes the Blood, nourishes Liver and Kidney Yin, nourishes Jing and fills the Marrow.
With Dang Gui, for Blood Deficiency marked by dizziness, palpitations, insomnia and menstrual dysfunction.
With Shan Zhu Yu, nourishes Kidney Yin and secures Jing, while restraining urination and sweating.
With Shan Zhu Yu and Shan Yao, for dizziness, weak and sore back, impotence, spermatorrhea and night sweats, due to Kidney and Liver Yin Deficiencies.
With Ren Shen, for Qi and Blood Deficiencies.
Cx. Eucommiae Du Zhong 6-15g Tonifies the Kidneys and Liver, strengthens the sinews and bones and tonifies Kidney Yang.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 6-12g Tonifies the Blood and regulates the menses, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood, disperses Cold and stops pain due to Blood Stasis.
Fr. Corni Shan Zhu Yu 3-30g Stabilizes the Kidneys, astringes Jing, tonifies Liver and Kidney Yin, strengthens Kidney Yang, stabilizes the menses and stops bleeding.
With Shu Di Huang and Shan Yao, for urinary frequency (day or night), dizziness, tinnitus and lower back pain due to Kidney Deficiency.
With Shu Di Huang, Gou Qi Zi and Shan Yao, for true Yin Deficiency
Fr. Lycii Gou Qi Zi 6-12g Nourishes and tonifies Liver and Kidney Blood and Yin and benefits Jing.
With Shu Di Huang, for Liver and Kidney Deficiencies.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Preparata Zhi Gan Cao 3-9g Tonifies the Spleen and augments Qi and moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Tonifies Yin and Blood
  • Benefits Yang and Qi
  • Strengthens Kidney Jing
  • Invigorates the Spleen

SYNDROMES

  • Severe injury to Yuan Qi and Blood
  • Kidney Jing Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Uterine prolapse
  • Soreness and weakness in the lower back
  • Severe lower abdominal distention
  • Urinary frequency which is worse at night
  • Tinnitus
  • Nocturnal emission
  • Profuse pink menstrual discharge
  • Nocturia
  • Hearing loss
  • Dizziness
  • Forgetfulness
  • Weakness of the lower limbs
  • Lack of spirit
  • T: Pale-red
  • C: Normal
  • P: Deep and frail or Deep and slow

TREATS

  • Uterine prolapse
  • Strangury
  • Renal tuberculosis
  • Chronic urinary tract infection
  • Vaginal prolapse
  • Polyuria
  • Tinnitus
  • Forgetfulness
  • Headache
  • Rectal prolapse
  • Late menstruation
  • Metrorrhagia
  • Hypothyroidism
  • Prostatitis
  • Optic atrophy
  • Constipation
  • Deafness
  • Weakness
  • Impotence
  • Nocturnal emissions
