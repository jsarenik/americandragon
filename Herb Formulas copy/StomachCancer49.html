HERB FORMULAS

STOMACH CANCER #49

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Realgar Xiong Huang 10g Relieves toxicity, dries Dampness and expels Phlegm.
With Peng Sha, for ulcerations of the mouth, tongue and throat.
With Ming Fan, for sores with redness, swelling, itching and pain.
Alumen Ming Fan 10g Relieves toxicity, dries Dampness, clears Heat,
expels Phlegm, restrains and inhibits sores, generates flesh and transforms putrefication.
Borneolum Bing Pian 6g Clears Heat, drains Fire, resolves toxicity, dissipates nodules and alleviates pain.
Borax Peng Sha 10g Relieves toxicity, reduces swellings, clears Heat,
dissolves Phlegm, dries Dampness, transforms stones and prevents putrefication.
Natrii Sulfas Mang Xiao 10g Clears Heat, drains Fire, reduces swelling and softens hardness.
Gypsum Fibrosum Shi Gao 10g Clears Heat in the Qi Stage, drains Fire, clears Blazing Stomach Fire and heals eczema, burns, ulcerated sores and wounds.
Cinnabaris* Zhu Sha 6g Expels Phlegm, clears Heat, relieves toxicity and prevents putrefaction.
With Bing Pian and Peng Sha for sores of the oral cavity topically.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Relieves toxicity
  • Dries Dampness
  • Clears Heat
  • Expels Phlegm
  • Softens hardness
  • Alleviates pain

SYNDROMES

  • Early-stage hard Stomach cancer

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Early stage:
    Often no symptoms
  • Upper abdominal pain
    Discomfort in the upper abdomen

TREATS

  • Stomach cancer
  • Gastric carcinoma

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • *Cinnabaris Zhu Sha is toxic and is no longer generally used.
