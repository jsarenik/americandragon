HERB FORMULAS

SHAO YAO GAN CAO TANG - 芍藥甘草湯 - PEONY AND LICORICE COMBINATION

English: Peony and Licorice Decoction
Also Known As: Peony and Licorice Combination
Decoction of Paeoniae and Glycyrrhizae

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Paeoniae Alba

 

Bai Shao 6-30g Nourishes the Blood, preserves Yin, softens the Liver and alleviates pain.
With Bai Shao, Regulates the Liver and Spleen and nourishes the sinews.
With Zhi Gan Cao for abdominal pain due to disharmony between the Liver and Stomach and muscle spasms, especially in the calf, due to Blood Deficiency.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Preparata Zhi Gan Cao 6-20g Tonifies and augments the Qi of the Middle Jiao, especially that of the Spleen.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Softens the Liver
  • Nourishes the Blood
  • Nourishes Yin
  • Moderates painful spasms
  • Relieves pain
  • Harmonizes the Middle Jiao
  • Replenishes Body Fluids

SYNDROMES

  • Pain in the calves with Blood Deficiency or injury to fluids
  • Cramps in the hands and abdominal pain that improves with pressure
  • Insufficiency of Yin and Yang
  • Disharmony between the Liver and Spleen

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Use caution for those with hypertension.
HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS
  • This formula may be effective in treating schizophrenia patients with neuroleptic-induced hyperprolactinemia.
  • This formula has been shown to decrease paclitaxel-induced myalgia/arthralgia.
  • This formula has been shown to be effective in treating menopausal symptoms induced by gonadotropin-releasing hormone agonists without a negative effect on serum estradiol levels.

NOTES

  • Some say the dosage between Peony and Licorice should be two to one.
