HERB FORMULAS

SHENG MAI SAN - 生脈散 - GENERATE THE PULSE POWDER

English: Generate the Pulse Powder
Also Known As: Pulse Engendering Powder

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Ginseng Ren Shen 3-15g Tonifies Heart, Spleen and Lung Qi and generates Fluids.
With Mai Men Dong, strongly generates Fluids.
With Wu Wei Zi, rescues Qi and Yin.
With Mai Men Dong and Wu Wei Zi, for Qi and Yin Deficiency.
(Rx. Codonopsis) (Dang Shen) (15-45g) (Substitutes for Ren Shen, if not critical.)
(Rx. Quinquifolii) (Xi Yang Shen) (15-45g) (Substitutes for Ren Shen, if not critical.)
Tub. Ophiopogonis Mai Men Dong 5-30g Nourishes Yin, moistens the Lungs, generates Fluids, stops cough, clears Heat from the Heart and eliminates irritability.
Fr. Schisandrae Wu Wei Zi 2-10g Astringes Lung Qi, generates Fluids, tonifies Qi and nourishes Kidney Yin.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Augments Qi
  • Generates Fluids
  • Clears Heat
  • Preserves Yin
  • Stops Excess perspiration

SYNDROMES

  • Lung and Kidney Qi Deficiency
  • Heart and Lung Qi Deficiency
  • Lung Qi and Yin Deficiency
  • Atrophy disorder (Wei Syndrome) due to Lung Heat with Fluid Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Chronic cough with sparse sputum
  • Sputum difficult to expectorate
  • Shortness of breath
  • Spontaneous perspiration
  • Dry mouth and tongue

  • Fatigue

  • Insomnia
  • Listless speech
  • T: Red and dry or Pink and dry
  • C: Thin and dry
  • P: Deficient and rapid or Irregular and slow or Thready and deficient or Consistently irregular

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Use extreme caution with high fever.
  • Use extreme caution for unresolved external cough.
  • Do not use in initial stage flu in summer with mild conditions.
  • Do not use if Fluids are not yet injured.
