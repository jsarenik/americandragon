HERB FORMULAS

SCHIZOPHRENIA #6

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Scutellariae Huang Qin 9g Drains Fire, detoxifies and calms ascending Liver Yang.
With Huang Lian, drains pathogenic Heat from the Upper and Middle Jiaos for redness, swelling and pain of the head and face, a dry mouth, a parched throat, ulcerations of the mouth and tongue.
Rz. Coptidis Huang Lian 9g Drains Fire, resolves Fire toxicity, clears Heart Fire and drains Stomach Fire.
Cx. Phellodendri Huang Bai 9g Drains Fire, relieves Fire toxicity and drains Kidney Fire.
With Long Gu and Mu Li, for irritability.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 9g Tonifies, activates and harmonizes the Blood, moistens the Intestines and unblocks the bowels.
Hb. Aloes Lu Hui 9g Purges and drains Fire, guides out accumulations, clears Heat and cools the Liver.
With Long Dan Cao, Huang Qin and Huang Lian, for Heat in the Liver channel with epigastric pain, dizziness, a headache and irritability.
Rx. Gentianae Long Dan Cao 9g Drains and pacifies Excess Liver Fire.
With Huang Lian, for red, swollen, painful eyes due to Excess Heat in the Liver channel.
With Da Huang, for rib pain, a bitter taste, red eyes, deafness, fright, mania and constipation due to Liver Fire.
Rx. et Rz. Rhei Da Huang 9g Drains Heat, purges accumulations, drains Fire, activates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, reduces Fire toxicity and eliminates Phlegm.
With Huang Lian, for distention, Heart palpitations, mental distress with facial congestion and epilepsy due to Excess Heat.
With Dang Gui, for Blood Stasis.
Natrii Sulfas Mang Xiao 9g Purges accumulations, guides out Stagnation, softens hardness, moistens Dryness, clears Heat and drains Fire.
With Da Huang, for constipation due to Heat in the Stomach and Intestines.
Rx. Bupleuri Chai Hu 12g Spreads Liver Qi, relieves Stagnation, disperses Wind-Heat and resolves Phlegm and congestion.
With Huang Qin, restores normal functioning of the Liver and Gallbladder.
Fossilia Ossis Mastodi Long Gu 30g Settles anxiety, calms the Spirit, calms the Liver and anchors and preserves Floating Yang,
Concha Ostreae Mu Li 30g Heavily settles and calms the Spirit, calms the Liver, benefits Yin and anchors Floating Yang.
Gypsum Fibrosum Shi Gao 30g Clears Heat in the Qi Stage, drains Fire, relieves irritability, quenches thirst, clears Excess Heat from the Lungs and clears Blazing Stomach Fire.
With Zhi Mu , cools Heat in the Yang Ming or Qi Level while protecting Stomach fluids to treat high fever, profuse sweating, insatiable thirst, a red face, aversion to Heat and a large, strong pulse.
With Huang Qi, for coughing with Excess Lung Heat.
Rz. Anemarrhenae Zhi Mu 6g Clears Heat, drains Fire from the Lungs and from the Stomach, nourishes Yin, moistens Dryness, generates Fluids, clears Deficiency Fire and quenches thirst.
With Huang Bai, clears Heat and Drains Fire for Yin Deficiency Fire.
With Huang Lian, for Blazing Stomach Fire with intense hunger, dry stools, a dry yellow tongue coat and a slippery rapid pulse.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 3g Clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity, moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs and guides the herbs to all twelve channels.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Heat
  • Drains Fire
  • Purges the Intestines

SYNDROMES

  • Excess Fire

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Manic behavior
  • Raving walking
  • Shouting
  • Abusing people
  • Climbing to high places
  • Singing
  • Removing one's clothes
  • Unusual strength
  • Foul breath
  • Red face
  • Abdominal pain and distention
  • Constipation
  • T: Red
  • C: Dark and yellow or Black and dry
  • P: Deep and wiry

TREATS

  • Schizophrenia
