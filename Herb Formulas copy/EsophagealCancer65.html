HERB FORMULAS

ESOPHAGEAL CANCER #65

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rz. Pinelliae Preparatum Zhi Ban Xia 10g Dries Dampness, transforms Phlegm, descends Rebellious Qi, dissipates nodules, reduces Stagnation and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Chen Pi, for a productive cough and a stifling sensation in the chest due to Phlegm-Damp Obstruction or Spleen Qi Deficiency.
Rx. Astragali Huang Qi 30g Tonifies Qi and Blood, Strengthens the Spleen Promotes the discharge of pus, generates flesh and expels toxins and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Dang Shen, for Qi Deficiency with general debility, anorexia, fatigue, tired extremities and spontaneous sweating.
With Dang Gui, for Qi and Blood Deficiency with chronic non-healing sores, numbness, painful obstruction and paresthesia.
With Gui Zhi, for Qi and Blood Deficiency and Ying and Wei Deficiency with numbness and painful obstruction of the musculature.
Rx. Codonopsis Dang Shen 20g Tonifies the Middle Jiao, augments Qi, tonifies the Lungs, assists herbs that drain downward when there is significant Qi Deficiency, nourishes the Blood and promotes the generation of Body Fluids.
With Dang Gui and Shu Di Huang, for Qi and Blood Deficiency with dizziness, weakness and lassitude.
With Huang Qi, for Lung and Spleen Qi Deficiency with fatigue, shortness of breath, diarrhea and anorexia.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 15g Tonifies, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood, disperses Cold, reduces swelling, expels pus, generates flesh and alleviates pain.
With Huang Qi, for Qi and Blood Deficiency.
With Bai Shao, regulates and harmonizes Qi and Blood.
With Shu Di Huang, for Heat symptoms due to Yin Deficiency and Devastated Blood.
With Chen Pi and Zhi Ban Xia, for Lung and Kidney Yin Deficiency with overflowing water turning to Phlegm, with cough, wheezing and nausea marked by profuse and salty sputum.
Rx. Paeoniae Alba Bai Shao 10g Nourishes the Blood, astringes Yin, adjusts the Ying and Wei, calms Liver Yang and Liver Wind and alleviates pain.
With Dang Gui and Shu Di HUang, for dizziness, blurred vision and dysmenorrhea due to Blood Deficiency or Stasis.
with Gui Zhi, harmonizes the Ying and Wei.
With Dang Gui, nourishes Yin and Blood.
Flos Inulae Xuan Fu Hua 10g Expels Phlegm, removes pathogenic water, resolves Phlegm Stagnation in the Lungs, descends Qi, stops vomiting and calms rebellious Stomach Qi.
With Zhi Ban Xi, for vomiting, coughing, wheezing and epigastric distention due to congested fluids.
Haematitum Dai Zhe Shi 30g Strongly descends rebellious Qi.
With Zhi Ban Xia, Xuan Fu Hua and Dang Shen, for vomiting, hiccup and belching due to rebellious Qi.
Rx. Clematidis Wei Ling Xian 30g Dispels Wind-Dampness, unblocks the channels, alleviates pain, softens and transforms fish bones lodged in the throat and reduces Phlegm and pathogenic water.
Sm. Impatientis Ji Xing Zi 10g Disperses Blood Stagnation, regulates Qi, softens hardness, disperses tumors, removes toxins and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Ram. Cinnamomi Gui Zhi 10g Releases the Exterior, assists Yang, adjusts the Ying and Wei, releases the muscle layer, warms the channels and collaterals to relieve pain, unblocks Yang, transforms Qi and thin mucus, warms and facilitates the flow of Qi through the channels and collaterals and Blood through the vessels, warms the Middle and directs Turbid Yin downward.
With Bai Shao, warms and tonifies the Middle Jiao.
Per. Citri Reticulatae Chen Pi 10g Regulates Qi, adjusts the Middle, relieves the diaphragm, dries Dampness, transforms Phlegm and descends Qi.
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 10g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, nourishes Yin and generates fluids.
With Shu Di Huang, for Heat symptoms due to Yin Deficiency and Devastated Blood.
Rx. Rehmanniae Preparata Shu Di Huang 10g Nourishes Jing and Blood, fills the Marrow, nourishes Liver and Kidney Yin and arrests coughing and wheezing.
With Dang Gui, for Blood Deficiency marked by dizziness, palpitations, insomnia and menstrual dysfunction.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Strengthens the Spleen
  • Benefits Qi
  • Inhibits the growth of cancer cells

SYNDROMES

  • Qi Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • The patient is in very poor condition
  • Only semi-solids and liquids may be swallowed
  • Sallow complexion
  • Fatigue
  • Anorexia
  • Abdominal and epigastric distention after eating
  • Four limbs feel tired and heavy
  • Maybe acid regurgitation or vomiting
  • Loose stools
  • Shortness of breath
  • Vomiting of water
  • T: Pale and flabby - may have teethmarks
  • C: White
  • P: Even, forceless and deficient

TREATS

  • Esophageal cancer
