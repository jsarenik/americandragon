HERB FORMULAS

MA XING SHI GAN TANG - 麻杏石甘湯 - EPHEDRA, APRICOT KERNEL, GYPSUM AND LICORICE DECOCTION

English: Ephedra, Apricot Kernel, Gypsum and Licorice Decoction
Also Known As: Ma Huang and Apricot Seed Combination
Decoction of Ephedra, Armeniacae, Gypsum and Licorice
Ma Xing Gan Shi Tang
Ma Huang Xing Ren Shi Gao Gan Cao Tang

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Hb. Ephedrae Ma Huang 4-12g Strong diaphoretic, ventilates Lung Qi and stops wheezing.
With Xing Ren, for cough and wheezing due to Wind-Cold Attacks the Lungs.
With Shi Gao and Xing Ren, for cough from Heat Obstructing the Lungs with wheezing, flaring of the nostrils and fever and Wind-edema.
Sm. Armeniacae Xing Ren 1-18g Disperses Lung Qi, stops cough and wheezing and resolves Phlegm.
With Ma Huang and Shi Gao, for Excess cough and wheezing with Lung Heat.
Gypsum Fibrosum Shi Gao 10-48g Clears Lung Heat, nourishes the Body Fluids and stops thirst.
With Ma Huang, for cough from Heat Obstructing the Lungs with wheezing, flaring of the nostrils and fever and Wind-edema.
With Ma Huang, Xing Ren and Zhi Gan Cao, for Lung Heat with cough and asthma.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Preparata Zhi Gan Cao 1-6g Harmonizes, tonifies Qi, moistens the Lungs, clears Heat, detoxifies, resolves Phlegm and stops cough.
 With Ma Huang and Xing Ren, for Wind-Cold Attacks the Lungs with coughing and/or wheezing.
With Shi Gao and Xing Ren, for Cough due to Lung Heat.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Disperses Wind-Heat
  • Stops Asthma
  • Clears the Lungs
  • Descends Qi

SYNDROMES

  • Wind-Heat Attacks the Lungs
  • Wind-Cold Transformed into Heat
  • Wen Bing - Qi Stage Lung Heat

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with asthma due to Wind-Cold.
  • Contraindicated when the pathogen lingers due to Qi Deficiency.
  • Contraindicated for children and the elderly with general weakness.
  • Contraindicated for those convalescing from chronic illness.
