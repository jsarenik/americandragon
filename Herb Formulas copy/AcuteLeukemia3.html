HERB FORMULAS

ACUTE LEUKEMIA #3

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Fr. Tritici Levis Fu Xiao Mai 30g Stops excessive sweat due to Deficiency, nourishes the Heart, calms the Spirit and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Gan Cao and Da Zao, for emotional disorders, disorientation, emotional instability, and irritability due to mild Deficiency of the Spleen Qi and Heart Blood.
Fr. Corni Shan Zhu Yu 30g Stabilizes the Kidneys, astringes Jing and Body Fluids, stops excessive sweating, tonifies Liver and Kidney Yin and strengthens Kidney Yang.
With Wu Wei Zi, for spermatorrhea, abnormal sweating, palpitations and shortness of breath due to Yin and Yang Deficiency of the Liver and Kidneys.
With Mu Li, for sweating.
Concha Ostreae Mu Li 30g Calms the Liver, benefits Yin, anchors Floating Yang, prevents leakage of fluids and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Long Gu and Bai Shao, for headache, dizziness and tinnitus and other symptoms associated with Yin Deficiency with Yang Rising.
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 30g Cools the Blood, nourishes Yin and generates fluids.
With Bai Shao, for Blood Deficiency Heat.
With Mai Men Dong and Nan sha Shen, for a dry mouth, thirst, dry lips and a red tongue due to Stomach Yin Deficiency.
Rx. Adenophorae Nan Sha Shen 30g Nourishes Yin, moistens the Lungs, expels Phlegm, nourishes the Stomach, generates fluids and clears Heat.
Rx. Codonopsis Dang Shen 30g Tonifies the Middle Jiao, augments Qi, tonifies the Lungs, nourishes the Blood and promotes the generation of Body Fluids.
With Wu Wei Zi and Mai Men Dong, for injury to Qi and fluids with shortness of breath and thirst.
Rx. Ginseng Ren Shen 12g Tonifies Qi, generates Body Fluids, stops thirst and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Wu Wei Zi and Mai Men Dong, for Qi and Yin Deficiency with shortness of breath and spontaneous sweat.
Rz. Dioscoreae Shan Yao 15g Tonifies the Spleen, nourishes Stomach Yin, stops diarrhea, tonifies Lung Qi, nourishes Lung Yin, tonifies Kidney Yin and astringes Jing.
With Mai Men Dong and Wu Wei Zi, for Lung Deficiency with cough.
With Dang Shen, for Spleen and Kidney Deficiency with fatigue, weakness, anorexia, and watery stools.
Rx. Paeoniae Alba Bai Shao 9g Nourishes the Blood, astringes Yin, softens the Liver and relieves pain.
With Wu Wei Zi and Fu Xiao Mai, for night sweats.
With Long Gu and Mu Li, for spontaneous sweating and night sweats due to Yin Deficiency.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 9g Tonifies the Spleen, augments Qi, moistens the Lungs, resolves Phlegm, clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity, inhibits the growth of cancer cells, moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs and guides the herbs to all twelve channels.
With Dang Shen, for anorexia, fatigue and loose stools due to Spleen Deficiency.
With Da Zao and Fu Xiao Mai, for dry or restless organ disorder.
Rx. Ophiopogonis Mai Men Dong 9g Nourishes Lung and Stomach Yin, generates Fluids, clears the Heart and eliminates irritability.
With Sheng Di Huang, for hematemesis or epistaxis due to Yin Deficiency with Excess Heat.
With Nan Sha Shen, for a dry throat, constipation, thirst and a red tongue with little coat due to pathogenic Warmth or Heat injuring the Stomach Yin or long-term Yin Deficiency.
With Suan Zao Ren and Sheng Di Huang, for irritability and insomnia due to Yin Deficiency.
Fossilia Ossis Mastodi Long Gu 9g Settles anxiety, calms the Spirit, calms the Liver, anchors and preserves Floating Yang and prevents the leakage of fluids.
With Shan Zhu Yu and Mu Li, for night sweats due to Yin Deficiency.
Sm. Ziziphi Spinosae Suan Zao Ren 9g Nourishes Heart Yin, tonifies Liver Blood, calms the Spirit, astringes sweat and generates fluids.
With Shan Zhu Yu, Mu Li and Wu Wei Zi, for night sweats.
Fr. Schisandrae Wu Wei Zi 3g Tonifies the Kidneys, astringes Jing, tonifies Qi, astringes sweat and generates fluids.
With Ren Shen and Mai Men Dong, for exhaustion of Qi and Yin with shortness of breath, cough, and thirst.
With Mu Li, for sweating.
With Suan Zao Ren and Sheng Di Huang, for heart Deficiency with insomnia, irritability, and forgetfulness.
Fr. Jujube Da Zao 10g Tonifies the Spleen and Stomach, augments Qi, nourishes the Blood, calms the Spirit, moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Tonifies Qi
  • Nourishes Yin
  • Nourishes the Blood
  • Restrains Yang
  • Astringes sweat
  • Inhibits the growth of cancer cells

SYNDROMES

  • Qi and Yin Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Fatigue
  • Spontaneous sweating
  • A pale face
  • Poor appetite
  • Loose stools
  • Dizziness
  • Weak voice/reluctance to speak
  • Five Sole Heat
  • Malar flush
  • Night sweats
  • Anxiety
  • Hot flashes
  • T: Pale, Pale red or Red
  • C: Thin white, Little or None
  • P: Deep and weak or Thready and rapid

TREATS

  • Acute mononuclear leukemia
