HERB FORMULAS

MA XING YI GAN TANG - 麻杏苡甘湯 - EPHEDRA, APROCOT KERNEL, COICIS AND LICORICE DECOCTION

English: Ephedra, Apricot Kernel, Coicis and Licorice Decoction
Also Known As: Ma Huang Xing Ren Yi Yi Gan Cao Tang
Ma Huang I Kan Tang
Ephedra, Apricot, Coix and Licorice Decoction
Ma-huang and Coix Combination

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Hb. Ephedrae Ma Huang 1.5-6g Induces sweating, releases the exterior, disseminates and facilitates Lung Qi, calms wheezing, stops coughing, promotes urination and reduces edema.
With Xing Ren, for Wind-Cold cough and wheezing.
Sm. Armeniacae Xing Ren 3-6g Stops cough and calms wheezing.
Sm. Coicis Yi Yi Ren 1.5-12g Promotes urination, leaches out Dampness, strengthens the Spleen and expels Wind-Dampness.
With Ma Huang, Xing Ren and Gan Cao, for generalized body aches due to Wind-Dampness.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 3g Moderates and harmonizes the properties of other herbs.
With Ma Huang and Xing Ren, for coughing or wheezing due to Wind-Cold Attacking the Lungs.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Releases the Exterior
  • Dispels Wind-Dampness
  • Resolves Phlegm

SYNDROMES

  • Externally contracted Wind-Dampness

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Mild generalized body aches
  • Slight aversion to Wind
  • Chronic respiratory complaints
  • Fever that worsens in the afternoon
  • Dry skin

TREATS

  • Rheumatic arthritis
  • Rheumatism
  • Neuralgia
  • Muscle pain
  • Keratosis palmaris
  • Keratosis plantaris
  • Warts

  • Sudamina
  • Frostbite
  • Dandruff
  • Asthma
  • Hemiplegia
  • Edema
  • Genital warts
  • Arthralgia
  • Painfull obstruction
