HERB FORMULAS

DA HUANG ZHI XUE JIAO NANG

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. and Rz. Rhei Da Huang 2-12g Drains Heat from the Blood and breaks up Blood Stasis.
With Sheng Di Huang, for Blazing Heart and Stomach Fire with epistaxis, and hemoptysis.
Rx. Scutellariae Huang Qin 3-18g Clears Heat, drains Dampness, drains Fire, relieves toxicity, cools the Blood, stops bleeding, invigorates the Blood and dispels Blood Stasis.
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 6-12g Clears Heat and cools and harmonizes the Blood.
With Mu Dan Pi, for Ying and Xue Stage Heat with a high fever, scarlet tongue, thirst, a rash all over the body, epistaxis and hemoptysis.
Cx. Moutan Mu Dan Pi 3-9g Cools the Blood and clears Heat.
With Chi Shao, Cools the Blood and disperses Stasis, for Ying Stage Heat with epistaxis, hemoptysis, hemafecia, hematuria, menorrhagia and skin rashes.
With Sheng Di Huang, for Ying and Xue Stage Heat with a high fever, scarlet tongue, thirst, a rash all over the body, epistaxis and hemoptysis.
With Chi Shao and Sheng Di Huang, for hematemesis and epistaxis due to Heat in the Xue level.
Rx. Paeoniae Rubra Chi Shao 4.5-15g Clears Heat and cools and activates the Blood.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Heat
  • Cools the Blood
  • Invigorates the Blood

SYNDROMES

  • Blood Heat

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • High fever
  • Confusion of mind
  • Delirium (mania)
  • Dark yellow urine
  • Excessive skin rashes all over body
  • Hemorrhage
  • Hematemesis
  • Hemoptysis
  • Epistaxis
  • Hemafecia
  • Hematuria
  • T: Deep red, dry or purple red.
  • C: None
  • P: Rapid or Thready and rapid or Thready and feeble

TREATS

  • Epistaxis
  • Hematemesis
  • High fever
  • Hematuria
  • Hemoptysis
  • Mania
  • Hemafecia
