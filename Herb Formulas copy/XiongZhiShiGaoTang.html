HERB FORMULAS

XIONG ZHI SHI GAO TANG -芎芷石膏湯 - SICHUAN LOVAGE, DFAHURIAN ANGELICA AND GYPSUM DECOCTION

English: Sichuan Lovage, Dahurian Angelica and Gypsum Decoction
Also Known As:  

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rz. Chuanxiong Chuan Xiong 15g Invigorates the Blood, promotes the movement of Qi, expels Wind and alleviates pain.
With Gao Ben, for severe headaches without Interior Fire.
With Chuan Xiong and Ju Hua, for Wind-Heat headaches.
Rx. Angelicae Dahuricae Bai Zhi 12g Expels Wind, eliminates Dampness, unblocks the nasal passages, dispels Cold and alleviates pain.
With Gao Ben, for Wind-Cold induced headaches, especially those at the vertex.
With Chuan Xiong, for headaches.
With Qiang Huo, for Wind-Cold Dampness causing fever, chills and headaches.
Gypsum Fibrosum Shi Gao 15-60g Clears Heat in the Qi Stage, drains Fire, relieves irritability and quenches thirst.
Rz. Ligustici Gao Ben 2-10g Expels Wind and Cold, dispels Wind-Dampness and alleviates pain.
With Bai Zhi, for Wind and/or Dampness affecting the face.
With Chuang Xiong and Bai Zhi, for headaches.
With Qiang Huo, for headache and stiffness of the nape of the neck due to Wind-Dampness in the Du channel.
Flos Chrysanthemi Ju Hua 6-20g Disperses Wind, clears Heat, calms Liver Yang and extinguishes Wind.
With Chuan Xiong and Shi Gao, for headache due to Wind-Heat or Liver Yang Rising.
Rx. et Rz. Notopterygii Qiang Huo 3-10g Expels Wind-Cold-Dampness, unblocks painful obstruction, guides Qi to the Tai Yang and Du channels and alleviates pain.
With Chuan Xiong, for headache and generalized body aches and pains associated with the common cold or painful obstruction.
With Bai Zhi, for headaches.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Extinguishes Wind
  • Clears Heat

SYNDROMES

  • Wind-Heat
  • Liver Wind Stirring Internally: Liver Fire Generates Wind

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Headache
  • Head feels swollen and splitting
  • Fever
  • Aversion to Wind
  • Red eyes and face
  • Thirst with a desire to drink
  • Constipation
  • Deep yellow urine
  • Dry cough or
  • Cough with sticky yellow sputum
  • Sore throat
  • T: Red or Red tipped
  • C: Yellow
  • P: Floating and rapid

TREATS

  • Trigeminal neuralgia
  • Cough
  • Facial pain
  • Headache
  • Sore throat
  • Red eyes
