HERB FORMULAS

QING RE GU JING TANG - 清热固经汤 - CLEAR HEAT AND CONSOLIDATE THE MENSES DECOCTION

English: Clear Heat and Consolidate the Menses Decoction
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Scutellariae Huang Qin 4.5g Clears Heat, drains Fire, detoxifies, cools the Blood, stops bleeding and calms the fetus.
With Zhi Zi, for irregular vaginal bleeding due to Liver Fire.
Fr. Gardeniae Zhi Zi 6g Clears Heat, reduces Fire, eliminates irritability, cools the Blood, relieves toxicity and stops bleeding by astringing.
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 9g Clears Heat and cools the Blood.
With E Jiao, for uterine hemorrhage due to Heat from Deficiency.
Cx. Lycii Di Gu Pi 6g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, drains Yin Deficiency Fire and stops bleeding.
With Huang Qin, for Blood Deficiency, Steaming Bone Disorder, or other recurrent afternoon fevers.
Rx. Sanguisorbae Di Yu 6g Cools the Blood and stops bleeding.
Nodus Nelumbinis Nuciferae Ou Jie 6g Stops bleeding, astringes leakage of Blood, removes Blood Stasis and eliminates Heat from the Blood.
Fr. Trachycarpi Zong Lu Zi 4.5g Astringes leakage of Blood and stops bleeding.
With E Jiao, for bleeding during pregnancy.
Colla Corii Asini E Jiao 6g Tonifies the Blood, nourishes the Blood and stops bleeding.
Plastrum Testudinis Gui Ban 12g Nourishes Yin, anchors the Yang, benefits the Kidneys, nourishes the Blood, stabilizes the Chong and Ren channels, cools the Blood and stops uterine bleeding.
Concha Ostreae Mu Li 3g Calms the Liver, benefits the Yin, anchors Floating Yang and prevents leakage of fluids.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 3g Moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Full-Heat and Fire
  • Cools Blood
  • Stops bleeding
  • Nourishes the Yin fluids

SYNDROMES

  • Blood Heat
  • Heat excess injuring Yin and Blood.

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Spotting or bleeding between periods
  • Flooding Blood, before period should begin or
  • Trickling blood for a long time after the end of the period
  • Bright red or dark red menstrual blood,
  • Red face
  • Thirst
  • Agitation
  • General feeling of Heat
  • Dark yellow urine
  • Constipation
  • T: Red
  • C: Dry-Yellow
  • P: Rapid (thin if Deficiency Heat) or Forceful, rapid and overflowing

TREATS

  • Metrorrhagia
  • Threatened miscarriage
