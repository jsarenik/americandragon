HERB FORMULAS

YI YI FU ZI BAI JIANG SAN - 薏苡附子敗醬散 - COIX, ACONITE AND PATRINIA POWDER

English: Coix, Aconite Accessory Root and Patrinia Powder
Also Known As: Coix, Aconite and Patrinia Powder
Coix, Aconite and Thlaspi Combination

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Sm., Coicis Yi Yi Ren 24-30g Strengthens the Spleen, resolves Dampness, clears Heat, expels pus and clears Damp-Heat.
Rx. Aconiti Lateralis Preparata Zhi Fu Zi 6-9g Disperses Cold and Dampness, warms the channels and stops pain.
Hb. Patriniae Bai Jiang Cao 15-18g Clears Heat, resolves toxicity and expels pus and reduces abscesses.
With Yi Yi Ren, for suppurations due to smoldering Damp-Heat.
With Yi Yi Ren and Zhi Fu Zi, for Suppurative Intestinal abscess without Heat symptoms.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Expels pus
  • Reduces abscess
  • Reduces swelling
  • Warms Yang
  • Disperses Phlegm

SYNDROMES

  • Intestinal abscess where pus has already formed due to Damp-Heat and Blood Stasis in the Lower Jiao

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Abdominal distention
  • Dry, scaly skin
  • Tightness
  • A mass-like appearance
  • Softness on palpation
  • Absence of thirst
  • Maybe an aversion to drinks
  • Tenderness and soreness of the lower abdomen
  • Lower abdominal cramps
  • No fever
  • Condition fails to improve
  • A right-sided abdominal swelling that is soft when touched and not very painful
  • Cold extremities
  • A gray or pale complexion
  • T: Pale
  • C: Thin and white
  • P: Rapid or Rapid, empty and weak

TREATS

  • Intestinal abscess
  • Acute and chronic appendicitis
  • Abscesses that fail to resolve
  • Pemphigus
  • Psoriasis
  • Endometritis
  • Metritis
  • Keloids
  • Focal peritonitis
  • Palm keritosis
  • Warts
  • Chronic sinusitis
  • Genital warts
  • Eczema
  • Neurodermatitis
  • Scleroderma
  • Prostatitis
  • Pelvic inflammatory disease
  • Acute and chronic cholecystitis
  • Chronic osteomyelitis
  • Hemorrhoidal fistula
  • Fungal diseases
  • Vaginal discharge
  • Abdominal fullness and distention

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with a high fever, a flooding, slippery pulse, severe abdominal pain and constipation.
