HERB FORMULAS

QI GUAN YAN PIAN

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Fol. Eriobotryae Pi Pa Ye 4.5-16g Transforms Phlegm, clears Lung Heat and descends Lung Qi.
With Xing Ren, for Lung Heat induced non-productive cough or cough with viscous sputum that is difficult to expectorate, with chest pain and a dry throat.
With Sang Ye, harmonizes the Lungs and Liver.
Fr. Jujube Da Zao 10-30g Tonifies the Spleen and Stomach, augments Qi and moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs.
Rx. Codonopsis Dang Shen 6-14g Tonifies the Middle Jiao,augments Qi, and tonifies the Lungs.
With Fu Ling, for Spleen and Stomach Deficiency.
Fol. Mori Sang Ye 4.5-14g Disperses and scatters Wind-Heat, clears Heat from the Lungs, stops coughing and moistens Dryness.
With Shi Gao, for Warm Dryness attacking the Lung with fever, a hacking cough and a dry, parched throat.
Rx. Peucedani Qian Hu 3-10g Expels Phlegm, descends Qi and expels Wind-Heat.
Sm. Armeniacae Xing Ren 3-10g Stops coughing, calms wheezing, moistens the Intestines and unblocks the bowels.
With Ma Huang, for coughing and wheezing due to Excess.
With Ma Huang and Shi Gao, for coughing, thirst and wheezing due to Excess with Lung Heat.
With Sang Ye, for headaches, fever and a dry non-productive cough from External Warm-Dryness disorders.
Rx. Polygalae Yuan Zhi 3-15g Expels Phlegm, opens the Orifices, clears the Lungs and stops coughing.
Exocarpium Citri Reticulatae Rubrum Ju Hong 3-9g Dries Dampness, transforms Phlegm and Descends Qi.
Bul. Fritillariae Thunbergii Zhe Bei Mu 3-12g Clears Heat, transforms Phlegm and stops coughing.
Flos Farfarae Kuan Dong Hua 4.5-10g Moistens the Lungs, descends Qi, stops coughing and transforms Phlegm.
With Xing Ren, for coughing and wheezing due to Phlegm Obstruction.
Fr. Aristolochiae Ma Dou Ling 3-10g Clears the Lungs, transforms Phlegm, stops coughing and calms wheezing.
Fr. Schisandrae Wu Wei Zi 3-9g Astringes the leakage of Lung Qi, stops coughing and tonifies Qi.
With Gan Jiang and Xi Xin, for Cold in the Lung channel with incessant coughing and wheezing.
With Gan Jian, Xi Xin and Ma Huang, for cough and wheezing from cold Thin Mucus.
With Ma Huang and Xi Xin, for coughing due to Wind-Cold.
Rz. Zingiberis Gan Jiang 3-10g Warms the Lungs and transforms thin mucus.
With Wu Wei Zi, Xing Ren and Ma Huang, for coughing and wheezing due to Cold congested Fluids blocking the descent of Lung Qi.
Rx. Platycodi Jie Geng 3-10g Opens the Lungs, spreads Lung Qi, expels Phlegm and benefits the throat.
Rx. Stemonae Bai Bu 3-12g Moistens the Lungs and stops coughing.
Hb. cum Rx. Asari Xi Xin 0.5-5g Disperses Wind-Cold and Internal Cold, alleviates pain, warms the Lungs and transforms Phlegm and Thin Mucus.
With Ma Huang, alleviates cough and wheezing due to Phlegm caused by Wind-Cold.
With Wu Wei Zi, regulates the opening and closing of the Lungs, alleviating cough and calming wheezing for patients with existing thin mucus who are exposed to Wind-Cold leading to acute wheezing and coughing of clear thin sputum.
With Ma Huang and Gan Jiang, for Cold Phlegm and Fluid Attacking the Lungs.
With Ma Huang, Gan Jiang and Wu Wei Zi, for Exterior Wind-Cold or Lung Cold.
Hb. Ephedrae Ma Huang 1.5-10g Induces sweating, releases the Exterior, disseminates and facilitates Lung Qi, calms wheezing and stops coughing.
Cx. Cinnamomi Rou Gui 1.5-5g Disperses deep Cold, warms the channels, unblocks the channels and vessels and alleviates pain.
Poria Fu Ling 9-18g Promotes urination, leaches out Dampness and strengthens the Spleen.
Pumex Fu Shi 6-15g Clears Heat from the Lungs and transforms Phlegm.
Fr. Perillae Zi Su Zi 3-10g Stops coughing, calms wheezing, descends Qi, dissolves Phlegm, moistens the Intestines and unblocks the stool.
With Zhe Bei Mu, for coughing and wheezing with copious, viscous sputum due to Phlegm Damp Obstruction.
With Xing Ren, for coughing and wheezing with constipation.
Rx. Scutellariae Huang Qin 3-18g Clears Heat and dries Dampness.
Gypsum Fibrosum Shi Gao 15-60g Clears Excess Heat from the Lungs.
With Ma Huang, for coughing from Heat Obstructing the Lungs with wheezing, flaring of the nostrils and fever.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Activates the Spleen
  • Dries Dampness
  • Expels Phlegm
  • Purges the Lungs
  • Warms Yang
  • Clears Heat

SYNDROMES

  • Phlegm-Damp Obstructs the Lungs in the elderly

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Cough with copious, thin, white, frothy sputum
  • Chest congestion with a full feeling
  • Dyspnea which is worse when lying down
  • Sometimes asthma
  • T: Pink
  • C: Thick, white and greasy or sticky
  • P: Slippery

TREATS

  • Cough
  • Bronchial asthma
  • Bronchitis
  • Bronchiectasis
  • Pneumonia
  • Emphysema
  • COPD
