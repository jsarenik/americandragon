HERB FORMULAS

HUAI HUA SAN - 槐花散 - SOPHORA JAPONICA FLOWER POWDER

English: Sophora Japonica Flower Powder
Also Known As: Sophora Powder
Sophora Flower Powder
Sophora Flower Formula

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Dry-fried Flos Sophorae Chao Huai Hua 12g Cools the Blood, cools the Liver and stops bleeding.
With Ce Bai Ye, for hemafecia, hematuria, uterine bleeding, hematemesis, and epistaxis.
With Jing Jie Sui and Fu Chao Zhi Ke, for hemafecia associated with Intestinal Wind.
With Ce Bai Ye, Jing Jie Sui and Fu Chao Zhi Ke, for hematochezia with bright red Blood caused by Damp-Heat in the Large Intestine.
Cacumen Platycladi Ce Bai Ye 12g Cools the Blood, stops bleeding and clears Damp-Heat.
Spica Schizonepetae Jing Jie Sui 6g Stops bleeding.
With Ce Bai Ye, Chao Huai Hua and Fu Chao Zhi Ke, for rectal bleeding from Intestinal Wind or hemorrhoids.
Bran-fried Fr. Aurantii Fu Chao Zhi Ke 6-9g Promotes the movement of Qi, reduces distention and pressure and resolves hardenings.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Cools the Intestines
  • Stops bleeding
  • Disperses Wind
  • Promotes the movement of Qi
  • Relaxes the Intestines

SYNDROMES

  • Wind-Heat in the Stomach and Intestines (Intestinal Wind)
  • Damp-Heat in the Stomach and Intestines

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Bright red bleeding from the Intestines during defecation that typically precedes, but may follow the passage of stool
  • Hematochezia
  • Hemafecia
  • Hemorrhoids with either bright red or dark red Blood
  • T: Red
  • P: Wiry and rapid or Soggy and rapid

TREATS

  • Amoebic dysentery
  • Colitis
  • Rectal prolapse
  • Rectal polyp
  • Ulcerative colitis
  • Hemorrhoids
  • Anal fissure
  • Rectal bleeding
  • Hemafecia

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Do not use long term.
  • Use with caution for those with Middle Jiao Deficiency Cold.
  • Contraindicated for those with rectal bleeding with symptoms of Q i or Yin Deficiency.
