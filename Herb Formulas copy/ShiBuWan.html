HERB FORMULAS

SHI BU WAN - 十补囊 - TEN SUPPLEMENTING PILLS

English: Ten Supplementing Pills
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Aconiti Lateralis Preparata Zhi Fu Zi 9g Restores Devastated Yang, warms Ming Men Fire, assists Heart, Kidney and Spleen Yang, disperses Cold and Dampness, warms the channels and stops pain.
With Ru Gui, powerfully tonifies Kidney Yang, for Kidney Yang Deficiency with lower back pain and weakness in the legs and knees.
Cx. Cinnamomi Rou Gui 3g Warms the Kidneys, Spleen and Heart, strengthens Yang and Ming Men Fire, disperses deep Cold, warms the channels, unblocks the channels and vessels and alleviates pain.
With Zhi Fu Zi, for Deficiency of Ming Men Fire with impotence or infertility.
Fr. Foeniculi Xiao Hui Xiang 9g Warms the Liver and Kidneys, expels Cold and alleviates pain.
With Bu Gu Zhi, for Kidney Yang Deficiency with clear, frequent, profuse urination or spermatorrhea or dysmenorrhea with lower back ache, late periods and coldness in the lower abdomen.
Fr. Litseae Bi Cheng Qie 6g Warms the Middle, stops pain and dispels Cold Qi from the Bladder.
Sm. Trigonellae Hu Lu Ba 9g Warms the Kidneys, disperses Dampness and alleviates pain.
With Zhi Fu Zi and Bu Gu Zhi, for pain and Cold sensations in the lower abdomen, lower extremities, and lower back.
With Xiao Hui Xiang, for Cold-type bulging disorders and abdominal distention due to Cold.
Salt stir-fried Rx. Morindae Officinalis Ba Ji Tian 9g Tonifies the Kidneys and strengthens Yang.
Rx. Aucklandiae Mu Xiang 6g Promotes the movement of Qi and alleviates pain.
Stir-fried Fr. Toosendan Chuan Lian Zi 9g Dredges Liver Qi, releases Stagnation, stops pain.
With Xiao Hui Xiang, strongly stops pain and regulates Qi to treat bulging disorders and mobile masses in the lower abdomen.
Rz. Corydalis Yin Yang Huo 9g Tonifies the Kidneys, strengthens Yang and increases the libido.
Fr. Psoraliae Bu Gu Zhi 9g Tonifies the Kidneys, strengthens Yang, stabilizes Jing and astringes urine.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Warms and supplements Kidney Yang

SYNDROMES

  • Deficiency of Ming Men Fire

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Ice-Cold genitals
  • Hernia
  • A retracted scrotum
  • Long-term cold genitals
  • Low back weakness and pain
  • Cold limbs
  • Aversion to Cold
  • Qi Deficiency
  • Lassitude
  • A bright white facial complexion
  • Day break diarrhea
  • Profuse, clear urination
  • Impotence
  • Spermatorrhea
  • Premature ejaculation
  • Possible hernia
  • Severe cases: Edema
  • T: Pale, fat and moist with possible teethmarks
  • C: White
  • P: Slow or Empty

TREATS

  • Cold genitals
  • Impotence
  • Spermatorrhea
  • Hernia
