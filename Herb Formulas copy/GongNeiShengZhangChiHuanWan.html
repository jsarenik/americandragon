HERB FORMULAS

GONG NEI SHENG ZHANG CHI HUAN WAN - 宫内生长迟丸 - INTRA-UTERINE GROWTH RETARDATION PILLS

English: Intra-Uterine Growth Retardation Pills
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. et Rz. Rhei Da Huang 180g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, drains Heat, purges accumulations, cools the Blood, reduces Fire toxicity and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Mang Xiao, for abscesses, swellings and toxic sores.
With Sheng Di Huang, for Deficiency Fire.
Rx. Scutellariae Huang Qin 150g Clears Heat, dries Dampness, drains Fire, detoxifies, cools the Blood, stops bleeding and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Da Huang, for constipation.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 210g Clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity, inhibits the growth of cancer cells, moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs and guides the herbs to all twelve channels.
Sm. Persicae Tao Ren 360g Breaks up Blood Stasis, invigorates Blood circulation, moistens the Intestines, unblocks the bowels and drains abscesses.
With Xing Ren, for Blood Stasis with pain in the chest, abdomen or throughout the body.
Sm. Armeniacae Xing Ren 360g Moistens the Intestines, unblocks bowels and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 600g Clears Heat, cools the Blood and nourishes Yin.
Resina Toxicodendri Gan Qi 60g Breaks up Blood Stasis, opens the channels and purges hardness.
With Da Huang, Shui Zhi and Tao Ren, for amenorrhea due to Blood Stasis.
Tabanus Meng Chong 70g Breaks up Blood Stasis, reduces abdominal masses, unblocks the Blood vessels, stops pain and treats uterine fibroids.
With Shui Zhi, resolves severe Blood Stasis with mania and abdominal masses or severe Blood accumulation following trauma.
With Shui Zhi and Da Huang, for amenorrhea from Blood Stasis.
With Shui Zhi, Sheng Di Huang and Tao Ren, for irregular menstruation.
Natrii Sulfas Mang Xiao 300g Purges accumulations, guides out Stagnation, softens hardness, moistens Dryness, clears Heat and reduces swelling.
With Da Huang and Gan Cao, for moderate constipation due to accumulation of Excess Heat.
Hirudo Shui Zhi 70g Breaks up and drives out Blood Stasis, reduces fixed masses and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Tao Ren and Meng Chong, for abdominal pain and hardness with black tarry stools.
With Tao Ren, Meng Chong and Da Huang, for Blood Stasis in the lower abdomen with amenorrhea and masses.
With Mang Xiao and Da Huang, for fever, delirium, abdominal distention, urinary incontinence and, in severe cases, a dull dusky-yellow discoloration of the skin from Blood Stasis due to Invasion of Heat.

  • Softens hardness

SYNDROMES
