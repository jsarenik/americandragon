HERB FORMULAS

ZI LU XIAN ZONG TANG JIA WEI - 紫鹿仙总汤加味 - PLACENTA AND DEER ANTLER IMMORTALS GATHERING DECOCTION WITH ADDED FLAVORS

English: Placenta and Deer Antler Immortals Gathering Decoction with Added Flavors
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Placenta Hominis Zi He Che 1-3g Tonifies the Liver and Kidneys, strengthens Jing, tonifies Qi and nourishes the Blood.
With Shu Di Huang, for chronic weakness and debility.
With Shu Di Huang, Dang Gui and Bai Shao, for Blood Deficiency.
Colla Cervi Cornus Lu Jiao Jiao 9g Nourish and tonifies Qi and Blood and stops bleeding.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 9g Tonifies, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood, disperses Cold and regulates the menses.
With Bai Shao, Shu Di Huang and Chuan Xiong, for menstrual irregularity, amenorrhea, and dysmenorrhea due to Blood Deficiency or Blood Stasis.
With Shu Di Huang, for Heat symptoms due to Yin Deficiency and Devastated Blood.
Rz. Chuanxiong Chuan Xiong 6-9g Invigorates the Blood and promotes the movement of Qi.
With Dang Gui, for Blood Deficiency causing menstrual problems including dysmenorrhea, scanty menses, and amenorrhea.
Rx. Rehmanniae Preparata Shu Di Huang 12-15g Nourishes Liver and Kidney Yin, nourishes Jing and Blood and fills the Marrow.
With Dang Gui, for Blood Deficiency marked by dizziness, palpitations, insomnia and menstrual dysfunction.
Rx. Paeoniae Alba Bai Shao 9g Nourishes the Blood, regulates menstruation, calms Liver Yang, softens the Liver and relieves pain.
With Dang Gui, nourishes Yin and Blood.
With Dang Gui and Shu Di HUang, for dizziness, blurred vision, and dysmenorrhea due to Blood Deficiency or Stasis.
Fr. Rubi Fu Pen Zi 9-15g Tonifies and stabilizes the Kidneys, astringes Jing and urine and tonifies Yang.
With Tu Si Zi, Gou Qi Zi and Wu Wei Zi, for impotence and spermatorrhea due to Kidney Deficiency.
With Gou Qi Zi, Tu Si Zi and Che Qian Zi, for lower back pain and impotence due to Kidney Deficiency.
Sm. Cuscutae Tu Si Zi 9-12g Strengthens Yang, nourishes Yin, astringes Jing and urine, benefits the marrow and tonifies the Kidneys and Liver.
With Che Qian Zi and Shu Di Huang, for blurred vision and spots before the eyes due to Kidney and Liver Deficiency.
Fr. Lycii Gou Qi Zi 9-15g Nourishes and tonifies Liver and Kidney Blood and Yin and benefits Jing.
With Shu Di Huang, for Liver and Kidney Deficiency.
Fr. Schisandrae Wu Wei Zi 9g Tonifies the Kidneys and astringes Jing.
Sm. Plantaginis Che Qian Zi 6-9g Promotes urination and clears Damp-Heat.
With Shu Di Huang and Tu Si Zi, for Liver and Kidney Deficiency causing cataracts with diminished vision.
With Shu Di Huang, Gou Qi Zi and Tu Si Zi, for Liver and Kidney Deficiency causing cataracts with diminished vision and dizziness
Rz. Curculiginis Xian Mao 9-15g Tonifies the Kidneys and strengthens Yang.
With Yin Yang Huo, for Menopausal hypertension.
Hb. Epimedii Yin Yang Huo 9-15g Tonifies the Kidneys, strengthens Yang and increases the libido.
Rx. Cyathulae Chuan Niu Xi 9-15g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, unblocks the menses and tonifies the Kidneys and Liver.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Nourishes the Kidneys
  • Nourishes Blood
  • Tonifies Jing

SYNDROMES

  • Kidney Yin and Jing Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • A young woman has reached 18 years of age and her menses
    have not come or
  • At the beginning of the tide it is comparatively slow or tardy, or
  • The menses has come but eventually becomes later and scantier and evolves into amenorrhea
  • A dark, gloomy facial complexion
  • Dizziness
  • Tinnitus
  • Soreness and weakness of the low back and knees
  • Possibly a dry mouth and throat
  • Tidal fever
  • Night sweats
  • Both cheeks tidally red
  • Five sole Heat
  • Dry skin
  • Dry, astringent eyelids
  • Constipation
  • Scanty, reddish urination
  • Heart palpitations
  • T: Red or Pale
  • C: Scanty
  • P: Deep and wiry or Deep and thready or Possibly floating at the inch and/or cubit positions if Yin fails to control Yang or Rapid if there is Heat

TREATS

  • Amenorrhea
