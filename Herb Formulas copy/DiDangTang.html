HERB FORMULAS

DI DANG TANG - 抵当汤 - APPROPRIATE DECOCTION - RHUBARB AND LEECH COMBINATION

English: Appropriate Decoction
Also Known As: Rhubarb and Leech Combination
Resistance Decoction

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Hirudo Shui Zhi 9-12g Breaks up and drives out Blood Stasis and reduces fixed masses.
With Da Huang, regulates Yin and Yang to quickly attack and break up Blood Stasis.
With Meng Chong, resolves severe Blood Stasis with mania, and abdominal masses or severe Blood accumulation following trauma.
With Tao Ren and Meng Chong, for abdominal pain and hardness with black tarry stools.
Tabanus Meng Chong 9-12g Breaks up Blood Stasis, reduces abdominal masses, unblocks the Blood vessels, heals injuries and stops pain.
With Shui Zhi and Da Huang, for amenorrhea from Blood Stasis.
Sm. Persicae Tao Ren 6-9g Breaks up Blood Stasis, invigorates Blood circulation, moistens the Intestines, unblocks the bowels and drains abscesses.
With Da Huang, for severe abdominal infection.
Wine-washed Rx. et Rz. Rhei Jiu Xi Da Huang 9g Cools Upper Jiao Fire and directs it downward to treat Large Intestine Heat toxin.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Breaks up and dispels Blood Stasis
  • Clears Heat

SYNDROMES

  • Blood Stagnation in the Lower Jiao due to Blood Heat

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Firmness and distention in the lower abdomen which is painful and gives resistance on pressure
  • Smooth urination
  • Manic behavior
  • Forgetfulness
  • Black stools that are easy to expel
  • Delayed menstruation
  • Amenorrhea
  • Irregular menstruation
  • Coagulated blood
  • Thirst
  • Dry mouth
  • Polyuria
  • Liver spots indicating stagnant blood found on the face, lips, tongue, limbs and finger nails
  • T: Purplish
  • C: Normal
  • P: Deep, slow and irregular

TREATS

  • Acute pelvic inflammatory disease
  • Leiomyoma
  • Endometriosis
  • Dysmenorrhea
  • Amenorrhea
  • Lochioschesis
  • Thrombophlebitis
  • Postpartum thrombophlebitis
  • Mania
  • Epilepsy
  • Schistosomiasis
  • Intrauterine myxedema
  • Anxiety
  • Hyperglycemia
  • Arteriosclerosis
  • Stroke
  • Benign prostatic hypertrophy
  • Testicular tuberculosis
  • Acute urinary retention
  • Angina pectoris
  • Cerebrovascular disease
  • Cor pulmonare
  • Schizophrenia
  • Icteric hepatitis
  • Chronic colitis
  • Habitual constipation
  • Gangrene
  • External trauma
  • Coronary Heart disease
  • Obesity
  • Irregular menstruation

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Use only for Excess-type Blood Stasis patterns in the Lower Jiao.
  • Contraindicated for those with Qi Deficiency.
  • Contraindicated during pregnancy.
