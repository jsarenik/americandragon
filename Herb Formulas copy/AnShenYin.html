HERB FORMULAS

AN SHEN YIN - 安神飲 - CALM THE SPIRIT DRINK

English: Calm the Spirit Drink
Also Known As: Spirit Pearls

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Sm. Zizyphi Spinosae Suan Zao Ren 9-18g Nourishes Heart Yin, tonifies Liver Blood, calms the Spirit, astringes sweat and generates fluids.
With Yuan Zhi, balances the contracting, introverted and dispersing, extroverted manifestations of Spirit to ensure the proper balance of openness and sobriety.
Caulis Polygoni Multiflori Ye Jiao Teng 9-30g Nourishes the Heart Yin and Blood, calms the Spirit, unblocks channels and dispels Wind-Dampness.
With Fu Shen, calms the Spirit.
Bul. Lilii Bai He 9-30g
 Clears the Heart, calms the Spirit, nourishes Stomach Yin, harmonizes the Middle Jiao, moistens the Lungs, clears Heat and stops coughing.
With Suan Zao Ren, for difficulty falling and staying asleep.
Poria Pararadicis Fu Shen 9-18g Quiets the Heart, calms the Spirit and soothes the nerves.
With Shi Chang Pu and Yuan Zhi, for insomnia and palpitations due to Spleen and Heart Insufficiency or Phlegm Obstructing the Heart.
Sm. Amomi Bai Dou Kou 2-10g Promotes the movement of Qi, aromatically transforms Dampness, strengthens the Stomach, warms the Middle Jiao, descends Qi and stops vomiting.
Rz. Acori Tatarinowii Shi Chang Pu 4 -9g Opens the Orifices, vaporizes Phlegm, removes filth, calms the Spirit, disperses Wind, transforms turbid Dampness, harmonizes the Middle Jiao, awakens the Spleen, promotes the movement of Qi, promotes Blood flow, reduces swelling and alleviates Wind-Damp Bi.
With Yuan Zhi and Fu Shen, for forgetfulness, tinnitus and impaired hearing.
Rz. Chuanxiong Chuan Xiong 3-10g Invigorates the Blood, promotes the movement of Qi, expels Wind and alleviates pain.
Sm. Caesalpiniae Descapetatae Yun Shi 9-15g Clears Heat, eliminates Dampness and kills parasites.
Sm. Persicae Tao Ren 4-10g Breaks up Blood Stasis , invigorates Blood circulation, moistens the Intestines and unblocks the bowels.
With Xing Ren, for constipation due Lung Qi Stagnation, Blood Stasis with pain in the chest, abdomen or throughout the body and wheezing and coughing due to Lung constraint.
Sm. Armeniacae Xing Ren 3-10g Stops cough, calms wheezing, moistens the Intestines and unblocks bowels.
Rx. Polygalae Yuan Zhi 3-15g Calms the Spirit, sedates the Heart and clears the channel joining the Heart and Kidney Expels Phlegm, opens the Orifices, clears the Lungs and stops coughing.
With Fu Shen and Suan Zao Ren, for irritability, disorientation, palpitations, anxiety and insomnia due to Heart Blood Deficiency or Qi Stagnation.
Talcum Hua Shi 9-18g Releases Summeheat, resolves Dampness and expels Damp-Heat.
Camptosorus Sibiricus Ma Deng Cao   A famous herbal folk medicine widely distributed in North China, which has good effects on vascular inflammation, diabetic complications and traumatic injury.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Calms the Shen
  • Soothes the Heart and Lungs by descending Qi
  • Nourishes Heart Blood and Lung Yin
  • Opens the Orifices of the Heart by removing Phlegm in the Upper Jiao
  • Promotes the movement of Qi
  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Aromatically transforms Dampness
  • Expels Damp-Heat

SYNDROMES

  • Heart Blood and Lung Yin Deficiency with Wind-Damp-Heat

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Palpitations
  • Irritability
  • Anxiety
  • Easily startled
  • Insomnia
  • Much dreaming
  • Poor memory
  • Dizziness
  • Pale face and lips
  • Fatigue
  • Loose stools
  • Poor appetite
  • Dizziness
  • Pale face
  • Weak voice
  • Reluctance to speak
  • Sweating with little or no exertion
  • Dry skin
  • Dry hair
  • Scanty periods
  • Severe, dry hacking cough or
  • A chronic dry cough
  • No sputum expectorated
  • Small amounts of difficult to expectorate sputum
  • Blood tinged sputum (when Yin Deficiency Fire is present)
  • Afternoon fever
  • Night sweats
  • Malar flush (especially in afternoon)
  • Five Sole Heat
  • Dry mouth and throat
  • Itching in throat
  • Hoarse voice, low voice or loss of voice
  • Emaciation
  • T: Pale or Red and dry
  • C: None or Thin and white
  • P: Thready and rapid or Weak or Thin and choppy

TREATS

  • Gu syndrome
  • Dry hair
