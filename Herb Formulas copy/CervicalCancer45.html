HERB FORMULAS

CERVICAL CANCER #45

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Flos Lonicerae Jin Yin Hua 20g Clears Heat, resolves Fire toxicity and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Dang Gui, reduces obstruction and toxicity for swollen, painful sores and necrosis of surrounding tissue.
Hb. Taraxaci Pu Gong Ying 20g Reduces abscesses, dissipates nodules, relieves Fire toxicity and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Jin Yin Hua and Zi Hua Di Ding, for all hot, painful, hard, and deep-rooted boils.
Sm. Benincasae Dong Gua Zi 20g Clears Heat, expels Phlegm, expels pus, moistens the Lungs and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Astragali Huang Qi 20g Tonifies Qi and Blood, promotes the discharge of pus, generates flesh, expels toxins and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Dang Gui, for Qi and Blood Deficiency with chronic non-healing sores, numbness, painful obstruction and paresthesia.
Hb. Hedyotis Diffusae` Bai Hua She She Cao 15g Clears Heat, strongly relieves Fire toxins, reduces abscesses and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Flos Sophorae Immaturus Huai Hua Mi 15g Cools the Blood and stops bleeding.
Olibanum Ru Xiang 10g Invigorates the Blood, promotes the movement of Qi, reduces swelling and generates flesh.
With Mo Yao, for chronic, ulcerated and non-healing sores.
Myrrh Mo Yao 10g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, alleviates pain, reduces swelling, generates flesh and promotes healing.
With Ru Xiang, disseminates and unblocks the organs and dredges the channels and collaterals.
Rz. Cyperi Carbonisatus Xiang Fu Tan 10g Spreads and regulates Liver Qi, regulates menstruation, alleviates pain and stops bleeding.
Massa Medicata Fermentata Shen Qu 10g Reduces Food Stagnation, promotes digestion, harmonizes the Stomach and Moves Qi.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 12g Tonifies, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood, reduces swelling, expels pus, generates flesh and alleviates pain.
With Huang Qi, for Qi and Blood Deficiency.
Hb. Violae Zi Hua Di Ding 12g Clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity, clears hot sores and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Pu Gong Ying, for breast abscesses, furuncles, boils and carbuncles and Damp-Heat jaundice.
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 12g Clears Heat, cools the Blood and nourishes Yin.
Rx. Ginseng Ren Shen 2g Powerfully tonifies Yuan Qi and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Huang Qi, for Qi Deficiency with general debility, anorexia and spontaneous sweat.
Sanguis Draconis Xue Jie 1g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, alleviates pain, stops bleeding, protects the surface of ulcers, prevents decay and generates flesh.
Lignum Aquilariae Chen Xiang 1g  

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Heat
  • Relieves toxicity
  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Breaks up Blood Stasis
  • Tonifies Qi
  • Inhibits the growth of cancer cells

SYNDROMES

  • Toxic Heat with Blood Stagnation and Qi Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Tumor develops quickly
  • Long-standing illness
  • Pain
  • Redness
  • Ulceration
  • Discharge of yellow or bloody, foul-smelling fluid
  • Fatigue
  • Fever
  • Anxiety
  • Dry mouth
  • Thirst
  • Dark urine
  • Bitter taste
  • Red eyes
  • Insomnia
  • Pain that is fixed, stabbing and /or severe
  • Pain is worse with pressure
  • Dark complexion
  • Fixed, palpable masses
  • Bleeding with clots
  • Purple lips
  • Purple nails
  • Blue, dark or purplish discoloration of skin in small or large areas,
  • Varicose and spider veins
  • Dry skin
  • Cold hands and feet
  • Tendency to hemorrhage dark blood or clots
  • Fatigue
  • Loose stools
  • Poor appetite
  • Dizziness
  • Pale face
  • Weak voice
  • Shortness of breath
  • Reluctance to speak
  • Sweating with little or no exertion
  • Palpitations
  • Insomnia
  • Dry skin
  • Dry hair
  • Poor memory
  • Paleness
  • Scanty periods
  • T: Red or Purple, possible purple or red spots or maybe Pale or Pale, thin and dry
  • C: White or Yellow and greasy or Thick, sticky, dry and yellow
  • P: Wiry or Rolling and rapid or Wiry, overflowing and rapid or Choppy or Wiry and choppy or Weak or Thin and choppy

TREATS

  • Metastasis of cervical cancer
