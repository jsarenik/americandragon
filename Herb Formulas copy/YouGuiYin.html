HERB FORMULAS

YOU GUI YIN - 右歸飲 - RESTORE THE RIGHT DRINK

English: Restore the Right (Kidney) Drink
Also Known As: Replenishing the Yang Decoction

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Rehmanniae Preparata Shu Di Huang 6-60g Nourishes Liver and Kidney Yin, nourishes Jing and Blood and fills the Marrow.
With Shan Zhu Yu, nourishes Kidney Yin and secures Jing, while restraining urination and sweating.
With Shan Zhu Yu and Shan Yao, for dizziness, weak and sore back, impotence, spermatorrhea and night sweats, due to Kidney and Liver Yin Deficiency.
Dry-fried Rz. Dioscoreae Chao Shan Yao 6-15g Tonifies the Spleen, tonifies Kidney Yin and astringes Jing.
Fr. Corni Shan Zhu Yu 3-12g Stabilizes the Kidneys, astringes Jing, tonifies Liver and Kidney Yin and strengthens Kidney Yang.
With Shan Yao, Shu Di Huang, and Gou Qi Zi, for true Yin Deficiency.
Fr. Lycii Gou Qi Zi 6-15g Nourishes and tonifies Liver and Kidney Blood and Yin and benefits Jing.
With Shu Di Huang, for Liver and Kidney Deficiency.
With Shu Di Huang, Rou Gui, Zhi Fu Zi and Shan Zhu Yu, for Kidney Yang Deficiency.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Preparata Zhi Gan Cao 3-6g Tonifies the Spleen and augments Qi and moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs.
Cx. Eucommiae Du Zhong 6-10g Tonifies the Kidneys and Liver and Tonifies Kidney Yang.
Cx. Cinnamomi Rou Gui 3-6g Warms the Kidneys, Spleen and Heart and strengthens the Yang and Ming Men Fire.
With Zhi Fu Zi, powerfully tonifies Kidney Yang, for Kidney Yang Deficiency with lower back pain and weakness in the legs and knees.
Rx. Aconiti Lateralis Preparata Zhi Fu Zi 3-9g Restores Devastated Yang, warms Ming Men Fire and assists Heart, Kidney and Spleen Yang.
With Rou Gui and Shu Di Huang, for Kidney Yang Deficiency with waning of Ming Men Fire with aversion to Cold, weak and sore back and legs, impotence, and urinary frequency.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Warms Kidney Yang
  • Replenishes Jing

SYNDROMES

  • Kidney Yang Deficiency
  • True Cold and False Heat
  • Atrophy disorder (Wei Syndrome) due to Kidney Qi and Yang Deficiency
