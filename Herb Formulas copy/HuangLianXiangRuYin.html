HERB FORMULAS

HUANG LIAN XIANG RU YIN - 黄连香薷饮 - COPTIS AND MOSLA DRINK

English: Coptis and Mosla Drink
Also Known As: Coptis and Elsholtzia Drink

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rz. Coptidis Huang Lian 6-9g Clears Heat and drains Dampness.
Cx. Magnoliae Officinalis Hou Po 6-9g Promotes the movement of Qi in the Middle Jiao, resolves Stagnation, descends Qi, dries Dampness and transforms Phlegm.
With Huang Lian, for acute diarrhea associated with sudden turmoil disorder from Damp-Heat.
Hb. Moslae Xiang Ru 6-9g Induces sweating, releases the Exterior, expels Summeheat, transforms Dampness, harmonizes the Spleen and Stomach Promotes urination and reduces swelling.
With Bai Bian Dou, for Sudden turmoil disorder due to externally contracted Cold in the summertime.
With Hou Po, for Exterior Cold and Interior Dampness presenting with fever, anhidrosis, headache, thirst, vomiting and diarrhea (frequently found in Summerheat-stroke).
With Huang Lian, reduces the vomiting if taken hot.
With Hou Po and Sheng Jiang, for nausea and vomiting.
Sm. Lablab Album Bai Bian Dou 6-9g Clears Summerheat and resolves Dampness.
(Rz. Zingiberis Recens) (Sheng Jiang) (12g) (Releases the Exterior, induces perspiration, disperses Cold, warms the Middle and stops vomiting.)

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Resolves the Exterior
  • Warms Cold
  • Dispels Summerheat
  • Eliminates irritability

SYNDROMES

  • Summerheat
  • Sudden turmoil disorder

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Acute abdominal pain
  • Fever and chills, fever more pronounced or
  • Tidal fever
  • Fever is unrelieved by sweating
  • Abdominal distention
  • Thirst
  • Sweat
  • Stifling sensation in the chest
  • Restlessness
  • Dry retching
  • Anorexia
  • Foggy headache
  • Irritability
  • Heaviness
  • Nausea
  • Severe diarrhea
  • Scanty urine
  • T: Red tip and edges
  • C: Greasy white or yellow
  • P: Floating or Rapid and soft

TREATS

  • Abdominal pain
  • Abdominal fullness and distention
  • Diverticulitis
  • Crohn's disease
  • Dysentery
  • Anorexia
  • Colitis
  • Ulcerative colitis
