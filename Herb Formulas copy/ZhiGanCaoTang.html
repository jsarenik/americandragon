HERB FORMULAS

ZHI GAN CAO TANG - 炙甘草湯 - PREPARED LICORICE DECOCTION

English: Honey- fried Licorice Decoction
Also Known As: Prepared Licorice Decoction
Baked or Roasted Licorice Combination or
Fu Mai Tang (Restore the Pulse Decoction).

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Preparata Zhi Gan Cao 3-12g Tonifies the Spleen, augments Qi and nourishes the Heart.
With Ren Shen, relieves palpitations and anxiety.
With Da Zao and Ren Shen, tonifies Spleen and Stomach Qi.
With Ren Shen, Gui Zhi, Sheng Di Huang and E Jiao, for Heart Qi and Blood Deficiency with an irregular heart beat and palpitations.
Rx. Ginseng Ren Shen 3-6g Calms the Shen, strongly tonifies Yuan Qi and tonifies the Heart.
(Rx. Codonopsis) (Dang Shen) 6-18g (Tonifies the Middle Jiao and augments Qi - may substitute for Ren Shen.)
(With Zhi Gan Cao, for anorexia, fatigue and loose stools due to Spleen Deficiency and palpitations due to Heart Qi Deficiency.)
Ram. Cinnamomi Gui Zhi 3-10g Assists the Heart Yang and warms and facilitates the flow of Yang Qi in the chest.
 With Ren Shen, strongly unblocks Heart Qi.
With Zhi Gan Cao, for palpitations and shortness of breath due to injured Yang of the chest.
With Zhi Gan Cao, Ren Shen and E Jiao, for palpitations and intermittent pulse due to Heart Yin and Yang Deficiency.
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 6-48g Clears Heat, cools Heart Fire and nourishes Blood and Heart Yin.
With E Jiao for coughing, hematemesis, epistaxis, and uterine hemorrhage due to Heat from Deficiency.
Tub. Ophiopogonis Mai Men Dong 6-10g Moistens Stomach and Lung Dryness, moistens the Intestines clears the Heart and eliminates irritability.
With Sheng Di Huang, for hematemesis or epistaxis due to Yin Deficiency with Excess Heat.
Colla Corii Asini E Jiao 2-10g Enriches Yin, nourishes the Blood and moistens Dryness.
Sm. Cannabis Huo Ma Ren 3-20g Nourishes Yin and moistens Intestines.
With Sheng Di Huang and Mai Men Dong, for Yin Deficiency with constipation.
Rz. Zingiberis Recens Sheng Jiang 3-9g Normalizes the flow of Qi at the center and warms the Middle.
With Da Zao, regulates Ying and Wei.
With Gui Zhi, warms and dispels thin mucus in the Stomach with nausea, hiccup, Stomach pain, acid reflux or vomiting of clear fluid.
Fr. Zizyphi Jujube Da Zao 3-6g Benefits the Spleen, nourishes the Heart, nourishes Blood, calms the Shen and moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs.
With Zhi Gan Cao and Ren Shen, tonifies Spleen and Stomach Qi.
With Sheng Jiang, enhances the effect of Blood tonics by facilitating digestion and absorption.
White Wine Bai Jiu   Assists movement.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Augments Qi
  • Nourishes Blood
  • Enriches Yin
  • Restores the Pulse

SYNDROMES

  • Heart Qi Deficiency
  • Qi, Blood and Yin Deficiency due to worry and emotional excess injuring Heart and Spleen
  • Irregular pulses (knotted and intermittent) due to Yang Deficiency and Yin Deficiency
  • Generalized numbness due to Exhaustion of Ying and Wei
  • Lung atrophy due to Yin Deficiency

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with Heat from Yin Deficiency.
  • Contraindicated for those with severe diarrhea

NOTES
