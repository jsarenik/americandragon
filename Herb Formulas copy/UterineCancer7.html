HERB FORMULAS

UTERINE CANCER #7

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Salviae Miltiorrhizae Dan Shen 15g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, cools the Blood, reduces abscesses and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Ru Xiang, for Blood Stasis causing swelling and pain.
Rx. Astragali Huang Qi 15g Tonifies Qi and Blood, promotes the discharge of pus, generates flesh, expels toxins, relieves numbness and pain and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Rubiae Qian Cao Gen 15g Cools and invigorates the Blood, stops bleeding, dispels Blood Stasis and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Endoconcha Sepiae Hai Piao Xiao 30g Restrains, holds in and stops bleeding, secures Jing, stops discharge, resolves Dampness, promotes healing and invigorates the Blood.
With Huang Qi and Qian Cao Gen, for excessive uterine bleeding.
Rx. Adenophorae Nan Sha Shen 30g Nourishes Yin, generates fluids and clears Heat.
Hb. Violae Zi Hua Di Ding 30g Clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Hb. Taraxaci Pu Gong Ying 30g Reduces abscesses, dissipates nodules, relieves Fire toxicity and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Fr. Broussonetiae Chu Shi Zi 30g Tonifies Liver and Kidney Yin, clears the Liver and augments Kidney Qi.
Plastrum Testudinis Gui Ban 30g Nourishes Yin, anchors Yang, benefits the Kidneys, nourishes the Blood, enriches Yin, stabilizes the Chong and Ren channels, cools the Blood, stops uterine bleeding and treats non-healing sores and ulcerations.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 10g Clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Pu Gong Ying, for abscesses or other toxic swellings.
Rx. Ampelopsis Bai Lian 10g Drains Fire, relieves toxicity, eliminates Dampness, resolves lumps, reduces swelling,
relieves pain, generates flesh and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Olibanum Ru Xiang 10g Invigorates the Blood, promotes the movement of Qi, reduces swelling and generates flesh.
Myrrh Mo Yao 10g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, alleviates pain, reduces swelling, generates flesh and promotes healing.
With Ru Xiang, for swollen, painful sores and sores that are hard but not painful.
Spina Gleditsiae Zao Jiao Ci 10g Draws out toxicity, discharges pus, invigorates the Blood, reduces swelling and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Colla Corii Asini E Jiao 30g Nourishes the Blood, stops bleeding and nourishes and moistens Yin.
Mel Feng Mi 60g Moistens the Intestines and eliminates toxins.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Heat
  • Relieves toxicity
  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Nourishes Yin

SYNDROMES

  • Toxic Heat with Yin Deficiency and Blood Stagnation

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Tumor develops quickly
  • Fluid inside the breast with a lump inside it
  • Long-standing illness
  • Pain
  • Redness
  • Ulceration
  • Discharge of yellow or bloody, foul-smelling fluid
  • Fatigue
  • Fever
  • Anxiety
  • Dry mouth
  • Thirst
  • Dark urine
  • Bitter taste
  • Red eyes
  • Insomnia
  • Pain that is fixed, stabbing and /or severe
  • Pan is worse with pressure
  • Dark complexion
  • Fixed, palpable masses
  • Bleeding with clots
  • Purple lips
  • Purple nails
  • Dry skin
  • Blue, dark or purplish discoloration of skin in small or large areas,
  • Varicose and spider veins
  • Cold hands and feet
  • Tendency to hemorrhage dark blood or clots
  • Facial complexion pale and sallow
  • Both cheeks slightly red
  • Dizziness
  • Dry eyes
  • Blurred vision
  • Spots before the eyes
  • Numbness of limbs
  • Flaccid paralysis (Wei syndrome), weakness
  • Tinnitus
  • Deafness
  • Forgetfulness
  • Low back and knees sore and painful
  • Throat dry and painful
  • Five Sole Heat
  • Low-grade fever
  • Night sweats
  • Irritability
  • Insomnia
  • Stools difficult to pass
  • Constipation
  • Irregular menses or
  • Scanty menses or
  • Amenorrhea
  • Vaginal discharge
  • T: Red
  • C: White or Yellow and greasy or Thick, sticky, dry and yellow
  • P: Wiry or Rolling and rapid or Wiry, overflowing and rapid or Thready and rapid

TREATS

  • Breast cancer with uterine cancer
  • Uterine cancer

NOTES

  • Boil the first 15 herbs for 1 hour. Clear the dregs, then add the E Jiao and honey to thicken the decoction. Take 3 times a day for 60 days as one course.
