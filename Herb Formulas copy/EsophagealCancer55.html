HERB FORMULAS

ESOPHAGEAL CANCER #55

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Bul. Fritillariae Cirrhosae Chuan Bei Mu 12g Clears Heat, transforms Phlegm, stops coughing, nourishes and moistens the Lungs, dissipates nodules and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Adenophorae Nan Sha Shen 12g Nourishes Yin, moistens the Lungs, dispels Phlegm, stops coughing, nourishes the Stomach, generates fluids and clears Heat.
Endoconcha Sepiae Hai Piao Xiao 15g Restrains, holds in and stops bleeding, resolves Dampness and promotes healing.
Halloysitum Rubrum Chi Shi Zhi 9g Astringes the Intestines, stops diarrhea, contains the Blood and stops bleeding.
Rx. Ophiopogonis Mai Men Dong 9g Moistens the Lungs, nourishes Yin, resolves Phlegm, stops coughing and nourishes Stomach Yin.
With Wu Mei, for Upper or Middle Jiao wasting and thirsting disorder with intense Dryness of the throat and an unquenchable thirst.
With Nan Sha Shen, for a dry cough with little Phlegm and a parched throat due to Lung Dryness or Heat injuring the Lung Yin and a dry throat, constipation, thirst and a red tongue with little coat due to pathogenic Warmth or Heat injuring the Stomach Yin or long-term Yin Deficiency.
With Sheng Di Huang, for Hematemesis or epistaxis due to Yin Deficiency with Excess Heat.
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 9g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, nourishes Yin and generates fluids.
With Nan Sha Shen and Mai Men Dong, for Dry mouth, thirst, dry lips and a red tongue due to Stomach Yin Deficiency.
Fr. Mume Wu Mei 6g Astringes leakage of Lung Qi, stops leakage of sweat, stops coughs, generates fluids, alleviates thirst and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 3g Moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs and guides the herbs to all twelve channels.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Heat
  • Transforms Phlegm
  • Nourishes Yin
  • Inhibits the growth of cancer cells

SYNDROMES

  • Yin Deficiency with Empty Fire Rising

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Progressive dysphagia
  • Blockage of swallowed food
  • Unsmooth swallowing with pain
  • Difficulty swallowing water
  • Vomiting immediately after eating
  • Burning pain in the Stomach
  • Chest and hypochondriac pain
  • A sensation of Heat in the chest
  • Five Sole Heat
  • Constipation
  • Dry mouth and throat
  • Episodic hematuria with fresh red Blood
  • The urine may be concentrated
  • T: Red
  • C: Little or None
  • P: Thready and rapid

TREATS

  • Esophageal cancer
