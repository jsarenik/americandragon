HERB FORMULAS

REN SHEN HU TAO TANG - 人参蛤蚧汤 - GINSENG AND WALNUT DECOCTION

English: Ginseng and Walnut Decoction
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Ginseng Ren Shen 9g Powerfully tonifies Yuan Qi and tonifies Lung Qi.
Sm. Juglandis Hu Tao Ren 9g Tonifies the Kidneys, replenishes Jing,
strengthens the back and knees, warms and astringes the Lungs, resolves cold Phlegm, arrests wheezing and helps the Kidneys grasp Qi.
Rz. Zingiberis Recens Sheng Jiang 3-6g Warms the Lungs and stops cough.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Tonifies the Lungs and Kidneys
  • Relieves wheezing and dyspnea

SYNDROMES

  • Lung and Kidney Deficiency
  • Inability of the Kidneys to grasp Qi

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Patients cannot sleep due to coughing and wheezing
  • Coughing
  • Wheezing
  • Fullness of the chest
  • Inability to lie supine
  • Cold extremities
  • Facial edema
  • Fullness in the chest
  • Dyspnea
  • Asthma
  • Soft voice
  • Cough with perspiration
  • Low back pain
  • T: Pale
  • C: White
  • P: Feeble

TREATS

  • Emphysema
  • COPD
  • Facial edema
  • Bronchial asthma
  • Chronic bronchitis
  • Dyspnea

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with External conditions.
  • Contraindicated for those with full Heat.
