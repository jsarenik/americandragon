HERB FORMULAS

MING MU DI HUANG WAN #2 - 明目地黄片二号 - IMPROVE VISION WITH REHMANNIA PILL #2

English: Improve Vision Pill with Rehmannia #2
Also Known As: Improve Eyesight Formula with Pills of Six Ingredients with Rehmannia

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Fr. Lycii Gou Qi Zi 6-9g Nourishes and tonifies Liver and Kidney Blood and Yin, benefits Jing and brightens the eyes.
With Shu Di Huang, for Liver and Kidney Deficiencies.
With Ju Hua, for Liver and Kidney Deficiencies with Jing and Blood unable to rise and nourish the eyes with tinnitus, headache, and visual impairment.
Flos Chrysanthemi Ju Hua 6-9g Disperses Wind, clears Heat, calms the Liver, clears the eyes, promotes the movement of Heart Qi and Blood and stimulates Blood circulation.
With Gou Qi Zi, for Dizziness and blurred vision due to Liver and Kidney Yin Deficiencies.
With Gou Qi Zi, Shu Di Huang, Mu Dan Pi and Shan Zhu Yu, for blurred vision and dizziness due to Liver and Kidney Yin Deficiency
Fr. Tribuli Bai Ji Li 6-9g Calms the Liver, anchors the Yang, Dispels Wind-Heat and brightens the eyes.
Concha Haliotidis Jue Ming Zi 20-30g Clears Liver Heat, clears the vision, benefits the eyes, calms the Liver, anchors Liver Yang and reduces blood pressure.
With Ju Hua, for hypertension.
Rx. Rehmanniae Preparata Shu Di Huang 10-15g Tonifies the Kidneys and nourishes Jing, Yin and Blood.
With Shan Zhu Yu, nourishes Kidney Yin and secures Jing, while restraining urination and sweating .
With Shan Zhu Yu and Shan Yao, for Dizziness, weak and sore back, impotence, spermatorrhea and night sweats, due to Kidney and Liver Yin Deficiencies.
With Shan Yao and Ze Xie, for ascendant Fire due to exhaustion of Kidney Yin.
With Shan Zhu Yu, Shan Yao and Ze Xie, for Xiao Ke with predominant polyuria.
Rz. Dioscoreae Shan Yao 9-12g Strengthens the Spleen , stabilizes the Kidneys and astringes Jing.
With Shu Di Huang and Shan Zhu Yu, for Kidney Deficiency with spermatorrhea, dizziness, tinnitus, lower back pain, impotence and night sweats.
Rz. Alismatis Ze Xie 9-12g Promotes urination and prevents Kidney Fire as a result of tonification.
With Fu Ling and Mu Dan Pi, regulates the Kidneys, Liver and Spleen, drains and tonifies.
With Fu Ling, facilitates the removal of water and leaches out Dampness to treat stagnant water and Dampness leading to edema, jaundice, diarrhea, Milky Lin, and scanty, difficult urination with abdominal distention.
With Mu Dan Pi, for steaming bone disorder, dizziness, and vertigo.
Fr. Corni Shan Zhu Yu 9-12g Nourishes Kidney Yin and Yang, astringes Jing and Yin and stops perspiration.
With Shu Di Huang, nourishes Kidney Yin and secures Jing, while restraining urination and sweating.
With Shan Yao, tonifies the Liver and Spleen.
With Shu Di Huang and Shan Yao, for urinary frequency (day or night), dizziness, tinnitus and lower back pain due to Kidney Deficiency.
With Shu Di Huang, Shan Yao and Gou Qi Zi, for true Yin Deficiency.
Cx. Moutan Mu Dan Pi 6-9g Clears Heat, cools the Blood clears and Deficiency Fire.
With Ju Hua, for dizziness and red, swollen eyes due to ascending Liver Fire.
With Ze Xie, Fu Ling and Shan Zhu Yu, for spermatorrhea due to Kidney Yin Deficiency with Heat.
Poria Fu Ling 9-12g Strengthens the Spleen and drains Dampness.
With Ze Xie, facilitates the removal of water and leaches out Dampness to treat stagnant water and Dampness leading to edema, jaundice, diarrhea, Milky Lin, and scanty, difficult urination with abdominal distention.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Replenishes the Yin of the Liver and Kidneys
  • Improves vision
  • Disperses Wind and Heat

SYNDROMES

  • Decreased acuity of vision due to Liver and Kidney Yin Deficiencies

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Blurry vision
  • Dry eyes
  • Painful eyes
  • Night blindness
  • Excessive tearing on exposure to Wind
  • T: Red
  • C: Little or none
  • P: Thready and rapid

TREATS

  • Blurry vision
  • Diabetes
  • Glaucoma
  • Hypertension
  • Weakness
  • Diminished vision
  • Ophthalmia
  • Ophthalmalgia
  • Fibromyalgia
