HERB FORMULAS

LIU YI SAN - 六一散 - SIX ONE POWDER

English: Six One Powder
Also Known As: Six-to-One Powder

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Talcum Hua Shi 18-30g (6) Clears Summerheat and drains Damp-Heat.
With Gan Cao, generates fluids.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 3-5g (1) Harmonizes the Middle Jiao, mildly clears Heat, and detoxifies.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Summerheat
  • Resolves Dampness
  • Tonifies Qi

SYNDROMES

  • Summerheat and Dampness
  • Lin syndrome
  • Damp-Heat skin disorders

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Fever
  • Sweating
  • Thirst
  • Irritability
  • Fidgeting
  • Nausea
  • Vomiting
  • Reddish urine
  • Pain from urinary stones
  • Dysuria
  • Urinary difficulty
  • Diarrhea
  • Scanty dark urine
  • Painful urination
  • Papules
  • Sores
  • T: Normal or red
  • C: Thin, yellow and greasy
  • P: Soggy and rapid or Forceful and rapid

TREATS

  • Upper respiratory tract infection
  • Urinary tract infection
  • Urolithiasis
  • Prickly heat
  • Cystitis
  • Diarrhea
  • Allergy
  • Infantile diarrhea
  • Oliguria
  • Furuncles
  • Eczema
  • Summer fevers
  • Urethritis
  • Acute gastroenteritis
  • Sunstroke
  • Stomach flu
  • Pertussis
  • Nephrolithiasis
  • Organophosphorus pesticide poisoning
  • Kidney failure

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Use caution with weak, elderly or Yin Deficient patients.
  • Contraindicated for those with frequent, copious urination.
  • Contraindicated for those with Summerheat without Dampness.
  • Contraindicated during pregnancy.
HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

NOTES

  • Mix the two ingredients with honey.
