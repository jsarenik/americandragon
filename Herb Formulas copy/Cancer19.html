HERB FORMULAS

CANCER #19

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Hb. Agrimoniae Xian He Cao 60g Astringes leakage of Blood, stops bleeding, tonifies Qi and Blood, reduces swelling, eliminates pus and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Hb. Solani Lyrati Shu Yang Quan 25g Dispels Heat and Dampness, removes toxins, regulates Qi, invigorates the Blood, dispels Phlegm and inhibits the growth of cancer cells
Sm. Arecae Bing Lang 9g Regulates Qi, reduces accumulations, drains downward, unblocks the bowels and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 3g Moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs and guides the herbs to all twelve channels.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Tonifies Qi and Blood
  • Clears Heat
  • Eliminates toxins
  • Stops bleeding
  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Resolves Phlegm
  • Inhibits the growth of cancer cells

SYNDROMES

  • Toxic Heat with Qi and Blood Stagnation and Phlegm

  • Persistent daytime drowsiness
  • Insomnia (persistent with much dreaming)
  • Restless sleep
  • Forgetfulness

  • C: Thick or Thin white or yellow
  • P: Rapid and forceful or Wiry and rapid or Choppy or Wiry and tight or Wiry and choppy or thready or Deep and choppy or Wiry or Rolling or Wiry and slippery

TREATS

  • Cancer (except leukemia)
