HERB FORMULAS

STOMACH CANCER #36 (TOPICAL)

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Ampelopsis Bai Lian 7.5g Drains Fire, relieves toxicity, eliminates Dampness, resolves lumps, reduces swelling,
relieves pain, promotes the healing of wounds and generates flesh.
Rz. Coptidis Huang Lian 7.5g Drains Fire, resolves Fire toxicity, clears Heat topically and drains Stomach Fire.
With Wu Zhu Yu, for epigastric and abdominal pain, acid regurgitation, ulcers, vomiting and epigastric upset due to Liver Fire or Stomach Heat.
With Huang Qin, drains pathogenic Heat from the Upper and Middle Jiaos.
With Huang Qin and Da Huang, for hematemesis and rectal bleeding due to Blood Heat.
Rx. Scutellariae Huang Qin 7.5g Drains Fire, detoxifies, cools the Blood and stops bleeding.
Rx. et Rz. Rhei Da Huang 7.5g Drains Heat, purges accumulations, invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, drains Heat from the Blood, clears Heat and reduces Fire toxicity.
With Huang Lian, for a feeling of fullness and distention due to Heat accumulation.
With Huang Lian and Huang Qin, for hematemesis or epistaxis due to Blood Heat.
With Dang Gui, for Blood Stasis.
Rz. Chuanxiong Chuan Xiong 7.5g Invigorates the Blood, promotes the movement of Qi, expels Wind and and alleviates pain.
With Dang Gui, harmonizes, nourishes and invigorates the Blood, disperses Blood Stasis and treats Wind-Damp Invasion blocking the Blood vessels causing pain, numbness, and paralysis.
Alumen Ming Fan 7.5g Relieves toxicity, purges Fire and dries Dampness topically.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 7.5g Tonifies, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood, disperses Cold, reduces swelling, expels pus, generates flesh and alleviates pain.
Fr. Evodiae Wu Zhu Yu 6g Warms the Middle Jiao, disperses Cold, promotes the movement of Qi, relieves Stagnation in the Liver channel, leads Fire downward and
alleviates pain.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Heat
  • Resolves toxicity
  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Breaks up Stasis

SYNDROMES

  • Hypertrophic Stomach cancer due to Blood Stasis

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Sharp, stabbing epigastric pain with fixed location
  • Epigastric or abdominal pain and distention
  • The pain may start intermittently but becomes more constant
  • Pain is severe after eating
  • The pain may radiate to the back
  • Dislike of pressure and Heat on the epigastrium
  • Pain predominates over distention or
  • Pain but no distention
  • Dysphagia
  • Epigastric rumbling
  • May be abdominal or epigastric lumps or masses
  • Nausea
  • Vomiting purple/black blood or "coffee grounds"
  • Indeterminate gnawing hunger
  • Anorexia
  • Weight loss
  • Weakness
  • Dry stools with blood like tar (melaena)
  • Hemafecia with dark, purple Blood or mixed dark and fresh Blood
  • Constipation
  • Dry, rough skin
  • Darkish, purplish or a blotchy complexion, lips and conjunctiva
  • Dark rings around the eyes
  • Spider naevi on the cheeks, epigastrium, trunk and around the inner ankle and knee
  • Maybe hemorrhoids
  • Maybe irregular menstruation with dark, purple, clotted Blood
  • With significant Deficiency from chronic Blood loss:
    A pale or sallow complexion
    Dizziness
  • T: Dark purple with purple spots or dots and dark, distended sublingual veins
  • C: White or Grayish white or Yellow
  • P: Wiry and choppy or Wiry or Choppy or Thready and choppy or Deep and thready

TREATS

  • Hypertrophic stomach cancer
