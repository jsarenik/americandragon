HERB FORMULAS

REN RE SAN

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Sm. Platycladi Bai Zi Ren 15g Nourishes the Heart and calms the Spirit.
Rx. Rehmanniae Preparata Shu Di Huang 20g Nourishes Jing and Blood, fills the Marrow and nourishes Liver and Kidney Yin.
Fr. Lycii Gou Qi Zi 25g Nourishes and tonifies Liver and Kidney Blood and Yin, benefits Jing and brightens the eyes.
With Shu Di Huang, for Liver and Kidney Deficiency.
With Ju Hua, for Liver and Kidney Deficiency with Jing and Blood unable to rise and nourish the eyes with tinnitus, headache, and visual impairment.
Fr. Corni Shan Zhu Yu 15g Stabilizes the Kidneys, astringes Jing and Body Fluids, tonifies Liver and Kidney Yin and strengthens Kidney Yang.
With Wu Wei Zi, for spermatorrhea, abnormal sweating, palpitations and shortness of breath due to Yin and Yang Deficiency of the Liver and Kidneys.
With Shu Di Huang, nourishes Kidney Yin and secures Jing, while restraining urination and sweating.
Fr. Schisandrae Wu Wei Zi 15g Quiets the Spirit, calms and contains Heart Qi, tonifies the Kidneys, astringes Jing and stops diarrhea and spermatorrhea
Rx. Ginseng Ren Shen 15g Tonifies Heart Qi, calms the Spirit and treats impotence.
Poria Fu Ling 15g Strengthens the Spleen, quiets the Heart, calms the Spirit and soothes the nerves.
Flos Chrysanthemi Ju Hua 10g Promotes the movement of Heart Qi and Blood and stimulates Blood circulation.
Fr. Aurantii Zhi Ke 10g Promotes the movement of Qi and reduces distention and pressure.
Cx. Cinnamomi Rou Gui 5g Warms the Kidneys, Spleen and Heart, strengthens Yang and Ming Men Fire and assists in the generation of Qi and Blood.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Augments Qi
  • Restores the Pulse
  • Calms the Shen
  • Supports Yang
  • Nourishes Jing

SYNDROMES

  • Heart and Gallbladder Qi Deficiency
