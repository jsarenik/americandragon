HERB FORMULAS

CERVICAL CANCER #31

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Bupleuri Chai Hu 10g Spreads Liver Qi and relieves Stagnation.
With Bai Shao, for Liver Blood Deficiency with Liver Qi Stagnation.
Ram. Cinnamomi Gui Zhi 10g Warms the channels and collaterals to relieve pain, adjusts the Ying and Wei, unblocks Yang and transforms Qi and warms and facilitates the flow of Qi through the channels and collaterals and Blood through the vessels.
Rx. Paeoniae Alba Bai Shao 15g Nourishes the Blood, regulates menstruation, softens the Liver and relieves pain.
With Chai Hu, for flank pain due to Liver Qi Stagnation.
Extremitas Radicis Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui Wei 15g Invigorates and harmonizes the Blood.
With Bai Shao, regulates and harmonizes Qi and Blood.
With Chai Hu and Bai Shao, for dysmenorrhea from Liver Qi Stagnation and Blood Deficiency.
Carapax Trionycis Bie Jia 15g Nourishes Yin, anchors Yang, invigorates the Blood, promotes menstruation, softens hardness, dissipates nodules and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Colla Corii Asini E Jiao 10g Tonifies and nourishes the Blood.
Rx. Codonopsis Dan Shen 10g Invigorates the Blood,d dispels Blood Stasis, nourishes the Blood, calms the Spirit and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Dang Gui Wei, for irregular menstruation.
Rx. Astragali Huang Qi 30g Tonifies Qi and Blood, strengthens the Spleen, raises the Yang Qi of the Spleen and Stomach, relieves numbness and pain and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Gui Zhi, for Qi and Blood Deficiency and Ying and Wei Deficiency with numbness and painful obstruction of the musculature.
With Sheng Ma and Chai Hu, for Spleen Qi Sinking with rectal and/or uterine prolapse or excessive uterine bleeding.
Rz. Cimicifugae Sheng Ma 10g Raises Yang, lifts sunken Qi and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Chai Hu, for uterine prolapse due to Yang Deficiency.
Colla Cervi Cornu Lu Jiao Jiao 10g Tonifies and nourishes Qi and Blood, tonifies the Kidneys, strengthens and warms Yang, regulates the Chong and Ren channels and stabilizes the Dai Channel.
Fluoritum Zi Shi Ying 15g Warms the Womb and warms and unblocks the Chong and Ren channels.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Regulates Qi
  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Tonifies Qi and Blood
  • Raises Qi and Yang
  • Warms the womb
  • Inhibits the growth of cancer cells

SYNDROMES

  • Qi and Blood Stagnation with Qi and Blood Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Pain that is fixed, stabbing and /or severe or
  • Pains that move around
  • Distention
  • Depression
  • Irritability
  • Pan is worse with pressure
  • Dark complexion
  • Fixed, palpable masses
  • Bleeding with clots
  • Purple lips
  • Purple nails
  • Dry skin
  • Blue, dark or purplish discoloration of skin in small or large areas,
  • Varicose and spider veins
  • Cold hands and feet
  • Tendency to hemorrhage dark blood or clots
  • Fatigue
  • Loose stools
  • Poor appetite
  • Dizziness
  • Pale face
  • Weak voice
  • Reluctance to speak
  • Sweating with little or no exertion
  • Palpitations
  • Insomnia
  • Dry hair
  • Poor memory
  • Paleness
  • Scanty periods
  • T: Reddish Purple with possible purple or red spots or
  • C: White
  • P: Choppy or Wiry and choppy or Weak, thin and choppy

TREATS

  • Cervical cancer

CONTRAINDICATIONS
HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

NOTES

  • Take with Xiao Jin Dan

MODIFICATIONS

For more severe Qi Deficiency: For metastasis to adjacent organs:
+ 30g Fr. Jujube Da Zao + 10g Sm. Vaccariae Wang Bu Liu Xing
For bleeding due to Yin Deficiency: + 10g Concha Arcae Wa Leng Zi
+ 3g Resina Draconis Xue Jie + 15g Sargassum Hai Zao
+ 15g Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang + 15g Thallus Eckloniae Kun Bu
+ 15g Rx. Scrophulariae Xuan Shen
