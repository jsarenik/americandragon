HERB FORMULAS

PSORIASIS #8

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Isatidis Ban Lan Gen 9-30g Drains Heat, relieves Fire toxicity, cools the Blood and benefits the throat.
Rz. Polygoni Cuspidati Hu Zhang 8-30g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, unblocks the channels and stops pain, clears Heat, resolves Dampness, discharges toxins and disperses swelling.
Cx. Dictamni Bai Xian Pi 4-16g Clears Heat, resolves Fire-toxicity, expels Wind, dries Dampness, clears Damp-Heat and stops itching.
Rz. Menispermi Bei Dou Gen 3-12g Clears Heat, resolves toxicity, resolves Dampness and reduces swelling.
Hb. Lonicerae Jin Yin Hua 6-20g Clears Heat and resolves Fire toxicity.
With Gan Cao, for toxic swellings and sores.
Rz. Smilacis Glabrae Tu Fu Ling 15-60g Relieves toxicity, eliminates Dampness, benefits the joints and clears Damp-Heat from the skin.
With Bai Xian Pi, for skin lesions associated with Damp-Heat.
Rx. Clematidis Wei Ling Xian 3-16g Dispels Wind-Dampness, unblocks the channels, alleviates pain, reduces Phlegm and pathogenic water.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 1.5-14g Clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity, antidote for many toxic substances, internally and topically and moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs.
Polyporus Zhu Ling 5-18g Promotes urination, leaches out Dampness and dispels Damp-Heat.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Detoxifies
  • Reduces inflammation of tissues

SYNDROMES

  • Toxic Heat

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Erythroderma type psoriasis Lesions develop and spread quickly
  • Lesions often coalesce together
  • Lesions may be yellow
  • Lesions are covered by fine
  • scales that shed easily
  • Protracted cases have moist, thick lesions
  • The nails are severely affected
  • Aversion to Cold
  • Restlessness
  • Itching
  • Burning
  • Pain
  • High fever
  • Thirst
  • Dry stools
  • Yellow urine
  • Constipation
  • Frequent urination
  • T: Scarlet
  • C: Thin and yellow
  • P: Wiry, slippery and rapid

TREATS

  • Psoriasis
