HERB FORMULAS

SUO QUAN WAN - 缩泉丸 - RESTRICT THE FOUNTAIN PILL

English: Restrict the Fountain Pill
Also Known As: Shut the Sluice Pill
Pills for Enuresis

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Fr. Alpiniae Oxyphyllae Yi Zhi Ren 9-12g Stabilizes and binds urine, warms the Kidneys, helps control urine gate and warms the Spleen to control Fluids.
 With Wu Yao, warms and contains the Kidney Qi so that it returns to its source and secures the urine to treat Lower Jiao Deficiency Cold with incontinence, urinary frequency or urinary dribbling (worse in the presence of Cold).
With Wu Yao and Shan Yao, for Urinary incontinence, or frequency due to Kidney and Spleen Yang Deficiency.
Rx. Linderae Wu Yao 6-12g Disperses Cold in Lower Jiao helping to transform Bladder Qi, restrain urine and disperses Lower Jiao Cold.
Dry-fried Rz. Dioscoreae Shan Yao 9-15g Strengthens the Spleen and Stomach, tonifies the Kidneys and nourishes Jing.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Warms the Kidneys
  • Dispels Cold
  • Stops urinary frequency
  • Stops leakage

SYNDROMES

  • Kidney Yang Deficiency: Kidney Qi Not Consolidated
  • Urinary frequency due to Spleen and Kidney Yang Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Frequent, clear, prolonged urination
  • Enuresis
  • Low back pain
  • Spermatorrhea
  • Dripping of urine after urination
  • Leukorrhea
  • T: Pale
  • C: White
  • P: Deep and frail

TREATS

  • Enuresis
  • Spermatorrhea
  • Stress incontinence
  • Knee pain
  • Impotence
  • Deafness
  • Leukorrhea
  • Diabetes
  • Overactive Bladder
  • Polyuria
  • Tinnitus
  • Low back pain

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Spicy and other stimulating types of food should not be eaten while taking this formula.
