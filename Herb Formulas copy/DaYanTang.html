HERB FORMULAS

DA YAN TANG - 大延汤 - GREAT CORYDALIS DECOCTION

English: Great Corydalis Decoction
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rz. Corydalis Yan Hu Suo 15g Invigorates the Blood, circulates Qi and stops pain.
Rx. Isatidis Ban Lan Gen 15g Drains Heat, relieves Fire toxicity and cools the Blood.
Rx. Astragali Huang Qi 15g Tonifies Qi and Blood, tonifies Wei Qi, stabilizes the Exterior, tonifies the Lungs, aids circulation of moisture downward from the face, promotes urination, reduces edema, promotes the discharge of pus, generates flesh, expels toxins and relieves pain.
Fol. Isatidis Da Qing Ye 12g Clears Heat, resolves Fire toxicity, cools the Blood and dissipates maculae.
With Jin Yin Hua, for Toxic Heat sores.
with Dan Shen, for icteric and anicteric hepatitis and cholecystitis.
Flos Lonicerae Jin Yin Hua 12g Clears Heat, resolves Fire toxicity, vents and disperses External Wind-Heat and clears Damp-Heat from the Lower Jiao.
With Gan Cao, for toxic swellings and sores.
With Huang Qin, for suppurating boils that have not ulcerated, or have ulcerated but are not discharging pus.
Rx. Codonopsis Dang Shen 12g Tonifies the Middle Jiao and augments Qi.
With Huang Qi, for Lung and Spleen Qi Deficiency with fatigue, shortness of breath, diarrhea and anorexia.
Rx. Lithospermi/Arnebiae Zi Cao 12g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, invigorates Blood circulation, relieves Fire toxins, encourages rashes and clears Damp-Heat from the skin.
With Da Qing Ye, for dense purpuric rash associated with severe Fire toxin.
Rx. Salviae Miltiorrhizae Dan Shen 12g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, clears Heat, soothes irritability, cools the Blood and reduces abscesses.
Rx. Scutellariae Huang Qin 9g Clears Heat, dries Dampness, drains Fire, detoxifies and calms ascending Liver Yang.
With Chai Hu, for Heat in the Shao Yang with alternating chills and fever and nausea.
Rx. Angelicae Dahuricae Bai Zhi 9g Expels Wind, eliminates Dampness, reduces swelling, eliminates toxins and expels pus.
With Gan Cao, for a Stomach ache.
Cx. Dictamni Bai Xian Pi 9g Clears Heat, resolves Fire-toxicity, expels Wind and dries Dampness.
Rx. Stephaniae Tetrandrae Han Fang Ji 6g Promotes urination, reduces edema especially in the lower body, expels Wind-Dampness, purges Damp-Heat and alleviates pain.
With Huang Qi, for Wind-Dampness with exterior Deficiency.
Rx. Bupleuri Chai Hu 6g Spreads Liver Qi, relieves Stagnation and reduces fever.
With Gan Cao, for hepatitis and upper right quadrant pain.
With Huang Qi, raises the Clear Yang due to abnormal descent of Qi.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 6g Tonifies the Spleen,d augments Qi, clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity, moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs and guides the herbs to all twelve channels.
With Dang Shen, for anorexia, fatigue and loose stools due to Spleen Deficiency.
With Jin Yin Hua, for Damp rashes or acne.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears and dredges the Liver
  • Clears Heat
  • Resolves toxins
  • Moves Qi
  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Disinhibits Dampness
  • Tonifies Qi

SYNDROMES

  • Toxic Damp-Heat

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Fever
  • Restlessness
  • Irritability
  • Thirst
  • Foul breath
  • Vomiting
  • Abdominal pain
  • Constipation
  • Scanty urine
  • Skin rashes
  • Epistaxis
  • Bleeding gums
  • Muscular spasms or
  • Convulsions
  • Hypochondriac and Stomach fullness and pain
  • Unclear thinking
  • Delirium
  • Epistaxis
  • Hematochezia
  • Bleeding from the mouth
  • Bruises easily
  • T: Deep red
  • C: Dry yellow
  • P: Rapid

TREATS

  • Herpes zoster
