HERB FORMULAS

HUA GAI SAN - 華蓋散 - CANOPY POWDER

English: Canopy Powder
Also Known As: Ma Huang and Morus Formula

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Ephedrae Ma Huang 4-30g Induces sweating, releases the exterior, disseminates and facilitates Lung Qi, calms wheezing and stops coughing.
With Xing Ren, for Wind-Cold cough and wheezing.
Cx. Mori Sang Bai Pi 4-30g Drains Heat from the Lungs, stops cough and calms wheezing.
With Di Gu Pi and Gan Cao, for cough with thick sputum, fever, irritability, and thirst due to Phlegm-Heat Obstructing the Lungs.
Fr. Perillae Zi Su Zi 4-30g Stops cough, calms wheezing, descends Qi, dissolves Phlegm, moistens the Intestines and unblocks the stool.
With Xing Ren, for cough and wheezing with constipation.
Sm. Armeniacae Xing Ren 4-30g Stops cough, calms wheezing, moistens the Intestines and unblocks bowels.
With Ma Huang and Gan Cao, for cough with profuse sputum, nasal obstruction and a hoarse voice.
Poria Rubra Chi Fu Ling 4-30g Clears Dampness and Heat.
Per. Citri Reticulatae Chen Pi 4-30g Regulates Qi, adjusts the Middle, relieves the diaphragm, dries Dampness, transforms Phlegm and descends Qi.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 1-15g Moistens the Lungs, resolves Phlegm, stops cough and moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs.
With Ma Huang and Xing Ren, for coughing or wheezing due to Wind-Cold Attacks the Lungs.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Disseminates Lung Qi
  • Releases the Exterior
  • Expels Phlegm
  • Stops coughing

SYNDROMES

  • Wind-Cold Attacks the Lungs

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Coughing with copious sputum
  • A stifling sensation in the chest and diaphragm
  • Stiff neck and upper back
  • Wheezing
  • Restlessness
  • Headache
  • Dizziness
  • Anorexia
  • Stuffy nose
  • Dyspnea
  • Nasal obstruction
  • P: Superficial

TREATS

  • Bronchial asthma
  • Nasal congestion
  • Cough
  • COPD
  • Vertigo
  • Upper respiratory tract infection
  • Neck stiffness
  • Common cold
  • Influenza
  • Pertussis in children
  • Bronchitis
  • Headache
  • Dyspnea

MODIFICATIONS

For Excess Phlegm with shortness of breath: For severe cough and wheezing:
+ Cx. Lycii Di Gu Pi + Rz. Anemarrhenae Zhi Mu
+ Rx. Platycodi Jie Geng + Bul. Fritillariae Thunbergii Zhe Bei Mu
For nasal congestion and a low pitched voice:
+ Rx. Saposhnikoviae Fang Feng
For severe Wind-Cold:
+ Hb. Menthae Bo He + +9g Rx. Saposhnikoviae Fang Feng
For severe cough:
+ 9g Rx. Asteris Zi Wan + 9g Rz. et Rx. Notopterygii Qiang Huo
+ 9g Flos Farfare Kuan Dong Hua
For frontal headache or severe nasal congestion: For a productive cough, thick, white mucus, and fullness in the chest and epigastrium:
+ 9g Rx. Angelicae Dahuricae Bai Zhi + 9g Rz. Atractylodis Cang Zhu
+ 9g Cx. Magnoliae Officinalis Hou Po
