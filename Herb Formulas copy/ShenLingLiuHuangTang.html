HERB FORMULAS

SHEN LING LIU HUANG TANG - 參苓六黄汤 - GINSENG AND PORIA SIX YELLOWS DECOCTION

English: Ginseng and Poria Six Yellows Decoction
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Astragali Huang Qi 30g Tonifies Qi and Blood, strengthens the Spleen, promotes urination, reduces edema and generates Body Fluids.
With Sheng Di Huang and Huang Bai, for Yin Deficiency with night sweats.
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 30g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, nourishes Yin and generates fluids.
Rx. Codonopsis Dang Shen 15g Tonifies the Middle Jiao, augments Qi, nourishes the Blood and promotes the generation of Body Fluids.
With Fu Ling, for Spleen and Stomach Deficiency with fatigue, a sensation of fullness in the abdomen, dizziness, lethargy, shortness of breath, anorexia and loose stools or diarrhea.
With Huang Qi, for Lung and Spleen Qi Deficiency with fatigue, shortness of breath, diarrhea and anorexia.
Poria Fu Ling 15g Promotes urination, leaches out Dampness, strengthens the Spleen, harmonizes the Middle Jiao and transforms Phlegm.
Rz. Polygonati Huang Jing 15g Tonifies Spleen Qi, nurtures Stomach Yin, .moistens Lung Yin, tonifies the Kidneys and strengthens Jing.
Sm. Plantaginis Che Qian Zi 15g Promotes urination, clears Damp-Heat and clears Liver Heat.
Rx. Achyranthis Bidentatae Niu Xi 12g Invigorates the Blood, expels Blood Stasis, nourishes the Liver and Kidney Yin and clears Damp-Heat in the Lower Jiao.
With Sheng Di Huang, powerfully clears Deficiency Fire Blazing Upward with pain and swelling of the gums, epistaxis, hematemesis and other hemorrhage in the upper body.
Rz. Coptidis Huang Lian 9g Clears Heat, drains Dampness and stops bleeding.
With Sheng Di Huang, for Insomnia and delirium due to unremitting Heat injuring the Ying level.
Cx. Phellodendri Huang Bai 9g Drains Damp-Heat especially from the Lower Jiao and drains Deficiency Heat.
Pollen Typhae Pu Huang 9g Invigorates Blood, dispels Blood Stasis and promotes urination.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Strengthens the Spleen
  • Tonifies Qi
  • Enriches Yin
  • Drains Heat

SYNDROMES

  • Qi and Yin Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Enduring low back and sacral soreness and pain
  • Perineal pain
  • Testicular sagging and distention
  • Symptoms worse with fatigue
  • Dizziness
  • Insomnia
  • Heart palpitations
  • T: Red
  • C: Little or None
  • Thready, wiry and rapid

TREATS

  • Prostatitis
