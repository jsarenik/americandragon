HERB FORMULAS

HE XUE TONG JING TANG - 和血通经汤 - HARMONIZE THE BLOOD AND FREE THE FLOW DECOCTION

English: Harmonize the Blood and Free the Flow of the Channels [or Menses] Decoction
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
RX. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 9g Tonifies, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood, disperses Cold, stops pain due to Blood Stasis and regulates the menses.
With Shu Di Huang, for Heat symptoms due to Yin Deficiency and Devastated Blood.
Rx. Rehmanniae Preparata Shu Di Huang 12-15g Nourishes the Blood, nourishes Liver and Kidney Yin, nourishes Jing and fills the Marrow.
Rz. Sparganii San Leng 9g Forcefully breaks up Blood Stasis, regulates Qi, alleviates pain and regulates menstruation.
Rz. Curcumae E Zhu 9g Invigorates Blood circulation, dispels Blood Stasis regulates Qi and alleviates pain.
With San Leng, for Qi and Blood Stagnation with clumping and accumulation leading to pain.
With Su Mu, breaks up Blood Stasis.
Rx. Aucklandiae Mu Xiang 6-9g Promotes the movement of Qi and alleviates pain.
Cx. Cinnamomi Rou Gui 3-9g Warms the Kidneys, Spleen and Heart and strengthens the Yang and Ming Men Fire, disperses deep Cold, warms and unblocks the channels and vessels and alleviates pain.
Flos Carthami Hong Hua 9g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, opens the channels, unblocks menstruation and alleviates pain.
With San Leng and E Zhu, for abdominal masses and accumulations.
With Su Mu, promotes the movement of Blood and unblocks menstruation.
Lignum Sappan Su Mu 9g Invigorates Blood circulation, dispels Blood Stasis, reduces swellings, opens the channels and alleviates pain.
Sanguis Draconis Xue Jie 3-6g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis and alleviates pain.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Breaks up Stasis
  • Dispels Cold

SYNDROMES

  • Blood Stagnation due to Cold

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • No period
  • Abdominal pain and distention which is worse with pressure
  • Leukorrhea
  • Lumbar soreness
  • Dark or pale green complexion
  • Headache
  • Stiff neck
  • Aversion to Cold
  • T: Dark red with purple spots
  • C: Normal
  • P: Deep and choppy or Deep, slow and tight

TREATS

  • Amenorrhea
