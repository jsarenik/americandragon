HERB FORMULAS

ZHENG AI WAN - 正癌囊 - PILLS FOR TREATING CANCER

English: Pills for Treating Cancer
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Olibanum Ru Xiang 150g Invigorates the Blood, promotes the movement of Qi, relaxes the sinews, activates the channels, alleviates pain, reduces swelling and generates flesh.
Myrrh Mo Yao 150g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, alleviates pain, reduces swelling, generates flesh and promotes healing.
With Ru Xiang, disseminates and unblocks the organs and dredges the channels and collaterals to treat epigastric, flank, abdominal and joint pain, abdominal pain during menstruation and irregular menstruation and swollen, painful sores and sores that are hard but not painful.
Rz. et Rx. Rhei Da Huang 60g Drains Heat, purges accumulations, invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, clears Heat, reduces Fire toxicity and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Fluoritum Zi Shi Ying 150g Warms the Womb and warms and unblocks the Chong and Ren channels.
Hb. Solani Lyrati Shu Yang Quan 150g Dispels Heat and Dampness, removes toxins, regulates the circulation of Qi, invigorates the Blood, dispels Phlegm and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Alumen Ming Fan 60g Relieves toxicity, purges Fire, dries Dampness, stops bleeding and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Stephaniae Cepharanthae Bai Yao Zi 180g Clears Heat, removes toxicity, alleviates pain, cools the Blood and stops bleeding.
Rx. Arnebiae seu Lithospermi Zi Cao 500g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, invigorates Blood circulation, relieves Fire toxins, clears Damp-Heat from the skin and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Hb. Agrimoniae Xian He Cao 500g Astringes leakage of Blood, stops bleeding, reduces swelling, eliminates pus and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Realgar Xiong Huang 90g Relieves toxicity, dries Dampness, expels Phlegm and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Sm. Alpiniae Katsumadai Cao Dou Kou 150g Dries Dampness, warms the Middle Jiao, stops vomiting and strengthens the Spleen.
Rx. Aucklandiae Mu Xiang 60g Promotes the movement of Qi, alleviates pain, dispels Damp-Heat and harmonizes the Liver and Spleen.
With Bai Shao, for diarrhea with profuse pus and Blood.
Resina Toxicodendri Gan Qi 15g Breaks up Blood Stasis, opens the channels and purges hardness.
Cx. Mori Sang Bai Pi 90g Drains Heat from the Lungs, promotes urination, reduces edema and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Huang Qin, drains Heat from the Lungs and stops coughing and wheezing.
Rx. Paeoniae Alba Bai Shao 400g Nourishes the Blood, regulates menstruation, astringes Yin, softens the Liver and relieves pain.
Wine-prepared Rx. Scutellariae Jiu Chao Huang Qin 30g Clears and eliminates Damp-Heat, drains Fire, detoxifies and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Damp-Heat
  • Relieves toxicity
  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Breaks up Stasis
  • Moves Qi
  • Inhibits the growth of cancer cells

SYNDROMES

  • Toxic Damp-Heat with Qi and Blood Stagnation

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Tumor develops quickly
  • Long-standing illness
  • Pain
  • Redness
  • Ulceration
  • Discharge of yellow or bloody, foul-smelling fluid
  • Fatigue
  • Fever
  • Anxiety
  • Dry mouth
  • Thirst
  • Dark urine
  • Bitter taste
  • Red eyes
  • Insomnia
  • Pain that is fixed, stabbing and /or severe
  • Pain is worse with pressure
  • Dark complexion
  • Fixed, palpable masses
  • Bleeding with clots
  • Purple lips
  • Purple nails
  • Dry skin
  • Blue, dark or purplish discoloration of skin in small or large areas,
  • Varicose and spider veins
  • Cold hands and feet
  • Tendency to hemorrhage dark blood or clots
  • Lethargy
  • Anorexia
  • Maybe jaundice
  • No thirst or
  • Thirst with no desire to drink
  • Nausea
  • Vomiting
  • Afternoon fever
  • Body aches
  • A sallow complexion
  • Scanty urination
  • Constipation or
  • Bloody diarrhea or
  • Dysentery
  • Chest discomfort
  • Epigastric pain and distention
  • Dull appearance
  • Maybe disorientation
  • Loose stools
  • Maybe skin vesicles
  • Heavy extremities
  • Lower Jiao pain
  • Heaviness
  • Headache
  • Dull Spirit
  • Abdominal distention
  • Mental fuzziness
  • T: Normal or Red or Purple, possible purple or red spots
  • C: Yellowish white and greasy or Thick, sticky, dry and yellow
  • P: Soft or Soft and rapid or maybe Slippery and rapid or Wiry or Rolling and rapid or Wiry, overflowing and rapid or Choppy or Wiry and choppy

TREATS

  • Cervical cancer, post radiotherapy or at the restorative stage
