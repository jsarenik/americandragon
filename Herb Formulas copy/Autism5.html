HERB FORMULAS

AUTISM #5

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Curcumae Yu Jin 12g Activates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, regulates Qi flow, clears the Heart and Pericardium and opens the Orifices.
Rz. Pinelliae Preparatum Zhi Ban Xia 10g Dries Dampness and transforms Phlegm.
With Fu Ling, prevents turbid Phlegm and Dampness from disturbing the Heart with dizziness, palpitations and insomnia.
With Yu Jin and Shi Chang Pu, for disorientation, irritability, dizziness and hearing problems due to Turbid Dampness Blocking the Orifices.
with Yu Jin, transforms Dampness, opens the Orifices and awakens the brain to treat Turbid Dampness that has blocked the clear Orifices, with loss of consciousness or confusion, tinnitus and forgetfulness.
Poria Fu Ling 10g Promotes urination, leaches out Dampness , quiets the Heart, calms the Spirit and soothes the nerves.
With Yuan Zhi, for insomnia and palpitations due to Spleen and Heart Insufficiency or Phlegm Obstructing the Heart.
With Zhi Ban Xia, for Phlegm, thin mucus and turbid Dampness.
With Zhi Ban Xia and Chen Pi, for nausea, vomiting, chest fullness and distention and anorexia due to congested fluids.
With Gan Cao, for Spleen and Heart Deficiency causing facial edema, palpitations, and shortness of breath.
With Shi Cang Pu and Yuan Zhi. for insomnia and palpitations due to Spleen and Heart Insufficiency or Phlegm Obstructing the Heart.
Arisaemae cum Bile Dan Nan Xing 10g Clears Heat, dissolves Phlegm, calms Wind and stops convulsions.
Fr. Aurantii Immaturus Zhi Shi 10g Breaks up Stagnant Qi, reduces accumulation, descends Qi, and transforms Phlegm.
With Chen Pi, strengthens the Middle Jiao.
Rx. Polygalae Yuan Zhi 10g Calms the Spirit, sedates the Heart, clears the channel joining the Heart and Kidneys, expels Phlegm and opens the Orifices.
With Yu Jin and Shi Chang Pu, for disorientation due to Phlegm Blocking the Heart Orifices.
Rx. Acori Tatarinowii Shi Chang Pu 10g Opens the Orifices, vaporizes Phlegm, calms the Spirit and promotes the flow of Qi and Blood.
With Yuan Zhi, for forgetfulness and dulled senses.
With Yuan Zhi and Fu Ling, for forgetfulness, tinnitus and impaired hearing.
With Yu Jin, for Qi, Blood or Phlegm Stagnation with palpitations, forgetfulness, moodiness or withdrawal-mania, hysteria, depression or reduced mental acuity.
Rz. Cyperi Xiang Fu 10g Spreads and regulates Liver Qi.
Per. Citri Reticulatae Chen Pi 10g Regulates Qi, adjusts the Middle, relieves the diaphragm, dries Dampness and transforms Phlegm.
With Zhi Ban Xia, for a stifling sensation in the chest and coughing with excessive white, viscous sputum due to Phlegm-Damp Obstruction.
With Zhi Ban Xia and Fu Ling, for Cough due to accumulation of Dampness and Phlegm.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 6g Resolves Phlegm, moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs and guides the herbs to all twelve channels

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • REgulates Qi
  • Resolves Phlegm
  • Dries Dampness
  • Strengthens the Spleen

SYNDROMES

  • Liver Qi Stagnation: Phlegm and Qi Stagnant and Blocked

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Apathy
  • Dementia
  • Muttering to oneself
  • Feeling of obstruction in throat as if a slice of roast meat were stuck there which cannot be coughed up nor swallowed down. This sensation becomes more prominent during emotional upset and subsides when relaxed
  • Distention and pain in the sides or flanks
  • Suffocating sensation in the chest
  • Tendency to sigh
  • Melancholy
  • Depression
  • Irritability
  • Inappropriate anger
  • Irregular menses
  • Dysmenorrhea
  • Breast distention and pain before and during menses
  • T: Pink
  • C: Thin and white
  • P: Slippery and wiry (slippery due to Phlegm)

TREATS

  • Autism
