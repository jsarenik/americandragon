HERB FORMULAS

PSORIASIS #3

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Flos Lonicerae Jin Yin Hua 30g Clears Heat, resolves Fire toxicity and vents and disperses External Wind-Heat.
 With Lian Qiao and Huang Qin, for unremitting high fever.
With Huang Qin, for suppurating boils that have not ulcerated, or have ulcerated but are not discharging pus.
With Pu Gong Ying, Lian Qiao and Ye Ju Hua, for sores, boils and furuncles.
With Gan Cao, for toxic swelling and sores.
Testa Glycinis Lu Dou Yi 30g Calms the Liver, descends Liver Yang, nourishes Kidney Yin to extinguish internal Wind, clears Heat and relieves toxicity.
With Jin Yin Hua, Lian Qiao and Pu Gong Ying, for swellings and abscesses.
Raw Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 10g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, nourishes Yin and generates fluids.
Fr. Forsythiae Lian Qiao 10g Clears Heat, especially in the Upper Jiao, relieves toxicity, reduces abscesses and dissipates clumps.
 With Ye Ju Hua, for initial stage warm febrile disease.
With Jin Yin Hua, for initial stage Wind-Heat.
With Ye Ju Hua, Jin Yin Hua, and Pu Gong Ying, for external sores and internal abscesses.
Hb. Taraxaci Pu Gong Ying 10g Reduces abscesses, dissipates nodules, clears the Liver and eyes, clears Heat, resolves Dampness, and relieves Fire toxicity.
Flos Chrysanthemi Indici Ye Ju Hua 10g Drains Fire and relieves toxicity.
 With Jin Yin Hua and Pu Gong Ying, for furuncles and hypertension.
Rx. Astragali Huang Qi 12g Strengthens the Spleen, tonifies Qi, generates Body Fluids, nourishes Blood, promotes urination, reduces edema, promotes the discharge of pus, generates flesh and expels toxins.
 With Dang Gui, for Qi and Blood Deficiencies with chronic non-healing sores, numbness, painful obstruction and paresthesias.
Sm. Coicis Yi Yi Ren 12g Promotes urination, leaches out Dampness, strengthens the Spleen, clears Heat, expels pus, clears Damp-Heat and nourishes the skin.
Cx. Dictamni Bai Xian Pi 12g Clears Heat, resolves Fire-toxicity, expels Wind, dries Dampness, clears Damp-Heat and stops itching.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 12g Tonifies Blood, regulates menses, invigorates and harmonizes Blood, reduces swelling, expels pus and generates flesh.
 With Huang Qi, for ulcerated abscesses with insufficient drainage or draining only a clear, thin discharge.
With Jin Yin Hua and Chi Shao, reduces swelling of abscesses and sores and alleviates pain.
Rx. Paeoniae Rubra Chi Shao 12g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, activates Blood and dispels Blood Stasis.
Rx. Scutellariae Huang Qin 6g Drains Fire and detoxifies.
 With Huang Lian, for high fever and irritability in warm-febrile disease.
With Huang Lian, Lian Qiao and Pu Gong Ying, for external or internal sores and abscesses.
Rz. Coptidis Huang Lian 6g Clears Heat, drains Dampness, drains Fire and relieves toxicity.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 6g Clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity and moderates and harmonizes the properties of the other herbs
 With Pu Gong Ying, Internally or externally for abscesses or other toxic swellings.
With Jin Yin Hua, for damp rashes or acne.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Cools the Blood
  • Clears Heat
  • Relieves Toxin
  • Eliminates Dampness
  • Clears fever
  • Reduces spots

SYNDROMES

  • Fire/Heat toxin induced psoriasis with pustular lesions

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Pustular lesions
  • Lesions develop and spread quickly
  • Lesions often coalesce together
  • Lesions are covered by fine scales that shed easily.
  • Itching
  • Burning
  • Pain
  • High fever
  • Thirst
  • Dry stools
  • Yellow urine
  • T: Scarlet
  • C: Thin
  • P: Wiry, slippery and rapid

TREATS

  • Psoriasis due to Fire/Heat toxin with pustular lesions
