HERB FORMULAS

CVA #3

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Aconiti Lateralis Preparata Zhi Fu Zi 9g Restores Devastated Yang, rescues from rebellion, warms Ming Men Fire, assists Heart, Kidney and Spleen Yang, disperses Cold and Dampness, warms the channels and stops pain.
With Ren Shen, for severe Deficiency of Source Qi and Collapse of Yang with cold extremities, sweating, weak breathing, dizziness and an extremely pale complexion.
With Bai Zhu, for Spleen Yang Deficiency with a cold, painful abdomen, vomiting and watery diarrhea.
With Bai Zhu and Gan Jiang, for the internal ascendance of Yin Cold leading to the lack of arousal of Spleen Yang with abdominal pain, Cold and watery diarrhea.
With Ren Shen and Gui Zhi, for weak Heart Yang with palpitations, shortness of breath and chest pain.
With Ren Shen, Bai Zhu and Gan Jiang, for Spleen Yang Deficiency.
With Gan Jiang, Gan Cao and Ren Shen, for Heart Yang collapse with vomiting, a fading pulse, icy extremities and profuse perspiration.
Rz. Zingiberis Gan Jiang 2.4g Warms the Middle, expels Cold, rescues Devastated Yang, expels Interior Cold and Warms the channels.
With Bai Zhu, for diarrhea due to Spleen Deficiency.
With Bai Zhu and Ren Shen, for Spleen Yang Deficiency with a cold abdomen, vomiting and diarrhea.
With Zhi Fu Zi, warms the Spleen and Kidneys, benefits Yang and disperses Cold.
With Gan Cao, for epigastric pain and vomiting due to Spleen and Stomach Deficiency Cold.
Rz. Atractylodis Macrocephalae Bai Zhu 9g Tonifies the Spleen, augments Qi, dries Dampness and promotes water metabolism.
With Gan Jiang, eliminates Cold Dampness in the Spleen with loss of taste, a sticky sensation in the mouth, nausea, vomiting, diarrhea and a thick, white tongue coat.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 3g Tonifies the Spleen, augments Qi, moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs and guides the herbs to all twelve channels.
With Ren Shen, relieves palpitations and anxiety.
Sm. Persicae Tao Ren 9g Breaks up Blood Stasis and invigorates Blood circulation.
Flos Carthami Hong Hua 9g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, opens the channels, unblocks menstruation and alleviates pain.
With Tao Ren, invigorates the Blood for all types of Blood Stagnation such as dysmenorrhea, amenorrhea, swollen painful sores and chest and abdominal pain.
Rx. Ginseng Ren Shen 9g Powerfully tonifies Yuan Qi, tonifies Heart Qi and calms the Spirit.
With Wu Wei Zi and Mai Men Dong, for Qi and Yin Deficiency with shortness of breath and spontaneous sweat.
Rx. Ophiopogonis Mai Men Dong 9g Moistens the Lungs, nourishes Stomach Yin, stops coughing, clears the Heart and eliminates irritability.
Fr. Schisandrae Wu Wei Zi 9g Tonifies Qi, tonifies the Kidneys, astringes Jing, stops diarrhea and spermatorrhea, astringes sweat, generates fluids, quiets the Spirit and calms and contains Heart Qi.
With Ren Shen and Gan Jiang, for Lung Deficiency with chronic coughing and wheezing.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Supports Zheng Qi
  • Consolidates Exhaustion
  • Strengthens Qi
  • Returns Yang
  • Invigorates the Blood

SYNDROMES

  • Phlegm Obstructs the Heart Orifice: Exhaustion Syndrome

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Sudden collapse with loss of consciousness
  • Mouth open and hands relaxed
  • Eyes closed
  • Limbs cold
  • Snoring
  • Faint breathing
  • Perspiration ("flows like oil")
  • Incontinence of urine and feces
  • T: Atonic
  • P: Minute and thready - about to collapse or
  • P: Superficial and big but with no root (with red face)

TREATS

  • Cerebrovascular accident
  • Post-stroke sequelae
  • Stroke
  • Hemiplegia
