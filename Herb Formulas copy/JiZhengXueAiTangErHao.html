HERB FORMULAS

JI ZHENG XUE AI TANG ER HAO - 急症血癌湯二号- ACUTE LEUKEMIA DECOCTION #2

English: Acute Leukemia Decoction #2
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. et Rz. Rhei Da Huang 9g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, drains Heat, purges accumulations, cools the Blood, reduces Fire toxicity and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Sheng Di Huang, for Deficiency Fire.
With Gan Cao, for vomiting immediately after eating.
Cx. Moutan Mu Dan Pi 3g Clears Heat, cools and harmonizes the Blood, clears Deficiency Fire, invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, drains pus and reduces swelling.
With Sheng Di Huang, for residual Warm pathogens which have injured the Yin and Kidney Yin Deficiency with steaming bones.
Rx. Scrophulariae Xuan Shen 9g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, nourishes Yin, softens hardness, dissipates Stagnation in the lymph nodes of the neck, drains Fire and relieves toxicity.
With Sheng Di Huang, for Excess or Deficiency Heat patterns.
With Mu Dan Pi, for erysipelas and purplish rashes.
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 9g Clears Heat, cools the Blood and nourishes Yin.
Fol. Isatidis Da Qing Ye 9g Clears Heat, resolves Fire toxicity, cools the Blood, dissipates maculae and treats leukemia.
Pulvis Glycyrrhizae Preparata Gan Cao Fen 4.5g Clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity, inhibits the growth of cancer cells, moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs and guides the herbs to all twelve channels.
Periostracum Cicadae Chan Tui 4.5g Disperses Wind, clears Heat and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Trichosanthis Tian Hua Fen 6g Drains Heat, generates Fluids, relieves toxicity, expels pus, reduces swelling and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clear Heat
  • Cools the Blood
  • Relieves toxicity
  • Inhibits the growth of cancer cells

SYNDROMES

  • Blood Heat

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Chronic, stubborn skin lesions
  • Sores
  • Enlarged lymph nodes
  • Nodules
  • Red itching skin
  • Hardness
  • T: Pale with red spots
  • P: Rapid and pecking

TREATS

  • Acute granulocytic leukemia
