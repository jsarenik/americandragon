HERB FORMULAS

KERATITIS #1

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rz. et Rx. Notopterygii Qiang Huo 9g Releases the exterior and disperses Cold.
Flos Chrysanthemi Indici Ye Ju Hua 9g Drains Fire and resolves toxicity.
Flos Lonicerae Jin Yin Hua 9g Clears Heat, resolves Fire toxicity and vents and disperses External Wind-Heat.
With lIan Qiao, for various Heat disorders.
With Huan Qin, for suppurating boils that have not ulcerated, or have ulcerated but are not discharging pus.
Fr. Gardeniae Zhi Zi 9g Clears Heat, reduces Fire, cools the Blood, relieves toxicity, reduces swelling and activates the Blood.
Rx. Isatidis Ban Lan Gen 9g Drains Heat, relieves Fire toxicity and cools the Blood.
With Jin Yin Hua, Liang Qiao and Jing Jie, for early-stage Wind-Heat.
With Qiang Huo, for Heat toxin affecting the head and neck.
Rx. Scutellariae Huang Qin 9g Drains Fire, detoxifies, cools the Blood and calms ascending Liver Yang.
With Zhi Zi, for sores due to Heat toxin.
With Qiang Huo, for Cold-Dampness at the Exterior with Heat in the Interior.
Fr. Forsythiae Lian Qiao 9g Clears Heat (especially in the Upper Jiao), relieves toxicity, reduces abscesses and dissipates clumps.
With Ban Lan Gen, for any externally contracted Heat disorder.
With Ye Ju HUa, for initial stage warm febrile disease.
With Jin Yin Hua, for Initial stage Wind-Heat.
Sm. Cassiae Jue Ming Zi 9g Clears Liver Heat, expels Wind-Heat, clears the vision and benefits the eyes.
Hb. Schizonepetae Jing Jie 6g Releases the exterior, expels Wind and relieves itching.
With Fang Feng, for initial-stage boils.
Rx. Saposhnikoviae Fang Feng 6g Releases the exterior, expels External Wind and alleviates pain.
With Jing Jie, expels Wind and stops itching.
With Jing Jie and Qiang Huo, for Wind-Cold.
With Fang Feng, jin Yin Hua and Lian Qiao, for early-stage carbuncles, furuncles, and abscesses.
Fol. Isatidis Da Qing Ye 6g Clears Heat, resolves Fire toxicity, cools the Blood and dissipates maculae.
With Jin Yin Hua, for Toxic Heat sores, erysipelas, mumps, or sore throat.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Releases the Exterior
  • Drains Heat
  • Relieves Fire toxicity
  • Cools the Blood

SYNDROMES

  • Wind-Heat

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Photophobia
  • Dacyorrhea with faint rough or uncomfortable sensationS
  • Punctate or dendritic opacity of the cornea or
  • Grayish-whitish discoid cloudiness
  • Ciliary hyperemia
  • Headache
  • Stuffy nose
  • T: Red tip
  • C: Thin and yellowish
  • P: Floating and rapid

TREATS

  • Keratitis
