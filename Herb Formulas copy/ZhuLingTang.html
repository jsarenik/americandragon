HERB FORMULAS

ZHU LING TANG - 猪苓湯 - POLYPORUS DECOCTION

English: Polyporus Decoction
Also Known As: Griffolia Decoction
Polyporus Combination

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Scl. Polypori Zhu Ling 3-15g Promotes urination, leaches out Dampness and dispels Damp-Heat.
With Fu Ling, for Lin syndrome, scanty urine, edema, loose stools, vaginal discharge, jaundice, leg Qi and diarrhea.
With Fu Ling and Ze Xie, for edema due to Spleen Deficiency.
With Fu Ling, Ze Xie, and Hua Shi, resolves clumping of water and Heat, clears Heat and promotes flow in the water pathways.
With Hua Shi and E Jiao, for fever, dysuria, thirst and irritability due to water and Heat Accumulation with Yin Deficiency.
Poria Fu Ling 3-15g Promotes urination, leaches out Dampness, transforms Phlegm, strengthens the Spleen and harmonizes the Stomach.
With Ze Xie, facilitates the removal of water and leaches out Dampness to treat stagnant water and Dampness leading to edema, jaundice, diarrhea, Milky Lin, and scanty, difficult urination with abdominal distention for.
With Zhu Ling, promotes urination for Lin Syndrome, scanty urine, edema, loose stools, vaginal discharge, jaundice, leg Qi and diarrhea.
With Ze Xie and Zhu Ling, strengthens its ability to promote urination and leach out Dampness for dysuria and Phlegm Accumulation.
Rz. Alismatis Ze Xie 3-10g Promotes urination, leaches out Dampness, unblocks deep water pathways involving the Kidneys and promotes water metabolism.
Talcum Hua Shi 3-20g Promotes urination and drains Heat from the Urinary Bladder (aids movement of gravel and stones and expels Damp-Heat).
With Zhu Ling and Ze Xie, for Stone Lin.
Colla Corii Asini E Jiao 3-10g Enriches Yin and prevents excess urination.
With Zhu Ling and Hua Shi, for Yin and Fluid Deficiencies with internal buildup of water and Heat leading to urinary dysfunction.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Promotes urination
  • Clears Heat
  • Nourishes the Yin

SYNDROMES

  • Shao Yin Heat Syndrome
  • Yang Ming Syndrome
  • Stagnation of Heat with water retention
  • Hot bloody urinary dysfunction (Blood Lin)
  • Diarrhea in infants due to Damp-Heat

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those without Heat.
  • Contraindicated for those with abundant Heat and severely damaged Yin.
  • Contraindicated for those with pronounced Dampness.
HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

NOTES

  • This formula is special in its ability to promote urination without damaging the Yin.
