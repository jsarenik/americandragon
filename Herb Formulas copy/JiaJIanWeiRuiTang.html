HERB FORMULAS

JIA JIAN WEI RUI TANG - 加減葳蕤湯 - MODIFIED SOLOMON'S SEAL DECOCTION

English: Modified Solomon's Seal Decoction
Also Known As: Modified Decoction of Polygonatum Rhizome

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rz. Polygonati Odorati Yu Zhu 6-12g Nourishes Yin, moistens Dryness, moistens the Lungs, nourishes the Stomach and generate fluids
With Bo He, Bai Wei, Jie Geng and Dan Dou Chi, for Yin Deficiency with Exterior Wind-Heat.
Bul. Allii Fistulosi Cong Bai 6g Releases the exterior and induces sweating.
With Dan Dou Chi, treats elderly or weak patients, pregnant women and children during any season for either Hot or Cold pathogens.
Rx. Platycodi Jie Geng 3-6g Opens the Lungs, spreads Lung Qi, expels Phlegm, benefits the throat, expels pus and opens and raises Lung Qi, directing the effects of other herbs to the upper body.
Sm. Sojae Preparata Dan Dou Chi 9-12g Releases the Exterior, eliminates irritability, harmonizes the Middle Jiao and relieves stuffy sensations in the chest.
With Cong Bai, for Colds associated with Yin Deficiency.
Hb. Menthae Haplocalycis Bo He 3-6g Disperses Wind-Heat, cools and clears the head and eyes and benefits the throat.
Rx. Cynanchi Atrati Bai Wei 1-10g Cools the Blood and clears Deficiency Heat.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Preparata Zhi Gan Cao 1-3g Moistens the Lungs, resolves Phlegm, stops cough and moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs.
With Jie Geng, for pain and swelling of the throat.
Fr. Jujube Da Zao 2-5pcs Tonifies the Spleen and Stomach, augments Qi, nourishes the Blood, calms the Spirit and moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Nourishes Yin
  • Clears Heat
  • Induces sweating
  • Releases the Exterior

SYNDROMES

  • Wind-Heat with Yin Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Fever
  • Slight chills
  • Little or no sweating
  • Headache
  • Dry throat
  • Cough
  • Slight aversion to Wind and Cold
  • Thick, difficult-to-expectorate sputum
  • Thirst
  • Irritability
  • Restlessness
  • T: Dark red
  • C: Little or None
  • P: Rapid

TREATS

  • Common cold postpartum or in the elderly
  • Influenza
  • Acute bronchitis with fever and cough
  • Headache
  • Acute tonsillitis
  • Acute pharyngitis
  • Cough
  • Upper respiratory tract infection

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for Exterior syndromes without Yin Deficiency.
HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

NOTES

MODIFICATIONS

For more severe Exterior disorders: For cough, dry throat and difficult-to-expectorate sputum:
+ Rx. Saposhnikoviae Fang Feng
+ Rx. Puerariae Ge Gen + Fr. Arctii Niu Bang
For thirst and irritability: + Per. Trichosanthis Gua Lou Pi
+ Hb. Phylostachys Nigrum Zhu Ye For more severe thirst and irritability:
+ Rx. Trichosanthis Tian Hua Fen
+ Rx. Trichosanthis Tian Hua Fen + Hb. Lophatheri Dan Zhu Ye
