HERB FORMULAS

SHEN SHENG FU ZI TANG - 參升 附子湯 - GINSENG, CIMICIFUGA AND PREPARED ACONITE DECOCTION

English: Ginseng, Cimicifuga and Aconite Prepared Decoction
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Aconiti Lateralis Preparata Zhi Fu Zi 9g Warms Ming Men Fire, assists Heart, Kidney and Spleen Yang, disperses Cold and Dampness, warms the channels and stops pain.
With Gan Jiang, warms the Spleen and Kidneys, benefits Yang, and disperses Cold for overabundance of Yin fluids due to Deficiency Cold.
With Ren Shen, for severe Deficiency of the Source Qi and Collapse of Yang.
With Huang Qi, for spontaneous sweat and chills due to Yang Deficiency.
With Xi Xin, disperses Cold and stops pain.
Rz. Zingiberis Gan Jiang 9g Warms the Lungs, transforms thin mucus, warms the Middle, expels Cold and dispels Wind-Dampness seeping into the Lower Jiao.
With Gan Cao, for epigastric pain and vomiting due to Spleen and Stomach Deficiency Cold.
With Ren Shen and Zhi Ban Xia, for vomiting due to Deficiency Cold.
Rx. Saposhnikoviae Fang Feng 15g Expels Wind-Dampness and alleviates pain.
With Huang Qi, prevents Wind from entering the skin.
Sm. Pruni Yu Li Ren 15g Moistens the Intestines, unblocks the bowels, promotes urination and reduces edema.
Rx. Ginseng Ren Shen 15g Powerfully tonifies Yuan Qi and tonifies Lung Qi.
With Huang Qi, for Lung Qi Deficiency.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 18g Tonifies, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood, disperses Cold, regulates the menses, stops cough and treats dyspnea.
With Huang Qi, for Qi and Blood Deficiency.
With Shu Di Huang, for Heat symptoms due to Yin Deficiency and Devastated Blood.
With Ju Pi and Zhi Ban Xia, for Lung and Kidney Yin Deficiency with overflowing water turning to Phlegm, with cough, wheezing, nausea marked by profuse and salty sputum.
Rz. Pinelliae Preparatum Zhi Ban Xia 21g Dries Dampness transforms Phlegm and harmonizes the stomach.
With Gan Jiang, for vomiting due to Cold with copious, frothy sputum with Cold signs and thin mucus in the Lungs.
With Ren Shen, for vomiting due to Stomach Deficiency.
With Huang Lian, for Cold and Heat in the Middle Jiao with abdominal fullness, nausea and vomiting.
Rz. Cimicifugae Sheng Ma 21g Releases the Exterior, clears Heat, relieves toxicity, raises Yang and lifts sunken Qi.
With Huang Lian, for ulcers of the gums and mouth with bad breath.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 24g Tonifies the Spleen, augments Qi, resolves Phlegm, stops cough, Clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity and moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs.
Rx. et Rz. Ligustici Gao Ben 24g Expels Wind and Cold Dispels Wind-Dampness and alleviates pain.
With Xi Xin, for Wind-Cold-Damp headache, vertex headaches, stiff neck and toothaches.
With Chuan Xiong and Xi Xin, for headache due to Cold and Dampness.
Rx. Bupleuri Chai Hu 30g Harmonizes the Exterior and Interior, spreads Liver Qi, relieves Stagnation, raises Yang Qi and resolves Phlegm and congestion.
With Zhi Ke, for a stifling sensation in the chest, abdominal pain, poor appetite, and irregular bowel movements.
With Huang Qi, raises the Clear Yang due to abnormal descent of Qi.
With Huang Qi, Sheng Ma and Ren Shen, for Central Qi Sinking.
Flos Althaeae Rosae Shu Gui Hua 15g Moistens the skin, astringes, protects mucous membranes, expels Phlegm, cools Heat, reduces inflammation, detoxifies, activates the Blood in the Lower Jiao and alleviates pain.
Per. Citri Reticulatae Ju Pi 15g Circulates Qi, disperses knots, and stops pain.
Rx. Astragali Huang Qi 30g Tonifies Qi and Blood, tonifies Wei Qi, stabilizes the Exterior, tonifies the Lungs, aids circulation of moisture downward from the face, promotes urination and reduces edema.
Rx. Rehmanniae Preparata Shu Di Huang 6g Nourishes Liver and Kidney Yin, nourishes Jing and Blood and arrests cough and wheezing.
With Ren Shen, for Qi, Blood and Yin Deficiency.
Cx. Phellodendri Huang Bai 9g Drains Damp-Heat especially from the Lower Jiao, drains Kidney Fire and relieves Fire toxicity.
With Xi Xin, for urinary frequency, urgency, discomfort, and pain.
Rz. Coptidis Huang Lian 9g Clears Heat, drains Dampness and resolves Fire toxicity.
With Zhi Ban Xia and Gan Jiang, for Cold and Heat complexes in the Intestines and Stomach marked by focal distention, nausea, vomiting and feelings of oppression in the chest.
With Xi Xi, for Swollen eyes and deafness.
Fr. Aurantii Zhi Ke 9g Promotes the movement of Qi and reduces distention and pressure.
With Sheng Ma, relaxes the bowels and encourages the downward flow of turbid Yin.
Rx. Asari Xi Xin 6g Expels Wind and Cold, warms the Lungs, transforms Phlegm and Thin Mucus and circulates water.
Rz. Chuanxiong Chuan Xiong 9g Invigorates the Blood and promotes the movement of Qi.
With Dang Gui, for Blood Deficiency causing menstrual problems including dysmenorrhea, scanty menses, and amenorrhea.
With Xi Xin, for Shao Yin Cold headache.
Fr. Viticis Man Jing Zi 9g Drains Dampness, expels Wind, clears Heat and relieves pain.
With Ren Shen and Huang Qi, for eye disease, tinnitus, dizziness, and deafness due to Deficiency.
With Gao Ben, for severe headache without Interior Fire.

  • Expels Cold
  • Strengthens the Lungs

  • Activates the Blood
