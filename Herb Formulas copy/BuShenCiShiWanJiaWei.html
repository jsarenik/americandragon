HERB FORMULAS

BU SHEN CI SHI WAN JIA WEI - 補腎磁石囊加味 - MODIFIED TONIFY THE KIDNEYS WITH MAGNETITE PILL

English: Modified Supplement the Kidneys with Magnetite Pill
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Sm. Cuscutae Tu Si Zi 12g Strengthens Yang, nourishes Yin, astringes Jing and urine, benefits the marrow, tonifies the Kidneys and Liver and improves vision.
With Ju Hua, for unclear vision.
Fr. Lycii Gou Qi Zi 12g Nourishes and tonifies Liver and Kidney Blood and Yin, benefits Jing and brightens the eyes.
With Shu Di Huang, for Liver and Kidney Deficiency.
With Ju Hua, for Liver and Kidney Deficiency with Jing and Blood unable to rise and nourish the eyes with tinnitus, headache and visual impairment.
Magnetitum Ci Shi 15g Anchors Liver Yang, calms the Spirit, benefits Yin, nourishes the Kidneys, augments the Liver and improves hearing and vision.
With Gou Qi Zi, Nu Zhen Zi and Ju Hua, for diminished vision due to Liver and Kidney Deficiency.
With Shu Di Huang, Gou Qi Zi and Ju Hua, for cataracts and other retinal problems.
Flos Chrysanthemi Ju Hua 12g Calms the Liver and clears the eyes.
With Gou Qi Zi and Nu Zhen Zi, for dizziness and blurred vision due to Liver and Kidney Yin Deficiency.
Hb. Cistanches Rou Cong Rong 10g Tonifies the Kidneys, strengthens Yang and
benefits Jing and marrow.
Concha Haliotidis Shi Jue Ming 15g Drains Liver Fire, pacifies the Liver and anchors and sedates Liver Yang Improves the vision and causes superficial visual obstruction to recede.
With Ju Hua, for eye redness, swelling and pain due to Liver Yang Rising.
Rx. Rehmanniae Preparata Shu Di Huang 12g Nourishes Liver and Kidney Yin.
Fr. Ligustri Lucidi Nu Zhen Zi 10g Nourishes and tonifies Liver and Kidney Yin, clears Deficiency Heat Improves vision.
With Shu Di Huang and Gou Qi Zi, for diminished visual acuity due to Liver and Kidney Yin Deficiency.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Enriches and supplements the Liver and Kidneys
  • Tonifies Jing
  • Brightens the eyes

SYNDROMES

  • Liver and Kidney Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Blurred vision
  • Dizziness
  • Tinnitus
  • Profuse dreaming
  • Low back and knee pain and weakness
  • Impotence
  • Seminal emissions
  • Dribbling urination
  • T: Pale
  • C: White
  • P: Thready and weak

TREATS

  • Myopia
  • Strangury
  • Low back pain
  • Spermatorrhea
  • Dizziness
  • Tinnitus
  • Impotence
