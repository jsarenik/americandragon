HERB FORMULAS

HONG TENG ER HAO FANG - 紅藤二号方 - SARGENTODOXA FORMULA #2 (RETENTION ENEMA)

English: Sargentodoxa Formula #2
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Caulis Sargentodoxae Hong Teng 9-30g Clears Heat, resolves toxicity, reduces abscesses, stops pain, invigorates the Blood and disperses Stasis.
Rz. Polygoni Cuspidati Hu Zhang 8-30g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, unblocks the channels, stops pain, discharges toxins and disperses swelling.
Hb. Patriniae Bai Jiang Cao 6-30g Clears Heat, resolves toxicity, expels pus, eliminates Phlegm, reduces abscesses internally and topically, invigorates the Blood and dispels Blood Stasis.
With Wu Ling Zhi and Xiang Fu, for Blood Stasis.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 3-15g Tonifies, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood, reduces swelling, expels pus, generates flesh and alleviates pain.
With Huang Qi, for ulcerated abscesses with insufficient drainage or which drain a clear thin discharge.
Rx. Salviae Miltiorrhizae Dan Shen 3-15g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, cools the Blood and reduces abscesses.
With Dang Gui, for irregular menstruation and retained lochia from Blood Stasis
Fr. Liquidamberis Lu Lu Tong 3-10g Promotes the movement of Qi, invigorates the Blood and unblocks the channels.
With Xiang Fu, for irregular, scanty periods.
Eupolyphaga/ Steleophaga Tu Bie Chong 3-12g Breaks up and drives out Blood Stasis, invigorates Blood circulation and disperses lumps and masses.
With Pu Huang and Wu Ling Zhi, for amenorrhea, pain and fixed, palpable masses.
Rz. Sparganii San Leng 3-10g Forcefully breaks up Blood Stasis, regulates Qi, alleviates pain, dissolves accumulations and regulates menstruation.
Rz. Curcumae E Zhu 3-10g Invigorates Blood circulation, dispels Blood Stasis, regulates Qi, dissolves accumulations and alleviates pain.
With San Leng, for Qi and Blood Stagnation with clumping and accumulation leading to pain with distending pain in the chest, epigastrium and abdomen, amenorrhea, dysmenorrhea, uterine myoma and ovarian cysts.
Faeces Trogopterori Wu Ling Zhi 3-10g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, stops bleeding and alleviates pain.
Pollen Typhae Pu Huang 3-12g Stops bleeding by astringing, invigorates the Blood and dispels Blood Stasis.
With Wu Ling Zhi, for Blood Stasis causing menstrual irregularity, dysmenorrhea and lochiostasis with chest and abdominal pain.
Spina Gleditsiae Zao Jiao Ci 3-10g Draws out toxicity, discharges pus, invigorates the Blood and reduces swelling.
Rx. Astragali Huang Qi 9-30g Tonifies Qi and Blood, promotes the discharge of pus, generates flesh, expels toxins, relieves numbness and pain.
With Dang Gui, for Qi and Blood Deficiency with chronic non-healing sores, numbness, painful obstruction and paresthesia.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Transforms Stasis
  • Softens the hard
  • Scatters nodulation

SYNDROMES

  • Blood Stagnation

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Pain that is fixed, stabbing and /or severe
  • Pain is worse with pressure
  • Dark complexion
  • Fixed, palpable masses
  • Bleeding with clots
  • Purple lips
  • Purple nails
  • Blue, dark or purplish discoloration of skin in small or large areas
  • Dry skin
  • Varicose and spider veins
  • Cold hands and feet
  • Tendency to hemorrhage dark blood or clots
  • T: Purple, possible purple or red spots
    Red purple if there is Heat
    Blue purple if there is Cold
  • C: White
  • P: Choppy or Wiry and choppy

TREATS

  • Fallopian tube blockage
