HERB FORMULAS

ARC #2

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rz. Polygonati Odorati Yu Zhu 6-30g Nourishes Yin, moistens Dryness, moistens the Lungs, nourishes the Stomach and generates fluids.
With Bai Wei, Bo He and Dan Dou Chi, for Externally contracted Wind-Heat with Yin Deficient constitutions.
With Sheng Di Huang, resolves cysts.
Rx. Cynanchi Bai Wei 3-12g Cools the Blood, clears Deficiency Heat, relieves toxicity and treats sores.
With Bo He and Yu Zhu, for External Wind-Heat with underlying Yin Deficiency.
Rx. Platycodi Jie Geng 3-10g Opens the Lungs, spreads Lung Qi, expels Phlegm, benefits the throat and raises Lung Qi, directing the effects of other herbs to the upper body.
With Yu Zhu, Bo He, Bai Wei and Dan Dou Chi, for Yin Deficiency with Exterior Wind-Heat.
With Gan Cao, disseminates Lung Qi, expels Phlegm, resolves toxicity and expels pus for productive coughing with hoarseness, pain and swelling of the throat due to Wind-Heat.
Hb. Menthae Haplocalycis Bo He 3-6g Disperses Wind-Heat, cools and clears the head and eyes, benefits the throat and vents rashes.
Sm. Sojae Preparata Dan Dou Chi 6-18g Releases the Exterior, eliminates irritability, harmonizes the Middle Jiao and relieves stuffy sensations in the chest.
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 5-30g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, nourishes Yin and generates fluids.
With Mai Men Dong for a dry throat, swelling of the throat and lymph nodes, irritability, a red tongue and a fast thready pulse due to Devastated Yin with Blazing Fire.

 
Rx. Ophiopogonis Mai Men Dong 6-15g Moistens the Lungs, nourishes Yin, stops coughing, nourishes Stomach Yin, generates Fluids, moistens the Intestines, clears the Heart and eliminates irritability.
With Xuan Shen, Jie Geng and Gan Cao, for an itchy, dry throat, thirst and a non-productive cough due to Lung Yin Deficiency.
With Sheng Di Huang, for hematemesis or epistaxis due to Yin Deficiency with Excess Heat.
With Sheng Di Huang and Xuan Shen, for thirst, constipation, irritability and a low-grade fever due to Stomach Yin Deficiency in the aftermath of warm febrile disease where the fluids have been injured.
Bul. Fritillariae Cirrhosae Chuan Bei Mu 3-12g Clears Heat, transforms Phlegm, stops coughing, nourishes and moistens the Lungs, transforms Phlegm, and dissipates nodules.
Rx. Scrophulariae Xuan Shen 7-30g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, nourishes Yin, transforms Phlegm-Heat, softens hardness, dissipates nodules, drains Fire and relieves toxicity.
With Jin Yin Hua and Gan Cao, for boils, carbuncles and furuncles.
With Sheng Di Huang for Excess or Deficiency Heat patterns.
With Sheng Di Huang and Mai Men Dong, for a sore, dry throat due to Deficiency Fire.
With Jie Geng and Gan Cao, for a sore throat with Dryness and pain.
Rx. Isatidis Ban Lan Gen 9-30g Drains Heat, relieves Fire toxicity, cools the Blood and benefits the throat.
Fol. Isatidis Da Qing Ye 9-30g Clears Heat, resolves Fire toxicity, cools the Blood and dissipates maculae.
With Jin Yin Hua, for Toxic Heat sores, erysipelas, mumps or a sore throat.
Flos Lonicerae Jin Yin Hua 6-20g Vents and disperses External Wind-Heat,clears Heat and resolves Fire toxicity.
With Gan Cao, for toxic swellings and sores.
With Gan Cao and Xuan Shen, for sloughing ulcers from Heat toxicity.
Concretio Bambusae Silicea Tian Zhu Huang 3-10g Clears and transforms Phlegm-Heat, cools the Heart and settles tremors and convulsions.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 6-14g Moistens the Lungs, resolves Phlegm, stops coughing, clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity, moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs and guides the herbs to all twelve channels.
With Jin Yin Hua, for Damp rashes or acne.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Nourishes Yin
  • Transforms Phlegm
  • Clears Heat
  • Expels Wind

SYNDROMES

  • Yin Deficiency with External Wind-Heat

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Lack of strength in the whole body
  • Five-Sole-Heat
  • Low back and knee stiffness and soreness
  • Seminal emission
  • Premature ejaculation
  • Palpitations
  • Insomnia
  • Low grade fever
  • Aversion to Wind
  • Thirst
  • Sore throat
  • Cough with little Phlegm
  • Chest pain
  • Lymphadenopathy in the back of the neck, underarms and groin
  • T: Red
  • C: Little or None
  • P: Floating, thready and rapid

TREATS

  • Aids Related Complex (ARC)
