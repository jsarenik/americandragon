HERB FORMULAS

ESOPHAGEAL CANCER #73

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Concha Ostreae Mu Li 60g Calms the Liver, benefits Yin, anchors Floating Yang, softens hardness, dissipates nodules and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Scrophulariae Xuan Shen 15g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, nourishes Yin, transforms Phlegm-Heat, softens hardness and dissipates nodules.
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 15g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, nourishes Yin and generates fluids.
With Xuan Shen, for Kidney Yin Deficiency with Fire Blazing with soreness and swelling in the throat, dry mouth and a parched tongue.
With Xuan Shen and Mai Men Dong, for swelling of the throat and lymph nodes, a dry throat, irritability, a red tongue and a fast thready pulse due to Devastated Yin with Blazing Fire.
With Bai Shao, for Blood Deficiency Heat.
Rx. Ophiopogonis Mai Men Dong 15g Nourishes Lung and Stomach Yin, stops coughing and generates Fluids.
With Sheng Di Huang, for hematemesis or epistaxis due to Yin Deficiency with Excess Heat.
Rx. Paeoniae Alba Bai Shao 15g Nourishes the Blood, regulates menstruation, astringes Yin, adjusts the Ying and Wei, calms Liver Yang and Liver Wind and alleviates pain.
Sm. Trichosanthis Gua Lou Ren 15g Clears Heat, transforms Hot Phlegm, cools and moistens the Lungs, expands the chest, dissipates nodules and eliminates pus.
Rx. Trichosanthis Tian Hua Fen 15g Clears and drains Lung Heat, transforms Phlegm, moistens Lung Dryness, generates Fluids and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Mu Li, for irritability and mental disorientation due to Lung and Stomach Yin Deficiency and ascending Heat with thirst.
Sm. Arecae Bing Lang 18g Regulates Qi, reduces accumulations, drains downward, unblocks the bowels and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Hb. Dendrobii Shi Hu 18g Nourishes Kidney, Stomach and Lung Yin, clears Heat, generates Fluids and reduces Heat from Deficiency.
With Mai Men Dong and Tian Hua Fen, for dry heaves and a shiny tongue due to Stomach Yin Deficiency.
With Sheng Di Huang and Xuan Shen, for a low grade fever, thirst, blurred vision and muscular aches and pains from injured Fluids due to Heat.
Pumex Fu Shi 30g Clears Heat from the Lungs, transforms Phlegm, softens hardness and dissipates Phlegm nodules.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 6g Moistens the Lungs, resolves Phlegm, stops coughing, inhibits the growth of cancer cells. moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs and guides the herbs to all twelve channels.
Fr. Forsythiae Lian Qiao 9g Clears Heat, especially in the Upper Jiao, relieves toxicity, reduces abscesses, dissipates clumps, clears Blood Heat and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Heat
  • Resolves Phlegm
  • Nourishes Yin and Blood
  • Dissipates nodules
  • Inhibits the growth of cancer cells

SYNDROMES

  • Phlegm-Heat with Yin and Blood Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Progressive dysphagia
  • Vomiting after eating
  • Cough with sticky, yellow, difficult to expectorate sputum
  • Rapid breathing with a harsh voice
  • Restless fever
  • Sore throat
  • Constipation
  • Dry stools
  • Mania
  • Shortness of breath
  • Dyspnea
  • Nausea
  • Chest and/or epigastric fullness
  • Pain in the ribs
  • Fatigue
  • Lack of energy
  • Five-Sole-Heat
  • Dry throat
  • Persistent thirst
  • Afternoon fever
  • Dry stools
  • Constipation
  • Scanty, dark-colored urine
  • Night sweats
  • Dizziness
  • Pale face
  • Palpitations
  • Insomnia
  • Dry skin
  • Dry hair
  • Poor memory
  • Paleness
  • Scanty periods
  • T: Scarlet or Red
  • C: Yellow or Yellow and greasy or Yellow and smooth or Little or None
  • P: Rapid and smooth or Slippery and rapid or Thready and rapid

TREATS

  • Esophageal cancer
