HERB FORMULAS

XIAO LUO WAN - 小瘰囊 - REDUCE SCROFULA PILL

English: Reduce Scrofula Pill
Also Known As: Xiao Lei Wan

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Scrophulariae Xuan Shen 10-15g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, nourishes Yin, softens hardness, dissipates nodules and transforms Phlegm-Heat.
With Mu Li and Zhe Bei Mu, for scrofula, goiter and other nodules due to Phlegm.
Concha Ostreae Mu Li 15-20g Softens hardness and dissipates nodules.
Bul. Fritillariae Thunbergii Zhe Bei Mu 10-15g Clears Heat, transforms Phlegm and dissipates nodules.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Heat
  • Resolves Phlegm
  • Softens Hardness
  • Dissipates nodules

SYNDROMES

  • Phlegm Fire

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Nodules on the neck that are firm and rubbery
  • Dry throat
  • Bitter taste in mouth
  • Dry mouth
  • T: Red
  • C: Normal
  • P: Rapid and slippery and/or Wiry

TREATS

  • Scrofula
  • Goiter
  • Acute lymphadenitis
  • Parotitis
  • AIDS/HIV
  • Hyperthyroidism
  • Cervical lymphadenopathy
  • Thyroiditis
  • Hyperplasia of the mammary glands

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with congealing due to Cold.
