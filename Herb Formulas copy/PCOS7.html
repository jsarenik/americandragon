HERB FORMULAS

PCOS #7

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Rehmanniae Preparata Shu Di Huang 12g Nourishes the Blood, nourishes Liver and Kidney Yin and nourishes Jing and Blood and fills the Marrow.
Colla Cervi Cornus Lu Jiao Jiao 4-12g Tonifies the Kidneys, strengthens Yang, warms Yang, regulates the Chong and Ren channels, stabilizes the Dai Channel, strengthens Jing and Blood and tonifies and nourishes Qi and Blood.
With Shu Di Huang, for infertility and thin vaginal discharge due to Kidney Deficiency Cold.
With Gou Ji, for copious vaginal discharge.
Sm. Cuscutae Tu Si Zi 6-18g Strengthens Yang, nourishes Yin, astringes Jing and urine, benefits the marrow tonifies the Kidneys and Liver and calms the fetus.
Fr. Rubi Fu Pen Zi 2-12g Tonifies and stabilizes the Kidneys, tonifies Yang and astringes Jing and urine.
Rz. Cibotii Gou Ji 4.5-15g Tonifies the Liver and Kidneys, expels Wind-Dampness, relieves Bi syndrome, warms and stabilizes the Kidneys and prevents leakage.
With Tu Si Zi, for Kidney disorders with Wind-Damp Bi manifesting as low back and knee pain.
Sm. Trigonella Hu Lu Ba 3-10g Warms the Kidneys, disperses Dampness and alleviates pain.
Hb. Epimedii Yin Yang Huo 12g Tonifies the Kidneys, strengthens Yang, dispels Wind-Cold-Dampness and warms and unblocks the flow of Yang Qi.
Rz. Polygonati Huang Jing 12g Tonifies the Kidneys and strengthens Jing.
Spica Prunellae Xia Ku Cao 6-18g Clears Hot Phlegm and dissipates nodules.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Tonifies Kidney Yang
  • Replenishes Jing
  • Scatters nodulation

SYNDROMES

  • Kidney Yang Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Delayed menstruation
  • Scanty and light-colored menses
  • Gradual amenorrhea
  • Occasional irregular menstruation
  • Ovarian cysts
  • Lumbago: low back feels cold, weak and aching
  • Feeling chilled
  • Aversion to cold
  • Cold limbs
  • General debility
  • Pale face
  • Urinary frequency with abundant, clear urine or
  • Oliguria with edema
  • Maybe nocturia
  • Lassitude
  • Withdrawn, subdued behavior
  • Knees feel cold and aching
  • Dizziness
  • Tinnitus
  • Impotence
  • Premature ejaculation
  • Low libido
  • Female infertility ("uterus cold and infertile")
  • Maybe anorexia
  • Maybe loose stools
  • Maybe chronic diarrhea or "cock crow" diarrhea
  • T: Pale and swollen
  • C: Thin and white
  • P: Deep and thready - maybe slow

TREATS

  • Polycystic ovary syndrome
