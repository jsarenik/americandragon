HERB FORMULAS

INFERTILITY #17

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Rehmanniae Preparata Shu Di Huang 9-30g Nourishes Jing and Blood and fills the Marrow.
With Dang Gui, for Blood Deficiency marked by dizziness, palpitations, insomnia and menstrual dysfunction.
Fr. Corni Shan Zhu Yu 6-12g Stabilizes the Kidneys, astringes Jing and Body Fluids, tonifies Liver and Kidney Yin, strengthens Kidney Yang, stabilizes the menses and stops bleeding.
With Shu Di Huang, nourishes Kidney Yin and secures Jing, while restraining urination and sweating.
With Dang Gui and Shu Di Huang, for night sweats due to Yin Deficiency.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 3-15g Tonifies the Blood and regulates the menses.
With Shu Di Huang, for Heat symptoms due to Yin Deficiency and Devastated Blood.
Rx. Paeoniae Alba Bai Shao 5-30g Nourishes the Blood, regulates menstruation, astringes Yin, softens the Liver and relieves pain.
With Dang Gui, nourishes Yin and Blood.
With Dang Gui and Shu Di Huang, for dizziness, blurred vision and dysmenorrhea due to Blood Deficiency or Stasis.
Placenta Hominis Zi He Che 1.5-5g Tonifies the Liver and Kidneys, strengthens Jing, tonifies Qi and nourishes the Blood.
With Shu Di Huang, for chronic weakness and debility.
With Shu Di Huang and Dang Gui, for insufficient lactation due to Blood Deficiency and anemia.
With Shu Di Huang, Dang Gui and Bai Shao, for Blood Deficiency.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Nourishes the Blood
  • Tonifies Jing

SYNDROMES

  • Jing and Blood Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Amenorrhea or
  • A sallow facial complexion
  • Delayed menstruation
  • Fatigue
  • Heavy and painful lower back
  • Dream-disturbed sleep
  • General weakness
  • Lack of interest in food or drink
  • Fever
  • Night sweats
  • Forgetfulness
  • Toothache
  • Dizziness
  • Tinnitus
  • Gum swelling
  • Vaginal discharge
  • General pitted edema which is worse in the lower body
  • Discharge of rice-water like urination without dysuria
  • Aversion to Cold
  • Impotence
  • Decreased libido
  • Emaciation
  • Loose teeth
  • Signs of premature aging
  • Abdominal Cold
  • Easily frightened
  • T: Pale or Pale and thick
  • C: Smooth and white
  • P: Moderate and weak or Deep and weak

TREATS

  • Infertility
