HERB FORMULAS

CANCER #23

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Cx. Dictamni Xu Duan 15g Tonifies the Liver and Kidneys, stops uterine bleeding, calms the fetus, prevents miscarriage, promotes the movement of Blood, alleviates pain and reduces swellings, abscesses and sores.
Sm. Coicis Yi Yi Ren 15g Strengthens the Spleen, resolves Dampness, Clears Damp-Heat and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Caulis Aristolochiae Manshuriensis Guan Mu Tong 9g Invigorates the Blood, unblocks the menses, promotes lactation and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Sm. Sojae Preparatum Dan Dou Chi 9g Releases the Exterior Eliminates irritability, harmonizes the Middle Jiao and relieves stuffy sensations in the chest.
Rx. Gentianae Long Dan Cao 9g Drains Damp-Heat from the Liver and Gallbladder channels and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rz. Dioscoreae Shan Yao 9g Tonifies Kidney Yin and astringes Jing.
Cx. Moutan Mu Dan Pi 6g Clears Heat, cools and harmonizes the Blood, clears Deficiency Fire, invigorates the Blood and dispels Blood Stasis.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Tonifies the Liver and Kidneys
  • Strengthens the Spleen
  • Resolves Dampness
  • Clears Damp-Heat
  • Inhibits the growth of cancer cells

SYNDROMES

  • Damp-Heat

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Maybe Yang jaundice (sclera and skin bright yellow)
  • Pain and fullness in ribs on both sides
  • Maybe pain in the right hypochondrium
  • Bitter taste in mouth
  • Nausea
  • Vomiting sour and bitter fluids
  • Anorexia
  • Maybe alternating chills and fever
  • Concentrated, dark urine
  • Abdominal distention and fullness
  • Restlessness
  • Feverishness
  • Women:
    Yellow vaginal discharge with foul smell
    Pruritis vulvae
  • Men:
    Pain, swelling and burning sensations of testicles
  • T: Pink with red tip and edges
  • C: Yellow and greasy or sticky
  • P: Wiry and rapid or Slippery and rapid
  • More Heat than Dampness:
    Bright yellow eyes and skin
    Fever
    Thirst
    Restless
    Nausea
    Dark, scanty urine
    T: Pink with red tip and edges
    C: Thick
    P: Wiry and rapid
  • More Dampness than Heat:
    Light yellow coloration
    Heaviness of the head and body
    Epigastric and abdominal fullness and distention
    Anorexia
    Thirst with no desire to drink
    Loose stools
    T: Pink with red tip and edges
    C: Whitish-gray
    P: Soft or slippery

TREATS

  • Cancer of the vulva
