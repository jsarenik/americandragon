HERB FORMULAS

SI MIAO WAN - 四妙丸 - FOUR MARVEL PILL

English: Four-Marvel Pill
Also Known As: Pill of Four Effective Ingredients

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Cx. Phellodendri Huang Bai 9-24g Drains Damp-Heat, especially from the Lower Jiao and drains Kidney Fire.
Rz. Atractylodis Cang Zhu 12-24g Strongly dries Dampness, tonifies the Spleen, and clears Dampness from the Lower Jiao.
With Huang Bai, for pain, swelling, and weakness of the lower extremities or skin lesions due to Wind-Dampness.
Rx. Achyranthis Bidentatae Niu Xi 12-24g Invigorates the Blood, expels Blood Stasis, clears Damp-Heat in the Lower Jiao and regulates water metabolism.
Sm. Coicis Yi Yi Ren 24-30g Promotes urination, leaches out Dampness, strengthens the Spleen, expels Wind-Dampness and clears Damp-Heat.
With Huang Bai, Cang Zhu and Niu Xi, for Damp-Heat pouring downward leading to atrophy and weakness of the lower extremities.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Eliminates Heat
  • Resolves Dampness

SYNDROMES

  • Damp-Heat in the Lower Jiao
  • Atrophy disorder (Wei syndrome)

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Pain in low back and knees
  • Weakness or atrophy of lower extremities
  • Scanty yellow urine
  • Red, hot, swollen and painful feet or knees
  • Edema in lower legs
  • Eczema
  • Painful and swollen feet
  • Pain and swelling of the joints
  • Leukorrhea (thick, yellow and foul-smelling)
  • Sores on lower extremities due to Damp
  • Severe numbness and weakness in lower extremities
  • T: Normal
  • C: Yellow and greasy
  • P: Soft and rapid
