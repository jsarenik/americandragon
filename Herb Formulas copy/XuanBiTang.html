HERB FORMULAS

XUAN BI TANG - 宣痺湯 - DISBAND PAINFUL OBSTRUCTION DECOCTION

English: Disband Painful Obstruction Decoction
Also Known As: Decoction to Relieve Bi Syndrome
Decoction for Removing the Meridian Obstruction

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Aristolochiae Guang Fang Ji 10-15g Expels Wind-Dampness, dispels Damp-Heat in the Upper Jiao, disperses superficial swelling, promotes urination, dries Dampness, stops pain and strengthens the Spleen (affects all three Jiaos).
With Yi Yi Ren, for Damp-Heat-induced extremity pain.
(Rx. Stephaniae Tetrandrae) (Han Fang Ji) (10-15g) (Expels Wind-Dampness, purges Damp-Heat, alleviates pain, promotes urination and reduces edema. Substitutes for Guang Fang Ji.)
(With Yi Yi Ren and Can Sha, for Wind-Damp Bi and Wind-Damp-Heat Bi.
Sm. Armeniacae Xing Ren 10-15g Regulates the Lung's water metabolism function.
Sm. Coicis Yi Yi Ren 15-20g Promotes urination, strengthens the Spleen, clears Heat, expels Wind-Dampness, soothes numbness and clears Damp-Heat.
With Can Sha, strengthens the Spleen, resolves Dampness and treats painful obstruction.
Faeces Bombycis Can Sha 9-10g Dispels Wind Dampness, harmonizes the Stomach and transforms turbid Dampness.
With Han Fang Ji, Yi Yi Ren and Lian Qiao, for Damp-Heat Bi and Wind-Damp Bi.
Rz. Pinelliae Preparatum Zhi Ban Xia 9-10g Dries Dampness, transforms Phlegm, descends Rebellious Qi, and reduces swelling.
With Can Sha, dries Dampness and eliminates turbidity.
Fr. Forsythiae Lian Qiao 9-10g Clears relatively superficial Heat and promotes urination.
Fr. Gardenia Zhi Zi 9-10g Clears Heat, eliminates irritability, drains Damp-Heat and reduces swelling.
With Hua Shi, Lian Qiao and Chi Xiao Dou, assists in clearing Heat and draining Dampness through the urine.
Talcum Hua Shi 15-20g Promotes urination and expels Damp-Heat.
Sm. Phaseoli Chi Xiao Dou 9-20g Promotes urination, clears Heat, clears Damp Heat and reduces swelling.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears and Resolves Damp-Heat
  • Unblocks the channels
  • Disbands painful obstruction

SYNDROMES

  • Damp-Heat Bi
  • Wei Syndrome due to Damp-Heat Accumulation (modified)

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Heat and pain in the joints
  • Reduced mobility
  • Fever and shaking chills or
  • Fever with sweating
  • Lusterless yellow complexion
  • Scanty, dark urine
  • Pain worse on cloudy or rainy days
  • Arthralgia involving one or several joints
  • Local redness and Heat of the extremities
  • Swelling with excruciating pain
  • Severe limitation of movement
  • Oliguria
  • Pain worse better the day and worse at night
  • Thirst
  • Dryness of the mouth
  • Irritability
  • Restlessness
  • Desire for cold drinks
  • Aversion to Heat
  • T: Pink or Red
  • C: Thin and yellow or Gray or Yellow greasy
  • P: Rolling and rapid maybe slippery

TREATS

  • Rheumatic fever
  • Rheumatic arthritis
  • Rheumatoid arthritis
  • Rheumatic Heart disease
  • Gouty arthritis
  • Connective tissue disorders
  • Mononeuropathy
  • Myasthenia gravis
  • Perspiration
  • Temporomandibular joint syndrome (TMJ)
  • Arthralgia
  • Subacute lupus erythematosus
  • Guillain-Barre syndrome
  • Fever
  • Erythema arthriticum
  • Polyneuropathy
  • Neuralgic aneuropathy
  • Low back pain
  • Osteoarthritis
  • Painful obstruction
