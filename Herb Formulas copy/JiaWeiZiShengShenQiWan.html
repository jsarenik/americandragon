HERB FORMULAS

JIA WEI ZI SHENG SHEN QI WAN - 加味資生腎氣丸 - MODIFIED BOLUS FOR BENEFITTING PATIENTS AND REINFORCING THE KIDNEY

English: Modified Bolus for Benefiting Patients and Reinforcing the Kidney
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Hb. Artemisiae Annuae Qing Hao 15g Clears Damp-Heat and deficiency fevers.
With Zhi Bie Jia, for the final stages of a Warm febrile disease when the Yin Fluids have already been damaged and the pathogen still lurks deep within the Yin level with night fever with chills in the morning, the fever recedes without sweating, emaciation, rapid pulse and a red tongue with little coating.
Carapax Trionycis Preparata Zhi Bie Jia 10g Nourishes Yin and anchors Yang Invigorates the Blood, softens hardness and dissipates nodules.
With Di Gu Pi and Qing Hao, for afternoon fever, night sweats, and steaming bone disorder due to Yin Deficiency.
Rz. Atractylodis Cang Zhu 15g Strongly dries Dampness, tonifies the Spleen and expels Wind-Dampness.
With Huang Bai, for skin lesions due to Wind-Dampness.
Cx. Phellodendri Huang Bai 6g Drains Kidney Fire, drains Fire and relieves Fire toxicity.
With Cang Zhu, eliminates Dampness and clears Heat for Damp-Heat sores.
Sm Phaseoli Chi Xiao Dou 15g Promotes urination, reduces edema, clears Damp Heat, disperses Blood Stasis and reduces swelling and Fire toxicity.
With Dang Gui, resolves toxicity, disperses Blood and expels pus, for Lung or Large Intestine abscess.
Rx. Astragali Huang Qi 30g Tonifies Qi and Blood, promotes urination, reduces edema, promotes the discharge of pus, generates flesh and expels toxins.
Rx. Stephaniae Tetrandrae Han Fang Ji 10g Promotes urination, reduces edema especially in the lower body, expels Wind-Dampness and alleviates pain.
With Huang Qi, for Wind-Dampness with exterior Deficiency.
Cx. Lycii Di Gu Pi 10g Cools the Blood and drains Yin Deficiency Fire.
Rz. Sparganii San Leng 30g Forcefully breaks up Blood Stasis, regulates Qi, dissolves accumulations and alleviates pain.
Caulis Lonicerae Ren Dong Teng 30g Clears Heat, resolves toxicity and dispels Wind-Dampness.
Rx. Salviae Miltiorrhizae Dan Shen 15g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, clears Heat, cools the Blood and reduces abscesses.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 10g Tonifies, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood, reduces swelling, expels pus, generates flesh and alleviates pain.
With Huang Qi, for ulcerated abscesses with insufficient drainage or which drain a clear thin discharge.
Flos Lonicerae Jin Yin Hua 30g Clears Heat and resolves Fire toxicity.
With Lian Qiao, for various Heat disorders.
With Dang Gui, reduces obstruction and toxicity for swollen, painful sores and necrosis of surrounding tissue.
Fr. Forsythiae Lian Qiao 10g Clears Heat, especially in the Upper Jiao, relieves toxicity, promotes urination, reduces abscesses and dissipates clumps.
Hb. Taraxaci Pu Gong Ying 10g Reduces abscesses, dissipates nodules, clears Heat, resolves Dampness and relieves Fire toxicity.
With Jin Yin Hua and Zi Hua Di Ding, for all hot, painful, hard, and deep-rooted boils.
Hb. Violae Zi Hua Di Ding 10g Clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity and clears hot sores, internally and externally.
Indigo Naturalis Qing Dai 6g Clears Heat, resolves Fire toxicity, cools the Blood, disperses Stagnation and reduces swelling.
Fr. Mume Wu Mei 10g Astringes leakage of Lung Qi, stops sweat, stops coughs and alleviates pain.
Rx. Scrophulariae Yuan Zhi 15g Calms the Spirit, reduces abscesses and dissipates swellings.
With Pu Gong Ying, for breast abscesses, furuncles, boils and carbuncles.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Heat,
  • Relieves Fire toxicity
  • Clears Deficiency fevers
  • Nourishes Yin
  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Strongly dries Dampness
  • Softens hardness
  • Dissipates nodules and swellings
  • Reduces abscesses

SYNDROMES

  • Toxic Heat with Yin Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Progressive dysphagia
  • Vomiting after eating
  • Local redness
  • Swelling and pain
  • A sensation of Heat in the chest
  • Five sole Heat
  • Constipation
  • Dry mouth and throat
  • Maybe fever and chills
  • T: Normal
  • C: Thin white or yellow
  • P: Rapid and forceful or Thready and rapid

TREATS

  • Esophageal cancer
