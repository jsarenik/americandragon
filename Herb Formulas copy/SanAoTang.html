HERB FORMULAS

SAN AO TANG - 三拗汤 - THREE-UNBINDING DECOCTION

English: Three-Unbinding Decoction
Also Known As: Ma Huang, Licorice and Apricot Seed Combination
San Niu Tang

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Hb. Ephedrae Ma Huang 3-9g Releases the exterior, disperses Cold, moves Lung Qi and stops wheezing.
With Xing Ren, for Wind-Cold cough and wheezing.
Sm. Armeniacae Xing Ren 3-9g Stops cough and wheezing.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 3-6g Moistens the Lungs, resolves Phlegm, stops cough and moderates and harmonizes the properties of other herbs.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Spreads Lung Qi
  • Releases the Exterior

SYNDROMES

  • Externally contracted Wind Cold

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Head and body aches
  • Nasal congestion
  • Coughing
  • Wheezing
  • Copious thin, white sputum
  • Aversion to Cold
  • Hoarseness
  • Chest distention
  • Gasping
  • Anhidrosis
  • Fullness in the chest
  • Sneezing
  • Distention and pain in the head
  • T: Normal
  • C: Thin white
  • P: Superficial or Normal

TREATS

  • Common cold
  • Loss of voice
  • Asthma
  • Pertussis
  • Influenza
  • Cough
  • Gasping
  • Bronchitis
  • Arthralgia
  • Headache
  • Chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD)
  • Pneumonia
  • Hoarseness
