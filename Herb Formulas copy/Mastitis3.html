HERB FORMULAS

MASTITIS #3 (TOPICAL)

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rz. Arisaematis Tian Nan Xing   Reduces swelling and alleviates pain (topically). Do not take internally.
Rz. Pinelliae Ban Xia   Dissipates nodules, reduces Stagnation, treats sores, skin ulcerations and carbuncles and reduces swelling (topically). Do not take internally.
Bombyx Batryticatus Jiang Can   Transforms Phlegm, softens hardness, eliminates toxins and dissipates nodules.
Rx. Angelicae Dahuricae Bai Zhi   Reduces swelling, eliminates toxins and expels pus.
Spina Gleditsiae Zai Jiao Zi   Draws out toxicity, discharges pus, invigorates the Blood and reduces swelling.
With Bai Zhi, for un-ulcerated abscesses or those with difficulty discharging pus.
Rx. Aconiti Kusnezoffii Cao Wu   Disperses Cold, scours out Wind, overcomes Dampness, stops pain, reduces swellings and treats abscesses and lesions (topically). Do not take internally.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Reduces swelling
  • Alleviates pain
  • Dissipates nodules
  • Reduces Stagnation
  • Transforms Phlegm
  • Softens hardness
  • Eliminates toxins
  • Expels pus

SYNDROMES

  • Sores and abscess due to Toxic Heat

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Acute infection of the breast
  • Chills and fever
  • Tachycardia
  • The breast is red hot
  • Distended, painful, swollen breast with lumps

TREATS

  • Mastitis

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Do not take internally.
HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

NOTES

  • Grind the above ingredients and mix with onion juice and honey. Form into a flat cake and apply to the affected area.
