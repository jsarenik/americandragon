HERB FORMULAS

XIE HUANG SAN - 瀉黃散 - DRAIN THE YELLOW POWDER

English: Drain the Yellow Powder
Also Known As: Xie Pi San
Drain the Spleen Powder
Ledebouriella and Gardenia Formula
Powder for Expelling Hidden Fire of the Spleen and Stomach
Spleen Heat Expelling Powder

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Gypsum Fibrosum Shi Gao 5-30g Clears Heat in the Qi Stage, drains Fire, relieves irritability, quenches thirst and clears Blazing Stomach Fire.
Fr. Gardeniae Zhi Zi 3-10g Clears Heat, reduces Fire, eliminates irritability and resolves Dampness.
Dry-fried Rx. Saposhnikoviae Chao Fang Feng 10-120g Releases the exterior, expels External Wind, raises Yang, expels Wind-Dampness and alleviates pain.
Hb. Agastaches/ Pogostemonis Huo Xiang 5-21g Aromatically transforms Dampness, releases the Exterior, dispels Summerheat and transforms turbidity.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 6-90g Clears Heat and relieves Fire toxicity and moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Drains Latent Fire from the Spleen and Stomach

SYNDROMES

  • Latent Fire in the Spleen and Stomach

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Mouth ulcers
  • Bad breath
  • Thirst
  • Frequent hunger
  • Dry mouth and lips
  • Epistaxis
  • Tongue thrusting in children
  • Irritability
  • T: Red
  • C: Normal or Yellow
  • P: Rapid

TREATS

  • Aphthous ulcers
  • Acute gastritis with halitosis
  • Behcet's disease
  • Oral thrush
  • Hordeolum
  • Atopic dermatitis
  • Myalgic encephalomyelitis
  • Lip pain
  • Ulcerating stomatitis
  • Halitosis
  • Rosacea
  • Eczema
  • Fever
  • Epistaxis
  • Epstein-Barr virus syndrome
  • Tongue pain

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with Stomach Yin Deficiency.
  • Contraindicated for those with tongue thrusting due to congenital Qi Deficiency.
HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

NOTES

  • Higher doses of Rx. Saposhnikoviae Fang Feng and Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao, may decrease the anti-inflammatory effect of the formula.
  • This formula has shown to reduce fever, relieve inflammation, tranquilize the mind, promote diuresis and promote the secretion of gastric juice.
