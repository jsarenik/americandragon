HERB FORMULAS

COLON CANCER #2

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Flos Lonicerae Jin Yin Hua 10g Clears Heat, resolves Fire toxicity, clears Damp-Heat from the Lower Jiao and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Gan Cao, for toxic swellings and sores.
With Dang Gui, reduces obstruction and toxicity for swollen, painful sores and necrosis of surrounding tissue.
Fr. Sophorae Huai Jiao 12g Clears and directs Heat downward, cools the Blood, moistens the Intestines and stops bleeding.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Tips Dang Gui Wei 12g Invigorates the Blood.
Spina Gleditsiae Zao Jiao Zi 10g Draws out toxicity, discharges pus, invigorates the Blood, reduces swelling and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Jin Yin Hua, for the initial stages of abscesses and boils.
Sm. Persicae Tao Ren 10g Breaks up Blood Stasis, invigorates Blood circulation, moistens the Intestines, unblocks the bowels and drains abscesses.
With Hong Hua, invigorates the Blood for all types of Blood Stagnation.
With Da Huang, for severe abdominal infections.
Rx. Pulsatillae Bai Tou Weng 15g Clears Heat, alleviates dysentery, cools the Blood and resolves Fire toxicity.
Concha Arcae Wa Leng Zi 6g Invigorates the Blood, dissolves Phlegm, resolves Stagnation, softens hardness, dissipates nodules and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Sm. Vaccariae Wang Bu Liu Xing 6g Promotes the movement of Blood, reduces swelling, drains abscesses and invigorates the channels.
Sm. Cassiae Jue Ming Zi 15g Calms the Liver, anchors Liver Yang, moistens the Intestines and unblocks the bowels.
With Dang Gui, for constipation due to dry Intestines.
Rz. Coptidis Huang Lian 10g Clears Heat, drains Dampness, drains Fire, resolves Fire toxicity, stops bleeding and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Sheng Di Huang, for insomnia and delirium due to unremitting Heat injuring the Ying level.
With Wu Mei, for Internal Heat with thirst, Dryness, cough and irritability.
Fr. Aurantii Zhi Ke 10g Promotes the movement of Qi, reduces distention and pressure and resolves hardenings.
Rx. Sanguisorbae Di Yu 30g Cools the Blood, stops bleeding, clears Toxic Heat, astringes fluids and generates flesh.
With Huai Jiao, for hot, bleeding hemorrhoids or other Intestinal bleeding.
With Gan Cao, for bleeding dysentery with rectal tenesmus, mucus and lower abdominal pain.
Sm. Arecae Bing Lang 10g Regulates Qi, reduces accumulations, drains downward and unblocks the bowels Promotes urination and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Wu Mei and Gan Cao, for fasciolopsiasis.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 12g Tonifies, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood, moistens the Intestines, unblocks the bowels, reduces swelling, expels pus, generates flesh and alleviates pain.
Fr. Mume Wu Mei 10g Generates fluids, alleviates thirst, astringes the Intestines, stops diarrhea, stops bleeding and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Angelicae Dahuricae Bai Zhi 10g Expels Wind, eliminates Dampness, reduces swelling, eliminates toxins, expels pus, alleviates discharge and treats leukorrhea and diarrhea.
With Gan Cao, for Stomach ache.
With Jin Yin Hua and Dang Gui, for Early-stage sores and carbuncles.
Rx. Trichosanthis Tian Hua Fen 10g Drains Heat, generates Fluids, relieves toxicity, expels pus, reduces swelling and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Paeoniae Rubra Chi Shao 10g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, relieves pain, clears Heat, cools the Blood, clears Liver Fire, reduces swelling from sores and abscesses and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Tao Ren, for Blood Stasis.
With Da Huang, for chronic accumulation in the abdomen, blockage of urine, constipation, Qi rushing upward to the Heart, abdominal distention and rebellious damage from food and drink.
Flos Carthami Hong Hua 6g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, opens the channels, unblocks menstruation, alleviates pain and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Colla Corii Asini E Jiao 10g Nourishes the Blood, stops bleeding, nourishes and moistens Yin and moistens the Lungs and Large Intestine.
With Huang Lian, for Heat toxin clumping in the Intestines and injuring the Blood collaterals causing Blood and pus in the stools especially during pregnancy or postpartum.
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 15g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, nourishes Yin and generates fluids.
Natrii Sulfas Mang Xiao 10g Purges accumulations, guides out Stagnation, softens hardness, moistens Dryness, clears Heat, drains Fire and reduces swelling.
With Da Huang, for constipation due to Heat in the Stomach and Intestines.
With Da Huang and Gan Cao, for moderate constipation due to accumulation of Excess Heat.
Rx. et Rz. Rhei Da Huang 10g Drains Heat, purges accumulations, drains Fire, transforms Dampness, promotes urination, drains Heat from the Blood, invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, reduces Fire toxicity, eliminates Phlegm and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Huang Lian, for a feeling of fullness and distention due to Heat Accumulation.
With Dang Gui, for Blood Stasis.
With Tao Ren and Hong Hua, for symptoms associated with Blood Stasis.
With Mang Xiao, for abscesses, swellings and toxic sores and eliminates obstruction in the Intestines for severe constipation with passage of liquid but no solid stool.
With Sheng Di Huang, for parched, dry stools with damaged Yin
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 3g Moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs, guides the herbs to all twelve channels and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Breaks up Stasis
  • Clears Heat
  • Cools the Blood
  • Resolves toxicity
  • Inhibits the growth of cancer cells

SYNDROMES

  • Blood Stagnation with Toxic Heat

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Chronic diarrhea with a feeling of incomplete evacuation or tenesmus
  • Purple or black stools which are sticky or tarry
  • Fixed or localized sharp or stabbing abdominal pain (usually in the lower left quadrant) which is worse with pressure
  • A dark complexion
  • Dark rings around the eyes
  • Purple nails
  • Spider naevi or vesicular abnormalities over the abdomen, face and legs (inner knee and ankle)
  • High fever may be worse at night
  • Thirst
  • Insomnia
  • Major mental problems
  • Dramatic change of personality
  • Confusion of mind
  • Delirium
  • Speech difficulty
  • Aphasia
  • Poor memory
  • Dizziness
  • Maybe fainting
  • Maybe coma
  • Excessive skin rashes all over body
  • Feverish extremities
  • Dark yellow urine
  • Hemorrhage
    Hematemesis
    Hemoptysis
    Epistaxis
    Hemafecia
    Hematuria
  • T:  Deep red or Deep red and dry or Purple red or Purple or Brown or Purple stasis spots, maybe dark, distended sublingual veins
  • C: None
  • P:  Rapid or Thready and rapid or Thready and feeble or Rapid and choppy

TREATS

  • Colon cancer
