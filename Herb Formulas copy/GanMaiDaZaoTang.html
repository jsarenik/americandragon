HERB FORMULAS

GAN MAI DA ZAO TANG - 甘麥大棗湯 - LICORICE, WHEAT AND JUJUBE DECOCTION

English: Licorice, Wheat and Jujube Decoction
Also Known As: Licorice and Jujube Combination

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx, Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 6-20g Tonifies the Spleen and augments Qi, clears Heat and relieves Fire toxicity, moderates spasms and alleviates pain.
With Da Zao and Xiao Mai, for restless organ syndrome.
Fr. Tritici Levis Xiao Mai 9-60g Nourishes Heart Qi and eliminate irritability to treat irritability, restlessness and insomnia as in restless organ disorder.
With Gan Cao and Da Zao, for emotional disorders, disorientation, emotional instability, and irritability due to mild Deficiency of the Spleen Qi and Heart Blood.
(Sm. Tritici Levis) (Fu Xiao Mai) (9-60g) (Substitutes for Xiao Mai in some sources.)
Fr. Jujube Da Zao 10-30g Tonifies the Spleen and Stomach, augments Qi, nourishes the Blood and calms the Spirit.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Nourishes the Heart
  • Calms the Shen
  • Harmonizes the Middle Jiao
  • Relieves acute conditions
  • Relaxes tension
  • Tonifies Spleen Qi

SYNDROMES

  • Heart Blood Deficiency with Liver Qi Stagnation
  • Restless Organ Syndrome
  • Dry Organ disorder

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Absent-mindedness
  • Frequent feelings of extreme sadness with an urge to cry
  • Inability to control one's emotions
  • Depression
  • Restless sleep
  • May be night sweats
  • Lack of mental focus
  • Tight abdominal muscles
  • Irritability
  • Abnormal speech or behavior
  • Disorientation
  • Frequent yawning
  • Sadness
  • Overly sensitive or self-conscious
  • Insomnia
  • T: Red or pale red
  • C: Scanty
  • P: Thready and rapid
