HERB FORMULAS

GUA LOU XIE BAI BAN XIA TANG - 瓜蒌薤白半夏汤 - TRICHOSANTHIS FRUIT, CHINESE GARLIC AND PINELLIA DECOCTION

English: Trichosanthis Fruit, Chinese Garlic and Pinellia Decoction
Also Known As: Trichosanthis Fruit, Chinese Chive and Pinellia Decoction
Trichosanthis Fruit, Chinese Chive and Pinellia Combination
Trichosanthis, Alium and Pinellia Decoction

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Fr. Trichosanthis Gua Lou 12-30g Clears Lung Heat, transforms Hot Phlegm and regulates Qi (unbinds the chest).
With Zhi Ban Xia, for focal distention in the chest and diaphragm due to clumping of Phlegm and Heat leading to Qi Stagnation and chest Bi due to clumping of sticky, thick and turbid Phlegm.
With Xie Bai and Zhi Ban Xia, for painful obstruction of the chest (Chest Bi) due to Cold turbidity stagnating in the Upper Jiao.
Bul. Allii Macrostemi Xie Bai 9-15g Unblocks the Yang Qi, disperses turbid Phlegm, alleviates pain, disperses hardenings, descends Qi, reduces Stagnation and disperses Cold Phlegm.
With Gua Lou, for chest Bi with profuse Phlegm.
Rz. Pinelliae Preparatum Zhi Ban Xia 9-12g Dries Dampness, reduces Stagnation, descends Rebellious Qi and transforms Phlegm.
White wine Bai Jiu 30-60-ml  

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Unblocks the Yang
  • Disperses Stagnation
  • Dispels Phlegm
  • Relieves chest fullness

SYNDROMES

  • Chest Bi due to Phlegm Stagnation where the pain is so severe that the patient cannot lie down.

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Chest fullness and pain
  • Dyspnea
  • Shortness of breath
  • Severe pain radiating to the back
  • Severe wheezing
  • Chest oppression
  • Severe cough
  • Disturbed sleep
  • T: Normal
  • C: White and greasy
  • P: Deep and wiry or Tight

TREATS

  • Coronary artery disease
  • Breast lumps
  • Fibrocystic breasts
  • Hyperlipoproteinemia
  • Upper back pain
  • Coronary Heart disease
  • Cough
  • Palpitations
  • Dyspnea
