HERB FORMULAS

XUAN MIAO SAN - 玄妙散 - WONDERFUL SCROPHULARIA POWDER

English: Wonderful Scrophularia Powder
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Scrophulariae Xuan Shen 9g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, nourishes Yin, transforms Phlegm-Heat, drains Fire and relieves toxicity.
Rx. Salviae Miltiorrhizae Dan Shen 9g Nourishes and invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, clears Heat, soothes irritability, the Blood and calms the Spirit.
Rx. Adenophorae Nan Sha Shen 12g Nourishes Yin, moistens the Lungs, dispels Phlegm and stops cough.
Poria Fu Ling 12g Quiets the Heart, calms the Spirit, soothes the nerves, strengthens the Spleen and transforms Phlegm.
Sm. Platycladi Bai Zi Ren 9g Nourishes the Heart, calms the Spirit and astringes Yin Deficiency sweat.
Rx. Ophiopogonis Mai Men Dong 9g Moistens the Lungs, nourishes Yin, stops cough, clears the Heart and eliminates irritability.
With Xuan Shen and Jie Geng, for an itchy, dry throat, thirst and a nonproductive cough due to Lung Yin Deficiency.
Rx. Platycodi Jie Geng 9g Opens the Lungs, spreads Lung Qi, expels Phlegm, benefits the throat and raises Lung Qi, directing the effects of other herbs to the upper body.
Bul. Fritillariae Cirrhosae Chuan Bei Mu 9g Clears Heat, transforms Phlegm, stops cough and nourishes and moistens the Lungs.
With Xing Ren, for a nonproductive cough from Lung Deficiency disorders.
Sm. Armeniacae Xing Ren 9g Stops cough and calms wheezing.
With Mai Men Dong, for a dry cough due to Dryness and Heat injuring the Lungs.
Flos Albizziae He Huan Hua 9g Relieves Stagnant Liver Qi, promotes the flow of Qi in the collaterals, harmonizes the Stomach and calms the Shen.
Hb. Lophatheri Dan Zhu Ye 3g Clears Heat and eliminates irritability.
Medulla Junci Deng Xin Cao 3g Clears Lung Heat, clears Heat from the Heart channel by directing Fire downward and calms the Spirit.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Heat
  • Nourishes Yin
  • Moistens the Lungs
  • Nourishes the Heart
  • Calms the Shen
  • Stops cough

SYNDROMES

  • Cough due to Lung and Heart Yin Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Severe, dry hacking cough
  • No sputum expectorated
  • Small amounts of difficult to expectorate sputum
  • Blood tinged sputum (when Yin Deficiency Fire is present)
  • Afternoon fever
  • Night sweats
  • Malar flush (especially in afternoon)
  • Five sole Heat
  • Dry mouth and throat
  • Itching in throat
  • Hoarse voice, low voice or loss of voice
  • Emaciation
  • Palpitations
  • Irritability
  • Easily startled
  • Insomnia
  • Much dreaming
  • Poor memory
  • Low grade fever
  • Restlessness
  • Thirst
  • T: Red and dry
  • C: Little or None
  • P: Thready and rapid

TREATS

  • Cough
  • Chronic bronchitis
  • Bronchiectasis
  • Asthma
  • Pulmonary tuberculosis
  • Chronic tonsillitis
  • Chronic pharyngitis
  • Silicosis
  • Emphysema
  • COPD
