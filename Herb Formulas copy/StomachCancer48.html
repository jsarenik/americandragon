HERB FORMULAS

STOMACH CANCER #48

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Haematitum Dai Zhe Shi 30g Calms the Liver, anchors Floating Yang, clears Liver Fire and strongly descends rebellious Qi.
With Zhi Ban Xia, Xuan Fu Hua and Dang Shen, for vomiting, hiccup, and belching due to rebellious Qi
Flos Inulae Xuan Fu Hua 10g Expels Phlegm, removes pathogenic water and calms rebellious Stomach Qi.
With Fu Hai Shi, for cough with viscous difficult-to-expectorate sputum and sensations of tightness in the chest due to Phlegm-Heat.
With Qing Shui Zhi Ban Xia, for vomiting, cough, wheezing and epigastric distention due to congested fluids.
With Dai Zhe Shi, Dang Shen and Qing Shui Zhi Ban Xia, for vomiting, hiccup or non-stop belching due to Spleen and Stomach Deficiency Cold along with accumulation of turbid Phlegm.
Scolopendra Wu Gong 8pcs Attacks and relieves Fire toxins, relieves abdominal masses, dissipates nodules and
stops pain.
Hirudo Shui Zhi 6g Breaks up and drives out Blood Stasis and reduces fixed masses.
Concha Ostreae Mu Li 30g Calms the Liver, benefits Yin, anchors Floating Yang, softens hardness, dissipates nodules, absorbs acidity and alleviates pain.
Os Costaziae Fu Hai Shi 15g Transforms Phlegm, softens hardness and dissipates Phlegm nodules.
Fr. Hordei Germinatus Mai Ya 10g Reduces Food Stagnation, improves digestion, strengthens the Stomach and facilitates the smooth flow of Liver Qi.
Rx. Codonopsis Dang Shen 25g Tonifies the Middle Jiao and augments Qi.
Endothelium Corneum Gigeriae Galli Ji Nei Jin 15g Strongly reduces Food Stagnation, improves the Spleen’s transportive function, transforms hardness and dissolves stones.
Fr. Perillae Zi Su Zi 9g Descends Qi, dissolves Phlegm, moistens the Intestines and unblocks the stool.
Caulis Bambusae in Taeniam Zhu Ru 15g Clears Heat, transforms Hot Phlegm and stops vomiting.
With Lu Gen, for irritability, thirst, and vomiting due to depletion of fluids caused by Heat.
Rz. Phragmitis Lu Gen 30g Clears Stomach Heat, regulates Stomach Qi, relieves thirst and stops vomiting
Clear Water Rz. Pinelliae Preparatum Qing Shui Ban Xia 12g Dries Dampness, transforms Phlegm, descends Rebellious Qi, stops vomiting, dissipates nodules, reduces Stagnation, treats sores, skin ulcerations and carbuncles and reduces swelling.
With Dang Shen, for vomiting due to Stomach Deficiency.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Dries Dampness
  • Transforms Phlegm
  • Calms the Liver
  • Descends rebellious Qi
  • Breaks up and drives out Blood Stasis
  • Reduces fixed masses
  • Stops vomiting
  • Dissipates nodules

SYNDROMES

  • Qi and Phlegm Stagnation

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Loss of appetite, especially for meat
  • Dull pain
  • Difficulty swallowing
  • Vomiting of saliva and undigested food
  • A rancid taste in the mouth
  • Epigastric distention and pain (worse when emotionally upset)
  • Distention and pain in the sides or flanks
  • Weakness
  • Weight loss
  • Distention and pain in the Stomach
  • Fetid belching or hiccupping
  • Acid regurgitation
  • Difficulty swallowing
  • Nausea
  • Vomiting
  • Suffocating sensation in the chest
  • Tendency to sigh
  • Melancholy
  • Depression
  • Irritability
  • Inappropriate anger
  • Irregular menses
  • Dysmenorrhea
  • Breast distention and pain before and during menses
  • T: Pink
  • C: Thick and white
  • P: Wiry or Thready and wiry or Deep and thready or Slippery and wiry

TREATS

  • Stomach cancer
  • Gastric cancer
