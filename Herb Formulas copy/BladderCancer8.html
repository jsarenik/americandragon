HERB FORMULAS

BLADDER CANCER #8 (TOPICAL)

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Hb. Violae Zi Hua Di Ding 120g Clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity and clears hot sores, internally and externally.
Rx. Asteris Zi Wan 90g Relieves coughs and expels Phlegm.
Hb. Dendrobii Shi Hu 90g Enriches Kidney Yin, clears Heat, generates fluids and augments Jing.
Fol. Camelliae Sinensis Lu Cha 30g Harmonizes the Stomach, descends Rebellious Stomach Qi and relieves diarrhea.
Flos Lonicerae Jin Yin Hua 30g Clears Heat, resolves Fire toxicity, clears Damp-Heat from the Lower Jiao and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Heat
  • Relieves toxicity
  • Expels Phlegm
  • Nourishes Yin

SYNDROMES

  • Toxic Heat with Phlegm and Yin Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Episodic hematuria with fresh red Blood
  • The urine may be concentrated
  • Dizziness
  • Tinnitus or deafness
  • Poor memory
  • Aching, soreness or weakness of the low back
  • Heel pain
  • Spermatorrhea or
  • Premature ejaculation
  • Aching in the bones
  • Aching in the leg and heel
  • Dry mouth
  • Irritability
  • Restlessness
  • Thirst
  • Nightsweats
  • Five sole Heat
  • Malar flush
  • Facial flushing
  • Low grade fever in the afternoon
  • Dry throat, maybe recurrent sore throat
  • Bone steaming fever
  • Dry stools and/or
  • Constipation
  • Heightened libido
  • If Heart Fire also Flares:
    Insomnia
    Frequent dreams
    Palpitations
  • If Liver Yang also Rises:
    Dizziness
    Blurred vision
  • T: Red
  • C: Little or None
  • P: Thready and rapid

TREATS

  • Bladder cancer

NOTES
