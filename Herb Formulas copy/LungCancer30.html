HERB FORMULAS

LUNG CANCER #30

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Asparagi Tian Men Dong 30g Nourishes Kidney Yin, clears Lung Heat, sedates Fire, moistens the Lungs, generates fluids, resolves Phlegm and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Mai Men Dong, for a dry cough, thirst and other symptoms due to Yin Deficiency and injured fluids.
Rx. Ophiopogonis Mai Men Dong 20g Moistens the Lungs, nourishes Yin and stops coughing.
With Xuan Shen, Jie Geng and Gan Cao, for an itchy, dry throat, thirst and a nonproductive cough due to Lung Yin Deficiency.
With Nan Sha Shen, for a dry cough with little Phlegm and a parched throat due to Lung Dryness or Heat injuring the Lung Yin.
Rx. Scrophulariae Xuan Shen 20g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, nourishes Yin, softens hardness, dissipates nodules, drains Fire and relieves toxicity.
Rx. Adenophorae Nan Sha Shen 30g Nourishes Lung Yin and clears Lung Heat.
Flos Lonicerae Jin Yin Hua 10g Clears Heat and resolves Fire toxicity and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Gan Cao, for toxic swellings and sores.
With Gan Cao and Xuan Shen, for sloughing ulcers from Heat toxicity.
Fr. Gardeniae Zhi Zi 12g Clears Heat, reduces Fire, eliminates irritability, cools the Blood, relieves toxicity, reduces swelling, invigorates the Blood and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Trichosanthis Tian Hua Fen 12g Drains Heat, generates Fluids, clears and drains Lung Heat, transforms Phlegm, moistens Lung Dryness, relieves toxicity, expels pus, reduces swelling and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Bul. Fritillariae Thunbergii Zhe Bei Mu 12g Clears Heat, transforms Phlegm, stops coughing, dissipates nodules and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Sm. Armeniacae Xing Ren 6g Stops coughing, calms wheezing and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Mai Men Dong, for a dry cough due to Dryness and Heat injuring the Lungs.
Rx. Platycodi Jie Geng 15g Opens the Lungs, spreads Lung Qi, expels Phlegm, expels pus and directs the effects of other herbs to the upper body.
With Gan Cao, for Lung abscess with expectoration of pus and pain in the chest and flanks.
Cx. Lycii Di Gu Pi 12g Drains Yin Deficiency Fire, Clears and drains Heat in the Lung and cools the Blood.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 3g Moistens the Lungs, resolves Phlegm, stops coughing, moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs, guides the herbs to all twelve channels and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Lung Heat
  • Resolves toxicity
  • Transforms Phlegm-Heat
  • Nourishes Lung Yin
  • Moistens the Lungs
  • Expels Phlegm
  • Inhibits the growth of cancer cells

SYNDROMES

  • Perverse Dryness Attacks the Lungs with Heat

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Dry cough with little or no sputum
  • Sputum is difficult to expectorate
  • Maybe hemoptysis
  • Cough may cause chest pain
  • Dry or sore throat
  • Hoarse voice
  • Dry lips and mouth
  • Thirst
  • Dryness in nose
  • Epistaxis
  • Chills and fever
  • Headache
  • Anhidrosis
  • Dry skin and hair
  • Constipation
  • T: Red tip
  • C: Thin, yellow and dry
  • P: Superficial, maybe rapid

TREATS

  • Lung cancer
