HERB FORMULAS

XIAO XIAN XIONG TANG - 小陷胸湯 - MINOR DECOCTION (FOR PATHOGENS) STUCK IN THE CHEST

English: Minor Decoction (for Pathogens) Stuck in the Chest
Also Known As: Minor Sinking into the Chest Decoction
Minor Trichosanthis Combination

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Fr. Trichosanthis Gua Lou 20-30g Clears Lung Heat, transforms Hot Phlegm, regulates Qi (unbinds the chest), reduces abscesses, dissipates nodules and eliminates pus.
With Huang Lian and Jiang Ban Xia, for Phlegm-Heat accumulating in the chest and epigastrium that is painful when pressed with coughing of thick, yellow sputum (Xiong Bi due to Phlegm and Heat Accumulation Beneath the Heart).
Rz. Coptidis Huang Lian 3-6g Clears Heat, drains Dampness, drains Fire and resolves Fire toxicity.
Ginger-fried Rz. Pinelliae Preparatum Jiang Ban Xia 9-12g Dries Dampness, transforms Phlegm, descends Qi and alleviates nausea.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Heat
  • Transforms Phlegm
  • Expands the chest
  • Dissipates Stagnation

SYNDROMES

  • Phlegm and Heat Accumulation Beneath the Heart
  • Phlegm-Heat Stagnation in the chest

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Focal distention in the chest and epigastrium with or without nodules which is painful when pressed
  • Coughing up yellow, viscous sputum
  • Constipation
  • A bitter taste in the mouth
  • T: Normal
  • C: Yellow and greasy
  • P: Slippery and floating or Slippery and rapid

CONTRAINDICATIONS
