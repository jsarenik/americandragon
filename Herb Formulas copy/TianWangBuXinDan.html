HERB FORMULAS

TIAN WANG BU XIN DAN - 天王補心丹 - EMPEROR OF HEAVEN'S SPECIAL PILL TO TONIFY THE HEART

English: Emperor of Heaven's Special Pill to Tonify the Heart
Also Known As: Ginseng and Zizyphus Formula
Emperor's Tea
The King's Mind Easing Tonic Pills
Celestial Emperor’s Heart Supplementing Elixir
The Heavenly King's Tonic Pills

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 1.3-30g Cools Heart Fire, clears Heat, cools the Blood and enriches Yin and Blood.
With Xuan Shen, for dry throat, irritability, a red tongue and a thready rapid pulse due to Yin Xu with Fire Flaring.
Rx. Ginseng Ren Shen 1-9g Tonifies Heart Qi and calms the Shen.
 With Fu Ling, assists Heart Qi.
With Mai Men Dong and Wu Wei Zi, for Qi and Yin Xu.
(Rx. Codonopsis) (Dang Shen) (3-27g) (Substitute for Ren Shen.)
Rx. Asparagi Tian Men Dong 1.3-15g Enriches the Yin and generates Fluids.
With Mai Men Dong, for Yin Xu with injured Fluids.
With Mai Men Dong and Xuan Shen, clears Xu Heat and nourishes Yin.
Rx. Ophiopogonis Mai Men Dong 1.3-15g Enriches the Yin and clears the Heart.
With Ren Shen and Wu Wei Zi, for exhaustion, increased Heart rate, profuse perspiration and wheezing due to severe Heart and Lung Xu.
Rx. Scrophulariae Xuan Shen 1-20g Enriches Yin and clears Heat.
Rx. Salviae Miltiorrhizae Dan Shen 1.3-20g Invigorates, cools and nourishes the Blood and clears Heat.
With Dang Gui, nourishes Blood to nourish the Heart without causing Stasis.
With Suan Zao Ren and Bai Zi Ren, for Heart Blood Xu with palpitations and insomnia.
Poria Fu Ling 1.3-15g Strengthens the Spleen and pacifies the Heart.
With Suan Zao Ren, Wu Wei Zi and Yuan Zhi, for palpitations and insomnia.
(Poria Pararadicis) (Fu Shen) (1.5-12g) (May substitute for Fu Ling.)
Rx. Polygalae Yuan Zhi 1.3-9g Calms the Shen, sedates the Heart and opens the channel between the Heart and Kidneys.
With Bai Zi Ren, calms the Shen.
With Fu Shen and Suan Zao Ren, for irritability, disorientation, palpitations, anxiety and insomnia due to Heart Blood Xu.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 1.3-9g Nourishes and invigorates Blood.
Fr. Schisandrae Wu Wei Zi 1.3-12g Tonifies Qi, tonifies Kidney Jing, nourishes the Heart, calms the Shen, generates Fluids and astringes excessive perspiration.
With Suan Zao Ren, astringes Heart Qi.
With Suan Zao Ren and Sheng Di Huang, for Heart Qi Xu with insomnia, irritability and forgetfulness.
Sm. Platycladi Bai Zi Ren 1.3-12g Nourishes the Heart and Kidneys, calms the Shen and astringes Yin Xu perspiration.
With Yuan Zhi and Suan Zao Ren, for insomnia, and palpitations due to Heart Blood Xu.
Sm. Zizyphi Spinosae Suan Zao Ren 1.3-12g Nourishes Heart Yin, augments Liver Blood, calms the Shen and astringes sweat.
With Wu Wei Zi, prevents leakage of Heart Qi.
Rx. Platycodi Jie Geng 1.3-9g Directs actions of the formula upwards.
Cinnabaris Zhu Sha 1-3g Sedates the Heart and anchors and calms the Shen.
With Suan Zao Ren, Bai Zi Ren and Dang Gui, for insomnia or palpitations with anxiety due to Blood Xu.
(Rz. Coptidis) (Huang Lian) (1.3g) (Clears Heart Fire - used in some versions.)
(Rz. Acori Tatarinowii) (Shi Chang Pu) (1.3g) (Opens Orifices and calms the Shen - used in some versions.)

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Enriches the Yin
  • Nourishes the Blood
  • Tonifies the Heart and Kidneys
  • Calms the Shen
  • Clears Heat

SYNDROMES

  • Heart Yin Xu
  • Heart and Kidney Yin Xu (with Fire Flaring)
  • Heart and Kidney Yin Xu with Heart Blood Xu

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with Spleen and Stomach weakness with loose stools.
  • Contraindicated for those with Phlegm-Damp Stagnation.
  • Do not take long term.
  • While taking this formula, avoid cilantro, garlic, radishes seafood and liquor.
HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

NOTES

  • Recommended for people who do a lot of reading.
  • Good for people who have taken tranquilizers and sleeping pills for a long time and they have lost their effect.
