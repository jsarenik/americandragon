HERB FORMULAS

QING LUO YIN - 清絡飲 - CLEAR THE COLLATERALS DRINK

English: Clear the Collaterals Drink
Also Known As: Clear the Collaterals Decoction

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Flos Lonicerae Recens Xian Jin Yin Hua 6g Cools Summerheat while remaining gentle on the Stomach.
Flos Lablab Recens Xian Bian Dou Hua 6g Relieves Summerheat, removes Dampness and controls bleeding.
Exocarpium Citrulli Xi Gua Cui Yi 6g Clears Summerheat, generates Fluids, promotes urination and cools Lung and Stomach Heat.
With He Ye, Bian Dou Hua and Jin Yin Hua, for Exterior conditions in the summer.
Per. Luffae Si Gua Luo 6g Unblocks the channels and collaterals, dispels Wind and expels Phlegm.
Fol. Nelumbinis Recens Xian He Ye 6g Relieves Summerheat and stops bleeding from Heat in the Blood.
Hb. Lophatheri Recens Xian Dan Zhu Ye 6g Promotes urination and clears Damp-Heat.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Resolves Summerheat
  • Clears the Lungs
  • Clears Heat

SYNDROMES

  • Summerheat injuring the Qi level of the Lung channel
  • Summerheat, unresolved by sweating

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Fever
  • Mild thirst
  • Unclear head
  • Blurred vision
  • Light-headedness
  • Slight distention of the head
  • T: Pink or Slightly red
  • C: Thin and white
  • P: Maybe soft or Soft and rapid

TREATS

  • Hyperthermia
  • Heat stroke
  • Rheumatic fever
  • Common cold
  • Influenza
  • Fever

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • This formula is insufficient for severe cases of Summerheat.
HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

NOTES

  • This formula may be taken in lower doses as a tea throughout the summer to prevent Summerheat.

MODIFICATIONS

+ Sm. Armeniacae Xing Ren + Sm. Coicis Yi Yi Ren

For fever:
