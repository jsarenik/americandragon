HERB FORMULAS

CEREBRAL THROMBOSIS #1

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Astragali Huang Qi 30g Tonifies Qi and Blood, strengthens the Spleen and relieves numbness and pain.
Rx. Salviae Miltiorrhizae Dan Shen 30g Activates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, clears Heat, soothes irritability, nourishes the Blood and calms the Spirit.
With Dang Gui, Hong Hua and Chuan Xiong, for external or traumatic injury with pain and bruising.
Haematitum Dai Zhe Shi 30g Calms the Liver, anchors Floating Yang and clears Liver Fire.
With Long Gu, Mu Li and Niu Xi, for dizziness, vertigo, headache and tinnitus due to Liver Yang Rising.
Fossilia Ossis Mastodi Long Gu 15g Calms the Liver and anchors and preserves Floating Yang.
Concha Ostreae Mu Li 15g Calms the Liver, benefits Yin and anchors Floating Yang.
Rx. Achyranthis Bidentatae Niu Xi 12g Activates the Blood, expels Blood Stasis, nourishes Liver and Kidney Yin, strengthens the sinews and bones and benefits the joints.
With Dai Zhe Shi and Mu Li, for dizziness and vertigo from Liver Yang Rising due to Liver and Kidney Yin Deficiency.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 9g Tonifies, activates and harmonizes the Blood.
With Huang Qi, for Qi and Blood Deficiency.
With Tao Ren and Hong Hua, for symptoms associated with Blood Stasis.
With Bai Shao, regulates and harmonizes Qi and Blood.
Rx. Paeoniae Rubra Chi Shao 9g Activates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, relieves pain, Clears Heat, cools the Blood and Clears Liver Fire.
With Dang Gui and Tao Ren, for Blood Stasis.
With Huang Qi and Di Long, for hemiplegia with Deficiency.
Rz. Chuanxiong Chuan Xiong 9g Activates the Blood, promotes the movement of Qi, expels Wind and alleviates pain.
With Dang Gui, harmonizes, nourishes and activates the Blood and disperses Blood Stasis.
With Dang Gui, Hong Hua and Tao Ren, for pain due to trauma and headache from Blood Stagnation.
With Huang Qi and Di Long, for numbness of the limbs.
With Huang Qi, Di Long, Hong Hua and Dang Gui, for numbness of the limbs and paralysis.
Pheretima Di Long 9g Drains Heat, settles convulsions, extinguishes Wind, stops spasms, unblocks and promotes movement in the channels and collaterals.
With Dang Gui, Huang Qi Chuan Xiong and Hong Hua, for hemiplegia or hemiparesis following Wind-Stroke.
Rx. Asparagi Tian Men Dong 9g Nourishes Kidney Yin, clears Lung Heat and sedates Fire.
Sm. Persicae Tao Ren 6g Breaks up Blood Stasis and activates Blood circulation.
With Dang Gui, Chi Shao and Hong Hua, for trauma with pain.
Flos Carthami Hong Hua 6g Activates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, opens the channels and alleviates pain.
With Tao Ren, activates the Blood for all types of Blood Stagnation.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Sedates the Liver
  • Extinguishes Wind
  • Nourishes Yin
  • Anchors Yang
  • Tonifies Qi
  • Activates the Blood
  • Unblocks the channels
  • Promotes movement in the limbs

SYNDROMES

  • Liver Wind Stirring Internally: Liver Yang (Liver Fire) Transforms into Wind with Qi and Yang Deficiency and Blood Stasis Obstructing the Channels

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Dizziness
  • Vertigo
  • A feeling of distention in the eyes
  • Tinnitus
  • Feverish sensation in the head
  • Headache
  • Irritability
  • Flushed face as if intoxicated
  • Swelling of the eyes
  • Frequent belching
  • Progressive motor dysfunction occurring over hours to days
  • Development of facial asymmetry
  • Sudden loss of consciousness
  • Spasms
  • Heat and pain of the brain
  • Mental confusion with moments of lucidity
  • Inability to fully recover after loss of consciousness
  • Hiccups
  • Belching
  • Irritable Heat in the Heart
  • Tremors
  • Limited mobility of the limbs
  • Hemiplegia
  • Paralysis
  • Atrophy of the lower limbs
  • Facial paralysis
  • Slurred speech
  • Drooling
  • Dry stools
  • Frequent urination
  • Urinary incontinence
  • Deviation of the eyes and mouth
  • Excess salivation
  • Cold extremities
  • Asthenia
  • T: Red
  • C: Thin and white
  • P: Wiry, Excess and long or Moderate

TREATS

  • Cerebral thrombosis
