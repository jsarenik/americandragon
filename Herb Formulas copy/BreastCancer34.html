HERB FORMULAS

BREAST CANCER #34

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Sm. Persicae Tao Ren 9g Breaks up Blood Stasis, invigorates Blood circulation and drains abscesses.
Flos Carthami Hong Hua 9g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, opens the channels, unblocks menstruation, alleviates pain and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Tao Ren, for for all types of Blood Stagnation.
Rx. Paeoniae Alba Bai Shao 12g Nourishes the Blood, regulates menstruation, astringes Yin, softens the Liver and relieves pain.
With Gan Cao, regulates the relationship between the Liver and Spleen.
Rx. Salviae Miltiorrhizae Dan Shen 15g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, clears Heat, cools the Blood, reduces abscesses and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Ru Xiang, for Blood Stasis causing swelling and pain.
Flos Lonicerae Jin Yin Hua 15g Clears Heat, resolves Fire toxicity and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Gan Cao, for Toxic swellings and sores.
With Huang Qin, for Suppurating boils that have not ulcerated, or have ulcerated but are not discharging pus.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 6g Clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity, moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs and guides the herbs to all twelve channels.
With Jin Yin Hua, Huang Qin, Pu Gong Ying and Zi Hua Di Ding, for Yang sores marked by redness, swelling and pain.
Hb. Taraxaci Pu Gong Ying 30g Reduces abscesses, dissipates nodules, clears Heat, resolves Dampness, relieves Fire toxicity, clears Blood Heat and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Jin Yin Hua and Zi Hua Di Ding, for All hot, painful, hard, and deep-rooted boils.
Hb. Violae Zi Hua Di Ding 30g Clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity and clears hot sores.
With Pu Gong Ying, for breast abscesses, furuncles, boils and carbuncles and Damp-Heat jaundice.
Rz. Paradis Chong Lou 10g Drains Heat, relieves Fire toxicity, reduces swelling and inflammation, stops pain and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Olibanum Ru Xiang 30g Invigorates the Blood, promotes the movement of Qi, reduces swelling, generates flesh and alleviates pain.
With Mo Yao, for chronic, ulcerated and non-healing sores.
Myrrh Mo Yao 3g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, alleviates pain and reduces swelling.
With Hong Hua, for Blood Stasis.
With Ru Xiang, for swollen, painful sores and sores that are hard but not painful.
Rx. Sophorae Flavescentis Ku Shen 10g Clears Heat, dries Dampness and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Scutellariae Huang Qin 30g Clears Heat, drains Dampness, drains Fire, detoxifies, cools the Blood and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Heat
  • Cools the Blood
  • Resolves toxicity
  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Breaks up Stasis
  • Inhibits the growth of cancer cells

SYNDROMES

  • Toxic Heat with Blood Stagnation

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Tumor develops quickly
  • Fluid inside the breast with a lump inside it
  • Long-standing illness
  • Pain
  • Heartburn
  • Redness
  • Ulceration
  • Discharge of yellow or bloody, foul-smelling fluid
  • Facial flushing
  • Ear reddening
  • Fatigue
  • Fever
  • Anxiety
  • Dry mouth
  • Thirst
  • Short, red urine
  • Bitter taste
  • Red eyes
  • Insomnia
  • T: Dark red with echymotic spots
  • C: White or Yellow and greasy or Thick, sticky, dry and yellow
  • P: Wiry or Rolling and rapid or Wiry, overflowing and rapid

TREATS

  • Breast cancer
