HERB FORMULAS

NU KE BAI ZI REN WAN - 女科柏子仁囊 - ARBORVITAE SEED PILLS FOR WOMEN'S DISORDERS

English: Arborvitae Seed Pills for Women's Disorders
Also Known As: Platycladus Seed Pill for Females
Bai Zi Ren Wan
Platycladus Seed Pill
Biota and Cyathula Formula

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Sm. Platycladi Bai Zi Ren 9-16g Nourishes the Heart calms the Spirit and Astringes Yin Deficiency sweat.
Rx. Rehmanniae Preparata Shu Di Huang 15-90g Nourishes the Blood, nourishes Liver and Kidney Yin, nourishes Jing and Blood and fills the Marrow.
Rx. Dipsaci Xu Duan 12-60g Tonifies the Liver and Kidneys, stops uterine bleeding, promotes the movement of Blood and alleviates pain.
Rx. Achyranthis Bidentatae Niu Xi 9-16g Activates the Blood, expels Blood Stasis, nourishes the Liver and Kidney Yin and descends Blood and Fire.
Hb. Selanginellae Tamariscinae Juan Bai 9-16g Stops bleeding, disperses Wind, activates the Blood circulation and regulates menstruation.
Hb. Lycopi Ze Lan 12-60g Activates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis and regulates menstruation.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Tonifies the Blood
  • Nourishes Yin
  • Calms the Shen
  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Unblocks menstruation

SYNDROMES

  • Irregular menstruation and amenorrhea due to Blood and Yin Deficiency
  • Irregular menstruation and amenorrhea due to Blood and Yin Deficiency with Blood Stasis

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Amenorrhea of gradual onset
  • Soreness of the lower back
  • Abdominal pain
  • Emaciation
  • Achy and uncomfortable flesh and bones of the hands and feet
  • Anxiety
  • Dizziness
  • Light headedness
  • Tidal fever
  • General disquietude
  • Gradually worsening afternoon fevers
  • T: Red
  • C: Scanty
  • P: Thready and rapid

TREATS

  • Menstrual disorders
  • Irregular menstruation
  • Anxiety
  • Dizziness
  • Amenorrhea
  • Tidal fever
  • Low back pain
