HERB FORMULAS

BREAST CANCER #45

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Hb. Aristolochiae Mollissimae Xun Gu Feng 24g Dispels Wind-Dampness, unblocks the channels and collaterals, stops digestive pain and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Astragali Huang Qi 24g Tonifies Qi and Blood, promotes the discharge of pus, generates flesh, expels toxins and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Asparagi Tian Men Dong 15g Nourishes Kidney Yin, moistens the Lungs, nourishes the Kidneys, generates Fluids, resolves Phlegm and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Mai Men Dong, for a dry cough, thirst and other symptoms due to Yin Deficiency and injured fluids.
Rx. Ophiopogonis Mai Men Dong 15g Moistens the Lungs, nourishes Stomach Yin, stops coughing and generates Fluids.
With Tian Men Dong, for Lung and Kidney Yin Deficiency with consumptive coughing and occasional hemoptysis.
With Sheng Di Huang, for hematemesis or epistaxis due to Yin Deficiency with Excess Heat.
Rx. Paeoniae Alba Bai Shao 12g Nourishes the Blood, regulates menstruation, astringes Yin, softens the Liver and relieves pain.
Rx. Paeoniae Rubra Chi Shao 12g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, relieves pain, clears Heat, cools the Blood, reduces swelling from sores and abscesses and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Bai Shao, for spasms and cramping pain associated with Blood Deficiency and Blood Stagnation.
With Mu Dan Pi, for Blood Stasis disorders.
Flos Lonicerae Jin Yin Hua 12g Clears Heat and resolves Fire toxicity.
With Lian Qiao, for various Heat disorders.
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 12g

Clears Heat, cools the Blood, nourishes Yin and generates fluids.
With Bai Shao, for Blood Deficiency Heat.
With Mu Dan Pi , for purpura or bleeding with maculae and blotches on the skin.

Hb. Artemisiae Annuae Qing Hao 12g Clears Deficiency fevers, cools the Blood, stops bleeding and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Cx. Moutan Mu Dan Pi 9g Clears Heat, cools and harmonizes the Blood, clears Deficiency Fire, invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, drains pus and reduces swelling.
With Chi Shao, cools the Blood and disperses Stasis.
With Chi Shao and Sheng Di Huang, for hematemesis and epistaxis due to Heat in the Xue level.
Fr. Forsythiae Lian Qiao 9g Clears Heat, relieves toxicity, reduces abscesses, dissipates clumps, clears Blood Heat, promotes urination and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 3g Moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs, inhibits the growth of cancer cells and guides the herbs to all twelve channels.
Pollen Typhae Pu Huang 24g Stops bleeding by astringing, invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis and promotes urination.
Sm. Coicis Yi Yi Ren 24g Strengthens the Spleen, resolves Dampness, clears Heat,d expels pus and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Spina Gleditsiae Zao Jiao Ci 14g Draws out toxicity, discharges pus, invigorates the Blood, reduces swelling and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Jin Yin Hua, for the initial stages of abscesses and boils.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Tonifies Qi
  • Nourishes Yin
  • Nourishes the Blood
  • Clears Heat
  • Cools and invigorates the Blood
  • Eliminates Dampness
  • Resolves toxicity
  • Inhibits the growth of cancer cells

SYNDROMES

  • Qi, Yin and Blood Deficiency with Blood Heat and Dampness

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Tumor has existed for a long time
  • Tumor has ulcerated
  • Cauliflower like tumor
  • Pale or yellow face
  • Fatigue
  • Weakness
  • Listlessness
  • Dizziness
  • Blurred vision
  • Shortness of breath
  • Anorexia
  • Indigestion
  • Chronic, stubborn skin lesions
  • Sores
  • Enlarged lymph nodes
  • Nodules
  • Malignant abscesses and sores
  • Syphilitic sores
  • Red itching skin
  • Hardness
  • Facial complexion pale and sallow
  • Both cheeks slightly red
  • Dry eyes
  • Spots before the eyes
  • Numbness of limbs
  • Flaccid paralysis (Wei syndrome), weakness
  • Tinnitus
  • Deafness
  • Forgetfulness
  • Low back and knees sore and painful
  • Throat dry and painful
  • Five Sole Heat
  • Low-grade fever
  • Night sweats
  • Irritability
  • Insomnia
  • Stools difficult to pass
  • Constipation
  • Men: Spermatorrhea
  • Women:
    Irregular menses or
    Scanty menses or
    Amenorrhea
    Vaginal discharge
  • T: Red or Pale red or Pale with red spots
  • C: None or White
  • P: Thready and rapid or Thready and weak or Deep, thready and weak or Rapid and pecking

TREATS

  • Post-operative recurrence of breast cancer
