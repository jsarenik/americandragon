HERB FORMULAS

SHEN QI DI HUANG TANG - 参芪地黄湯 - GINSENG, ASTRAGTALUS AND REHMANNIA DECOCTION

English: Ginseng, Astragalus and Rehmannia Decoction
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Ginseng Ren Shen 1-9g Tonifies Qi.
With Shu Di Huang, for Qi and Yin Deficiency.
With Huang Qi, for Qi Deficiency with general debility, anorexia, and spontaneous sweat.
Rx. Astragali Huang Qi 9-30g Tonifies Qi and Blood.
Rx. Rehmanniae Preparata Shu Di Huang 8-30g Tonifies the Kidneys and nourishes Jing, Yin and Blood.
With Shan Zhu Yu, nourishes Kidney Yin and secures Jing, while restraining urination and sweating.
Fr. Corni Shan Zhu Yu 4-20g Nourishes Kidney Yin and Yang, astringes Jing and Yin and stops perspiration.
With Shan Yao, tonifies the Liver and Spleen.
With Shu Di Huang and Shan Yao, for urinary frequency (day or night), dizziness, tinnitus and lower back pain due to Kidney Deficiency.
Rx. Dioscoreae Shan Yao 4-15g Strengthens the Spleen , stabilizes the Kidneys and astringes Jing.
With Shu Di Huang and Shan Zhu Yu, for Kidney Deficiency with spermatorrhea, dizziness, tinnitus, lower back pain, impotence and night sweats.
Poria Fu Ling 3-30g Strengthens the Spleen and drains Dampness.
With Ze Xie, facilitates the removal of water and leaches out Dampness to treat stagnant water and Dampness leading to edema, jaundice, diarrhea, Milky Lin, and scanty, difficult urination with abdominal distention.
Cx. Moutan Mu Dan Pi 3-15g Clears Heat, cools the Blood clears and Deficiency Fire.
Rz. Alismatis Ze Xie 3-30g Promotes urination and prevents Kidney Fire as a result of tonification.
With Fu Ling and Mu Dan Pi, regulates the Kidneys, Liver and Spleen, drains and tonifies.
With Fu Ling, promotes urination.
With Mu Dan Pi, for steaming bone disorder, dizziness, and vertigo.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Tonifies the Spleen
  • Augments Qi
  • Supplements the Kidneys
  • Nourishes Yin

SYNDROMES

  • Metabolic Syndrome
  • Dry Heat with Blood and Phlegm Stagnation
  • Qi and Yin Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Lassitude
  • Fatigue
  • Shortness of breath
  • Dizziness
  • Tinnitus
  • Palpitations
  • Emaciation
  • Lack of strength
  • Dry, rough eyes
  • Possible distended feeling in eyes
  • A pale, lusterless face
  • Possible malar flush
  • Restless
  • Insomnia
  • Poor memory
  • Scanty, yellow urination or
  • Profuse, clear urination
  • Dry, parched throat
  • Tasteless feeling in the mouth
  • Low back and knee soreness and
  • Weakness
  • Low grade fever
  • Five Sole heat
  • Possible sweating
  • Possible tremors of the hands and tongue
  • Scanty intake
  • Abdominal distention after meal
  • Loose stools
  • T: Red
  • C: Thin or Peeled
  • P: Empty and rapid or Thready and rapid

TREATS

  • Hyperglycemia
  • Coronary Heart disease
  • Obesity
  • Uremia
  • Polyuria
  • Low back pain
  • Forgetfulness
  • Fatigue
  • Dyspnea
  • Arteriosclerosis
  • Stroke
  • Chronic Kidney failure
  • Oliguria
  • Chronic glomerulonephritis
  • Knee pain
  • Weakness
  • Palpitations
