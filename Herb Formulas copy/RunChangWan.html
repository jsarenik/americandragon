HERB FORMULAS

RUN CHANG WAN - 潤腸丸 - MOISTEN THE INTESTINES PILL

English: Moisten the Intestines Pill (from Master Shen's Book)
Also Known As: Pill for Lubricating the Intestines

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. et Rz. Rhei Da Huang 10-15g Drains Heat, purges accumulations, invigorates the Blood and dispels Blood Stasis.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Cauda Dang Gui Wei 10-15g Tonifies, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood, moistens the Intestines and unblocks the bowels.
Rz. seu Rx. Notopterygii Qiang Huo 10-15g Releases the exterior and expels Wind.
Sm. Persicae Tao Ren 10-30g Breaks up Blood Stasis, invigorates Blood circulation, moistens the Intestines and unblocks the bowels.
Sm. Cannabis Huo Ma Ren 30-37.5g Nourishes, moistens and lubricates the Intestines and nourishes the Yin.
With Tao Ren and Dang Gui, for habitual constipation.
Mel Feng Mi 30g Moistens the Intestines.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Moistens the Intestines
  • Promotes defecation
  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Disperses Wind

SYNDROMES

  • Constipation (usually severe) due to Wind-Heat entering the Intestines and injuring the Spleen and Stomach
  • Constipation due to improper diet or overwork
  • Constipation due to combined Blood Deficiency, Blood Stasis and Internal Wind due to Blood Deficiency
  • Constipation due to Heat Attacking the Lungs and Intestines and consuming Body Fluids

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Habitual constipation
  • Lusterless skin and nails
  • Dry mouth
  • Unquenchable thirst
  • Constipation in the elderly or weak individuals
  • Dizziness
  • Dry stools that are not difficult to pass
  • Palpitations
  • Lack of appetite
  • Lethargy
  • T: Dry and thin
  • C: Little or None
  • P: Thready

TREATS

  • Chronic constipation
  • Constipation in the initial stage of trauma to the lumbar vertebrae
  • Constipation as sequela to cerebrovascular accident
  • Constipation associated with hypertension
  • Dry skin
  • Constipation in anemic or elderly people
  • Constipation in association with prolapse of the lumbar intervertebral discs or compressed fracture of the intervertebral discs
  • Hemorrhoids

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Use with caution during pregnancy.
HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

NOTES

  • This formula is stronger than Run Chang Wan #1 and is used for more severe conditions.
