HERB FORMULAS

BAO XIN JIANG ZHI TANG - 保心降脂汤 - PROTECT THE HEART AND REDUCE FAT DECOCTION

English: Protect the Heart and Reduce Fat Decoction
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Salviae Miltiorrhizae Dan Shen 20g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, nourishes the Blood and calms the Spirit.
Rx. Polygoni Multiflori He Shou Wu 10g Tonifies the Liver and Kidneys, nourishes the Blood and nourishes Jing.
With Sang Ji Sheng, tonifies Kidney Yin and Liver Blood to nourish the sinews and bones and treat dizziness, blurred vision.
Rx. Puerariae Ge Gen 10g Relieves Heat, generates fluids, raises Spleen Yang and alleviates symptoms of hypertension.
Hb. Taxilli Sang Ji Sheng 10g Tonifies Liver and Kidney Yin and nourishes the Blood.
Rz. Polygonati Huang Jing 10g Tonifies Spleen Qi, nurtures Stomach Yin, tonifies the Kidneys and strengthens Jing.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 6g Tonifies the Spleen, augments Qi, moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs and guides the herbs to all twelve channels.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Dispels Blood Stasis
  • Nourishes the Blood
  • Tonifies Liver and Kidney Yin
  • Tonifies Spleen Qi

SYNDROMES

  • Liver and Kidney Yin Deficiency with Spleen Qi Deficiency and Heart Blood Stasis

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Facial complexion pale and sallow
  • Both cheeks slightly red
  • Dizziness
  • Dry eyes
  • Blurred vision
  • Spots before the eyes
  • Numbness of limbs
  • Flaccid paralysis (Wei syndrome), weakness
  • Tinnitus
  • Deafness
  • Forgetfulness
  • Low back and knees sore and painful
  • Throat dry and painful
  • Five Sole Heat
  • Low-grade fever
  • Night sweats
  • Irritability
  • Insomnia
  • Stools difficult to pass
  • Constipation
  • Spermatorrhea
  • Irregular menses or
  • Scanty menses or
  • Amenorrhea
  • Vaginal discharge
  • Fatigue
  • Anorexia
  • Four limbs feel tired and heavy
  • Abdominal and epigastric distention after eating
  • Maybe acid regurgitation or vomiting
  • Loose stools
  • Shortness of breath
  • Palpitations
  • Stabbing pain in the anterior region over the Heart
  • Suffocating sensation in the chest
  • Maybe drawing pain down the internal aspect of arm (especially the left arm)
  • Pain (in chest and/or arm) tends to be severe and intermittent
  • T: Dark red with purple spots or Purple body or Pale and flabby - may have teethmarks or Red
  • C: Little or White
  • P: Minute (scarcely perceptible) and thready or Knotted or Even, forceless and deficient or Thready and rapid

TREATS

  • Hyperlipoproteinemia
