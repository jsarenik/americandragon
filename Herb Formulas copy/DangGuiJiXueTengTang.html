HERB FORMULAS

DANG GUI JI XUE TENG TANG - 当归鸡血藤汤 - DANG GUI AND SPATHOLOBUS DECOCTION

English: Dang Gui and Spatholobus Decoction
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 15g Tonifies, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood, reduces swelling and alleviates pain.
With Bai Shao, regulates and harmonizes Qi and Blood.
Rx. Rehmanniae Preparata Shu Di Huang 15g Nourishes Jing and Blood and fills the Marrow.
With Dang Gui, for Blood Deficiency marked by dizziness, palpitations, insomnia and menstrual dysfunction.
Arillus Longan Long Yan Rou 6g Tonifies and augments the Heart and Spleen, nourishes the Blood and calms the Spirit.
Rx. Paeoniae Alba Bai Shao 9g Nourishes the Blood, regulates menstruation and relieves pain.
With Dang Gui and Shu Di Huang, for dizziness, blurred vision and dysmenorrhea due to Blood Deficiency or Stasis.
Rx. Salviae Miltiorrhizae Dan Shen 9g Invigorates the Blood,d dispels Blood Stasis, nourishes the Blood and calms the Spirit.
With Dang Guy, for irregular menstruation and retained lochia from Blood Stasis due to trauma.
Caulis Spatholobi Ji Xue Teng 15g Invigorates and tonifies the Blood, activates the channels and relaxes the sinews.
With Dang Gui, Shu Di Huang and Bai Shao, for Blood Deficiency after traumatic injury.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Tonifies Qi and Blood

SYNDROMES

  • Post-traumatic Qi and Blood Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Aching and pain around the shoulder
  • Inhibited bending and stretching
  • The pains worse upon waking or after a prolonged period of inactivity
  • The pain is better after use
  • The pain is worse after taxation
  • Possible muscle withering and weakness of the shoulder area
  • Sinew vessel contractions and spasms
  • Insensitivity of the skin
  • An enduring condition which does not heal
  • A lusterless facial complexion
  • Fatigue
  • Lack of strength
  • Loose stools
  • Poor appetite
  • Dizziness
  • Pale face
  • Weak voice
  • Shortness of breath
  • Reluctance to speak
  • Sweating with little or no exertion
  • Palpitations
  • Insomnia
  • Dry skin
  • Dry hair
  • Poor memory
  • Paleness
  • Scanty periods
  • T: Pale , maybe teethmarks
  • C: Thin and white
  • P:Deep, thready and forceless

TREATS

  • Scapulohumeral periarthritis
  • Leukopenia
  • Dry hair
  • Thrombocytopenia during radiotherapy or chemotherapy
