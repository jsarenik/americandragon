HERB FORMULAS

ALOPECIA TOPICAL #5

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Hb Artemisiae Scopariae Yin Chen Hao 9-30g Clears Heat and facilitates the resolution of Dampness topically.
Flos Chrysanthemi Ju Hua 4.5-20g Disperses Wind, clears Heat and detoxifies.
Hb. Menthae Haplocalycis Bo He 1.5-30g Disperses Wind-Heat and cools and clears the head.
Rx. Saposhnikoviae Fang Feng 3-10g Releases the Exterior and expels External Wind.
Rx. Ligustici Gao Ben 2-10g Expels Wind and Cold and dispels Wind-Dampness.
Fr. Viticis Man Jing Zi 6-12g Disperses Wind and clears Heat.
Hb. Schizonepetae Jing Jie 3-10g Releases the Exterior, expels Wind and relieves itching.
With Bo He, alleviates itching.
With Fang Feng, for pruritic itching.
Moschus She Xiang 0.03-0.1g Activates the Blood, dissipates masses, reduces swellings, detoxifies, opens the channels and alleviates pain. (Add after the other herbs have been decocted.)

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Heat
  • Eliminates Dampness
  • Disperses Wind-Heat

SYNDROMES

  • Alopecia due to Wind-Heat and Dampness

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Hair loss

TREATS

  • Alopecia

CONTRAINDICATIONS
HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

NOTES

  • Make a decoction (musk is added separately) and wash the head with it. Let it set in the head for 5 minutes and repeat, then rinse with warm water.
