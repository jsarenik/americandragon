HERB FORMULAS

SHENG MA GE GEN TANG - 升麻葛根湯 - CIMICIFUGA AND KUDZU DECOCTION

English: Cimicifuga and Kudzu Decoction
Also Known As: Cimicifuga and Pueraria Combination
Decoction of Cimicifugae and Pueraria

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rz. Cimicifugae Sheng Ma 2-6g Releases the exterior and vents rashes.
With Ge Gen, for early stage measles with incomplete expression of the rash with fever along with relatively superficial Yang Ming disorders.
Rx. Puerariae Ge Gen 3-12g Disperses the muscle layer to assist in venting rashes and raises fluids to expel Heat.
With Sheng Ma and Gan Cao, for Early stage measles where the rash has not yet appeared.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 1.5 -6g Relieves toxicity, helps Clear Heat and vents rashes.
Rx. Paeoniae Rubra Chi Shao 3-9g Cools the Blood.
With Gan Cao, prevents other herbs from injuring the Qi and Yin.
(Rz. Zingiberis Recens) (Sheng Jiang) (2-6g) (Detoxifies - included in some versions.)

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Expels Exterior Wind-Heat
  • Releases muscle layer
  • Vents rashes

SYNDROMES

  • Measles
  • Externally contracted Heat collecting in the Lung and Stomach
  • Early-stage Warm febrile diseases

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Early-stage measles or rashes that do not surface evenly
  • Fever and chills
  • Headache
  • Generalized body aches
  • Sneezing
  • Cough
  • Red eyes
  • Tearing
  • Thirst
  • Intolerance of Wind
  • T: Red and dry
  • P: Floating and rapid.

TREATS

  • Measles
  • Influenza
  • Scarlet fever
  • Chicken pox
  • Herpes zoster
  • Herpes simplex
  • Headache
  • Fever
  • Sore throat
  • Epistaxis
  • Hemopthalmia
  • Tonsillitis
  • Skin diseases
  • Boils
  • Cough
  • Chills
  • Red eyes

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • This formula may cause exterior Deficiency in rashes that surface evenly.
  • Contraindicated for those with measles toxin sinking internally (symptoms: shortness of breath, rough breathing, cough, wheezing).
  • Use caution for those with warm pathogen Heat diseases.
  • Contraindicated if the measles are fully erupted and the Heat has gone deeper with rapid breathing, dyspnea and cough.
