HERB FORMULAS

LUNG CANCER #39

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Fr. Trichosanthis Gua Lou 15g Clears Lung Heat, transforms Hot Phlegm, regulates Qi, unbinds the chest, reduces abscesses, dissipates nodules and eliminates pus.
Bul. Allii Xie Bai 10g Unblocks Yang Qi, disperses turbid Phlegm, alleviates pain, disperses hardenings and Cold Phlegm and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Gua Lou, for Chest Bi with profuse Phlegm.
Rx. Scutellariae Huang Qin 9g Clears Heat, dries Dampness, drains Fire, detoxifies and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Sang Bai Pi, Gua Lou and Xing Ren, for acute and chronic coughing due to Lung Heat.
With Zhi Zi, powerfully clears and drains lurking Heat from the Lungs with fever, fullness in the chest, coughing with yellow sputum and epistaxis or hematemesis.
Cx. Mori Sang Bai Pi 30g Drains Heat from the Lungs, stops coughing, calms wheezing and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
 With Huang Qin,drains Heat from the Lungs and stops coughing and wheezing.
Per. Citri Rubrum Ju Hong 6g Dries Dampness, transforms Phlegm and Descends Qi.
Fr. Gardeniae Zhi Zi 10g Clears Heat, reduces Fire, cools the Blood, relieves toxicity, reduces swelling, invigorates the Blood and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Mu Dan Pi, resolves Qi Level Stagnation leading to Heat and Heat in the Blood.
Cx. Moutan Mu Dan Pi 10g Clears Heat, cools and harmonizes the Blood, clears Deficiency Fire, invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, drains pus and reduces swelling.
Rx. Scrophulariae Xuan Shen 15g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, nourishes Yin, softens hardness, dissipates nodules, transforms Phlegm-Heat, drains Fire and relieves toxicity.
Cx. Lycii Di Gu Pi 15g Clears and drains Heat in the Lung, cools the Blood and drains Yin Deficiency Fire.
With Sang Bai Pi, for Lung Heat coughing or wheezing.
Hb. Artemisiae Annuae Qing Hao 15g Clears Deficiency fevers, clears Liver Heat, cools the Blood and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Bie Jia, for the final stages of a Warm febrile disease when the Yin Fluids have already been damaged and the pathogen still lurks deep within the Yin level with night fever and chills in the morning, the fever recedes without sweating, emaciation, rapid pulse and a red tongue with little coating.
Carapax Trionycis Bie Jia 15g Nourishes Yin, anchors Yang, invigorates the Blood, softens hardness, dissipates nodules and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Sm. Armeniacae Xing Ren 6g Stops coughing, calms wheezing and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Nourishes Yin
  • Moistens the Lungs
  • Resolves Phlegm

SYNDROMES

  • Lung Yin Deficiency due to Lung Heat

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Severe, dry hacking cough
  • No sputum expectorated
  • Small amounts of difficult to expectorate sputum
  • Blood tinged sputum (when Yin Deficiency Fire is present)
  • Afternoon fever
  • Night sweats
  • Malar flush (especially in afternoon)
  • Five Sole Heat
  • Dry mouth and throat
  • Itching in throat
  • Hoarse voice, low voice or loss of voice
  • Emaciation
  • T: Red and dry
  • C: None
  • P: Thready and rapid

TREATS

  • Lung cancer
