HERB FORMULAS

ZUO GUI WAN - 左歸丸 - RESTORE THE LEFT (KIDNEY) PILL

English: Restore the Left (Kidney) Pill
Also Known As: Left Gold Pill
Replenishing the Yin (Left) Pills

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Rehmanniae Preparata Shu Di Huang 24-60g Enriches Kidney Yin and nourishes Jing and Blood.
With Gou Qi Zi and Shan Zhu Yu, nourishes Liver Blood.
With Gui Ban, for dizziness, weak and sore back, impotence, spermatorrhea and night sweats due to Kidney and Liver Yin Deficiency.
Rx. Dioscoreae Shan Yao 12-30g Benefits the Yin and the Spleen tonifies the Kidneys and astringes Jing.
With Shu Di Huang and Shan Zhu Yu, for Kidney Deficiency with spermatorrhea and night sweats.
Fr. Lycii Gou Qi Zi 12-30g Nourishes Blood, tonifies the Liver and kidneys, nourishes Yin and benefits Jing.
Fr. Corni Shan Zhu Yu 12-30g Astringes Jing, tonifies Liver and Kidney Yin and strengthens Kidney Yang.
With Shu Di Huang and Shan Yao, for urinary frequency (day or night), dizziness, tinnitus and lower back pain due to Kidney Deficiency.
Rx. Cyathulae Chuan Niu Xi 12-25g Invigorates the channels and Blood, and treats atrophy and spasm of the lower extremities.
Sm. Cuscutae Tu Si Zi 12-30g Tonifies Kidney Yang, tonifies Kidney and Liver Yin, and astringes Jing and urine.
Colla Cervi Cornus Lu Jiao Jiao 12-30g Nourishes and tonifies Jing, Qi, and Blood.
 With Shu Di Huang, for Kidney Deficiency Cold with impotence and spermatorrhea (men), infertility and thin vaginal discharge (women) and physical retardation in children.
Colla Plastrum Testudinis Gui Ban Jiao 12-30g Nourishes Yin, anchors Yang, benefits the Kidneys strengthens bones and nourishes Blood. (Strongest Yin tonic).

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Nourishes Yin
  • Tonifies the Kidneys
  • Supplements Jing
  • Benefits Marrow

SYNDROMES

  • Kidney Jing Insufficiency
  • Liver and Kidney Yin Deficiency (especially essence and marrow)
  • Atrophy disorder (Wei Syndrome) due to Liver and Kidney Deficiency

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Use with caution and modify for those with Spleen and Stomach Deficiency.
  • Contraindicated for those with signs of Excess Heat.
  • This formula is not suited for long-term use.
HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

NOTES

  • This is a stronger version of Zuo Gui Yin and is used in more severe cases.
