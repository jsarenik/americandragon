HERB FORMULAS

FU REN BU YIN PIAN

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Cx. Albiziae He Huan Pi 12g Calms the Spirit, relieves constraint, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood, stops pain, regulates Qi, dissipates swellings and reduces abscesses.
With Bai Shao, for anxiety, irritability, worry and insomnia.
Caulis Spatholobi Ji Xue Teng 12g Invigorates and tonifies the Blood, invigorates the channels and relaxes the sinews.
With Dang Gui, Chuan Xiong and Bai Shao, for menstrual disorders.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 9g Tonifies, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood, disperses Cold, stops pain due to Blood Stasis and regulates the menses.
With Bai Shao, regulates and harmonizes Qi and Blood.
With Chuan Xiong, harmonizes, nourishes and invigorates the Blood and disperses Blood Stasis.
Rx. Salviae Miltiorrhizae Dan Shen 9g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, clears Heat, soothes irritability, nourishes the Blood and calms the Spirit.
Fr. Ligustri Lucidi Nu Zhen Zi 9g Nourishes and tonifies Liver and Kidney Yin and clears Deficiency Heat.
Sm. Cuscutae Tu Si Zi 9g Strengthens Yang, nourishes Yin, astringes Jing and urine and benefits the marrow Tonifies the Kidneys and Liver
Rx. Dipsaci Xu Duan 9g Tonifies the Liver and Kidneys, strengthens the sinews and bones, stops uterine bleeding, promotes the movement of Blood and alleviates pain.
Rx. Paeoniae Alba Bai Shao 7g Nourishes the Blood, regulates menstruation, astringes Yin, adjusts the Ying and Wei, softens the Liver and relieves pain.
With Dang Gui, nourishes Yin and Blood.
Poria Fu Ling 7g Promotes urination, leaches out Dampness, strengthens the Spleen and harmonizes the Middle Jiao.
Rx. Paeoniae Rubra Chi Shao 7g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis,clears Heat, cools the Blood and relieves pain.
With Chuan Xiong and Dang Gui, for Blood-Stasis induced gynecological disorders such as immobile abdominal masses and amenorrhea with abdominal pain.
With Bai Shao, for spasms and cramping pain associated with Blood Deficiency and Blood Stagnation.
Rz. Chuanxiong Chuan Xiong 5g Invigorates the Blood and promotes the movement of Qi.
With Dang Gui, for Blood Deficiency causing menstrual problems including dysmenorrhea, scanty menses, and amenorrhea.
Flos Carthami Hong Hua 5g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, opens the channels, unblocks menstruation and alleviates pain.
With Chuan Xiong, for chest and abdominal pain due to Qi and Blood Stasis.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Removes Stasis
  • Nourishes Yin
  • Strengthens Yang

SYNDROMES

  • Yin and Yang Deficiency with Qi and Blood Stagnation

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Failure to conceive

TREATS

  • Infertility
