HERB FORMULAS

BAO YUAN TANG - 保元汤 - PRESERVE THE PRIMAL DECOCTION

English: Preserve the Primal Decoction
Also Known As: Bao Yuan Dan
Preserve the Basal Pill

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Astragali Huang Qi 6-30g Tonifies the Spleen, augments Qi and Wei Qi, and raises Spleen Yang.
 With Ren Shen, for Qi Deficiency with general debility, anorexia, fatigue, tired extremities and spontaneous sweat.
Rx. Ginseng Ren Shen 6-10g Strongly tonifies Yuan Qi, tonifies Lung and Heart Qi, strengthens the Spleen and Stomach and calms the Shen.
 With Huang Qi, for Lung Qi Deficiency.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Preparata Zhi Gan Cao 3g Tonifies the Spleen, augments Qi and harmonizes.
With Ren Shen, relieves palpitations and anxiety.
Cx. Cinnamomi Rou Gui 1.5-3g Warms the Kidneys, Spleen and Heart and strengthens the Yang and Ming Men Fire, disperses deep Cold, warms and unblocks the channels and vessels, alleviates pain and assists the generation of Qi and Blood.
Rz. Zingiberis Gan Jiang 3g Warms the Middle, disperses Cold, warms the Lungs and unblocks the pulse.
With Zhi Gan Cao, for epigastric pain and vomiting due to Spleen and Stomach Deficiency Cold.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Tonifies Qi
  • Warms Yang

SYNDROMES

  • Heart Qi Deficiency
  • Heart Yang Deficiency
  • Heart and Lung Qi Deficiency
  • Spleen and Stomach Qi Deficiency with Cold and Yang Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Fatigue
  • Lethargy
  • Shortness of breath
  • Aversion to Cold
  • Maybe chest pain
  • Maybe diarrhea
  • Maybe vomiting
  • Low grade fever
  • Palpitations
  • T: Pale
  • C: White
  • P: Deep

TREATS

  • Consumption (when low grade fever is due to floating Yang from Qi Deficiency)
  • Palpitations
  • Cough
  • COPD
  • Chronic Kidney failure
  • Thromboangitis obliterans
  • Anemia
  • Thrombocytopenic purpura
  • Neurosis
  • Hypotension
  • Allergic purpura
  • Fatigue
  • Spontaneous perspiration
  • Sick sinus syndrome
  • Smallpox or chicken pox with (pox-like Qi Deficiency rash with recessed heads)
  • Cardiac arrhythmia
  • Common cold
  • Emphysema
  • Asthma
  • AIDS/HIV
  • Lupus erythematosis
  • Diarrhea
  • Neurasthenia
  • Hypothyroidism
  • Weakness
  • Congestive Heart failure
  • Dyspnea
