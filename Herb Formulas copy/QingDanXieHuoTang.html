HERB FORMULAS

QING DAN XIE HUO TANG - 清胆泻火汤 - CLEAR THE GALLBLADDER AND DRAIN FIRE DECOCTION

English: Clear the Gallbladder and Drain Fire Decoction
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Bupleuri Chai Hu 15g Resolves Shao Yang disorders, reduces fever, spreads Liver Qi and relieves Stagnation.
With Huang Qi, restores normal function of the Liver and Gallbladder.
With Zhi Ban Xia and Huang Qin, for Shao Yang disorders with alternating chills and fever and a sensation of fullness in the hypochondria and chest.
With Long Dan Cao and Zhi Zi, for Excess Heat in the Liver and/or Gallbladder channels.
Rx. Scutellariae Huang Qin 15g Clears Heat, dries Dampness and calms ascending Liver Yang.
With Zhi Zi, for Damp-Heat and jaundice.
With Zhi Zi, Yin Chen Hao and Da Huang, for Damp-Heat with jaundice.
With Chai Hu, for Heat in the Shao Yang with alternating chills and fever and nausea.
Rz. Pinelliae Preparatum Zhi Ban Xia 9g Dries Dampness, transforms Phlegm, descends Rebellious Qi and stops vomiting.
With Huang Qin, for Phlegm-Heat induced Rebellious Qi with cough, nausea, and vomiting.
Hb. Artemisiae Scopariae Yin Chen Hao 30g Clears Damp-Heat from the Liver and Gallbladder and relieves jaundice.
With Zhi Zi, strongly relieves Damp-Heat jaundice.
With Da Huang, for early-Stage Damp-Heat jaundice when Heat is stronger than Dampness.
With Zhi Zi and Da Huang, for Damp-Heat jaundice with abdominal distention, fever, constipation, and urinary dysfunction and gallstones.
Fr. Gardeniae Zhi Zi 9g Clears Heat, reduces Fire. eliminates irritability and drains Damp-Heat.
With Yin Chen Hao and Da Huang, for jaundice due to Damp-Heat in the Liver/Gallbladder.
With Lian Qiao, for Heat in the Heart channel with sores in the mouth or on the tongue and dark, scanty urine or urinary discomfort.
With Da Huang, for constipation.
Rx. Gentianae Long Dan Cao 9g Drains Damp-Heat from the Liver and Gallbladder channels.
With Da Huang, for rib pain, bitter taste, red eyes, jaundice, deafness, hot dysenteric disorders , Dampness and swelling of the scrotum, vomiting, epistaxis, fright, mania and constipation due to Liver Fire.
With Chai Hu and Huang Qin, for Liver Fire or Liver and Gallbladder Damp-Heat.
With Yin Chen Hao, Zhi Zi and Da Huang, for Damp-Heat jaundice with bitter taste in the mouth and fullness and pain in the chest from Liver/Gallbladder Damp-Heat.
Rx. Curcumae Yu Jin 9g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, regulates Qi flow, benefits the Gallbladder and reduces jaundice.
With Chai Hu, for Liver Qi and Blood Stagnation with flank pain and distention, dysmenorrhea and irregular menstruation.
With Dan Shen, for Heat with Blood Stasis.
With Yin Chen Hao and Zhi Zi, for jaundice with flank pain, constriction and distention of the chest, scanty urine, and anorexia due to Damp-Heat febrile disease.
Rx. Aucklandiae Mu Xiang 9g Promotes the movement of Qi, dispels Damp-Heat, harmonizes the Liver and Spleen and
alleviates pain.
With Chai Hu, Yin Chen Hao and Yu Jin, for Damp-Heat causing flank pain and distention and a bitter taste in the mouth.
Rx. et Rz. Rhei Da Huang 9g Drains Heat, purges accumulations, transforms Dampness, promotes urination, invigorates the Blood and dispels Blood Stasis.
With Zhi Zi, Huang Qin, Lian Qiao and Mang Xiao, for strong pathogenic Heat in the Upper and Middle Jiaos.
Natrii Sulfas Mang Xiao 9g Purges accumulations, guides out Stagnation, clears Heat and drains Fire.
With Da Huang, for constipation due to Heat in the Stomach and Intestines.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Spreads Liver Qi
  • Unblocks the Interior
  • Resolves Dampness

SYNDROMES

  • Liver and Gallbladder Stagnant Heat

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Severe, colicky, recurrent right-sided hypochondriac pain
  • Pain may radiate to the shoulder and upper back
  • Alternating fever and chills
  • Bitter taste in the mouth
  • Dry throat and mouth
  • Abdominal distention
  • T: Red
  • C: Yellow and dry
  • P: Wiry and rapid or Wiry slippery and rapid or Flooding and rapid

TREATS

  • Acute cholecystitis
  • Cholelithiasis
  • Jaundice
  • Acute pancreatitis
