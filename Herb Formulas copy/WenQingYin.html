HERB FORMULAS

WEN QING YIN - 温清飲 - WARMING AND CLEARING DRINK - DANG GUI AND GARDENIA COMBINATION

English: Warming and Clearing Drink
Also Known As: Warming and Clearing Decoction
Wen Qing San
Warming and Clearing Powder
Dang Gui and Gardenia Combination

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rz. Coptidis Huang Lian 3-4.5g Clears Heat, drains Dampness, drains Fire, resolves Fire toxicity and stops bleeding.
With Huang Qin, drains pathogenic Heat from the Upper and Middle Jiaos for high fever, redness, swelling and pain of the head and face, dry mouth, parched throat, ulcerations of the mouth and tongue, epistaxis, rashes and irritability in warm-febrile disease.
With Huang Qin and Zhi Zi, for high fever, irritability, dry mouth and throat, and dark urine due to Fire toxin in the three burners.
Rx. Scutellariae Huang Qin 3-4.5g Clears Heat, dries Dampness, Drains Fire, detoxifies, cools the Blood and stops bleeding.
With Zhi Zi, powerfully clears and drains lurking Heat from the Lungs with fever, fullness in the chest, cough with yellow sputum and epistaxis or hematemesis and treats Damp-Heat and jaundice and sores due to Heat toxin.
With Huang Lian and Bai Shao, for Damp-Heat dysentery with fever and tenesmus.
With Bai Shao, Zhi Zi and Huang Bai, for Damp-Heat jaundice.
With Bai Shao, cools Heat and nourishes the Blood.
With Huang Lian, Zhi Zi and Huang Bai, for lesions and sores with redness, swelling and pain.
Cx. Phellodendri Huang Bai 3-4.5g Drains Damp-Heat especially from the Lower Jiao, drains Kidney Fire, drains Fire and relieves Fire toxicity.
With Zhi Zi, eliminates pathogenic Dampness from the Liver/Gallbladder for Yang type jaundice with noticeable fever, markedly yellow/orange skin and eyes, thirst, and a red tongue with a yellow coat.
Fr. Gardeniae Zhi Zi 3-4.5g Clears Heat, reduces Fire, eliminates irritability, clears Heat, resolves Dampness , cools the Blood and relieves toxicity (stops bleeding by astringing).
Rx. Rehmanniae Preparata Shu Di Huang 3-4.5g Nourishes the Blood and nourishes Liver and Kidney Yin.
With Dang Gui, for Blood Deficiency marked by dizziness, palpitations, insomnia and menstrual dysfunction.
With Dang Gui and Huang Lian, for Blood Deficiency with Heat leading to irregular menstruation and infertility.
With Dang Gui, Bai Shao and Chuan Xiong, for anemia.
Rz. Chuanxiong Chuan Xiong 3-4.5g Invigorates the Blood and promotes the movement of Qi.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 3-4.5g Tonifies, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood, disperses Cold, reduces swelling, expels pus, generates flesh and alleviates pain.
With Bai Shao, for Blood Deficiency Heat.
With Shu Di Huang, for Heat symptoms due to Yin Deficiency and Devastated Blood.
With Chuan Xiong, harmonizes, nourishes and invigorates the Blood and disperses Blood Stasis.
Rx. Paeoniae Alba Bai Shao 3-4.5g Nourishes Blood and astringes Yin.
With Dang Gui and Shu Di Huang, for dizziness, blurred vision, and dysmenorrhea due to Blood Deficiency or Stasis.
With Huang Lian and Huang Qin, for Damp-Heat dysenteric disorders.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Drains Fire
  • Resolves toxicity
  • Tonifies the Blood
  • Stops bleeding
  • Warms the menses
  • Cools the Blood

SYNDROMES

  • Chronic, continuous uterine bleeding due to Heat
  • Gynecological disorders characterized by Blood Deficiency and Heat

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Chronic, continuous uterine bleeding
  • A pale complexion
  • Stabbing pain in the abdomen
  • Alternating chills and fever
  • Irregular menstruation with profuse bleeding
  • Abdominal pain
  • Fever
  • Thirst
  • Itchy skin
  • Heat in the upper body with Cold in the lower body
  • Irritability and restlessness despite fatigue and exhaustion
  • Mood swings
  • A sallow complexion
  • Emaciation
  • T: Dry with a red tip
  • C: Thin white
  • P: Deep and rapid

TREATS

  • Eczema, dermatitis and urticaria characterized by dry, itchy and inflamed skin
  • Uterine bleeding (metrorrhagia)
  • Excessive menstrual bleeding (menorrhagia)
  • Leukorrhea
  • Cystitis
  • Uteritis
  • Menopause
  • Infertility
  • Urinary tract infection
  • Uterine fibroids
  • Fever
  • Abdominal pain
  • Thrombocytopenic purpura
  • Irregular menstruation
  • Aphthae
  • Neurodermatitis
  • Lupus erythematosis
  • Gastric ulcer
  • Occult bleeding
  • Pruritis
  • Tinea
  • Dermal diseases due to anemia
  • Genital warts
  • Acne
  • Dysentery
  • AIDS/HIV
  • Dermatitis
  • Fatigue
  • Postpartum uterine bleeding
