HERB FORMULAS

HYPERTHYROID #6

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
.Actions
Rx, Astragali Huang Qi 30g Tonifies Qi and Blood, promotes urination, reduces edema and generates Body Fluids.
With Dang Shen, for Qi Deficiency with general debility, anorexia, fatigue, tired extremities and spontaneous sweat.
With Mai Men Dong and Wu Wei Zi, for night sweats.
Rx. Codonopsis Dang Shen 15g Tonifies the Middle Jiao and augments Qi
With Mai Men Dong and Wu Wei Zi, for injury to Qi and fluids with shortness of breath and thirst.
Rx. Ophiopogonis Mai Men Dong 15g Nourishes Yin, moistens the Lungs, generates Fluids, stops cough, clears Heat from the Heart and eliminates irritability.
With Sheng Di Huang, for hematemesis or epistaxis due to Yin Deficiency with Excess Heat.
Fr. Schisandrae Wu Wei Zi 10g Astringes Lung Qi, generates Fluids, tonifies Qi and nourishes Kidney Yin.
With Mu Li, for sweating.
With Huang Qi, for Yang Deficiency with spontaneous sweat.
With Mu Li and Huang Qi, for spontaneous sweating due to Qi Deficiency.
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 30g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, nourishes Yin and generates fluids.
With Bai Shao, for Blood Deficiency Heat.
Fr. Lycii Gou Qi Zi 15g Nourishes and tonifies the liver and Kidneys, brightens eyes and enriches Yin.
Rx. Dioscoreae Shan Yao 15g Strengthens the Spleen, stabilizes the Kidneys and nourishes Jing.
With Mai Men Dong and Wu Wei Zi, for Lung Deficiency with cough.
With Dang Shen, for Spleen and Kidney Deficiency with fatigue, weakness, anorexia, and watery stools.
Fr. Corni Shan Zhu Yu 10g Nourishes Kidney Yin and Yang, astringes essence and stops excessive perspiration.
With Shan Yao, tonifies the Liver and benefits the Spleen.
With Wu Wei Zi, for spermatorrhea, abnormal sweating, palpitations and shortness of breath due to Yin and Yang Deficiency of the Liver and Kidneys.
Rx. Paeoniae Alba Bai Shao 15g Nourishes the Blood, astringes Yin and Calms Liver Yang and Liver Wind.
Rz. Cyperi Xiang Fu 10g Spreads and regulates Liver Qi.
Rx. Polygoni Multiflori He Shou Wu 15g Tonifies the Liver and Kidneys, nourishes the Blood and nourishes Jing.
Concha Ostreae Mu Li 15g Calms the Liver, benefits Yin, anchors Floating Yang, prevents leakage of fluids, softens hardness and dissipates nodules.
With Shan Zhu Yu, for sweating.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 6g Tonifies the Spleen, augments Qi and moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs.
With Dang Shen, for Anorexia, fatigue and loose stools due to Spleen Deficiency.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Tonifies Qi
  • Nourishes Yin

SYNDROMES

  • Qi and Yin Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Neck enlargement
  • Exophthalmia
  • Lassitude
  • Fatigue
  • Shortness of breath
  • Dizziness
  • Tinnitus
  • Palpitations
  • Emaciation
  • Lack of strength
  • Dry, rough eyes
  • Possible distended feeling in eyes
  • A pale, lusterless face
  • Possible malar flush
  • Restless
  • Insomnia
  • Poor memory
  • Dry, parched throat
  • Tasteless feeling in the mouth
  • Low back and knee soreness and
  • weakness
  • Low grade fever
  • Five sole heat
  • Possible sweating
  • Possible tremors of the hands and tongue
  • Scanty intake
  • Abdominal distention after meal
  • Loose stools
  • T: Red
  • C: Thin or Peeled
  • P: Empty and rapid or Thready and rapid

TREATS

  • Hyperthyroidism
