HERB FORMULAS

JIE GAN JIAN - 解肝煎 - RESOLVE THE LIVER DECOCTION

English: Resolve the Liver Decoction
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Per. Citri Reticulatae Chen Pi 4.5g Regulates Qi, adjusts the Middle, relieves the diaphragm, dries Dampness, transforms Phlegm and descends Qi.
With Sheng Jiang, for vomiting and hiccups due to Disharmony of the Stomach Qi and Cold-Dampness Obstructing the Middle with nausea and hiccup.
With Zhi Ban Xia, for a stifling sensation in the chest and cough with excessive white, viscous sputum due to Phlegm-Damp Obstruction.
With Zhi Ban Xia, Fu Ling and Hou Po, enhances the Phlegm transforming, Spleen strengthening properties.
With Sha Ren, for Dampness obstructing the Spleen's transforming and transporting functions with reduced intake of food and diarrhea and belching, nausea, and a stifling sensation in the epigastrium due to Disharmony of the Stomach Qi.
With Zhi Ban Xia and Fu Ling, for cough due to accumulation of Dampness and Phlegm.
With Zhi Ban Xia and Chen Pi, for nausea, vomiting, chest fullness and distention and anorexia due to congested fluids.
With Zhi Ban Xia, Chen Pi and Sheng Jiang, for congested fluids in the Stomach causing nausea and vomiting.
Rz. Pinelliae Preparatum Zhi Ban Xia 4.5g Dries Dampness, transforms Phlegm, descends Rebellious Qi and stops vomiting.
 With Hou Po, treats Phlegm-Dampness with Qi Stagnation to open Phlegm and Qi Obstruction with a sensation of fullness in the chest with coughing and wheezing, nausea, vomiting and a sensation of distention in the hypochondrium.
With Hou Po, Zi Su Ye and Fu Ling, for Plum pit Qi Stagnation.
Cx. Magnoliae Officinalis Hou Po 4.5g Promotes the movement of Qi in the Middle Jiao, resolves Stagnation, descends Qi, dries Dampness and transforms Phlegm.
With Zhi Ban Xia and Fu Ling, for Phlegm Obstructing the Qi, leading to coughing, vomiting or a sense of something stuck in the throat.
Poria Fu Ling 4.5g Promotes urination, leaches out Dampness, strengthens the Spleen, harmonizes the Middle Jiao and transforms Phlegm.
With Zhi Ban Xia, expels Phlegm and stops nausea and vomiting, hiccups, coughs with clear, profuse Phlegm and loose and watery stools.
Fol. Perillae Zi Su Ye 3g Promotes the movement of Spleen and Stomach Qi.
With Sha Ren and Chen Pi, for a stifling sensation and fullness in the chest and abdomen.
With Zhi Ban Xia and Hou Po, for Spleen and Stomach Qi Stagnation with accumulation of Phlegm.
Rx. Paeoniae Alba Bai Shao 3g Nourishes Blood, calms Liver Yang and Liver Wind, softens the Liver and relieves pain.
Fr. Amomi Sha Ren 2.1g Promotes the movement of Qi, aromatically transforms Dampness, strengthens the Spleen, stops vomiting warms the Middle and stops diarrhea.
With Hou Po, for constrained Dampness with Qi Stagnation leading to distention and fullness in the epigastrium and abdomen.
Rz. Zingiberis Recens Sheng Jiang 3g Warms the Middle and stops vomiting.
With Zhi Ban Xia, for vomiting due to many causes including Stomach Cold.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Smoothes the movement of Qi
  • Harmonizes the Middle Jiao
  • Transforms Dampness
  • Calms the fetus

SYNDROMES

  • Liver Qi Stagnation
  • Middle Jiao Damp Stagnation

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Fullness, distention and pain in the chest and flanks
  • Diarrhea
  • Restless fetus

TREATS

  • Abdominal distention
  • Gastritis
  • Fetal restlessness
  • Abdominal pain
  • Indigestion
