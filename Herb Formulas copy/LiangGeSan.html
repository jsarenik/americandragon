HERB FORMULAS

LIANG GE SAN - 涼膈散 - COOL THE DIAPHRAGM POWDER

English: Cool the Diaphragm Powder
Also Known As: Forsythia and Rhubarb Formula
The Diaphragm Cooling Powder

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. et Rz. Rhei Da Huang 4.5-10g Drains Heat and Fire, purges accumulations, transforms Dampness, promotes urination, Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis and reduces Fire toxicity.
With Mang Xiao, eliminates obstruction in the Intestines and treats abscess, swellings and toxic sores.
With Mang Xiao and Gan Cao (Tiao Wei Cheng Qi Tang), drains Yang Ming Heat.
With Huang Qin, Zhi Zi and Lian Qiao, for strong pathogenic Heat in the Upper and Middle Jiaos.
Natrii Sulfas Mang Xiao 3-10g Softens hardness, purges accumulations, clears Heat, drains Fire, reduces swelling and eliminates Stagnation.
With Da Huang, for constipation due to Colon Heat.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 2.5-10g Clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity and harmonizes.
Rx. Scutellariae Huang Qin 3-12g Drains Fire, clears Heat, dries Dampness, detoxifies and clears Lung and Liver Heat.
With Zhi Zi, powerfully clears and drains lurking Heat from the Lungs with fever, fullness in the chest, cough with yellow sputum and epistaxis or hematemesis, Clears Damp-Heat and jaundice, treats sores due to Heat toxin and irregular vaginal bleeding due to Liver Fire
Fr. Gardeniae Zhi Zi 3-12g Clears Heat from all three Jiaos through the urine and resolves Dampness.
Fr. Forsythiae Lian Qiao 3-20g Clears Lung Heat, relieves toxicity, reduces abscesses and dissipates clumps .
Hb. Menthae Haplocalycis Bo He 2.5-6g Helps release Upper Jiao Heat, benefits the head and throat and relieves Liver Qi Stagnation.
Hb. Lophatheri Dan Zhu Ye 2-12g Clears Heat from the Lung, Stomach and Heart, promotes urination and clears Damp-Heat.
Mel (honey) Feng Mi   Protects the Stomach and prevents abdominal pain caused by purgatives.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Drains Fire
  • Unblocks the bowels by Clearing the Upper Jiao and Draining the Middle and Lower Jiaos

SYNDROMES

  • Colon Heat
  • Heart Fire Flaring Up
  • Heat Stagnation (unformed Heat in the Upper Jiao with Heat Accumulation in the Middle Jiao)
  • Constipation with Heat in the Upper Jiao
  • Qi Stage Gallbladder Heat

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Reduce or omit Da Huang when constipation is gone or there is mild abdominal pain, pus in stool and/or reduced energy.
  • Weak patients.
  • Contraindicated during pregnancy.
