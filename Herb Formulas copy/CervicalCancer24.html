HERB FORMULAS

CERVICAL CANCER #24 (TOPICAL)

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Fr. Bruceae Ya Dan Zi 4.5g Cools Heat, resolves toxicity, expels Dampness, cools the Blood, softens hardness topically and treats cancer caused by Toxic Heat.
Rx. Aconiti Lateralis* Sheng Fu Zi 4.5g Treats carbuncles and pyogenic infections and inhibits the growth of cancer cells topically.
Calomelas Qing Fen 4.5g Resolves toxicity generates flesh, promotes the healing of sores and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Realgar Xiong Huang 9g Relieves toxicity topically and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Arsenicum Sablimatum* Pi Shuang 6g Erodes sores, deteriorates ulcerous cells and kills malignant cells topically.
Sm. Strychni* Sheng Ma Qian Zi 4.5g Unblocks the channels and collaterals, disperses clumps, cools Blood Heat to reduce swelling, alleviates pain and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Indigo Naturalis Qing Dai 9g Clears Heat, resolves Fire toxicity, cools the Blood, reduces maculae, disperses Stagnation, reduces swelling and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Borax Peng Sha 6g Relieves toxicity, reduces swellings, clears Heat, dissolves Phlegm, transforms stones and prevents putrefication.
Fr. Mume Wu Mei 15g Treat corns and warts and eliminates dead, noxious flesh such as that surrounding toxic sores topically.
Borneolum Bing Pian 1.5g Clears Heat, alleviates pain, dissipates nodules, alleviates itching and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Moschus She Xiang 3g Invigorates the Blood, dissipates masses, reduces swellings, detoxifies, opens the channels, alleviates pain and inhibits the growth of cancer cells topically.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Cools Heat
  • Resolves toxicity
  • Treats cancer caused by Toxic Heat

SYNDROMES

  • Toxic Heat

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Tumor develops quickly
  • High fever
  • Thirst
  • Epistaxis
  • Spots on the skin
  • Constipation
  • Scanty, red urine
  • Hemorrhaging
  • Loss of consciousness
  • May be ascites (late stage)
  • Weakness
  • Bleeds easily
  • T: Red
  • C: Yellow
  • P: Tight or Thready and rapid

TREATS

  • Cervical cancer

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • *This formula is toxic. Do not take internally.
HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

NOTES

  • Grind the ingredients into a fine powder and scatter on the affected area.
