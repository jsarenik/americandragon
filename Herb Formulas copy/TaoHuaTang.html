HERB FORMULAS

TAO HUA TANG - 桃花汤 - PEACH BLOSSOM DECOCTION

English: Peach Blossom Decoction
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Halloysitum Rubrum Chi Shi Zhi 30-48g Astringes the Intestines, stops diarrhea, astringes the Blood and stops bleeding.
Rz. Zingiberis Gan Jiang 3-10g Warms the Middle, expels Cold, rescues Devastated Yang and expels Interior Cold.
Sm. Oryzae Geng Mi 30g Tonifies Yang and tonifies Qi.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Warms the Middle
  • Dispels Cold
  • Astringes the Intestines
  • Stops dysenteric disorders

SYNDROMES

  • Dysentery and diarrhea due to Middle Jiao Deficiency Cold

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Dysentery with dark blood and pus in the stool
  • Abdominal pain which is better with warmth and pressure
  • Dysuria
  • T: Pale
  • C: Normal
  • P: Slow and frail or Faint and thin

TREATS

  • Chronic dysentery
  • Bacterial dysentery
  • Amoebic dysentery
  • Enteritis
  • Ulcerative colitis
  • Chronic colitis
  • Crohn's disease
  • Peptic ulcer
  • Irritable bowel syndrome
  • Rectal cancer
  • Hemorrhoids
  • Dysfunctional uterine bleeding
  • Celiac syndrome
  • Leukorrhagia
  • Rectal ulcer
  • Rectal cancer
  • Periproctosis
  • Abdominal fullness and distention
  • Diarrhea

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with dysenteric disorders due to Damp-Heat.
