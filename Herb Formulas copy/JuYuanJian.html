HERB FORMULAS

JU YUAN JIAN - 举元煎 - LIFT THE SOURCE DECOCTION

English: Lift the Source Decoction
Also Known As: Decoction for Raising Primordial Energy

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Honey-Fried Rx. Astragali Mi Zhi Huang Qi 9-24g Tonifies Qi and Blood, strengthens the Spleen, raises the Yang Qi of the Spleen and Stomach and generates Body Fluids.
With Ren Shen, for Qi Deficiency with general debility, anorexia, fatigue, tired extremities and spontaneous sweat.
With Bai Zhu, for Spleen Qi Deficiency with weakness, lassitude and loose stools.
With Sheng Ma, raises the Yang Qi of the Spleen, allowing turbid Yin to descend and be eliminated, treating chronic diarrhea, a dragging sensation in the abdomen, fatigue, shortness of breath, uterine bleeding and prolapses.
Rx. Ginseng Ren Shen 9-15g Tonifies Qi, generates Body Fluids and stops thirst.
With Bai Zhu, tonifies the transformation and transportation functions of the Spleen.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Preparata Zhi Gan Cao 3-6g Tonifies the Spleen, augments Qi and moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs.
With Ren Shen, relieves palpitations and anxiety.
Dry-Fried Rz. Cimicifugae Chao Sheng Ma 1.5-2.1g Raises Yang and lifts Sunken Qi.
With Bai Zhu, for Dai channel spasms manifested as dragging sensations in the lower body, usually accompanied by vaginal discharge.
Rz. Atractylodes Macrocephalae Bai Zhu 3-10g Tonifies the Spleen, augments Qi and calms the fetus.
With Ren Shen and Huang Qi, for restless fetus due to Qi Deficiency.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Tonifies Qi in order to tonify the Blood
  • Raises Yang Qi

SYNDROMES

  • Collapse due to Qi Deficiency leading to severe Blood loss and Devastated Yang

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Pale, watery Blood
  • Palpitations
  • Shortness of breath
  • Laconic speech
  • A sinking sensation in the lower abdomen
  • Fatigue
  • A shiny, pale complexion
  • Abdominal fullness
  • Lower back pain
  • T: Pale
  • C: Moist and thin
  • P: Deficient and frail or Weak and smooth

TREATS

  • Menorrhagia
  • Organ prolapse
  • Rectal prolapse
  • Postpartum uterine bleeding
  • Leukemia
  • Vaginal pain
  • Abdominal fullness and distention
  • Metrorrhagia
  • Fetal restlessness
  • Aplastic anemia
  • Threatened miscarriage
  • Uterine fibroids
  • Fatigue
  • Prolonged menstruation
