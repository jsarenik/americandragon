HERB FORMULAS

ZHEN REN HUO MING YIN - 真人活命飲 - TRUE MAN'S DECOCTION TO REVITALIZE LIFE

English: True Man's Decoction to Revitalize Life
Also Known As: Angelica and Mastic Combination

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Angelicae Dahuricae Bai Zhi 3g Reduces swelling, eliminates toxins and expels pus.
With JIn Yin Hua and Dang Gui Wei, for early-stage sores and carbuncles.
With JIn Yin Hua, Chuan Shan Jia and Tian Hua Fen, for dermatological disorders with abscesses and swelling.
Bul. Fritillariae Zhe Bei Mu 3g Clears Heat and dissipates nodules, transforms Phlegm and stops cough.
Rx. Saposhnikoviae Fang Feng 3g Releases the exterior and expels External Wind, expels Wind-Dampness and alleviates pain.
Extremitas Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui Wei 3g Reduces swelling, expels pus, generates flesh, alleviates pain, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood and disperses Cold.
 With Jin Yin Hua, reduces obstruction and toxicity for swollen, painful sores and necrosis of surrounding tissue.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 3g Clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity and moderates and harmonizes the properties of other herbs.
With Jin Yin Hua, for damp rashes or acne.
Dry-fried Sp. Gleditsiae Chao Zao Jiao Ci 3g Draws out toxicity, discharges pus, invigorates the Blood and reduces swelling.
With JIn Yin Hua for Initial stages of abscesses and boils.
With Bai Zhi, for Un-ulcerated abscesses or those with difficulty discharging pus.
Liquid-fried Squama Manitis Zhi Chuan Shan Jia 3g Reduces swelling and promotes the discharge of pus.
With Zao Jiao Ci, for suppurative, inflamed skin lesions that have pustulated but remain closed.
Rx. Trichosanthis Tian Hua Fen 3g Clears and drains Lung Heat, transforms Phlegm, moistens Lung Dryness, relieves toxicity, expels pus and reduces swelling.
With Jin Yin Hua, Zao Jiao Ci and Zhe Bei Mu, reduces swelling and pus.
Myrrh Mo Yao 3g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, alleviates pain, reduces swelling, generates flesh and promotes healing.
With Ru Xiang, for swollen, painful sores and sores that are hard but not painful.
Olibanum Ru Xiang 3g Invigorates the Blood, promotes the movement of Qi, reduces swelling and generates flesh.
With Mo Yao, for chronic, ulcerated and non-healing sores.
Flos Lonicerae Jin Yin Hua 9g Clears Heat and resolves Fire toxicity.
With Gan Cao, for toxic swelling and sores.
Per. Citri Reticulatae Chen Pi 9g Dries Dampness and transforms Phlegm.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Heat
  • Eliminates toxins
  • Reduces swelling
  • Softens hardness
  • Activates Blood circulation
  • Stops pain

SYNDROMES

  • Abscesses and sores due to accumulation of Heat toxins and Stagnation of Qi and Blood
  • Abscesses and sores due to accumulation of Heat toxins and Phlegm Fire

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Local redness
  • Swelling and pain
  • Maybe fever and chills
  • T: Normal
  • C: Thin white or yellow
  • P: Rapid and forceful

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Use with caution for those with Spleen and Stomach Deficiencies.
  • Use with caution for those with Qi and Blood Deficiencies.
  • Contraindicated for Yin (deep-rooted) sores or if the lesions have already ulcerated.
  • Side effects are nausea and vomiting.
HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

NOTES

  • This formula is indicated for early stages of abscesses and sores.
