HERB FORMULAS

FANG FENG TANG - 防風湯 - SAPOSHNIKOVIA DECOCTION

English: Saposhnikovia Decoction
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Saposhnikoviae Fang Feng 3-12g Releases the exterior, expels External Wind, expels Wind-Dampness and alleviates pain.
With Qin Jiao, for Wind Damp Bi with spasms of the tendons or numbness of the limbs and pain due to Wind-Dampness in patients with Blood Deficiency.
With Ma Huang, dispels Wind-Cold from the Exterior.
Rx. Puerariae Ge Gen 4-21g Discharges Exterior conditions. releases the muscles, especially of the neck and upper back, relieves Heat and generates fluids.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 3-15g Tonifies, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood, regulates the menses, disperses Cold and stops pain due to Blood Stasis.
Rx. Paeoniae Rubra Chi Shao 4.5-15g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, relieves pain, clears Heat and cools the Blood.
Rx. Ephedrae Ma Huang 1.5-10g Induces sweating, releases the exterior, promotes urination, reduces edema and warms and disperses Cold pathogens.
Rx. Gentianae Macrophyllae Qin Jiao 3-12g Expels Wind-Dampness, opens the channels and soothes the sinews and collaterals.
Rx. Scutellariae Huang Qin 3-18g Clears Heat, dries Dampness, drains Fire and detoxifies.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Relieves Wind-Cold-Damp
  • Produces sweat
  • Invigorates Blood circulation
  • Relieves pain
  • Disperses Heat

SYNDROMES

  • Wind-Damp Bi: Wind Predominant

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Migrating pain
  • Multiple joints or muscular areas
  • Sometimes changes area
  • Usually affects wrist, elbow or knee joint
  • Sometimes chills and fever
  • T: Normal
  • C: Thin and white
  • P: Superficial

TREATS

  • Rheumatoid arthritis
  • Fever
  • Lateral epicondylitis
  • Tennis elbow
  • Periarthritis of the shoulder
  • Shoulder pain
  • Gout
  • Osteoarthritis
  • Carpal tunnel syndrome
  • Elbow pain
  • Epitrochleitis
  • Neck pain and stiffness
  • Toe pain
  • Hip pain
