HERB FORMULAS

STOMACH CANCER #43

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Sm. Persicae Tao Ren 10g Breaks up Blood Stasis, invigorates Blood circulation and drains abscesses.
Flos Carthami Hong Hua 10g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, opens the channels and alleviates pain.
With Hong Hua, for Chest and abdominal pain due to Qi and Blood Stasis.
With Yi Mu Cao, for Blood Stasis with abdominal pain.
With San Leng and E Zhu, for abdominal masses and accumulations.
With Tao Ren, invigorates the Blood for all types of Blood Stagnation.
Rx. Paeoniae Rubra Chi Shao 8g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, clears Heat, cools the Blood, reduces swelling from sores and abscesses and relieves pain.
With Chuan Xiong and Dang Gui, for Blood-Stasis induced gynecological disorders such as immobile abdominal masses and amenorrhea with abdominal pain.
With Tao Ren, for Blood Stasis.
With Xiang Fu, for Flank pain, abdominal pain, and dysmenorrhea due to Qi and Blood Stagnation.
with Mu Dan Pi, for Blood Stasis disorders.
Rz. Chuanxiong Chuan Xiang 8g Invigorates the Blood, promotes the movement of Qi and alleviates pain.
With Xiang Fu, for Qi Stagnation combined with Blood Stasis.
with Dang Gui, harmonizes, nourishes and invigorates the Blood and disperses Blood Stasis.
With Dang Gui, Tao Ren and Hong Hua, for abdominal pain and headache from Blood Stagnation.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 10g Tonifies, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood, disperses Cold, reduces swelling, expels pus, generates flesh and alleviates pain.
With Tao Ren and Hong Hua, for symptoms associated with Blood Stasis.
With Xiang Fu, tonifies the Blood and moves Qi.
With Wu Yao, disperses Cold and stops pain, for abdominal pain due to External Cold.
Fr. Aurantii Zhi Ke 10g Promotes the movement of Qi, reduces distention and pressure, resolves hardenings and removes stagnated food.
Cx. Moutan Mu Dan Pi 10g Clears Heat, cools, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, drains pus and reduces swelling.
Rz. Sparganii San Leng 10g Forcefully breaks up Blood Stasis, regulates Qi, dissolves accumulations and alleviates pain.
With E Zhu, for Qi and Blood Stagnation with clumping and accumulation leading to pain with distending pain in the chest, epigastrium and abdomen.
With E Zhu, Dang Gui, Hong Hua and Tao Ren, for Blood Stasis with immobile abdominal masses.
With Yan Hu Suo, for pain due to Blood Stasis.
Rz. Curcumae E Zhu 10g Invigorates Blood circulation, dispels Blood Stasis, regulates Qi, dissolves accumulations and alleviates pain.
With San Leng, for Qi and Blood Stagnation with clumping and accumulation leading to pain with distending pain in the chest, epigastrium and abdomen.
Rz. Cyperi Xiang Fu 10g Spreads and regulates Liver Qi and alleviates pain.
With Yan Hu Suo, treats everything from Liver Qi Stagnation to Deep Blood Stasis with pain anywhere in the body.
Rx. Linderae Wu Yao 10g Promotes movement of Qi and alleviates pain.
With Xiang Fu, unblocks Qi in both the Qi and Blood levels, releases constraint from the Liver and Gallbladder, descends Qi in the chest and abdomen and warms Qi which has been slowed by pathogenic Cold.
Rz. Corydalis Yan Hu Suo 10g Invigorates the Blood, circulates Qi and stops pain.
With Wu Ling Zhi, for chest and abdominal pain due to Blood Stagnation.
With Chuan Xiong, for body aches and headaches due to Blood Stagnation.
Faeces Trogopterori Wu Ling Zhi 10g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, transforms Stasis, stops bleeding and
alleviates pain.
With Xiang Fu, for epigastric pain.
With Xiang Fu and Yan Hu Suo, for chest and epigastric pain due to Qi and Blood Stagnation.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Breaks up Stasis
  • Cools Blood Heat
  • Promotes the movement of Qi

SYNDROMES

  • Blood Stagnation

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Sharp, stabbing epigastric pain with fixed location
  • Epigastric or abdominal pain and distention
  • The pain may start intermittently but becomes more constant
  • Pain is severe after eating
  • The pain may radiate to the back
  • Dislike of pressure and Heat on the epigastrium
  • Pain predominates over distention or
  • Pain but no distention
  • Dysphagia
  • Epigastric rumbling
  • May be abdominal or epigastric lumps or masses
  • Nausea
  • Vomiting purple/black blood or "coffee grounds"
  • Indeterminate gnawing hunger
  • Anorexia
  • Weight loss
  • Weakness
  • Dry stools with blood like tar (melaena)
  • Hemafecia with dark, purple Blood or mixed dark and fresh Blood
  • Constipation
  • Dry, rough skin
  • Darkish, purplish or a blotchy complexion, lips and conjunctiva
  • Dark rings around the eyes
  • Spider naevi on the cheeks, epigastrium, trunk and around the inner ankle and knee
  • Maybe hemorrhoids
  • Maybe irregular menstruation with dark, purple, clotted Blood
  • With significant Deficiency from chronic Blood loss:
    A pale or sallow complexion
    Dizziness
  • T: Dark purple with purple spots or dots and dark, distended sublingual veins
  • C: White or Grayish white or Yellow
  • P: Wiry and choppy or Wiry or Choppy or Thready and choppy or Deep and thready

TREATS

  • Stomach cancer
  • Gastric cancer

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated during pregnancy.
