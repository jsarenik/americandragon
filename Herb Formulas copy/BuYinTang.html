HERB FORMULAS

BU YIN TANG - 補陰湯 - SUPPLEMENT YIN DECOCTION

English: Supplement Yin Decoction
Also Known As: Dang Gui and Rehmannia Combination

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Ginseng Ren Shen 2-9g Powerfully tonifies Yuan Qi.
With Shu Di Huang, for Qi and Yin Deficiency.
Rx. Paeoniae Alba Bai Shao 2-9g Nourishes the Blood, regulates menstruation, astringes Yin,softens the Liver and calms Liver Yang.
With Dang Gui, nourishes Yin and Blood.
With Dang Gui and Shu Di Huang, for dizziness, blurred vision, and dysmenorrhea due to Blood Deficiency or Stasis.
Per. Citri Reticulatae Chen Pi 2-9g Regulates Qi, adjusts the Middle, relieves the diaphragm, resolves Dampness and helps prevent Stagnation from tonic herbs.
Rx. Achyranthis Bidentatae Niu Xi 2-9g Invigorates the Blood expels Blood Stasis, nourishes Liver and Kidney Yin and clears Damp-Heat in the Lower Jiao.
With Du Zhong, for low back and extremity pain and weakness due to Bi pain or Kidney Deficiency.
With Sheng Di Huang, powerfully clears Deficiency Fire Blazing Upward with pain and swelling of the gums, epistaxis, hematemesis and other hemorrhage in the upper body.
Fr. Psoraliae Bu Gu Zhi 2-9g Tonifies the Kidneys, strengthens Yang and stabilizes Jing.
With Xiao Hui Xiang, for Kidney Yang Deficiency with clear, frequent, profuse urination or spermatorrhea or dysmenorrhea with lower back ache, late periods and coldness in the lower abdomen.
Cx. Eucommiae Du Zhong 2-14g Tonifies Kidney Yang, aids the smooth flow of Qi and Blood and calms the fetus.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 3-14g Tonifies, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood, disperses Cold, stops pain due to Blood Stasis and regulates the menses.
With Bai Shao, regulates and harmonizes Qi and Blood.
With Shu Di Huang, for Heat symptoms due to Yin Deficiency and Devastated Blood.
Poria Fu Ling 3-4.5g Promotes urination, strengthens the Spleen and harmonizes the Middle Jiao.
Fr. Foeniculi Xiao Hui Xiang 1-4.5g Warms the Liver and Kidneys, expels Cold and alleviates pain.
Rz. Anemarrhenae Zhi Mu 1-4.5g Clears Heat, drains Fire, nourishes Yin, moistens Dryness, generates Fluids, clears Deficiency Fire and quenches thirst.
With Huang Bai and Shu Di Huang, for Yin Deficiency with Empty Fire.
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 1-4.5g Clears Heat, cools the Blood and nourishes Yin.
With Dang Gui, for Blood Deficiency Heat.
With Shu Di Huang, for Heat symptoms due to Yin Deficiency and Devastated Blood.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 1-4.5g Tonifies the Spleen,augments Qi and moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs.
Cx. Phellodendri Huang Bai 1-4.5g Drains Damp-Heat especially from the Lower Jiao and drains Kidney Fire.
With Zhi Mu, for Yin Deficiency fever with steaming bones and night sweats and Lower Jiao Damp-Heat.
With Dang Gui and Niu Xi, for sore and weak low back and legs.
Rx. Rehmanniae Preparata Shu Di Huang 1-4.5g Nourishes Jing and Blood, fills the Marrow, and nourishes Liver and Kidney Yin.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Tonifies Yin
  • Regulates Qi and Blood Circulation
  • Tonifies Kidney Yang
  • Eliminates Kidney Fire
  • Kidney Yin and Yang Deficiency with Blood Deficiency

SYNDROMES

  • Spleen and Kidney Yang Deficiency
  • Kidney Yin Deficiency
  • Kidney Jing Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Low back pain on arising from sitting
  • Dry skin
  • A slack abdomen
  • Pale complexion
  • T: Pale and Dry
  • C: Little or None
  • P: Weak, thready and rapid

TREATS

  • Sciatica
  • Spinal cord inflammation
  • Chronic nephritis
  • Hernia
  • Osteoporosis
  • Lumbago
  • Ephidrosis
  • Deformed spondylitis
  • Infertility
  • Dry skin
