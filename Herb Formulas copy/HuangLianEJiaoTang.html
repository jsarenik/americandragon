HERB FORMULAS

HUANG LIAN E JIAO TANG - 黃蓮阿膠湯 - COPTIS AND ASS-HIDE GELATIN DECOCTION

English: Coptis and Ass-Hide Gelatin Decoction
Also Known As: Coptis and Gelatin Combination
Decoction of Coptis and Gelatin
Coptis and Asini Combination

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
 Rz. Coptidis Huang Lian 3-12g Clears Heat, drains Dampness, drains Fire, resolves Fire toxicity, stops bleeding, clears Heart Fire and drains Stomach Fire.
With Huang Qin, clears Fire from the Heart.
With Huang Qin and E Jiao, calms the Shen.
With E Jiao and Bai Shao, for insomnia and irritability due to overwhelming Heart Fire and Yin and Blood Deficiencies.
Rx. Scutellariae Huang Qin 2-12g Clears Heat, dries Dampness, drains Fire, detoxifies, cools the Blood and stops bleeding.
With Huang Lian, drains pathogenic Heat from the Upper and Middle Jiaos for high fever, redness, swelling and pain of the head and face, dry mouth, parched throat, ulcerations of the mouth and tongue, epistaxis, rashes and irritability in warm-febrile disease and explosive diarrhea and dysentery with burning pain in the anus or fitted, filthy stools and abdominal pain.
With Huang Lian and Bai Shao, for Damp-Heat dysentery with fever and tenesmus.
With Bai Shao, for dysenteric disorders with abdominal pain due to Heat entering the Interior.
Colla Corii Asini E Jiao 3-12g Nourishes the Blood, stops bleeding and nourishes Kidney Yin.
With E Jiao, for Heat toxin clumping in the Intestines, injuring the Blood collaterals causing Blood and pus in the stools.
With Huang Qin, Bai Shao and Ji Zi Huan, for irritability and insomnia due to Yin Deficiency and severely injured fluids usually after a fever.
Rx. Paeoniae Alba Bai Shao 2.5-12g Nourishes the Blood, nourishes and astringes Yin, softens the Liver and relieves pain .
With E Jiao in harmonizes the Heart and Kidneys.
With Huang Qin and Huang Lian, for Damp-Heat dysenteric disorders.
(Egg Yolk) (Ji Zi Huang) (2 yolks) (Drains Fire, Generates Blood. Not included in some versions.)

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Nourishes Kidney Yin
  • Descends Qi
  • Purges Heart Fire
  • Eliminates irritability
  • Calms the Shen

SYNDROMES

  • Yin Deficiency Fire
  • Fire due to Kidney and Heart Yin Deficiencies
  • Shao Yin Heat
  • Blood Level Heat with dysenteric disorders due to Heat toxin
