HERB FORMULAS

YOU GUI WAN - 右歸丸 - RESTORE THE RIGHT PILL

English: Restore the Right Pill  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Lateralis Aconiti Preparata Zhi Fu Zi 3-18g Warms Ming Men Fire and assists Heart, Kidney and Spleen Yang.
With Shu Di Huang and Rou Gui, for Kidney Yang Deficiency with waning of Ming Men Fire with aversion to Cold, weak and sore back and legs, impotence, and urinary frequency.
With Rou Gui, powerfully tonifies Kidney Yang, for Kidney Yang Deficiency with lower back pain and weakness in the legs and knees and Deficiency of Ming Men Fire with impotence or infertility.
Cx. Cinnamomi Rou Gui 3-12g Warms the Kidneys, Spleen and Heart and strengthens Yang and Ming Men Fire and assists the generation of Qi and Blood.
Colla Cervi Cornus Lu Jiao Jiao 6-12g Tonifies the Kidneys, strengthens the Yang and nourishes Qi and Blood.
With Shu Di Huang, for impotence and spermatorrhea (men) and infertility and thin vaginal discharge (women) due to Kidney Deficiency Cold.
Rx. Rehmanniae Preparata Shu Di Huang 18-24g Tonifies the Blood, nourishes the Yin and nourishes Jing and Blood.
With Dang Gui, for Blood Deficiency marked by dizziness, palpitations, insomnia and menstrual dysfunction.
With Shan Zhu Yu, nourishes Kidney Yin and secures Jing, while restraining urination and sweating.
Fr. Corni Shan Zhu Yu 9g Stabilizes the Kidneys, astringes Jing, tonifies Liver and Kidney Yin and strengthens Kidney Yang.
With Dang Gui and Shu Di Huang, for night sweats due to Yin Deficiency.
With Shu Di Huang and Shan Yao , for urinary frequency (day or night), dizziness, tinnitus and lower back pain due to Kidney Deficiency.
Dry-fried Rz. Dioscoreae Chao Shan Yao 12g Strongly tonifies the Kidneys and astringes
Jing.
With Shan Zhu Yu, for Kidney Deficiency with spermatorrhea and night sweats.
Dry-fried Fr. Lycii Chao Gou Qi Zi 6-12g Nourishes and tonifies the Liver and Kidneys, benefits Jing and brightens the eyes.
With Shu Di Hang, for Liver and Kidney Deficiency.
With Shu Di Huang, Zhi Fu Zi and Rou Gui, for Kidney Yang Deficiency.
Sm. Cuscutae Tu Si Zi 12g Strengthens Yang, nourishes Yin, astringes Jing and urine and benefits marrow.
With Du Zhong, enriches and tonifies the Kidneys and Liver, astringes Jing, nourishes Kidney Yin , brightens the eyes and encourages the movement of Primal Yang to treat spermatorrhea, premature ejaculation and lower back pain from Kidney Deficiency.
Cx. Eucommiae Du Zhong 12g Tonifies the Kidneys and Liver, tonifies Kidney Yang and strengthens the sinews and bones.
With Shan Zhu Yu and Tu Si Zi, for Impotence and frequent urination due to Deficiency Cold of the Kidneys.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 6-9g Tonifies, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood and disperses Cold.
With Shu Di Huang, for Heat symptoms due to Yin Deficiency and Devastated Blood.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Warms and tonifies Kidney Yang
  • Replenishes Jing
  • Tonifies the Blood

SYNDROMES

  • Kidney Yang Deficiency with waning of Ming Men Fire (with Spleen and Stomach Deficiency Cold)
  • Atrophy disorder (Wei Syndrome) due to Kidney Qi and Yang Deficiency

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated when Kidney Deficiency is accompanied by Dampness or turbidity.
  • Avoid cold and raw foods while taking this formula.
HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS
  • This formula has been shown to be effective in reversing corticosteroid-induced immune suppression.
