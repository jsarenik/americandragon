HERB FORMULAS

GUI ZHI FU LING WAN - 桂枝茯苓丸 - CINNAMON AND PORIA PILLS - CINNAMON AND HOELEN COMBINATION

English: Cinnamon and Poria Pills
Also Known As: Cinnamon and Hoelen Formula
Pills of Cinnamon and Poria

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Ram. Cinnamomi Gui Zhi 2-12g Unblocks Yang, transforms Qi, warms the Middle and directs Turbid Yin downward, warms the channels, collaterals and Blood through the vessels to relieve pain and warms and facilitates the flow of Yang Qi in the chest.
With Bai Shao, warms and tonifies the Middle Jiao.
With Fu Ling, unblocks the Yang and facilitates the removal of congested fluids associated with Yang Deficiency edema.
With Fu Ling and Bai Shao, for Heart and Spleen Yang Deficiency with palpitations, edema and shortness of breath.
With Dan Shen, for palpitations and chest pain due to Heart Yang Deficiency and subsequent painful obstruction due to Blood Stasis blocking the channels.
With Tao Ren and Mu Dan Pi, for gynecological discomfort due to Blood Stasis and lower abdominal masses.
With Tao Ren, Mu Dan Pi and Fu Ling, for amenorrhea and abdominal pain due to Cold Invasion and Blood Stasis.
Poria Fu Ling 2-15g Promotes urination, resolves Dampness, strengthens the Spleen, harmonizes the Middle Jiao and transforms Phlegm.
With Gui Zhi, warms and transforms fluids, unblocks Yang and promotes urination.
Rx. Paeoniae Rubra Chi Shao 2-12g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, activates Blood, dispels Blood Stasis and eliminates pain.
With Tao Ren, for Blood Stasis induced menstrual disorders, especially those characterized by early period, excessive bleeding, and thick purple clots.
With Bai Shao, for spasms and cramping pain associated with Blood Deficiency and Blood Stagnation.
With Mu Dan Pi, for Blood Stasis disorders (amenorrhea, menstrual pain, traumatic injury).
(Rx. Paeoniae Alba) (Bai Shao) (2-12g) (Nourishes Blood, regulates menstruation, benefits Yin, astringes weak Ying Qi, softens the Liver and relieves pain.)
With Gui Zhi, harmonizes the Ying and Wei and warms and tonifies the Middle Jiao.)
Cx. Moutan Mu Dan Pi 2-12g Activates Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, drains pus and reduces swelling.
With Tao Ren and Gui Zhi, for amenorrhea and abdominal pain due to Blood Stasis.
With Chi Shao, for irregular menstruation due to Heat and obstruction from Blood Deficiency.
With Tao Ren, Gui Zhi, Fu Ling and Chi Shao, for amenorrhea, dysmenorrhea with severe pain, Heat, masses, lumps and tumors due to Blood Stasis.
Sm Persicae Tao Ren 2-12g Dispels Blood Stasis and invigorates Blood circulation.
(Rx. Salviae Miltiorrhizae) (Dan Shen) (2-12g) (Invigorates the Blood and dispels Blood Stasis. - May be added)

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Use with extreme caution during pregnancy or postpartum.
  • Use only with confirmed diagnosis of Blood Stasis.
  • This formula is not intended for serious conditions of Blood Stagnation or Excess Heat in the Blood.
HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS
  • This formula has been shown to treat menopausal symptoms induced by gonadotropin-releasing hormone agonists without a negative effect on the serum estradiol levels.
