HERB FORMULAS

HUANG LIAN JIE DU TANG WU WEI XIAO DU YIN HE CAI - 黃連解毒湯五味消毒飲合彩 COMBINED COPTIS DECOCTION TO RELIEVE TOXIN AND FIVE-INGREDIENT DECOCTION TO ELIMINATE TOXIN

English: Combined Coptis Decoction to Relieve Toxin and Five-Ingredient Decoction to Eliminate Toxin
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Hb. Taraxaci Pu Gong Ying 15g Clears Heat and resolves Dampness.
 With Jin Yin Hua, for all hot, painful, hard, and deep-rooted boils.
Flos Lonicerae Jin Yin Hua 15g Clears Heat, resolves Fire toxicity, vents and disperses External Wind-Heat and clears Damp-Heat from the Lower Jiao.
With Jin Yin Hua, for suppurating boils that have not ulcerated, or have ulcerated but are not discharging pus.
Hb. Violae Zi Hua Di Ding 15g Clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity and clears Hot sores.
With Pu Gong Ying, for breast abscesses, furuncles, boils, carbuncles and Damp-Heat jaundice.
Rz. Coptidis Huang Lian 6g Clears Heat, drains Dampness, drains Fire and relieves toxicity.
 With Huang Qin and Zhi Zi, for high fever, irritability, dry mouth and throat, and dark urine due to Fire toxin in the three burners.
With Sheng Di Huang, for insomnia and delirium due to unremitting Heat injuring the Ying level.
Rx. Scutellariae Huang Qin 6g Clears Heat, resolves Fire toxins, invigorates the Blood, reduces swellings, promotes urination and reduces edema.
Cx. Phellodendri Huang Bai 6g Drains Damp-Heat, especially from the Lower Jiao, drains Deficiency Fire, drains Fire and relieves toxicity.
Charred Fr. Gardeniae Zhi Zi Tan 6g Clears Heat, reduces Fire, drains Damp-Heat, cools Blood and relieves toxicity.
Raw Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 10g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, nourishes Yin and generates fluids.
Rx. Paeoniae Rubra Chi Shao 10g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, activates Blood and dispels Blood Stasis.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Cools Blood
  • Clears Heat
  • Relieves Toxin

SYNDROMES

  • Fire/Heat Toxin induced psoriasis

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Lesions develop and spread quickly
  • Lesions often coalesce together
  • Lesions are covered by fine scales that shed easily Itching
  • Burning Pain
  • High fever
  • Yellow urine
  • Thirst
  • Dry stools
  • T: Scarlet
  • C: Thin
  • P: Wiry, slippery and rapid

TREATS

  • Psoriasis due to Fire/Heat Toxin
  • Allergic dermatitis
