HERB FORMULAS

HOU ZHENG WAN - 喉症囊 - THROAT DISEASE PILL (TOPICAL)

English: Throat Disease Pill
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Isatidis Ban Lan Gen 9-30g Drains Heat, relieves Fire toxicity, cools the Blood and benefits the throat.
Venenum Bufonis Chan Su 0.01-0.03g Relieves toxicity, reduces swellings, alleviates pain, Attacks toxins, reduces swellings resists cancer and stops panic.
With Niu Huang , topically for sores due to Heat toxin, throat Bi or periodontal disorders.
With Niu Huang, Xiong Huang and Bing Pian, for extreme soreness and pain in the throat.
Natrii Sulfas Exsiccatus Xuan Ming Fen 4-18g Clears Heat, drains Fire and reduces swelling,for ulcers of the mouth.
Calculus Bovis Niu Huang 0.15-1g Drains Heat and resolves Fire toxicity.
Borneolum Bing Pian 0.01-0.9g Clears Heat, drains Fire, resolves toxicity, alleviates pain, dissipates nodules and alleviates itching.
With Xiong Huang, Peng Sha and Gan Cao, for thrush, white mouth or mycotic stomatitis topically.
Fumi Carbonisatus Pulvis Bai Cao Shuang 0.9-4.5g Controls bleeding and disperses Accumulations.
Realgar Xiong Huang 0.15-0.36g Relieves toxicity and dries Dampness.
Borax Peng Sha 1.5-6g Relieves toxicity, reduces swellings and prevents putrefication.
Indigo Naturalis Qing Dai 1.5-3g Clears Heat, resolves Fire toxicity, cools the Blood, reduces swelling and reduces maculae.
With Peng Sha, for ulcerations of the mouth, tongue and throat.
Vessica Fel Ursi Xiong Dan 2-5g Clears Heat and resolves Fire toxicity.
With Bing Pian, topically to relieve hemorrhoidal swelling, pain and inflammation due to toxic Heat

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Heat
  • Resolves toxins
  • Stops pain

SYNDROMES

  • Damp-Heat skin lesions

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Urticaria
  • Papular urticaria
  • Mainly appears on the limbs in various sizes
  • Erosion
  • Exudate
  • Scabs
  • Rupture of the vesicles
  • Intense itching
  • Abdominal pain
  • Nausea
  • Vomiting
  • Dry stools
  • Excessive or frequent defecation
  • T: Swollen or Red
  • C: Yellow and sticky
  • P: Floating and rapid

TREATS

  • Papular urticaria
  • Mouth and throat ulcers
