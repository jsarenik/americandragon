HERB FORMULAS

SAN JIAO #1

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Scutellariae Huang Qin 9-12g Clears Heat and Drains Fire especially from the Upper Jiao, dries Dampness to treat Damp-Heat in Stomach and Intestines.
With Zhi Ban Xia, for Phlegm-Heat induced Rebellious Qi with cough, nausea and vomiting.
With Huang Lian, drains pathogenic Heat from the Upper and Middle Jiaos for high fever, redness, swelling and pain of the head and face, dry mouth, parched throat, ulcerations of the mouth and tongue, epistaxis, rashes and irritability in warm-febrile disease and explosive diarrhea and dysentery with burning pain in the anus or fitted, filthy stools and abdominal pain.
Rz. Coptidis Huang Lian 3-6g Drains Fire, relieves toxicity, clears Heat and drains Dampness for Damp-Heat in the Stomach and Intestines.
With Zhi Ban Xia, for Cold and Heat in the Middle Jiao with abdominal fullness, nausea and vomiting.
Poria Fu Ling 3-6g Promotes urination, leaches out Dampness, strengthens the Spleen, harmonizes the Middle Jiao and transforms Phlegm.
With Zhi Ban Xia expels Phlegm and stops nausea and vomiting, hiccups, coughs with clear, profuse Phlegm and loose and watery stools .
With Zhi Ban Xia and Chen Pi for congested fluids with vomiting, nausea, chest distention and anorexia.
Rz. Pinelliae Preparatum Zhi Ban Xia 6-9g Dries Dampness, transforms Phlegm, descends Rebellious Qi and harmonizes the Stomach to stop vomiting.
With Chen Pi, for epigastric and abdominal distention, nausea, vomiting and productive cough due to Phlegm- Stagnation.
With Huang Qin for Phlegm-Heat induced Rebellious Qi with cough, nausea and vomiting.
With Hou Po for Phlegm induced cough, vomiting and epigastric and abdominal distention.
Sm. Arecae Bing Lang 3-6g Promotes Qi movement, reduces accumulations, leads out Stagnation by mildly draining downward and unblocking bowels and promotes urination.
Cx. Magnoliae Officinalis Hou Po 9-12g Promotes Qi movement, transforms Dampness, resolves Stagnation, warms and transforms Phlegm and descends Rebellious Qi.
With Huang Lian, for acute diarrhea associated with sudden turmoil disorder from Damp-Heat.
Per Citri Reticulatae Chen Pi 6-9g Regulates Qi, adjusts the Middle, relieves the diaphragm, dries Dampness, transforms Phlegm and descends Qi.
With Zhi Ban Xia, for a stifling sensation in the chest and cough with excessive white, viscous sputum due to Phlegm-Damp Obstruction.
With Zhi Ban Xia and Fu Ling, for cough due to accumulation of Dampness and Phlegm.
With Zhi Ban Xia, Fu Ling and Hou Po, enhances the Phlegm transforming, Spleen strengthening properties.
Hb. Agastaches Huo Xiang 6-9g Aromatically transforms Dampness, harmonizes the Middle Jiao and stops vomiting.
With Zhi Ban Xia, for nausea and vomiting.
With Zhi Ban Xia, Fu Ling and Hou Po, for early stages of Damp-Warmth when Dampness predominates with fever, a stifling sensation in the chest, nausea, a sense of being encumbered a sticky sensation in the mouth and a lack of thirst.
Rx. Platycodi Jie Geng 6-9g Opens and disseminates Lung Qi, expels Phlegm and directs the other herbs to the Upper Jiao.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Eliminates Heat and Dampness

SYNDROMES

  • Upper Jiao Damp-Heat
  • Middle Jiao Damp-Heat
  • Middle Jiao Phlegm-Heat
  • Middle Jiao Damp-Stagnation

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Heaviness in the head and extremities
  • Dry mouth
  • Thirst with no desire to drink
  • Afternoon fever
  • Anorexia
  • Lethargy
  • Jaundice
  • Maybe no thirst
  • Body aches
  • Sallow complexion
  • Scanty urine
  • Diarrhea
  • Chest discomfort
  • Dull appearance
  • Sometimes disorientation
  • Loose stool
  • Sometimes skin vesicles
  • Abdominal distention
  • Uncomfortable bowel movements
  • Nausea and vomiting
  • Cough
  • T: Normal
  • C: Greasy white or yellow
  • P: Soft and rapid

TREATS

  • Eczema
  • Impetigo
  • Migraines
  • Jaundice
  • Nausea/Vomiting
  • Diarrhea
  • Enteritis
  • Colitis
  • Cough
  • Fever
  • Abdominal fullness and distention
  • Anorexia
