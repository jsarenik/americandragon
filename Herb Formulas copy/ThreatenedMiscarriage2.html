HERB FORMULAS

THREATENED MISCARRIAGE #2

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Ginseng Ren Shen 6g Powerfully tonifies Yuan, Spleen, Stomach, Lung and Heart Qi.
With Shu Di Huang, for severe Qi and Blood Deficiency.
With Bai Zhu, tonifies the transformation and transportation functions of the Spleen.
Cx. Eucommiae Du Zhong 9g Tonifies the Kidneys and Liver, aids the smooth flow of Qi and Blood and calms the fetus.
Rx. Paeoniae Alba Bai Shao 9g Nourishes the Blood, regulates menstruation, softens the Liver and relieves pain.
With Gan Cao, for abdominal pain due to disharmony between the Liver and Stomach.
Rx. Rehmanniae Preparata Shu Di Huang 12g Nourishes the Blood and nourishes Liver and Kidney Yin.
Rz. Atractylodis Macrocephalae Bai Zhu 9g Tonifies the Spleen, augments Qi, dries Dampness and calms the fetus.
With Ren Shen and Huang Qi, for a restless fetus due to Qi Deficiency.
With Shu Di Huang and Bai Shao, for a restless fetus due to Blood Deficiency, often with palpitations.
With E Jiao, for a miscarriage with bleeding.
Per. Citri Reticulatae Chen Pi 9g Regulates Qi, adjusts the Middle, relieves the diaphragm and helps prevent Stagnation from tonic herbs.
With Bai Zhu, for anorexia and other symptoms of Damp Obstruction due to Spleen Deficiency.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Preparata Zhi Gan Cao 6g Tonifies the Spleen, augments Qi, moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs and guides the herbs to all twelve channels.
With Bai Shao, moderates spasms and alleviates pain, for painful spasms of the leg or abdomen.
Rx. Astragali Huang Qi 15g Tonifies Qi and Blood, strengthens the Spleen and raises the Yang Qi of the Spleen and Stomach.
With Ren Shen, for Qi Deficiency with general debility, anorexia, fatigue, tired extremities and spontaneous sweating.
With Bai Zhu, for Spleen Qi Deficiency with weakness, lassitude and loose stools.
Colla Corii Asini E Jiao 30g Tonifies and nourishes the Blood and stops bleeding.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Augments Qi
  • Generates Blood
  • Strengthens the Kidneys
  • Calms the fetus

SYNDROMES

  • Qi and Blood Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Threatened abortion during the first trimester, with slight uterine bleeding of light-colored and thin Blood
  • Fetal unrest
  • Low back soreness
  • Aches and weakness in the waist and knees
  • Lower abdominal insidious pain
  • Abdominal distention with vague pain
  • There may be a history of habitual abortion
  • A sensation of downward sagging
  • A lusterless, pallid facial complexion
  • Essence spirit withering
  • Heart palpitations
  • Shortness of breath
  • Lassitude
  • Weak limbs
  • Fatigue
  • Pale face
  • Shortness of breath
  • T: Pale or Dusky
  • C: Thin and white
  • P: Empty and slippery or Thready, slippery and forceless or Weak

TREATS

  • Threatened miscarriage
  • Vaginal bleeding during pregnancy
