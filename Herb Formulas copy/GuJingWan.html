HERB FORMULAS

GU JING WAN - 固精丸 - STABILIZE THE MENSES PILL

English: Stabilize the Menses Pill
Also Known As: Pill for Arresting Uterine Bleeding
Pills for Menorrhagia

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Prepared Plastrum Testudinis Zhi Gui Ban 9-30g Nourishes Yin, anchors the Yang, nourishes the Blood, enriches Yin, stabilizes the Chong and Ren channels, cools the Blood and stops uterine bleeding.
Dry-Fried Rx. Paeoniae Alba Chao Bai Shao 15-30g Nourishes the Blood and regulates menstruation.
With Huang Qin, cools Heat and nourishes the Blood for calming the fetus which is disturbed by Heat.
(Rx. Paeoniae Rubra) (Chi Shao) (6-9g) (Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, relieves pain, clears Heat and cools the Blood. May substitute for Chao Bai Shao.)
(With Xiang Fu, for flank pain, abdominal pain, and dysmenorrhea due to Qi and Blood Stagnation.)
Dry-Fried Rx. Scutellariae Chao Huang Qin 6-30g Cools the Blood, stops bleeding, clears Heat and calms the fetus.
Cx. Ailanthi Chun Pi 3-22.5g Clears Heat, astringes Intestines, dries Dampness, stops diarrhea and stops bleeding.
With Gui Ban, Bai Xiao, Huang Qin, Huang Bai and Xiang Fu, for continuous uterine bleeding.
Dry-Fried Cx. Phellodendri Chao Huang Bai 6-9g Drains Kidney Fire.
Rz. Cyperi Xiang Fu 6-9g Spreads and regulates Liver Qi, regulates menstruation and alleviates pain.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Enriches Yin
  • Clears Heat
  • Clears Deficiency Heat
  • Stops bleeding
  • Stabilizes the Menses

SYNDROMES

  • Blood Heat due to Heat in the Chong and REN channels
  • Yin Deficiency Heat

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Continuous menstruation or
  • Uterine bleeding that alternates between spotting and gushing
  • The Blood is very red, thick and sticky and may contain dark-purple clots
  • A sensation of Heat and irritability in the chest
  • Menstruation may be delayed
  • Dry mouth
  • Leukorrhea
  • Dysuria
  • Abdominal pain
  • Dark urine
  • Red urine
  • Red face
  • Difficulty defecating
  • T: Red
  • C: Normal or Yellow
  • P: Wiry or Wiry and rapid or Tight and rapid or Slippery and rapid

TREATS

  • Dysfunctional uterine bleeding
  • Habitual miscarriage
  • Gastrointestinal bleeding
  • Menorrhagia
  • Irregular menstruation
  • Vaginal discharge
  • Chronic pelvic inflammatory disease
  • Threatened miscarriage
  • Metrorrhagia
  • Prolonged menstruation

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with Blood Heat due to Blood Stagnation.
  • Contraindicated for those with Blood Stagnation.
  • Contraindicated for those with Qi Deficiency.
