HERB FORMULAS

STOMACH CANCER #19

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Hb. Taraxaci Pu Gong Ying 12g Reduces abscesses, dissipates nodules, clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity and cools the Blood.
Rz. Cyperi Xiang Fu 12g Spreads and regulates Liver Qi, regulates menstruation and alleviates pain.
With Dang Gui, tonifies the Blood and moves Qi, for premenstrual symptoms, menstrual pain and irregular menstruation.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 12g Tonifies, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood, disperses Cold, reduces swelling, expels pus, generates flesh and stops pain due to Blood Stasis.
With Bai Shao, regulates and harmonizes Qi and Blood.
With Wu Yao, disperses Cold and stops pain, for abdominal pain due to External Cold, Cold dysmenorrhea and postpartum abdominal pain.
Hb. Lobeliae Chinensis Ban Bian Lian 12g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, reduces toxicity, promotes urination and reduces edema.
Hb. Scutellariae Barbatae Ban Zhi Lian 12g Clears Heat, resolves Fire toxins, invigorates the Blood, reduces swellings, mildly dispels Blood Stasis, promotes urination, reduces edema stops bleeding and treats various types of cancer.
With Bai Hua She She Cao and Ba Yue Zha, for cancers of the digestive tract.
Hb. Hedyotis Diffusae Bai Hua She She Cao 12g Clears Heat, strongly relieves Fire toxins, promotes urination and reduces abscesses.
With Ban Zhi Lian, for cancer.
Rx. Paeoniae Alba Bai Shao 9g Nourishes the Blood, regulates menstruation and adjusts the Ying and Wei.
With Dang Gui, nourishes Yin and Blood.
Hb. Violae Zi Hua Di Ding 9g Clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity and clears hot sores.
With Pu Gong Ying, for breast abscesses, furuncles, boils and carbuncles.
Rz. Paridis Chong Lou 9g Drains Heat, relieves Fire toxicity, reduces swelling and inflammation and stops pain.
With Bai Hua She She Cao and Ban Zhi Lian, for snakebite.
Fr. Aurantii Zhi Ke 9g Promotes the movement of Qi, reduces distention and pressure and resolves hardenings.
With Yu Jin, for Liver Qi Stagnation with a stifling sensation in the chest and fullness in the epigastrium and abdomen with Heat signs and stabbing pain in the chest, flanks and abdomen due to Blood Stagnation.
Rx. Aucklandiae Mu Xiang 9g Promotes the movement of Qi and alleviates pain.
With Zhi Ke and Yan Hu Suo, for epigastric and abdominal pain and distention due to Qi Stagnation.
With Bai Shao, for diarrhea with profuse pus and Blood.
With Wu Yao, for lower abdominal Coldness and pain.
Rx. Linderae Wu Yao 9g Promotes movement of Qi, warms the Kidneys, disperses Cold and alleviates pain.
With Xiang Fu, unblocks Qi in both the Qi and Blood levels, releases constraint from the Liver and Gallbladder, descends Qi in the chest and abdomen and warms Qi which has been slowed by pathogenic Cold, painful, Cold Qi leading to abdominal distention, borborygmus and diarrhea and Qi and Blood Stagnation with menstrual irregularity, pain in the lower abdomen and generalized pain and distention throughout the body.
With Mu Xiang and Zhi Ke, for epigastric and abdominal pain and distention due to Cold Stagnation and Qi Obstruction.
With Mu Xiang, Xiang Fu and Zhi Ke, for dysmenorrhea due to Stagnant Qi.
With Dang Gui and Xiang Fu, for menstrual pain.
Disperses Cold and stops pain, for abdominal With Dang Gui, for pain due to External Cold.
Sm. Persicae Tao Ren 9g Breaks up Blood Stasis, invigorates Blood circulation and drains abscesses.
Rx. Curcumae Yu Jin 9g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, regulates Qi flow, speeds healing of chronic sores, clears Heat, cools the Blood and alleviates pain.
Rz. Corydalis Yan Hu Suo 6g Invigorates the Blood, circulates Qi and stops pain.
With Xiang Fu, for a wide range of applications from Liver Qi Stagnation to deep Blood Stasis and a wide variety of pain anywhere in the body including chest pain, distending pain in the breast, Cold pain in the epigastrium, pain due to bulging disorders in the lower body and dysmenorrhea from Qi and Blood Stagnation
With Xiang Fu and Dang Gui, for dysmenorrhea from Qi Stagnation and Blood Stasis.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Heat
  • Relieves Fire toxicity
  • Cools the Blood
  • Reduces abscesses
  • Dissipates nodules
  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Breaks up Stasis
  • Circulates Qi
  • Warms the Kidneys
  • Disperses Cold
  • Stops pain

SYNDROMES

  • Stomach cancer due to Qi and Blood Stagnation with Cold and Heat

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Epigastric pain
  • A hard, palpable lump in the epigastrium
  • Facial color dark and dull or pale
  • Emaciation
  • Sides and ribs distended on both sides (maybe with sharp pain)
  • Enlargement of the Liver and Spleen
  • Suffocating sensation in chest
  • Tendency to sigh
  • Vomit that looks like bean juice
  • Melancholy
  • Depression
  • Low back pain
  • Irritability
  • Black stool
  • Inappropriate anger
  • Mental fatigue
  • Irregular menses
  • Dysmenorrhea
  • Breast distention and pain before and during menses
  • T: Dark purple (maybe purple spots on edge of tongue) or Red
  • C: White
  • P: Wiry, choppy and slow or Small and choppy or Thready and rapid

TREATS

  • Stomach cancer
  • Esophageal cancer
  • Antral gastric cancer
