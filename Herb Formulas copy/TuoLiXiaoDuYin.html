HERB FORMULAS

TUO LI XIAO DU YIN - 托裡消毒飲 - DRAIN THE INTERIOR AND DETOXIFY DECOCTION

English: Drain the Interior and Detoxify Decoction
Also Known As: Support the Interior and Eliminate Toxin Drink
Expel Pus from Within Detoxifying Powder
Gleditsia Combination

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Ginseng Ren Shen 3g Tonifies Qi and strengthens the Spleen and Stomach.
With Bai Zhu, tonifies the transformation and transportation functions of the Spleen.
With Bai Zhu, Gan Cao and Fu Ling for Spleen and Stomach Deficiency with diarrhea, abdominal distention and fatigue.
Rz. Atractylodes Macrocephalae Bai Zhu 3g Tonifies Qi, strengthens the Spleen and dries Dampness.
With Huang Qi for Spleen Qi Deficiency with weakness, lassitude and loose stools.
With Fu Ling and Gan Cao, for Spleen and Stomach Qi Deficiency.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 1-2g Tonifies Qi, strengthens the Spleen, clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity and harmonizes.
With Bai Zhi for pain associated with ulcers.
With Jie Geng, for pain and swelling of the throat.
With Jin Yin Hua, for damp rashes or acne.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 3-5g Nourishes Blood, reduces swelling, expels pus and generates flesh.
With Huang Qi for ulcerated abscesses with insufficient drainage or draining only a clear thin discharge.
With Bai Shao, regulates and harmonizes Qi and Blood and treats Blood Deficiency Heat.
With Chuan Xiong, harmonizes, nourishes and invigorates the Blood and disperses Blood Stasis.
with Jin Yin Hua, reduces obstruction and toxicity for swollen, painful sores and necrosis of surrounding tissue.
Rx. Chuanxiong Chuan Xiong 3g Invigorates the Blood and promotes the movement of Qi.
With Dang Gui, Huang Qi and Zao Jiao Ci, for chronic non-healing sores with or without pus in deficient patients.
Rx. Paeoniae Alba Bai Shao 3g Nourishes Blood and preserves Yin.
With Dang Gui, nourishes Yin and Blood.
With Ren Shen and Huang Qi, restrains sweating from Deficiency.
Flos Lonicerae Jin Yin Hua 1-3g Clears Heat and relieves Fire toxicity.
With Zao Jiao Ci, for the initial stage of abscesses and boils.
With Gan Cao, for Toxic swelling and sores.
Rx. Angelicae Dahuricae Bai Zhi 1-2g Reduces swelling, expels pus, expels Dampness and alleviates discharge.
With Jie Geng and Bai Zhi, for ulcerated boils where pus is not discharged. With Gan Cao for pain associated with ulcers.
With Jin Yin Hua and Dang Gui, for early-stage sores and carbuncles.
Rx. Platycodi Jie Geng 2-3g Resolves Phlegm, promotes discharge of pus, aids suppuration and directs formula to the upper body.
With Bai Zhi for ulcerated boils where pus is not discharged.
With Gan Cao disseminates Lung Qi, expels Phlegm, resolves toxicity, and expels pus for productive cough with hoarseness, pain and swelling of the throat due to Wind-Heat.
Rx. Astragali Huang Qi 1.5-3g Tonifies Qi and Blood, nourishes the muscles, tonifies the Spleen, promotes the discharge of pus and generates flesh.
With Bai Zhu, for Spleen Qi Deficiency with weakness, lassitude and loose stools. With Dang Gui , for ulcerated abscesses with insufficient drainage or draining only a clear thin discharge.
With Ren Shen, for Qi Deficiency with general debility, anorexia, fatigue, tired extremities and spontaneous sweat.
Sp. Gleditsiae Zao Jiao Ci 2g Dispels Phlegm, dissipates Stagnation, reduces swelling and aids suppuration.
With Bai Zhi, for difficulty discharging pus from abscesses.
Poria Fu Ling 3-5g Tonifies Qi, strengthens the Spleen and transforms Phlegm.
With Ren Shen and Bai Zhu, for weak and deficient Spleen with fatigue, anorexia and loose stools.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Strengthens the Spleen
  • Tonifies Qi and Blood
  • Eliminates Dampness
  • Expels pus
  • Draws out toxicity
  • Promotes the generation of flesh

SYNDROMES

  • Spleen Deficiency with Accumulation of Pathogenic Dampness
  • Toxic sores in deficient patients

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Intermittent discharge of pus
  • The sores either do not suppurate or only leak pus slowly and fail to heal
  • Viscous, thin fluid
  • Mental and physical fatigue
  • Lassitude
  • Lack of luster
  • Poor appetite
  • Loose stools
  • Fever
  • T: Pale
  • C:Thin white
  • P:Thready and weak or Rapid and weak

TREATS

  • Chronic purulent otitis media
  • Suppurative lymphadenitis
  • Carbuncles
  • Gangrene
  • Otorrhea
  • Boils
  • Mastitis
  • Cellulitis
  • Herpes simplex
  • Gingivitis
  • Abscesses
  • Inflamed cysts
  • Bed sores
  • Otitis media
  • Conjunctivitis
  • Genital eruptions
  • Hordeolum
  • Diverticulitis
  • Herpes zoster
  • Periodontitis

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated during pregnancy because it contains Sp. Gleditsiae Zao Jiao Ci.
