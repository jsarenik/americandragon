HERB FORMULAS

BILE DUCT CANCER #1

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Hb. Cirsii Xiao Ji 15g Cools the Blood, stops bleeding, disperses Blood Stasis, resolves toxicity, reduces swelling and abscesses, clears Damp-Heat, promotes urination, promotes the function of the Gallbladder and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Spora Lygodii Hai Jin Sha 12g Clears Heat, promotes urination, unblocks painful urinary dysfunction, expels stones, eliminates Damp-Heat, reduces edema and decreases jaundice.
With Guang Jin Qian Cao, Yu Jin and Ji Nei Jin, for gallstones.
Hb. Artemisiae Scopariae Yin Chen Hao 12g Clears Damp-Heat from the Liver and Gallbladder, relieves jaundice and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Chai Hu, Guang Jin Qian Cao and Yu Jin, for cholecystitis or cholelithiasis.
Rx. Curcumae Yu Jin 12g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, regulates Qi flow, speeds healing of chronic sores, alleviates pain, clears Heat, cools the Blood, benefits the Gallbladder and reduces jaundice.
With Chai Hu, for Liver Qi and Blood Stagnation with flank pain and distention, dysmenorrhea and irregular menstruation.
With Guang Jin Qian Cao, for pain due to Stone Lin.
Endothelium Corneum Gigeriae Galli Ji Nei Jin 9g Transforms hardness, dissolves stones and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Guang Jin Qian Cao, for Urinary or biliary calculi.
Rx. Scutellariae Huang Qin 9g Clears Heat, dries Dampness, drains Fire, detoxifies, cools the Blood and stops bleeding and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Chai Hu, for Heat in the Shao Yang with alternating chills and fever and nausea.
Rx. Bupleuri Chai Hu 6g Reduces fever, spreads Liver Qi and relieves Stagnation.
With Huang Qin, restores the normal functioning of the Liver and Gallbladder.
With Gan Cao, for hepatitis and upper right quadrant pain.
Rx. Aucklandiae Mu Xiang 9g Promotes the movement of Qi, alleviates pain, dispels Damp-Heat and harmonizes the Liver and Spleen.
With Chai Hu, Yin Chen Hao and Yu Jin, for Damp-Heat causing flank pain and distention and a bitter taste in the mouth.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 6g Clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity, inhibits the growth of cancer cells, moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs and guides the herbs to all twelve channels
Hb. Desmodii Styracifolii Guang Jin Qian Cao 30g Clears Heat, promotes urination, unblocks painful urinary dysfunction and expels stones.
Rx. Adinae Shui Yang Mei 30g Dispels Heat, relieves toxicity and relieves pain

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Cools the Blood
  • Stops bleeding
  • Disperses Blood Stasis
  • Resolves toxicity
  • Reduces swelling and abscesses
  • Clears Damp-Heat
  • Promotes urination
  • Promotes the function of the Gallbladder
  • Inhibits the growth of cancer cells

SYNDROMES

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Bile duct cancer
  • High fever
  • Diarrhea
  • Upper abdominal pain
  • Discomfort in the upper abdomen
  • Anorexia
  • Nausea
  • Vomiting
  • Weight loss
  • Melaena
  • Epigastric focal distention
  • Fullness and tightness with very slight or no pain
  • Dry heaves
  • Borborygmus
  • Diarrhea
  • Edema
  • Ascites
  • Anemia
  • Local redness
  • Swelling and pain
  • Maybe fever and chills
  • Hepatomegaly
  • Abdominal tumor
  • The tumor is firm and irregular
  • Subcardiac distention
  • Heartburn
  • Resistance below the Heart
  • Soft epigastrium
  • Ascites
  • T: Normal or with a red tip
  • C: Thin, yellow and greasy
  • P: Wiry and rapid or Wiry, tight and rapid

TREATS

  • Cancer of the bile duct
