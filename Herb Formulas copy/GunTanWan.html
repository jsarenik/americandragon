HERB FORMULAS

GUN TAN WAN - 滾痰丸 - VAPORIZE PHLEGM PILL - LAPIS AND SCUTE FORMULA

English: Vaporize Phlegm Pill
Also Known As: Meng Shi Gun Tan Wan
Lapis and Scute Formula
Flushing Away Roiling Phlegm Pill

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Calcined Lapis Chloriti Duan Meng Shi 3-12g Drives out Phlegm, especially stubborn or recurrent Phlegm, descends Qi, calms wheezing and eliminates hidden accumulations of Phlegm.
With Huang Qin and Da Huang, for old, stubborn Phlegm causing wheezing, coughing, palpitations with anxiety, mania and constipation.
Rx. and Rz. Rhei Da Huang 9-24g Clears Heat, eliminates Phlegm and unblocks obstruction from the Yang organs.
Rx. Scutellariae Huang Qin 9-24g Clears Upper Jiao Fire to prevent scorching the Fluids and stops bleeding.
With Da Huang, for constipation.
Lignum Aquilariae Resinatum Chen Xiang 1.5-4.5g Regulates Qi, rapidly descends Qi and removes Phlegm.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Drains Fire
  • Drives out Phlegm

SYNDROMES

  • Phlegm-Fire Disturbs the Heart
  • Phlegm-Heat in the Liver and Gallbladder

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Do not take as a decoction.
  • Contraindicated for those with a weak constitution.
  • Contraindicated during pregnancy.
  • Contraindicated postpartum.
  • Contraindicated for those with a lack of Heat with stubborn Phlegm.
