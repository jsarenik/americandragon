HERB FORMULAS

AUTISM #2

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Pseudostellariae Tai Zi Shen 12g Strengthens the Spleen, tonifies Qi and generates Body Fluids.
With Wu Wei Zi, nourishes Fluids.
Rx. Astragali Huang Qi 12g Tonifies Qi and Blood, strengthens the Spleen, raises the Yang Qi of the Spleen and Stomach and generates Body Fluids.
With Bai Zhu, for Spleen Qi Deficiency with weakness, lassitude and loose stools.
With Dang Gui, for Qi and Blood Deficiency.
With Fu Xiao Mai, for spontaneous sweating due to Qi Deficiency and nightsweats due to Yin Deficiency.
Poria Fu Ling 9g Promotes urination, leaches out Dampness , quiets the Heart, calms the Spirit and soothes the nerves.
With Bai Zhu, strengthens the Spleen and resolves Dampness.
With Gan Cao, for Spleen and Heart Deficiency causing facial edema, palpitations, and shortness of breath.
With Yuan Zhi, for insomnia and palpitations due to Spleen and Heart Insufficiency or Phlegm Obstructing the Heart.
Rz. Atractylodis Macrocephalae Bai Zhu 9g Tonifies the Spleen, augments Qi, dries Dampness, promotes water metabolism, stabilizes the Exterior and stops sweating.
With Huang Qi and Fu Xiao Mai, for unrelenting spontaneous sweat.
With Fu Ling and Gan Cao, for Spleen and Stomach Qi Deficiency.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 9g Tonifies, activates and harmonizes the Blood.
Rx. Polygalae Yuan Zhi 9g Calms the Spirit, sedates the Heart, clears the channel joining the Heart and Kidney, expels Phlegm and opens the Orifices.
Rx. Acori Tatarinowii Shi Chang Pu 9g Opens the Orifices, vaporizes Phlegm, calms the Spirit and promotes Blood flow of Qi and Blood.
With Yuan Zhi, for forgetfulness and dulled senses.
With Yuan Zhi and Fu Ling, for forgetfulness, tinnitus and impaired hearing.
Rx. Schisandrae Wu Wei Zi 9g Tonifies Qi, tonifies the Kidneys, astringes Jing, quiets the Spirit and calms and contains Heart Qi.
With Huang Qi, for Yang Deficiency with spontaneous sweat.
Fr. Tritici Levis Fu Xiao Mai 15g Stops excessive sweating due to Deficiency, nourishes the Heart and calms the Spirit.
With Da Zao and Gan Cao, for emotional disorders, disorientation, emotional instability and irritability due to mild Deficiency of the Spleen Qi and Heart Blood.
Fr. Jujube Da Zao 20g Tonifies the Spleen and Stomach, augments Qi, nourishes the Blood and calms the Spirit.
With Gan Cao and Da Zao, for Restless (Dry) Organ Disorder.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 6g Tonifies the Spleen, augments Qi, resolves Phlegm, moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs and guides the herbs to all twelve channels.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Tonifies Qi and Blood
  • Removes Phlegm Obstruction of the Orifices
  • Calms the Shen

SYNDROMES

  • Heart Blood Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Changeable interests
  • Divided attention
  • Amnesia
  • Dreaminess
  • Stuttering
  • Problems in formation of phrases and sentences
  • Shyness
  • Inattention
  • Depression
  • Mood swings
  • Obsessions
  • Palpitations
  • Irritability
  • Anxiety
  • Easily startled
  • Insomnia
  • Much dreaming
  • Poor memory
  • Dizziness
  • Pale face and lips
  • T: Pale
  • C: White
  • P: Thready and weak

TREATS

  • Autism
