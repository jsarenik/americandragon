HERB FORMULAS

ZHONG CONG PING YUAN TANG - 重蒼冯源汤 - HEAVY DOSAGE XANTHIUM DEEP-SOURCE DECOCTION

English: Heavy Dosage Xanthium Deep-Source Decoction
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Fr. Xanthii Cang Er Zi 30g Disperses Wind and Dampness and opens the nasal passages.
With Xin Yi Hua, for chronic sinusitis.
With Xin Yi Hua and Bai Zhi, for headaches and nasal obstruction.
With Xin Yi Hua, Shi Gao and Bai Zhi, for acute Wind-Heat patterns.
Fr. Forsythiae Lian Qiao 20g Clears Heat, especially in the Upper Jiao, and relieves toxicity.
Rx. Scrophulariae Xuan Shen 20g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, nourishes Yin, drains Fire and relieves toxicity.
With Jie Geng and Gan Cao, for sore throat with Dryness and pain.
Cx. Mori Sang Bai Pi 20g Drains Heat from the Lungs, stops coughing and calms wheezing.
Rx. Platycodi Jie Geng 18g Opens the Lungs, spreads Lung Qi, expels Phlegm and directs the effects of other herbs to the upper body.
With Gan Cao, disseminates Lung Qi, expels Phlegm, resolves toxicity and expels pus for Excess disorders in the Upper Jiao with no disorder in the Lower Jiao.
Gypsum Fibrosum Shi Gao 15g Clears Heat in the Qi Stage, drains Fire, relieves irritability, quenches thirst and clears Excess Heat from the Lungs.
Cx. Moutan Mu Dan Pi 15g Clears Heat and cools and harmonizes the Blood.
Hb. Agastaches seu Pogostemmi Huo Xiang 15g Aromatically transforms Dampness and transforms turbidity.
Rx. Angelicae Dahuricae Bai Zhi 12g Expels Wind, eliminates Dampness, unblocks the nasal passages, dispels Cold, reduces swelling, eliminates toxins, expels pus and alleviates pain.
With Xin Yi Hua and Cang Er Zi, for headaches due to nasal congestion with thick putrid discharge.
Flos Magnoliae Xin Yi Hua 12g Expels Wind-Cold and unblocks the nasal passages.
With Cang Er Zi, strongly unblocks nasal obstruction and sinus headache due to Wind-Cold or Wind-Heat for sinusitis and rhinitis with loss of sense of smell.
Hb. Schizonepetae Jing Jie 9g Releases the Exterior and expels Wind.
With Jie Geng and Gan Cao, for acute coughing associated with External disorders.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 9g Moistens the Lungs, resolves Phlegm, stops coughing, clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity, moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs and guides the herbs to all twelve channels.
Hb. Ephedrae Ma Huang 6g Induces sweating, releases the Exterior, disseminates and facilitates Lung Qi, calms wheezing, stops coughing and warms and disperses Cold pathogens.
With Shi Gao, for coughing from Heat Obstructing the Lungs with wheezing, flaring of the nostrils and fever.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Disperses Wind
  • Eliminates Dampness
  • Clears Heat
  • Unblocks the nasal passages

SYNDROMES

  • Acute sinusitis due to Wind-Damp-Heat

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Acute sinusitis

TREATS

  • Acute sinusitis

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Use for only a short period of time.
HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

NOTES

MODIFICATIONS

For fever and chills due to Wind-Heat: For irascibility and a bitter taste in the mouth due to Liver Fire:
+ 9g Rx. Scutellariae Huang Qin
+ 9g Flos Lonicerae Jin Yin Hua + 9g Rx. Gentianae Long Dan Cao
For mouth sores, dry stools and thirst due to Stomach Fire: + 9g Fr. Gardeniae Zhi Zi
   
+ 3-6g Rx. et Rz. Rhei Da Huang    
+ 3-6g Natrii Sulfas Mang Xiao
