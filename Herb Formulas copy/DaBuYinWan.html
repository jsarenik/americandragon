HERB FORMULAS

DA BU YIN WAN - 大補陰丸 - GREAT TONIFY THE YIN PILL

English: Great Tonify the Yin Pill
Also Known As: Da Bu Wan
Major Yin Supplementation Pill

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Rehmanniae Preparata Shu Di Huang 6-30g Tonifies the Liver and Kidneys and nourishes Yin.
 With Gui Ban, controls Kidney Fire.
With Gui Ban, Huang Bai and Zhi Mu, enhances formula’s ability to treat Deficiency Heat.
Deep-fried Plastrum Testudinis Su Jiu Gui Ban 6-30g Nourishes Yin, anchors Yang and benefits the Kidneys.
Deep-fried Cx. Phellodendri Chao Huang Bai 4-20g Drains Damp-Heat especially from the lower Jiao and drains Kidney Fire (False Heat).
Wine-fried Rx. Anemarrhenae Jiu Chao Zhi Mu 4-20g Clears Heat, drains Fire, nourishes Yin, moistens Dryness, nourishes the Lungs and generates Fluids.
With Chao Huang Bai, clears Heat drains Fire, treats Yin Deficiency with night sweats, dizziness and vertigo, and nourishes and protects Yin.
Pig Spinal Cord Zhu Ji Shui 25-50g Strengthens the bones and marrow and cools the Blood.
(Apis Mel) (Feng Mi)   (Moderates the bitter, drying properties of Huang Bai.)

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Nourishes Yin
  • Descends Fire

SYNDROMES

  • Liver Yin Deficiency with Yang Rising
  • Lung and Kidney Yin Deficiency
  • Liver and Kidney Yin Deficiency (with Fire Flaring)
  • Atrophy disorder (Wei Syndrome) due to Liver and Kidney Deficiency

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with Excess Fire.
  • Use caution with poor appetite and loose stools.
  • Avoid foods that are acrid and spicy.
HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

NOTES
