HERB FORMULAS

YI HAO XUAN JI (TOPICAL)

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Realgar Xiong Huang 0.15-0.36g Relieves toxicity.
With Huang Bai and Bing Pian, topically for scabies and eczema.
With Peng Sha, for ulcerations of the mouth, tongue and throat.
With Ming Fan, topically for nasal polyps, Wind-Damp sores or tinea itching or itching from dermatosis and sores with redness, swelling, itching and pain.
With Bing Pain, for herpes zoster.
Resina Pini Song Xiang 0.5-1g Dries Dampness, dispels Wind, alleviates pain, discharges pus, removes toxins and generates flesh.
Fossilia Ossis Mastodi Long Gu 15-30g Topically generates muscles and astringes ulcers.
With Ming Fan, topically as a powder for chronic non-healing sores and ulcers.
Calomelas Qing Fen 0.1-0.2g Strongly kills parasites, generates flesh, resolves toxicity and promotes the healing of sores.
Borax Peng Sha 1.5-6g Relieves toxicity and prevents putrefication.
With Bing Pian and Ming Fan, for swelling and ulcerations of the mouth, tongue, and throat.
Alumen Ming Fan 0.6-1.5g Relieves toxicity, dries Dampness, kills parasites, alleviates itching, restrains and inhibits sores, generates flesh and transforms putrefication.
Fol. Ilexis Dong Qing Ye 10-30g Dispels Heat and Toxins from the skin, cools the Blood and stops bleeding (topically).
Borneolum Bing Pian 0.01-0.9g Clears Heat, drains Fire, resolves toxicity,alleviates pain, dissipates nodules and alleviates itching.
Mel Feng Mi 15-30g Eliminates toxins.
Cx. Phellodendri Huang Bai 3-12g Drains Fire and relieves Fire toxicity topically.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Heat
  • Resolves toxicity
  • Alleviates pain
  • Reduces swelling
  • Generates flesh
  • Promotes healing

SYNDROMES

  • Toxic Heat

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Cervicitis
  • Cervical polyps
  • Profuse, yellow, sticky discharge
  • In some patients the discharge is white but like bean curd
  • Foul odor
  • Vulvar itch
  • Scanty dark urine
  • Chest tightness
  • Sticky mouth
  • Reduced appetite
  • Vague pains in the lower abdomen
  • T: Red
  • C: Yellow and greasy or Thick and yellow
  • P: Soft and mildly rapid

TREATS

  • Cervicitis (mild erosion)
  • Cervical polyps

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Do not use internally.
HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

NOTES

  • Apply topically.
