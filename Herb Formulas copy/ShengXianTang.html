HERB FORMULAS

SHENG XIAN TANG - 升陷汤 - RAISE THE SUNKEN DECOCTION

English: Raise the Sunken Decoction
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Astragali Huang Qi 18-30g Tonifies Qi and Blood, strengthens the Spleen, raises the Yang Qi of the Spleen and Stomach and tonifies the Lungs.
 With Sheng Ma, raises the Yang Qi of the Spleen, allowing turbid Yin to descend and be eliminated, treating chronic diarrhea, a dragging sensation in the abdomen, fatigue, shortness of breath, uterine bleeding and prolapses.
With Sheng Ma and Chai Hu, for Spleen Qi Sinking with rectal and/or uterine prolapse or excessive uterine bleeding.
Rz. Anemarrhenae Zhi Mu 9g Clears Heat, drains Fire, nourishes Yin, moistens Dryness and counteracts the Heat generated by Huang Qi.
Rx. Bupleuri Chai Hu 4.5-9g Spreads Liver Qi, relieves Stagnation and raises Yang Qi.
With Huang Qi, raises the Clear Yang due to abnormal descent of Qi.
Rx. Platycodi Jie Geng 3-4.5g Opens the Lungs, spreads Lung Qi, and leads Qi to the chest.
Rz. Cimicifugae Sheng Ma 3-9g Raises Yang and lifts Sunken Qi.
With Chai Hu and Huang Qi, for prolapse of internal organs.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Tonifies Qi
  • Lifts prolapse
  • Raises sunken Qi in the chest

SYNDROMES

  • Sinking of Gathering Qi in the chest

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Shortness of breath
  • Severe dyspnea
  • Rapid breathing
  • P: Deep, slow and frail

TREATS

  • Cardiovascular disorders
  • Myasthenia gravis
