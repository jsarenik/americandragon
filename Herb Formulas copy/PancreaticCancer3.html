HERB FORMULAS

PANCREATIC CANCER #3

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Concha Ostreae Mu Li 35g Softens hardness, dissipates nodules and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Spica Prunellae Xia Ku Cao 20g Clears Liver Fire, clears Hot Phlegm, dissipates nodules and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.|
With Chai Hu, for neck nodules due to Liver Qi Stagnation.
With Xian Fu and Chai Hu, for Liver Qi Stagnation.
Thallus Eckloniae Kun Bu 15g Resolves Phlegm, softens Phlegm nodules, and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Sargassum Hai Zao 15g Reduces Phlegm,d softens areas of hardness and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Kun Bu, for goiter and scrofula.
Rz. Cyperi Xiang Fu 15g Spreads and regulates Liver Qi and alleviates pain.
With Chai Hu, for Qi Stagnation.
Fr. Aurantii Zhi Ke 15g Promotes the movement of Qi, reduces distention and pressure and resolves hardenings.
With Jie Gang, regulates the flow of Qi in the chest and aids in the transformation of Phlegm for, chest congestion.
Sm. Persicae Tao Ren 15g Breaks up Blood Stasis and invigorates Blood circulation.
Rx. Scutellariae Huang Qin 15g Clears Heat, dries Dampness, drains Fire, detoxifies and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Chai Hu, for Heat in the Shao Yang with alternating chills and fever and nausea.
With Xia Ku Cao, for dizziness and headache due to extreme rebellion of Liver Fire.
With Xia Ku Cao and Mu Li, for scrofula due to accumulated obstruction of Liver Fire.
Rx. Bupleuri Chai Hu 15g Spreads Liver Qi, relieves Stagnation, disperses Wind-Heat and resolves Phlegm and congestion.
With Huang Qin, restores the normal functioning of the Liver and Gallbladder.
With Huang Qin and Zhi Ban Xia, for Shao Yang disorders with alternating chills and fever and a sensation of fullness in the hypochondria and chest.
With Zhi Ke, for a stifling sensation in the chest, abdominal pain, poor appetite and irregular bowel movements.
Rz. Pinelliae Preparatum Zhi Ban Xia 15g Dries Dampness, transforms Phlegm, harmonizes the Stomach, dissipates nodules, reduces Stagnation and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Xia Ku Cao, for Phlegm-Heat induced insomnia.
With Huang Qin, for Phlegm-Heat induced Rebellious Qi with cough, nausea, and vomiting.
Flos Carthami Hong Hua 10g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, opens the channels, alleviates pain and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Tao Ren, invigorates the Blood for all types of Blood Stagnation such as dysmenorrhea, amenorrhea, swollen painful sores and chest and abdominal pain.
Rx. Platycodi Jie Geng 10g Opens the Lungs, spreads Lung Qi, expels Phlegm and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Circulates Qi
  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Breaks up Stasis
  • Eliminates Phlegm

SYNDROMES

  • Qi Blood and Phlegm Stagnation

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Facial color dark and dull
  • Emaciation
  • Sides and ribs distended on both sides (maybe with sharp pain)
  • Enlargement of the Liver and Spleen
  • Suffocating sensation in chest
  • Tendency to sigh
  • Melancholy
  • Depression
  • Irritability
  • Inappropriate anger
  • Irregular menses
  • Dysmenorrhea
  • Breast distention and pain before and during menses
  • Anorexia
  • Edema
  • Abdominal distention
  • Chest and hypochondriac distention
  • Dizziness
  • Nausea
  • Vomiting
  • Thirst with no desire to drink
  • Maybe no thirst
  • Palpitations
  • Shortness of Breath
  • T: Varies from Normal to Dark purple (maybe purple spots on edge of tongue)
  • C: Thick and slippery
  • P: Wiry, choppy and slow or Slippery, full and maybe wiry

TREATS

  • Pancreatic cystadenoma
