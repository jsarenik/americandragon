HERB FORMULAS

BAI HU GUI ZHI TANG - 白虎加桂枝湯 - WHITE TIGER PLUS CINNAMON TWIG DECOCTION

English: White Tiger Plus Cinnamon Twig Decoction
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Gypsum Fibrosum Recens Sheng Shi Gao 20-48g Clears Heat, drains Fire, clears excess Heat from the Lungs, clears Blazing Stomach Fire, nourishes Body Fluids and stops thirst.
With Zhi Mu, for high fever from Wind-Heat, Qi Stage Heat or Yang Ming Heat with irritability and extreme thirst.
Rz. Anemarrhenae Zhi Mu 9-18g Clears Heat, drains Fire, Nourishes Yin, moistens Dryness and generates fluids.
Ram. Cinnamomi Gui Zhi 5-9g Releases external Wind-Cold from the muscle layer, harmonizes the Ying and Wei, warms the channels and collaterals, relieves pain, disperse, Cold, unblocks Yang, transforms Qi, warms and facilitates the flow of Yang Qi in the chest and warms and facilitates the flow of Blood through the vessels.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Preparata Zhi Gan Cao 3-6g Alleviates pain, and moderates and harmonizes the properties of the other herbs.
Sm. Oryzae Geng Mi 6-30g Tonifies Yang, tonifies Qi, stops restlessness, stops thirst and generates fluids.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Heat
  • Generates Fluids
  • Stops pain

SYNDROMES

  • Wind-Damp-Heat Bi
  • Warm malarial disorders with intense Internal Heat and mild External Cold

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Fever maybe high
  • Maybe slight chills
  • Perspiration
  • Nervousness
  • Thirst
  • Irritability
  • Arthralgia involving one or several joints
  • Dry mouth and tongue
  • Localized redness and Heat
  • Swelling of the joints with burning pain
  • Severe limitation of movement
  • Aversion to Heat
  • Restlessness
  • Flushed face
  • Desire for cold drinks
  • Pain worse at night
  • Pain relieved by Cold
  • Occasional vomiting
  • T: Pink or Red
  • C: White or Yellow
  • P: Wiry and rapid or Slippery and rapid or Big and forceful

TREATS

  • Rheumatoid arthritis
  • Rheumatism
  • Arthritis
  • Perspiration
  • Brachial plexus neuritis
  • Wrist and hand pain
  • Rheumatic fever
  • Hip pain
  • Warm malarial disorders
  • Arthralgia
  • Fever
  • Scapulohumeral periarthritis
  • Gout
  • Toe Pain
  • Whole body pain
