HERB FORMULAS

LUNG CANCER #62

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Bul. Lilii Bai He 15g Nourishes Yin, moistens the Lungs, clears Heat, stops coughing and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 15g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, nourishes Yin, generates fluids and increases saliva.
With Mai Men Dong, Bei Sha Shen and Nan Sha Shen, for a dry mouth, thirst, dry lips and a red tongue due to Stomach Yin Deficiency.
Flos Lonicerae Jin Yin Hua 15g Clears Heat, resolves Fire toxicity and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Huang Qin and Yi Yi Ren, for intestinal abscess.
Rx. Adenophorae Nan Sha Shen 12g Nourishes Yin, moistens the Lungs, dispels Phlegm and stops coughing.
Rx. Glehniae Bei Sha Shen 12g Nourishes Lung Yin and clears Lung Heat.
Rx. Asparagi Tian Men Dong 12g Nourishes Kidney Yin, clears Lung Heat, sedates Fire, moistens the Lungs, nourishes the Kidneys, generates Fluids, resolves Phlegm and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Mai Men Dong, for a dry cough, thirst and other symptoms due to Yin Deficiency and injured fluids.
With Bai Hua She She Cao, for Lung cancer, breast cancer, lymphatic cancer and malignant lymphoma.
Rx. Ophiopogonis Mai Men Dong 12g Moistens the Lungs, nourishes Yin and stops coughing.
With Tian Men Dong, for Lung and Kidney Yin Deficiency with consumptive coughing and occasional hemoptysis.
With Bei Sha Shen and Nan Sha Shen, for a dry cough with little Phlegm and a parched throat due to Lung Dryness or Heat injuring the Lung Yin.
With Sheng Di Huang, for hematemesis or epistaxis due to Yin Deficiency with Excess Heat.
Rz. Imperatae Bai Mao Gen 30g Cools the Blood, stops bleeding, promotes urination, clears Heat from the Stomach and Lungs and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Scutellariae Huang Qin 9g Clears Heat, dries Dampness, drains Fire, detoxifies, cools the Blood, stops bleeding and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Hb. Hedyotis Diffusae Bai Hua She She Cao 30g Clears Heat, strongly relieves Fire toxins, reduces abscesses, promotes urination and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Tian Men Dong, for breast cancer and lymphatic cancer.
Hb. Houttuyniae Yu Xing Cao 30g Clears Heat, relieves toxicity, reduces swellings and abscesses, expels pus, drains Damp-Heat and promotes urination and inhibits the growth of cancer cells (acts especially on the Lungs).
Fol. Cycadis Revolutae Feng Wei Jiao Ye 15g Regulates Qi, invigorates the Blood, breaks up Blood Stasis, dispels Wind, eliminates toxins and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Sm. Coicis Yi Yi Ren 15g Strengthens the Spleen, resolves Dampness, clears Heat, expels pus and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Per. Citri Reticulatae Chen Pi 9g Regulates Qi, adjusts the Middle, relieves the diaphragm and dries Dampness.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Nourishes Lung Yin
  • Clears Heat
  • Resolves toxicity
  • Stops bleeding
  • Eliminates Dampness
  • Moves Qi
  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Inhibits the growth of cancer cells

SYNDROMES

  • Lung Yin Deficiency due to Lung Heat

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Cough with little yellow sputum
  • Spitting saliva
  • Wheezing
  • Shortness of breath
  • Dry, uncomfortable sensation in throat
  • Hiccups
  • Dry mouth
  • Coughing up difficult-to-extectorate Phlegm
  • Scanty urination
  • Hoarseness
  • Thirst
  • Asthma
  • Flushed face
  • Five sole Heat
  • Maybe a high fever
  • No chills
  • Aversion to Heat
  • Cough
  • Asthma
  • Restlessness
  • Thirst
  • Dark yellow urine
  • Dyspnea
  • Chest pain
  • Thick yellow Phlegm
  • T: Red and dry
  • C: Thick and yellow, maybe dry and sticky
  • P:  Rapid, maybe slippery, maybe forceful or Thready and rapid or Deficient and rapid

TREATS

  • Lung cancer
