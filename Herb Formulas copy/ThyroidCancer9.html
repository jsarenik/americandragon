HERB FORMULAS

THYROID CANCER #9

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Spica Prunellae Xia Ku Cao 15g Clears Liver Fire, clears Hot Phlegm, dissipates nodules and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Zhe Bei Mu, cools and relieves toxicity to disperse and transform Phlegm and treat goiter, scrofula and swollen or inflamed lymph glands.
Flos Lonicerae Jin Yin Hua 30g Clears Heat, resolves Fire toxicity and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Lian Qiao, for various Heat disorders.
With Gan Cao, for toxic swellings and sores.
Fr. Forsythiae Lian Qiao 15g Clears Heat especially in the Upper Jiao, relieves toxicity, reduces abscesses, dissipates clumps and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Xia Ku Cao, Zhe Bei Mu and Xuan Shen, for scrofula and tuberculosis adenitis.
Rx. Scrophulariae Xuan Shen 15g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, nourishes Yin, softens hardness, dissipates nodules, transforms Phlegm-Heat, drains Fire and relieves toxicity.
With Mu Li, Zhe Bei Mu and Xia Ku Cao, for scrofula, goiter and other nodules due to Phlegm.
With Jin Yin Hua and Gan Cao, for boils, carbuncles and furuncles.
Bul. Fritillariae Thunbergii Zhe Bei Mu 10g Clears Heat, transforms Phlegm, clears Heat, dissipates nodules and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Lian Qiao, clears Heat toxin, transforms turbid Phlegm, opens Stagnation and disperses clumped swellings to treat cough with thick yellow sputum, scrofula and goiter from Phlegm-Fire.
With Xuan Shen and Mu Li, for nodules without abscess.
Concha Ostreae Mu Li 15g Softens hardness, dissipates nodules and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rz. Dioscoreae Bulbiferae Huang Yao Zi 10g Resolves Phlegm, dissipates nodules, reduces masses, clears Heat, reduces toxicity and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Thallus Eckloniae Kun Bu 15g Resolves Phlegm, softens Phlegm nodules and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Xia Ku Cao, for goiter.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 10g Clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Heat
  • Relieves Toxicity
  • Transforms Phlegm-Heat
  • Dissipates nodules
  • Inhibits the growth of cancer cells

SYNDROMES

  • Toxic Phlegm-Heat

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Mouth erosion, ulceration or corrosion
  • Tumor develops quickly
  • Long-standing illness
  • Pain
  • Redness
  • Ulceration
  • Discharge of yellow or bloody, foul-smelling fluid
  • Fatigue
  • Fever
  • Anxiety
  • Dry mouth
  • Thirst
  • Dark urine
  • Bitter taste
  • Red eyes
  • Cough with sticky, yellow, difficult to expectorate sputum
  • Insomnia
  • Rapid breathing with a harsh voice
  • Restless fever
  • Sore throat
  • Constipation
  • Dry stools
  • Mania
  • Shortness of breath
  • Dyspnea
  • Nausea
  • Vomiting
  • Chest and/or epigastric fullness
  • Pain in the ribs
  • T: Scarlet
  • C: Yellow or Yellow and greasy or Yellow and smooth
  • P: Rapid and smooth or Slippery and rapid

TREATS

  • Late-stage thyroid cancer with mouth erosion
