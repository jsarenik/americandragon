HERB FORMULAS

WU PI YIN - 五皮飲 - FIVE PEEL DECOCTION

English: Five Peel Decoction
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Cx. Lycii Di Gu Pi 9-15g Cools the Blood, drains Yin Deficiency Fire, drains Floating Fire in the Kidney channel and clears and drains Heat in the Lung.
Cx. Zingiberis Recens Sheng Jiang Pi 3-9g Promotes urination and reduces edema.
Cx. Poria Cocos Fu Ling Pi 9-15g Promotes urination, resolves Dampness, strengthens the Spleen and harmonizes the Middle Jiao.
Cx. Acanthopanacis Wu Jia Pi 6-9g Dispels Wind-Dampness, nourishes the Liver and Kidneys, strengthens sinews and bones, transforms Dampness and reduces swelling.
With Da Fu Pi and Fu Ling Pi, for mild superficial edema.
Per. Arecae Da Fu Pi 9-15g Promotes movement of Qi, descends Qi, expels Dampness, promotes urination and reduces edema.
With Sheng Jiang Pi and Fu Ling Pi, for mild edema or superficial facial edema.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Resolves Dampness
  • Reduces edema
  • Regulates Qi
  • Strengthens the Kidneys
  • Reduces swelling

SYNDROMES

  • Spleen Deficiency with vigorous Dampness and Qi Stagnation (Pi Shui - Skin Water)

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Generalized edema
  • Sensation of heaviness, distention and fullness in the epigastrium and abdomen
  • Labored, heavy breathing
  • Urinary difficulty
  • Asthma
  • Chest distention
  • Edema during pregnancy
  • General edema in summer
  • Gasping
  • Rapid breathing
  • T: Normal
  • C: White and slippery
  • P: Thready and moderate or slippery

TREATS

  • Edema during pregnancy
  • Protein-deficiency edema
  • Ascites from cirrhosis of the Liver
  • Congestive Heart failure
  • Urticaria
  • Obesity
  • Gasping
  • Whole body edema
  • Menopausal edema
  • Cardiac edema
  • Nephritic edema
  • Dropsy
  • Nephritis
  • Abdominal fullness and distention
  • Edema
  • Kidney failure

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • For those with severe Kidney Deficiency use Kidney tonifying herbs.
