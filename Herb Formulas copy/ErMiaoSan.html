HERB FORMULAS

ER MIAO SAN - 二妙散 - TWO MARVEL POWDER

English: Two-Marvel Powder
Also Known As: Powder of Two Effective Ingredients

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Cx. Phellodendri Huang Bai 6-15g Drains Damp-Heat, especially from the Lower Jiao and drains Kidney Fire.
Rz. Atractylodis Cang Zhu 6-15g Strongly dries Dampness, tonifies the Spleen, and clears Dampness from the Lower Jiao.
With Huang Bai, for pain, swelling, and weakness of the lower extremities due to Wind-Dampness.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Eliminates Heat and Dampness

SYNDROMES

  • Damp-Heat in the Lower Jiao
  • Low back pain due to Heat

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Pain in low back and knees
  • Aches and pains in the bones and sinews
  • Weakness or atrophy of lower extremities
  • Scanty yellow urine
  • Red, hot, swollen and painful feet or knees
  • Edema in lower legs
  • Leukorrhea (thick, yellow, foul-smelling)
  • Sores on lower extremities due to Damp
  • Redness, swelling and pain of the knees with burning sensations
  • Oliguria
  • Eczema
  • T: Normal
  • C: Yellow and greasy
  • P: Soft and and rapid

  • Vaginitis

  • Foot pain
