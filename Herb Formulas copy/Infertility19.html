HERB FORMULAS

INFERTIITY #19

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Asparagi Tian Men Dong 5-15g Nourishes Kidney Yin, clears Lung Heat and sedates Fire.
Rx. Ophiopogonis Mai Men Dong 6-15g Nourishes Lung and Stomach Yin, generates Fluids, clears the Heart and eliminates irritability.
With Tian Men Dong, for Lung and Kidney Yin Deficiency with consumptive coughing and occasional hemoptysis.
Rz. Acori Tatarinowii Shi Chang Pu 1.5-10g Opens the Orifices, calms the Spirit, transforms turbid Dampness, harmonizes the Middle Jiao, awakens the Spleen, promotes Blood flow and promotes the movement of Qi.
With Yuan Zhi and Fu Ling, for forgetfulness, tinnitus and impaired hearing.
Poria Fu Ling 9-18g Promotes urination, leaches out Dampness and strengthens the Spleen.
With Shu Chang Pu and Yuan Zhi, for insomnia and palpitations due to Spleen and Heart Insufficiency or Phlegm Obstructing the Heart.
Rx. Codonopsis Dang Shen 6-14g Tonifies the Middle Jiao, augments Qi, nourishes the Blood and promotes the generation of Body Fluids.
Fr. Alpiniae Oxyphyllae Yi Zhi Ren 3-10g Warms the Kidneys and astringes Jing and urine.
Fr. Lycii Gou Qi Zi 5-18g Nourishes and tonifies Liver and Kidney Blood and Yin and benefits Jing.
Cx. Lycii Di Gu Pi 6-15g Cools the Blood and drains Yin Deficiency Fire.
Rx. Polygalae Yuan Zhi 3-15g Calms the Spirit, sedates the Heart and clears the channel joining the Heart and Kidney.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Nourishes the Kidney Yin
  • Generates Blood
  • Regulates the Chong and Ren channels
  • Supports the Liver
  • Calms the Shen
  • Augments Jing

SYNDROMES

  • Kidney Yin Deficiency with severe Blood Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Long-term infertility
  • Early menstruation
  • Possibly a history of late menarche
  • Irregular periods
  • Infrequent menstruation
  • Decreased menses with light colored Blood and no blood clots
  • Delayed cycle
  • Scanty periods
  • Pale Blood
  • Maybe copious, watery menses
  • Amenorrhea
  • Loss of libido
  • Emaciation
  • Dizziness
  • Night sweating
  • Blurred vision
  • Palpitations
  • Insomnia
  • Tinnitus
  • Hearing loss
  • A weak lower body
  • Five Sole Heat or
  • Low-grade fever in the afternoon
  • Back soreness
  • Aches and weakness in the waist and legs
  • Dry mouth
  • Dry bowl movements
  • Dry vagina
  • Cold limbs
  • A dusky complexion
  • Dull-pale complexion
  • Insidious pain after menstruation
  • Tiredness
  • Withered, sallow complexion
  • Depression
  • Palpitations
  • Breathlessness
  • Pale complexion
  • Blurred vision
  • Insomnia
  • Poor memory
  • Dry skin
  • Constipation
  • A thin, weak body
  • T: Red or Pale red or Pale and thin
  • C: Little or None or Thin and white
  • P: Thready and rapid or Floating and Empty or Choppy or Fine or Thin and soft

TREATS

  • Infertility
