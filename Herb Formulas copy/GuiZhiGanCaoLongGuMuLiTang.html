HERB FORMULAS

GUI ZHI GAN CAO LONG GU MU LI TANG - 桂枝甘草龙骨牡蛎汤 - CINNAMON TWIG, DRAGON BONE, AND OYSTER SHELL DECOCTION

English: Cinnamon Twig, Licorice, Dragon Bone and Oyster Shell Decoction
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Ram. Cinnamomi Gui Zhi 3-10g Unblocks Yang, transforms Qi and warms, warms and facilitates the flow of Qi through the channels and collaterals and Blood through the vessels and facilitates the flow of Yang in the chest.
With Zhi Gan Cao, for palpitations and shortness of breath due to Heart Yang Deficiency.
With Mu Li and Long Gu, for irritability and insomnia due to floating Yang in the Upper Jiao and injured Yin in the Lower Jiao due to Heart Yang Deficiency.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Preparata Zhi Gan Cao 6-15g Tonifies the Spleen and augments Qi.
Fossilia Ossis Mastodi Long Gu 6-30g Settles and calms the Shen, calms the Liver and anchors and preserves floating Yang.
Concha Ostreae Mu Li 6-30g Heavily settles and calms the Shen, benefits Yin and anchors Floating Yang.
With Zhi Gan Cao and Gui Zhi, for restlessness, irritability, palpitations and spontaneous sweating due to Floating Yang.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Unblocks Yang Qi
  • Benefits Yin
  • Calms the Shen
  • Eliminates irritability

SYNDROMES

  • Heart Yang Deficiency
  • Heart Yin or Yang disorder where the Shen drifts upward

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Irritability
  • Restlessness
  • Spontaneous perspiration
  • Palpitations
  • Shortness of breath
  • T: Pale
  • C: Greasy and white
  • P: Floating and slow or Slow and irregular

TREATS

  • Irritability
  • Restlessness
  • Nocturnal emission due to lack of balance of Yin and Yang
  • Atrioventricular block with palpitations
  • Hypogonadism
  • Thromboangitis obliterans
  • Cardiac neurosis
  • Fatigue
  • Congestive Heart failure
  • Coronary Heart disease
  • Spontaneous perspiration
  • Palpitations
  • Rheumatic Heart disease
  • Sinus bradycardia
  • Neurasthenia
  • Eyelid twitch
  • AIDS/HIV
  • Weakness
  • Facial edema
  • Atrial fibrillation
  • Dyspnea

NOTES

  • This formula was originally used for irritability caused by improper use of the warm-needle acupuncture that scorched the Heart Yin and injured the Heart Yang.
