HERB FORMULAS

SENILE VAGINITIS #1

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Hb. Epimedii Yin Yang Huo 9g Tonifies the Kidneys, strengthens Yang, dispels Wind-Cold-Dampness and warms and unblocks the flow of Yang Qi.
With Shan Yao, enhances its functions.
Rz. Curculiginis Xian Mao 9g Tonifies the Kidneys, strengthens Yang, expels Cold and eliminates Dampness.
With Yin Yang Huo, accentuates the effects of the herb.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 9g Tonifies, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood, disperses Cold, reduces swelling, expels pus, generates flesh and alleviates pain.
Fr. Lycii Gou Qi Zi 9g Nourishes and tonifies Liver and Kidney Blood and Yin and benefits Jing.
Rx. Dioscoreae Shan Yao 12g Tonifies the Spleen, nourishes Stomach Yin, tonifies Kidney Yin and astringes Jing.
Rx. Codonopsis Dang Shen 12g Tonifies the Middle Jiao, augments Qi, nourishes the Blood and promotes the generation of Body Fluids.
With Fu Ling, for Spleen and Stomach Deficiency with fatigue, a sensation of fullness in the abdomen, dizziness, lethargy, shortness of breath, anorexia and loose stools or diarrhea.
Poria Fu Ling 12g Promotes urination, leaches out Dampness, strengthens the Spleen and harmonizes the Middle Jiao.
Fr. Rubi Fu Pen Zi 9g Tonifies and stabilizes the Kidneys, astringes Jing and urine and tonifies Yang.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Warms and supplements the Spleen and Kidneys
  • Warms Cold
  • Transforms Dampness

SYNDROMES

  • Spleen and Kidney Yang Deficiency with Cold-Dampness

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Excessive, pale colored menstruation
  • Excessive white, watery vaginal discharge
  • Low back ache
  • Fatigue
  • Weakness
  • Aversion to Cold
  • Cold hands and feet
  • Dizziness
  • Vertigo
  • Excessive nocturia
  • T: Pale
  • C: White
  • P: Thready and soft

TREATS

  • Senile vaginitis
