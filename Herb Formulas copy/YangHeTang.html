HERB FORMULAS

YANG HE TANG - 陽和湯 - YANG HEARTENING DECOCTION

English: Yang Heartening Decoction
Also Known As: Balmy Yang Decoction

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Rehmanniae Preparata Shu Di Huang 20-30g Nourishes the Blood and Jing and nourishes Liver and Kidney Yin.
With Ma Huang, warms and unblocks the channels and collaterals, for Yin furuncles caused by Cold-Damp Obstructing the Channels.
Colla Cervi Cornus Lu Jiao Jiao 6-9g Tonifies the Kidneys, strengthens Yang, tonifies the DU Channel, strengthens Jing and Blood, strengthens the sinews and bones, benefits marrow, tonifies and nourishes Qi and Blood and heals chronic chronic Yin sores and boils.
With Shu Di Huang, for impotence and spermatorrhea (men) and infertility and thin vaginal discharge (women) due to Kidney Deficiency Cold.
Cx. Cinnamomi Rou Gui 2-3g Warms the Kidneys, Spleen and Heart, strengthens the Yang and Ming Men Fire,disperses deep Cold, warms the channels, unblocks the channels and vessels, alleviates pain assists the generation of Qi and Blood.
With Shu Di Huang and Ma Huang, for Yin-type localized swellings that blend into the surrounding tissues.
With Shu Di Huang, Ma Huang , Bai Jie Zi and Lu Jiao Jiao, for Yin sores.
Rz. Zingiberis Preparata Pao Jiang 1.5-6g Warms the Middle, expels Cold, dispels Wind-Dampness seeping into the Lower Jiao, expels Interior Cold and warms the channels.
With Gan Cao, for epigastric pain and vomiting due to Spleen and Stomach Deficiency Cold.
Sm. Sinapis Bai Jie Zi 6g Warms the Lungs, regulates Qi, expels Phlegm, dissipates nodules, unblocks the collaterals and alleviates pain.
With Rou Gui and Shu Di Huang, for joint pain and Yin-type deep-rooted boils due to Phlegm-Dampness Obstructing the channels.
Hb. Ephedrae Ma Huang 1.5-3g Induces sweating, releases the Exterior and warms and disperses Cold pathogens.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 2-3g Moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Warms the Yang
  • Tonifies the Blood
  • Disperses Cold
  • Eliminates Stagnation

SYNDROMES

  • Yin-Type abscess due to Yang Deficiency and Interior Cold Stagnation
  • Blood and Yang Deficiency with Cold

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Localized painful swellings without a head that bleed into the surrounding tissues and do not affect the color or texture of the skin
  • The swellings are not hot to the touch
  • No thirst
  • Swollen and painful joints
  • Wasting of the muscles of the thigh and calf
  • T: Very pale or Pale-pink
  • C: White
  • P: Thready and forceless or Deep and slow

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with Yang-Type swellings.
  • Contraindicated for those with Yin Deficiency.
  • Contraindicated for those with any type of swelling that has ulcerated for a long time.
  • Do not alter the dosage of Hb. Ephedrae Ma Huang.
