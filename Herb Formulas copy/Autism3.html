HERB FORMULAS

AUTISM #3

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rz. Pinelliae Preparatum Zhi Ban Xia 3-15g Dries Dampness and transforms Phlegm.
With Fu Ling, prevents turbid Phlegm and Dampness from disturbing the Heart with dizziness, palpitations and insomnia.
With Hou Po, for Phlegm-Dampness with Qi Stagnation.
With Hou Po, Zi Su Ye and Fu Ling, for Plum pit Qi Stagnation.
With Yu Jin and Shi Chang Pu, for disorientation, irritability, dizziness and hearing problems due to Turbid Dampness Blocking the Orifices.
Cx. Magnoliae Officinalis Hou Po 3-10g Promotes the movement of Qi in the Middle Jiao, resolves Stagnation, descends Rebellious Qi, dries Dampness and reduces Phlegm.
With Zhi Ban Xia and Fu Ling, for Phlegm Obstructing the Qi.
Fr. Perillae Zi Su Zi 3-10g Stops cough, calms wheezing, descends Qi and dissolves Phlegm.
With Bai Jie Zi and Lai Fu Zi, for Wheezing from Phlegm and thin mucus leading to Qi Stagnation, focal distention in the chest, anorexia and indigestion.
Poria Fu Ling 9-18g Promotes urination, leaches out Dampness , quiets the Heart, calms the Spirit and soothes the nerves.
With Yuan Zhi, for insomnia and palpitations due to Spleen and Heart Insufficiency or Phlegm Obstructing the Heart.
With Zhi Ban Xia, for Phlegm, thin mucus and turbid Dampness.
Arisaemae cum Bile Dan Nan Xing 2-5g Clears Heat, dissolves Phlegm, calms Wind and stops convulsions.
Rx. Curcumae Yu Jin 3-15g Activates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, regulates Qi flow, clears the Heart and Pericardium and opens the Orifices.
With Shi Chang Pu and Zhu Li, for Damp-Heat veiling the clear Orifices with focal distention, a stifling sensation in the chest and epigastrium and muddled consciousness.
Rx. Acori Tatarinowii Shi Chang Pu 3-10g Opens the Orifices, vaporizes Phlegm, calms the Spirit and promotes the flow of Qi and Blood.
With Yuan Zhi, for forgetfulness and dulled senses.
With Yuan Zhi and Fu Ling, for forgetfulness, tinnitus and impaired hearing.
with Yu Jin, transforms Dampness, opens the Orifices and awakens the brain to treat Turbid Dampness that has blocked the clear Orifices, with loss of consciousness or confusion, tinnitus and forgetfulness
Rx. Polygalae Yuan Zhi 3-15g Calms the Spirit, sedates the Heart, clears the channel joining the Heart and Kidney, expels Phlegm and opens the Orifices.
With Yu Jin and Shi Chang Pu, for disorientation due to Phlegm Blocking the Heart Orifices.
Fol. Perillae Zi Su Ye 3-10g Promotes the movement of Spleen and Stomach Qi, improves Lung function and expands the chest.
With Zhi Ban Xia and Hou Po, for Spleen and Stomach Qi Stagnation with accumulation of Phlegm.
Sm. Sinapis Bai Jie Zi 3-15g Warms the Lungs, regulates Qi and expels Phlegm.
Sm. Raphani Lai Fu Zi 4.5-12g Reduces Food Stagnation, transforms accumulation, promotes digestion, eliminates distention, descends Qi and reduces Phlegm.
With Zhi Ban Xia, for Damp-Phlegm induced cough and wheezing.
With Zi Su Zi and Bai Jie Zi, for chronic cough and wheezing, especially due to excessive Phlegm disorders.
With Bai Jie Zi, restores the smooth flow of Qi and reduces Phlegm.
Succus Bambusae Zhu Li 30-60g Clears Heat, transforms Phlegm and penetrates to the channels.
Styrax Su He Xiang 0.3-3g Opens the Orifices and penetrates turbidity.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Dries Dampness
  • Transforms Phlegm
  • Regulates Qi
  • Activates the Blood
  • Opens the Orifices
  • Clears Heat
  • Calms the Shen

SYNDROMES

  • Phlegm Confuses the Heart Orifices

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Taciturn and uncommunicative behavior
  • Dementia
  • Lack of sympathy
  • Apathy
  • Soliloquy
  • Mental confusion
  • Clouding of consciousness
  • Talking to oneself
  • Acting as if no one else is present
  • Dull expression with blank stare
  • Odd behavior
  • Maybe loss of consciousness
  • Maybe falling to the ground
  • Sound of phlegm in throat with gurgling
  • T: Pink
  • C: White and greasy
  • P: Deep and slippery; maybe wiry

TREATS

  • Autism
