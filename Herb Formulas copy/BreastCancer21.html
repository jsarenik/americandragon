HERB FORMULAS

BREAST CANCER #21

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Bul. Fritillariae Thunbergii Zhe Bei Mu 15g Clears Heat, transforms Phlegm, dissipates nodules and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Margarita Zhen Zhu 0.3g Clears the Liver, generates flesh and promotes healing.
Calculus Bovis Niu Huang 3g Drains Heat, resolves Fire toxicity, clears the Liver, relieves Fire toxicity, extinguishes Wind, stops tremors and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With She Xing, Ru Xiang and Mo Yao, for boils, carbuncles, fibrocystic breast disease and a wide variety of painful swellings.
With Zhen Zhu, for sores due to Heat toxin.
Moschus She Xiang 3g Activates the Blood, dissipates masses, reduces swellings, detoxifies, alleviates pain, opens the channels and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Ru Xiang and Mo Yao, for carbuncles, sores and hot swellings.
Sanguis Draconis Xue Jie 3g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, protects the surface of ulcers, prevents decay, generates flesh and alleviates pain.
With Ru Xiang and Mo Yao, for pain, swelling or hemorrhage, especially in the chest and abdomen.
Fr. Amomi Rotundus Bai Dou Kou 1.5g Promotes the movement of Qi, aromatically transforms Dampness, strengthens the Stomach, warms the Middle Jiao, descends Qi and stops vomiting.
Olibanum Ru Xiang 30g Invigorates the Blood, promotes the movement of Qi, relaxes the sinews, invigorates the channels, reduces swelling, generates flesh and alleviates pain.
Myrrh Mo Yao 30g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, alleviates pain, reduces swelling, generates flesh and promotes healing.
With Ru Xiang, for swollen, painful sores and sores that are hard but not painful.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Dispels Blood Stasis
  • Promotes the movement of Qi
  • Clears Heat
  • Resolves Fire toxicity
  • Transforms Phlegm
  • Dissipates masses
  • Alleviates pain
  • Inhibits the growth of cancer cells

SYNDROMES

  • Qi, Blood and Phlegm Stagnation with Toxic Heat

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Red face
  • Chest pain
  • Ulceration of the tumor
  • Maybe a bloody secretion
  • Foul odor
  • Short urine
  • Restless fever
  • Sore throat
  • Constipation
  • Dry stools
  • Mania
  • Shortness of breath
  • Dyspnea
  • Nausea
  • Vomiting
  • Chest and/or epigastric fullness
  • Pain in the ribs
  • T: Reddish purple or Scarlet
  • C:Little or Yellow or Yellow and greasy or Yellow and smooth
  • P: Wiry and rapid or Rapid and smooth or Slippery and rapid or Choppy and rapid

TREATS

  • Advanced breast cancer

HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

NOTES

  • Grind the ingredients into a powder and make boluses with honey. Take 3g one a day.
