HERB FORMULAS

HYPERTENSION #2

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Fossilia Ossis Mastodi Long Gu 15g Calms the Liver, anchors and preserves Floating Yang and prevents leakage of fluids.
With Mu Li and Dai Zhe Shi, for insomnia, dizziness, irritability, and blurred vision associated with Liver Yin Deficiency with Liver Yang Rising.
Concha Ostreae Mu Li 15g Calms the Liver, benefits Yin, anchors Floating Yang and prevents leakage of fluids.
With Long Gu and Bai Shao, for headaches, dizziness, tinnitus and other symptoms associated with Yin Deficiency with Yang Rising.
With Shan Zhu Yu, for sweating.
Haematitum Dai Zhe Shi 15g Calms the Liver, anchors Floating Yang and clears Liver Fire.
With Lon Gu, Mu Li and Bai Shao, for hypertension with irritability, dizziness and constipation.
Rx. Astragali Huang Qi 15g Tonifies Qi and Blood, strengthens the Spleen, raises the Yang Qi of the Spleen and Stomach and generates Body Fluids.
With Ren Shen and Dang Shen, for Qi Deficiency with general debility, anorexia, fatigue, tired extremities and spontaneous sweating.
Fr. Ligustri Lucidi Nu Zhen Zi 15g Nourishes and tonifies Liver and Kidney Yin and clears Deficiency Heat.
Rx. Paeoniae Alba Bai Shao 12g Nourishes the Blood, astringes Yin and calms Liver Yang and Liver Wind.
With Long Gu and Mu Li, for spontaneous sweating and night sweats due to Qi and Yin Deficiency.
With Huang Qi and Ren Shen, restrains sweating from Deficiency.
Hb. Ecliptae Mo Han Lian 12g Nourishes and tonifies Liver and Kidney Yin.
With Nu Zhen Zi, for dizziness, blurred vision and premature graying of the hair due to severe Liver and Kidney Yin Deficiency with ascendant Yang.
Rx. Dioscoreae Shan Yao 12g Tonifies the Spleen, nourishes Stomach Yin, tonifies Lung Qi, nourishes Lung Yin and tonifies Kidney Yin.
With Dang Shen, for Spleen and Kidney Deficiency with fatigue, weakness, anorexia, and watery stools.
Fr. Corni Shan Zhu Yu 12g Stabilizes the Kidneys, astringes Jing and Body Fluids, stops excessive sweating, tonifies Liver and Kidney Yin and strengthens Kidney Yang.
With Huang Qi and Dang Shen, for spontaneous sweating due to Yang or Qi Deficiency.
Fr. Perillae Zi Su Zi 9g Stops cough, calms wheezing, descends Qi and dissolves Phlegm.
Rx. Codonopsis Dang Shen 9g Tonifies the Middle Jiao, augments Qi, nourishes the Blood and promotes the generation of Body Fluids.
With Huang Qi, for Lung and Spleen Qi Deficiency with fatigue, shortness of breath, diarrhea and anorexia.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Tonifies Qi
  • Nourishes Yin

SYNDROMES

  • Qi and Yin Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Dizziness which is worse on exertion
  • Tinnitus
  • Dry, rough eyes
  • A dry throat
  • Tidal fever
  • Fatigue
  • Weakness
  • Spontaneous sweating
  • Shortness of breath
  • T: Fat and pale with teethmarks
  • C: Little or None
  • P: Thready, forceless and maybe rapid

TREATS

  • Hypertension
