HERB FORMULAS

DANG GUI SHAO YAO SAN - 當歸芍藥散 - DANG GUI AND PEONY POWDER

English: Dang Gui and Peony Powder
Also Known As: Angelica and Peony Powder
Tangkuei and Peony Formula
Tangkuei and Poria Powder
Dang Gui Fu Ling San

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 3-12g Tonifies, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood.
With Bai Shao, regulates and harmonizes Qi and Blood and treats Blood Deficiency Heat.
With Chuan Xiong, harmonizes, nourishes and invigorates the Blood and disperses Blood Stasis for abdominal pain
With Bai Shao and Chuan Xiong, harmonizes the function of and softens the Liver.
With Bai Shao, Fu Ling, Bai Zhu and Ze Xie, for diarrhea during pregnancy with severe abdominal pain.
Rx. Paeoniae Alba Bai Shao 4-48g Softens the Liver, stops pain and nourishes the Blood.
With Bai Zhu, treats concurrent problems of the Liver and Spleen.
With Dang Gui, nourishes Yin and Blood.
Rz. Atractylodis Macrocephalae Bai Zhu 4-12g Tonifies the Spleen, tonifies Qi and dries Dampness.
With Fu Ling and Ze Xie, strengthens Spleen and leaches out Dampness.
Poria Fu Ling 4-12g Promotes urination, resolves Dampness, strengthens the Spleen and harmonizes the Middle Jiao.
With Ze Xie, facilitates the removal of water and leaches out Dampness to treat stagnant water and Dampness leading to edema, jaundice, diarrhea, Milky Lin, and scanty, difficult urination with abdominal distention.
Rz. Alismatis Ze Xie 4-24g Promotes urination and resolves Dampness.
With Bai Zhu, for Spleen Deficiency leading to accumulation of Dampness with urinary difficulty, loose stools, diarrhea Milky Lin or vaginal discharge.
Rz. Chuanxiong Chuan Xiong 3-24g Activates Blood and regulates Qi.
Rice Wine     (Facilitates the flow and disperses.)

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Nourishes Liver Blood
  • Softens the Liver
  • Spreads Liver Qi
  • Strengthens the Spleen
  • Resolves Dampness
  • Reinforces the Spleen and Kidney

SYNDROMES

  • Abdominal pain due to Spleen and Kidney Deficiency
  • Hypofunction of the Liver and Spleen with Stagnation of Blood and Qi Stagnation due to Dampness
  • Problems with pregnancy

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Use with caution during pregnancy.
HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS
  • Concurrent use of this formula with colchicine was associated with improvement in learning acquisition deficit induced bu colchicine.
  • This formula may reduce menopausal symptoms induced by gonadotropin-releasing hormone agonists without negative effects on serum estradiol levels.
