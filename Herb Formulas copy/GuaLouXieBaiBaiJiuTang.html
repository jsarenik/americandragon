HERB FORMULAS

GUA LOU XIE BAI BAI JIU TANG -栝楼薤白白酒汤 - TRICHOSANTHIS FRUIT, CHINESE GARLIC AND WINE DECOCTION

English: Trichosanthis Fruit, Chinese Garlic and Wine Decoction
Also Known As: Trichosanthis Fruit Chinese Chive and Wine Decoction
Trichosanthis, Bakeri and Vinegar Combination
Decoction of Trichosanthis, Macrostem Onion and White Wine
Decoction of Trichosanthis, Bakeri and White Wine

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Fr. Trichosanthis Gua Lou 2-24g Expels Phlegm, clears Heat and disperses clumps of Phlegm in the chest.
Bul. Allii Xie Bai 4.5-12g Moves Qi, warms and unblocks Yang, disperses Cold Phlegm and alleviates pain.
With Gua Lou, for chest Bi with profuse Phlegm.
White Wine Bai Jiu 30-1,400ml Enhances the action of Xie Bai, by encouraging the flow of Yang.
(Vinegar)     (May substitute for White Wine.)

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Unblocks Yang
  • Promotes the movement of Qi
  • Expels Phlegm
  • Resolves hardenings

SYNDROMES

  • Chest pain due to Heart Yang Deficiency
  • Heart Blood Stagnation
  • Pain due to Heart Qi Stagnation with Phlegm

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Pain in the chest which often radiates to the upper back
  • Pain may radiate to upper shoulders, neck and epigastrium
  • Asthma
  • Wheezing
  • Cough
  • Spontaneous perspiration
  • Shortness of breath
  • Spitting saliva
  • Wheezing cough with copious sputum
  • T: Pale or Pink
  • C: Thick and greasy
  • P: Deep and wiry or Tight

TREATS

  • Esophageal spasm
  • Angina pectoris
  • Chronic obstructive pulmonary disease
  • Intercostal neuralgia
  • Costochondritis
  • Hyperventilation disorders
  • Cough
  • Chronic obstructive pulmonary disease
  • AIDS/HIV
  • Congestive Heart failure
  • Dyspnea
  • Neck pain
  • Chronic bronchitis
  • Cardiac asthma
  • Cardiac neurosis
  • Myocardial infarction
  • Cardiac valvular disease
  • Pleuritis
  • Cor pulmonare
  • Thromboangitis obliterans
  • Palpitations
  • Spontaneous perspiration
  • Shoulder pain
  • Upper and middle back pain

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with an allergy to alcohol.
  • Contraindicated for those with chest pain due to Lung consumption.
  • Contraindicated for those with pain due to Phlegm-Heat.
  • Do not use long term.
