HERB FORMULAS

QI GE SAN - 启膈散 - OPEN UP THE DIAPHRAGM POWDER

English: Open up the Diaphragm Powder
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Glehniae/Adenophorae Sha Shen 9-15g Nourishes Lung and Stomach Yin, clears Heat, generates fluids, tonifies Liver and Kidney Yin and dispels Phlegm.
Rx. Salviae Miltiorrhizae Dan Shen 9-10g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, clears Heat, cools the Blood and reduces abscesses.
Poria Fu Ling 3-12g Strengthens the Spleen, harmonizes the Middle Jiao, promotes urination and leaches out Dampness.
Bul. Fritillariae Cirrhosae Chuan Be Mu 4.5-12g Clears Heat, transforms Phlegm and dissipates nodules.
Rx. Curcumae Yu Jin 1.5-12g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, regulates Qi flow, clears Heat, cools the Blood, speeds healing of chronic sores and alleviates pain.
With Dan Shen, for Heat with Blood Stasis causing chest Bi.
Per. Amomi Sha Ren Ke 1.2-10g Promotes the movement of Qi, aromatically transforms Dampness, strengthens the Spleen and stops vomiting.
Calyx Nelumbinis He Ye Di 3g Raises Yang Qi, strengthens the Spleen's transportive and transformative functions, dispels Dampness and harmonizes the Stomach.
(Fol. Nelumbinis) (He Ye) (3g) (Substitutes for He Ye Di.)
Testa Oryzae Chu Tou Kang 1.5g Opens the Stomach, descends Qi and treats occlusion.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Regulates Qi flow
  • Transforms Phlegm
  • Opens Qi Stagnation
  • Moistens Dryness

SYNDROMES

  • Dysphagia due to Qi Stagnation

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Difficult to swallow food
  • Swallowing with a choking sensation
  • Swallowing eventually becomes impossible
  • Dull or burning pain
  • Distention with indistinct pain that is relieved by belching, dry retching or spitting up of Phlegm or mucus
  • A feeling of esophageal constriction
  • Dry mouth and throat
  • Focal distention of the chest and diaphragm
  • Difficult bowel movements
  • Severe weight loss
  • Gradual emaciation
  • Sometimes acute pain
  • Maybe severe coughing
  • Maybe aspiration of fluids
  • Maybe fever
  • Maybe aortic erosion
  • T: Red
  • C: White
  • P: Thready and wiry

TREATS

  • Early stage esophageal cancer
  • Chronic esophagitis
  • Stomach cancer with dysphagia
  • Reflux esophagitis
  • Esophageal diverticuli
  • Chronic pharyngitis
  • Dysphagia
  • Hiatus hernia

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with Blood Stasis.
  • Contraindicated for those with Yin Deficiency and desiccated fluids.
  • Contraindicated for those with Qi and Yang Deficiency.
  • Contraindicated for those with the common cold, influenza or fever.
  • Use caution during pregnancy.
  • Use caution with children
HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

NOTES

  • Take 5-10g a day orally. Dissolve the powder into 400ml of boiled hot water, drink 200ml in the morning and 200ml in the evening.
