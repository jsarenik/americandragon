HERB FORMULAS

SAN MIAO WAN - 三妙丸 - THREE MARVEL PILL

English: Three Marvel Pill
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Cx. Phellodendri Huang Bai 12g Drains Damp-Heat especially from the Lower Jiao and drains Kidney Fire.
With Cang Zhu and Chuan Niu Xi, for Damp-Heat leg Qi.
Soaked Rz. Atractylodis Jin Cang Zhu 18g Strongly clears Dampness from the Lower Jiao, expels Wind-Dampness and tonifies the Spleen.
With Cang Zhu, eliminates Dampness and clears Heat.
With Huang Bai, for pain, swelling, and weakness of the lower extremities, atrophy disorder (Wei Syndrome), vaginal discharge and Damp-sores due to Wind-Dampness.
Rx. Cyathulae Chuan Niu Xi 6g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, expels Wind, drains Dampness, tonifies the Kidneys and Liver and strengthens the tendons and bones.
(Rx. Achyranthis Bidentatae) (Huai Niu Xi) (6g) (Substitutes for Chuan Niu Xi.)
Rz. Zingiberis Recens Sheng Jiang 3g Normalizes the flow of Qi at the center.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Heat
  • Dries Dampness

SYNDROMES

  • Damp-Heat in the Lower Jiao
  • Weakness of bones and sinews due to Liver and Kidney Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Red hot swollen painful joints
  • Leg numbness and pain
  • Burning sensations in the feet
  • Weak low back
  • Weak lower limbs
  • Atrophy of the lower limbs
  • Scanty, yellow urine
  • Lower back pain
  • Thick, yellow, foul smelling vaginal discharge
  • Lower limb sores due to Dampness
  • T: Pink and maybe swollen
  • C: Yellow and greasy
  • P:Rapid

TREATS

  • Rheumatism
  • Rheumatoid arthritis
  • Rheumatic arthritis
  • Gout
  • Vaginitis
  • Numbness
  • Thick, yellow, foul smelling vaginal discharge
  • Premenstrual breast distention
  • Hip pain
  • Beriberi
  • Paresthesia
  • Rashes
  • Eczema
  • Cervicitis
  • Amyotrophic lateral sclerosis (ALS)
  • Arthralgia
  • Fibrocystic breast disease
