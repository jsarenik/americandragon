HERB FORMULAS

STOMACH CANCER #29

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Lignum Aquilariae Resinatum Chen Xiang 15g Promotes movement of Qi, descends Qi, regulates the Middle, dispels Cold, warms the Spleen and Stomach, stops vomiting and alleviates pain.
With Mu Xiang, for rebellious Middle Jiao Qi with belching, hiccup, nausea and vomiting, epigastric and abdominal distention, a stifling sensation in the epigastrium and diarrhea.
With Mu Xiang and Bing Lang, for constipation due to Liver Qi Stagnation.
Rx. Aucklandiae Mu Xiang 10g Promotes the movement of Qi and alleviates pain.
With Bing Lang, for Qi Stagnation in the Stomach and Intestines due to Food Stagnation.
With Bing Lang and Da Huang, for epigastric and abdominal fullness, distention and pain with constipation.
Flos Caryophylli Ding Xiang 6g Warms the Middle Jiao, relieves pain, descends Qi, warms the Kidneys and assists Yang.
Resina Pini Song Xiang 15g Discharges pus, removes toxins and generates flesh.
Lignum Dalbergiae Odiferae Jiang Xiang 30g Invigorates the Blood, promotes the movement of Qi and alleviates pain.
Rx. Curcumae Yu Jin 30g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, regulates Qi flow, speeds healing of chronic sores, clears Heat, cools the Blood and alleviates pain.
Rz. Curcumae E Zhu 15g Invigorates Blood circulation, dispels Blood Stasis, regulates Qi, dissolves accumulations and alleviates pain.
With Mu Xiang, for Food Stagnation with abdominal distention, fullness, and pain.
Fr. Amomi Sha Ren 15g Promotes the movement of Qi, aromatically transforms Dampness, strengthens the Spleen and stops vomiting.
With Mu Xiang, increases the flow of Qi in the digestive system while reducing distention and Food Stagnation, alleviating pain and strengthening the Spleen.
With Mu Xiang and Zhi Shi, for Qi Stagnation due to Food Stagnation.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 20g Tonifies, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood, disperses Cold, moistens the Intestines, unblocks the bowels, reduces swelling, expels pus, generates flesh and alleviates pain.
With Xiang Fu, tonifies the Blood and moves Qi.
Rx. Paeoniae Rubra Chi Shao 25g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, reduces swelling from sores and abscesses and relieves pain.
With Xiang Fu, for flank pain, abdominal pain, and dysmenorrhea due to Qi and Blood Stagnation.
Massa Medicata Fermentata Shen Qu 30g Moves Qi, reduces Food Stagnation, promotes digestion and harmonizes the Stomach.
With Bing Lang, for distention of the abdomen due to Food Stagnation.
With Mu Xiang and Sha Ren, for abdominal pain associated with Food Stagnation.
Sm. Arecae Bing Lang 30g Regulates Qi, reduces accumulations, drains downward and unblocks the bowels.
Fr. Aurantii Immaturus Zhi Shi 30g Breaks up Stagnant Qi, reduces accumulation, descends Qi, unblocks the bowels, removes Stagnant Food, transforms Phlegm, reduces distention and resolves hardenings.
With Da Huang, for constipation and abdominal swelling due to Food Stagnation and Qi Stagnation.
Eupolyphaga/Steleophaga Tu Bie Chong 20g Breaks up and drives out Blood Stasis, invigorates Blood circulation and disperses lumps and masses.
With Da Huang, breaks up fixed abdominal masses from Blood Stasis.
Rz. Cyperi Xiang Fu 20g Spreads and regulates Liver Qi and alleviates pain.
With Sha Ren and Mu Xiang, for ulcers and digestive disturbance.
Natrii Sulfas Mang Xiao 25g Purges accumulations, guides out Stagnation, softens hardness, clears Heat, drains Fire and moistens Dryness.
With Da Huang, for constipation due to Heat in the Stomach and Intestines.
Fr. Amomi Rotundus Bai Dou Kou 15g Promotes the movement of Qi, strengthens the Stomach, warms the Middle Jiao, descends Qi and stops vomiting.
With Sha Ren, for fullness and a stifling sensation in the chest, epigastric pain and fullness, nausea, vomiting, diarrhea, anorexia and a thick tongue coat due to Qi Obstruction from Cold-Dampness.
With Ding Xiang, for belching due to Stomach Cold.
Rz. Cibotii Gou Ji 20pcs Tonifies the Liver and Kidneys.
With Dang Gui, for long term disease leading to lower extremity swelling.
Gecko Ge Jie 20 Tonifies the Kidneys and Lungs, strengthens Yang, tonifies Jing and Blood, controls and stabilizes Kidney Qi and stabilizes Ming Men Fire.
Moschus She Xiang 10g Activates the Blood, dissipates masses, reduces swellings, detoxifies, opens the channels and alleviates pain.
Rx. et Rz. Rhei Da Huang 15g Drains Heat, purges accumulations, invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, reduces Fire toxicity and eliminates Phlegm.
With Dang Gui, for Blood Stasis.
With Mang Xiao, eliminates obstruction in the Intestines for severe constipation with passage of liquid but no solid stool and abscess, swellings and toxic sores.
With Chi Shao, for chronic accumulation in the abdomen, blockage of urine, constipation, Qi rushing upward to the Heart, abdominal distention and rebellious damage from food and drink.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Circulates Qi
  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Breaks up Stasis
  • Dissipates masses
  • Purges accumulations
  • Alleviates pain

SYNDROMES

  • Stomach cancer due to Qi and Blood Stagnation

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Epigastric distention and vague pain
  • Dysphagia
  • Belching
  • Salivation
  • Dry mouth and throat
  • A hard, palpable lump in the epigastrium
  • Facial color dark and dull or pale
  • Emaciation
  • Sides and ribs distended on both sides (maybe with sharp pain)
  • Enlargement of the Liver and Spleen
  • Suffocating sensation in chest
  • Tendency to sigh
  • Vomit that looks like bean juice
  • Melancholy
  • Depression
  • Irritability
  • Black stool
  • Inappropriate anger
  • Mental fatigue
  • Irregular menses
  • Dysmenorrhea
  • Breast distention and pain before and during menses
  • T: Dark purple (maybe purple spots on edge of tongue) or Red
  • C: Normal or Greasy
  • P: Wiry, choppy and slow or Small and choppy or Thready and rapid or Thready and taut

TREATS

  • Stomach cancer

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

NOTES

  • Grind all of the ingredients into a fine powder and for into a bolus the size of a green bean with 30g of melted lard, 250g of honey and 20 drops of white cocks Blood. Take 9g three times a day.
