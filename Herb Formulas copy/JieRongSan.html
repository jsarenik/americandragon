HERB FORMULAS

JIE RONG SAN - 蚧茸散 - GECKO AND DEER ANTLER POWDER

English: Gecko and Deer Antler Powder
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Gecko Ge Jie 1pair Tonifies the Kidneys, strengthens Yang, tonifies Jing and Blood, controls and stabilizes Kidney Qi and stabilizes Ming Men Fire.
With Lu Rong, for impotence due to Kidney Yang Deficiency.
Cornu Cervi Pantotrichum Lu Rong 20g Tonifies the Kidneys, strengthens Yang, and tonifies and nourishes Qi and Blood.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Tonifies the Kidneys
  • Warms Yang
  • Benefits Jing

SYNDROMES

  • Kidney Yang Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Low back and knees feel cold, weak and aching
  • Feeling chilled
  • Aversion to Cold
  • Cold limbs
  • General debility
  • Pale or waxy pale complexion
  • Urinary frequency with abundant, clear urine or
  • Oliguria with edema
  • Maybe nocturia
  • Maybe incontinence
  • The urge to urinate may increase with exposure to Cold
  • The urge to urinate is worse with prolonged standing and lifting
  • Lower abdominal distention
  • Lassitude
  • Listlessness
  • Fatigue
  • Cold feet
  • Withdrawn, subdued behavior
  • A partial erection
  • Lack of erection on waking
  • Knees feel cold and aching
  • Dizziness
  • Tinnitus
  • Impotence
  • Spermatorrhea
  • Premature ejaculation
  • Low libido
  • Female infertility ("uterus cold and infertile")
  • Maybe anorexia
  • Maybe loose stools or
  • Constipation
  • Maybe chronic diarrhea or "cock crow" diarrhea
  • T: Pale and swollen or Pale, moist and swollen
  • C: Thin and white
  • P: Deep and thready - maybe slow or Slow and weak

TREATS

  • Polyuria
  • Lumbago
  • Spermatorrhea
  • Fatigue
  • Constipation
  • Impotence
  • Premature ejaculation
  • Knee pain
  • Edema
  • Urinary incontinence
