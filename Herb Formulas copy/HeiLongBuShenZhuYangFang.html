HERB FORMULAS

HEI LONG BU SHEN ZHU YANG FANG - 黑龙補腎助陽方 - BLACK DRAGON SUPPLEMENT THE KIDNEYS AND ASSIST YANG FORMULA

English: Black Dragon Supplement the Kidneys and Assist Yang Formula
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Caulis Spatholobi Ji Xue Teng 15g Invigorates and tonifies the Blood.
Rx. Astragali Huang Qi 12g Tonifies Qi and Blood, strengthens the Spleen and raises the Yang Qi of the Spleen and Stomach.
With Hong Shen, for Qi Deficiency with general debility, anorexia, fatigue, tired extremities and spontaneous sweat.
Rx. Polygoni Multiflori He Shou Wu 12g Tonifies the Liver and Kidneys, nourishes the Blood and nourishes Jing.
With Dang Gui and Bi Shao, for irregular menstruation and uterine bleeding from exhausted Liver and Kidneys.
Rx. Paeoniae Alba Bai Shao 12g Nourishes the Blood, regulates menstruation, astringes Yin and adjusts the Ying and Wei.
With Gan Cao, regulates the relationship between the Liver and Spleen.
With Dang Gui, nourishes Yin and Blood.
With Hong Shen, restrains sweating from Deficiency.
Fr. Lycii Gou Qi Zi 12g Nourishes and tonifies Liver and Kidney Blood and Yin.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 9g Tonifies, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood and disperses Cold.
With Huang Qi, for Qi and Blood Deficiency.
With Bai Shao, regulates and harmonizes Qi and Blood.
Rx. Asparagi Tian Men Dong 9g Nourishes Kidney Yin, moistens the Lungs and generates fluids.
Rx. Ophiopogonis Mai Men Dong 9g Nourishes Lung and Stomach Yin, generates Fluids, clears the Heart and eliminates irritability.
With Tian Men Dong, for Lung and Kidney Yin Deficiency with consumptive cough and occasional hemoptysis.
Hb. Epimedii Yin Yang Huo 9g Tonifies the Kidneys, strengthens Yang and increases the libido.
Rz. Curculiginis Xian Mao 9g Tonifies the Kidneys and strengthens Yang.
With Yin Yang Huo, for chills and impotence with Cold sperm, cold extremities and infertility.
Fr. Corni Shan Zhu Yu 9g Stabilizes the Kidneys, astringes Jing and Body Fluids, tonifies Liver and Kidney Yin, strengthens Kidney Yang, stabilizes the menses and stops bleeding.
Sm. Cuscutae Tu Si Zi 9g Strengthens Yang, nourishes Yin, astringes Jing and urine and benefits the Kidneys and Spleen.
Rx. Aconiti Lateralis Preparata Zhi Fu Zi 6g Warms Ming Men Fire and assists Heart, Kidney and Spleen Yang.
With Huang Qi, for spontaneous sweat and chills due to Yang Deficiency.
Rx. Ginseng Rubra Hong Shen 3g Tonifies Qi, generates Body Fluids and stops thirst.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 3g Tonifies the Spleen, augments Qi, moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs and guides the herbs to all twelve channels.
With Hong Shen, relieves palpitations and anxiety.
Cx. Cinnamomi Rou Gui 3g Warms the Kidneys, Spleen and Heart, strengthens Yang and Ming Men Fire and assists in the generation of Qi and Blood.
With Zhi Fu Zi, powerfully tonifies Kidney Yang, for Kidney Yang Deficiency with lower back pain and weakness in the legs and knees.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Nourishes Yin
  • Strengthens Yang

SYNDROMES

  • Yin and Yang Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • A somber, white facial complexion
  • Dizziness
  • Lack of strength
  • Low back and lower leg soreness and weakness
  • Seminal emission
  • Decreased sexual desire
  • Sometimes hot, sometimes cold
  • Spontaneous perspiration
  • Night sweats
  • Tinnitus
  • Heart palpitations
  • T: Pale
  • C: White or Little
  • P:Deep, thready and forceless or Deep, thready and rapid

TREATS

  • Aplastic anemia
  • Night sweats
  • Spermatorrhea
  • Palpitations
  • Spontaneous perspiration
