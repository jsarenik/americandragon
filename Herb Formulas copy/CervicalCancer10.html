HERB FORMULAS

CERVICAL CANCER #10

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Aconiti Lateralis Preparata Zhi Fu Zi 6g Warms Ming Men Fire and assists Heart, Kidney and Spleen Yang and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Bai Zhu, for Spleen Yang Deficiency with a cold, painful abdomen, vomiting and watery diarrhea.
With Fu Ling, for lower body edema and dysuria.
With Bai Zhu and Fu Ling. for Kidney and Spleen Yang Deficiency with retention of pathogenic water with abdominal pain, which is worse with Cold, urinary difficulty, and deep aching and heaviness in the extremities.
With Rou Gui, powerfully tonifies Kidney Yang, for Kidney Yang Deficiency with lower back pain and weakness in the legs and knees.
Cx. Cinnamomi Rou Gui 3g Warms the Kidneys, Spleen and Heart, strengthens Yang and Ming Men Fire, disperses deep Cold, warms the channels, unblocks the channels and vessels and alleviates pain.
Rz. Atractylodis Macrocephalae Bai Zhu 10g Tonifies the Spleen, augments Qi, dries Dampness, promotes water metabolism and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Fu Ling and Yi Yi Ren, for Spleen and Stomach Deficiency with Dampness.
Fr. Evodiae Wu Zhu Yu 6g Warms the Middle Jiao, disperses Cold, promotes the movement of Qi, relieves Stagnation in the Liver channel, alleviates pain, warms the Spleen, stops diarrhea and expels Damp-Cold.
Rx. Codonopsis Dang Shen 10g Tonifies the Middle Jiao and augments Qi.
With Fu Ling, for Spleen and Stomach Deficiency with fatigue, a sensation of fullness in the abdomen, dizziness, lethargy, shortness of breath, anorexia and loose stools or diarrhea.
With Bai Zhu, for Spleen Qi Deficiency with anorexia, loose stools and vomiting.
Poria Fu Ling 12g Promotes urination, leaches out Dampness, strengthens the Spleen, harmonizes the Middle Jiao and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Bai Zhu, strengthens the Spleen and resolves Dampness.
With Dang Shen and Bai Zhu, for a weak and deficient Spleen with fatigue, anorexia and loose stools.
Sm. Coicis Yi Yi Ren 15g Strengthens the Spleen resolves Dampness, clears Heat, expels pus and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Fu Ling, for Spleen Deficiency with Excess Dampness and edema.
With Fu Ling and Bai Zhu, for Spleen Deficient diarrhea.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Warms the Kidneys
  • Strengthens the Spleen
  • Eliminates Dampness

SYNDROMES

  • Spleen and Kidney Yang Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Listlessness
  • Chest fullness
  • Poor appetite
  • Lassitude
  • Low back and knee soreness and pain
  • Leukorrhagia
  • Defecation with dry feces first, then loose
  • T: Normal
  • C: Thin and white or Yellowish
  • P:Moderate or Taut

TREATS

  • Cervical cancer
