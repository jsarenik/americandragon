HERB FORMULAS

CERVICAL CANCER #26

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rz. Dioscoreae Shan Yao 15g Tonifies Kidney Yin and astringes Jing.
With Huang Bai and Che Qian Zi, for Spleen Deficiency with Damp-Heat leading to thick, yellow, foul-smelling vaginal discharge.
Cx. Moutan Mu Dan Pi 10g Clears Heat, cools and harmonizes the Blood, clears Deficiency Fire, invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, clears Liver Fire, drains pus and reduces swelling.
Fr. Corni Shan Zhu Yu 15g Stabilizes the Kidneys, astringes Jing and Body Fluids, tonifies Liver and Kidney Yin, strengthens Kidney Yang, stabilizes the menses and stops bleeding.
Rz. Alismatis Ze Xie 10g Promotes urination, leaches out Dampness and drains Kidney Fire.
With Mu Dan Pi, for steaming bone disorder, dizziness and vertigo.
With Sheng Di Huang and Huang Bai, for Kidney Deficiency Fire.
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 15g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, nourishes Yin and generates fluids.
With E Jiao, for uterine hemorrhage due to Heat from Deficiency.
With Mu Dan Pi, for Kidney Yin Deficiency with steaming bones.
Sm. Plantaginis Che Qian Zi 10g Promotes urination, clears Damp-Heat and clears Liver Heat.
Fr. Trichosanthis Gua Lou 15g Reduces abscesses, dissipates nodules and eliminates pus.
Rx. Dipsaci Xu Duan 15g Tonifies the Liver and Kidneys, strengthens the sinews and bones, stops uterine bleeding, invigorates the Blood, alleviates pain, generates flesh and reduces swellings, abscesses and sores.
With Sang Ji Sheng and E Jiao, for uterine bleeding from Kidney Deficiency.
Hb. Taxilli Sang Ji Sheng 15g Tonifies Liver and Kidney Yin, strengthens the sinews and bones, expels Wind-Dampness, nourishes the Blood and calms the womb.
Hb. Agrimoniae Xian He Cao 15g Astringes leakage of Blood, stops bleeding, reduces swelling, eliminates pus and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With E Jiao, for many types of bleeding disorders such as hematuria, bleeding during pregnancy and metrorrhagia, especially when bleeding is due to Yin and Blood Deficiency.
Colla Corii Asini E Jiao 15g Nourishes the Blood, stops bleeding and nourishes and moistens Yin.
Concha Ostreae Mu Li 30g Calms the Liver, benefits Yin, anchors Floating Yang, prevents leakage of fluids,softens hardness, dissipates nodules and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Shan Zhu Yu and Shan Yao, for irregular uterine bleeding or vaginal discharge.
Spica Prunellae Xia Ku Cao 15g Clears Liver Fire, clears Hot Phlegm, dissipates nodules and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Cx. Phellodendri Huang Bai 6g Drains Damp-Heat especially from the Lower Jiao, drains Kidney Fire, relieves Fire toxicity and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Che Qian Zi, for burning, painful, hesitant urination.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Nourishes the Kidneys and Liver
  • Nourishes the Blood
  • Clears Heat
  • Cools the Blood
  • Stops bleeding
  • Dissipates nodules
  • Inhibits the growth of cancer cells

SYNDROMES

  • Kidney and Liver Deficiency with Fire Rising

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Facial complexion pale and sallow
  • Both cheeks slightly red
  • Dizziness
  • Dry eyes
  • Blurred vision
  • Spots before the eyes
  • Numbness of limbs
  • Flaccid paralysis (Wei syndrome), weakness
  • Tinnitus
  • Deafness
  • Forgetfulness
  • Low back and knees sore and painful
  • Throat dry and painful
  • Five sole Heat
  • Low-grade fever
  • Night sweats
  • Irritability
  • Insomnia
  • Stools difficult to pass
  • Constipation
  • Men:
    Spermatorrhea
  • Women:
    Irregular menses or
    Scanty menses or
    Amenorrhea
    Vaginal discharge
  • T: Red
  • C: None
  • P: Thready and rapid

TREATS

  • Cervical cancer
