HERB FORMULAS

THROMBOPHLEBITIS #3

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Codonopsis Dang Shen 10-15g Tonifies the Middle Jiao, augments Qi, nourishes the Blood and promotes the generation of Body Fluids.
With Fu Ling, for Spleen and Stomach Deficiency with fatigue, a sensation of fullness in the abdomen, dizziness, lethargy, shortness of breath, anorexia and loose stools or diarrhea.
With Bai Zhu, for Spleen Qi Deficiency with anorexia, loose stools and vomiting.
With Dang Gui and Shu Di Huang, for Qi and Blood Deficiency with dizziness, weakness and lassitude.
With Bai Zhu, Dang Gui and Bai Shao, for Qi and Blood Deficiency.
Poria Fu Ling 12-30g Dries Dampness and strengthens the Spleen.
With Bai Zhu, strengthens the Spleen and resolves Dampness.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 15-30g Tonifies and invigorates the Blood, moistens the Intestines and regulates the relationship between the Liver and Kidneys.
With Bai Shao, regulates and harmonizes Qi and Blood and treats Blood Deficiency Heat.
With Shu Di Huang, for Heat symptoms due to Yin Deficiency and Devastated Blood.
With Chuan Xiong, harmonizes, nourishes and invigorates the Blood and disperses Blood Stasis.
Rx. Astragali Huang Qi 12-30g Tonifies Qi and Blood, strengthens the Spleen, raises the Yang Qi of the Spleen and Stomach and relieves numbness and pain.
With Dang Shen, for Qi Deficiency with general debility, anorexia, fatigue, tired extremities and spontaneous sweating.
With Bai Zhu, for Spleen Qi Deficiency with weakness, lassitude and loose stools.
With Dan Gui, for Qi and Blood Deficiency with chronic non-healing sores, numbness, painful obstruction and paresthesia.
Rx. Salviae Miltiorrhizae Dan Shen 12-30g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, nourishes the Blood and calms the Spirit.
Rz. Chuanxiong Chuan Xiong 12g Invigorates the Blood, activates Qi, releases constraint and alleviates pain.
With Dang Gui, harmonizes, nourishes and invigorates the Blood and disperses Blood Stasis for Blood Deficiency.
With Dang Gui, Shu Di Huang and Bai Shao, for a headache from Blood Deficiency.
Rz. Atractylodis Macrocephalae Bai Zhu 12-30g Tonifies the Spleen, augments Qi and dries Dampness.
Rx. Paeoniae Alba Bai Shao 12-30g Tonifies Blood and preserves Yin.
With Shu Di Huang, strongly tonifies Liver Blood.
With Shu Di Huang and Dang Gui, for dizziness, blurred vision, and dysmenorrhea due to Blood Deficiency or Stasis.
With Dang Gui, nourishes Yin and Blood.
Rx. Rehmanniae Preparata Shu Di Huang 12-30g Strengthens the Liver and Kidneys, and nourishes the Yin of the Blood.
With Dang Gui, for Blood Deficiency marked by dizziness, palpitations, insomnia and menstrual dysfunction.
With Bai Shao, Dang Gui and Chuan Xiong, for anemia.
With Ren Shen, Bai Zhu, Fu Ling, Dang Gui and Bai Shao, for anemia with fatigue and weakness.
With Dang Gui, Bai Shao and Chuan Xiong for anemia.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Tonifies Qi
  • Nourishes the Blood

SYNDROMES

  • Qi and Blood Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Fatigue
  • Loose stools
  • Poor appetite
  • Dizziness
  • Pale face
  • Weak voice
  • Shortness of breath
  • Reluctance to speak
  • Sweating with little or no exertion
  • Palpitations
  • Insomnia
  • Dry skin
  • Dry hair
  • Poor memory
  • Paleness
  • Scanty periods
  • T: Pale or Pale, thin and dry
  • C: Thin and white
  • P: Weak or Thin and choppy

TREATS

  • Thrombophlebitis
