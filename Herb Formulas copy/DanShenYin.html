HERB FORMULAS

DAN SHEN YIN - 丹參飲 - SALVIA DRINK

English: Salvia Drink
Also Known As: Salvia Decoction
Decoction of Salvia Root

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Salviae Miltiorrhizae Dan Shen 15-30g Invigorates Blood, transforms Blood Stasis and alleviates pain.
With Sha Ren and Tan Xiang, smooths the flow of Qi and Blood to stop pain.
Lignum Santali Albi Tan Xiang 3-12g Dispels Cold, warms the Middle Jiao, regulates Qi, alleviates pain, removes Blood Stasis and improves digestion.
Fr. Amomi Sha Ren 3-9g Promotes Qi movement, relaxes the Middle Jiao, Warms the Spleen and alleviates pain.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Dispels Blood Stasis
  • Promotes Qi Movement
  • Alleviates pain

SYNDROMES

  • Blood Stagnation in the Stomach
  • Qi and Blood Stagnation in the Middle Jiao

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Abdominal or epigastric pain which may radiate upwards to the back or scapula
  • Poor appetite
  • Shortness of breath
  • Nausea
  • Hypochondriac pain
  • Heart pain
  • Stomach pain
  • Hiccups
  • Stuffiness in chest
  • Constipation
  • Epigastric pain
  • Anorexia
  • T: Purple with purple spots
  • C: Normal
  • P: Choppy or Wiry and tight

TREATS

  • Angina pectoris
  • Chronic gastritis
  • Chronic hepatitis
  • Chronic pancreatitis
  • Acute pancreatitis
  • Chronic atrophic gastritis
  • Gastralgia
  • Coronary Heart disease
  • Epigastric pain
  • Nausea
  • Constipation
  • Hemafecia
  • Dyspnea
  • Esophageal varicies
  • Cholecystitis
  • Peptic ulcer
  • Primary or secondary dysmenorrhea
  • Duodenal ulcer
  • Headache
  • Hiccups
  • Stomach cancer
  • Vomiting
  • Abdominal pain
  • Anorexia
  • Hematemesis
  • Sick sinus syndrome

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated during pregnancy.
  • Contraindicated for those with bleeding or hemorrhage.
