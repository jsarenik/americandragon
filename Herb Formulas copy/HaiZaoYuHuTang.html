HERB FORMULAS

HAI ZAO YU HU TANG - 海藻玉壺湯 - SARGASSUM DECOCTION FOR THE JADE FLASK

English: Sargassum Decoction for the Jade Flask
Also Known As: Sargassum Jade Pot Decoction
The Seaweed Decoction
Sargassum and Du Huo Combination

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Hb. Sargassii Hai Zao 15g Clears Heat, resolves Phlegm and softens Phlegm nodules.
Thallus Eckloniae Kun Bu 9-15g Resolves Phlegm, softens Phlegm nodules, clears Heat and reduces swelling.
With Hai Zao, for goiter and scrofula.
Hb. Fritillariae Thunbergii Zhe Bei Mu 9-15g Clears Heat, transforms Phlegm and dissipates nodules.
Rz. Pinelliae Preparatum Zhi Ban Xia 3-14g Transforms Phlegm and dissipates nodules.
With Zhe Bei Mu, for scrofula or Phlegm-Dampness induced cough.
Per. Citri Reticulatae Chen Pi 3-9g Regulates Qi, tonifies the Spleen (improves its transportive functions), adjusts the Middle, dries Dampness, transforms Phlegm and descends Qi.
Fr. Forsythiae Lian Qiao 6-12g Clears Heat (especially in the Upper Jiao), relieves toxicity, reduces abscesses and dissipates clumps.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 12-30g Reduces swelling and expels pus.
Rx. Chuanxiong Chuan Xiong 12-18g Activates Blood and regulates Qi.
Rx. Angelicae Pubescentis Du Huo 6-9g Dispels Wind-Dampness and opens the channels.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 3-6g Resolves Phlegm, stops cough clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity and moderates and harmonizes the properties of other herbs.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Resolves Phlegm
  • Dissolves hardenings
  • Reduces and disperses goiters and tumors

SYNDROMES

  • Liver Qi and Blood Stagnation due to Iodine Deficiency
  • Qi, Blood, Damp and Phlegm Stagnation

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Hard, immovable lumps anterior or posterior to the ear
  • Soft, painless lumps
  • Fullness in the chest
  • Hypochondriac pain
  • Sighing
  • Anxiety
  • Insomnia
  • T: Swollen and purple
  • C: Sticky
  • P: Wiry

TREATS

  • Goiter
  • Hypothyroid
  • Breast lumps
  • Stomach cancer
  • Thyroid cancer
  • Panniculitis
  • Acute and simple lymphadenitis
  • Hashimoto's thyroiditis
  • Acne
  • Fibrocystic breasts
  • Hyperplasia of the mammary glands
  • Sjogren's syndrome
