HERB FORMULAS

WU MEI WAN - 鳥梅丸 - MUME PILL

English: Mume Pill
Also Known As: Mume Formula
Mume Elixir, Wu Mei Dan
Mume Stomach Pacifying Pill Wu Mei An Wei Wan
Anti-parasitic Mume Pill Sha Chong Wu Mei Wan
Stomach Pacifying Pill An Wei Wan

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Fr. Mume Wu Mei 3-30g Eliminates roundworms and alleviates pain.
With Chuan Jiao, Xi Xin and Gan Jing, for roundworms causing abdominal pain, irritability, and vomiting after eating.
With Huang Lian, for Internal Heat with thirst, Dryness, cough and irritability.
Per. Zanthoxyli Chuan Jiao 1-12g Warms the Middle Jiao, disperses Cold, relieves diarrhea, kills parasites and alleviates abdominal pain.
With Xi Xin, expels parasites, Warms the organs.
with Wu Mei and Huang Lian, for abdominal pain and vomiting due to roundworms, especially in cases of Deficiency Cold.
Hb. cum Rx. Asari Xi Xin 1-28g Disperses Wind-Cold and Internal Cold and alleviates pain.
With Gui Zhi, facilitates the flow of Yang Qi.
Rz. Coptidis Huang Lian 3-48g Clears Heat and drains Dampness.
With Huang Bai, attacks worms.
Cx. Phellodendri Huang Bai 3-18g Drains Damp-Heat especially from the Lower Jiao.
Rz. Zingiberis Gan Jiang 5-30g Warms the Middle and expels Cold.
With Zhi Fu Zi and Gui Zhi, warms the interior and disperses Internal Cold.
With Huang Lian, for epigastric pain and distention, dysentery, indeterminate gnawing hunger, vague abdominal pain or discomfort, belching, and nausea.
Rx. Aconiti Lateralis Preparata Zhi Fu Zi 3-18g Disperses Cold and Dampness, warms the channels and stops pain.
With Ren Shen, for severe Deficiency of the Source Qi and Collapse of Yang with cold extremities, sweating, weak breathing, dizziness, and an extremely pale complexion.
With Xi Xin, for Cold damage both Internal and External with aversion to Cold, headache and generalized body aches with aching joints.
Ram. Cinnamomi Gui Zhi 3-18g Releases the exterior, assists the Yang, adjusts the Ying and Wei, releases the muscle layer, warms the channels and collaterals to relieve pain.
Rx. Ginseng Ren Shen 3-18g Powerfully tonifies Yuan Qi and Spleen and Stomach Qi.
With Dang Gui, tonifies Qi and nourishes Blood, preventing injury to normal Qi.
(Rx. Codonopsis) (Dang Shen) (6-54g) (Substitutes for Ren Shen.)
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 2-12g Tonifies Blood, regulates the menses, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood and disperses Cold.
With Gui Zhi and Xi Xin, for patients with Blood Deficiency who experience cold extremities due to Cold entering the channels.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Warms the Internal organs
  • Warms the Middle Jiao
  • Warms the Intestines
  • Drains Heat
  • Replenishes Qi
  • Dispels parasites
  • Calms roundworms
  • Drains the Liver
  • Calms the Stomach
  • Promotes Blood circulation
  • Relieves pain

SYNDROMES

  • Collapse from roundworms
  • Jue Yin Stage Cold and Heat
  • Chronic diarrhea or dysentery
  • Stomach Heat with Kidney Deficiency

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with explosive diarrhea.
  • Contraindicated for those with Damp-Heat dysentery.
  • Use with caution during pregnancy.
