HERB FORMULAS

TONG LIN YI SHEN PEI SHI TANG - 痛淋益金呸石汤 - FREE THE FLOW OF STRANGURY, BOOST THE KIDNEYS AND EXPEL STONES DECOCTION

English: Free the Flow of Strangury, Boost the Kidneys and Expel Stones Decoction
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Hb. Lysimachiae Jin Qian Cao 30g Promotes urination, unblocks painful urinary dysfunction and expels stones from the urinary tract.
Spora Lygodii Hai Jin Sha 30g Clears Heat, promotes urination, eliminates Damp-Heat, unblocks painful urinary dysfunction and expels stones.
With Jin Qian Cao, addresses all of the channels associated with painful urinary dysfunction, eliminating the causes of stone formation in the urinary tract and directly acting to expel and transform stones.
Hb. Plantaginis Che Qin Zi 30g Promotes urination and clears Damp-Heat.
Fol. Pyrrosiae Shi Wei 15g Clears Damp-Heat, unblocks painful urinary dysfunction and clears stones.
Sm. Vaccariae Wang Bu Liu Xing 15g Promotes the movement of Blood and invigorates the channels.
Fr. Psoraliae Bu Gu Zhi 15g Tonifies the Kidneys, strengthens Yang and stabilizes Jing.
Rx. Dipsaci Xu Duan 9g Tonifies the Liver and Kidneys, promotes the movement of Blood and alleviates pain.
Fr. Lycii Gou Qi Zi 9g Nourishes and tonifies Liver and Kidney Blood and Yin and benefits Jing.
With Shu Di Huang, for Liver and Kidney Deficiency.
Rx. Rehmanniae Preparata Shu Di Huang 9g Nourishes the Blood, nourishes Liver and Kidney Yin.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Nourishes and reinforces the Liver and Kidneys
  • Strengthens Yang
  • Dispels Damp-Heat
  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Disperses stones

SYNDROMES

  • Kidney Yang Deficiency with Damp-Heat

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Lumbago: low back feels cold, weak and aching
  • Feeling chilled
  • Aversion to Cold
  • Cold limbs
  • General debility
  • Fatigue
  • Weakness
  • Pale face
  • Urinary frequency with abundant, clear or turbid urine or
  • Oliguria with edema
  • Possible terminal dribbling and/or slight pain
  • Maybe occasional discharge of sand or stones
  • Maybe nocturia
  • Lassitude
  • Withdrawn, subdued behavior
  • Knees feel cold and aching
  • Dizziness
  • Tinnitus
  • Impotence
  • Spermatorrhea
  • Premature ejaculation
  • Low libido
  • Female infertility ("uterus cold and infertile")
  • Maybe anorexia
  • Maybe loose stools
  • Maybe chronic diarrhea or "cock crow" diarrhea
  • Urinary frequency
  • Urinary urgency
  • Painful urination or
  • Continuous dripping of urine or
  • Sudden interruption to the urinary stream
  • Turbid urine or
  • Bladder:
    Hematuria
    Obstruction of the flow
  • Kidney:
    Slightly bloody urine
    Enlarged Kidney
  • Ureter:
    Slightly bloody urine
  • Urethra:
    Pain in the penis
  • Sand and stones in the urine
  • In Damp-Heat Pouring Downward there may also be:
    Fever
    Thirst
    Dry mouth
    Low back pain
  • In Damp-Heat Crystallizes in Urinary Bladder there may also be:
    Colicky pain in the low back, possibly radiating to the groin
  • T: Pale red and swollen or Normal
  • C: Yellowish or Yellow and sticky
  • P: Slippery and rapid or Deep and wiry or Rolling or Deep and thready - maybe slow

TREATS

  • Urolithiasis
