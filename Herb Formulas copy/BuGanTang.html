HERB FORMULAS

BU GAN TANG - 補肝汤 - TONIFY THE LIVER DECOCTION

English: Tonify the Liver Decoction
Also Known As: Reinforce the Liver Decoction

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 9-12g Nourishes, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood.
With Bai Shao, regulates and harmonizes Blood.
With Bai Shao, Shu Di Huang and Chuan Xiong, for menstrual irregularity, amenorrhea and dysmenorrhea due to Blood Deficiency or Stasis.
With Shu Di Huang, for Heat symptoms due to Yin Deficiency and Devastated Blood.
With Chuan Xiong, harmonizes, nourishes and invigorates the Blood and disperses Blood Stasis.
Rz. Chuanxiong Chuan Xiong 6-9g Invigorates the Blood and promotes Qi movement.
With Dang Gui, for Blood Deficiency causing menstrual problems.
Rx. Paeoniae Alba Bai Shao 9-15g Nourishes the Blood, regulates the menses, calms Liver Yang and preserves Yin.
With Zhi Gan Cao, for muscle spasms, especially in the calf, due to Blood Deficiency.
Rx. Rehmanniae Preparata Shu Di Huang 15-24g Nourishes Blood and nourishes Liver and Kidney Yin.
With Dang Gui and Bai Shao, for dizziness, insomnia, palpitations and menstrual dysfunction due to Blood Deficiency.
Sm. Zizyphi Spinosae Suan Zao Ren 6-9g Nourishes Heart Yin, augments Liver Blood, calms the Shen and astringes perspiration.
Fr. Chaenomelis Mu Gua 6-9g Increases Qi and Blood circulation, unblocks the channels and harmonizes the Stomach
With Dang Gui and Bai Shao, for muscle spasms due to Blood Deficiency.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Preparata Zhi Gan Cao 3-6g Tonifies Qi, moderates spasms, alleviates pain and moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs.
With Dang Gui, moderates spasms.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Tonifies and Regulates Blood
  • Nourishes Liver Yin

SYNDROMES

  • Liver Wind Stirring Internally: Liver Blood Deficiency Generates Wind
  • Liver Yin and Blood Deficiency
