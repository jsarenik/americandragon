HERB FORMULAS

QI XUE XU TANG - 气血虚汤 - QI AND BLOOD DEFICIENCY DECOCTION

English: Qi and Blood Deficiency Decoction
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Codonopsis Dang Shen 9g Tonifies the Middle Jiao, augments Qi, nourishes the Blood and promotes the generation of Body Fluids.
With Dang Gui and Shu Di Huang, for Qi and Blood Deficiency with dizziness, weakness and lassitude.
With Huang Qi, for Lung and Spleen Qi Deficiency with fatigue, shortness of breath, diarrhea and anorexia.
With Shu Di Huang and He Shou Wu, for Blood Deficiency.
Rx. Astragali Huang Qi 12g Tonifies Qi and Blood, strengthens the Spleen and raises Spleen Yang.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 12g Tonifies the Qi of the Blood and activates and harmonizes the Blood.
With Huang Qi, for Qi and Blood Deficiency.
With Bai Shao, regulates and harmonizes Qi and Blood.
With Shu Di Huang, for Heat symptoms due to Yin Deficiency and Devastated Blood.
Rx. Rehmanniae Preparata Shu Di Huang 12g Nourishes the Blood and nourishes Liver and Kidney Yin.
With Dang Gui, for Blood Deficiency marked by dizziness, palpitations, insomnia and menstrual dysfunction.
Rx. Paeoniae Alba Bai Shao 9g Nourishes the Blood and calms Liver Yang.
With Dang Gui, nourishes Yin and Blood.
With Dang Gui and Shu Di Huang, for dizziness, blurred vision, and dysmenorrhea due to Blood Deficiency or Stasis.
Colla Corii Asini E Jiao 9g Tonifies and nourishes the Blood.
With Dang Shen and Dang Gui, for Blood Deficiency with dizziness and palpitations.
With Dang Gui, Bai Shao and Shu Di Huang, for anemia.
With Dang Gui, Bai Shao, Huang Qi and Shu Di Huang, for anemia with Qi Deficiency.
Carapax Trionycis Bie Jia 15g Nourishes Yin, anchors Yang and activates the Blood.
Rx. Polygoni Multiflori He Shou Wu 12g Tonifies the Liver and Kidneys, nourishes the Blood and nourishes Jing.
With Dang Gui and Bai Shao, for Irregular menstruation and uterine bleeding from exhausted Liver and Kidneys.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Tonifies Qi
  • Nourishes the Blood
