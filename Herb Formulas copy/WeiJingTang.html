HERB FORMULAS

WEI JING TANG - 葦莖湯 - REED DECOCTION - DECOCTION OF PHRAGMITIS STEM

English: Reed Decoction
Also Known As: Decoction of Phragmitis Stem

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rz. Phragmitis Lu Gen 10-60g Clears Lung Heat and promotes urination. (especially helpful for Lung abscess).
With Yi Yi Ren and Dong Gua Zi , for Lung abscess with purulent Blood streaked sputum.
Sm. Coicis Yi Yi Ren 10-30g Clears Lung Heat, drains Dampness and disperses pus from the upper body.
With Dong Gua Zi, for Lung or Large Intestinal abscess that has formed pus.
With Dong Gua Zi and Tao Ren, for Lung abscess.
Sm. Benincasae Dong Gua Zi 10-24g Clears and transforms Phlegm-Heat, resolves Dampness and eliminates pus.
With Tao Ren, has a mild laxative effect which helps relieve Phlegm-Heat through the stool.
Sm. Persicae Tao Ren 5-10g Invigorates the Blood, eliminates Blood Stasis, moistens Intestines, unblocks the bowels and drains abscesses.
With Yi Yi Ren and Dong Gua Ren, for Lung abscess.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Heat from the Lungs
  • Transforms Phlegm
  • Drives out Stasis
  • Discharges pus

SYNDROMES

  • Phlegm-Heat Congests the Lungs
  • Lung Abscess due to Wind-Heat toxin obstructing the Lungs with Phlegm and Blood Stasis
  • Lung Heat
  • Recuperation from febrile diseases
  • Eye disorders due to upward blazing of Heat toxin

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Cough with foul-smelling, and / or thick,
    difficult to expectorate sputum
  • Cough may have no sputum
  • Sputum may be blood streaked
  • Mild chest pain
  • Dry, scaly skin
  • Slight fever
  • Red rashes
  • Thirst
  • T: Red
  • C: Yellow and greasy
  • P: Slippery and rapid

TREATS

  • Recuperation from febrile disease
  • Acute and chronic bronchitis
  • Bronchiectasis
  • Pneumonia
  • Pertussis
  • Hemoptysis
  • Lung abscess
  • Hemoptysis
  • Lung cancer
  • Dry skin
  • Asthmatic bronchitis
  • Upper respiratory tract infection
  • Measles
  • Lung abscess
  • Eye disorders
  • Cough
  • Chronic obstructive pulmonary disease
  • Fever
  • Dyspnea

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated during pregnancy.
HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

NOTES

  • With appropriate modifications, this formula can treat all types of Internal abscesses.
