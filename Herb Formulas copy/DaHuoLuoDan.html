HERB FORMULAS

DA HUO LUO DAN - 大活絡丹 - MAJOR INVIGORATE THE COLLATERALS SPECIAL PILL

English: Major Invigorate the Collaterals Special Pill
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Liquor-Soaked Agkistrodon/Bungarus Jin Bai Hua She 60g Powerfully unblocks the channels, extinguishes Wind, dispels Wind from the skin, dispels Wind from the sinews and settles jitteriness and convulsions.
With Dang Gui, He Shou Wu and Bai Shao, for chronic rashes.
With Quan Xie, Tian Ma and Dang Gui, for post-stroke complications such as muscle paralysis and hemiplegia.

With Di Long, for Bi syndrome.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Dispels Wind
  • Tonifies Qi and Blood
  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Disperses Cold
  • Clears Heat
  • Opens the channels and collaterals
  • Relieves pain

SYNDROMES

  • Stubborn Bi
  • Chronic Bi

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Chronic Bi with many recurrences
  • Joints are stiff and deformed
  • Areas surrounding the joints are slightly purple
  • Pain is excruciating
  • Fixed location
  • Maybe painful numbness
  • Joint may be red, swollen and painful or
  • Icy cold and worse in cold weather and better with warmth
  • Maybe fever
  • T: Purple spots
  • C: White
  • P: Thin and choppy

TREATS

  • Paralysis or hemiplegia in post stroke patients
  • Atrophic painful obstruction
  • Stubborn Bi
  • Rheumatoid arthritis
  • Neck pain and stiffness
  • Phlegm syncope
  • Deep-rooted sores
  • Multiple abscesses
  • Chronic Bi
  • Sciatica

NOTES

  • Os Tigris Hu Gu, Moschus She Xiang and Cornu Rhinoceri Xi Jiao are derived from endangered species. They are either eliminated from the formula or are replaced by substitutes.
  • Make into small pills and wrap with Gold Foil Jin Bo. Take one pill with aged grain-based liquor once daily.
