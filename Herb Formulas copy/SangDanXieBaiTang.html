HERB FORMULAS

SANG DAN XIE BAI TANG - 桑丹瀉白泻 - MULBERRY LEAF AND MOUTAN DECOCTION TO DRAIN THE WHITE

English: Mulberry Leaf and Moutan Decoction to Drain the White
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Cx. Mori Sang Bai Pi 12g Drains Heat from the Lungs, stops cough and calms wheezing.
With Di Gu Pi and Gan Cao, for cough with thick sputum, fever, irritability, and thirst due to Phlegm-Heat Obstructing the Lungs.
Fol. Mori Sang Ye 9g Calms the Liver, clears Heat from the Lungs, stops cough, moistens Dryness, cools the Blood and stops bleeding.
Cx. Moutan Mu Dan Pi 4.5g Clears Heat, cools and harmonizes the Blood and clears Liver Fire.
Cx. Lycii Di Gu Pi 15g Clears and drains Heat in the Lung.
With Sang Bai Pi, for Lung Heat cough or wheezing.
Caulis Bambusae in Taeniam Zhu Ru 6g Clears Heat, transforms Hot Phlegm, cools the Blood and stops bleeding.
Bul. Fritillariae Cirrhosae Chuan Bei Mu 9g Clears Heat, transforms Phlegm, stops cough and nourishes and moistens the Lungs.
Sm. Oryzae Geng Mi 9g Tonifies the Qi of the Lungs, Spleen and Intestines, stops restlessness, stops thirst and generates fluids.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Preparata Zhi Gan Cao 1.8g Moistens the Lungs, resolves Phlegm, stops cough and moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs.
Fr. Jujube Da Zao 4g Moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears the Liver
  • Drains and protects the Lungs

SYNDROMES

  • Liver Fire Scorching the Lungs

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Cough that causes pain in the chest and flanks
  • An inability to bend or rotate the trunk
  • May be hemoptysis or
  • Coughing up Blood-streaked sputum
  • T: Rather dry and red
  • C: Little or none
  • P: Rapid and wiry

TREATS

  • Cough
  • Pleurisy
  • Pertussis
  • Chronic bronchitis
  • Upper respiratory tract infection
  • Acute tonsillitis
  • Chronic tonsillitis
  • Chronic chest infection
