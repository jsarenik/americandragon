HERB FORMULAS

DA QIN JIAO TANG - 大秦艽湯 - MAJOR LARGE GENTIAN DECOCTION

English: Major Large Gentian Decoction
Also Known As: Major Gentiana Macrophylla Decoction
Major Gentian Combination
Major Gentiana Qin Jiao Decoction,
Decoction of Gentiana Macrophylla

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Gentianae Macrophyllae Qin Jiao 4-90g Clears Heat, expels Wind-Dampness, relaxes the sinews, clears Deficiency Heat and resolves Dampness (good for cramps in the limbs).
With Fang Feng, Du Huo and Qiang Huo for Wind-Cold-Damp Bi.
With Dang Gui and Bai Shao, for Blood Deficiency with hemiplegia, especially with excess muscle tension in the upper extremities.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 2-60g Tonifies the Spleen, tonifies Qi clears Heat, moderates spasms, alleviates pain and moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of the other herbs.
With Bai Shao, for abdominal pain, intestinal spasms and spasms of the calf or other muscles.
With Dang Gui for Wind-Damp Invasion blocking the Blood vessels causing pain, numbness and paralysis.
Rz. Chuanxiong Chuan Xiong 2-60g Activates the Blood, regulates Qi, expels Wind-Cold and alleviates pain (good for headaches).
With Dang Gui for Wind-Damp Invasion blocking the Blood vessels causing pain, numbness, and paralysis.
With Sheng Di Huang and Bai Shao, for headache due to Blood Deficiency.
With Xi Xin, for Wind-Cold headaches and Shao Yin Cold headaches.
With Dang Gui, Shu Di Huang, and Bai Shao, for headache from Blood Deficiency.
Rx. Angelica Sinensis Dang Gui 2-60g Nourishes the Blood, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood, disperses Cold and stops pain.
With Bai Shao, regulates and harmonizes Qi and Blood and treats Blood Deficiency Heat.
With Gan Cao, moderates spasms and relieves abdominal pain.
With Shu Di Huang, for Heat symptoms due to Yin Deficiency and Devastated Blood.
With Chuan Xiong, harmonizes, nourishes and invigorates the Blood and disperses Blood Stasis.
With Qiang Huo, Chuan Xiong and Qin Jian, for postpartum Bi syndrome.
Rx. Paeoniae Alba Bai Shao 2-60g Nourishes Blood, astringes Yin, soothes Liver, relieves pain and adjusts the Ying and Wei.
With Dang Gui, nourishes Yin and Blood.
With Dang Gui and Shu Di Huang, for dizziness, blurred vision and dysmenorrhea due to Blood Deficiency or Stasis.
With Du Huo and Qin Jiao, for chronic Bi Syndrome with injury to Qi and Blood leading to spasms of the extremities.
Hb. cum Rx. Asari Xi Xin 1-15g Releases exterior Wind and Cold, disperses internal Cold, warms the Lungs, alleviates pain, disperses and unblocks the Qi of the nasal orifices and circulates water.
With Sheng Di Huang for headache and toothache due to Wind-Heat.
With Chuan Xiong, for Wind-Cold headache, toothache and Bi pain.
With Chuan Xiong and Fang Feng, for Bi syndrome.
With Bai Zhi, for Wind-Cold toothache.
With Qiang Huo and Fang Feng, for Wind-Cold.
With Du Huo, for joint pain due to Wind-Cold-Dampness.
Rz. et Rx. Notopterygii Qiang Huo 2-30g Releases the exterior, dispels Wind-Cold-Dampness from the upper Tai Yang, opens the channels, vitalizes vessels, stops pain and guides Qi to the Tai Yang and Du Channels.
With Chuan Xiong for headache and generalized body aches and pains associated with the common cold or painful obstruction.
With Fang Feng for pain due to Wind-Dampness.
With Du Huo, for Wind-Dampness. (Qiang Huo is better for superficial Wind-Dampness and upper extremities and Du Huo is better for deeper Wind-Dampness and lower extremities).
With Bai Zhi for headaches.
With Bai Zhi, Fang Feng, Xi Xin and Chuan Xiong, for Wind-Cold with aversion to Cold, fever, headaches and muscle aches.
Rx. Saposhnikoviae Fang Feng 2-30g Releases the exterior, expels Wind-Cold-Dampness, stops pain, stops spasms and moves Qi
With Qin Jiao, for Wind Dampness Bi with spasms of the tendons or numbness of the limbs and pain due to Wind-Dampness in patients with Blood Deficiency.
With Qiang Huo and Dang Gui, for Wind-Cold-Damp Bi.
Rx. Scutellariae Huang Qin 2-30g Clears Lung Heat, drains Fire and dries Dampness.
Gypsum Fibrosum Shi Gao 4-60g Clears Heat, drains Fire, clears excess Heat from the Lungs, and relieves muscles.
With Shu Di Huang, for headache, toothache, and thirst due to raging fire caused by devastated Yin.
With Sheng Di Huang, for Excessive Fire with injured fluids.
Rx. Angelica Dahuricae Bai Zhi 2-60g Expels Wind, alleviates pain and headache, and expels Dampness.
With Xi Xin, for Wind-Cold induced headaches, sinus headaches and orbital headaches.
With Chuan Xiong, for headaches.
Rz. Atractylodes Macrocephalae Bai Zhu 2-30g Tonifies the Spleen, tonifies Qi, dries Dampness, and stabilizes the exterior.
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 2-30g Clears Heat, cools the Blood and nourishes Yin and Blood.
With Bai Shao, for Blood Deficiency Heat.
With Shu Di Huang, for Heat symptoms due to Yin Deficiency and devastated Blood.
Rx. Rehmanniae Preparata
Shu Di Huang
2-30g Nourishes Blood and Yin and strengthens Liver and Kidneys.
With Dang Gui and Bai Shao, for dizziness, palpitations, insomnia and menstrual dysfunction due to Blood Deficiency.
Poria Fu Ling 2-30g Promotes urination, resolves Dampness, strengthens the Spleen, harmonizes the Middle Jiao, pacifies the Heart, calms the Shen and soothes the nerves.
With Bai Zhu, strengthens the Spleen and resolves Dampness.
Rx. Angelica Pubescentis Du Huo 2-60g Releases the exterior, dispels Wind-Cold-Dampness, opens the channels, vitalizes vessels and stops pain.
With Qin Jiao, expels Wind-Cold-Damp, relieves arthralgia and stops pain.
With Xi Xin and Qin Jiao, for Wind-Damp Bi causing numbness, pain and soreness in the neck, back and lower extremities.
With Fang Feng, for numbness in the legs.
With Qiang Huo, for joint pain and numbness

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Dispels Wind
  • Clears Heat
  • Circulates Qi
  • Dredges the channels
  • Nourishes and invigorates the Blood
  • Harmonizes the Ying and Wei

SYNDROMES

  • Early-stage and middle-stage Wind Stroke
  • Wind-Heat Bi
  • Wind-Predominant Bi

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Whole body numbness
  • Deviation of mouth and eyes
  • Numbness of skin
  • Dysphagia due to stiffness of the tongue
  • Hemiplegia
  • Rigidity of the extremities
  • Spasms of the extremities
  • Aversion to Cold
  • Fever
  • Chills
  • Aching joints
  • Tingling of the skin
  • Paralysis of the extremities
  • Numbness and lack of sensation in the hands and feet
  • Common cold (with above symptoms)
  • Inability to walk
  • Wandering joints pain
  • T: Pink or Red
  • C: White or Thin yellow
  • P: Superficial or Superficial and wiry or superficial and tight or Wiry and thin

TREATS

  • Apoplexy
  • Stroke
  • Rheumatism
  • Bell's palsy
  • Rheumatoid arthritis
  • Cerebral hemorrhage
  • Arthritis
  • Muscle atrophy
  • Numbness
  • Dysphagia
  • Sjogren's Syndrome
  • Facial paralysis
  • Rheumatic arthritis
  • Multiple neuritis
  • Thrombotic stroke
  • Cerebral embolism
  • Hemiplegia
  • Fibromyalgia
  • Paresthesia
  • Fever
  • Aphasia
  • Polymyositis

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those in a coma.
  • Contraindicated for those with convulsions.
  • Contraindicated for those with Internal Wind.
  • Use with extreme caution for those with Blood or Yin Deficiency.
HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

NOTES

  • Modern research says this formula acts to relieve inflammation, stop fever, calm the mind, stop pain and promote blood circulation.
  • This formula can be used when Wind, Cold, Dampness and Heat are severe.
