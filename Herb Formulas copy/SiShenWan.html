HERB FORMULAS

SI SHEN WAN - 四神丸 - FOUR MIRACLE PILL - PSORALIA AND MYRISTICA FORMULA

English: Four Miracle Pill
Also Known As: Four Divinity Pill
Psoralia and Myristica Formula
Pills of Four Miraculous Drugs

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Fr. Psoraliae Bu Gu Zhi 9-120g Tonifies the Kidneys, strengthens Yang, stops diarrhea, tonifies and warms the Spleen and treats Ming Men Deficiency Fire.
With Rou Dou Kou, Wu Wei Zi and Wu Zhu Yu, for daybreak diarrhea with abdominal pain, and borborygmus, better after evacuation, and a deep thready pulse with a white tongue coat.
Fr. Evodiae Wu Zhu Yu 6-30g Warms the Middle Jiao, disperses Cold, warms the Spleen, stops diarrhea, dries Dampness and expels Damp-Cold.
With Wu Wei Zi and Bu Gu Zhi, for daybreak diarrhea associated with Spleen and Kidney Yang Deficiency.
With Sheng Jiang, for vertex migraine headaches.
Sm. Myristicae Rou Dou Kou 6-60g Astringes Intestines, warms the Middle Jiao, stops diarrhea and treats chronic intractable or daybreak diarrhea due to Spleen and Kidney Yang Deficiency.
With Bu Gu Zhi, for daybreak diarrhea due to Spleen and Kidney Deficiency Cold.
With Bu Gu Zhi and Wu Wei Zi, for daybreak diarrhea due to Spleen and Kidney Deficiency Cold.
Fr. Schisandrae Wu Wei Zi 6-60g Replenishes Qi and stops diarrhea.
With Bu Gu Zhi and Rou Dou Kou, for Kidney Deficiency with chronic dysentery and daybreak diarrhea.
With Wu Zhu Yu, fried together as a powder for chronic diarrhea due to Spleen and Kidney Deficiency Cold.
Rz. Zingiberis Recens Sheng Jiang 1-240g Disperses Cold and warms the middle.
With Da Zao, alleviates epigastric pain, nausea and vomiting by strengthening the Spleen, protects Stomach Qi and reduces irritation of the Gastrointestinal tract by other herbs.
Fr. Jujube Da Zao 3-100g Strengthens the Spleen and tonifies Qi.
With Sheng Jiang, alleviates epigastric pain, nausea, and vomiting by strengthening the Spleen and enhances effects of other Qi and Blood tonics by facilitating their digestion and absorption.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Warms and Tonifies the Spleen and Kidneys
  • Astringes the Intestines
  • Stops diarrhea

SYNDROMES

  • Diarrhea due to Spleen and Kidney Yang Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Daybreak ("cock-crow") diarrhea
  • Low appetite
  • Inability to digest food
  • Sore low back
  • Cold limbs
  • Fatigue
  • Lethargy
  • Periumbilical pain
  • Maybe abdominal pain
  • Borborygmus just before dawn
  • T: Pale
  • C: Thin and white
  • P: Deep, forceless and slow

TREATS

  • Daybreak diarrhea
  • Chronic colitis
  • Allergic colitis
  • Chronic dysentery
  • Constipation
  • Spermatorrhea
  • Chronic pancreatitis
  • Alzheimer's disease
  • Abdominal Cold
  • Celiac disease
  • Hepatitis C
  • Fatigue
  • Autonomic dystonia of the intestinal tract
  • Tuberculosis of the colon
  • Irritable bowel syndrome
  • Lumbago
  • Enuresis
  • Tabes dorsalis
  • Abdominal fullness and distention
  • AIDS/HIV
  • Diarrhea
  • Crohn's disease
  • Anorexia
  • Ulcerative colitis

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with Stomach or Intestinal Stagnation.
  • Do not eat cold or raw foods while using this formula.
