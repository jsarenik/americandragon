HERB FORMULAS

INFERTILITY #38

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Fr. Evodiae Wu Zhu Yu

 

 1.5-10g Warms the Middle Jiao, disperses Cold, promotes the movement of Qi, alleviates pain, warms the Spleen, stops diarrhea and expels Damp-Cold.
Rx. Aconiti Lateralis Preparata Zhi Fu Zi 1.5-15g Warms Ming Men Fire,d assists Heart, Kidney and Spleen Yang, disperses Cold and Dampness, warms the channels and stops pain.
With Ren Shen, for severe Deficiency of Source Qi and Collapse of Yang with cold extremities, sweating, weak breathing, dizziness and an extremely pale complexion.
With Rou Gui, for Deficiency of Ming Men Fire with impotence or infertility.
With Fu Ling, for lower body edema and dysuria.
Cx. Cinnamomi Rou Gui 1.5-5g Warms the Kidneys, Spleen and Heart, strengthens Yang and Ming Men Fire, disperses deep Cold, warms the channels, unblocks the channels and vessels and alleviates pain.
With Wu Zhu Yu, for cold hernial disorders with abdominal pain.
Rx. Ginseng Ren Shen 1-9g Powerfully tonifies Yuan Qi and tonifies Spleen and Stomach Qi.
With Zhi Fu Zi, for collapsed Qi and Devastated Yang with spontaneous sweat, ice cold extremities and shortness of breath.
Per. Citri Reticulatae Chen Pi 3-9g Regulates Qi, adjusts the Middle, relieves the diaphragm, dries Dampness, descends Qi and transforms Phlegm.
Poria Fu Ling 9-18g Promotes urination, leaches out Dampness and strengthens the Spleen.
Fr. Schisandrae Wu Wei Zi 1.5-9g Tonifies the Kidneys, astringes Jing and tonifies Qi.
With Wu Zhu Yu, for chronic diarrhea due to Spleen and Kidney Deficiency Cold.
Rz. Acori Tatarinowii Shi Chang Pu 1.5-10g Transforms turbid Dampness, harmonizes the Middle Jiao, awakens the Spleen, promotes the movement of Qi and promotes Blood flow.
With Ren Shen and Fu Ling, for forgetfulness and dulled senses.
With Hou Po and Chen Pi, for pain and distention of the chest and epigastrium due to Turbid dampness Obstructing the Middle Jiao.
Rx. Angelicae Dahuricae Bai Zhi 3-10g Expels Wind, eliminates Dampness, dispels Cold and alleviates pain.
With Xi Xin, for Wind-Cold induced headaches, sinus headaches, orbital headaches and toothaches aggravated by cold air.
With Dang Gui, for vaginal infections.
Rx. Ampelopsis Bai Lian 3-16g Drains Fire, relieves toxicity, eliminates Dampness, reduces swelling and relieves pain.
Cx. Magnoliae Officinalis Hou Po 3-10g Promotes the movement of Qi in the Middle Jiao, resolves Stagnation, descends Qi, dries Dampness and transforms Phlegm.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 3-15g Tonifies, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood, disperses Cold and regulates the menses.
Rx. Achyranthis Bidentatae Niu Xi 6-16g Invigorates the Blood, expels Blood Stasis, nourishes Liver and Kidney Yin, and clears Damp-Heat in the Lower Jiao.
Hb. cum Rx. Asari Xi Xin 0.5-5g Disperses Wind-Cold and Internal Cold, alleviates pain and transforms Phlegm and Thin Mucus.
With Zhi Fu Zi, disperses Cold and stops pain.
Olibanum Ru Xiang 3-10g Invigorates the Blood, promotes the movement of Qi, relaxes the sinews, activates the channels and alleviates pain.
With Mo Yao, for Blood Stasis obstructing the channels.
Myrrh Mo Yao 3-12g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, alleviates pain and reduces swelling.
With Ru Xiang, for abdominal pain during menstruation and irregular menstruation.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Warms the channels
  • Transforms Phlegm
  • Regulates the menses
  • Clears Heat
  • Eliminates toxicity
  • Promotes fertility

SYNDROMES

  • Phlegm-Damp with Cold Stagnation

  • Amenorrhea
  • Mid cycle pain
  • Vaginal discharge
  • Adhesions
  • Obesity or overweight
  • General feeling of heaviness

  • A pale complexion
  • Puffiness
  • Dizziness
  • Lack of strength
  • Possible swelling and edema of the lower limbs
  • Chest tightness
  • Palpitation
  • A feeling of stuffiness in the chest
  • Anorexia
  • Nausea
  • Copious viscid vaginal discharge
  • Pain, distention, fullness and cold sensation in lower abdomen
  • Cold limbs
  • Dislike of cold and preference for warmth
  • Lower abdominal pain radiating to the testicles
  • May be localized sensation of Cold
  • Menstrual pain and hardness
  • Hypochondriac pain
  • Abdominal pain
  • Mobile abdominal masses
  • Flank pain
  • Leukorrhea
  • T: Pale or Fat
  • C: White or White and greasy
  • P: Slippery or Wiry and slippery or Slippery, soft and thready or Thready and retarded or Deep and slow or Wiry and tight or Deep and wiry or Slow or Deep and Excess

TREATS

  • Infertility
