HERB FORMULAS

MU FANG JI TANG - 木防己汤 - COCCULUS DECOCTION

English: Cocculus Decoction
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Aristolochiae seu Cocculi Mu Fang Ji 9g Dispels Wind, unblocks the channels and collaterals, eliminates Dampness, promotes urination, resolves toxicity and reduces swellings.
Gypsum Fibrosum Shi Gao 18g Clears Heat in the Qi Stage, drains Fire, relieves irritability, quenches thirst and clears Excess Heat from the Lungs.
With Ren Shen, for Excessive Heat with Qi Deficiency.
Rx. Ginseng Ren Shen 12g Powerfully tonifies Yuan and Lung Qi.
Ram. Cinnamomi Gui Zhi 6g Releases the Exterior, warms the channels and collaterals to relieve pain, assists Heart Yang, warms and facilitates the flow of Yang Qi in the chest, warms and facilitates the flow of Qi through the channels and collaterals and Blood through the vessels.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Activates Water circulation to eliminate Stagnation
  • Descends Qi

SYNDROMES

  • Water Accumulation in the Upper Jiao

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Shortness of breath
  • Increased dyspnea when lying down
  • Asthma
  • Abdominal pain
  • Chest and epigastric fullness and distention
  • A dark facial complexion
  • Scanty urination
  • Edema
  • P: Deep and tight

TREATS

  • Congestive Heart failure
  • Cardiac asthma
  • Edema due to nephritis
  • Nephritic edema
  • Dyspnea
  • Bronchial asthma
  • Pitting edema
  • Hypertension
  • Osteoarthritis
