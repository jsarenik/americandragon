HERB FORMULAS

BREAST CANCER #24

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Hb. Taraxaci Pu Gong Ying 15g Reduces abscesses, dissipates nodules, clears the Liver, clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Jin Yi Hua and Zi Hua Di Ding, for all hot, painful, hard, and deep-rooted boils.
Hb. Violae Zi Hua Di Ding 15g Clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity and clears hot sores.
With Pu Gong Ying, for breast abscesses, furuncles, boils and carbuncles.
Prepared Squama Manitis Pao Shan Jia 10g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, unblocks menstruation, promotes lactation, reduces swelling, promotes the discharge of pus and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Pu Gong Ying, for inflammatory lesions of the skin that have not yet pustulated.
With Bai Zhi, Pu Gong Ying and Zi Hua Di Ding, for sores without pus.
Rx. Astragali Huang Qi 20g Tonifies Qi and Blood, promotes the discharge of pus, generates flesh, expels toxins, relieves numbness and pain and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Dang Gui, for Qi and Blood Deficiency with chronic non-healing sores, numbness, painful obstruction and paresthesias.
Fr. Trichosanthis Gua Lou 20g Clears Lung Heat, transforms Hot Phlegm, regulates Qi, reduces abscesses, dissipates nodules and eliminates pus.
With Pu Gong Ying and Jin YIn Hua, for breast carbuncles.
Flos Lonicerae Jin Yin Hua 20g Clears Heat and resolves Fire toxicity.
With Gan Cao, for toxic swellings and sores.
With Pu Gong Ying and Zi Hua Di Ding, for sores, lesions carbuncles, ulceration of the skin and furuncles.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 10g Tonifies, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood, regulates the menses, disperses Cold, reduces swelling, expels pus, generates flesh and alleviates pain.
With Huang Qi, for ulcerated abscesses with insufficient drainage or which drain a clear thin discharge.
With Jin Yin Hua, reduces obstruction and toxicity for swollen, painful sores and necrosis of surrounding tissue.
Rx. Trichosanthis Tian Hua Fen 10g Drains Heat, generates Fluids, transforms Phlegm, relieves toxicity, expels pus, reduces swelling and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Angelicae Dahuricae Bai Zhi 6g Expels Wind, eliminates Dampness, dispels Cold, reduces swelling, eliminates toxins, expels pus and alleviates pain.
With Jie Geng, for ulcerated boils when pus has not been discharged.
With Dang Gui, for vaginal infections.
With Jin Yin Hua and Dang Gui, for Early-stage sores and carbuncles.
Rx. Platycodi Jie Geng 9g Expels Phlegm and pus.
With Gan Cao, for Excess disorders in the Upper Jiao with no disorder in the Lower Jiao.
With Bai Zhi, for ulcerated abscesses where pus is being discharged slowly.
Rx. Paeoniae Rubra Chi Shao 12g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, relieves pain, clears Heat, cools the Blood, clears Liver Fire, reduces swelling from sores and abscesses and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Bul. Allii Xie Bai 10g Unblocks Yang Qi, disperses turbid Phlegm, alleviates pain, disperses hardenings, disperses Cold Phlegm and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rz. Corydalis Yan Hu Suo 9g Invigorates the Blood, circulates Qi and stops pain.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 6g Clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity, inhibits the growth of cancer cells, moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs and guides the herbs to all twelve channels.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Reduces abscesses
  • Dissipates nodules
  • Clears the Liver
  • Clears Heat
  • Relieves Fire toxicity
  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Dispels Blood Stasis
  • Circulates Qi
  • Inhibits the growth of cancer cells

SYNDROMES

  • Liver Qi Stagnation

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Thirst
  • Chest and rib oppression and pain
  • Anxiety
  • Easily angered
  • Breast full and painful
  • Dry mouth
  • Dry pharynx
  • Dizziness
  • T: Dark
  • C: White or Light yellow
  • P: Wiry

TREATS

  • Breast cancer
