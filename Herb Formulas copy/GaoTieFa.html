HERB FORMULAS

GAO TIE FA - 膏贴沷 - CLEANSING PASTE (TOPICAL)

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Aucklandiae Mu Xiang 10g Promotes the movement of Qi, dispels Damp-Heat and alleviates pain.
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 15g Clears Heat and cools the Blood.
Resina Pini Song Xiang 10g Dries Dampness, dispels Wind, discharges pus, removes toxins, generates flesh and alleviates pain.
Sanguis Draconis Xue Jie 10g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, stops bleeding, protects the surface of ulcers, prevents decay, generates flesh and alleviates pain.
With Mo Yao and Ru Xiang, for pain, swelling or hemorrhage.
With Er Cha, Mo Yao and Ru Xiang, for bruising, swelling and pain.
With Ming Fan, stops bleeding without causing Stasis for sores that have perforated but fail to heal.
Olibanum Ru Xiang 10g Invigorates the Blood, promotes the movement of Qi, reduces swelling, generates flesh and alleviates pain.
Myrrh Mo Yao 10g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, alleviates pain, reduces swelling, generates flesh and promotes healing.
With Ru Xiang, topically for skin lesions and ulcerations swollen, painful sores and sores that are hard but not painful.
Catechu Er Cha 10g Drains Dampness and absorbs suppuration Stops bleeding and generates flesh.
With Xue Jie, Ru Xiang and Mo Yao, topically as a powder for non-healing oozing sores.
Alumen Ming Fan 10g Purges Fire, relieves toxicity, dries Dampness, alleviates itching, restrains and inhibits sores, generates flesh and transforms putrefication.
With Xiong Huang, topically for Wind-Damp-Heat toxin in the muscles and skin with severe itching that results in a yellow exudate after scratching.
With Er Cha, topically for bleeding from trauma.
Realgar Xiong Huang 0.6g Relieves toxicity, dries Dampness, expels Phlegm and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Ming Fan, for Wind-Damp sores and sores with redness, swelling, itching and pain.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Purges Fire
  • Relieves toxicity
  • Dries Dampness
  • Moves Qi
  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Stops bleeding
  • Discharges pus
  • Generates flesh

SYNDROMES

  • Topically for Toxic Heat with Blood Stagnation

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Tumor develops quickly
  • Fluid inside the breast with a lump inside it
  • Long-standing illness
  • Pain
  • Redness
  • Ulceration
  • Discharge of yellow or bloody, foul-smelling fluid
  • Fatigue
  • Fever
  • Anxiety
  • Dry mouth
  • Thirst
  • Dark urine
  • Bitter taste
  • Red eyes
  • Insomnia
  • T: Red
  • C: White or Yellow and greasy or Thick, sticky, dry and yellow
  • P: Wiry or Rolling and rapid or Wiry, overflowing and rapid

TREATS

  • Huge, infected mammary carcinoma
  • Breast cancer
