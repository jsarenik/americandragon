HERB FORMULAS

LUNG CANCER #31

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Fr. Trichosanthis Gua Lou 15g Clears Lung Heat, transforms Hot Phlegm, regulates Qi, unbinds the chest, reduces abscesses, dissipates nodules and eliminates pus.
Bul. Allii Xie Bai 15g Unblocks Yang Qi, disperses turbid Phlegm, alleviates pain, disperses hardenings and Cold Phlegm and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Gua Lou, for chest Bi with profuse Phlegm.
Exocarpium Citri Grandis Ju Hong 6g Regulates Qi, adjusts the Middle, relieves the diaphragm, dries Dampness, Descends Qi and transforms Phlegm.
Bul. Fritillariae Thunbergii Zhe Bei Mu 15g Clears Heat, transforms Phlegm, dissipates nodules, stops coughing and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Hb. Ephedrae Ma Huang 10g Disseminates and facilitates Lung Qi, calms wheezing and stops coughing.
With Xing Ren, for Wind-Cold cough and wheezing.
With Xing Ren and Shi Gao, for coughing from Heat Obstructing the Lungs with wheezing, flaring of the nostrils and fever.
Sm. Armeniacae Xing Ren 6g Stops coughing, calms wheezing and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Ma Huang and Shi Gao, for coughing with profuse sputum, nasal obstruction and a hoarse voice.
Cx. Mori Sang Bai Pi 15g Drains Heat from the Lungs, stops coughing, calms wheezing and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Flos Lonicerae Jin Yin Hua 30g Clears Heat, resolves Fire toxicity and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Gan Cao, for toxic swellings and sores.
With Shi Gao, Ma Huang, Xing Ren and Gan Cao, for bronchitis with Heat signs.
Fr. Gardeniae Zhi Zi 15g Clears Heat, reduces Fire, eliminates irritability, drains Damp-Heat, cools the Blood, relieves toxicity, reduces swelling, invigorates the Blood and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 3g Moistens the Lungs, resolves Phlegm, stops coughing, moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs and guides the herbs to all twelve channels and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Gypsum Fibrosum Shi Gao 30g Clears Excess Heat from the Lungs.
With Ma Huang, Shi Gao and Gan Cao for Lung Heat with coughing and asthma.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clear Heat
  • Resolve Phlegm
  • Inhibits the growth of cancer cells

SYNDROMES

  • Phlegm-Heat

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Cough with sticky, yellow, difficult to expectorate sputum
  • Rapid breathing with a harsh voice
  • Restless fever
  • Sore throat
  • Constipation
  • Dry stools
  • Mania
  • Shortness of breath
  • Dyspnea
  • Nausea
  • Vomiting
  • Chest and/or epigastric fullness
  • Pain in the ribs
  • T: Scarlet
  • C: Yellow or Yellow and greasy or Yellow and smooth
  • P: Rapid and smooth or Slippery and rapid

TREATS

  • Lung cancer
