HERB FORMULAS

SHEN FU TANG - 蔘附湯 - GINSENG AND PREPARED ACONITE DECOCTION

English: Ginseng and Prepared Aconite Decoction
Also Known As: Decoction of Ginseng and Aconite

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Ginseng Ren Shen 12-30g Strongly tonifies Yuan, Spleen, Lung, and Heart Qi.
With Zhi Fu Zi, for Collapsed Qi and Devastated Yang with spontaneous sweat, ice cold extremities, shortness of breath.
Rx. Aconiti Lateralis Preparata Zhi Fu Zi 6-30g Restores Devastated Yang, rescues from rebellion, warms the Ming Men Fire, assists Heart, Kidney and Spleen Yang, disperses Cold and Dampness, warms the channels and stops pain.
 With Ren Shen, for severe Deficiency of the Source Qi and Collapse of Yang with cold extremities, sweating, weak breathing, dizziness, and an extremely pale complexion.
(Rz. Zingiberis Recens) (Sheng Jiang) (3-6g) (Decoct in water with Da Zao.)
(Fr. Jujube) (Da Zao) (3-6g) (Decoct in water with Sheng Jiang.)

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Restores Yang
  • Strongly tonifies Yuan Qi
  • Rescues Qi from collapse due to Vanquished Yang

SYNDROMES

  • Heart Yang Deficiency Exhaustion: Vanquished Yang
  • Phlegm Obstructs the Heart Orifice: Exhaustion Syndrome
  • Shao Yin Cold

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Cold extremities
  • Spontaneous perspiration
  • Weak breathing
  • Shortness of breath
  • Dizziness
  • An extremely pale face
  • Intolerance of Cold
  • Cold abdominal pain
  • Loose stools
  • Upset stomach
  • T: Pale
  • P: Minute (almost imperceptible)

TREATS

  • Congestive Heart failure
  • Myocardial infarction
  • Cardiogenic shock
  • Postpartum hemorrhage
  • Post-surgical bleeding
  • Common cold
  • Dysentery
  • Bleeding peptic ulcer
  • Cor pulmonare
  • Cholecystitis
  • Hematemesis
  • Coronary Heart disease
  • Scleroderma
  • Dyspnea
  • Urinary incontinence
  • Rheumatic fever
  • Cough and asthma associated with AIDS/HIV
  • Uterine bleeding
  • Hypovolemic shock
  • Prostration
  • Apoplexy
  • Pulmonary failure
  • Influenza
  • Dizziness
  • Asthma
  • Cholelithiasis
  • Palpitations
  • Epistaxis
  • Spontaneous perspiration
  • Indigestion
  • Pneumonia
  • Lupus erythematosus
  • AIDS/HIV

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Not for extended use.
  • Do not use any substitutes for Rx. Ginseng Ren Shen.
HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

NOTES

  • Use only for emergency conditions.
  • Discontinue use once patient has regained consciousness.
