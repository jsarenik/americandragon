HERB FORMULAS

QING WEI SAN - 清胃散 - CLEAR THE STOMACH POWDER - COPTIS AND REHMANNIA FORMULA

English: Clear the Stomach Powder
Also Known As: Coptis and Rehmannia Formula
Powder for Clearing Away Stomach Heat

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rz. Coptidis Huang Lian 1-10g Clears Stomach Fire, drains Heat, relieves toxicity and stops bleeding.
Rz. Cimicifugae Sheng Ma 1-10g Relieves toxicity, raises and disperses Heat and guides other herbs upwards.
With Huang Lian, clears and drains Heart and Stomach Fire with gum swelling, toothache and ulcerations of the mouth and tongue.
With Sheng Di Huang, for toothache from Stomach Fire.
Cx. Moutan Mu Dan Pi 1.5-9g Cools the Blood and clears Heat.
With Sheng Di Huang, for Ying and Xue Stage Heat with a high fever, scarlet tongue, thirst, a rash all over the body, epistaxis and hemoptysis or residual Warm pathogen which has injured the Yin and Kidney Yin Deficiency with steaming bones.
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 9-15g Cools the Blood, nourishes Yin, cools Heart Fire and generates Fluids.
With Sheng Ma, cools Heat in the Lungs and Stomach stopping hemoptysis and hematemesis.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 9-12g Reduces swelling, expels pus, generates flesh and alleviates pain.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Drains Stomach Fire
  • Cools Blood
  • Nourishes Yin

SYNDROMES

  • Stomach Fire Blazing
  • Stomach Heat

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Toothache (especially when pain extends into the head)
  • Toothache is worse with Heat and better with Cold
  • Facial swelling
  • Fever
  • Bad breath
  • Dry mouth and tongue
  • Bleeding gums
  • Sores on the gums
  • Maybe scanty, red urine
  • Swollen, painful tongue
  • Swollen, painful lips
  • Swollen, painful jaw
  • Pain is better with Cold and worse with Heat
  • Swelling and pain of the throat
  • Feverish cheeks
  • Temporal headache
  • T: Red
  • C: Little or Yellow
  • P: Slippery, big and rapid
