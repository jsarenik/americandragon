HERB FORMULAS

ALOE AND SCIRPUS FORMULA

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Per. Citri Reticulatae Viride Qing Pi 3-10g Spreads Liver Qi, breaks up Stasis, dissipates accumulations, d Dries Dampness and transforms Phlegm.
With San Leng and E Zhu, for masses where prolonged Qi Stagnation has led to Blood Stagnation.
Fr. Aurantii Zhi Ke 3-10g Promotes the movement of Qi, reduces distention and pressure and resolves hardenings.
Rx. Aucklandiae Mu Xiang 3-10g Promotes the movement of Qi, strengthens the Spleen, prevents Stagnation and alleviates pain.
With Qing Pi, for abdominal pain, distention and belching.
Fr. Toosendan Chuan Lian Zi 3-15g Dredges Liver Qi, releases Stagnation, conducts Heat out from below and stops pain.
Rz. Sparganii San Leng 3-10g Forcefully breaks up Blood Stasis, regulates Qi, dissolves accumulations, regulates menstruation and alleviates pain.
With E Zhu, for Qi and Blood Stagnation with clumping and accumulation leading to pain with distending pain in the chest, epigastrium and abdomen, amenorrhea, dysmenorrhea, uterine myoma and ovarian cysts.
Rz. Curcumae E Zhu 3-10g Activates Blood circulation, dispels Blood Stasis, regulates Qi, dissolves accumulations and alleviates pain.
Flos Caryophylli Ding Xiang 1-5g Warms the Middle Jiao, descends Qi, warms the Kidneys, assists Yang and relieves pain.
Fr. Foeniculi Xiao Hui Xiang 3-12g Warms the Liver and Kidneys, expels Cold, regulates Qi, harmonizes the Stomach and alleviates pain.
With Chuan Lian Zi, strongly stops pain and regulates Qi to treat bulging disorders and mobile masses in the lower abdomen.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Activates the Blood
  • Circulates Qi
  • Warms the Kidneys and Spleen

SYNDROMES

  • Blood Stagnation

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Hard enlarged masses
  • Fixed pain
  • A dark gray complexion
  • Emaciation
  • Lassitude
  • Anorexia
  • Fever (or feverish feeling)
  • Aversion to Cold
  • Amenorrhea
  • T: Purple
  • P: Choppy or Taut and smooth

TREATS

  • Uterine fibroids
  • Amenorrhea
  • Uterine myoma
