HERB FORMULAS

ACUTE LEUKEMIA #2

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Sm. Strychni Ma Qian Zi 1.5g Unblocks the channels and collaterals, disperses clumps, cools Blood Heat to reduce swelling, alleviates pain and inhibits the activity of leukemia cells.
With Gan Cao, for numbness and paralysis of the extremities.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 4g Tonifies the Spleen, augments Qi, clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity, resolves Phlegm, inhibits the growth of cancer cells, moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs and guides the herbs to all twelve channels.
With Dang Shen, for anorexia, fatigue and loose stools due to Spleen Deficiency.
Rz. Paradis Chong Lou 12g Drains Heat, relieves Fire toxicity, reduces swelling and inflammation, stops pain and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Sophorae Tonkinensis Shan Dou Gen 9g Clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity, benefits the throat, disperses swellings and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With She Gan, for Phlegm-Heat in the throat with a swollen, sore throat and Phlegm that is difficult to expectorate.
Rz. Belamcandae She Gan 6g Clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity, improves the throat and transforms Phlegm.
Rx. Rubiae Qian Cao Gen 9g Cools and invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, stops bleeding and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 6g Tonifies, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood.
With Gan Cao, moderates spasms and relieves abdominal pain.
Rx. Codonopsis Dang Shen 6g Tonifies the Middle Jiao, augments Qi, nourishes the Blood and promotes the generation of Body Fluids.
With Huang Qi, for Lung and Spleen Qi Deficiency with fatigue, shortness of breath, diarrhea and anorexia and decreases platelet aggregation.
Rx. Astragali Huang Qi 30g Tonifies Qi and Blood, relieves numbness and pain and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Dang Gui, for Qi and Blood Deficiency with chronic non-healing sores, numbness, painful obstruction and paresthesias.
Hb. Pteris Feng Wei Cao 16g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, eliminates toxins, stops bleeding, drains Dampness, stops diarrhea, reduces inflammation and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Calculus Bovis Niu Huang 0.6g Drains Heat, resolves Fire toxicity and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Heat
  • Resolves toxicity
  • Tonifies Qi
  • Nourishes the Blood

SYNDROMES

  • Toxic Heat with Qi and Blood Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Long-standing illness
  • Pain
  • Redness
  • Fatigue
  • Fever
  • Anxiety
  • Dry mouth
  • Thirst
  • Dark urine
  • Bitter taste
  • Red eyes
  • Insomnia
  • Fatigue
  • Loose stools
  • Poor appetite
  • Dizziness
  • Pale face
  • Weak voice
  • Reluctance to speak
  • Sweating with little or no exertion
  • Palpitations
  • Insomnia
  • Dry skin
  • Dry hair
  • Poor memory
  • Paleness
  • Scanty periods
  • T: Red or Pale or Pale, thin and dry
  • C: Thin and white or White or Yellow and greasy or Thick, sticky, dry and yellow
  • P: Weak or Thin and choppy or Wiry or Rolling and rapid or Wiry, overflowing and rapid

TREATS

  • Acute leukemia
