HERB FORMULAS

SI NI SAN - 四逆散 - FRIGID EXTREMITIES POWDER

English: Frigid Extremities Powder
Also Known As: Bupleurum and Chih-Shih Formula
Powder for Relieving Depression with Cold Limbs

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Bupleuri Chai Hu 5-12g Resolves Shao Yang disorders, reduces fever,resolves Phlegm and congestion, disperses Wind-Heat, regulates Qi, relieves Stagnation and eliminates Heat.
Fr. Aurantii Immaturus Zhi Shi 2-12g Breaks up Stagnant Qi, reduces accumulation, descends Qi, unblocks the bowels and removes stagnant food to relieve Stagnation in the Middle Jiao and help restore the Spleen's function of transformation and transportation.
With Zhi Shi for abdominal pain due to Qi and Blood Stagnation.
Rx. Paeoniae Alba Bai Shao 4-24g Nourishes the Liver, nourishes the Blood, regulates menstruation, astringes and protects the Yin, calms Liver Yang, alleviates pain and adjusts the Ying and Wei.
With Zhi Gan Cao, moderates painful abdominal spasms.
With Chai Hu for flank pain due to Liver Qi Stagnation.
Honey-fried Rx. Glycyrrhizae Zhi Gan Cao 1.5-9g Tonifies the Spleen, augments Qi and moderates and harmonizes the properties of other herbs.
With Bai Shao, moderates painful abdominal spasms.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Releases the Exterior
  • Regulates Liver and Spleen
  • Relieves Stagnation
  • Spreads Liver Qi
  • Eliminates Heat in the Interior
  • Relieves mental depression

SYNDROMES

  • Jue Yin Heat
  • Shao Yin Heat
  • Liver and Spleen Disharmony (Liver Attacking the Spleen) with costal or abdominal pain and menstrual complaints
  • Gu Syndrome
