HERB FORMULAS

ZHI JING SAN - 止痙散 - STOP SPASMS POWDER

English: Stop Spasms Powder
Also Known As: Anticonvulsant Powder

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Scorpio Quan Xie 10-20g Extinguishes Wind, stops tremors and convulsions, tracks down Wind, unblocks the collaterals, attacks and relieves Fire toxins and
stops pain.
With Wu Gong, for muscle twitches of the extremities, rigidity and spasms of the entire body to the point of opisthotonis, trismus or convulsions.
Scolopendra Wu Gong 10-20g Extinguishes Wind, stops spasms and convulsions, attacks and relieves Fire toxins, unblocks collaterals and stops pain.
With Quan Xie, for Wind due to high fever leading to seizures and convulsions, epilepsy, stubborn migraines, Wind-Damp Bi pain and tetany.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Extinguishes Wind
  • Relieves spasms
  • Alleviates pain

SYNDROMES

  • Liver Wind Stirring Internally: Extreme Heat Generates Wind

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Muscle twitches of the extremities
  • Rigidity and spasms of the entire body
  • Opisthotonis
  • Cramps
  • Trismus
  • Convulsions
  • Maybe loss of consciousness
  • High fever
  • Contractures
  • Spasms
  • Neck stiffness
  • Convulsions
  • Oculogyric crises
  • Coma
  • Possibly death
  • T: Red or Deep red
  • P: Wiry and rapid

TREATS

  • Trigeminal neuralgia
  • Stroke
  • Arthralgia
  • Meningitis
  • Cluster headache
  • Cramps
  • Seizures
  • Parkinson's disease
  • Stubborn headaches
  • Migraine headaches
  • Encephalitis B
  • Tetanus
  • Pediatric epilepsy
  • Convulsion
  • Epilepsy
  • Neck stiffness and pain

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated during pregnancy
  • Do not take in large doses of for an extended period of time.
HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

NOTES

  • Grind equal amounts of the ingredients into a powder. Take 0.9 to 1.4g two to four times daily with warm water.
