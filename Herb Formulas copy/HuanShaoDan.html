HERB FORMULAS

HUAN SHAO DAN - 還少丹 - REJUVENATION SPECIAL PILL

English: Rejuvenation Special Pill
Also Known As: Return to Youth Pill  
Pill for Restoring Yang
Lycium Formula

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Rehmanniae Preparata Shu Di Huang 9-18g Nourishes the Jing and Blood and nourishes Liver and Kidney Yin.
With Shan Zhu Yu, nourishes Kidney Yin and secures Jing, while restraining urination and sweating.
With Shan Zhu Yu and Shan Yao, for dizziness, weak and sore back, impotence, spermatorrhea and night sweats due to Kidney and Liver Yin Deficiencies.
With Shan Zhu Yu, Shan Yao and Wu Wei Zi, for cough, shortness of breath and accelerated respiration.
Rz. Dioscoreae Shan Yao 9-45g Tonifies the Kidney Yin, astringes Jing, tonifies the Spleen, nourishes Stomach Yin, stops diarrhea, tonifies Lung Qi and nourishes Lung Yin.
Wine-soaked Rx. Achyranthis Bidentatae Niu Xi 9-45g Invigorates the Blood, expels Blood Stasis and nourishes the Liver and Kidney Yin.
With Du Zhong, for low back and extremity pain and weakness due to Bi pain or Kidney Deficiency.
Fr. Lycii Gou Qi Zi 9-15g Nourishes and tonifies Liver and Kidney Blood and Yin, benefits Jing and brightens the eyes.
Fr. Corni Shan Zhu Yu 9-30g Stabilizes the Kidneys, astringes Jing and Body Fluids, tonifies Liver and Kidney Yin and strengthens Kidney Yang.
With Wu Wei Zi, for spermatorrhea, abnormal sweating, palpitations and shortness of breath due to Yin and Yang Deficiencies of the Liver and Kidneys.
With Du Zhong, for low back pain and weakness.
Poria Fu Ling 9-30g Strengthens the Spleen, harmonizes the Middle Jiao, quiets the Heart, calms the Spirit and soothes the nerves.
Wine and Ginger Juice-fried Cx. Eucommiae Du Zhong 9-30g Tonifies the Kidneys and Liver, strengthens the sinews and bones, aids the smooth flow of Qi and Blood and tonifies Kidney Yang.
With Gou Qi Zi, for low back pain.
Rx. Polygalae Yuan Zhi 9-30g Calms the Spirit and sedates the Heart and clears the channel joining the Heart and Kidney.
Fr. Schisandrae Wu Wei Zi 6-30g Tonifies the Kidneys, astringes Jing, stops diarrhea and spermatorrhea, quiets the Spirit and calms and contains Heart Qi.
Wine-steamed Fr. Broussonetiae Chu Shi Zi 6-30g Tonifies the Liver and Kidney Yin, clears the Liver and clears the eyes and augments Kidney Qi.
Dry-fried Fr. Foeniculi Xiao Hui Xiang 6-30g Warms the Liver and Kidneys, expels Cold, and alleviates pain, regulates Qi, and harmonizes the Stomach.
Wine-soaked Rx. Morindae Officinalis Ba Ji Tian 9-30g Tonifies the Kidneys and strengthens Yang, disperses Wind-Cold-Damp and strengthens sinews and bones.
With Niu Xi, soaked in wine for impotence.
Hb. Cistanches Rou Cong Rong 12-30g Tonifies the Kidneys, strengthens the Yang, benefits Jing and marrow and warms the Womb.
With Du Zhong, for high blood pressure.
With Du Zhong and Ba Ji Tian, for deficient Kidney induced pain and weakness in the lower back with a feeling of weakness in the sinews and bones.
With Ba Ji Tian, tonifies Yang without injuring Yin.
Rz. Acori Tatarinowii Shi Chang Pu 6-15g Opens the Orifices, vaporizes Phlegm removes filth, calms the Spirit, disperses Wind, promotes Blood flow reduces swelling, alleviates Wind-Damp Bi, transforms turbid Dampness, harmonizes the Middle Jiao, awakens the Spleen and promotes the movement of Qi.
Fr. Jujube Da Zao 6-15g Tonifies the Spleen and Stomach, augments Qi, nourishes the Blood, calms the Spirit and moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs.
(Rz. Zingiberis Recens) (Sheng Jiang) (6-15g) (Warms the Middle and reduces the toxicity of the other herbs - included in some versions.)
(With Da Zao, alleviates epigastric pain, nausea, and vomiting by strengthening the Spleen, protects Stomach Qi and reduces irritation of the gastrointestinal. tract by other herbs and prevents tonics from generating Dampness in the Middle Jiao.)

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Tonifies the Spleen and Kidneys
  • Nourishes the Heart
  • Enriches the Yin
  • Nourishes the Blood and Yang
  • Augments Qi

SYNDROMES

  • Jing and Blood Deficiency due to Kidney, Heart and Spleen Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Fatigue
  • Heavy and painful lower back
  • Dream-disturbed sleep
  • General weakness
  • Lack of interest in food or drink
  • Fever
  • Night sweats
  • Seminal emission
  • Forgetfulness
  • Toothache
  • Dizziness
  • Gum swelling
  • General pitted edema which is worse in the lower body
  • Vaginal discharge
  • Discharge of rice-water like urination without dysuria
  • Aversion to Cold
  • Impotence
  • Decreased libido
  • Emaciation
  • Loose teeth
  • Signs of premature aging
  • Abdominal Cold
  • Easily frightened
  • T: Pale or Pale and thick
  • C: Smooth and white
  • P: Moderate and weak or Deep and weak

  • Decreased libido

CONTRAINDICATIONS

HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

NOTES
