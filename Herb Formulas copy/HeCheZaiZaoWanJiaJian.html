HERB FORMULAS

HE CHE ZAI ZAO WAN JIA JIAN - 河車再造囊加減 - MODIFIED PLACENTA RENEWAL PILL

English: Modified Placenta Renewal Pill
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Placenta Hominis Zi He Che 1.5-5g Tonifies the Liver and Kidneys, strengthens Jing, tonifies Qi and nourishes the Blood.
With Shu Di Huang, for chronic weakness and debility.
Hb. Cistanches Rou Con Rong 6-21g Tonifies the Kidneys, strengthens Yang,
benefits Jing and marrow and warms the Womb.
With Du Zhong and Ba Ji Tian, for deficient Kidney induced pain and weakness in the lower back with a feeling of weakness in the sinews and bones.
With Du Zhong, for high blood pressure.
Rx. Asparagi Tian Men Dong 5-15g Nourishes Kidney Yin, clears Lung Heat, sedates Fire, moistens the Lungs, nourishes the Kidneys, generates Fluids and resolves Phlegm.
With E Jiao, for Fire or Dryness in the Lungs and Kidneys with emaciation, tidal afternoon fever, parched throat and a dry cough with Blood streaked-sputum.
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 5-30g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, nourishes Yin and generates fluids.
With E Jiao, for coughing, hematemesis, epistaxis and uterine hemorrhage due to Heat from Deficiency.
With Bai Shao, for Blood Deficiency Heat.
With Shu Di Huang, for Heat symptoms due to Yin Deficiency and Devastated Blood.
Rx. Rehmanniae Preparata Shu Di Huang 9-30g Nourishes Jing and Blood, fills the Marrow and nourishes Liver and Kidney Yin.
With Gui Ban, for dizziness, insomnia, tinnitus, forgetfulness, afternoon fevers and night sweats due Yin Deficiency with Yang Rising.
Cx. Eucommiae Du Zhong 8-16g Tonifies the Kidneys and Liver, strengthens the sinews and bones, aids the smooth flow of Qi and Blood and tonifies Kidney Yang.
Rx. Morindae Officinalis Ba Ji Tian 6-15g Tonifies the Kidneys and strengthens Yang.
With Rou Cong Rong, tonifies Yang without injuring Yin.
Colla Corii Asini E Jiao 3-16g Tonifies the Blood and nourishes and moistens Yin.
With Tian Men Dong, for Fire or Dryness in the Lungs and Kidneys with emaciation, afternoon fever, parched throat and a dry cough with blood streaked-sputum.
Plastrum Testudinis Gui Ban 9-45g Nourishes Yin, anchors Yang, benefits the Kidneys, strengthens the bones, nourishes the Blood, enriches Yin and stabilizes the Chong and Ren channels.
With Bie Jia, calms the Liver, subdues Yang and extinguishes Wind to treat vertigo and tinnitus associated with Yin Deficiency with Yang Rising and deficient Wind moving internally.
With Bie Jia and E Jiao, for a low grade afternoon fever and night sweats due to Yin Deficiency with ascendant Yang.
Carapax Trionycis Bie Jia 9-30g Nourishes Yin and anchors Yang Invigorates the Blood.
With Gui Ban, for steaming bones and night sweats due to Liver and Kidney Yin Deficiency with Heat Rising.
With Gui Ban, Sheng Di Huang and Bai Shao, for Extreme injury to Yin leading to internal stirring of Liver Wind and tremors.
Rx. Paeoniae Alba Bai Shao 5-30g Nourishes the Blood, astringes Yin and adjusts the Ying and Wei.
With Gan Cao, for abdominal pain due to disharmony between the Liver and Stomach, epigastric, hypochondriac or flank pain and muscle spasms, pain and numbness, especially in the calf, due to Blood Deficiency.
With Sheng Di Huang and E Jiao, for Internal Stirring of Liver Wind, as in the aftermath of a febrile disease.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 3-14g Tonifies the Spleen, augments Qi, moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs and guides the herbs to all twelve channels.
With Bai Shao., moderates spasms and alleviates pain, for painful spasms of the leg or abdomen.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Reinforces the Kidneys
  • Benefits Jing
  • Boosts Qi
  • Nourishes the Blood

SYNDROMES

  • Kidney Jing Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Dizziness
  • Tinnitus
  • Low back and knees weak and aching
  • Hair loss
  • Graying or whitening of the hair
  • Loose teeth
  • Tooth loss
  • Unclear vision
  • Weak sexual function
  • Slow movement
  • Slow, dull, inappropriate responses
  • Atrophy and weakness of the feet with difficulty walking
  • Poor memory
  • Senility (premature or mature)
  • Dull mind
  • Brittle bones
  • Gradual deterioration of the hearing
  • Exhausted Spirit
  • In children:
    Retardation in mental and/or physical development
    Failure to thrive
    Late or incomplete closing of the fontanel
    Poor bone development
    Brittle bones
  • T: Pale
  • C: White
  • P: Weak

TREATS

  • AIDS/HIV/ARC
