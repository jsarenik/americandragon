HERB FORMULAS

BREAST CANCER #5

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Calculus Bovis Niu Huang 10g Clears the Liver, relieves Fire toxicity and drains Heat.
Olibanum Ru Xiang 15g Invigorates the Blood, promotes the movement of Qi, reduces swelling and generates flesh.
With Mo Yao, for traumatic injury pain, Blood Stasis obstructing the channels with epigastric, flank abdominal and joint pain and chronic, ulcerated and non-healing sores.
Myrrh Mu Yao 15g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, alleviates pain, reduces swelling, generates flesh and promotes healing.
With Ru Xiang, for swollen, painful sores and sores that are hard but not painful.
Rx. Astragali Huang Qi 30g Tonifies Qi and Blood, promotes the discharge of pus, generates flesh and expels toxins.
Pseudobulbus Cremastrae/Pleiones Shan Ci Gu 30g Clears Heat, resolves toxicity, reduces abscesses, reduces swelling and dissipates nodules
Spica Prunellae Xia Ku Cao 30g Clears Liver Fire, clears Hot Phlegm and dissipates nodules.
Rx. Notoginseng San Qi 60g Stops bleeding, invigorates the Blood, transforms Blood Stasis, reduces swelling, alleviates pain and removes extravasated Blood.
Rx Polygoni Multiflori He Shou Wu 60g Relieves Fire Toxin.
Sm. Coicis Yi Yi Ren 60g Strengthens the Spleen, resolves Dampness, clears Damp-Heat, clears Heat and expels pus.
Hb. Violae Zi Hua Di Ding 60g Clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity and clears hot sores.
Rz. Curcumae E Zhu 60g Invigorates Blood circulation, dispels Blood Stasis regulates Qi, dissolves accumulations and alleviates pain.
Hb. Epimedii Yin Yang Huo 60g Tonifies the Kidneys and strengthens Yang.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Relieves Fire toxicity
  • Drains Heat
  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Dispels Blood Stasis
  • Promotes the movement of Qi
  • Stops bleeding

SYNDROMES

  • Post surgical Blood Stagnation with Toxic Heat

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Post breast cancer surgery

TREATS

  • Post breast cancer surgery
