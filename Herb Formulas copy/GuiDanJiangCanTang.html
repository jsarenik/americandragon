HERB FORMULAS

GUI DAN JIANG CAN TANG - 鬼丹僵蠶汤 - BUCHNARIA, MOUTAN AND BATRYTICATUS DECOCTION

English: Buchnaria, Moutan and Batryticatus Decoction
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Hb. Buchnariae Gui Yu Jian 10g Clears Heat, cools the Blood and relieves toxicity.
Cx. Moutan Mu Dan Pi 10g Clears Heat, cools, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, drains pus and reduces swelling.
With Chi Shao, for purpuric rashes, hematemesis and Heat injuring the Ying and Xue levels.
With Chi Shao and Sheng Di Huang, for hematemesis and epistaxis due to Heat in the Xue level.
Rx. Salviae Miltiorrhizae Dan Shen 10g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, clears Heat, soothes irritability, cools the Blood and reduces abscesses.
With Mu Dan Pi and Sheng Di Huang, for Ying Stage Heat with high fever, irritability, hemoptysis, epistaxis, and subcutaneous bleeding.
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 10g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, nourishes Yin and generates fluids.
Rx. Paeoniae Rubra Chi Shao 10g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, relieves pain, clears Heat,d cools the Blood and reduces swelling from sores and abscesses.
Rx. Arnebiae/ Lithospermi Zi Cao 10g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, invigorates Blood circulation, relieves Fire toxins, encourages rashes, clears Damp-Heat from the skin and aids the healing of tissues.
Rx, Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 10g Tonifies, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood, reduces swelling, expels pus, generates flesh and alleviates pain.
Bombyx Batryticatus Jiang Can 10g Dispels Wind, disperses Wind-Heat, stops itching and pain, transforms Phlegm, softens hardness, eliminates toxins and dissipates nodules.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Scatters Wind
  • Clears Heat
  • Dries Dampness

SYNDROMES

  • Wind-Heat with Pre-existing Dampness

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Red lesions
  • Blisters
  • Fever
  • Sore throat
  • Dry mouth
  • Sore joints
  • Dry stools
  • T: Red tip
  • C: Thin or sticky yellow
  • P: Slippery and rapid

TREATS

  • Erythema multiforme
