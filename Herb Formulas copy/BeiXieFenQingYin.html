HERB FORMULAS

BEI XIE FEN QING YIN - 萆薢分清飲 - DIOSCOREA DECOCTION TO SEPARATE THE CLEAR - TOKORO COMBINATION

English: Dioscorea Hypoglauca Decoction to Separate the Clear
Also Known As: Tokoro Drink to Separate the Clear
Dioscorea Separate and Clear Cold Decoction
Tokoro Combination
Bi Xie Fen Qing Yin

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rz. Dioscoreae Hypoglaucae Bei Xie 4-12g Separates the pure from the turbid, and resolves turbid Dampness in the Lower Jiao.
With Wu Yao and Yi Zhi Ren, for Yang Deficiency causing frequent urination or urinary incontinence.
With Wu Yao, Yi Zhi Ren and Shi Chang Pu, for urine like rice porridge with no pain or Heat signs.
Fr. Alpiniae Oxyphyllae Yi Zhi Ren 4-15g Warms the Kidneys, retains Jing, astringes urine, warms the Spleen and stops diarrhea and salivation.
Rx. Linderae Wu Yao 4-9g Promotes the movement of Qi, alleviates pain, facilitates the Middle, disperses Cold and warms the Kidneys.
Rz. Acori Tatarinowii Shi Chang Pu 3-9g Harmonizes the Middle Jiao and transforms turbid Dampness.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Warms the Kidneys
  • Drains Dampness
  • Separates the turbid from the clear

SYNDROMES

  • Milky Lin (cloudy urination) due to Kidney Yang Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Frequent cloudy, milky urination
  • Maybe greasy urine
  • Thick, white vaginal discharge
  • T: Pale
  • C: White
  • P: Sinking, slow and weak

TREATS

  • Chronic prostatitis
  • Nephrotic syndrome
  • Acute exacerbations of chronic nephritis
  • Chronic pelvic inflammatory disease
  • Trichomonas
  • Dysuria
  • Galacturia
  • Turbid urine
  • Urinary tract infections
  • Vaginal itch
  • Oliguria
  • Low back pain
  • Spermatorrhea
  • Gonorrhea
  • Chronic cystitis
  • Urethritis
  • Chyluria
  • Polyuria
  • Chronic pyelonephritis
  • Impotence
  • Leukorrhagia
  • Pelvic cellulitis
  • Candida albicans
  • Thick, white vaginal discharge
  • Enuresis
  • Strangury

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with cloudy, painful urinary dysfunction due to Damp-Heat in the Bladder.
  • Contraindicated for those with Kidney Yin Deficiency.
