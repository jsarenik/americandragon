HERB FORMULAS

HUANG LIAN JIE DU TANG - 黃連解毒湯 - COPTIS DECOCTION TO RELIEVE TOXICITY

English: Coptis Decoction to Relieve Toxicity
Also Known As: Antiphlogistic Decoction of Coptis
Coptis and Scute Combination
Coptis Decoction to Resolve Toxicity

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rz. Coptidis Huang Lian 6-12g Clears Heat, drains Dampness, drains Fire and relieves toxicity.
With Huang Qin, drains pathogenic Heat from the Upper and Middle Jiaos for high fever, redness, swelling and pain of the head and face, dry mouth, parched throat, ulcerations of the mouth and tongue, epistaxis, rashes and irritability in warm-febrile disease and for explosive diarrhea and dysentery with burning pain in the anus or fitted, filthy stools and abdominal pain
With Huang Qin and Zhi Zi, for high fever, irritability, dry mouth and throat, and dark urine due to Fire toxin in the three burners.
Rx. Scutellariae Huang Qin 6-10g Clears Heat, resolves Fire toxins, invigorates the Blood, reduces swellings, promotes urination and reduces edema.
With Zhi Zi, powerfully clears and drains lurking Heat from the Lungs with fever, fullness in the chest, cough with yellow sputum and epistaxis or hematemesis to treat Damp-Heat and jaundice, sores due to Heat toxin and irregular vaginal bleeding due to Liver Fire.
Cx. Phellodendri Huang Bai 6-19g Drains Damp-Heat, especially from the Lower Jiao, drains Deficiency Fire, drains Fire and relieves toxicity.
With Zhi Zi Tan, for Lower Jiao Damp-Heat leading to hot, painful urinary dysfunction.
Charred Fr. Gardeniae Zhi Zi Tan 6-12g Clears Heat, reduces Fire, drains Damp-Heat, cools Blood and relieves toxicity.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Heat
  • Drains Fire
  • Relieves toxicity from all three Jiaos
  • Relieves inflammation
  • Stops bleeding
  • Purges Damp-Heat
  • Purges the Gallbladder

SYNDROMES

  • Obstruction of the Three Jiaos by Fire Toxin which pervades both the Exterior and Interior
  • Gu Syndrome
  • Lung Heat

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Use only for Excess patients with strong constitutions.
  • Do not use long term (it can injure Yin).
  • Contraindicated for those with Stomach Yin Deficiency with a red tongue and no coat.
HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

NOTES

  • This formula has been shown to have a marked effect in preventing iatrogenic mucositis such as stomatitis and gastrointestinal mucosal injury when treating leukemia patients with cytotoxic drugs.
  • This formula may be effective in treating aspirin-and ethanol-induced gastric lesions.
