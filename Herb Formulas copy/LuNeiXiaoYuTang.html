HERB FORMULAS

LU NEI XIAO YU TANG

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Moschus She Xiang 0.03-0.1g Strongly opens the Orifices and Luo vessels, revives the Spirit, unblocks closed disorders, activates the Blood, opens the channels and alleviates pain.
Rz. Chuanxiong Chuan Xiong 3-10g Invigorates the Blood and promotes the movement of Qi.
With Xiang Fu, for Qi Stagnation combined with Blood Stasis.
Sanguis Draconis Xue Jie 0.3-0.6g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, alleviates pain and stops bleeding.
Rx. Salviae Miltiorrhizae Dan Shen 3-15g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, nourishes the Blood and calms the Spirit.
With Ru Xiang, for Blood Stasis causing swelling and pain.
Sm. Persicae Tao Ren 4-10g Breaks up Blood Stasis and invigorates Blood circulation.
With Hong Hua, invigorates the Blood for all types of Blood Stagnation.
Flos Carthami Hong Hua 3-10g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, opens the channels, unblocks menstruation and alleviates pain.
With Chuan Xiong, for chest and abdominal pain due to Qi and Blood Stasis.
With San Leng and E Zhu, for abdominal masses and accumulations.
Rx. Paeoniae Alba Bai Shao 5-30g Nourishes the Blood, astringes Yin, adjusts the Ying and Wei, softens the Liver, calms Liver Yang and Liver Wind and alleviates pain.
Rx. Paeoniae Rubra Chi Shao 4.5-15g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, relieves pain, clears Heat, cools the Blood, clears Liver Fire, relieves eye pain and reduces swelling from sores and abscesses.
With Ru Xiang and Mo Yao, for bruising, swelling, and pain from traumatic injury.
With Tao Ren, Hong Hua and Chuan Xiong, for Blood Stagnation in the chest.
With Ru Xiang, Mo Yao, San Leng and E Zhu, for palpable abdominal masses.
Olibanum Ru Xiang 3-10g Invigorates the Blood, promotes the movement of Qi, reduces swelling and generates flesh.
With Mo Yao, for traumatic injury pain and Blood Stasis obstructing the channels with epigastric, flank abdominal and joint pain.
Rx. Sparganii San Leng 3-10g Forcefully breaks up Blood Stasis, regulates Qi and alleviates pain.
With E Zhu, for Qi and Blood Stagnation with clumping and accumulation leading to pain with distending pain in the chest, epigastrium and abdomen, amenorrhea, dysmenorrhea, uterine myoma and ovarian cysts.
Myrrh Mo Yao 3-12g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, alleviates pain and reduces swelling.
With Hong Hua, for chest and abdominal pain, amenorrhea and dysmenorrhea due to Blood Stasis.
Rz. Curcumae E Zhu 3-10g Invigorates Blood circulation, dispels Blood Stasis regulates Qi and alleviates pain.
Rz. Cyperi Xiang Fu 6-14g Spreads and regulates Liver Qi and alleviates pain.
Eupolyphaga/Steleophaga Tu Bie Chong 3-12g Breaks and drives out up Blood Stasis, invigorates Blood circulation and disperses lumps and masses.
Rz. Zingiberis Gan Jiang 3-10g Warms the Middle, expels Cold, warms the channels (unblocks the pulse) and stops bleeding.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Activates the Blood
  • Opens the channels
  • Alleviates pain
  • Forcefully breaks up Blood Stasis
  • Regulates Qi

SYNDROMES

  • Blood Stagnation

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Headache
  • Vertigo
  • Chronic tinnitus
  • Hair loss
  • Dark purple complexion or
  • Blue or green facial complexion with or without rosacea or
  • Vitilago
  • Darkness around the eyes
  • Brandy nose
  • Progressive emaciation
  • Abdominal distention
  • Purplish discoloration of the sinews
  • Tidal fever
  • Dizziness
  • Deafness
  • Pale face
  • Cyanosis
  • Phthisis
  • Muscle weakness
  • Sudden loss of vision
  • T: Dark or with petechiae
  • C: Normal
  • P: Choppy

TREATS

  • Acute and chronic epidural hemorrhage
