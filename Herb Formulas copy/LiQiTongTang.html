HERB FORMULAS

LI QI TONG TANG - 理氣通湯 - REGULATE QI AND CLEAR STAGNATION DECOCTION

English: Regulate Qi and Clear Stagnation Decoction
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Sm. Citri Reticulatae Ju He 10g Promotes the movement of Qi, dissipates clumps and stops pain.
Rx. Bupleuri Chai Hu 10g Spreads Liver Qi and relieves Stagnation.
With Bai Shao, for vertigo, dizziness and thoracic and costal pain due to Liver Qi Stagnation.
With Zhi Ke, for a stifling sensation in the chest, abdominal pain, poor appetite and irregular bowel movements.
Fr. Aurantii Zhi Ke 10g Promotes the movement of Qi and reduces distention and pressure.
Rx. Paeoniae Alba Bai Shao 10g Nourishes the Blood, astringes Yin, adjusts the Ying and Wei, softens the Liver and relieves pain.
With Chai Hu, for flank pain due to Liver Qi Stagnation.
Hb. Lycopodii Shen Jin Cao 15g Relaxes the sinews, invigorates the collaterals and invigorates the Blood
Sm. Cnidii She Chang Zi 12g Warms and tonifies the Kidneys and strengthens Yang.
Sm. Alli Tuberosi Jiu Cai Zi 12g Supplements the Liver, warms the Kidneys, fortifies Yang and astringes Jing.
Bul. Allii Xie Bai 9g Unblocks Yang Qi, alleviates pain, descends Qi and reduces Stagnation.
Sm. Vaccariae Wang Bu Liu Xing 9g Promotes the movement of Blood and invigorates the channels.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Soothes the Liver
  • Clears Stagnation
  • Supports Yang

SYNDROMES

  • Liver Qi Stagnation

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Impotence
  • Inability to get or sustain an erection
  • Loss of libido
  • Distention and pain in the sides or flanks
  • Suffocating sensation in the chest
  • Hypochondriac tightness or distention
  • Vague aches and pains
  • Dizziness
  • Occasional fatigue, better with exercise
  • Abdominal distention
  • Flatulence
  • Tendency to sigh
  • Melancholy
  • Depression
  • Irritability
  • Inappropriate anger
  • Alternating constipation and diarrhea
  • T: Pink
  • C: Thin and white
  • P: Wiry

TREATS

  • Impotence
