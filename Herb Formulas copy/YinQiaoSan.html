HERB FORMULAS

YIN QIAO SAN - 银翘散 - HONEYSUCKLE AND FORSYTHIA POWDER

English: Honeysuckle and Forsythia Powder
Also Known As: Lonicera and Forsythia Combination
Powder of Lonicera and Forsythia

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Flos Lonicerae Jin Yin Hua 9-30g Disperses Wind, clears Heat and relieves toxicity.
With Gan Cao, for toxic swelling and sores.
With Lian Qiao and Niu Bang Zi , for Wei Stage and Qi Stage Heat and pneumonia with Heat signs
With Lian Qiao, Jing Jie and Bo He, for Wind-Heat Attacks the Lungs.
With Lian Qiao, Jing Jie and Niu Bang Zi, for throat swelling and pain
Fr. Forsythiae Lian Qiao 9-30g Disperses Wind, clears Heat and relieves toxicity.
With Niu Bang Zi and Bo He, for Wei Stage Heat.
With Jin Yin Hua, for Initial stage Wind-Heat.
Rx. Platycodi Jie Geng 3-12g Disperses the Lungs, stops cough, resolves Phlegm and descends Qi.
With Gan Cao, for hoarseness and throat swelling and pain due to Wind-Heat Attacks the Lungs.
Fr. Arctii Niu Bang Zi 6-12g Disperses Wind-Heat, benefits the throat, relieves Heat and toxins, and spreads Lung Qi.
 With Bo He, for sores due to Wind-Heat, dry throat, cough with yellow sputum, incomplete expression of measles or pruritic rash.
With Jie Geng and Bo He, and Jing Jie, for throat inflammation and productive cough due to Wind-Heat Attacks the Lungs.
With Jin Yin Hua, Bo He, Lian Qiao, and Jie Geng, for Sore throat caused by a febrile disorder or Wind-Heat.
Hb. Menthae Bo He 3-6g Relieves Wind-Heat and benefits the throat.
 With Jing Jie, for Wind-Heat.
With Jing Jie, Lian Qiao and Jin Yin Hua, for Wind-Heat Attacks the Lungs.
With Jing Jie, Niu Bang Zi and Lian Qiao, promotes the eruption of measles
With Niu Bang Zi, for Wind-Heat manifesting as sores, rashes, dry throat and cough with yellow sputum.
Sm. Sojae Preparata Dan Dou Chi 3-15g Relieves the exterior.
With Niu Bang Zi and Lian Qiao, for Wind-Heat.
With Bo He, Jing Jie and Niu Bang Zi, for early stage Wind-Heat with fever and headache.
Hb. Lophatheri Dan Zhu Ye 3-10g Generates Fluids, alleviates thirst and clears Heat.
Hb. Schizonepetae Jing Jie 3-9g Relieves the exterior.
 With Bo He, for Wind in the head.
With Bo He, Jie Geng and Gan Cao, for acute cough associated with Wind.
With Bo He, Jin Yin Hua and Lian Qiao, for External Wind-Heat with fever, headache and sore throat.
With Bo He, Liang Qiao and Jie Geng, for Wind-Heat.
Rz. Phragmitis Lu Gen 6-30g Generates Fluids, alleviates thirst and clears Lung Heat.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 3-6g Harmonizes, generates Fluids, alleviates thirst, moistens the Lungs, resolves Phlegm, stops cough and clears Heat.
With Jie Geng, for a sore throat in which the throat is not very red.
With Jin Yin Hua, for damp rashes or acne.
With Niu Bang Zi, strongly clears throat and Lungs to treat Wind-Heat Attacks the Lungs or Fire toxin flaring upwards.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Disperses Wind-Heat
  • Cools Heat
  • Relieves toxicity

SYNDROMES

  • Wind-Heat Attacks the Lungs
  • Wen Bing - Wei Stage Wind-Heat
  • Dry Heat
  • Summer Heat with Dampness
  • Damp Heat

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Dry cough
  • Sore, red, swollen throat
  • Slight thirst, with desire to drink cold liquids
  • Runny or blocked nose
  • Fever with little perspiration
  • Slight chills
  • Maybe high fever
  • Aversion to Wind
  • Headache
  • May be dyspnea with flared nostrils
  • T: Red tip
  • C: Thin and yellow or white
  • P: Superficial and rapid

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • This formula must be significantly modified to treat Damp-Heat.
  • Contraindicated for Wind-Cold.
