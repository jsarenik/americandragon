HERB FORMULAS

DING ZHI WAN -定志丸 - SETTLE THE EMOTIONS PILL

English: Settle the Emotions Pill
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Ginseng Ren Shen 9g Tonifies Heart Qi and calms the Spirit
With Fu Ling, Shi Chang Pu and Yuan Zhi, for confusion and forgetfulness.
Poria Fu Ling 9g Quiets the Heart, calms the Spirit and soothes the nerves.
With Shi Chang Pu and Yuan Zhi, for insomnia and palpitations due to Spleen and Heart Insufficiency or Phlegm Obstructing the Heart.
Rz. Acori Tatarinowii Shi Chang Pu 6g Opens the Orifices, vaporizes Phlegm, removes filth, calms the Spirit and disperses Wind.
With Yuan Zhi and Fu Ling, for forgetfulness, tinnitus, and impaired hearing.
With Ren Shen, Fu Ling and Yuan Zhi, for forgetfulness and dulled senses.
Rx. Polygalae Yuan Zhi 6g Calms the Spirit and sedates the Heart and clears the channel joining the Heart and Kidney.
With Ren Shen, Fu Ling and Shi Chang Pu, calms the spirit.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Calms the Shen
  • Tonifies the Heart Qi
  • Pacifies timidity

SYNDROMES

  • Heart and Gallbladder Qi Deficiency
  • Heart Qi Deficiency and Stagnation due to Phlegm Turbidity

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Insomnia or frequent waking, often early in the morning
  • Unable to fall back to sleep
  • Nightmares
  • Waking terrified
  • Easily frightened or startled
  • Easily unsettled by seeming trivial events
  • Apprehension
  • Restlessness
  • Fearfulness
  • Inappropriate worry
  • Timidity
  • Phobias
  • Anxiety (frequent and severe)
  • Feeling disheartened
  • Forgetfulness
  • Frequent panic attacks
  • Palpitations
  • Shortness of breath
  • Incessant laughter and glee
  • Lethargy
  • Fatigue
  • Depression
  • Postnatal depression
  • Spontaneous sweating
  • Worry
  • Dizziness
  • T: Normal or Pale (chronic or congenital cases may show a deep narrow crack to the tip
  • C: Thin and white
  • P: Wiry and thready or Weak, thready and rapid

TREATS

  • Anxiety neurosis
  • Mental disorders
  • Obsessive-compulsive disorder
  • Seizures
  • Palpitations
  • Bi-polar disorder
  • Anxiety
  • Sinus tachycardia
  • Premenstrual syndrome
  • Spontaneous perspiration
  • Dyspnea
  • Postnatal depression
  • Depression
  • Myopia
  • Cardiac arrhythmia
  • Insomnia
  • Neurosis
  • Panic attacks
  • Neurasthenia
  • Fatigue
  • Spermatorrhea

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Modify for those with Yin or Blood Deficiency.
HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

NOTES

MODIFICATIONS

+ 6g Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui + 6g Fr. Schisandrae Wu Wei Zi
+ 6g Rx. Paeoniae Alba Bai Shao + 9g Sm. Platycladi Bai Zi Ren

+ 6g. Caulis Polygoni Multiflori Ye Jiao Teng

+ 6g Fr. Hordei Germinatus Mai Ya
