HERB FORMULAS

DA JIAN ZHONG TANG - 大建中湯 - MAJOR CONSTRUCT THE MIDDLE DECOCTION - MAJOR ZANTHOXYLU COMBINATION

English: Major Construct the Middle Decoction
Also Known As: Major Zanthoxylum Combination
Major Decoction for Restoring the Normal Function of the Middle Burner

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Per. Zanthoxyli Chuan Jiao 2-9g Stimulates the Yang of the Middle Jiao, attacks and dispels Cold, disperses accumulations, stops pain and kills worms.
With Gan Jian, Ren Shen and Yi Tang, for Coldness and pain in the chest with vomiting and inability to eat.
Rz. Zingiberis Gan Jiang 5-12g Warms the Middle Jiao, expels Interior Cold, rescues Devastated Yang and transforms Phlegm.
Rx. Ginseng Ren Shen 3-9g Tonifies Spleen and Stomach Qi.
With Yi Tang, tonifies and strengthens the middle Qi, ameliorating painful spasms.
Maltosum Yi Tang 18-60g Tonifies the Spleen and moderates spasmodic abdominal pain and Coldness.
With Chuan Jiao, Ren Shen and Gan Jiang, for Middle Jiao Yang Deficiency with ascendant Yin marked by chest and abdominal pain, cold limbs and vomiting.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Warms and tonifies Middle Jiao Yang
  • Directs rebellious Qi downward
  • Relieves pain

SYNDROMES

  • Middle Jiao Yang Deficiency with Cold retention.
  • Jue Yin Cold

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Excruciating epigastric and abdominal pain to the point of not being able to to be touched
  • May be borborygmus.
  • Vomiting to the point of not being able to eat
  • Superficial skin pulsations throughout the abdomen
  • May be fluid sounds
  • May be cold limbs
  • Strong sensation of Cold in the chest and epigastrium.
  • Severe pain in the head and feet
  • T: Pale or Dark purple
  • C: White and greasy or Slippery
  • P: Thin and tight or slow and wiry or Deep and thready or Hidden

TREATS

  • Gastric ulcer
  • Gastric spasms
  • Duodenal ulcer
  • Chronic gastritis
  • Chronic pancreatitis
  • Chronic cholecystitis
  • Urinary calculi
  • Peritonitis
  • Intestinal spasms
  • Slow motility of the Intestines
  • Morphine-induced constipation in cancer patients
  • Nausea/Vomiting
  • Weakness
  • Jaundice
  • Indigestion
  • Cholelithiasis
  • Uncomplicated intestinal obstruction.
  • Biliary ascariasis
  • Parasites (round worms)
  • Hernia
  • Intestinal spasms
  • Intestinal flaccidity
  • Intestinal colic
  • Acute appendicitis
  • Postoperative ileus
  • Severe constipation
  • Obstructive bowel disease
  • Fatigue
  • Headache
  • Gastroenteritis
  • Chemotherapy for breast cancer

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with Internal Stagnation.
  • Contraindicated for those with Damp-Heat.
  • Contraindicated for those with Yin Deficiency.
  • Contraindicated for those with Blood Deficiency.
