HERB FORMULAS

BAO JIAN MEI JIAN FEI CHA - 拼音名 - BOJENMI CHINESE TEA

English: Maintaining Vigor and Beauty and Reducing Fat Tea
Also Known As: Bojenmi Chinese Tea

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Fol. Camelliae Sinensis Lu Cha 50g Harmonizes the Stomach, descends Rebellious Stomach Qi, relieves diarrhea, dispels Dampness, removes stagnant Food, promotes diuresis and removes toxin.
Fr. Crataegi Shan Zha 9g Reduces and guides out Food Stagnation, transforms accumulations and Blood Stasis, invigorates Blood circulation, dissipates clumps and stops diarrhea.
With Shen Qu, for general indigestion and Food Stagnation.
With Mai Ya and Shen Qu, for abdominal distention, belching, diarrhea and anorexia due to Food Stagnation.
Spica Prunellae Xia Ku Cao 8g Clears Liver Fire, clears Hot Phlegm and dissipates nodules.
Poria Fu Ling 4g Promotes urination, leaches out Dampness , strengthens the Spleen, harmonizes the Middle Jiao and transforms Phlegm.
With Ze Xie, facilitates the removal of water and leaches out Dampness to treat stagnant water and Dampness leading to edema, jaundice, diarrhea, Milky Lin, and scanty, difficult urination with abdominal distention.
Sm. Phaseoli Radiati Lu Dou 4g Resolves Summerheat, relieves thirst, clears Heat and relieves toxins.
Hb. Agastaches/Pogostemonis Huo Xiang 4g Aromatically transforms Dampness, releases the exterior, dispels Summerheat, transforms turbidity, harmonizes the Middle Jiao and stops vomiting.
Fr. Hordei Germinatus Mai Ya 3.5g Reduces Food Stagnation, improves digestion and strengthens the Stomach.
Per. Citri Reticulatae Chen Pi 3.5g Regulates Qi, adjusts the Middle, relieves the diaphragm, dries Dampness, transforms Phlegm and Descends Qi.
Massa Medicata Fermentata Shen Qu 3.5g Reduces Food Stagnation, promotes digestion, harmonizes the Stomach and Moves Qi.
Sm. Cassiae Jue Ming Zi 3g Clears the Liver, moistens the Intestines and unblocks the bowels.
With Dang Gui, for constipation due to dry Intestines.
Sm. Pharbitidis Qian Niu Zi 3g Drives out water through the urine and stool, descends Lung Qi, drives out Phlegm and thin mucus, unblocks the bowels, removes Damp-Heat, expels intestinal parasites and reduces Stagnation.
Rz. Alismatis Ze Xie 2.5g Promotes urination and leaches out Dampness.
With Fu Ling, facilitates the removal of water and leaches out Dampness to treat stagnant water and Dampness leading to edema, jaundice, diarrhea, Milky Lin, and scanty, difficult urination with abdominal distention.
Sm. Raphani Lai Fu Zi 2g Reduces Food Stagnation, transforms accumulation, promotes digestion, eliminates distention, descends Qi and reduces Phlegm.
With Shan Zha, Shen Qu and Chen Pi, for abdominal distention, borborygmus, belching, and acid regurgitation due to Food Stagnation in the Stomach or Intestines.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Disperses Dampness
  • Moves Qi
  • Tonifies Stomach and Spleen
  • Transforms Phlegm

SYNDROMES

  • Nutritional impairment due to Spleen Qi Deficiency and Stagnation of Dampness or Phlegm

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Obesity with a long history of a bad diet
  • Dislike of speaking
  • Sweating on exertion
  • Aversion to Cold
  • Puffy face
  • Poor appetite
  • Abdominal fullness after eating
  • Fatigued spirit
  • Weakness of the extremities
  • Somnolence
  • Loose stools
  • Clear, thin vaginal discharge
  • Bland taste in the mouth
  • T: Pale with teethmarks
  • C: White
  • P: Fine and weak

TREATS

  • Atherosclerosis
  • Hypercholesterolemia
  • Hyperlipidemia
  • Poor appetite
  • Fatigue
  • Hypertension
  • Obesity
  • Abdominal Fullness and distention
  • Weakness
