HERB FORMULAS

STOMACH CANCER #22

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Haematitum Dai Zhe Shi 15g Calms the Liver, clears Liver Fire and strongly descends rebellious Qi.
With Zhi Ban Xia, Xuan Fu Hua and Dang Shen, for vomiting, hiccup, and belching due to rebellious Qi.
Flos Inulae Xuan Fu Hua 6g Calms rebellious Stomach Qi.
With Fu Hai Shi, for Cough with viscous difficult-to-expectorate sputum and sensations of tightness in the chest due to Phlegm-Heat.
With Zhi Ban Xia, for vomiting, cough, wheezing and epigastric distention due to congested fluids.
Clear Water Rz. Pinelliae Qing Shui Ban Xia 12g Dries Dampness, transforms Phlegm, harmonizes the Stomach, dissipates nodules and reduces Stagnation for weak patients.
Hirudo Shui Zhi 6g Breaks up and drives out Blood Stasis and reduces fixed masses.
Scolopendra Wu Gong 4pcs Attacks and relieves Fire toxins, relieves abdominal masses and dissipates nodules.
Concha Ostreae Mu Li 30g Calms the Liver, softens hardness and dissipates nodules.
Os Costaziae Fu Hai Shi 15g Softens harnesses and dissipates Phlegm nodules
Rx. Codonopsis Dang Shen 15g Tonifies the Middle Jiao, augments Qi, restores the constitution and assists herbs that drain downward when there is significant Qi Deficiency.
Endothelium Corneum Gigeriae Galli Ji Nei Jin 9g Strongly reduces Food Stagnation, improves the Spleen’s transportive function, transforms hardness and dissolves stones.
Fr. Hordei Germinatus Mai Ya 9g Reduces Food Stagnation, improves digestion, strengthens the Stomach and facilitates the smooth flow of Liver Qi.
Fr. Perillae Zi Su Zi 9g Descends Qi and dissolves Phlegm.
Dry-fried Caulis Bambusae in Taeniam Chao Zhu Ru 15g Clears Heat, transforms Hot Phlegm and stops vomiting.
With Lu Gen, for irritability, thirst, and vomiting due to depletion of fluids caused by Heat.
Rz. Phragmitis Lu Gen 30g Clears Stomach Heat, regulates Stomach Qi, relieves thirst and stops vomiting.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Calms the Liver
  • Strongly descends rebellious Qi
  • Transforms Phlegm
  • Harmonizes the Stomach
  • Relieves abdominal masses
  • Augments Qi
  • Clears Heat

SYNDROMES

  • Liver Qi Stagnation Attacks the Spleen and Stomach

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Distention and pain in the Stomach
  • Epigastric distention and pain (worse when emotionally upset)
  • Distention and pain in the sides or flanks
  • Weakness
  • Weight loss
  • Fetid belching or hiccupping
  • Acid regurgitation
  • Difficulty swallowing
  • Nausea
  • Vomiting
  • Anorexia
  • Suffocating sensation in the chest
  • Tendency to sigh
  • Melancholy
  • Depression
  • Irritability
  • Inappropriate anger
  • Irregular menses
  • Dysmenorrhea
  • Breast distention and pain before and during menses
  • T: Pink
  • C: Thin and white
  • P: Wiry or Thready and wiry or Deep and thready

TREATS

  • Stomach cancer
