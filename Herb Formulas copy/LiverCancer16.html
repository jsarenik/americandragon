HERB FORMULAS

LIVER CANCER #16

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Pseudostellariae Tai Zi Shen 30g Strengthens the Spleen, tonifies Qi, generates Body Fluids and treats chronic febrile disorders.
Rx. Astragali Huang Qi 30g Tonifies Qi and Blood, promotes the discharge of pus, generates flesh, expels toxins, relieves numbness and pain and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Salviae Miltiorrhizae Dan Shen 15g Invigorates and nourishes the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, cools the Blood, reduces abscesses and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Curcumae Yu Jin 15g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, regulates Qi flow, speeds healing of chronic sores and alleviates pain.
Flos Campsis Ling Xiao Hua 12g Dispels Blood Stagnation, unblocks the channels and vessels and disperses abdominal masses.
Sm. Persicae Tao Ren 10g Breaks up Blood Stasis, invigorates Blood circulation and drains abscesses.
Fr. Akebiae Ba Yue Zha 20g Dredges the Liver, regulates Qi, controls pain, expels Phlegm, disperses clumps, invigorates the Blood, removes toxicity and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rz. Cyperi Xiang Fu 9g Spreads and regulates Liver Qi and alleviates pain.
Carapax Trionycis Bie Jia 12g Nourishes Yin, anchors Yang, invigorates the Blood, softens hardness,d dissipates nodules and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Huang Qi and Long Gu, for chronic non-healing sores and ulcerations.
Scorpio Quan Xie 10g Tracks down Wind, unblocks the collaterals, stops pain, attacks and relieves Fire toxins, dissipates nodules and rashes and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Concha Ostreae Mu Li 15g Calms the Liver, benefits Yin, anchors Floating Yang, softens hardness, dissipates nodules and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Fossilia Ossis Mastodi Long Gu 15g Calms the Liver and anchors and preserves Floating Yang.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Tonifies Qi
  • Regulates Qi
  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Breaks up Stasis
  • Expels Phlegm
  • Dissipates nodules
  • Inhibits the growth of cancer cells

SYNDROMES

  • Qi Deficiency with Qi and Blood Stagnation (late stage)

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Fatigue
  • Loose stools
  • Anorexia
  • Dizziness
  • Pale face
  • Weak voice
  • Reluctance to speak
  • Sweating with little or no exertion
  • Pain that is fixed, stabbing and /or severe or
  • Pains that move around
  • Distention
  • Depression
  • Irritability
  • Pan is worse with pressure
  • Dark complexion
  • Fixed, palpable masses
  • Bleeding with clots
  • Purple lips
  • Purple nails
  • Dry skin
  • Blue, dark or purplish discoloration of skin in small or large areas,
  • Varicose and spider veins
  • Cold hands and feet
  • Tendency to hemorrhage dark blood or clots
  • T: Pale purple, possible purple or red spots
    Red purple if there is Heat
    Blue purple if there is Cold
  • C: White
  • P: Empty or Choppy or Wiry and choppy

TREATS

  • Liver cancer
