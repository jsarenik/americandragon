HERB FORMULAS

SHAO YAO GAN CAO FU ZI TANG - 芍药甘草附子汤 - PEONY, LICORICE AND ACONITE DECOCTION

English: Peony, Licorice and Aconite Decoction
Also Known As: Peony, Licorice and Aconite Combination

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Paeoniae Alba  Bai Shao 3-9g Nourishes the Blood, astringes Yin, adjusts the Ying and Wei, softens the Liver and relieves pain.
With Gan Cao, for abdominal pain due to disharmony between the Liver and Stomach and epigastric, hypochondriac or flank pain.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Preparata   Zhi Gan Cao 3-9g Tonifies the Spleen, augments Qi, moderates spasms, alleviates pain and moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs.
Rx. Aconiti Lateralis Preparata Zhi Fu Zi 0.5-3g Warms Ming Men Fire, assists Heart, Kidney and Spleen Yang, disperses Cold and Dampness, warms the channels and stops pain.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Softens the Liver
  • Warms Yang
  • Benefits Yin
  • Moderates painful spasms
  • Relieves pain
  • Harmonizes the Middle Jiao

SYNDROMES

  • Acute cramping due to Yang Deficiency
  • Shang Han Tai Yang which shows Yang Deficiency symptoms after diaphoresis

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Acute muscular cramping
  • Cold limbs
  • Fear of Cold
  • Chills
  • Convulsions
  • Anxiety
  • Back spasms
  • Knee spasms
  • Spasms around the waist
  • Spasms of the gastrocnemius
  • Sciatica
  • Low back pain
  • Stiff shoulders
  • Pain on urination
  • P: Deep and slow

TREATS

  • Acute muscular cramping
  • Sciatica
  • Muscular rheumatism
  • Intestinal hernia
  • Biliary colic
  • Renal colic
  • Hemorrhoidal colic
  • Low back pain
