HERB FORMULAS

BIE JIA JIAN WAN - 鳖甲煎丸 - TURTLE SHELL PILLS

English: Turtle Shell Pills
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Carapax Trionycis Bie Jia 90g Nourishes Yin, anchors Yang, invigorates the Blood, promotes menstruation, softens hardness and dissipates nodules.
With Chai Hu and Tu Bie Chong, for chronic malarial disorders with organomegaly and hypochondriac pain.
Rz. Belamcandae She Gan 22.5g Clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity, transforms Phlegm, clears the Lungs and eliminates wheezing.

With Da Huang, for constipation.

With Gan Jiang, warms the Spleen and Kidneys, benefits Yang, and Disperses Cold.

With Zhi Fu Zi, for overabundance of Yin fluids due to Deficiency Cold.

With Gan Jiang, for acute diarrhea from Cold attacking the Middle.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Removes Stasis
  • Circulates Qi
  • Resolves Phlegm
  • Eliminates accumulations
  • Softens and resolves hard masses

SYNDROMES

  • Qi, Blood and Phlegm Stagnation

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Immobile masses below the hypochondrium or in the abdomen
  • Abdominal pain
  • Emaciation
  • Poor appetite
  • Alternating chills and fever
  • Amenorrhea
  • Hard abdominal masses that produce pain
  • Aggravated by pressure
  • Hypochondriac distention
  • T: Purple with echymoses
  • P: Choppy

TREATS

  • Uterine myoma
  • Malaria with splenomegaly
  • Chronic hepatitis
  • Hepatomegaly
  • Jaundice
  • Abdominal masses
  • Amenorrhea
  • Cirrhosis of the Liver
  • Schistosomiasis
  • Splenomegaly
  • Liver cancer
  • Abdominal pain
  • Poor appetite

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Use caution for those with Zheng Qi Deficiency.
HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

NOTES

  • 1.5kg of plant ash is steeped in 5kg of millet wine and then the ash is filtered out to get the decoction, in which Bie Jia is decocted into a gum. All the other drugs are ground into fine powder. The gum and the powder are made with honey into pills. 3g of the pills are taken with warm boiled water each time, 3 times daily.

MODIFICATIONS

With general weakness and Deficiency: For decreased Food intake and indigestion:
+ Rx. Astragali Huang Qi + Fr. Crataegi Shan Zha
+ Rz. Atractylodis Macrocephalae Bai Zhu + Massa Medicata Fermentata Shen Qu
+ Rx. Rehmanniae Preparata Shu Di Huang + Endothelium Corneum Gigeriae Galli Ji Nei Jin
+ Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui
