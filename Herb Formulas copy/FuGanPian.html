HERB FORMULAS

FU GAN PIAN - 浮肝片 - TABLET FOR TREATING HEPATIC CARCINOMA

English: Tablet for Treating Hepatic Carcinoma
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Pseudostellariae Tai Zi Shen 30g Strengthens the Spleen, tonifies Qi, generates Body Fluids and treats chronic febrile disorders.
Hb. Dendrobii Shi Hu 15g Nourishes Yin, clears Heat, generates fluids, enriches Kidney Yin and reduces Heat from Deficiency.
Hb. Hedyotis Diffusae Bai Hua She She Cao 30g Clears Heat, strongly relieves Fire toxins, reduces abscesses, promotes urination and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Caulis Sargentodoxae Hong Teng 15g Clears Heat, resolves toxicity, reduces abscesses, stops pain, invigorates the Blood, disperses Stasis and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Fol. Cycadis Revolutae Feng Wei Jiao Ye 15g Regulates Qi, invigorates the Blood, breaks up Blood Stasis, eliminates toxins and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Fr. Akebiae Ba Yue Zha 15g Dredges the Liver, regulates Qi, controls pain, expels Phlegm, disperses clumps, promotes urination, invigorates the Blood, removes toxin and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Fr. Toosendan Chuan Lian Zi 15g Dredges Liver Qi, releases Stagnation, stops pain and conducts Heat out from below.
Sargassum Hai Zao 15g Reduces Phlegm, softens areas of hardness, promotes urination and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Paeoniae Rubra Chi Shao 12g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, relieves pain, clears Heat, cools the Blood, clears Liver Fire, reduces swelling from sores and abscesses and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Sophorae Flavescentis Ku Shen 9g Clears Heat, dries Dampness and protects the Liver.
Fr. Gardeniae Zhi Zi 15g Clears Heat, reduces Fire, drains Damp-Heat, cools the Blood, relieves toxicity, reduces swelling, invigorates the Blood and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Sparganii San Leng 10g Forcefully breaks up Blood Stasis, regulates Qi, alleviates pain, dissolves accumulations, regulates menstruation and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With E Zhu, for Qi and Blood Stagnation with clumping and accumulation with distending pain in the chest, epigastrium and abdomen, amenorrhea, dysmenorrhea, uterine myoma and ovarian cysts and hepatosplenomegaly with distention, pain and immobile, palpable epigastric, sub-costal and abdominal masses
Rz. Curcumae E Zhu 10g Invigorates Blood circulation, dispels Blood Stasis, regulates Qi, dissolves accumulations, alleviates pain and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Rhapontici Lou Lu 9g Clears Heat, resolves toxicity, drains abscesses and swellings, expels pus and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Sm. Coicis Yi Yi Ren 15g Strengthens the Spleen, resolves Dampness, relieves pain, clears Heat, expels pus, clears Damp-Heat and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Hb. Solani Lyrati Shu Yang Quan 30g Dispels Heat and Dampness, removes toxins, regulates Qi, invigorates the Blood, dispels Phlegm and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Concha Ostreae Mu Li 30g Calms the Liver, benefits Yin, anchors Floating Yang, softens hardness, dissipates nodules, alleviates pain and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Carapax Trionycis Bie Jia 15g Nourishes Yin, anchors Yang, invigorates the Blood, promotes menstruation, softens hardness, dissipates nodules and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Sm. Plantaginis Che Qian Zi 15g Promotes urination, clears Damp-Heat, clears Liver Heat and expels Phlegm.
Hb. Artemisiae Scopariae Yin Chen 30g Clears Damp-Heat from the Liver and Gallbladder, relieves jaundice and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Zhi Zi, strongly relieves Damp-Heat jaundice.
Rz. Paridis Chong Lou 20g Drains Heat, relieves Fire toxicity, reduces swelling and inflammation, stops pain and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Xia Ku Cao, for scrofula.
Rx. Platycodi Jie Geng 6g Opens the Lungs, spreads Lung Qi, expels Phlegm and expels pus.
Spica Prunellae Xia Ku Cao 15g Clears Liver Fire, clears Hot Phlegm, dissipates nodules and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Heat
  • Eliminates Toxins
  • Clears Damp-Heat
  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Breaks up Stasis
  • Resolves Phlegm
  • Dissipates nodules
  • Inhibits the growth of cancer cells

SYNDROMES

  • Toxic Heat with Blood Stagnation and Phlegm
