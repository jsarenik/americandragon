HERB FORMULAS

LUPUS #7

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Poria Fu Ling 12g Strengthens the Spleen and harmonizes the Middle Jiao.
With Dang Shen, Suan Zao Ren and Long Yan Rou, for insomnia due to Spleen and Heart Insufficiency.
With Suan Zao Ren, Wu Wei Zi and Yuan Zhi, for palpitations and insomnia.
Rx. Codonopsis Dang Shen 9g Tonifies the Middle Jiao, augments Qi and nourishes the Blood.
With Fu Ling, for Spleen and Stomach Deficiency with fatigue, a sensation of fullness in the abdomen, dizziness, lethargy, shortness of breath, anorexia and loose stools or diarrhea.
With Wu Wei Zi and Mai Men Dong, for injury to Qi and fluids with shortness of breath and thirst.
Rx. Astragali Huang Qi 9g Tonifies Blood and Spleen Qi.
With Dang Gui, generates and nourishes Blood.
With Long Yan Rou, Dang Gui and Suan Zao Ren, for insomnia and other signs of Qi and Blood Deficiency.
With Dang Shen, for Qi Deficiency with general debility, anorexia, fatigue, tired extremities and spontaneous sweating.
With Mai Men Dong and Wu Wei Zi, for night sweats.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 9g Nourishes and harmonizes the Blood.
With Huang Qi, for Qi and Blood Deficiency.
Rx. Scrophulariae Xuan Shen 9g Enriches Yin and clears Heat.
Rx. Salviae Miltiorrhizae Dan Shen 9g Invigorates, cools and nourishes the Blood and clears Heat.
With Dang Gui, nourishes Blood to nourish the Heart without causing Stasis.
With Suan Zao Ren and Bai Zi Ren, for Heart Blood Xu with palpitations and insomnia.
Rx. Ophiopogonis Mai Men Dong 9g Enriches the Yin and clears the Heart.
With Huang Qi, Dang Gui and Wu Wei Zi, for irritability due to severe loss of fluids due to sweating.
Fr. Schisandrae Wu Wei Zi 9g Tonifies Qi, tonifies Kidney Jing, nourishes the Heart, calms the Shen, generates Fluids and astringes excessive perspiration.
With Suan Zao Ren, astringes Heart Qi.
Sm. Platycladi Bai Zi Ren 9g Nourishes the Heart and Kidneys, calms the Shen and astringes Yin Xu perspiration.
With Suan Zao Ren, strongly quiets the Spirit, calms the emotions, enriches Yin and Blood, stops night sweats and treats palpitations, irritability and insomnia due to Yin and Blood Deficiency failing to nourish the Heart and Liver.
With Yuan Zhi and Suan Zao Ren, for insomnia, and palpitations due to Heart Blood Xu.
Sm. Zizyphi Spinosae Suan Zao Ren 9g Nourishes Heart Yin, augments Liver Blood, calms the Shen and astringes sweat.
With Wu Wei Zi, prevents leakage of Heart Qi.
With Yuan Zhi, balances the contracting, introverted and dispersing, extroverted manifestations of Spirit to ensure the proper balance of openness and sobriety.
With Wu Wei Zi, Huang Qi and Dang Shen, for spontaneous sweating.
Rx. Polygalae Yuan Zhi 9g Calms the Shen, sedates the Heart and opens the channel between the Heart and Kidneys.
With Bai Zi Ren, calms the Shen.
With Huang Qi, Dang Gui and Long Yan Rou, for forgetfulness, palpitations and insomnia.
Arillus Longan Long Yan Rou 9g Nourishes the Blood, calms the Shen, and tonifies and nourishes the Heart and Spleen.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Reinforces and Benefits the Heart and Spleen
  • Tonifies Qi
  • Nourishes the Blood

SYNDROMES

  • Heart Blood and Spleen Qi Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Palpitations
  • Insomnia
  • Much dreaming
  • Easily startled
  • Poor memory
  • Shortness of breath
  • Chest oppression
  • Profuse sweating
  • Four limbs tired and lethargic
  • Cold extremities
  • Weakness
  • Emaciation
  • Weak voice
  • Disinclination to speak
  • Low grade fever
  • Loss of appetite
  • Distention and fullness of the epigastrium and abdomen
  • Loose stools
  • Sallow complexion
  • Copious, pale menses
  • Uterine bleeding or
  • Light menstrual flow or
  • Amenorrhea
  • T: Pale or Pale and swollen
  • C: White
  • P: Thready and weak, maybe rapid or Possibly Tight and choppy

TREATS

  • Lupus erythematosis
