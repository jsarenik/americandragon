HERB FORMULAS

BAI XIAN PI YIN - 白鲜皮饮 - DICTAMNUS DRINK

English: Dictamnus Drink
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Cx. Dictamni Bai Xian Pi 15g Clears Heat, resolves Fire-toxicity, expels Wind, dries Dampness, clears Damp-Heat and stops itching.
With Sheng Di Huang, Dang Gui and Chan Tui, for rashes due to Blood Deficiency.
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 15g Clears Heat, cools the Blood and nourishes Yin.
Rx. Paeoniae Rubra Chi Shao 15g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, relieves pain, clears Heat and cools the Blood.
Rx. Salviae Miltiorrhizae Dan Shen 15g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, clears Heat, soothes irritability and cools and nourishes the Blood.
Rx. Scutellariae Huang Qin 15g Clears Heat, dries Dampness, drains Fire and detoxifies.
Periostracum Cicadae Chan Tui 9g Disperses Wind, clears Heat, vents rashes and relieves itching.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 12g Tonifies, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood, reduces swelling, expels pus, generates flesh and alleviates pain.
With Jin Yin Hua, reduces obstruction and toxicity for swollen, painful sores and necrosis of surrounding tissue.
Rz. Atractylodis Cang Zhu 9g Strongly dries Dampness, tonifies the Spleen, induces sweating and expels Wind-Dampness.
Hb. Schizonepetae Jing Jie 9g Releases the Exterior, expels Wind, vents rashes and relieves itching.
With Dang Gui, for itching and tremors of the hands and feet from Wind due to Blood Deficiency.
Rx. Saposhnikoviae Fang Feng 9g Releases the Exterior and expels External Wind.
With Jing Jie, expels Wind and stops itching for pruritic rashes.
With Jing Jie and Chan Tui, for dermatological disorders with rash, irritability and itching.
Flos Lonicerae Jin Yin Hua 15g Clears Heat, resolves Fire toxicity and vents and disperses External Wind-Heat.
Rx. Arnebiae seu Lithospermi Zi Cao 9g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, invigorates Blood circulation, relieves Fire toxins and encourages rashes.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Nourishes the Blood
  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Dispels Wind
  • Stops itching

SYNDROMES

  • Yin and Blood Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Clusters of red, flat pimples
  • Severe itching
  • T: Pale
  • C: Yellowish or Thin and white
  • P: Floating and rapid

TREATS

  • Neurodermatitis
  • Pityriasis rosae
  • Urticaria
  • Seborrheic dermatitis
