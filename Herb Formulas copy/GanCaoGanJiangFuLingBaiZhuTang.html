HERB FORMULAS

GAN CAO GAN JIANG FU LING BAI ZHU TANG - 甘草干姜茯苓白术汤 - LICORICE, GINGER, PORIA AND WHITE ATRACTYLODES DECOCTION

English: Licorice, Ginger, Poria, and White Atractylodes Decoction
Also Known As: Gan Jiang Ling Zhu Tang

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rz. Zingiberis Gan Jiang 9-12g Warms the Middle, expels Cold, rescues Devastated Yang and expels Interior Cold.
With Bai Zhu, for diarrhea due to Spleen Deficiency.
With Gan Cao, for epigastric pain and vomiting due to Spleen and Stomach Deficiency Cold.
Wit Fu Ling, Gan Cao and Bai Zhu, for Cold-Dampness in the Lower Jiao leading to a sense of heaviness and Cold in the lower back.
Rz. Atractylodis Macrocephalae Bai Zhu 6-9g Tonifies the Spleen, augments Qi, dries Dampness and promotes water metabolism.
With Gan Jiang, eliminates Cold Dampness in the Spleen with loss of taste, a sticky sensation in the mouth, nausea, vomiting, diarrhea and a thick, white tongue coat.
With Fu Ling and Gan Cao, for Spleen and Stomach Qi Deficiency.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 6-9g Tonifies the Spleen, augments Qi, moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs and guides the herbs to all twelve channels.
Poria Fu Ling 12-15g Promotes urination, leaches out Dampness, strengthens the Spleen and harmonizes the Middle Jiao.
With Bai Zhu, strengthens the Spleen and resolves Dampness.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Warms Yang
  • Strengthens the Spleen
  • Dispels Cold
  • Eliminates Dampness

SYNDROMES

  • Lower Jiao Cold-Damp

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Cold pain in the lower back and body
  • Sciatica
  • Copious urination
  • Extreme heavy sensation in the lower back
  • Absence of thirst
  • T: Pale
  • C: White
  • P: Deep, thready and moderate

TREATS

  • Low back pain
  • Sciatica
  • Lumbago
  • Enuresis
  • Gastritis
  • Diarrhea
  • Lumbar disc disease
  • Chronic pelvic inflammatory disease
  • Acute gastroenteritis
  • Allergic rhinitis
  • Superficial gastritis
  • Atrophic gastritis
  • Duodenal ulcer
  • Arthritis
  • Impotence
  • Acute lumbar strain
  • Renal calculi
  • Benign prostatic hypertrophy
  • Eczema
  • Chronic bronchitis

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with heaviness of the body and backache due to Damp-Heat.
