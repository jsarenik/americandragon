STOMACH CANCER #35

Also Known As:

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Tonifies Qi
  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Circulates Qi
  • Eliminates Phlegm
  • Nourishes Yin
  • Nourishes the Blood

SYNDROMES

  • Stomach cancer with low-grade metastasis of the lymph nodes

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Early stage:
    Often no symptoms
    Upper abdominal pain
    Discomfort in the upper abdomen
  • Middle stage:
    Anorexia
    Progressive dysphagia
    Nausea
    Weight loss
    Melaena
  • Late stage:
    Vomiting after eating
    Edema
    Ascites
    Anemia
    Hepatomegaly
    Abdominal tumor
    The tumor is firm and irregular

TREATS

  • Stomach cancer with low-grade metastasis of the lymph nodes

NOTES

  • Permeate the ingredients in a decoction of the last four ingredients, grind into a fine powder and make into a bolus with honey. Take 15g three times dally. Increase the dosage until the swelling and pain is eliminated (should take about one week).
