HERB FORMULAS

HEMATITE AND ASTER COMBINATION

English: Hematite and Aster Combination
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Haematitum Dai Zhe Shi 15g Calms the Liver, anchors Floating Yang and, clears Liver Fire, strongly descends rebellious Qi, cools the Blood and stops bleeding.
With Sheng Di Huang and Bai Shao, for hematemesis and epistaxis.
Poria Fu Ling 12g Promotes urination, leaches out Dampness, strengthens the Spleen, harmonizes the Middle Jiao and transforms Phlegm.
With Ze Xie, facilitates the removal of water and leaches out Dampness to treat stagnant water and Dampness leading to edema, jaundice, diarrhea, Milky Lin, and scanty, difficult urination with abdominal distention.
With Gan Cao, for Spleen and Heart Deficiencies causing facial edema, palpitations, and shortness of breath.
Rx. Oryzae Glutinosae Nuo Dao Gen 12g Stops sweat due to Deficiency and reduces low-grade fevers.
With Sheng Di Huang, for Yin Deficiency Heat.
Rx. Paeoniae Alba Bai Shao 9g Nourishes Blood, astringes Yin, adjusts the Ying and Wei, calms Liver Yang and Liver Wind, softens the Liver and alleviates pain.
With Gan Cao, regulates the relationship between the Liver and Spleen, nourishes the sinews and treats abdominal pain due to disharmony between the Liver and Stomach.
Rz. Alismatis Ze Xie 9g Promotes urination, leaches out Dampness and drains Kidney Fire.
Rx. Asteris Zi Wan 6g Relieves coughs and expels Phlegm.
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 4.5g Clears Heat,cools the Blood, nourishes Yin and generates fluids.
With Bai Shao, for Blood Deficiency Heat.
Periostracum Cicadae Chan Tui 3g Disperses Wind, clears Heat, stops spasms and extinguishes Liver Wind.
Endothelium Corneum Gigeriae Galli Ji Nei Jin 3g Strongly reduces Food Stagnation, improves the Spleen’s transportive function, astringes Jing and urine and stops enuresis.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 3g Tonifies the Spleen, augments Qi, moistens the Lungs, resolves Phlegm, stops cough, clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity and moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs.
Rz. Coptidis Huang Lian 3g Clears Heat, drains Dampness, drains Fire, resolves Fire toxicity and stops bleeding.
With Sheng Di Huang, for insomnia and delirium due to unremitting Heat injuring the Ying level.
Fr. Jujube Da Zao 3g Tonifies the Spleen and Stomach, augments Qi, nourishes the Blood, calms the Spirit and moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Calms the Liver
  • Cools the Blood
  • Stops Bleeding
  • Promotes urination
  • Eliminates Dampness
  • Tonifies the Spleen
  • Clears Heat
  • Relieves toxicity
  • Nourishes Yin

SYNDROMES

  • Qi, Yin and Blood Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Impaired vision
  • Dry eyes
  • Motor impairment of joints
  • Numbness of limbs
  • Dryness and itchiness of skin
  • Mental disorders
  • Palpitations
  • Insomnia
  • Dream disturbed sleep
  • Low back pain
  • Tidal fever
  • Night sweats
  • Tinnitus or deafness
  • Poor memory
  • Spermatorrhea or premature ejaculation
  • Aching in bones
  • Aching in leg and heel
  • Dry mouth
  • Thirst
  • Five sole heat
  • Malar flush
  • Low grade fever in the afternoon
  • Dry throat, maybe recurrent sore throat
  • Dry stools and/or constipation
  • Urine short and red
  • Heightened libido
  • Dizziness
  • Blurred vision
  • Dislike of speaking
  • Lassitude
  • Spontaneous sweating
  • All worse on exertion.
  • T: Pale or Red or Pale pink
  • C: Thin and white or None
  • P: Deficient or Thready and rapid

TREATS

  • Post radiotherapy
