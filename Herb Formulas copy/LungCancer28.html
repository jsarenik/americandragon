HERB FORMULAS

LUNG CANCER #28

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Fr. Luffae Si Gua Luo 150g Resolves toxicity, reduces swelling, unblocks the channels and collaterals, dispels Wind and expels Phlegm.
Bul. Fritillariae Cirrhosae Chuan Bei Mu 45g Clears Heat, transforms Phlegm, stops coughing, nourishes and moistens the Lungs, clears Heat, dissipates nodules and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Xing Ren, for a non-productive cough from Lung Deficiency disorders.
With Zhe Bei Mu, for scrofula and abscesses.
Bul. Fritillariae Thunbergii Zhe Bei Mu 45g Clears Heat, transforms Phlegm, stops coughing, dissipates nodules and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rz. Curcumae Yu Jin 60g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, regulates Qi flow, speeds healing of chronic sores, clears Heat, cools the Blood and alleviates pain.
Sm. Armeniacae Xing Ren 90g Stops coughing, calms wheezing, moistens the Intestines, unblocks the bowels and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Sargassum Hai Zao 90g Reduces Phlegm, softens areas of hardness and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Sm. Lepidii seu Descuraniae Ting Li Zi 90g Drains the Lungs, reduces Phlegm and calms wheezing.
Fr. Jujube Da Zao 60g Nourishes the Blood, calms the Spirit and moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs.
With Ting Li Zi, for coughing, wheezing and chest fullness due to Phlegm accumulation in the chest.
Flos Lonicerae Jin Yin Hua 240g Clears Heat, resolves Fire toxicity and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Gan Cao, for toxic swellings and sores.
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 90g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, nourishes Yin and generates fluids.
With Mai Men Dong and Nan Sha Shen, for a dry mouth, thirst, dry lips and a red tongue due to Stomach Yin Deficiency.
Rx. Adenophorae Nan Sha Shen 90g Nourishes Yin, moistens the Lungs, dispels Phlegm, stops coughing, nourishes the Stomach, generates fluids and clears Heat.
Per. Citri Reticulatae Chen Pi 90g Regulates Qi, adjusts the Middle, relieves the diaphragm, transforms Phlegm and descends Qi.
Calculus Bovis Niu Huang 4.5g Drains Heat and resolves Fire toxicity and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Jin Yin Hua and Gan Cao, for sores, carbuncles and boils.
Gypsum Fibrosum Shi Gao 60g Clears Heat in the Qi Stage, drains Fire, relieves irritability, quenches thirst and clears Excess Heat from the Lungs.
With Sheng Di Huang, for excessive Fire with injured fluids.
Cx. Mori Sang Bai Pi 120g Drains Heat from the Lungs, stops coughing, calms wheezing and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Fol. Eriobotryae Pi Pa Ye 90g Transforms Phlegm, clears Lung Heat, descends Lung Qi, harmonizes the Stomach, clears Stomach Heat and descends Stomach Qi.
With Xing Ren, for Lung Heat induced non-productive cough, or cough with viscous sputum that is difficult to expectorate, with chest pain and a dry throat.
Exocarpium Citri Reticulatae Ju Hong 60g Regulates Qi, adjusts the Middle, relieves the diaphragm, transforms Phlegm and descends Qi.
Rx. Ophiopogonis Mai Men Dong 90g Moistens the Lungs, nourishes Yin, stops coughing, nourishes Stomach Yin and generates Fluids.
With Nan Sha Shen, for a dry cough with little Phlegm and a parched throat due to Lung Dryness or Heat injuring the Lung Yin.
With Sheng Di Huang, for hematemesis or epistaxis due to Yin Deficiency with Excess Heat.
Fr. Perillae Zi Su Zi 90g Stops coughing, calms wheezing, descends Qi, dissolves Phlegm, moistens the Intestines and unblocks the stool.
With Ting Li Zi, for accumulation of Phlegm and thin mucus leading to Lung Qi Stagnation and blockage with coughing and wheezing which is worse when lying down, edema of the tissues of the face and head, stifling fullness and discomfort in the chest and reduction in the flow of urine.
With Xing Ren, for coughing and wheezing with constipation.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 30g Moistens the Lungs, resolves Phlegm, stops coughing, clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity, inhibits the growth of cancer cells, moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs and guides the herbs to all twelve channels.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Cools the Blood
  • Clears Heat
  • Relieves toxicity
  • Moistens the Lungs
  • Regulates Qi
  • Inhibits the growth of cancer cells

SYNDROMES

  • Heat and Dryness of the Lungs

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Dry cough with little or no sputum
  • Sputum is difficult to expectorate
  • Asthma
  • Shortness of breath
  • Thick yellow Phlegm
  • High fever
  • No chills
  • Maybe hemoptysis
  • Cough may cause chest pain
  • Dry or sore throat
  • Hoarse voice
  • Dry lips and mouth
  • Thirst
  • Dryness in nose
  • Epistaxis
  • Aversion to Heat
  • Cough
  • Headache
  • Anhidrosis
  • Dry skin and hair
  • Constipation
  • Restlessness
  • Dark yellow urine
  • Dyspnea
  • Chest pain
  • T: Red
  • C: Thick and yellow, maybe dry and sticky or Thin, yellow and dry
  • P:  Rapid, maybe slippery, maybe forceful

TREATS

  • Lung cancer
