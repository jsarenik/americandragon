HERB FORMULAS

MALIGNANT LYMPHOMA #4

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Arctii Niu Bang Gen 15g Disperses Wind-Heat, benefits the throat, relieves toxicity and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Trichosanthis Tian Hua Fen 15g Drains Heat, generates Fluids, clears and relieves toxicity, expels pus, reduces swelling and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Bupleuri Chai Hu 9g Resolves Shao Yang disorders, reduces fever, harmonizes the Exterior and Interior, spreads Liver Qi, relieves Stagnation, disperses Wind-Heat and resolves Phlegm and congestion.
Bul. Bolbostemma Paniculatum Tu Bei Mu 12g Clears Heat, removes toxins, disperses Stagnation, eliminates pathogenic External Phlegm, reduces sores and swellings and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Sophorae Tonkinensis Shan Dou Gen 30g Clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity, disperses swellings and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Ban Lan Gen, for severe pain and swelling of the throat, teeth, and gums.
Rz. Smilacis Glabrae Tu Fu Ling 30g Relieves toxicity, eliminates Dampness and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Nidus Vespae Lu Feng Fang 30g Relieves toxicity, expels Wind, alleviates pain and treats cancerous tumors.
Rx. Isatidis Ban Lan Gen 30g Drains Heat, relieves Fire toxicity, cools the Blood and benefits the throat.
With Niu Bang Zi, Lian Qiao and Xuan Shen, for febrile disorders.
Rx. Scrophulariae Xuan Shen 30g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, nourishes Yin, softens hardness, dissipates nodules, drains Fire and relieves toxicity.
Hb. Bidentis Xiang Feng Cao 30g Reduces inflammation, clears Heat, removes toxicity, promotes urination, clears Heat and Dampness from the Intestines, invigorates the Blood, dissipates Blood Stasis, reduces swelling, drains pus, generates flesh and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Hb. Euphorbiae Humifusae Di Jin Cao 30g Invigorates and cools the Blood, circulates Qi, clears Heat and expels toxins
Fr. Forsythiae Lian Qiao 30g Clears Heat (especially in the Upper Jiao), relieves toxicity, reduces abscesses, dissipates clumps and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Ban Lan Gen, for any externally contracted Heat disorder.
With Ban Lan Gen and Niu Bang Gen, for tonsillitis.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Disperses Wind-Heat
  • Benefits the throat

  • Relieves toxicity
  • Invigorates the Blood
  • I inhibits the growth of cancer cells
