HERB FORMULAS

LING JIAO GOU TENG TANG - 羚角芶藤湯 - ANTELOPE HORN AND UNCARIA DECOCTION

English: Antelope Horn and Uncaria Decoction
Also Known As: Ling Yang Gou Teng Tang

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Cornu Saigae Tataricae Ling Yang Jiao 1-4.5g Pacifies the Liver, extinguishes Wind, clears Heat and toxins, anchors Yang, and stops spasms and convulsions.
With Gou Teng, Sheng Di Huang and Ju Hua, for convulsions and opisthotonis associated with Liver Wind generated by Warm Febrile Disease.
With Gou Teng, Sheng Di Huang, Ju Hua and Bai Shao, for dizziness and vertigo.
(Cornu Naemorhedis) (Shan Yang Jiao) (10-45g) (Pacifies the Liver, and treats Liver Fire or Liver Yang Rising. It may substitute for Ling Yang Jiao.)
(Concha Margaritaferae) (Zhen Zhu Mu) (10-45g) (Calms the Liver, anchors the Yang and drains Liver Fire. It may substitute for Ling Yang Jiao.)
Ram. Uncariae Gou Teng 9-20g Balances the liver and Pericardium, calms Wind and extinguishes Fire.
Fol. Mori Sang Ye 6-9g Clears the Liver and eyes.
 With Ju Hua, clears Heat and dispels Wind from the Lungs and Liver and strengthens the formula’s Wind-extinguishing functions.
With Ju Hua, Ling Yang Jiao and Gou Teng, for cramps and spasms due to Liver Wind.
Flos Chrysanthemi Ju Hua 9-15g Calms the Liver and extinguishes Wind.
With Gou Teng, Sheng Di Huang and Bai Shao, for headaches due to Liver Wind with Yin Deficiency Fire.
Rx. Paeoniae Alba Bai Shao 6-12g Calms Liver Yang and astringes Yin.
With Sheng Di Huang, nourishes Yin and generates Fluids to soften the Liver and relax sinews.
With Gou Teng and Ju Hua, for headache and dizziness due to Liver Yang Rising.
With Gan Cao, for abdominal pain due to disharmony between the Liver and Stomach and epigastric, hypochondriac or flank pain.
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 9-20g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, nourishes the Yin and Blood, cools Heart Fire and increases Fluids.
With Bai Shao, for Blood Deficiency Heat.
Bul. Fritillariae Cirrhosae Chuan Bei Mu 9-12g Clears Heat and transforms Phlegm.
With Zhu Ru, clears Heat and transforms Phlegm.
Scl. Poria Pararadicis Fu Shen 9-12g Transforms Phlegm, pacifies the Heart, calms the Shen and treats irritability and restlessness caused by Heat attacking the Shen.
Caul. Bambusae In Taeniam Zhu Ru 6-15g Clears Heat and transforms Hot Phlegm.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 1-3g Resolves Phlegm, clears Heat, detoxifies and harmonizes.
With Bai Shao, strengthens Yin, relaxes sinews and moderates painful spasms.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Cools the Liver
  • Extinguishes Wind
  • Increases Fluids
  • Relaxes the sinews
  • Stops convulsions

SYNDROMES

  • Phlegm Obstructs the Heart Orifice-Tense Type (Yang Obstruction)
  • Liver Wind Stirring Internally: Extreme Heat Generates Wind
  • Wen Bing Xue Level Heat (Xue Fen) Generating Wind
  • Phlegm-Heat

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Persistent high fever
  • Irritability
  • Restlessness
  • Dizziness
  • Vertigo
  • Twitching
  • Spasms of the extremities
  • Insomnia
  • Tinnitus
  • Bitter taste in the mouth
  • Deafness
  • Dry throat
  • Stiff neck with convulsions
  • Severe headache
  • Loss of consciousness
  • Maybe opisthotonos
  • Maybe expectoration of thick, yellow sputum
  • T: Deep red, dry or burnt with prickles
  • C: Yellow and dry
  • P: Wiry and rapid or Wiry and smooth

TREATS:

  • Essential hypertension
  • Hypertensive encephalopathy
  • Cerebrovascular accident
  • Encephalitis B
  • Meningitis
  • Pneumonia
  • Stroke
  • Aphasia
  • Glaucoma
  • Catarrhal otitis media
  • Headache
  • Dizziness
  • Kidney failure
  • Fever
  • Tinnitus
  • Bad breath
  • Facial paralysis
  • Post-concussion syndrome
  • Lupus erythematosis
  • Eclampsia
  • Convulsions
  • Mania with convulsions
  • Chromaffinoma
  • Dysentery
  • Shingles
  • Post-stroke sequelae
  • Meniere’s disease
  • Insomnia
  • Vertigo
  • Ebola
  • Constipation
  • Deafness
  • Epistaxis
  • Hemiplegia
  • Liver cancer
  • Seizures and epilepsy during pregnancy
  • Stiff neck
