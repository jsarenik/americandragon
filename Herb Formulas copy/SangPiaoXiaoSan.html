HERB FORMULAS

SANG PIAO XIAO SAN - 桑螵蛸散 - MANTIS-EGG CASE POWDER

English: Mantis-Egg Case Powder
Also Known As: Mantis Formula

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Ootheca Mantidis Sang Piao Xiao 9-30g Tonifies the Kidneys, assists Yang, retains Jing and astringes urine.
With Long Gu, Gui Ban, Yuan Zhi and Fu Shen, for spermatorrhea and nocturnal emissions due to Heart and Kidney Deficiency.
Fossilia Ossis Mastodi Long Gu 15-30g Settles anxiety, calms the Spirit and prevents leakage of fluids.
With Sang Piao Xiao, powerfully astringes and tonifies for enuresis, urinary frequency, premature ejaculation, nocturnal emissions and vaginal discharge due to unstable Kidney Yang.
With Sang Piao Xiao and Gui Ban, for urinary frequency and incontinence due to Heart and Kidney Deficiency.
With Shi Chang Pu and Yuan Zhi, for a restless Shen.
Vinegar-fried Plastrum Testudinis Cu Chao Gui Ban 15-30g Nourishes Yin and anchors Yang with an enhanced ability to subdue Yang. Benefits the Kidneys, nourishes the Blood, enriches Yin, stabilizes the Chong and Ren channels and tonifies the Heart.
Rx. Ginseng Ren Shen 9-30g Powerfully tonifies Yuan Qi, tonifies Heart Qi, calms the Spirit and treats impotence.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 9-30g Tonifies, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood, disperses Cold and stops pain due to Blood Stasis.
Rx. Polygalae Yuan Zhi 6-30g Calms the Spirit, sedates the Heart and clears the channel joining the Heart and Kidney.
Rz. Acori Tatarinowii Shi Chang Pu 6-30g Opens the Orifices, vaporizes Phlegm, removes filth and calms the Spirit.
With Ren Shen, Fu Shen and Yuan Zhi, for forgetfulness and dulled senses.
Poria Pararadicis Fu Shen 12-30g Quiets the Heart, calms the Spirit and soothes the nerves.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Regulates and tonifies the Heart and Kidneys
  • Stabilizes Jing
  • Stops seminal emissions

SYNDROMES

  • Heart and Kidney Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Frequent urination
  • Cloudy urine that resembles rice-water
  • Seminal emissions
  • Enuresis
  • Disorientation
  • Constipation
  • Forgetfulness
  • Fatigue
  • Anorexia
  • T: Pale or Light pink
  • C: Thin and white
  • P: Thin, slow and frail or Deep and weak

TREATS

  • Chronic nephritis
  • Cystitis
  • Frequent urination
  • Enuresis
  • Seminal emissions
  • Neurasthenia
  • Diabetes
  • Tubular acidosis
  • Prostatic hypertrophy
  • Hypothyroidism
  • Chyluria
  • Chronic nephritis
  • Sarcoidosis
  • Constipation
  • Pediatric enuresis
  • Neurosis
  • Uterine prolapse
  • Autonomic dystonia
  • Forgetfulness
  • Hyperparathyroidism
  • Nephrotic syndrome
  • Diabetes
  • Urinary incontinence
  • Fatigue
  • Amyloidosis
  • Chronic glomerulonephritis

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with Damp-Heat in the Lower Jiao.
  • Contraindicated for those with Fire in the Lower Jiao.
