HERB FORMULAS

YIN HUA HU ZHANG TANG JIA JIAN - 银花胡杖汤加減 - MODIFIED LONICERA AND POLYGONI CUSPIDATI DECOCTION

English: Modified Lonicera and Polygoni Cuspidati Decoction
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Flos Lonicerae Jin Yin Hua 15g Clears Heat, resolves Fire toxicity, vents and disperses External Wind-Heat and cools the Blood.
With Dang Gui, reduces obstruction and toxicity for swollen, painful sores and necrosis of surrounding tissue.
Rz. Polygoni Cuspidati Hu Zhang 15g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, unblocks the channels, stops pain, clears Heat, resolves Dampness, discharges toxins and disperses swelling.
Rx. Salviae Miltiorrhizae Dan Shen 15g Invigorates, cools and nourishes the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, clears Heat and soothes irritability.
With Mu Dan Pi and Sheng Di Huang, for Ying Stage Heat.
Caulis Spatholobi Ji Xue Tang 15g Invigorates and tonifies the Blood.
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 15g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, nourishes Yin and generates fluids.
Extremitas Radicis Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui Wei 12g Invigorates and harmonizes the Blood, disperses Cold, reduces swelling, expels pus, generates flesh and stops pain due to Blood Stasis.
Rx. Paeoniae Rubra Chi Shao 12g Clears Heat, cools and Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, reduces swelling from sores and abscesses and relieves pain.
With Mu Dan Pi and Sheng Di Huang, for Heat entering the Xue Stage causing maculae or hemorrhage.
Flos Sophorae Immaturus Huai Hua Mi 12g Cools the Blood.
Fol. Isatidis Da Qing Ye 12g Clears Heat, resolves Fire toxicity, cools the Blood and dissipates maculae.
With Jin Yin Hua, for toxic Heat sores, and erysipelas.
Cx. Moutan Mu Dan Pi 10g Clears Heat,cools and harmonizes the Blood, clears Deficiency Fire, invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, drains pus and reduces swelling.
With Chi Shao, cools the Blood and disperses Stasis, for Ying Stage Heat with skin rashes.
With Sheng Di Huang, for Ying and Xue Stage Heat.
Rx. Arnebiae/Lithospermi Zi Cao 10g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, invigorates Blood circulation, relieves Fire toxins, encourages rashes clears Damp-Heat from the skin and aids healing of tissues.
Rz. Menispermi Bei Dou Gen 10g Clears Heat, resolves toxicity, resolves Dampness, reduces swelling, dispels Wind and stops pain.
Rx. Adenophora Nan Sha Shen 10g Nourishes Yin, moistens the Lungs, nourishes the Stomach, generates fluids and clears Heat.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Cools the Blood
  • Relieves toxicity
  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Reduces erythema

SYNDROMES

  • Blood Heat

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • This pattern occurs in the early stages of an initial attack or subsequent episode of guttate or plaque psoriasis.
  • Small coin sized red maculae located on the scalp, body, extremities, and other areas that increase rapidly
  • The lesions may manifest as bright red or dark red papules, maculopapules or macular patched of varying sizes scattered throughout the body or distributed mostly on the trunk and limbs
  • Sometimes lesions start on the scalp before spreading gradually to other parts of the body
  • New lesions appear continuously,
  • The area involved is large
  • There is a covered silvery-white scale
  • The scales are heaped up
  • The scales are easily shed when scratched
  • The scales elicit the characteristic Auspitz’s sign on removal of the underlying moist layer.
  • Usually affects people under age 40
  • Itching may be intense
  • Worse during the summer
  • Aversion to Heat
  • Irritability
  • Restlessness
  • Thirst
  • Dry throat
  • Dry tongue
  • Bitter taste in the mouth
  • Dry stools
  • Constipation
  • Yellow, scanty urine
  • T: Red or Dark red
  • C: Thin yellow or white or Yellow and grimy
  • P: Slippery or Taut and rapid or Thin and rapid or Wiry and rapid, or Slippery and rapid.
  • With Dryness:
    Lesions are on the elbows, knees or lumbar area
    They are reddish at the beginning
    White covered lesions become fissures
    Pain
    T: Red
    C: None or Thin yellow
    P: Thready and rapid

TREATS

  • Psoriasis
