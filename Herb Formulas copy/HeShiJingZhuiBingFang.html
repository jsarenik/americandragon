HERB FORMULAS

HE SHI JING ZHUI BING FANG - 佫师颈椎病方 - MASTER HE'S CERVICAL VERTEBRA DISEASE FORMULA

English: Master He's Cervical Vertebra Disease Formula
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Rehmanniae Preparata Shu Di Huang 15g Nourishes the Blood and nourishes Liver and Kidney Yin.
Pollen Typhae Pu Huang 15g Activates the Blood and dispels Blood Stasis.
Rx. Drynariae Gu Sui Bu 15g Tonifies Kidney Yang, strengthens the sinews and bones and activates the Blood.
Caulis Spatholobi Ji Xue Teng 15g Activates and tonifies the Blood, activates the channels and relaxes the sinews.
Hb. Pyrolae Lu Xian Cao 12g Dispels Wind-Dampness, strengthens the sinews and bones, augments the Kidneys and activates Blood circulation.
Rx. Salviae Miltiorrhizae Dan Shen 9g Activates the Blood and dispels Blood Stasis.
Ram. Mori Sang Zhi 9g Dispels Wind-Dampness, unblocks the channels and collaterals, benefits the joints and stops spasms.
Fr. Hordei Germinatus Mai Ya 9g Reduces Food Stagnation, improves digestion, strengthens the Stomach and facilitates the smooth flow of Liver Qi.
Hb. Cistanches Rou Cong Rong 9g Tonifies the Kidneys and strengthens the Yang.
Extremitas Radicis Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui Wei 9g Activates and harmonizes the Blood and alleviates pain.
Scolopendra Wu Gong 6g Extinguishes Wind, stops spasms and convulsions, unblocks the collaterals and stops pain.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Nourishes Yin
  • Tonifies Yang
  • Activates the Blood
  • Dispels Wind-Dampness
  • Unblocks the channels and collaterals
  • Stops pain

SYNDROMES

  • Yin and Yang Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Head, neck, shoulder and upper back aching and pain
  • Maybe distending pain in the head
  • Numbness and lack of strength in the four limbs
  • Low back and knee soreness and weakness
  • Dizziness
  • Blurred vision
  • Tidal redness
  • Night sweats
  • A dry mouth and throat
  • Cold feet
  • Decreased libido
  • Loose stools
  • T: Red or Pale pink
  • C: Little or none or white
  • P: Thready and rapid or Deep and slow

TREATS

  • Cervical spondylosis
