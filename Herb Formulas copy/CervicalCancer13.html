HERB FORMULAS

CERVICAL CANCER #13

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Margarita* Zhen Zhu 1.5g Promotes healing and generates flesh.
Cinnabaris* Zhu Sha 30g Clears Heat, relieves toxicity and prevents putrefaction.
Gold Foil Jin Bo 15pcs Clears Heat, removes toxins and transforms pus.
Talcum Hua Shi 80g Promotes urination, drains Heat from the Urinary Bladder, expels Damp-Heat and stops bleeding due to Heat.
Rz. Coptidis Huang Lian 4g Clears Heat, drains Dampness, drains Fire, resolves Fire toxicity, stops bleeding and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Calculus Bovis Niu Huang 4g Drains Heat, resolves Fire toxicity and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Zhen Zhu, for sores due to Heat toxin.
Rx. Notoginseng San Qi 30g Stops bleeding, transforms Blood Stasis, invigorates the Blood, reduces swelling and alleviates pain.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears heat
  • Relieves toxicity
  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Stops bleeding
  • Alleviates pain
  • Inhibits the growth of cancer cells

SYNDROMES

  • Toxic Heat with Blood Stagnation

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Pain
  • Bleeding
  • Tumor develops quickly
  • Long-standing illness
  • Redness
  • Ulceration
  • Discharge of yellow or bloody, foul-smelling fluid
  • Fatigue
  • Fever
  • Anxiety
  • Dry mouth
  • Thirst
  • Dark urine
  • Bitter taste
  • Red eyes
  • Insomnia
  • Pain that is fixed, stabbing and /or severe
  • Pain is worse with pressure
  • Dark complexion
  • Fixed, palpable masses
  • Bleeding with clots
  • Purple lips
  • Purple nails
  • Dry skin
  • Blue, dark or purplish discoloration of skin in small or large areas,
  • Varicose and spider veins
  • Cold hands and feet
  • Tendency to hemorrhage dark blood or clots
  • T: Red or Purple, possible purple or red spots
    Red purple if there is Heat
    Blue purple if there is Cold
  • C: White or Yellow and greasy or Thick, sticky, dry and yellow
  • P: Wiry or Rolling and rapid or Wiry, overflowing and rapid or Choppy or Wiry and choppy

TREATS

  • Cervical cancer marked by pain and bleeding

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Zhu Sha is toxic use extreme caution internally.
HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

NOTES

  • * Cook Zhu Zhu together with been curd for 24 hours before decocting.
