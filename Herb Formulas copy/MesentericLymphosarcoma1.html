HERB FORMULAS

MESENTERIC LYMPHOSARCOMA

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Astragali Huang Qi 30g Tonifies Qi and Blood, promotes the discharge of pus, generates flesh, expels toxins, relieves numbness and pain and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 9g Tonifies, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood, reduces swelling, expels pus, generates flesh and alleviates pain.
With Huang Qi, for Qi and Blood Deficiency.
With Tao Ren and Hong Hua, for amenorrhea, pain, swelling and other symptoms associated with Blood Stasis.
Rx. Adenophorae Nan Sha Shen 9g Nourishes Yin, moistens the Lungs, dispels Phlegm, stops coughing, nourishes the Stomach, generates fluids and clears Heat.
Rx. Salviae Miltiorrhizae Dan Shen 9g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, cools the Blood, reduces abscesses and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Spica Prunellae Xia Ku Cao 15g Clears Hot Phlegm, dissipates nodules and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Chai Hu, for neck nodules due to Liver Qi Stagnation.
Sargassum Hai Zao 15g Reduces Phlegm, softens areas of hardness and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Thallus Eckloniae Kun Bu 15g Resolves Phlegm, softens Phlegm nodules, promotes urination, clears Heat, reduces swelling and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Hai Zao, for goiter and scrofula.
Sm. Sinapis Bai Jie Zi 9g Warms the Lungs, regulates Qi, expels Phlegm, promotes Qi movement, dissipates nodules, unblocks the collaterals and alleviates pain.
Fr. Luffae Retinervus Si Gua Lou 9g Resolves toxicity, reduces swelling, unblocks the channels and collaterals, dispels Wind and expels Phlegm.
Rz. Dioscoreae Bulbiferae Huang Yao Zi 9g Resolves Phlegm, dissipates nodules, reduces masses, clears Heat, reduces toxicity, cools the Blood, stops bleeding and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Hai Zao, Mu Li and Kun Bu, for thyroid swellings.
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 15g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, nourishes Yin and generates fluids.
With Xuan Shen, for Excess or Deficiency Heat patterns including Kidney Yin Deficiency with Fire Blazing with soreness and swelling in the throat and lymph nodes, a dry mouth and a parched tongue.
Rx. Scrophulariae Xuan Shen 15g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, nourishes Yin, softens hardness, dissipates nodules, drains Fire and relieves toxicity.
Rx. Bupleuri Chai Hu 9g Spreads Liver Qi, relieves Stagnation, disperses Wind-Heat and resolves Phlegm and congestion.
With Qing Pi, for intercostal pain due to Qi obstruction in the Liver channel.
Per. Citri Reticulatae Viride Qing Pi 9g Spreads Liver Qi, breaks up Stagnant Qi, dissipates accumulations, reduces Food Stagnation, dries Dampness and transforms Phlegm.
With Bai Jie Zi, for a cough with chest and flank pain due to congested fluids.
With Chai Hu, restores the Qi circulation between the Upper and Lower Jiaos to treat long-term Qi Stagnation moving to Blood Stasis with fixed pain and occasional palpable lumps.
Sm. Persicae Tao Ren 9g Breaks up Blood Stasis and invigorates Blood circulation.
Flos Carthami Hong Hua 9g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, opens the channels, unblocks menstruation, alleviates pain and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Tao Ren, for invigorates the Blood for all types of Blood Stagnation.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Strengthens Qi
  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Unblocks the channels
  • Dissipates nodules
  • Inhibits the growth of cancer cells

SYNDROMES

  • Qi Deficiency with Qi and Blood Stagnation

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Interstitial bleeding
  • Tarry stool
  • Pernicious anemia
  • Maybe massive hemorrhage leading to death
  • Interstitial obstruction
  • Intra-abdominal masses
  • Maybe perforation of the abdominal wall
  • Chills and fever
  • Flushed face
  • Palpitations
  • Bluish or purplish discoloration of the skin or mucous membranes

TREATS

  • Mesenteric lymphosarcoma
