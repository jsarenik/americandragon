HERB FORMULAS

MA HUANG LIAN QIAO CHI XIAO DOU TANG - 连翘赤小豆汤 - EPHEDRA, FORSYTHIA, AND ADUKI BEAN DECOCTION

English: Ephedra, Forsythia and Aduki Bean Decoction
Also Known As: Ephedra, Forsythia and Red Bean Decoction
Ephedra, Forsythia and Rice Bean Decoction

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Hb. Ephedrae Ma Huang 6-9g Induces sweating, releases the exterior, disseminates and facilitates Lung Qi, calms wheezing, stops coughing, promotes urination, reduces edema and warms and disperses Cold pathogens.
|With Xing Ren, for Wind-Cold cough and wheezing.
Fr. Forsythiae Lian Qiao 6-15g Clears Heat, especially in the Upper Jiao, relieves toxicity, clears Blood Heat and promotes urination.
Sm. Armeniacae Xing Ren 6-9g Stops cough and calms wheezing.
Fr. Jujube Da Zao 3-6g Moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herb.
With Sheng Jiang, for External Wind-Cold.
Cx. Catalpae Zi Bai Pi 9g Dispels Heat Toxins.
(Cx. Mori) (Sang Bai Pi) (6-9g) (Drains Heat from the Lungs, stops cough, calms wheezing, promotes urination and reduces edema. Usually substitutes for Zi Bai Pi.)
Sm. Phaseoli Chi Xiao Dou 9-30g Promotes urination, reduces edema, clears Damp- Heat, disperses Blood Stasis, reduces swelling and reduces Fire toxicity.
With Da Zao, for Deficiency edema.
With Ma Huang, Lian Qiao and Sang Bai Pi, for Damp-Heat jaundice (mild case).
Rz. Zingiberis Recens Sheng Jiang 3-6g Releases the Exterior, induces perspiration, disperses Cold, warms the Lungs, stops cough and reduces the toxicity of the other herbs.
With Ma Huang, strongly creates sweating to treat all externally contracted Wind-Cold disorders.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 3-6g Moistens the Lungs, resolves Phlegm, stops cough, clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity and moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs.
With Ma Huang, Xing Ren and Sheng Jiang, for coughing or wheezing due to Wind-Cold Attacks the Lungs.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Resolves the Exterior
  • Dissipates Toxins
  • Clears Heat
  • Eliminates Dampness

SYNDROMES

  • Wind-Heat
  • Damp-Heat with unexpelled Wind-Heat pattern and jaundice

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Generalized yellowing
  • Aversion to Cold
  • Fever
  • Anhidrosis
  • Body aches
  • Aversion to Wind
  • Thirst
  • Nasal congestion
  • Sore throat
  • Edema
  • Constipation
  • Sneezing with clear watery nasal discharge
  • Itchy throat
  • Irritability
  • A stifling sensation in the chest
  • Heat in the joints with pain and swelling
  • Itching
  • Dysuria
  • Expectoration of yellow Phlegm
  • Dark, scanty urination or bloody urination
  • T: Red
  • C: Yellow
  • P: Floating or Slippery and rapid

TREATS

  • Urticaria
  • Skin rash due to food allergy
  • Jaundice
  • Rheumatoid arthritis
  • Chronic glomerulonephritis
  • Constipation
  • Acute pyelonephritis
  • Acute icteric hepatitis
  • Edema
  • Fever
  • Osteoarthritis
  • Polymyositis
