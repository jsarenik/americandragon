HERB FORMULAS

BLADDER CANCER #4

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Flos Lonicerae Jin Yin Hua 30g Clears Heat, resolves Fire toxicity, clears Damp-Heat from the Lower Jiao and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Nodus Nelumbinis Rhizomatis Ou Jie 15g Stops bleeding, astringes leakage of Blood,
removes Blood Stasis and eliminates Heat from the Blood
Rz. Alismatis Ze Xie 6g Promotes urination, leaches out Dampness, drains Kidney Fire and drains Damp-Heat from the lower Jiao.
Cx. Phellodendri Huang Bai 12g Drains Damp-Heat especially from the Lower Jiao, drains Kidney Fire, drains Fire, relieves Fire toxicity and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Zhi Zi, for Lower Jiao Damp-Heat leading to hot, painful urinary dysfunction.
Fr. Gardeniae Zhi Zi 12g Clears Heat, reduces Fire Drains Damp-Heat, cools the Blood, relieves toxicity, reduces swelling, invigorates the Blood and inhibits the activation of cancer cells.
Polyporus Zhu Ling 12g Promotes urination, leaches out Dampness, dispels Damp-Heat, inhibits the growth of cancer cells and reduces the size of tumors.
With Ze Xie, drains Damp, unblocks and drains urinary pathways and drains Heat from the Bladder.
Hb. Violae Zi Hua Di Ding 15g Clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity and clears hot sores (Chuang Yung), internally and externally.
Rz. Dryopteris Guan Zhong 15g Clears Heat, relives Fire toxicity, cools the Blood and stops bleeding.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Heat
  • Resolves Fire toxicity
  • Promotes urination
  • Leaches out Dampness
  • Clears Damp-Heat from the Lower Jiao
  • Inhibits the growth of cancer cells
  • Cools the Blood
  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Stops bleeding

SYNDROMES

  • Toxic Heat and Dampness

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Hematuria
  • Blood clots causing pain on urination
  • Pain in the pubic and perineal areas
  • Pain at the end of the penis
  • Anal pain
  • Low back and waist pain and aching
  • Polyuria
  • Oliguria
  • Frequent urination
  • Local redness
  • Swelling and pain
  • Maybe fever and chills
  • T: Normal
  • C: Thin white or yellow
  • P: Rapid and forceful

TREATS

  • Bladder cancer
