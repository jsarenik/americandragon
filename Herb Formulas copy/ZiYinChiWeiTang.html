HERB FORMULAS

ZI YIN CHI WEI TANG - 紫阴持卫汤 - NOURISH, PRESERVE AND PROTECT YIN DECOCTION

English: Nourish, Preserve and Protect Yin Decoction
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Rehmanniae Preparata Shu Di Huang 20-40g Nourishes Liver and Kidney Yin, nourishes Jing and Blood and fills the Marrow.
Rx. Polygoni Multiflori He Shou Wu 40g Tonifies the Liver and Kidneys, nourishes the Blood and nourishes Jing.
Fr. Lycii Gou Qi Zi 20g Nourishes and tonifies Liver and Kidney Blood and Yin, benefits Jing and brightens the eyes.
With Shu Di Huang, for Liver and Kidney Deficiency.
Rx. Dioscoreae Shan Yao 15g Tonifies Kidney Yin and astringes Jing.
Actinolitum Yang Qi Shi 15-30g Warms the Kidneys and the Lower Jiao, warms the Ming Men and fortifies the Yang.
Hb. Epimedii Yin Yang Huo 5-10g Tonifies the Kidneys, strengthens Yang and increases the libido.
With Shan Yao, for a stronger action.
Hb. Ephedrae Ma Huang 1-3g Induces sweating, releases the Exterior and warms and disperses Cold pathogens.
With Shu Di Huang, for coughing and wheezing associated with Kidney Yin Deficiency.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Nourishes Yin
  • Reinforces the Kidneys

SYNDROMES

  • Kidney Yin Deficiency
