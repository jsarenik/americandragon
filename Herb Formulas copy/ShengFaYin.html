HERB FORMULAS

SHENG FA YIN - 生发饮 - GENERATING HAIR BEVERAGE

English: Generating Hair Beverage
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Polygoni Multiflori He Shou Wu 15g Tonifies the Liver and Kidneys, nourishes Blood, nourishes Jing and expels Wind from the skin by nourishing the Blood.
With Gou Qi Zi, Bu Gu Zhi and Tu Si Zi, for premature graying of hair due to Kidney and Liver Deficiency.
Fr. Mori Sang Shen 15g Tonifies the Blood and enriches Yin.
Rx. Astragali Huang Qi 15g Tonifies Qi and Blood .
With Jiu Dang Gui, for Qi and Blood Deficiency.
Fr. Lycii Gou Qi Zi 15g Nourishes and tonifies Liver and Kidney Blood and Yin and benefits Jing.
With Shu Di HUang, for Liver and Kidney Deficiency.
Sm. Cuscutae Tu Si Zi 15g Strengthens Yang, nourishes Yin, astringes Jing and urine, benefits the marrow and tonifies the Kidneys and Liver.
Rx. Scrophulariae Xuan Shen 15g Clears Heat and cools the Blood.
With Sheng Di Huang, for Kidney Yin Deficiency with Fire Blazing.
Wine-washed Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Jiu Dang Gui 9g Tonifies, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood and disperses Cold for Blood Stasis with Blood Deficiency.
With Shu Di Huang, for Heat symptoms due to Yin Deficiency and Devastated Blood.
With Chuan Xiong, harmonizes, nourishes and invigorates the Blood and disperses Blood Stasis.
Wine-fried Rz Chuanxiong Jiu Chuan Xiong 3g Invigorates the Blood, promotes the movement of Qi and alleviates pain.
Fr. Psoraliae Bu Gu Zhi 12g Tonifies the Kidneys, strengthens Yang and stabilizes Jing.
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 12g Clears Heat, cools the Blood and nourishes Yin.
With Shu Di Huang, for Heat symptoms due to Yin Deficiency and Devastated Blood.
Rx. Rehmanniae Preparata Shu Di Huang 12g Nourishes Jing and Blood, fills the Marrow and nourishes Liver and Kidney Yin.
With Jiu Dang Gui, for Blood Deficiency.
Rx. Codonopsis Dang Shen 12g Tonifies the Middle Jiao, augments Qi, nourishes the Blood and promotes the generation of Body Fluids.
With Jiu Dang Gui and Shu Di Huang, for Qi and Blood Deficiency.
With Huang Qi, for Lung and Spleen Qi Deficiency.
With Shu Di Huang and He Shou Wu, for Blood Deficiency.
Sm. Sesame Nigri Hei Zhi Ma 24g Nourishes and fortifies Liver and Kidney Yin, nourishes Jing and Blood and extinguishes Wind.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Enriches the Kidney Jing
  • Nourishes the Liver Blood
  • Tonifies Qi

SYNDROMES

  • Kidney Jing and Liver Blood Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Premature graying of the hair
  • Hair loss
  • Loose teeth
  • Spontaneous emissions
  • Nocturnal emissions
  • Weak and sore low back and knees

TREATS

  • Premature graying of the hair
  • Dental problems
  • Premature aging
  • Decreased sperm motility
  • Lumbago
  • Aplastic anemia
  • Chronic infectious hepatitis
  • Knee pain
  • Prematurely gray hair
  • Alopecia
  • Male infertility
  • Nocturnal emissions
  • Spermatorrhea
  • Low sperm count
  • Frequent urination
  • Osteoporosis
  • Menopause syndrome
