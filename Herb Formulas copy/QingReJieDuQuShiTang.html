HERB FORMULAS

QING RE JIE DU QU SHI TANG - 淸熱解毒驱湿汤 - CLEAR HEAT, DISSOLVE TOXINS AND RID DAMPNESS DECOCTION

English: Clear Heat, Dissolve Toxins and Rid Dampness Decoction
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 9g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, nourishes Yin and generates Fluids.
Rx. Scutellariae Huang Qin 9g Clears Heat, dries Dampness, drains Fire and detoxifies.
With Huang Lian, drains pathogenic Heat from the Upper and Middle Jiaos for high fever, redness, swelling and pain of the head and face, a dry mouth, a parched throat, ulcerations of the mouth and tongue, epistaxis, rashes and irritability in warm-febrile disease.
Cx. Phellodendri Huang Bai 6g Drains Damp-Heat especially from the Lower Jiao, drains Fire and relieves Fire toxicity.
With Huang Lian and Lian Qiao, for skin abscesses and lesions.
Hb. Artemisiae Scopariae Yin Chen Hao 9g Clears Damp-Heat from the Liver and Gallbladder.
With Huang Qin, for Damp-Heat skin rashes.
Flos Lonicerae Jin Yin Hua 12g Clears Heat, resolves Fire toxicity and clears Damp-Heat from the Lower Jiao.
With Lian Qiao, for various Heat disorders.
With Lian Qiao and Huang Qin, for unremitting high fever.
With Gan Cao, for toxic swellings and sores.
With Huang Qin, for suppurating boils that have not ulcerated, or have ulcerated but are not discharging pus.
Fr. Forsythiae Lian Qiao 9g Clears Heat, relieves toxicity and clears Blood Heat.
With Jin Yin Hua, for initial-stage Wind-Heat.
Rx. Sophorae Flavescentis Ku Shen 9g Clears Heat, dries Dampness, disperses Wind, kills parasites and stops itching.
Hb. Lophatheri Dan Zhu Ye 9g Clears Heat, eliminates irritability, promotes urination and clears Damp-Heat.
Rz. Coptidis Huang Lian 9g Clears Heat, drains Dampness, drains Fire, resolves Fire toxicity, clears Heat and stops bleeding.
With Sheng Di Huang, for insomnia and delirium due to unremitting Heat injuring the Ying level.
Rx. Stemonae Bai Bu 6g Expel parasites and kills lice.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 6g Clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity, moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs and guides the herbs to all twelve channels.
With Jin Yin Hua, for Damp rashes or acne.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Heat
  • Resolves toxicity
  • Elininates Dampness

SYNDROMES

  • Toxic Heat

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Pain and itch both inside and outside the vagina which is difficult to bear
  • Watery, yellow exudate
  • Possible bloody Fluid exudate
  • Heart vexation
  • Thirst with a preference for cold drinks
  • Dry mouth
  • Possible alternating Heat and Cold
  • Constipation
  • Feces possibly gluey and sticky
  • Muddy, turbid urination
  • Internl vagina swollen and distended
  • Red face
  • T: Red
  • C: Yellow and greasy
  • P: Wiry and slow or Wiry and rapid

TREATS

  • Pruritis vulva
