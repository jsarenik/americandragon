HERB FORMULAS

LUNG CANCER #35

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Hb. Houttuyniae Yu Xing Cao 30g Clears Heat, relieves toxicity, reduces swellings and abscesses, expels pus (acts especially on the Lungs) and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Hb. Cassiae Occidentalis Wang Jiang Nan Zi 30g Clears Liver Fire, strengthen the Stomach, clears the Lungs, disperses swelling, removes toxicity, relaxes the bowels, clears vision and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Adenophorae Nan Sha Shen 30g Nourishes Yin, moistens the Lungs, dispels Phlegm and stops coughing.
Spica Prunellae Xia Ku Cao 15g Clears Liver Fire, brightens the eyes, clears Hot Phlegm, dissipates nodules and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Sargassum Hai Zao 15g Reduces Phlegm, softens areas of hardness and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Squama Manitis* Chuan Shan Jia 10g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, reduces swelling, promotes the discharge of pus and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Hb. Hedyotis Diffusae Bai Hua She She Cao 80g Clears Heat, strongly relieves Fire toxins, reduces abscesses and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Arnebiae seu Lithospermi Zi Cao 30g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, invigorates Blood circulation, relieves Fire toxins, moistens the Intestines, unblocks the bowels, promotes urination, aids healing of tissues and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Actinidiae Teng Li Gen 30g Clears Heat, removes toxicity, invigorates the Blood, transforms Phlegm, dissipate nodules and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Liver Fire
  • Clears Heat
  • Resolves toxicity
  • Moistens the Intestines
  • Transforms Phlegm
  • Reduces abscesses
  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Inhibits the growth of cancer cells

SYNDROMES

  • Lung cancer due to Liver Fire Scorching the Lungs

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Coughing fits with hemoptysis
  • Burning pain in chest and sides
  • Dizziness
  • Face and eyes red
  • Bitter taste in mouth
  • Dry lips
  • Short-tempered
  • Irascibility
  • Restlessness
  • Maybe dark urine
  • Maybe constipation
  • T: Red
  • C: Thin, yellow and dry
  • P: Wiry and rapid

TREATS

  • Lung cancer

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Squama Manitis is an endangered species and is no longer used (see modifications for suitable substitutes).
