HERB FORMULAS

YUE HUA WAN - 月華丸 - MOONLIGHT PILL

English: Moonlight Pill
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Asparagi Tian Men Dong 30g Nourishes the Kidney Yin, clears Lung Heat, sedates Fire, moistens the Lungs, generates fluids and resolves Phlegm.
With Mai Men Dong, for a dry cough, thirst, and other symptoms due to Yin Deficiency and injured fluids.
Rx. Ophiopogonis Mai Men Dong 30g Moistens the Lungs, nourishes Yin and stops cough.
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 30g Nourishes Yin, generates fluids and increases saliva.
With Shu Di Huang, for Heat symptoms due to Yin Deficiency and Devastated Blood.
With Mai Men Dong and Sha Shen, for a dry mouth, thirst, dry lips and a red tongue due to Stomach Yin Deficiency.
Rx. Rehmanniae Preparata Shu Di Huang 30g Strongly enriches Yin Arrests cough and wheezing
Rz. Dioscoreae Shan Yao 30g Tonifies Lung Qi, nourishes Lung Yin and tonifies Kidney Yin.
Rx. Stemonae Bai Bu 30g Moistens the Lungs and stops cough.
With Sha Shen, for cough due to Lung Heat injuring the Qi and fluids or Lung consumption.
With Chuan Bei Mu, for Cough and chest pain due to Phlegm Heat.
With Chuan Bei Mu and Sha Shen, for pertussis.
Rx. Glehniae/ Adenophorae Sha Shen 30g Nourishes Lung Yin, clears Lung Heat, moistens the Lungs, dispels Phlegm and stops cough.
With Mai Men Dong, for a dry cough with little Phlegm and a parched throat due to Lung Dryness or Heat injuring the Lung Yin.
With Mai Men Dong and Sang Ye, for chronic, dry, non-productive cough with marked reduction of fluids due to Lung Yin Deficiency.
With Tian Men Dong, Sheng Di Huang, Chuan Bei Mu , Bai Bu and E Jiao, for chronic dry cough with no sputum due to Lung Yin Deficiency.
Bul. Fritillariae Cirrhosae Chuan Bei Mu 30g Clears Heat, transforms Phlegm, stops cough and nourishes and moistens the Lung.
Colla Corii Asini E Jiao 30g Nourishes and moistens Yin and moistens the Lungs and Large Intestine.
With Mai Men Dong and Sha Shen, for a dry cough with blood streaked sputum due to Lung Yin Deficiency.
With Tian Men Dong, for Fire or Dryness in the Lungs and Kidneys with emaciation, afternoon fever, parched throat and a dry cough with blood streaked-sputum.
Poria Fu Ling 15g Promotes urination,leaches out Dampness, strengthens the Spleen and transforms Phlegm.
Lutrae Jecur Shui Ta Gan 15g Nourishes Yin, eliminates Heat, calms cough, stops bleeding.
Rx. Notoginseng San Qi 15g Stops bleeding, invigorates the Blood and transforms Blood Stasis.
White Flos Chrysanthemi Bai Ju Hua 60g Nourishes the Liver and clears the eyes for diminished vision due to Kidney and Liver Yin Deficiencies.
Fol. Mori Sang Ye 60g Disperses and scatters Wind-Heat, clears Heat from the Lungs, stops cough, moistens Dryness, cools the Blood and stops bleeding.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Enriches Yin
  • Moistens the Lungs
  • Controls coughing
  • Stops bleeding

SYNDROMES

  • Lung and Kidney Yin Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Chronic cough
  • Consumptive cough
  • Tidal fever
  • Five sole Heat
  • Emaciation
  • Dry, nonproductive cough
  • Cough with Blood-streaked sputum
  • Dry mouth
  • Dry throat
  • A sensation of fullness in the chest
  • Anorexia
  • Shortness of breath
  • Laconic speech
  • Difficult bowel movements
  • Scanty urine
  • T: Red and dry
  • C: Little or None
  • P: Thready and rapid

TREATS

  • Cough
  • Chronic bronchitis
  • Pertussis
  • Pleurisy
  • Upper respiratory tract infection
  • Emphysema
  • Hemoptysis
  • Bronchiectasis
  • Middle or late stage tuberculosis
  • Chronic tonsillitis
  • Chronic chest infection
  • Pneumonia
  • COPD
  • Asthma
  • Dyspnea
