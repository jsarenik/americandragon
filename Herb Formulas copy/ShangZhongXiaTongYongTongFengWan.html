HERB FORMULAS

SHANG ZHONG XIA TONG YONG TONG FENG WAN - 上中下通用痛风囊 - PILL TO TREAT PAINFUL WIND ANYWHERE

English: Pill to Treat Painful Wind Anywhere
Also Known As: Upper, Middle and Lower General Pill for Wind-Pain

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Wine-fried Cx. Phellodendri Jiu Chao Huang Bai 9-21g Drains Damp-Heat especially from the Lower Jiao, drains Kidney Fire and treats Blood Stagnation with Heat.
With Cang Zhu, eliminates Dampness and clears Heat for Lower Jiao Damp-Heat with pain, swelling and weakness in the lower limbs.
Rz. Atractylodis Cang Zhu 9-21g Strongly dries Dampness, tonifies the Spleen and clears Dampness from the Lower Jiao.
With Chuan Xiong, Bai Zhi and Qiang Huo, for Exterior Wind-Cold-Dampness.
With Huang Bai, for pain, swelling, and weakness of the lower extremities or skin lesions due to Wind-Dampness.
Rz. Arisaematis Preparata Zhi Tian Nan Xing 9-31g Dries Dampness and expels Phlegm.
With Cang Zhu, for Wind-Damp painful obstruction.
Dry-fried Massa Medicata Fermentata Chao Shen Qu 9g Reduces Food Stagnation, promotes digestion, moves Qi and harmonizes the Stomach.
Rz. Chuanxiong Chuan Xiong 9g Activates the Blood, promotes the movement of Qi, expels Wind and alleviates pain.
With Gui Zhi, expels Wind-Dampness, warms the channels and collaterals, facilitates joint movement and relieves Bi pain for joint and limb pain due to an External Wind-Cold invasion.
Sm. Persicae Tao Ren 9g Breaks up Blood Stasis and activates Blood circulation.
With Hong Hua, activates the Blood for all types of Blood Stagnation.
Rx. Gentianae Long Dan Cao 3-9g Drains Damp-Heat from the Liver and Gallbladder channels.
Rx. Stephaniae Han Fang Ji 9g Promotes urination, reduces edema especially in the lower body, expels Wind-Dampness, alleviates pain and purges Damp-Heat.
Rx. Angelicae Dahuricae Bai Zhi 9g Expels Wind, expels Dampness, alleviates discharge and treats leukorrhea and diarrhea.
With Qiang Huo, Gui Zhi and Chuan Xiong, for muscle aches and pains due to Wind-Dampness.
Rz. seu Rx. Notopterygii Qiang Huo 3-6g Releases the Exterior, disperses Cold, expels Wind-Cold-Dampness, unblocks painful obstruction and alleviates pain.
With Chuan Xiong, for headaches and generalized body aches and pains associated with the common cold or painful obstruction.
With Cang Zhu, for a heavy-headed sensation.
Wine-fried Rx. Clematidis Jiu Chao Wei Ling Xian 3-9g Disperses Cold and unblocks the collaterals and is used for both mobile painful obstruction due to Wind and intensely painful obstruction due to Cold.
With Qiang Huo, for Wind-Damp Bi causing joint pain.
Ram. Cinnamomi Gui Zhi 3-9g Releases the Exterior, assists Yang, adjusts the Ying and Wei, warms the channels and collaterals to relieve pain, unblocks Yang and transforms Qi and thin mucus.
Flos Carthami Hong Hua 3-6g Activates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, opens the channels, unblocks menstruation and alleviates pain.
With Chuan Xiong, for chest and abdominal pain due to Qi and Blood Stasis.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Heat
  • Dries Dampness
  • Activates the Blood
  • Dispels Wind

SYNDROMES

  • Painful Wind Disorders
  • Wind-Damp-Heat with Blood Stagnation

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Sore, achy joints
  • The pain moves from joint to joint

TREATS

  • Sciatica
  • Trigeminal neuralgia
  • Late-stage trauma
  • Osteoarthritis
  • Low back pain
  • Lupus erythematosis
  • Gout
  • Arthralgia
  • Rheumatism
  • Painful obstruction

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated during pregnancy.
  • Do not use for the debilitated without modifications.
