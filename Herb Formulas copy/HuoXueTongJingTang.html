HUO XUE TONG JING TANG - 活血通经汤- QUICKEN THE BLOOD AND STIMULATE MENSTRUATION DERCOCTION

English: Quicken the Blood and Stimulate Menstruation Decoction
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 10g Tonifies, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood.
Rx. Polygoni Multiflori He Shou Wu 20g Tonifies the Liver and Kidneys, nourishes the Blood and nourishes Jing.
Hb. Leonuri Yi Mu Cao 20g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Stasis, clears Heat and resolves toxicity.
With Dang Gui, for Blood Stasis with infertility, irregular menstruation, lower abdominal pain and fullness, and scanty menstruation.
Caulis Spatholobi Ji Xue Teng 15g Invigorates and tonifies the Blood.
Rx. Achyranthis Bidentatae Niu Xi 15g Invigorates the Blood, expels Blood Stasis and nourishes Liver and Kidney Yin.
Fr. Lycii Gou Qi Zi 15g Nourishes and tonifies Liver and Kidney Blood and Yin and benefits Jing.
Sanguis Draconis Xue Jie 5g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis and alleviates pain.
Rice Wine Mi Jiu
