HERB FORMULAS

JIA WE ER MIAO WAN - 加味二妙丸 - AUGMENTED TWO-MARVELS PILL

English: Augmented Two-Marvels Pill
Also Known As: Modified Decoction of Two Effective Ingredients

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rz. Atractylodis Cang Zhu 9-120g Clears Dampness from the Lower Jiao.
Cx. Phellodendri Huang Bai 6-60g Drains Damp-Heat especially from the Lower Jiao.
With Cang Zhu, eliminates Dampness and clears Heat for Lower Jiao Damp-Heat with pain, swelling, and weakness in the lower limbs.
Rx. Cyathulae Chuan Niu Xi 30g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, expels Wind and drains Dampness, tonifies the Kidneys and Liver and strengthens the tendons and bones.
Cauda Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui Wei 6-30g Invigorates and harmonizes the Blood, disperses Cold and stops pain due to Blood Stasis.
With Chuan Xiong, harmonizes, nourishes and invigorates the Blood and disperses Blood Stasis.
Rx. Stephaniae Tetrandrae Han Fang Ji 6-30g Promotes urination, reduces edema especially in the lower body, expels Wind-Dampness, purges Damp-Heat and alleviates pain.
Rz. Dioscoreae Hypoglaucae Bei Xie 6-30g Resolves turbid Dampness in the Lower Jiao, expels Wind-Dampness, relaxes the sinews and unblocks the Luo channels.
Processed Plastrum Testudinis Zhi Gui Ban 6-30g Nourishes Yin, anchors Yang, benefits the Kidneys and strengthens the bones.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Heat
  • Resolves Dampness
  • Activates the Blood in the channels and collaterals

SYNDROMES

  • Atrophy disorder (Wei Syndrome) due to Damp-Heat
  • Damp-Heat lodged in the Lower Jiao affecting the bones, sinews and lumbar area

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Heaviness of the limbs
  • Weak and flaccid extremities
  • Occasional numbness
  • Slight swelling in the affected extremities
  • Most often affects the lower extremities
  • Possibly fever
  • Stifling sensation in the chest and epigastrium
  • Obesity
  • Lower back pain with a burning sensation
  • Yellow, foul-smelling vaginal discharge
  • Edema
  • Skin problems
  • A dry mouth
  • Irritability
  • A sensation of Heat that begins at the dorsum of the foot and slowly progresses up the leg to the waist
  • Dark, scanty, painful and difficult urination
  • Reddish urine
  • T: Red
  • C: Yellow and greasy
  • P: Soft and rapid and possibly slippery

TREATS

  • Atrophy (Wei) Syndrome
  • Myasthenia gravis
  • Multiple sclerosis
  • Polyneuritis
  • Guillain-Barre disease
  • Acute myelitis (inflammation of the spinal cord)
  • Progressive muscular dystrophy
  • The sequelae of poliomyelitis
  • Eczema
  • Chronic nephritis
  • Weakness
  • Urinary tract infection
  • Vaginitis
  • Acute infectious multiple neuritis
  • Progressive myodystrophy
  • Progressive myodystrophia
  • Hysterical paralysis
  • Periodic paralysis
  • Facial paralysis
  • Low back pain
  • Vaginal discharge
  • Gout
  • Beriberi
  • Edema
