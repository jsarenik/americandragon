HERB FORMULAS

GE GEN HUANG QIN HUANG LIAN TANG - 葛根黃蓮黃苓湯 - KUDZU, SCUTELLARIA AND COPTIS DECOCTION

English: Kudzu, Scutellaria and Coptis Decoction
Also Known As: Kudzu, Coptis and Scute Decoction
Kudzu, Coptis and Scutellaria Decoction
Pueraria, Coptis, and Scute Combination
Decoction of Pueraria, Scutellaria, and Coptis

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Puerariae Ge Gen 6-24g Discharges Exterior conditions, Raises Yang and releases the muscles especially of the neck and upper back, relieves Heat, generates fluids and relieves thirst.
With Huang Qin and Huang Lian, for Heat induced diarrhea and dysenteric disorders due to Damp-Heat.
Rx. Scutellariae Huang Qin 3-12g Clears Lung Heat, drains Fire and detoxifies.
With Huang Lian, drains pathogenic Heat from the upper and middle Jiaos for high fever, redness, swelling and pain of the head and face, dry mouth, parched throat, ulcerations of the mouth and tongue, epistaxis, rashes irritability in warm-febrile disease, toothache and explosive diarrhea and dysentery with burning pain in the anus or fitted, filthy stools and abdominal pain.
Rz. Coptidis Huang Lian 3-10g Drains Fire, relieves toxicity, clears Heart Fire and drains Stomach Fire.
With Huang Lian, Huang Qin and Ge Gen, for External Heat with diarrhea or dysentery.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Preparata Zhi Gan Cao 2-6g Moderates and harmonizes the properties of other herbs.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Releases the Exterior
  • Clears Internal Yang Ming Heat
  • Raises Yang to stop diarrhea

SYNDROMES

  • Improper treatment of Tai Yang Stage with purgatives (c).
  • Lower Jiao Damp Heat with lingering exterior symptoms.
  • Diarrhea and dysentery whether or not there is Wind Invasion.

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with dysentery or diarrhea with no fever and a slow, submerged pulse (Deficiency Cold).
HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

NOTES

  • For diaper rash: Best results are obtained when the formula is used both internally and topically as a wash. Take internally four times a day. Wash three times a day.
