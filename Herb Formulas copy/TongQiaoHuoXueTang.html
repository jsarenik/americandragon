HERB FORMULAS

TONG QIAO HUO XUE TANG - 通竅活血汤 - OPEN THE PORTALS AND QUICKEN THE BLOOD DECOCTION

English: Open the Portals and Quicken the Blood Decoction
Also Known As: Unblock the Orifices and Invigorate the Blood Pills
Decoction to Invigorate the Blood Circulation and Open the Orifices
Decoction for Activating Blood Circulation
Persica and Musk Combination

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Paeoniae Rubra Chi Shao 3-15g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Stasis, clears Heat and cools the Blood.
With Chuan Xiong for abscesses, boils and trauma induced pain.
With Tao Ren, Hong Hua and Chuan Xiong, for Blood Stagnation in the chest.
Flos Carthami Hong Hua 6-15g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis and stops pain.
With Chuan Xiong, for chest and abdominal pain due to Qi and Blood Stasis.
With Tao Ren, invigorates the Blood for all types of Blood Stagnation such as dysmenorrhea, amenorrhea, swollen painful sores and chest and abdominal pain.
Rz. Chuanxiong Chuan Xiong 3-15g Invigorates the Blood, moves Qi, expels Wind and stops pain.
With Hong Hua for Blood Stasis in the chest.
Sm. Persicae Tao Ren 6-15g Breaks up Blood Stasis.
With Hong Hua, for Rheumatoid arthritis.
Bul. Allii Fistulosi Cong Bai 3-15g Releases the exterior, induces sweat, disperses Cold, unblocks the Yang, relieves toxicity and disperses clumps.
Fr. Jujube

 

Da Zao 3-6g Tonifies the Spleen, tonifies Qi, nourishes the Blood, calms the Spirit and moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of the other herbs.
With Sheng Jiang, nourishes the Blood and prevents injury from harsh Blood movers and assists the movement of Qi.
Rz. Zingiberis Recens Sheng Jiang 3-10g Releases the exterior, expels Cold, Warms the Lungs, transforms Phlegm and disperses and unblocks the Qi of the nasal orifices.
Moschus She Xiang 0.1-0.5g Opens orifices, revives the Spirit, invigorates the Blood, reduces swelling and stops pain.
Rice Wine   250g Helps to invigorate the Blood.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Dispels Blood Stasis
  • Opens the orifices

SYNDROMES

  • Blood Stasis in the head, face and upper body
  • Childhood nutritional impairment
