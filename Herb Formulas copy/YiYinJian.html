HERB FORMULAS

YI YIN JIAN - 一荫健 - ONE YIN DECOCTION

English: One Yin Decoction
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 6g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, nourishes Yin and generates fluids.
With Shu Di Huang, for Heat symptoms due to Yin Deficiency and Devastated Blood.
Rx. Rehmanniae Preparata Shu Di Huang 6g Nourishes the Blood, nourishes Liver and Kidney Yin, strongly enriches Yin, nourishes Jing and fills the Marrow.
Rx. Paeoniae Alba Bai Shao 6g Nourishes the Blood, regulates menstruation, astringes Yin, softens the Liver and relieves pain.
Rx. Ophiopogonis Mai Men Dong 6g Nourishes Yin.
With Sheng Di Huang, for hematemesis or epistaxis due to Yin Deficiency with Excess Heat.
Rz. Anemarrhenae Zhi Mu 6g Clears Heat, drains Fire, nourishes Yin, moistens Dryness, generates Fluids, clears Deficiency Fire and quenches thirst.
Cx. Lycii Di Gu Pi 6g Cools the Blood, drains Yin Deficiency Fire, drains Kidney Fire and eliminates lurking Heat.
Rz. Polygonati Huang Jing 6g Tonifies Spleen Qi, nurtures Stomach Yin, moistens Lung Yin, tonifies the Kidneys and strengthens Jing.
Rx. Salviae Miltiorrhizae Dan Shen  4g Invigorates, cools and nourishes the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, clears Heat and soothes irritability.
Fr. Aurantii Zhi Ke 3g Promotes the movement of Qi, reduces distention and pressure and resolves hardenings.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Nourishes Yin
  • Clears Deficiency Heat

SYNDROMES

  • Yin Deficiency with Dry Blood
  • Kidney and Liver Yin Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Dizziness
  • Dry eyes
  • Tiredness
  • Blurred vision
  • Insomnia
  • Lower back ache
  • Night sweating
  • Tinnitus
  • Five sole Heat
  • T: Red
  • C: Little or none
  • P: Thready and rapid

TREATS

  • Amenorrhea
  • Late menstruation
