HERB FORMULAS

WU TOU TANG - 烏頭湯 - ACONITE DECOCTION

English: Aconite Decoction
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Aconiti Preparata Zhi Chuan Wu 9-15g Expels Wind-Dampness, disperses Cold, warms and activates the Channels and stops pain.
With Ma Huang and Bai Shao, for generalized joint pain with restrictions in range of motion.
Hb. Ephedrae Ma Huang 6-9g Releases the exterior, induces sweating, promotes urination and disperses Cold.
With Zhi Chuan Wu, for Wind-Cold Bi.
Rx. Paeoniae Alba Bai Shao 6-15g Alleviates pain and nourishes Blood.
With Gan Cao, for muscle spasms especially in the calf, due to Blood Deficiency.
Rx. Astragali Huang Qi 6-15g Strengthens the Spleen, tonifies Qi, tonifies Wei Qi, stabilizes the Exterior and nourishes Blood.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 3-9g Tonifies the Spleen, tonifies Qi, alleviates pain and moderates and harmonizes the properties of the other herbs.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Disperses Cold
  • Reinforces Yang
  • Replenishes Qi and Blood
  • Stops pain
  • Warms the channels
  • Disperses Wind

SYNDROMES

  • Wind-Cold-Damp Bi - Cold Predominant
  • Long-term Wind-Cold Dampness invades the channels
  • Cold Bi in patients with Qi Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Severe difficulty in flexion and extension
  • Severe pain
  • Cold hands and feet
  • Aversion to Cold
  • Fatigue
  • Loose Stools
  • Severe pain and coldness of muscles and joints
  • Severely restricted motion of joints
  • Cold extremities
  • Whole body pain, especially in the hands and feet
  • Anorexia
  • Heavy feeling in muscles
  • Catches cold easily
  • No thirst
  • T: Pale with slight teethmarks
  • C: Greasy and white
  • P: Deep and thready or Wiry, tight and slow or Slightly slippery

TREATS

  • Osteoarthritis
  • Panarthralgia
  • Painful obstruction
  • Scapulohumeral periarthritis
  • Neck pain and stiffness
  • Shoulder pain
  • Whole body pain
  • Leg Qi
  • Arthralgia
  • Carpal tunnel syndrome
  • Brachial plexus neuritis
  • Upper back pain
  • Low back pain
  • Hip pain

NOTES
