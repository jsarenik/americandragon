ERB FORMULAS

SAN ZI YANG QIN TANG - 三子養親湯 - THREE SEED DECOCTION TO NOURISH ONE'S PARENTS

English: Three Seed Decoction to Nourish One’s Parents
Also Known As: Decoction of Three Seeds Nourishing the Parents

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Sm. Sinapis Bai Jie Zi 6-9g Warms the Lungs, regulates Lung Qi, resolves Phlegm and relaxes the diaphragm.
With Lai Fu Zi restores the smooth flow of Qi and reduces Phlegm to treat coughs and wheezing especially when the chest and diaphragm feel full of Phlegm, particularly Cold-Phlegm, and the descent of Lung Qi is impaired.
With Lai Fu Zi and Zi Su Zi for cough with copious, clear, bubbly sputum, and pain, fullness, and distention of the chest due to Cold-Phlegm Obstructs the chest and flanks.
Fr. Perillae Zi Su Zi 6-9g Stops cough, calms wheezing, descends Qi and dissolves Phlegm.
Sm. Raphani Lai Fu Zi 6-9 Reduces Food Stagnation, transforms accumulation, eliminates distention, descends Qi and reduces Phlegm.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Descends Qi
  • Relaxes the diaphragm
  • Reduces Food Stagnation
  • Resolves Phlegm

SYNDROMES

  • Cold in the Lungs with Food Stagnation
  • Cold Phlegm Obstructs the Lungs
  • Phlegm Retention with Qi Stagnation
  • Han Tan
  • Elderly person with a weak Spleen and Stomach and Phlegm due to Food Stagnation
  • Emergency Rebellious Qi Conditions

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Cough
  • Wheezing
  • Copious sputum
  • Chest distention
  • Anorexia
  • Indigestion
  • Hiccup
  • Shortness of breath
  • T: Normal
  • C: Greasy, white
  • P: Slippery

TREATS

  • Chronic bronchitis
  • Bronchial asthma
  • Obesity
  • Hiccup
  • Chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD)
  • Nausea/Vomiting
  • Globus hystericus
  • Indigestion
  • Bronchiectasis
  • Emphysema
  • Pediatric asthma
  • Diaphragm spasms
  • Vascular Bi
  • Cough
  • Cor pulmonare
  • Anorexia
  • Upper respiratory tract infection
  • Dyspnea
