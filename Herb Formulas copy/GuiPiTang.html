HERB FORMULAS

GUI PI TANG - 归脾汤 - RESTORE THE SPLEEN DECOCTION - GINSENG AND LONGAN COMBINATION

English: Restore the Spleen Decoction
Also Known As: Ginseng and Longan Combination
Decoction to Strengthen Heart and Spleen
Angelica Splenic Decoction

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Ginseng Ren Shen 3-15g Tonifies Spleen, Heart and Yuan Qi.
With Bai Zhu, tonifies the transformation and transportation functions of the Spleen.
With Huang Qi, Bai Zhu and Zhi Gan Cao, tonifies the Spleen to generate Blood.
With Bai Zhu and Fu Ling, for Spleen and Stomach Qi Deficiency.
With Dang Gui, Long Yan Rou and Yuan Zhi, for Heart and Spleen Deficiency.
(Rx. Codonopsis) (Dang Shen) (9-45g) (May substitute for Ren Shen.)
Rx. Astragali Huang Qi 2-30g Tonifies Blood and Spleen Qi.
With Dang Gui, generates and nourishes Blood.
With Ren Shen, for Qi Deficiency with general debility, anorexia, and spontaneous sweat.
With Bai Zhu, for Spleen Qi Deficiency with weakness, lassitude and loose stools.
With Long Yan Rou, Dang Gui and Suan Zao Ren, for insomnia and other signs of Qi and Blood Deficiency.
Rz. Atractylodis Macrocephalae Bai Zhu 3-30g Strengthens the Spleen and dries Dampness.
Poria Fu Ling 3-30g Strengthens the Spleen and harmonizes the Middle Jiao.
With Ren Shen and Huang Qi, tonifies the Spleen.
With Ren Shen and Bai Zhu for weak and deficient Spleen with fatigue, anorexia and loose stools.
With Gan Cao, for Spleen and Heart Deficiency causing facial edema, palpitations, and short-ness of breath.
With Dang Shen, Suan Zao Ren and Long Yan Rou, for insomnia due to Spleen and Heart Insufficiency.
(Scl. Poria Pararadicis) (Fu Shen) (3-30g) (Usually used in place of Fu Ling. Pacifies the Heart, calms the Shen and soothes the nerves.)
(With Suan Zao Ren, calms the Shen.)
Sm. Zizyphi Spinosae Suan Zao Ren 3-30g Nourishes Heart Yin and tonifies Liver Blood.
 With Ren Shen, Fu Shen and Long Yan Rou, for insomnia, palpitations and irritability due to Spleen Qi and Heart Blood Deficiency.
Arillus Longan Long Yan Rou 3-30g Nourishes the Blood, calms the Shen, and tonifies and nourishes the Heart and Spleen.
With Ren Shen, Dang Gui and Suan Zao Ren, for palpitations and insomnia due to Heart Blood Deficiency, especially in cases with Spleen Qi Deficiency.
Rx. Aucklandiae Mu Xiang 1-15g Regulates Qi, strengthens the Spleen and prevents indigestion due to the rich nature of the other herbs.
With Bai Zhu, especially effective.
Fried Rx. Glycyrrhizae Zhi Gan Cao 1-7.5g Harmonizes and tonifies Qi.
 With Fu Ling and Dang Shen, strengthens the Spleen, augments Qi and treats palpitations due to Heart Qi Deficiency.
With Ren Shen, relieves palpitations and anxiety.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 2-30g Nourishes and harmonizes the Blood.
With Huang Qi, for many Blood Deficiency patterns.
Fried Rx. Polygalae Zhi Yuan Zhi 1-30g Calms the Shen, sedates the Heart and opens Orifices.
With Suan Zao Ren, it is especially effective because it balances the contracting, introverted and dispersing, extroverted manifestations of Spirit to ensure the proper balance of openness and sobriety.
With Fu Shen and Suan Zao Ren, for symptoms of Heart Blood Deficiency.
With Suan Zao Ren, Bai Zhu and Dang Gui, for palpitations and insomnia due to Qi Deficiency.
With Huang Qi, Dang Gui and Long Yan Rou, for forgetfulness, palpitations and insomnia.
Rx. Zingiberis Recens Sheng Jiang 1-12g Aids in strengthening the Spleen.
With Da Zao, improves the appetite and regulates the Ying and Wei, facilitating the actions of the formula.
Fr. Jujube Da Zao 1-3g Nourishes the Blood, calms the Shen, tonifies the Qi and harmonizes.
With Bai Zhu and Dang Shen, for weakness and lassitude due to Spleen and Stomach Deficiency.
With Sheng Jiang, prevents tonics from generating Dampness in the Middle Jiao.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Augments Qi
  • Nourishes the Blood
  • Strengthens the Spleen
  • Nourishes the Heart

SYNDROMES

  • Spleen Not Governing Blood
  • Heart Blood Deficiency
  • Heart (Blood) and Spleen (Qi) Deficiency due to worry (excessive deliberation or obsession)
  • Atrophy disorder (Wei Syndrome) due to Spleen and Stomach Qi Deficiency
