HERB FORMULAS

ENURESIS #1

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Rehmanniae Preparata Shu Di Huang 18g Nourishes Yin and Blood and tonifies the Kidneys.
With Shan Zhu Yu, nourishes Kidney Yin and secures Jing, while restraining urination and sweating.
Fr. Corni Shan Zhu Yu 9g

Nourishes Kidney Yin and Yang and astringes Yin.
With Shan Yao, tonifies the Liver and benefits the Spleen.
With Jin Ying Zi, for spermatorrhea or premature ejaculation.
with Shu Di Huang and Shan Yao, for urinary frequency (day or night), dizziness, tinnitus and lower back pain due to Kidney Deficiency.
With Fu Pen Zi, Sha Yuan Zi, Jin Yin Zi and Tu Si Zi, for terminal dribbling after urinating.

Rx. Dioscoreae Shan Yao 9g Tonifies Kidney Yin and astringes Jing
With Shu Di Huang and Shan Zhu Yu, for Kidney Deficiency with spermatorrhea, dizziness, tinnitus, lower back pain, impotence and night sweats.
Sm. Astragali Complanati Sha Yuan Zi 9g Tonifies Kidney Yang and astringes Jing.
With Fu Pen Zi, for impotence, premature ejaculation, enuresis, incontinence and vaginal discharge.
with Qian Shi, for nocturnal emission and spermatorrhea.
Sm. Euryales Qian Shi 9g Stabilizes the Kidneys and astringes Jing.
Fr. Rosae Laevigatae Jin Ying Zi 9g Stabilizes Kidneys, astringes urine and retains Jing.
With Qian Shi, for spermatorrhea, urinary frequency, or vaginal discharge due to Kidney Deficiency.
Stamen Nelumbinis Lian Xu 9g Stabilizes the Kidneys, astringes Jing and clears Heart Heat.
With Jin Ying Zi and Qian Shi, for vaginal discharge form Kidney Deficiency.
Calcined Concha Ostreae Duan Mu Li 20g Calms the Liver, benefits Yin, anchors Floating Yang and prevents leakage of fluids.
Fr. Rubi Fu Pen Zi 9g Tonifies and stabilizes the Kidneys, astringes Jing and urine and tonifies Yang.
Fr. Schisandrae Wu Wei Zi 9g Tonifies the Kidneys, astringes Jing and stops diarrhea and spermatorrhea.
Sm. Cuscutae Tu Si Zi 9g Strengthens Yang, nourishes Yin, astringes Jing and urine, benefits the marrow and tonifies the Kidneys.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Supplements the Kidneys
  • Secures the Bladder

SYNDROMES

  • Kidney Yang Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Enuresis or
  • Urinary incontinence
  • Lumbago: low back feels cold, weak and aching
  • Feeling chilled
  • Aversion to Cold
  • Cold limbs
  • General debility
  • Pale face
  • Urinary frequency with abundant, clear urine or
  • Oliguria with edema
  • Maybe nocturia
  • Lassitude
  • Withdrawn, subdued behavior
  • Knees feel cold and aching
  • Dizziness
  • Tinnitus
  • Impotence
  • Spermatorrhea
  • Premature ejaculation
  • Low libido
  • Female infertility ("uterus cold and infertile")
  • Maybe anorexia
  • Maybe loose stools
  • Maybe chronic diarrhea or "cock crow" diarrhea
  • T: Pale and swollen
  • C: Thin and white
  • P: Deep and thready - maybe slow

TREATS

  • Enuresis
