FLAT WARTS #1

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Magnetitum Ci Shi 30g Anchors Liver Yang, calms the Spirit, benefits Yin, nourishes the Kidneys, augments the Liver.
Concha Ostreae Mu Li 30g Softens hardness and dissipates nodules.
Concha Margaritaferae Usta Zhen Zhu Mu 30g Calms the Liver, anchors the Yang, drains Liver Fire, promotes healing and reduces itching of the skin.
Okra Gom Bo 30g Smoothes the skin, prevents the eruption of pimples and acne, treat itchiness and moistens the skin.
Flos Carthami Hong Hua 9g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, opens the channels and alleviates pain.
With Tao Ren, invigorates the Blood for all types of Blood Stagnation.
Sm. Persicae Tao Ren 9g Breaks up Blood Stasis, invigorates Blood circulation and drains abscesses.
Squama Manitis Chuan Shan Jia 9g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, reduces swelling and promotes the discharge of pus.
With Zao Jiao Zi, for suppurative, inflamed skin lesions that have pustulated but remain closed.
Spina Gleditsiae Zao Jiao Ci 9g Draws out toxicity, discharges pus, invigorates the Blood and reduces swelling.
With Jin Yin Hua, for initial stages of abscesses and boils.
Flos Lonicerae Jin Yin Hua 9g Clears Heat and resolves Fire toxicity.
Cx. Phellodendri Huang Bai 9g Drains Fire and relieves Fire toxicity.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Heat
  • Restrains Yang
  • Softens the hard
