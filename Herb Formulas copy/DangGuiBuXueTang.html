HERB FORMULAS

DANG GUI BU XUE TANG - 當歸補血湯 - DANG GUI DECOCTION TO TONIFY THE BLOOD

English: Tangkuei Decoction to Tonify the Blood
Also Known As: Tangkuei and Astragalus Combination
Blood Tonifying Decoction with Chinese Angelica
Angelica Blood Supplementing Decoction

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Astragali Huang Qi 10-30g Strongly tonifies Spleen and Lung Qi to reinforce Blood and generate flesh.
 With Dang Gui, for Qi and Blood Deficiencies with chronic non-healing sores, numbness, Bi Syndrome, paresthesia and thirst in patients with a weak pulse
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 2-9g Generates flesh, nourishes and invigorates Blood and replenishes the Qi of the Blood.
With Huang Qi, for excessive blood loss with pale face, weakness, fatigue and / or low grade fever.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Tonifies Qi
  • Generates Blood
  • Promotes healing of wounds

SYNDROMES

  • Heart Blood Deficiency
  • Heart and Liver Blood Deficiency
  • Spleen Not Governing Blood
  • Yin ulcers
  • Fever and headache due to Blood loss

  • Postpartum irregular menstruation
  • Slow healing of wounds
  • Excessive Blood loss after labor
  • T: Pale
  • P: Flooding, big, forceless and Deficient when pressed hard or Big and weak

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with Yin Deficiency with tidal fever.
  • Contraindicated for those with Excess conditions.
