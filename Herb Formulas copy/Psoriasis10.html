HERB FORMULAS

PSORIASIS #10

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 30g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, nourishes Yin and generates fluids.
With Xuan Shen, for Excess or Deficiency Heat patterns.
With Xuan Shen and Shui Niu Jiao, for Heat in the Ying Stage.
Rz. Coptidis Huang Lian 20g Clears Heat, drains Dampness, drains Fire and resolves Fire toxicity.
With Sheng Di Huang, for insomnia and delirium due to unremitting Heat injuring the Ying level.
Flos Lonicerae Jin Yin Hua 20g Clears Heat, resolves Fire toxicity and vents and disperses External Wind-Heat.
With Lian Qiao, for various Heat disorders.
With Lian Qiao, Sheng Di Huang, Xuan Shen and Zhu Ye, vents Heat to the Qi Level from the Ying Level.
With Xuan Shen and Dan Shen, for Ying level Heat with insomnia, irritability, skin blotches, dry mouth with a dark-purplish tongue.
Fr. Forsythiae Lian Qiao 20g Clears Heat (especially in the Upper Jiao), relieves toxicity, reduces abscesses, dissipates clumps and clears Blood Heat.
With Jin Yin Hua, for initial-stage Wind-Heat.
Rx. et Rz. Rhei Da Huang 5g Drains Heat, purges accumulations, transforms Dampness, drains Heat from the Blood and reduces Fire toxicity.
With Huang Lian, for a feeling of fullness and distention due to Heat accumulation.
Hb. Phylostachys Zhu Ye 15g Clears Heat, relieves irritability, generates Body Fluids and promotes urination.
With Lian Qiao. for Wind-Heat with thirst.
Rx. Salviae Miltiorrhizae Dan Shen 15g Invigorates, cools and nourishes the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, clears Heat and soothes irritability.
With Sheng Di Huang, Zhu Ye and Xuan Shen, for Ying or Xue Level Heat.
With Xuan Shen, Jin Yin Hua and Lian Qiao, for palpitations and insomnia due to Heat.
Rx. Scrophulariae Xuan Shen 15g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, nourishes Yin, drains Fire and relieves toxicity.
With Sheng Di Huang, Lian Qiao and Shui Niu Jiao, for Ying Stage Heat.
Caulis Akebiae Mu Tong 15g Promotes urination.
Cornu Bubali Shui Niu Jiao 15g Clears Heat from the Ying and Xue stages, relieves Fire toxicity, cools the Blood and stops bleeding.
With Sheng Di Huang, for Loss of consciousness and delirium, with epistaxis, hematemesis, or purpura due to Blood Heat.
With Huang Lian and Xuan Shen, for high fever, loss of consciousness, delirium, hematemesis, epistaxis, and purpura associated with warm febrile disease.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 15g Clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity and moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs.
With Jin Yin Hua, for damp rashes or acne.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Heat from the Ying
  • Relieves Fire Toxin
  • Nourishes the Yin
  • Invigorates the Blood

SYNDROMES

  • Ying Stage Heat

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • High fever worse at night
  • Severe irritability
  • Restlessness
  • Thirst
  • Delirium
  • Faint indistinct purpura and erythema
  • Insomnia
  • Maculae
  • Eyes that are open or closed for a prolonged period of time
  • T: Scarlet and dry
  • C: Little
  • P: Thready and rapid or Rapid

TREATS

  • Psoriasis
