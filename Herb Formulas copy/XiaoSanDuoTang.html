HERB FORMULAS

XIAO SAN DUO TANG - 消三多汤 - DISPERSE THE THREE PROFUSIONS DECOCTION

English: Disperse the Three Profusions Decoction
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Gypsum Fibrosum Shi Gao 30g Clears Heat in the Qi Stage, drains Fire, relieves irritability, quenches thirst, clears Excess Heat from the Lungs and clears Blazing Stomach Fire.
With Ren Shen, for excessive Heat with Qi Deficiency.
with Ren Shen and Mai Men Dong, for excessive Heat with Qi and Yin Deficiency.
Rz. Polygonati Huang Jing 15g Tonifies Spleen Qi, nurtures Stomach Yin, moistens Lung Yin, tonifies the Kidneys, strengthens Jing and relieves wasting and thirsting.
With Ren Shen, for weakness and debility, reduced appetite, fatigue and emaciation in the aftermath of a prolonged illness or as a result of a chronic wasting disease.
With Shi Gai, Tian Hua Fen, Shi Gao and Huang Lian, for wasting and thirsting disorder with relatively more Heat.
Rx. Paeoniae Alba Bai Shao 15g Nourishes the Blood, astringes Yin, adjusts the Ying and Wei, softens the Liver and relieves pain.
Rx. Dioscoreae Shan Yao 15g Tonifies the Spleen, nourishes Stomach, Lung and Kidney Yin. tonifies Lung Qi and astringes Jing.
With Shi Gao, Tian Hua Fen, Mai Men Dong and Zhi Mu, for wasting and thirsting disorder (Xiao Ke) with Internal Heat.
Rx. Polygoni Multiflori He Shou Wu 15g Tonifies the Liver and Kidneys, nourishes the Blood and nourishes Jing.
Rz. Anemarrhenae Zhi Mu 9g Clears Heat and drains Fire from the Lungs and Stomach Nourishes Yin and moistens Dryness Generates Fluids, clears Deficiency Fire and quenches thirst.
With Huang Lian, for Blazing Stomach Fire with intense hunger, dry stools, dry, yellow tongue coat and a slippery rapid pulse.
With Tian Hua Fen and Mai Men Dong, for thirst and irritability due to Lung and Stomach Dryness.
With Shi Gao, cools Heat in the Yang Ming or Qi Level while protecting Stomach fluids to treat high fever, profuse sweating, insatiable thirst, a red face, aversion to Heat and a large, strong pulse.
Rz. Coptidis Huang Lian 9g Clears Heat, drains Dampness, drains Stomach Fire and resolves toxicity.
Colla Corii Asini E Jiao 9g Tonifies the Blood, nourishes and moistens Yin and moistens the Lungs and Large Intestine.
With Huang Lian, for Heat toxin clumping in the Intestines and injuring the Blood collaterals causing Blood and pus in the stools.
Rx. Trichosanthis Tian Hua Fen 9g Drains Heat, generates Fluids, clears and drains Lung Heat, transforms Phlegm and moistens Lung Dryness.
Rx. Ophiopogonis Mai Men Dong 9g Moistens the Lungs, nourishes Stomach Yin, moistens the Intestines and generates Fluids.
Cx. Lycii Di Gu Pi 9g Cools the Blood, drains Yin Deficiency Fire, drains Kidney Fire, eliminates lurking Heat and clears and drains Heat in the Lung.
Rx. Ginseng Ren Shen 5g Tonifies Yuan Qi, Spleen, Stomach and Lung Qi, generates Body Fluids and stops thirst.
With Shi Gao and Zhi Mu, for Heat injuring the Qi and Yin fluids with fever, irritability, intense thirst and profuse sweating.
with Mai Men Dong, Tian Hua Fen and Zhi Mu, for wasting and thirsting disorder with intense unquenchable thirst.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Tonifies the Kidneys, Spleen and Liver
  • Tonifies Qi
  • Nourishes Yin
  • Clears Heat

SYNDROMES

  • Kidney, Spleen, Liver, Qi and Yin Deficiency with Excess Heat
