HERB FORMULAS

ENDOMETRIOSIS #5

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Ram. Cinnamomi Gui Zhi 9g Warms the channels and collaterals to relieve pain, unblocks Yang, transforms the Qi and thin mucus and warms and facilitates the flow of Qi through the channels and collaterals and Blood through the vessels.
Rx. Paeoniae Rubra Chi Shao 12g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, clears Heat, cools the Blood and relieves pain.
With Mu Dan Pi, for Blood Stasis disorders (amenorrhea, menstrual pain, traumatic injury).
Cx. Moutan Mu Dan Pi 12g Clears Heat, cools and harmonizes the Blood, invigorates the Blood and dispels Blood Stasis.
With Chi Shao, for irregular menstruation due to Heat and obstruction from Blood Deficiency.
With Gui Zhi, for chest and abdominal pain due to Blood Stagnation.
Sm. Persicae Tao Ren 9g Breaks up Blood Stasis and invigorates Blood circulation.
Thallus Eckloniae Kun Bu 12g Resolves Phlegm, promotes urination and reduces swelling.
Rz. Sparganii San Leng 12g Forcefully breaks up Blood Stasis, regulates Qi, regulates menstruation and alleviates pain.
With E Zhu, for Qi and Blood Stagnation with clumping and accumulation leading to pain with distending pain in the chest, epigastrium and abdomen, amenorrhea, dysmenorrhea, uterine myoma and ovarian cysts.
Rz. Curcumae E Zhu 12g Invigorates Blood circulation, dispels Blood Stasis regulates Qi and alleviates pain.
Carapax Trionycis Bie Jia 15g Nourishes Yin, anchors Yang, invigorates the Blood, promotes menstruation, softens hardness and dissipates nodules.
Poria Fu Ling 15g Promotes urination, leaches out Dampness, strengthens the Spleen, harmonizes the Middle Jiao and transforms Phlegm.
With Gui Zhi, warms and transforms fluids, unblocks Yang and promotes urination.
Hb. Cynomorii Suo Yang 15g Tonifies the Kidneys, benefits Jing and marrow, strengthens Yang, nourishes Liver Yin and Blood and strengthens sinews and bones.
Hb. Epimedii Yin Yang Huo 30g Tonifies the Kidneys, strengthens Yang and warms and unblocks the flow of Yang Qi.
Eupolyphaga/Steleophaga Tu Bie Chong 15g Breaks and drives out up Blood Stasis, invigorates Blood circulation and disperses lumps and masses.
With Bie Jia, Mu Dan Pi and San Leng, for immobile abdominal masses due to Blood Stasis.
Sm. Vaccariae Wang Bu Liu Xing 12g Promotes the movement of Blood, invigorates the channels, reduces swelling and drains abscesses.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Dispels Blood Stasis
  • Clears Heat
  • Cools the Blood
  • Relieves pain
  • Warms the channels and collaterals
  • Tonifies the Kidneys

SYNDROMES

  • Blood Stagnation with Kidney Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Pain in the lower abdomen and pelvic region
  • Heavy and/or irregular periods
  • Spotting between periods or before periods
  • Infertility
  • Difficulty conceiving
  • Pain during sexual intercourse
  • Abdominal swelling
  • Painful bowel movements, especially during menstruation
  • Bleeding from the rectum during menstruation
  • Lower abdominal pain during menstruation
  • Low back pain
  • Painful urination during menstruation
  • Sometimes no symptoms at all
  • Other symptoms may include:
    Headaches
    Low grade fevers
    Depression
    Hypoglycemia
    Anxiety
    Susceptibility to infections and allergies
  • T: Purple or Purple spots
  • C: Normal
  • P: Choppy

TREATS

  • Endometriosis
