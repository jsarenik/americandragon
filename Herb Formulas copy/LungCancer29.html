HERB FORMULAS

LUNG CANCER #29

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Scutellariae Huang Qin 15g Clears Heat and dries Dampness, Drains Fire and detoxifies Cools the Blood and stops bleeding and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Zhi Zi, powerfully clears and drains lurking Heat from the Lungs with fever, fullness in the chest, cough with yellow sputum and epistaxis or hematemesis.
Fr. Gardeniae Zhi Zi 15g Clears Heat, reduces Fire, drains Damp-Heat, cools the Blood, relieves toxicity, reduces swelling, invigorates the Blood and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Scrophulariae Xuan Shen 18g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, nourishes Yin, softens hardness, dissipates nodules, transforms Phlegm-Heat, drains Fire and relieves toxicity.
With Jin Yin Hua and Gan Cao, for boils, carbuncles and furuncles.
With Sheng Di Huang and Mai Men Dong, for a sore, dry throat due to Deficiency Fire.
With Sheng Di Huang, for Excess or Deficiency Heat patterns.
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 18g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, nourishes Yin, generates fluids.
Flos Lonicerae Jin Yin Hua 30g Clears Heat, resolves Fire toxicity and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Gan Cao, for toxic swellings and sores.
With Gan Cao and Xuan Shen, for sloughing ulcers from Heat toxicity.
With Huang Qin, for suppurating boils that have not ulcerated, or have ulcerated but are not discharging pus.
Rx. Ophiopogonis Mai Men Dong 15g Moistens the Lungs, nourishes Yin and stops coughing.
With Sheng Di Huang, for hematemesis or epistaxis due to Yin Deficiency with Excess Heat.
Sm. Armeniacae Xing Ren 6g Stops coughing, calms wheezing and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Mai Men Dong, for a dry cough due to Dryness and Heat injuring the Lungs.
Hb. Menthae Bo He 3g Disperses Wind-Heat, cools and clears the head and eyes and benefits the throat.
Flos Chrysanthemi Ju Hua 10g Disperses Wind, clears Heat and detoxifies.
With Bo He, for Wind-Heat patterns with fever, chills, headache, dizziness, sore eyes and sore throat.
Fr. Aurantii Zhi Ke 10g Promotes the movement of Qi, reduces distention and pressure and resolves hardenings.
Fr. Trichosanthis Gua Lou 15g Clears Lung Heat, transforms Hot Phlegm, regulates Qi, reduces abscesses, dissipates nodules and eliminates pus.
Bul. Fritillariae Thunbergii Zhe Bei Mu 10g Clears Heat, transforms Phlegm, stops coughing, dissipates nodules and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 3g Moistens the Lungs, resolves Phlegm, stops coughing, clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity, inhibits the growth of cancer cells, moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs and guides the herbs to all twelve channels.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Heat
  • Resolves toxicity
  • Cools the Blood
  • Regulates Qi
  • Inhibits the growth of cancer cells

SYNDROMES

  • Lung cancer due to Toxic Heat with Qi Stagnation

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Hemoptysis
  • Aversion to Heat
  • Cough
  • Asthma
  • Local redness, swelling and pain
  • Maybe fever and chills
  • T: Normal
  • C: Thin white or yellow
  • P: Rapid and forceful

TREATS

  • Lung cancer
