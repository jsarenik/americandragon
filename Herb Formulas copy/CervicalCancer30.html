HERB FORMULAS

CERVICAL CANCER #30

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Carapax Trionycis Bie Jia 15g Nourishes Yin, anchors Yang, invigorates the Blood, promotes menstruation, softens hardness, dissipates nodules and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Concha Ostreae Mu Li 15g Benefits Yin, anchors Floating Yang, softens hardness, dissipates nodules and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Sheng Di Huang and Bie Jia, for internal stirring of Wind from Deficiency.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Preparata Zhi Gan Cao 15g Tonifies the Spleen, augments Qi, alleviates pain, inhibits the growth of cancer cells, moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs and guides the herbs to all twelve channels.
Ram. Cinnamomi Gui Zhi 10g Warms the channels and collaterals to relieve pain and warms and facilitates the flow of Qi through the channels and collaterals and Blood through the vessels.
Rz. Zingiberis Recens Sheng Jiang 10g Warms the Middle, stops vomiting and reduces the toxicity of other herbs.
With Gui Zhi and Da Zao, harmonizes the Ying and Wei to drive out pathogens.
Rx. Ginseng Ren Shen 15g Tonifies Qi, generates Body Fluids, stops thirst and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Colla Corii Asini E Jiao 10g Tonifies and nourishes the Blood, stops bleeding and nourishes and moistens Yin.
Sm. Cannabis Huo Ma Ren 10g Nourishes, moistens and lubricates the Intestines, nourishes Yin, clears Heat and promotes healing of sores.
With Sheng Di Huang and Mai Men Dong, for Yin Deficiency with constipation.
Fr. Jujube Da Zao 30g Tonifies the Spleen and Stomach, augments Qi, nourishes the Blood, calms the Spirit and moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs.
With Sheng Jiang, nourishes the Blood, prevents injury from harsh Blood movers, assists the movement of Qi and enhances the effects of other Qi and Blood tonifying herbs by facilitating their digestion and absorption.
Rx. Ophiopogonis Mai Men Dong 15g Nourishes Yin and moistens the Intestines.
With Sheng Di Huang, for hematemesis or epistaxis due to Yin Deficiency with Excess Heat.
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 15g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, nourishes Yin, and generates fluids.
With E Jiao, for uterine hemorrhage due to Heat from Deficiency.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Nourishes the Liver and Kidneys
  • Nourishes the Blood
  • Cools the Blood
  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Breaks up Stasis
  • Softens hardness
  • Dissipates nodules
  • Nourishes Yin
  • Tonifies Qi

SYNDROMES

  • Kidney and Liver Yin Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Low back weakness and pain
  • Dizziness
  • Tinnitus
  • Five Sole Heat
  • Dreaminess
  • A bitter taste in the mouth
  • A dry throat
  • Dry stools
  • T: Red
  • C: Little or None
  • P: Thready and rapid

TREATS

  • Cervical cancer

HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

NOTES

  • Make into water pills. Take 9g per day.
