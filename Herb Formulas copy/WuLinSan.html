HERB FORMULAS

WU LIN SAN - 五淋散 - POWDER FOR FIVE TYPES OF PAINFUL URINARY DYSFUNCTION

English: Powder for Five Types of Painful Urinary Dysfunction
Also Known As: Powder for Five Types of Painful Urinary Dribbling
Gardenia and Hoelen Formula

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Scl. Poria Rubra Chi Fu Ling 9-18g Clears Dampness and Heat.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 7-15g Tonifies Blood, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood and disperses Cold (stops pain due to Blood Stasis).
With Gan Cao, moderates spasms and relieves abdominal pain.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 3-7g Clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity and moderates and harmonizes the properties of other herbs.
Rx. Paeoniae Rubra Chi Shao 9-18g Invigorates the Blood. dispels Blood Stasis, relieves pain, clears Heat and cools the Blood.
Fr. Gardeniae Zhi Zi 6-15g Clears Heat, reduces Fire, eliminates irritability, resolves Dampness (drains Damp-Heat), cools the Blood and relieves toxicity (stops bleeding by astringing).

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Heat
  • Cools the Blood
  • Promotes urination
  • Unblocks painful urinary dysfunction

SYNDROMES

  • Damp-Heat, bloody painful urinary dysfunction

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Rough, painful urination that is red with multiple tiny stones
  • Fatigue
  • Dark colored urine
  • Maybe acute lower abdominal pain
  • Maybe acute pain in the abdomen and umbilical area
  • Maybe hernia

TREATS

  • Urethritis
  • Cystitis
  • Urinary calculi
  • Postpartum urinary disorder
  • Hematuria
  • Acute prostatitis
  • Fatigue
  • Pyelonephritis
  • Dysuria during pregnancy
  • Gonorrhea
  • Appendicitis
  • Renal calculi
  • Prostatic hypertrophy
  • Oliguria
  • Acute glomerulonephritis
  • Edema during pregnancy
  • Hernia

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Use only for Excess type painful urinary dysfunction.
