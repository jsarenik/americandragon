HERB FORMULAS

TIAO CHONG REN YANG XUE TANG

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Rehmanniae Preparata Shu Di Huang 9-30g Nourishes Jing and Blood, fills the Marrow and nourishes Liver and Kidney Yin.
Sm. Cuscutae Tu Si Zi 6-18g Strengthens Yang, nourishes Yin, astringes Jing and urine and benefits the marrow Tonifies the Kidneys and Liver
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Preparata Zhi Gan Cao 6-14g Tonifies the Spleen and augments Qi.
Rz. Dioscoreae Shan Yao 9-30g Tonifies the Spleen, nourishes Stomach Yin, tonifies Kidney Yin and astringes Jing.
Stamen Nelumbinis Lian Xu 1.5-9g Stabilizes the Kidneys, astringes Jing, clears Heart Heat and stops bleeding.
Poria Fu Ling 9-18g Promotes urination, leaches out Dampness, strengthens the Spleen, quiets the Heart, calms the Spirit and soothes the nerves.
With Gan Cao, for Spleen and Heart Deficiency causing facial edema, palpitations and shortness of breath.
Hb. Taxilli Sang Ji Sheng 9-30g Tonifies Liver and Kidney Yin, strengthens the sinews and bones, expels Wind-Dampness, nourishes the Blood and calms the womb.
Fr. Lycii Gou Qi Zi 5-18g Nourishes and tonifies Liver and Kidney Blood and Yin and benefits Jing.
With Shu Di Huang, for Liver and Kidney Deficiency.
Sm. Platycladi Bai Zi Ren 3-18g Nourishes the Heart, calms the Spirit, moistens the Intestines, unblocks the bowels and astringes Yin Deficiency sweat.
Arillus Longan Long Yan Rou 6-30g Tonifies and augments the Heart and Spleen, nourishes the Blood and calms the Spirit.
Fr. Jujube Da Zao 10-30g Tonifies the Spleen and Stomach, augments Qi, nourishes the Blood and calms the Spirit.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Tonifies and Nourishes Ying, Yang Qi, Blood and Jing
  • Tonifies the Liver, Kidneys and Spleen
  • Nourishes the Heart
  • Calms the Shen

SYNDROMES

  • Liver and Kidney Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Facial complexion pale and sallow
  • Both cheeks slightly red
  • Dizziness
  • Dry eyes
  • Blurred vision
  • Spots before the eyes
  • Numbness of limbs
  • Flaccid paralysis (Wei syndrome), weakness
  • Tinnitus
  • Deafness
  • Forgetfulness
  • Low back and knees sore and painful or cold, weak and aching
  • Throat dry and painful
  • Five Sole Heat
  • Low-grade fever
  • Night sweats
  • Irritability
  • Insomnia
  • Stools difficult to pass
  • Constipation
  • Irregular menses or
  • Scanty menses or
  • Amenorrhea
  • Vaginal discharge
  • Feeling chilled
  • Aversion to Cold
  • Cold limbs
  • General debility
  • Pale face
  • Urinary frequency with abundant, clear urine or
  • Oliguria with edema
  • Maybe nocturia
  • Lassitude
  • Withdrawn, subdued behavior
  • Knees feel cold and aching
  • Low libido
  • Infertility ("uterus cold and infertile")
  • Maybe anorexia
  • Maybe loose stools
  • Maybe chronic diarrhea or "cock crow" diarrhea
  • T: Varies
  • C: Thin and white or None
  • P: Deep and thready - maybe slow or Thready and rapid

TREATS

  • Endocrine dyscrasia infertility
