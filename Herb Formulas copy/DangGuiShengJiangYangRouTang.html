HERB FORMULAS

DANG GUI SHENG JIANG YANG ROU TANG - 当归生姜羊肉湯- MUTTON STEW WITH DANG GUI AND FRESH GINGER

English: Mutton Stew with Dang Gui and Fresh Ginger
Also Known As: Mutton Stew with Tangkuei and Fresh Ginger
Decoction of Angelica Sinensis, Zingiberis Recens and Mutton

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Angelica Sinensis Dang Gui 9-30g Tonifies, Invigorates and harmonizes the Blood, disperses Cold, stops pain due to Blood Stasis and regulates the menses.
Rz. Zingiberis Recens Sheng Jiang 15-40g Releases the Exterior, induces perspiration, disperses Cold, warms the Middle, stops vomiting and adjusts the Ying and Wei, normalizing the flow of Qi at the center.
Mutton Yang Rou 48-80g Sweet, acrid and Warm, it strongly tonifies Ming Men Fire, nourishes the Blood and tonifies Qi.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Warms the Interior
  • Nourishes the Blood
  • Regulates the Liver function
  • Alleviates pain

SYNDROMES

  • Interior Deficiency with Cold Blood
  • Spleen Qi Deficiency with Blood Deficiency and Liver Cold

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Postpartum abdominal pain
  • Cold abdominal hernial pain
  • Hypochondriac pain
  • Spasmodic pain in the flanks
  • Sallow complexion
  • Fatigue
  • Dizziness
  • Tinnitus
  • The pain is better with warmth and pressure
  • Swollen, indurated scrotum
  • T: Pale
  • C: White
  • P: Deep, wiry and forceless or Deep, wiry and choppy or Soft and feeble

TREATS

  • Postpartum weakness
  • Hernia
  • Leukorrhea
  • Male infertility
  • Deficient Bi
  • Fatigue
  • Painful obstruction
  • Thrombocytopenic purpura
  • Dysmenorrhea
  • Abdominal pain
  • Lumbago
  • Tinnitus
  • Postpartum abdominal pain
