HERB FORMULAS

ZENG YE CHENG QI TANG - 增液承氣湯 - INCREASE THE FLUIDS AND ORDER THE QI DECOCTION

English: Increase the Fluids and Order the Qi Decoction
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Scrophulariae Xuan Shen 30g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, and nourishes Yin.
With Sheng Di Huang, for Excess or Deficiency Heat patterns.
With Sheng Di Huang and Mai Men Dong, for constipation due to Dryness and sore, dry throat due to Deficiency Fire.
Rx. Ophiopogonis Mai Men Dong 24g Moistens the Lungs, nourishes Yin, nourishes Stomach Yin, generates fluids and moistens the Intestines.
With Sheng Di Huang, for hematemesis or epistaxis due to Yin Deficiency with Excess Heat.
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 24g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, nourishes Yin and generates fluids.
With Da Huang, for Blazing Heart and Stomach Fire with epistaxis, and hemoptysis, Deficiency Fire and parched, dry stools with damaged Yin.
Rx. et Rz. Rhei  Da Huang 9-12g Drains Heat, purges accumulations, drains Fire and drains Heat from the Blood.
With Mai Men Dong, Xuan Shen and Sheng Di Huang, for constipation with Heat consuming Body Fluids.
Natrii Sulfas Mang Xiao 1.5-4.5g Purges accumulations, guides out Stagnation, softens hardness, moistens Dryness, clears Heat and drains Fire.
With Da Huang, for constipation due to Heat in the Stomach and Intestines.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Generates Fluids
  • Drains Heat
  • Unblocks the bowels
  • Enriches Yin

SYNDROMES

  • Yang Ming Xu with constipation
  • Yin Deficiency constipation which has not responded to Zeng Ye Tang

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Constipation
  • Dry stools that cannot easily be expelled, even with purgatives
  • Thirst
  • Distention and fullness of the epigastrium and abdomen
  • Dry mouth
  • Dry lips
  • T: Red, dry and thin
  • C: Little or None or thin and yellow or Burnt and yellow
  • P: Thready and slightly rapid or weak and forceless

TREATS

  • Habitual constipation
  • Hemorrhoids
  • Aphthous ulcers
  • Chronic pancreatitis
  • Sequelae of infectious diseases
  • Skin diseases
  • Anal fissures
  • Postpartum anuria
  • Gingivitis
  • Periodontitis
  • Glaucoma
  • Dizziness
  • Fever
  • Neck stiffness
  • Irritable bowel syndrome
  • Hyperthyroid conditions
  • Foundation of formulas to treat oral side effects of radiation
  • Toothache
  • Dry mucus membranes
  • Renal failure
  • Epistaxis
  • Intestinal tuberculosis
  • Hypertension
  • Headache
  • Kidney failure
  • Abdominal fullness and distention
  • Dry skin

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Use only for constipation due to Yin Deficiency combined with dry stool in the Intestines.
