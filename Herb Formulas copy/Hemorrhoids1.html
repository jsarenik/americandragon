HERB FORMULAS

HEMORRHOIDS #1

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Terra Flava Usta Zao Xin Tu 24g Warms the Blood, stops bleeding, warms and harmonizes the Stomach (Middle Jiao) and stops vomiting and diarrhea.
 With Bai Zhu, Zhi Fu Zi and Pao Jiang, for diarrhea and dysentery due to Spleen and Stomach Deficiency Cold.
Rx. Astragali Huang Qi 18g Tonifies Qi and Blood, strengthens the Spleen, raises the Yang Qi of the Spleen and Stomach, promotes the discharge of pus, generates flesh and expels toxins.
With Zhi Fu Zi, for Yang Deficiency with spontaneous sweat.
With Bai Zhu, for Spleen Qi Deficiency with weakness, lassitude and loose stools.
With Sheng Ma, raises the Yang Qi of the Spleen, allowing turbid Yin to descend and be eliminated, treating chronic diarrhea, a dragging sensation in the abdomen, fatigue, shortness of breath, uterine bleeding and prolapses.
Rz. Atractylodis Macrocephalae Bai Zhu 9g Tonifies the Spleen, augments Qi, dries Dampness and promotes water metabolism.
With Pao Jiang, for bleeding due to Deficiency of Spleen Yang with hemafecia or hematuria and frequent diarrhea due to Spleen and Stomach Deficiency Cold.
Rx. Aconiti Lateralis Preparata Zhi Fu Zi 9g Warms Ming Men Fire, assists Heart, Kidney and Spleen Yang, disperses Cold and Dampness, warms the channels and stops pain.
 With Bai Zhu, for Spleen Yang Deficiency with a Cold, painful abdomen, vomiting and watery diarrhea.
With Pao Jiang, warms the Spleen and Kidneys, benefits Yang and disperses Cold.
With Bai Zhu and Pao Jiang, for Internal ascendance of Yin Cold leading to lack of arousal of Spleen Yang with abdominal pain and Cold with watery diarrhea.
Rz. Cimicifugae Sheng Ma 9g Raises Yang and lifts sunken Qi.
Fr. Sophorae Huai Jiao 9g Cools the Blood, stops bleeding and lubricates the Intestines.
With Di Yu, for hot, bleeding hemorrhoids or other Intestinal bleeding.
Rx. Sanguisorbae Di Yu 9g Cools the Blood and stops bleeding.
Blast-fried Rz. Zingiberis Pao Jiang 6g Stops bleeding associated with Deficiency Cold.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Warms Yang
  • Strengthens the Spleen
  • Nourishes the Blood
  • Stops bleeding
  • Stops diarrhea

SYNDROMES

  • Spleen Yang Deficiency
  • Middle Jiao Deficiency Cold

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Sallow complexion
  • Anorexia (maybe with vomiting)
  • Four limbs feel tired and heavy
  • Abdominal and epigastric distention after eating
  • Maybe acid regurgitation or vomiting
  • Loose stools or watery stools containing undigested food
  • Pain in the epigastrium with cold sensations (pain is relieved by warmth)
  • Shortness of breath
  • Cold limbs
  • Feeling chilled
  • Frequent, clear urination
  • Mental fatigue
  • Maybe scanty urine with edema
  • Maybe clear, watery vaginal discharge
  • T: Pale
  • C: Thin and white
  • P: Deep and weak

TREATS

  • Hemorrhoids with enduring diarrhea
