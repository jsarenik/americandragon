HERB FORMULAS

HUANG TU TANG - 黃土湯 - YELLOW EARTH DECOCTION

English: Yellow Earth Decoction
Also Known As: Decoction of Ignited Yellow Earth
Decoction of Baked Yellow Earth
Decoction of Terra Flava Usta
Fu Long Gan (Fu Lung Kan) Combination

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Terra Flava Usta Zao Xin Tu 18-50g Warms the Blood, stops bleeding, warms and harmonizes the Stomach (Middle Jiao) and stops vomiting and diarrhea.
 With E Jiao and Huang Qin, for hematochezia with Cold in the Lower Jiao.
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 9-12g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, Nourishes Yin and generates fluids.
With E Jiao, for coughing, hematemesis, epistaxis, and uterine hemorrhage due to Heat from Deficiency.
Colla Corii Asini E Jiao 9-12g Nourishes the Blood, stops bleeding and nourishes and moistens Yin.
Rz. Atractylodis Macrocephalae Bai Zhu 6-9g Tonifies the Spleen, augments Qi, dries Dampness and promotes water metabolism.
Rx. Aconiti Lateralis Preparata Zhi Fu Zi 3-9g Warms Ming Men Fire, assists Heart, Kidney and Spleen Yang, disperses Cold and Dampness, warms the channels and stops pain.
 With Bai Zhu, for Spleen Yang Deficiency with a Cold, painful abdomen, vomiting and watery diarrhea.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 6-9g Tonifies the Spleen, augments Qi and moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs.
Baked Rx. Aconiti Lateralis Bao Fu Zi 6-9g Warms the Spleen and Kidneys, for Cold pain in the epigastrium and abdomen, nausea and diarrhea due to Deficiency Cold.
Rx. Scutellariae Huang Qin 6-9g Clears Heat, dries Dampness, cools the Blood and stops bleeding.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Warms Yang
  • Strengthens the Spleen
  • Nourishes the Blood
  • Stops bleeding

SYNDROMES

  • Spleen Yang Deficiency
  • Middle Jiao Deficiency Cold

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Hematochezia
  • Hematemesis
  • Hemoptysis
  • Epistaxis
  • Metrorrhagia
  • Cold extremities
  • A wan or sallow complexion
  • Dark red or pale Blood
  • General fatigue
  • Hematuria
  • A lack of taste
  • No thirst
  • Pale face
  • T: Pale or Dull red
  • C: White
  • P: Deep, thready and weak

TREATS

  • Chronic hemorrhagic gastritis
  • Peptic ulcer
  • Metrorrhagia
  • Hemoptysis
  • Epigastric pain
  • Chronic pancreatitis
  • Esophageal varicies
  • Hemafecia
  • Fatigue
  • Thrombocytopenic purpura
  • Ulcerative colitis
  • Duodenal ulcer
  • Hemorrhoidal bleeding
  • Epistaxis
  • Stomach cancer
  • Chronic gastritis
  • Hematuria
  • Crohn's disease
  • Colitis
  • Hematemesis
  • Spontaneous perspiration
  • Cirrhosis of the Liver

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with bleeding due to Excess Heat
  • Contraindicated when External pathogens are present.
