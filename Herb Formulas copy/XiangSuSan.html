HERB FORMULAS

XIANG SU SAN - 杏苏散 - CYPERUS AND PERILLA LEAF POWDER

English: Cyperus and Perilla Leaf Powder
Also Known As:  

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Fol. Perillae Zi Su Ye 2-9g Releases the Exterior , disperses Cold, promotes the movement of Spleen and Stomach Qi, harmonizes the Middle Jiao, relieves intestinal gas, alleviates seafood poisoning, improves Lung function, expands the chest and relieves Cold induced wheezing.
With Xiang Fu, releases the Exterior in a patient with pre-existing Qi Stagnation with chills, fever, headache, anhidrosis, a stuffy chest, belching and flatulence.
With Chen Pi, for Wind-Cold with Qi Stagnation and chest congestion.
Dry-fried Rz. Cyperi Chao Xiang Fu 2-9g Regulates pain due to Qi Stagnation.
With Zi Su Ye, for nasal congestion, epigastric and abdominal distention and discomfort associated with externally contracted disorders.
Per. Citri Reticulatae Chen Pi 1-6g Regulates Qi, adjusts the Middle, relieves the diaphragm, dries Dampness, transforms Phlegm and descends Qi.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Preparata Zhi Gan Cao 1-6g Moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Regulates Qi
  • Releases the Exterior

SYNDROMES

  • Wind-Cold with Qi Stagnation in the Interior

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Aversion to Cold
  • Fever
  • Headache
  • Anhidrosis
  • Anorexia
  • Belching
  • Aching shoulders
  • Tinnitus
  • Amenorrhea
  • A gasping cough
  • Focal distention in the chest and epigastrium
  • A stifling sensation in the chest and epigastrium
  • Vertigo
  • Nausea
  • T: Normal
  • C: Thin and white
  • P: Floating or Floating and sinking
