HERB FORMULAS

WU ZHI AN ZHONG YIN - 五汁安中饮 - FIVE JUICE DRINK TO CALM THE MIDDLE

English: Five Juice Drink to Calm the Middle
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Chinese Chive Juice Jiu Cai Zhi 20ml Warms the Stomach and treats chronic Stomach Qi Reversal.
Cow's Milk Niu Ru 2 cups Moistens the Lungs and Stomach.
Ginger Juice Sheng Jiang Zhi 6ml Alleviates nausea, breaks up Phlegm and reduces coughing.
Pear Juice Li Zhi 20ml Moistens the Lungs and Stomach, cools and helps dissolve Phlegm.
Lotus Juice Ou Zhi 20ml Eliminates Heat from the Blood, for Lung and Stomach Heat.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Nourishes and moistens Yin and Fluids
  • Clears Heat
  • Disperses accumulations

SYNDROMES

  • Yin and Fluid Deficiency of the Throat and Esophagus due to Heat

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Difficulty swallowing maybe with a burning of painful sensation
  • Food is difficult to swallow but Fluids are easy
  • After eating, the bulk of the ingested food may be vomited up, possibly mixed with mucus
  • Gradual weight loss
  • Dryness oft the mouth and throat
  • Dry skin
  • Thirst
  • Pain in the chest and upper back
  • An uncomfortable burning sensation in the epigastrium
  • A hoarse or croaky voice
  • Five-Sole-Heat
  • Low-grade afternoon fever
  • Nightsweats
  • Facial flushing
  • A tendency toward constipation
  • T: Red and dry with multiple surface cracks
  • C: Maybe yellow
  • P: Wiry, thready and rapid

TREATS

  • Dysphagia
  • Reflux esophagitis
  • Esophageal cancer
