HERB FORMULAS

JIE JING ZHI TONG SAN - 解痉止痛散 - RESOLVE SPASMS AND STOP PAIN POWDER

English: Resolve Spasms and Stop Pain Powder
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rz. Zingiberis Gan Jiang 60g Dispels Wind-Dampness seeping into the Lower Jiao and warms the channels.
Rx. Aconiti Lateralis Preparata Zhi Fu Zi 60g Disperses Cold and Dampness, warms the channels and stops pain.
With Gan Jiang, for Cold-Damp Bi.
Scolopendra Wu Gong 45g Extinguishes Wind, stops convulsions and spasms, unblocks the collaterals and stops pain.
With Quan Xie, for Wind-Damp Bi pain.
Scorpio Quan Xie 45g Extinguishes Wind, stops tremors and convulsions, unblocks the collaterals and stops pain.
Eupolyphaga/Steleophaga Tu Bie Chong 45g Breaks up and drives out Blood Stasis and invigorates Blood circulation.
Hirudo Shui Zhi 45 Breaks up and drives out Blood Stasis.
Succinum Hu Po 45g Invigorates the Blood, dissipates Stasis.
Cornu Cervi Pantotrichum Lu Jiao 45g Invigorates Blood, disperses Stagnation and reduces swelling and pain from Blood Stagnation.
Flos Daturae Yang Jin Hua 20g Dispels Wind-Damp, relieves spasms and stops pain.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Warms Yang
  • Scatters Cold
  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Transforms Stasis
  • Unblocks the channels
  • Stops pain

SYNDROMES

  • Cold and Blood Stagnation in the channels

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Extremities are cyanotic and difficult to warm up
  • Abdominal pain with icy cold hands and feet
  • P: Thready or Imperceptible

TREATS

  • Raynaud's disease
