HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Poria Fu Ling 9-18g Promotes urination, leaches out Dampness and strengthens the Spleen.
Fol. Photiniae Shi Nan Ye 6-12g Dispels Wind, dredges the channels and tonifies the Kidneys.
Rz. Cibotii Gou Ji 4.5-15g Tonifies the Liver and Kidneys, warms and stabilizes the Kidneys and prevents leakage.
With Lu Jiao Shuang, for Deficiency Cold of the Chong and Ren channels causing vaginal discharge.
Sm. Trigonellae Hu Lu Ba 3-10g Warms the Kidneys, disperses Dampness and alleviates pain.
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 5-30g Clears Heat, cools the Blood and nourishes Yin.
With Shu Di Huang, for Heat symptoms due to Yin Deficiency and Devastated Blood.
Rx. Rehmanniae Preparata Shu Di Huang 9-30g Nourishes Liver and Kidney Yin, nourishes Jing and Blood and fills the Marrow.
Fluoritum Zi Shi Ying 9-16g Warms the Womb and warms and unblocks the Chong and Ren channels.
Hb. Epimedii Yin Yang Huo 3-15g Tonifies the Kidneys, strengthens Yang and increases the libido.
With Xian Mao, for menopausal hypertension.
Hb. Cistanches Rou Cong Rong 6-21g Tonifies the Kidneys, strengthens Yang,
benefits Jing and marrow and warms the Womb.
With Yin Yang Huo, for decreased sexual desire.
Fr. Ligustri Lucidi Nu Zhen Zi 4.5-18g Nourishes and tonifies Liver and Kidney Yin.
Hb. Curculiginis Xian Mao 3-10g Tonifies the Kidneys and strengthens Yang.
Cornu Cervi Degelatinatum Lu Jiao Shuang 3-5g Tonifies Jing and Blood for Cold, Deficient uterine bleeding and vaginal discharge.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Nourishes and tonifies the Liver and Kidneys
  • Warms Jing
  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Transforms Stasis

SYNDROMES

  • Kidney Jing Deficiency with Liver and Kidney Yin Deficiency and Blood Stagnation

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Dizziness
  • Tinnitus
  • Low back and knees weak and aching
  • Hair loss
  • Graying or whitening of the hair
  • Weak sexual function
  • Atrophy and weakness of the feet with difficulty walking
  • Poor memory
  • Senility (premature or mature)
  • Brittle bones
  • Gradual deterioration of the hearing
  • Pain that is fixed, stabbing and /or severe
  • Pain is worse with pressure
  • Dark complexion
  • Fixed, palpable masses
  • Bleeding with clots
  • Purple lips
  • Purple nails
  • Dry skin
  • Blue, dark or purplish discoloration of skin in small or large areas
  • Varicose and spider veins
  • Cold hands and feet
  • Tendency to hemorrhage dark blood or clots
  • Facial complexion pale and sallow
  • Both cheeks slightly red
  • Dry eyes
  • Blurred vision
  • Spots before the eyes
  • Numbness of limbs
  • Flaccid paralysis (Wei syndrome), weakness
  • Deafness
  • Forgetfulness
  • Low back and knees sore and painful
  • Throat dry and painful
  • Five Sole Heat
  • Low-grade fever
  • Night sweats
  • Irritability
  • Insomnia
  • Stools difficult to pass
  • Constipation
  • Irregular menses or
  • Scanty menses or
  • Amenorrhea
  • Vaginal discharge
  • T: Red or Purple, possible purple or red spots
  • C: White or None
  • P: Thready and rapid or Choppy or Wiry and choppy

TREATS

  • Endocrine dyscrasia infertility
