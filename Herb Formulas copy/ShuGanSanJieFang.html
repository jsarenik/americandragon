HERB FORMULAS

SHU GAN SAN JIE FANG - 舒肝散结方 - DREDGE THE LIVER AND SCATTER NODULATION FORMULA

English: Dredge the Liver and Scatter Nodulation Formula
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Salviae Miltiorrhizae Dan Shen 15g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, cools the Blood and reduces abscesses.
Spica Prunellae Xia Ku Cao 15g Clears Hot Phlegm and dissipates nodules.
With Xuan Shen, Kun Bu and Mu Li, for nodules due to Phlegm Fire.
with Chai Hu, for neck nodules due to Liver Qi Stagnation.
With Zhe Bei Mu, cools and relieves toxicity to disperse and transform Phlegm and treat goiter, scrofula and swollen or inflamed lymph glands.
With Xiang Fu and Chai Hu, for Liver Qi Stagnation.
Rx. Scrophulariae Xuan Shen 15g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, nourishes Yin, softens hardness and dissipates nodules.
With Mu Li and Zhe Bei Mu, for scrofula, goiter, and other nodules due to Phlegm.
Concha Ostreae Mu Li 12g Calms the Liver, benefits Yin, anchors Floating Yang, softens hardness and dissipates nodules.
With Chai Hu, Dan Shen and chi Shao, for sub-costal pain and swelling.
Pumex Fu Shi 12g Softens hardness and dissipates Phlegm nodules.
With Mu Li, Hai Zao, Kun Bu and Zhe Bei Mu, for scrofula and goiter from Phlegm-Heat.
Rx. Bupleuri Chai Hu 9g Spreads Liver Qi, relieves Stagnation, disperses Wind-Heat and resolves Phlegm and congestion.
Rx. Paeoniae Rubra Chi Shao 9g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, relieves pain and reduces swelling from sores and abscesses.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 9g Tonifies, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood, reduces swelling, expels pus, generates flesh and alleviates pain.
Rx. Achyranthis Bidentatae Niu Xi 9g Invigorates the Blood, expels Blood Stasis and nourishes Liver and Kidney Yin.
Thallus Eckloniae Kun Bu 9g Resolves Phlegm and softens Phlegm nodules.
With Hai Zao, for goiter and scrofula.
With Hai Zao, Xia Ku Zao, Xuan Shen and Zhe Bei Mu, for scrofula, goiter and other nodules.
Sargassum Hai Zao 9g Reduces Phlegm and softens areas of hardness.
Bul. Fritillariae Thunbergii Zhe Bei Mu 9g Clears Heat, transforms Phlegm and dissipates nodules.
With Xuan Shen, Mu Li and Xia Ku Cao, for scrofula due to Phlegm-Fire with swelling and pain at multiple sites.
With Xuan Shen and Mu Li, for nodules without abscess.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Dredges the Liver
  • Regulates Qi
  • Transforms Phlegm
  • Scatters nodulation

SYNDROMES

  • Liver Stagnation with Phlegm Nodulation

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Urinary strangury and obstruction which is worse with emotional upset or stress
  • Lower abdominal distention
  • Irritability
  • A quick temper
  • Insomnia
  • Maybe flatulence
  • T: Normal or Slightly dark
  • C: White and slimy
  • P: wiry and slippery

TREATS

  • Benign prostatic hypertrophy
  • Strangury
