HERB FORMULAS

PI PA QING FEI YIN - 枇杷清肺饮 - ERIOBOTRYA DECOCTION TO CLEAR THE LUNG

English: Eriobotrya Decoction to Clear the Lung
Also Known As: Loquat Decoction to Clear the Lung
Decoction of Folium Eriobotrya for Removing Heat from the Lung

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Fol. Eriobotrya Pi Pa Ye 6-15g Transforms Phlegm, clears Lung Heat and descends Lung Qi.
With Huang Lian, Zhi Zi and Sang Bai Pi, for cough with copious yellow Phlegm due to Lung Heat.
Cx. Mori Sang Bai Pi 6-9g Drains Heat from the Lungs, stops cough and calms wheezing.
Rz. Coptidis Huang Lian 3-9g Clears Heat, drains Dampness, drains Fire, resolves Fire toxicity, clears Heat and stops bleeding.
Cx. Phellodendri Huang Bai 3g Drains Damp-Heat especially from the Lower Jiao, drains Fire and relieves Fire toxicity.
Rx. Ginseng Ren Shen 1g Tonifies Qi, generates Body Fluids and stops thirst.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 1g Moistens the Lungs, resolves Phlegm, stops cough, clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity and moderates and harmonizes the harsh properties of other herbs.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Cool the Blood
  • Eliminate Dampness
  • Invigorate the Blood
  • Clear Lung Heat

SYNDROMES

  • Damp-Heat in the Lungs
  • Lung Heat
  • Blood Heat

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Redness of the bulb of the nose
  • The redness blanches upon pressure
  • Red papules, tubercles and acne and inflammatory infiltration around them
  • Whiteheads or blackheads
  • Dry nose
  • Dry mouth
  • Burning sensation
  • Constipation
  • Dry stool
  • T: Red or Red tip
  • C: Thin and yellow
  • P: Wiry and slippery or Slippery and rapid

TREATS

  • Acne
  • Rosacea
