HERB FORMULAS

QIANG ZHENG SAN - 牽正散 - LEAD TO SYMMETRY POWDER

English: Lead to Symmetry Powder
Also Known As: Restoring to the Normal Position Powder

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rz. Typhonii Preparatum Zhi Bai Fu Zi 12-16g Dries Dampness, transforms Phlegm, expels Wind and stops spasms.
With Jiang Can and Quan Xie, for facial paralysis and hemiplegia due to Wind-Stroke and Phlegm Obstruction of the channels and post-stroke deviation of the eyes and mouth.
Bombyx Batryticatus Jiang Can 6-12g Extinguishes Wind, transforms Phlegm and stops spasms and convulsions.
Scorpio Quan Xie 3-6g Extinguishes Wind, stops tremors and convulsions, unblocks the collaterals and stops pain.
With Jiang Can, for Wind-Dampness.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Dispels Wind
  • Resolves Phlegm
  • Stops spasms

SYNDROMES

  • Wind-Stroke
  • Wind-Phlegm Obstructing the channels of the head and face

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Deviation of the eyes and mouth
  • Paralysis of the skin
  • Continuous, uncontrolled salivation
  • Fever
  • Sore, painful joints
  • Facial paralysis
  • Difficulty speaking
  • Intolerance of Cold
  • Spasms of the limbs
  • T: Normal
  • C: Thin and white
  • P: Wiry or Taut and thready or Floating and rapid

TREATS

  • Facial paralysis
  • Trigeminal neuralgia
  • Facial spasms
  • Bell's palsy
  • Tourette syndrome
  • Numbness
  • Vascular headache
  • Facial pain
  • Hemiplegia
  • Migraine headache
  • Cerebrovascular accident
  • Peripheral neuritis
  • Parkinson's disease
  • Paresthesia
  • Aphasia

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • This formula should only be used for Wind-Stroke due to Wind-Cold and Phlegm.
  • Contraindicated unless modified for deviation of the mouth or hemiplegia due to Internal Liver Wind Rising.
  • Contraindicated unless modified for deviation of the mouth or hemiplegia due to Qi Deficiency and Blood Stagnation.
  • Bai Fu Zi and Wu Gong are toxic. Do not exceed a dosage of 6gt.
  • Contraindicated during pregnancy.
