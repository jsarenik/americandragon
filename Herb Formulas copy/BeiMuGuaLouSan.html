HERB FORMULAS

BEI MU GUA LOU SAN - 貝母瓜蔞散 - FRITILLARIA AND TRICHOSANTHIS FRUIT POWDER

English: Fritillaria and Trichosanthis Fruit Powder
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Bul. Fritillariae Cirrhosae Chuan Bei Mu 4.5-12g Moistens the Lungs, clears Heat, transforms Phlegm and stops cough.
With Gua Lou Ren and Tian Hua Fen, for cough with Phlegm and Dryness in the Lungs.
Fr. Trichosanthis Gua Lou Ren 3-12g Clears Heat, moistens Dryness, regulates Qi and expels Phlegm to remove obstruction from the chest and diaphragm.
Rx. Trichosanthis Tian Hua Fen 2-12g Clears and drains Lung Heat, transforms Phlegm, moistens Lung Dryness, generates fluids and relieves toxicity.
With Fu Ling and Ju Hong, treats Spleen Deficiency and Stagnation.
Poria Fu Ling 2-10g Promotes urination. resolves Dampness, strengthens the Spleen, harmonizes the Middle Jiao and transforms Phlegm.
Per. Citri Erythrocarpi Ju Hong 2-6g Regulates Qi, dries Dampness, transforms Phlegm, descends Qi, harmonizes the Middle and regulates the Stomach.
(Per. Citri Reticulatae) (Chen Pi) (2-6g) (Substitute for Ju Hong.)
Rx. Platycodi Jie Geng 2-9g Opens the Lungs, expels Phlegm and pus, benefits the throat and directs the effects of other herbs to the upper body.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Moistens the Lungs
  • Clears Heat
  • Regulates Qi
  • Transforms Phlegm
  • Stops cough

SYNDROMES

  • Phlegm-Heat and Dryness in the Lung
  • Phlegm-Dryness in the Lungs
  • Lung Yin Deficiency with Phlegm-Fire

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Cough
  • Dry, sore throat
  • Wheezing
  • Dyspnea
  • Shortness of breath
  • Polydipsia
  • Deep- seated Phlegm which is difficult to expectorate
  • Polyuria
  • T: Red, dry
  • C: Little
  • P: Thin, rapid and strong

TREATS

  • Sore throat
  • Bronchitis
  • Tracheitis
  • Aphonia
  • Emphysema
  • Nasal Dryness
  • Pneumonia
  • Chronic tonsillitis
  • COPD
  • Dyspnea
  • Scrofula
  • Asthma
  • Pulmonary tuberculosis
  • Pharyngitis
  • Cervical lymphadenopathy
  • Cervical lymphadenitis
  • Thyroiditis
  • Upper diabetes mellitus (initial stage)
  • Dry cough
  • Pertussis

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with cough due to Yin Deficiency.
