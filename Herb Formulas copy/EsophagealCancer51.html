HERB FORMULAS

ESOPHAGEAL CANCER #51

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rz. Pinelliae Preparatum Zhi Ban Xia 12g Dries Dampness, transforms Phlegm; descends Rebellious Qi; stops vomiting, Dissipates nodules; reduces Stagnation; treats sores, skin ulcerations and carbuncles and reduces swelling and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Hou Po, for Plum-pit Qi Stagnation.
Caulis Bambusae in Taeniam Zhu Ru 12g Clears Heat, transforms Hot Phlegm and stops vomiting.
Flos Inulae Xuan Fu Hua 12g Resolves Phlegm Stagnation in the Lungs and calms rebellious Stomach Qi.
With Zhi Ban Xia, for vomiting, cough, wheezing and epigastric distention due to congested fluids.
Sm. Impatientis Ji Xing Zi 12g Disperses Blood Stagnation, regulates Qi, softens hardness, disperses tumors, removes toxins and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Cynanchi Paniculati Xu Chang Qing 12g Removes Wind and Dampness, invigorates the Blood, stops pain, resolves toxins and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Concha Ostreae Mu Li 12g Calms the Liver, benefits Yin, anchors Floating Yang, softens hardness, dissipates nodules and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Haematitum Dai Zhe Shi 15g Calms the Liver, anchors Floating Yang, clears Liver Fire and strongly descends rebellious Qi.
Rx. Adenophorae Nan Sha Shen 15g Nourishes Yin, clears Heat , moistens the Lungs, dispels Phlegm, generates fluids and stops cough.
Rx. Isatidis Ban Lan Gen 15g Drains Heat, relieves Fire toxicity, cools the Blood and benefits the throat.
With Shan Dou Gen, for severe pain, swelling and redness of the throat, teeth and gums due to Internal accumulation of intense Heat.
Sargassum Hai Zao 15g Reduces Phlegm and softens areas of hardness and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Asparagi Tian Men Dong 9g Nourishes Kidney Yin, clears Lung Heat, sedates Fire, generates fluids, resolves Phlegm and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Hb. Dendrobii Shi Hu 9g Nourishes Yin, clears Heat and generates fluids.
With Zhu Ru, harmonizes the Stomach by descending Qi and replenishing Yin Fluids to treat hunger with no desire to eat with recurrent nausea, vomiting or dry heaves, thirst and dry mouth due to Stomach Yin Deficiency.
Rx. Paeoniae Alba Bai Shao 9g Nourishes the Blood, astringes Yin, softens the Liver and relieves pain.
Rx. Sophorae Subprostratae Shan Dou Gen 9g Clears Heat, relieves Fire toxicity, disperses swellings, improves the throat and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Ban Lan Gen, for severe pain and swelling of the throat, teeth and gums.
Catharsius Qiang Lang 6g Relieves constipation, attacks toxins, disperses Blood Stagnation and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Aucklandiae Mu Xiang 6g Promotes the movement of Qi and alleviates pain.
Flos Caryophylli Ding Xiang 6g Warms the Middle Jiao, relieves pain, descends Qi, warms the Kidneys and assists Yang.
Fr. Toosendan Chuan Lian Zi 9g Dredges Liver Qi, releases Stagnation, stops pain and conducts Heat out from below.
Cx. Magnoliae Officinalis Hou Po 9g Promotes the movement of Qi in the Middle Jiao, resolves Stagnation, descends Rebellious Qi and reduces Phlegm.
Lignum Aquilariae Chen Xiang 10g Promotes movement of Qi, descends Qi, alleviates pain and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Mu Xiang, relieves pain.
Spica Prunellae Xia Ku Cao 10g Clears Liver Fire, clears Hot Phlegm, dissipates nodules and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Heat
  • Resolves Phlegm
  • Regulates and descends Qi
  • Dissipates Nodules
  • Softens hardness
  • Inhibits the growth of cancer cells

SYNDROMES

  • Phlegm-Heat

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Progressive dysphagia
  • Vomiting after eating
  • Cough with sticky, yellow, difficult to expectorate sputum
  • Rapid breathing with a harsh voice
  • Restless fever
  • Sore throat
  • Constipation
  • Dry stools
  • Mania
  • Shortness of breath
  • Dyspnea
  • Nausea
  • Chest and/or epigastric fullness
  • Pain in the ribs
  • T: Scarlet
  • C: Yellow or Yellow and greasy or Yellow and smooth
  • P: Rapid and smooth or Slippery and rapid

TREATS

  • Lymphatic metastasis of esophageal cancer
