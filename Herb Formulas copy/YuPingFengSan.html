HERB FORMULAS

YU PING FENG SAN - 玉屏風散 - JADE WINDSCREEN POWDER

English: Jade Windscreen Powder
Also Known As: The Jade Screen Powder

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
(Honey-prepared) Rx. Astragali (Mi Zhi) Huang Qi 10-120g Strengthens the Spleen, raises the Yang Qi of the Spleen and Stomach, tonifies Wei Qi, stabilizes the exterior and tonifies the Lungs (aids circulation of moisture downward from the face).
With Fang Feng, stabilizes the exterior without causing the pathogenic influences to remain.
Rz. Atractylodis Macrocephalae Bai Zhu 9-60g Strengthens the Spleen, tonifies Qi. stabilizes the exterior and stops sweating.
With Huang Qi, for Spleen Qi Deficiency with weakness, lassitude and loose stools.
With Huang Qi and Fang Feng, for spontaneous sweat with aversion to Wind .
Rx. Saposhnikoviae Fang Feng 9-60g Releases the exterior and expels External Wind.
With Huang Qi, prevents Wind from entering the skin.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Tonifies Qi
  • Stabilizes the exterior
  • Stops sweat

SYNDROMES

  • Spontaneous sweating due to Qi Deficiency
  • Spontaneous sweating due to Wei Qi Deficiency
  • Lung Qi Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Aversion to Wind and Cold
  • Spontaneous sweat
  • Recurrent colds
  • Shiny, pale complexion
  • Increased susceptibility to external invasion
  • T: Pale or pink
  • C: White
  • P: Floating, Deficient and soft

TREATS

  • Common cold
  • Influenza
  • Chronic rhinitis
  • Allergic rhinitis
  • Chronic sinusitis
  • Upper respiratory tract infection
  • Exacerbation of chronic bronchitis
  • Mild bronchitis (especially in children)
  • Hyperthyroidism
  • Bronchitis
  • Nephrotic syndrome
  • Cough
  • Cor Pulmonare
  • Autonomic dystonia
  • AIDS/HIV
  • Fatigue
  • Spontaneous perspiration
  • Postpartum convulsions
  • AIDS/HIV/ARC
  • Prevents colds and respiratory tract infection
  • Chronic glomerulonephritis
  • Urticaria
  • Bronchial asthma
  • Abnormal perspiration
  • Facial paralysis
  • Neurodermatitis
  • Pneumonia
  • Herpes zoster
  • Hay fever
  • Chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD)
  • Emphysema
  • Chronic fatigue syndrome
  • Nephritis
  • Dyspnea
  • Upper back pain
  • Spontaneous sweating and night sweats associated with AIDS/HIV

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with Excess patterns.
  • Contraindicated for those with night sweats due to Yin Deficiency.
