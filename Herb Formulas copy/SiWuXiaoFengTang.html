HERB FORMULAS

SI WU XIAO FENG TANG - 四物消風汤 - ELIMINATE WIND WITH THE FOUR SUBSTANCES DECOCTION

English: Eliminate Wind with the Four Substances Decoction
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 5-30g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, nourishes Yin and generates fluids.
With Shu Di Huang, for Heat symptoms due to Yin Deficiency and Devastated Blood.
Rx. Rehmanniae Preparata Shu Di Huang 9-30g Nourishes the Blood and nourishes Liver and Kidney Yin.
With Dang Gui, for Blood Deficiency marked by dizziness, palpitations, insomnia and menstrual dysfunction.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 3-15g Tonifies, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood, reduces swelling, expels pus, generates flesh and alleviates pain.
With Chuan Xiong, harmonizes, nourishes and invigorates the Blood and disperses Blood Stasis.
With Shu Di Huang, for Heat symptoms due to Yin Deficiency and Devastated Blood.
Rz. Chuanxiong Chuan Xiong 3-10g Invigorates the Blood, promotes the movement of Qi, expels Wind and alleviates pain.
Hb. Taxilli Sang Ji Sheng 9-20g Tonifies Liver and Kidney Yin, strengthens the sinews and bones, expels Wind-Dampness, nourishes the Blood and benefits the skin.
Rx. Paeoniae Rubra Chi Shao 4.5-15g Invigorates the Blood, clears Heat, cools the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis and reduces swelling from sores.
With Chuan Xiong and Dang Gui, for abscesses, boils, and trauma induced pain.
Rx. Salviae Miltiorrhizae Dan Shen 3-15g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, clears Heat, soothes irritability, cools the Blood and reduces abscesses.
Hb. Schizonepetae Jing Jie 3-10g Releases the exterior, expels Wind, vents rashes and relieves itching.
With Fang Feng, for pruritic itching.
With Dang Gui and Chuan Xiong , for itching from Wind due to Blood Deficiency.
Rx. Saposhnikoviae Fang Feng 3-10g Releases the exterior and expels External Wind and Wind-Dampness.
Cx. Dictamni Bai Xian Pi 4-16g Clears Heat, resolves Fire-toxicity, expels Wind, dries Dampness, clears Damp-Heat and stops itching.
With Ku Shen, for Damp-Heat sores that itch.
With Ji Xue Teng, for abdominal pain due to Blood Stasis.
Rx. Sophorae Flavescentis Ku Shen 3-15g Clears Heat, dries Dampness, disperses Wind, kills parasites and stops itching.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Nourish the Blood
  • Eliminate Wind
  • Clear Heat
  • Dispel Damp

SYNDROMES

  • Wind-Damp-Heat with Blood Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Thickened skin with lichenoid changes
  • Localized skin eruptions with clear borders
  • Small amount of desquamation
  • Itching
  • Erosion

TREATS

  • Chronic eczema
  • Lip pain
  • Psoriasis
