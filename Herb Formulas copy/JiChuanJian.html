HERB FORMULAS

JI CHUAN JIAN - 济川煎 - BENEFIT THE RIVER DECOCTION

English: Benefit the River Decoction
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Wine-prepared Hb. Cistanches* Jiu Cong Rong 6-30g Tonifies the Kidneys, strengthens the Yang,
benefits Jing, moistens the Intestines and facilitates the passage of stool.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 9-15g Tonifies, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood, disperses Cold, moistens the Intestines and unblocks the bowels.
With Jiu Cong Rong, for constipation due to Dryness of the Intestines.
With Jiu Cong Rong and Niu Xi, for chronic constipation due to Qi and Blood Deficiency.
Rx. Achyranthis Bidentatae Niu Xi 6-12g Nourishes Liver and Kidney Yin, invigorates the Blood and expels Blood Stasis.
Rz. Alismatis Ze Xie 2-9g Promotes urination and leaches out Dampness
Fr. Aurantii Zhi Ke 3-9g Promotes the movement of Qi and reduces distention and pressure.
With Sheng Ma, for Relaxes the bowels and encourages the downward flow of turbid Yin.
Rz. Cimicifugae Sheng Ma 1.5-6g Raises Yang and lifts sunken Qi.
With Dang Gui, raises the clear Qi, allowing turbidity to descend and moistening the Intestines.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Warms the Kidneys
  • Nourishes the Blood
  • Strengthens Jing
  • Moistens the Intestines
  • Unblocks the bowels

SYNDROMES

  • Constipation due to Kidney Yang Deficiency
  • Constipation during convalescence

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Constipation
  • Clear, copious urine
  • Maybe scanty urination with pitting edema
  • A Cold sensation in the back
  • Intolerance of Cold
  • Dizziness
  • Vertigo
  • Soreness and weakness of the low back and knees
  • Cold extremities
  • T: Pale
  • C: Thin and white
  • P: Thready

TREATS

  • Constipation
  • Constipation in the elderly
  • Oliguria
  • Dizziness
  • Intestinal tuberculosis
  • Habitual constipation
  • Polyuria
  • Postpartum constipation
  • Vertigo
  • Anemia

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with constipation due to Heat.
HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

NOTES

  • * Wine-prepared Cistanche Jiu Cong Rong is better able to tonify the Kidney Yang but less moistening for the Intestines.
