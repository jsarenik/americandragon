HERB FORMULAS

QING YING TANG - 清營湯 - CLEAR THE NUTRITIVE LEVEL DECOCTION

English: Clear the Nutritive Level Decoction
Also Known As: Decoction to Dispel Pathogenic Heat from the Ying System

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Cornu Rhinoceri Xi Jiao 2-9g Clears Heat from the Ying, clears the Heart, calms the Spirit, cools Blood and breaks up Stasis.
 With Sheng Di Huang for Blood Heat.
(Cornu Bubali) (Shui Niu Jiao) (15-120g) (Substitute for Xi Jiao.)
Rx. Scrophulariae Xuan Shen 3-30g Clears Heat cools the Blood, nourishes Yin, drains Fire and relieves toxicity.
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 5-30g Cools the Blood and nourishes Yin.
Tb. Ophiopogonis Mai Men Dong 3-30g Nourishes Yin generates Fluids, clears the Heart and eliminates irritability.
Flos Lonicerae Jin Yin Hua 3-45g Clears Heat, relieves toxicity and cools the Blood.
 With Lian Qiao, Huang Lian, and Dan Zhu Ye, clears Qi Stage Heat and detoxifies.
Fr. Forsythiae Lian Qiao 2-20g Clears Heat, relieves toxicity and clears Blood Heat.
Rz. Coptidis Huang Lian 1-10g Drains Fire, relieves toxicity and clears Heart Fire
With Sheng Di Huang for insomnia and delirium due to unremitting Heat injuring the Ying level
Rx. Salviae Miltiorrhizae Dan Shen 2-6g Assists in cooling the Blood and prevents Stasis.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Heat from the Ying
  • Relieves Fire Toxin
  • Nourishes the Yin
  • Invigorates the Blood

SYNDROMES

  • Ying Stage Heat (Re)
  • Ying Stage Heat Attacks the Pericardium

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • High fever worse at night
  • Severe irritability
  • Restlessness
  • Thirst
  • Delirium
  • Faint indistinct purpura and erythema
  • Insomnia
  • Dizziness
  • Vertigo
  • Maculae
  • Eyes that are open or closed for a prolonged period of time
  • T: Scarlet and dry
  • C: Little
  • P: Thready and rapid or Rapid
