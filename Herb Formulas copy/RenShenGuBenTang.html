HERB FORMULAS

REN SHEN GU BEN TANG - 人參股本汤 - GINSENG DECOCTION TO STRENGTHEN THE ROOT

English: Ginseng Decoction to Strengthen the Root
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Ginseng Ren Shen 6g Tonifies Lung Qi, generates Body Fluids and stops thirst.
With Shu Di Huang, for Qi and Yin Deficiency.
With Tian Men Dong, for Qi and Yin Deficiency with a fever, thirst, irritability, shortness of breath and a dry, red tongue.
(Rx. Codonopsis) (Dang Shen) (12g) (Substitutes for Ren Shen.)
Rx. Asparagi Tian Men Dong 20g Nourishes Kidney Yin, clears Lung Heat, sedates Fire, moistens the Lungs, generates fluids and resolves Phlegm.
With Ren Shen and Shu Di Huang, for Debility and low grade fever due to Yin Deficiency.
Rx. Ophiopogonis Mai Men Dong 20g Moistens the Lungs, nourishes Yin and stops coughing.
With Tian Men Dong, for Lung and Kidney Yin Deficiency with consumptive coughing and occasional hemoptysis.
With Sheng Di Huang, for Hematemesis or epistaxis due to Yin Deficiency with Excess Heat.
Rx. Rehmanniae Preparata Shu Di Huang 20g Nourishes Liver and Kidney Yin,nourishes Jing and Blood and arrests coughing and wheezing.
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 20g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, nourishes Yin and generates fluids.
With Shu Di Huang, for Heat symptoms due to Yin Deficiency and Devastated Blood.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Fire from the Lungs
  • Nourishes Yin and Blood

SYNDROMES

  • Spleen Yin Deficiency
  • Kidney Yin Deficiency with Lung Heat

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Consumptive cough
  • Consumptive fever
  • Hemoptysis
  • Night sweating
  • Spontaneous sweating
  • T: Red tongue
  • C: Little or None
  • P: Weak, thready and rapid

TREATS

  • Tuberculosis
  • Late menstruation
  • Herpes genitalia
