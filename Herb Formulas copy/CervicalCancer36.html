HERB FORMULAS

CERVICAL CANCER #36

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Concha Arcae Wa Leng Zi 15g Invigorates the Blood, dissolves Phlegm, resolves Stagnation, softens hardness, dissipates nodules and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Gypsum Fibrosum Shi Gao 20g Clears Heat in the Qi Stage, drains Fire, relieves irritability, quenches thirst and heals eczema, burns and ulcerated sores and wounds.
Rx. Arnebiae/Lithospermi Zi Cao 15g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, invigorates Blood circulation, relieves Fire toxins, clears Damp-Heat from the skin,aids healing of tissues and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rz. Dryopteris Guan Zhong 15g Cools the Blood and stops bleeding.
Rz. Smilacis Glabrae Tu Fu Ling 30g Clears Damp-Heat from the skin and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Sm. Alpiniae Katsumadai Cao Dou Kou 10g Dries Dampness, warms the Middle Jiao, stops vomiting and strengthens the Spleen.
Rx. Pseudostellariae Tai Zi Shen 15g Strengthens the Spleen, tonifies Qi, generates Body Fluids and treats chronic febrile disorders.
Rx. Astragali Huang Qi 30g Tonifies Qi and Blood, promotes the discharge of pus, generates flesh, expels toxins, relieves numbness and pain and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Dang Gui, for Qi and Blood Deficiency with chronic non-healing sores, numbness, painful obstruction and paresthesia.
Rx. Rubiae Qian Cao Gan 15g Cools and invigorates the Blood, stops bleeding, dispels Blood Stasis and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Hai Piao Xiao and Huang Qi, for Qi Deficient uterine bleeding.
With Zi Cao and Han Lian Cao, cools the Blood to treat various dermatological disorders characterized by itching.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 15g Tonifies, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood, regulates the menses, reduces swelling, expels pus, generates flesh and alleviates pain.
With Huang Qi, for Qi and Blood Deficiency.
Endoconcha Sepiae Hai Piao Xiao 15g Restrains, holds in and stops bleeding.
With Huang Qi and Qian Cao Gen, For excessive uterine bleeding.
Flos Lonicerae Jin Yin Hua 30g Clears Damp-Heat from the Lower Jiao and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Dang Gui, reduces obstruction and toxicity for swollen, painful sores and necrosis of surrounding tissue.
Rx. Scutellariae Barbatae Ban Zhi Lian 15g Clears Heat, resolves Fire toxins, invigorates the Blood, reduces swellings, treats various types of cancer, mildly dispels Blood Stasis and stops bleeding.
Rz. Coptidis Huang Lian 6g Clears Heat, drains Dampness, stops bleeding and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Hb. Ecliptae Han Lian Cao 15g Nourishes and tonifies Liver and Kidney Yin, cools the Blood,stops bleeding and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Olibanum Ru Xiang 10g Invigorates the Blood, promotes the movement of Qi, reduces swelling, generates flesh and alleviates pain.
Rx. Sophorae Tonkinensis Shan Dou Gen 10g Clears Heat, resolves Fire toxicity, disperses swellings, relieves Damp-Heat jaundice and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Heat
  • Removes toxicity
  • Resolves Dampness
  • Stops diarrhea and bleeding
  • Inhibits the growth of cancer cells

SYNDROMES

  • Damp-Heat in the Lower Jiao with Damp Predominant

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Lethargy
  • Anorexia
  • Maybe jaundice
  • No thirst or
  • Thirst with no desire to drink
  • Nausea
  • Vomiting
  • Afternoon fever
  • Body aches
  • A sallow complexion
  • Scanty urination
  • Constipation or
  • Bloody diarrhea or
  • Dysentery
  • Chest discomfort
  • Epigastric pain and distention
  • Dull appearance
  • Maybe disorientation
  • Loose stools
  • Maybe skin vesicles
  • Heavy extremities
  • Lower Jiao pain
  • Heaviness
  • Headache
  • Dull Spirit
  • Abdominal distention
  • Mental fuzziness
  • T: Normal
  • C: Yellowish white and greasy
  • P: Soft or Soft and rapid or maybe Slippery and rapid

TREATS

  • Cervical cancer
