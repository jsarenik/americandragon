HERB FORMULAS

YU SHU DAN - 紫金锭 - JADE PIVOT SPECIAL PILL

English: Jade Pivot Special Pill
Also Known As: Knoxia and Moleplant Lozenge
Zi Jin Ding 玉枢丹

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Pseudobulbus Cremastrae/ Pleiones Shan Ci Gu 90g Clears Heat, resolves toxicity, reduces abscesses, dissipates nodules, resolves toxicity and reduces swelling.
With She Xiang, for abscesses and carbuncles.
With She Xiang and Wu Bei Zi, for abscesses, deep-rooted boils and scrofula.
With She Xiang and Hong Da Ji, for toxic sores.
Rx. Euphorbiae Pekinensis Hong Da Ji 45g Drains water downward and drives out thin mucus. Reduces swelling and dissipates nodules topically.
Sm. Euphorbiae Lathyridis Qian Jin Zi 30g Expels water and resolves swelling.
Galla Chinensis Wu Bei Zi 90g Astringes Intestines and stops diarrhea. Relieves Fire toxicity, clears Dampness and reduces swellings topically.
Moschus She Xiang 9g Strongly opens the Orifices and Luo vessels, revives the Spirit and unblocks closed disorders Activates the Blood, dissipates masses, reduces swellings, detoxifies, opens the channels and alleviates pain
Realgar Xiong Huang 30g Dries Dampness, expels Phlegm, stops malarial disorders and settles Wind. Relieves toxicity and kills parasites topically.
With Shan Ci Gu, Zhu Sha and Qian Jin Zi, for malaria.
With Zhu Sha, for seizures, epilepsy and convulsions.
Cinnabaris Zhu Sha 30g Sedates the Heart, calms the Spirit, expels Phlegm and sedates jitteriness and convulsions while stopping tremors. Clears Heat, relieves toxicity and prevents putrefaction topically.
Sm. Oryzae Glutinosae Nuo Mi 30-60g Augments the Spleen and Stomach and Tonifies Qi.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Transforms Phlegm
  • Opens the Orifices
  • Breaks the Dirty
  • Clears Toxicity
  • Reduces Swelling topically
  • Disperses masses topically
  • Stops pain

SYNDROMES

  • Toxic Heat with Phlegm Retention

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Distending full painful sensation in the Stomach and Intestines
  • Vomiting
  • Diarrhea
  • Swelling
  • Chest and hypochondriac pain
  • Inability to swallow even fluid
  • Pinkish vomit
  • Dull complexion
  • Weight loss
  • Dry skin
  • Dry mouth and throat
  • Constipation
  • Stools like pebbles
  • Violent cramping abdominal pain
  • Nausea
  • Tenesmus
  • Restlessness
  • Mental confusion bordering on delirium

TREATS

  • Food poisoning
  • Furuncles
  • Diarrhea
  • Malaria
  • Constipation
  • Boils
  • Nausea/Vomiting
  • Delirium
  • Spontaneous perspiration
  • Dry skin

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated during pregnancy.
