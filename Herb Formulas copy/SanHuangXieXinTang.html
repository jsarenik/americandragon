HERB FORMULAS

SAN HUANG XIE XIN TANG - 三黃瀉心湯 - DRAIN THE EPIGASTRIUM POWDER

English: Drain the Epigastrium Powder
Also Known As: Xie Xin Tang
Coptis and Rhubarb Combination
Decoction to Purge Heat from the Heart and Stomach
Yellow Purge the Heart Decoction

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. et Rz. Rhei Da Huang 1-15g Purges and drains Fire and treats upper body bleeding.
With Huang Lian and Huang Qin, drains Heat from the San Jiao mainly through the stool.
Rz. Coptidis Huang Lian 1-9g Drains Fire, dries Dampness, clears Heart and Stomach Fire and stops bleeding.
With Huang Qin, drains Heat from the Upper and Middle Jiaos.
Rx. Scutellariae Huang Qin 1-15g Drains Fire, dries Dampness, detoxifies and stops bleeding.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Drains Fire
  • Relieves toxicity
  • Drains Dampness
  • Dispels noxious Heat and toxins from the Heart and Stomach

SYNDROMES

  • Heart Fire Flaring Up
  • Toxic Heat or Fire of the Heart and Stomach
  • Stomach Fire
  • Damp-Heat Accumulation

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated during pregnancy.
  • Contraindicated during nursing.
  • Contraindicated for those with Spleen and Stomach Deficiency Cold.
  • Do not take large doses or for an extended period of time.
