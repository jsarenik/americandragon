HERB FORMULAS

ER ZHI WAN - 二至丸 - TWO SOLSTICE PILL

English: Two-Solstice Pill
Also Known As: Two Ultimate Pill
Two Supreme Pill

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Fr. Ligustri Lucidi Nu Zhen Zi 500g Nourishes and tonifies the Liver and Kidney Yin, clears Deficiency Heat and improves vision.
With Mo Han Lian, strengthens the Yin to blacken the hair.
Hb. Ecliptae Han Lian Cao 500g Nourishes and tonifies the Liver and Kidney Yin, cools the Blood and stops bleeding.
With Nu Zhen Zi, for dizziness, blurred vision, and premature graying of the hair due to severe Liver and Kidney Yin Deficiency with ascendant Yang.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Nourishes Kidney and Liver Yin and Blood

SYNDROMES

  • Kidney and Liver Yin Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Weak and sore lower back and knees
  • Weakness and atrophy of the legs
  • Dry, parched mouth and throat
  • Dizziness
  • Blurred vision
  • Insomnia
  • Dream disturbed sleep
  • Spermatorrhea
  • Prematurely gray hair
  • Premature hair loss
  • A bitter taste in the mouth
  • Nocturnal emissions
  • Hypermenorrhea
  • T: Red and dry
  • C: Little or None
  • P: Thready and rapid

TREATS

  • Insomnia
  • Hepatitis B
  • Threatened miscarriage
  • Hemorrhage
  • Hemoptysis
  • Hematochezia
  • Obesity
  • Tourette syndrome
  • Premature ovarian failure, prevention
  • Coronary Heart disease
  • Neurasthenia
  • Dizziness
  • Spermatorrhea
  • Metrorrhagia
  • Painful obstruction
  • Chronic nephritis
  • Aplastic anemia
  • Neurasthenia
  • Palpitations
  • Menorrhagia
  • Hematuria
  • Prostatitis
  • Prolonged menstruation
  • Alopecia
  • Hyperglycemia
  • Lumbago
  • Nocturnal emissions
  • Constipation
  • Post-menopausal uterine bleeding
  • Prematurely gray hair

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Use with caution for those with weak digestion.
  • Contraindicated for those with atrophy caused by Damp-Heat Invasion.
HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

NOTES

  • This formula can replace Six Ingredient Pill with Rehmannia Liu Wei Di Huang Wan, especially when chronic bleeding is manifest.
