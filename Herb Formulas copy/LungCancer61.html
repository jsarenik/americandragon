HERB FORMULAS

LUNG CANCER #61

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rz. Atractylodis Macrocephalae Bai Zhu 10g Tonifies the Spleen, augments Qi, dries Dampness, promotes water metabolism and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Gan Jiang, eliminates Cold Dampness in the Spleen with loss of taste, a sticky sensation in the mouth, nausea, vomiting, diarrhea and a thick, white tongue coat.
With Dang Shen and Gan Jiang, for Deficiency Cold of the Middle Jiao with abdominal pain, distention, vomiting and diarrhea.
Poria Fu Ling 10g Promotes urination, leaches out Dampness, strengthens the Spleen and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rz. Arisaematis Preparatum Zhi Tian Nan Xing 1-g Dries Dampness, expels Phlegm, reduces swelling, alleviates pain and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Ginseng Ren Shen 10g Tonifies Lung Qi, generates Body Fluids, stops thirst and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Bai Zhi and Fu Ling, for Spleen and Stomach Qi Deficiency with Dampness with anorexia, diarrhea, vomiting, abdominal distention and fatigue.
With Bai Zhu, tonifies the transformation and transportation functions of the Spleen.
With Huang Qi, for Lung Qi Deficiency.
Rz. Curculiginis Xian Mao 10g Tonifies the Kidneys,strengthens Yang, expels Cold and eliminates Dampness.
Fr. Psoraliae Bu Gu Zhi 10g Tonifies the Kidneys, strengthens Yang and stabilizes Jing.
Nidus Vespae Lu Feng Fang 10g Relieves toxicity, expels Wind, dries Dampness, stops pain, treats tumors and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Bombyx Batryticatus Jiang Can 10g Transforms Phlegm, softens hardness, eliminates toxins, dissipates nodules and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Astragali Huang Qi 30g Tonifies Qi and Blood, tonifies the Lungs, promotes the discharge of pus, expels toxins, generates Body Fluids, relieves numbness and pain and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Ren Shen, for Qi Deficiency with general debility, anorexia, fatigue, tired extremities and spontaneous sweating.
With Bai Zhu, for Spleen Qi Deficiency with weakness, lassitude and loose stools.
Rx. Pseudostellariae Tai Zi Shen 30g Strengthens the Spleen, tonifies Qi, tonifies the Lungs and generates Body Fluids.
With Wu Wei Zi, nourishes Fluids.
Rx. Codonopsis Lanceolatae Shan Hai Luo 30g Clears Lung Heat, nourishes Yin, resolves swelling, removes toxicity, discharges pus, eliminates Phlegm and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Fr. Schisandrae Wu Wei Zi 9g Astringes the leakage of Lung Qi, stops coughing, tonifies the Kidneys, astringes Jing and tonifies Qi.
With Ren Shen and Gan Jiang, for Lung Deficiency with chronic coughing and wheezing.
With Huang Qi, for Yang Deficiency with spontaneous sweat.
Rz. Zingiberis Gan Jiang 6g Warms the Middle, expels Cold, warms the Lungs and transforms thin mucus.
With Bai Zhu and Ren Shen, for Spleen Yang Deficiency.
Cordyceps Dong Chong Xia Cao 3g Tonifies the Kidneys, strengthens Yang, augments Jing, nourishes Lung Yin, transforms Phlegm and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Tonifies Qi
  • Tonifies the Kidneys
  • Tonifies Yang
  • Eliminates Dampness
  • Clears Lung Heat
  • Strengthens the Lungs
  • Generates Fluids
  • Astringes the Lungs

SYNDROMES

  • Toxic Damp-Heat in the Lungs with Qi and Yang Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Tumor develops quickly
  • Long-standing illness
  • Pain
  • Redness
  • Ulceration
  • Discharge of yellow or bloody, foul-smelling fluid
  • Fatigue
  • Fever
  • Anxiety
  • Dry mouth
  • Thirst
  • Dark urine
  • Bitter taste
  • Red eyes
  • Insomnia
  • Anorexia
  • Abdominal distention and discomfort
  • Suffocating sensation in the chest
  • Maybe abdominal pain on palpation
  • Dislike of oily foods
  • Nausea
  • Vomiting
  • Lassitude
  • Maybe thirst or thirst with no desire to drink
  • Diarrhea with foul smell or
  • Constipation
  • Sweet sticky taste or bitter taste in mouth
  • Chills
  • Cold limbs
  • Inability to get warm
  • Loose stools
  • Profuse clear urine
  • Spontaneous sweating
  • Lassitude
  • T: Pale red
  • C: White or Thick and greasy
  • P: Slow, deep and weak or Wiry or Rolling and rapid or Wiry, overflowing and rapid or Soft and rapid

TREATS

  • Lung cancer
