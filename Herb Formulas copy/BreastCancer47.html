HERB FORMULAS

BREAST CANCER #47

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rx. Astragali Huang Qi 30g Tonifies Qi and Blood, strengthens the Spleen, promotes the discharge of pus, generates flesh, expels toxins, relieves numbness and pain and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Dang Gui, for Qi and Blood Deficiency with chronic non-healing sores, numbness, painful obstruction and paresthesia.
Concha Ostreae Mu Li 30g Softens hardness, dissipates nodules and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rz. Arisaematis Preparatum Zhi Tian Nan Xing 30g Dries Dampness, expels Phlegm, reduces swelling, alleviates pain and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Cx. Moutan Mu Dan Pi 30g Clears Heat, cools and harmonizes the Blood, clears Deficiency Fire, invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, drains pus and reduces swelling.
With Chi Shao, for Blood Stasis disorders.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 9g Tonifies, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood, regulates the menses, reduces swelling, expels pus, generates flesh and alleviates pain.
With Bai Shao, regulates and harmonizes Qi and Blood.
Rx. Paeoniae Rubra Chi Shao 12g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, relieves pain and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Paeoniae Alba Bai Shao 12g Nourishes the Blood, regulates menstruation, astringes Yin, adjusts the Ying and Wei, softens the Liver and relieves pain.
With Dang Gui, nourishes Yin and Blood.
Nidus Vespae Lu Feng Fang 12g Relieves toxicity, expels Wind, alleviates pain and itching and treats tumors.
Rz. Atractylodis Macrocephalae Bai Zhu 12g Tonifies the Spleen, augments Qi, dries Dampness, promotes water metabolism and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Fr. Psoraliae Bu Gu Zhi 12g Tonifies the Kidneys, strengthens Yang, stabilizes Jing, astringes urine, tonifies and warms Spleen Yang and stops diarrhea.
Per. Trichosanthis Gua Lou Pi 15g Clears and moistens the Lungs and transforms Hot Phlegm.
Sm. Vaccariae Wang Bu Liu Xing 15g Promotes the movement of Blood, promotes lactation, invigorates the channels, reduces swelling and drains abscesses.
With Huang Qi and Dang Gui, for insufficient lactation due postpartum Qi and Blood Deficiency.
Spica Prunellae Xia Ku Cao 15g Clears Hot Phlegm, dissipates nodules and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Sargassum Hai Zao 15g Reduces Phlegm and softens areas of hardness and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Flos. Citri Sarcodactylis Fo Shou Hua 15g Regulates Lung Qi and transforms Phlegm.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Tonifies Qi and Blood
  • Strengthens the Spleen
  • Softens hardness
  • Dissipates nodules
  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Dispels Blood Stasis
  • Relieves pain
  • Inhibits the growth of cancer cells

SYNDROMES

  • Qi Deficiency with Blood Stagnation

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Fatigue
  • Loose stools
  • Anorexia
  • Dizziness
  • Pale face
  • Weak voice
  • Reluctance to speak
  • Sweating with little or no exertion
  • Pain that is fixed, stabbing and /or severe
  • Pain is worse with pressure
  • Dark complexion
  • Fixed, palpable masses
  • Bleeding with clots
  • Purple lips
  • Purple nails
  • Dry skin
  • Blue, dark or purplish discoloration of skin in small or large areas
  • Varicose and spider veins
  • Cold hands and feet
  • Tendency to hemorrhage dark blood or clots
  • T: Pale-purple, possible purple spots
  • C: White
  • P: Empty and choppy or Empty, wiry and choppy

TREATS

  • Breast cancer with brain metastasis
