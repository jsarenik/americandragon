HERB FORMULAS

CHRONIC LEUKEMIA #3

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Hb. Senecionis Scandens Qian Li Guang 30g Clears Heat, eliminates toxins and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Hb. Hedyotis Diffusae Bai Hua She She Cao 30g Clears Heat, strongly relieves Fire toxins, reduces abscesses and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Hb Solani Nigri Long Kui 30g Clears Heat, resolves toxicity, invigorates the Blood, reduces swellings and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Hb. Agrimoniae Xian He Cao 30g Astringes leakage of Blood, stops bleeding, tonifies Qi and Blood and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Glehniae Bei Sha Shen 30g Nourishes Lung and Stomach Yin, clears Lung Heat and tonifies Liver and Kidney Yin.
Flos Lonicerae Jin Yin Hua 18g Clears Heat, resolves Fire toxicity and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Dang Gui, reduces obstruction and toxicity for swollen, painful sores and necrosis of surrounding tissue.
Rx. Salviae Miltiorrhizae Dan Shen 18g Invigorates the Blood, dispels Blood Stasis, clears Heat, soothes irritability, cools the Blood, reduces abscesses, nourishes the Blood, calms the Spirit and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Atractylodis Macrocephalae Bai Zhu 15g Tonifies the Spleen, augments Qi, dries Dampness and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Rx. Astragali Huang Qi 12g Tonifies Qi and Blood, strengthens the Spleen, raises the Yang Qi of the Spleen and Stomach, promotes the discharge of pus, generates flesh, expels toxins, relieves numbness and pain and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
With Bai Zhu, for Spleen Qi Deficiency with weakness, lassitude and loose stools.
With Dang Gui, for Qi and Blood Deficiency with chronic non-healing sores, numbness, painful obstruction and paresthesia.
Rx. Angelicae Sinensis Dang Gui 12g Tonifies, invigorates and harmonizes the Blood, disperses Cold, reduces swelling, expels pus, generates flesh and alleviates pain.
With Huang Qi, for Qi and Blood Deficiency.
Fr. Psoraliae Bu Gu Zhi 12g Tonifies the Kidneys, strengthens Yang, stabilizes Jing, astringes urine, tonifies and warms Spleen Yang and stops diarrhea.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Heat
  • Resolves toxicity
  • Nourishes Yin
  • Tonifies Qi
  • Nourishes the Blood
  • Tonifies Yang
  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Inhibits the growth of cancer cells

SYNDROMES

  • Toxic Heat with Qi, Blood and Yang Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Long-standing illness
  • Pain
  • Redness
  • Fatigue
  • Fever
  • Anxiety
  • Dry mouth
  • Thirst
  • Dark urine
  • Bitter taste
  • Red eyes
  • Insomnia
  • Fatigue
  • Loose stools
  • Poor appetite
  • Dizziness
  • Pale face
  • Weak voice
  • Reluctance to speak
  • Sweating with little or no exertion
  • Palpitations
  • Insomnia
  • Dry skin
  • Dry hair
  • Poor memory
  • Paleness
  • Scanty periods
  • Chills
  • Cold limbs
  • Inability to get warm
  • Loose stools
  • Profuse clear urine
  • Spontaneous sweating
  • Lassitude
  • T: Red or Pale or Pale, thin and dry
  • C: Thin and white or White or Yellow and greasy or Thick, sticky, dry and yellow
  • P: Weak or Thin and choppy or Wiry or Rolling and rapid or Wiry, overflowing and rapid

TREATS

  • Chronic leukemia
