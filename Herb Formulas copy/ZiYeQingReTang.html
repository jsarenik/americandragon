HERB FORMULAS

ZI YE QING RE TANG - 滋液清热汤 - NOURISH FLUIDS AND CLEAR HEAT DECOCTION

English: Nourish Fluids and Clear Heat Decoction
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Hb. Artemisiae Annuae Qiang Huo 3-10g Releases the Exterior and disperses Cold.
Rx. Cynanchi Atrati Bai Wei 3-12g Cools the Blood and clears Deficiency Heat.
With Di Gu Pi, for fevers, steaming bone disorder and night sweats due to Yin Deficiency Heat.
Rx. Rehmanniae Sheng Di Huang 5-30g Clears Heat, cools the Blood, nourishes Yin, and generates fluids.
With Bai Shao, for Blood Deficiency Heat.
Rz. Anemarrhenae Zhi Mu 6-12g Clears Heat, drains Fire, nourishes Yin, moistens Dryness, generates Fluids and clears Deficiency Fire.
Cx. Moutan Mu Dan Pi 6-12g Clears Heat, cools and harmonizes the Blood and clears Deficiency Fire.
With Sheng Di Huang, for a residual Warm pathogen which has injured the Yin.
With Zhi Mu and Sheng Di Huang, for menopause with Yin Deficiency and Heat.
Rx. Glycyrrhizae Gan Cao 3-14g Clears Heat and relieves Fire toxicity.
Sm. Zizyphi Spinosae Suan Zao Ren 9-18g Nourishes Heart Yin, tonifies Liver Blood, calms the Spirit, astringes sweat and generates fluids.
With Zhi Mu, nourishes Liver Blood and Heart Yin to clear Deficiency Fire.
Fr. Jujube Da Zao 10-30g Nourishes the Blood and calms the Spirit.
With Gan Cao and Fu Xiao Mai, for dry or restless organ disorder.
Caulis Polygoni Multiflori Ye Jiao Teng 9-30g Nourishes the Heart Yin and Blood, calms the Spirit and astringes sweat.
Fr. Tritici Levis Fu Xiao Mai 9-30g Stops excessive sweating due to Deficiency, nourishes the Heart and calms the Spirit.
With Di Gu Pi, for sweating associated with steaming bone disorder.
With Gan Cao and Da Zao, for emotional disorders, disorientation, emotional instability and irritability.
Rx. Bupleuri Chai Hu 3-12g Resolves Shao Yang disorders, reduces fever, spreads Liver Qi and relieves Stagnation.
With Bai Shao, for Liver Blood Deficiency with Liver Qi Stagnation.
With Gan Cao, for Wind-Heat fever.
Rx. Paeoniae Alba Bai Shao 5-30g Nourishes the Blood, astringes Yin, regulates menstruation and calms Liver Yang.
With Gan Cao, regulates the relationship between the Liver and Spleen.
With Chai Hu, for Flank pain due to Liver Qi Stagnation.
Cx. Lycii Di Gu Pi 6-15g Cools the Blood and drains Yin Deficiency Fire.
Cx. Phellodendri Huang Bai 3-12g Drains Deficiency Heat.
With Zhi Mu, for Yin Deficiency fever with steaming bones and night sweats.
With Fu Xiao Mai, for night sweats.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Nourishes Yin
  • Clears Deficiency Heat
  • Nourishes the Heart
  • Clams the Shen

SYNDROMES

  • Late-Stage Febrile Disease with Yin and Body Fluid Deficiency

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Lingering fever from a febrile disease
  • Vomiting
  • Irritability
  • Thirst with a desire to drink
  • Parched mouth, lips and throat
  • Choking cough
  • Restlessness
  • Insomnia
  • Nausea
  • Anorexia
  • Profuse perspiration
  • A dry mouth
  • Indigestion
  • Listlessness
  • Chest oppression
  • Ulcerations of the mouth
  • Coarse skin
  • Cough
  • Stifling sensation in the chest or epigastrium
  • Dyspnea
  • Night sweats
  • Hemoptysis
  • Sore throat
  • Throat sores
  • Facial flushing
  • Constipation
  • A dry mouth and throat
  • Wheezing
  • Steaming bones
  • Hoarseness
  • T: Red or Red, dry and cracked
  • C: Little or None
  • P: Thready and rapid

TREATS

  • Menopause syndrome
  • Hot flashes
  • Night sweats
  • Mood swings
  • Irritability
  • Dyspnea
