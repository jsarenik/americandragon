HERB FORMULAS

GUI LU ER XIAN JIAO - 龟鹿二仙胶 - TORTOISE SHELL AND DEER ANTLER TWO IMMORTALS SYRUP

English: Tortoise Shell and Deer Antler Two Immortals Syrup
Also Known As: Deer and Turtle Two Immortals Gelatin
Magic Glue of Tortoise Plastron and Antler Horn

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Mature Cornu Cervi Pantotrichum Lu Jiao 5000g (10-20g) Tonifies the Kidneys and the DU Channel, strengthens Yang, Jing and Blood, strengthens the sinews and bones, benefits marrow and tonifies and nourishes Qi and Blood.
With Ren Shen, strongly tonifies Qi, strongly warms the Ming Men to generate Qi and benefits Qi, Blood, Yin, Yang and Jing.
Plastrum Testudinis Gui Ban 2500g
(5-10g) 		Nourishes Yin, anchors Yang, benefits the Kidneys, strengthens the bones, nourishes the Blood, enriches Yin and stabilizes the Chong and Ren channels.
With Lu Jiao, for weakness and debility due to Kidney Deficiency.
With Ren Shen, Lu Jiao and Gou Qi Zi, for Kidney Yin and Yang Deficiencies with weak bones, spermatorrhea or nocturnal emissions, weight loss and blurred vision.
Fr. Lycii Gou Qi Zi 900g
(3-6g)		 Nourishes and tonifies Liver and Kidney Blood and Yin and benefits Jing.
Rx. Ginseng Ren Shen 450g
(3-6g)		 Powerfully tonifies Yuan Qi and treats impotence.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Nourishes and replenishes Yin
  • Tonifies Jing
  • Augments Qi
  • Tonifies and strengthens Yang
  • Nourishes the Blood

SYNDROMES

  • Kidney Yin and Yang Deficiency with Insufficiency of Jing and Blood in the Du, Chong and Ren channels

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Emaciation
  • Weakness
  • Spermatorrhea
  • Blurred vision
  • Aching and weak low back and knees
  • Daybreak diarrhea
  • Weight loss
  • Dizziness
  • Nocturnal emissions
  • T: Pink or Pale
  • C: White or None
  • P: Thready and frail or Deep, slow and weak

TREATS

  • Severe anemia
  • Endocrine-related developmental disorders
  • Impotence
  • Nervous exhaustion
  • Nocturnal emissions
  • Hematuria
  • Knee pain
  • Weakness
  • Spermatorrhea
  • Osteoporosis
  • Hypothyroidism
  • Polyuria
  • Hypotension
  • Lumbago
  • Diarrhea
  • Dizziness
  • Menopause syndrome

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • Contraindicated for those with Spleen and Stomach Deficiency.
  • Contraindicated for those with Yin Deficiency Fire.
HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

NOTES

  • Prepared as a syrup. Take 3-9g doses with wine or lightly salted warm water.
