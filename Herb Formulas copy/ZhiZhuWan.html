HERB FORMULAS

ZHI ZHU WAN - 枳术囊 - UNRIPE BITTER ORANGE AND ATRACTYLODES PILL

English: Unripe Bitter Orange and Atractylodes Pill
Also Known As: Pills of Immature Bitter Orange and White Atractylodes

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Rz. Atractylodis Macrocephalae Bai Zhu 10-18g
60g decoction 		Tonifies the Spleen, augments Qi, dries Dampness and promotes water metabolism.
With Zhi Shi, for Spleen Deficiency with Qi Stagnation with anorexia, epigastric focal distention, and fullness.
Fr. Aurantii Immaturus Zhi Shi 5-9g
30g decoction 		Breaks up Stagnant Qi, reduces accumulation, descends Qi, unblocks the bowels, removes Stagnant Food, transforms Phlegm, reduces distention and resolves hardenings.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Tonifies the Spleen
  • Reduces focal distention

SYNDROMES

  • Spleen Qi Deficiency with epigastric and abdominal distention
  • Spleen Qi Deficiency with Qi Stagnation and Food Retention

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Loss of appetite
  • Chest, epigastric and abdominal focal distention
  • Acid regurgitation
  • T: Normal
  • C: White or Yellow and greasy
  • P: Deficient or Slippery

TREATS

  • Obesity
  • Chronic gastritis
  • Anorexia
  • Gastroptosis
  • Gastroenteritis
  • Acid reflux
  • Urticaria
  • Indigestion
  • Chronic colitis
  • Peptic ulcer
  • Indigestion in children
  • Cholecystitis
  • Abdominal fullness and distention
  • Chronic hepatitis
  • Rectal prolapse

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

CONTRAINDICATIONS
  • While taking this formula, avoid raw, Cold or stimulating foods>
HERB/DRUG INTERACTIONS

NOTES

  • Grind into a powder and mix with rice fried in Fol Nelumbinis He Ye and form into pills.
  • Take 6-9g twice per day.
