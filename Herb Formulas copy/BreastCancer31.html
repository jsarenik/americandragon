HERB FORMULAS

BREAST CANCER #31

English:  
Also Known As:

HERBS AND ACTIONS

Pharmaceutical Latin
Pin Yin
Dosage
Actions
Sm. Vaccariae Wang Bu Liu Xing 30g Invigorates the Blood, promotes lactation, invigorates the channels, reduces swelling and drains abscesses.
Hb. Euphorbiae Helioscopiae Ze Qi 30g Promotes urination, reduces edema, disperses swelling and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Flos Lonicerae Jin Yin Hua 30g Clears Heat, resolves Fire toxicity and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Pleione and Nutgalls Formula Yu Shu Tan 12g Clears Heat, resolves toxicity, clears Dampness, resolves swelling, invigorates the Blood, breaks up Blood Stasis, eliminates masses and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.
Borneolum Bing Pian 0.6g Clears Heat, drains Fire, resolves toxicity, alleviates pain, dissipates nodules and inhibits the growth of cancer cells.

FORMULA ACTIONS

  • Clears Heat
  • Resolves toxicity
  • Clears Dampness
  • Resolves swelling
  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Breaks up Blood Stasis
  • Eliminates masses
  • Inhibits the growth of cancer cells

SYNDROMES

  • Toxic Heat

CLINICAL MANIFESTATIONS

  • Tumor develops quickly
  • Fluid inside the breast with a lump inside it
  • Long-standing illness
  • Pain
  • Redness
  • Ulceration
  • Discharge of yellow or bloody, foul-smelling fluid
  • Fatigue
  • Fever
  • Anxiety
  • Dry mouth
  • Thirst
  • Dark urine
  • Bitter taste
  • Red eyes
  • Insomnia
  • T: Red
  • C: White or Yellow and greasy or Thick, sticky, dry and yellow
  • P: Wiry or Rolling and rapid or Wiry, overflowing and rapid

TREATS

  • Breast cancer
