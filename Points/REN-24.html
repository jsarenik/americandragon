POINT: REN-24 (CONCEPTION VESSEL-24)  

NAME: CHENGJIANG

English: Container of Fluids
Also Known As: Contain Fluid
Receiving Fluid
Source Receptacle
Receiving Sauce
Receiving Liquid
Saliva Receiver
Guishi (Ghost City)

LOCATION:

  • In the depression in the center of the mentolabial groove.
    Note: the mentolabial groove runs horizontally, approximately midway between the chin and the lower lip.
  • On the chin, in the depression below the middle of the lower lip.

NEEDLING:

  • Puncture obliquely upward 0.2 to 0.3 cun.
  • Moxibustion is applicable.
  • Slanted insertion, directed upward, 0.3 to 0.5 cun. Sensation: local distention and pain.
  • Transverse-oblique insertion directed superiorly 0.2 to 0.3 cun.
  • Transverse insertion along the lower lip to join with ST-4 Dicang.

COMMAND FUNCTIONS:

  • Meeting point of the Large Intestine, Stomach and DU channels.
  • Exit point
  • Sun Si-miao Ghost point

ACTIONS:

  • Expels Exterior Wind
  • Extinguishes Wind
  • Benefits the face
  • Regulates the REN channel

CONTRAINDICATIONS:

NOTES:

  • This is a good point if the REN channel is stuck or traumatized.
  • Good for patients who are mentally or physically withered.
  • It causes fluid secretions to flow freely and enhances the Spirit and mind.
  • It is mostly used for Exterior Wind affecting the face.
  • It is good for the treatment of psychosis and epilepsy.
