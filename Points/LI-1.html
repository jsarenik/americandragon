POINT: LI-1 (LARGE INTESTINE-1)

NAME: SHANGYANG

English: Shang Yang
Also Known As: Commercial Sunlight
Musical Yang
Merchant of Yang
Metal Yang
Trade Yang
Metal's Note Yang

LOCATION:

  • About 0.1 cun from the base of the index fingernail at the radial side.
  • On the dorsal aspect of the index finger, at the junction of the lines drawn along the radial border of the nail and the base of the nail, approximately 0.1 cun from the corner of the nail.

NEEDLING:

  • Straight insertion, 0.2 to 0.3 cun, or prick to bleed.
  • Perpendicular or oblique insertion directed proximally 0.1 to 0.2 cun, or prick to bleed.

COMMAND FUNCTIONS:

  • Metal point
  • Horary point
  • Jing-Well point

ACTIONS:

  • Dispels Wind-Heat
  • Clears Heat
  • Brightens the eyes
  • Benefits the throat
  • Calms the mind
  • Expels Wind
  • Scatters Cold
  • Reduces swelling
  • Alleviates pain
  • Revives consciousness

CONTRAINDICATIONS:

  • Use moxibustion with caution.

NOTES:

  • This is considered as a "dental point".
  • this point helps to break through to a stubborn, rigid, masked patient and is good for the mental side of Large Intestine problems.
  • This point is effective in Excess patterns to remove obstructions quickly.
