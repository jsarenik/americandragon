POINT: ST-20 (STOMACH-20)

NAME: CHENGMAN

English: Supporting Fullness
Also Known As: Support Fullness
Receiving Fullness

LOCATION:

  • On the abdomen 5 cun above the umbilicus, 2 cun lateral to REN-13 Shangwan.
  • On the abdomen 5 cun above the umbilicus, 2 cun lateral the midline, level with REN-13 Shangwan.

NEEDLING:

  • Puncture perpendicularly 0.5-1 cun.
  • Moxibustion is applicable
  • Straight insertion 0.5-0.8 cun.
  • Caution: In thin patients, deep needling may penetrate the peritoneal cavity.
                   Deep needling at right ST-20 Chengman may penetrate an enlarged Liver.

COMMAND FUNCTIONS:

ACTIONS:

  • Harmonizes the Middle Jiao
  • Descends Rebellious Lung or Stomach Qi

INDICATIONS:

  • Abdominal distention
  • Vomiting
  • Gastric pain
  • Anorexia
  • Hematemesis
  • Hemoptysis
  • Difficult ingestion
  • Diarrhea
  • Hardness and pain of the lateral costal region
  • Vomiting
  • Hiccup
  • Acute and chronic gastritis
  • Borborygmus
  • Colic
  • Indigestion
  • Shortness of breath
  • Wheezing
  • Dyspnea with raised shoulders
