POINT: REN-3 (CONCEPTION VESSEL-3)  

NAME: ZHONGJI

English: Middle Pole
Also Known As: Yuan Barrier
Central Pole
Utmost Middle
Middle Summit
Middle Extremity

LOCATION:

  • On the midline of the lower abdomen, 4 cun inferior to the umbilicus and 1 cun superior to the pubic symphysis
  • 4 cun directly below the navel

NEEDLING:

  • Perpendicular insertion, 0.5 to 1 cun.
  • Caution: Deep insertion will penetrate a full Bladder which should therefore be emptied before treatment.
  • Moxibustion is applicable.
  • Straight insertion 1 to 2 cun. Sensation: local distention and soreness possibly extending into the genital region

COMMAND FUNCTIONS:

  • Front-Mu point of the Bladder
  • Meeting point of The REN channel with the Spleen, Liver, Small Intestine, Bladder, Gall Bladder, San Jiao and Kidney channels

ACTIONS:

  • Benefits the Bladder
  • Regulates Qi transformation
  • Drains Damp-Heat (from the Bladder, uterus and genitals)
  • Drains Dampness
  • Treats leukorrhea
  • Benefits the uterus
  • Regulates menstruation
  • Dispels Stagnation
  • Benefits the Lower Jiao
  • Fortifies the Kidneys
  • Promotes urination
  • Assists the transforming function of Qi

CONTRAINDICATIONS:

  • Contraindicated during pregnancy.
  • Do not use this point if there is a major imbalance in the pulses.

NOTES:

  • This point is generally favored for Excess conditions while REN-4 Guanyuan is favored for Deficiency conditions.
  • This point is also indicated for edema.
  • This is a heavy duty moxa point.
  • This is a general local point for Bladder disorders and specifically assists the transforming functions of Qi. It aids in transforming Body Fluids into urine.
  • This is a very powerful point for the testes and pancreas.
  • It is good for yo-yoing pulses.
