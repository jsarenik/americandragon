POINT: PC-7 

NAME: DALING (

English: Great Mound
Also Known As: Big Mound
Big Tomb
Grand Mound
Great Tomb
Great Hill

LOCATION:

  • At the wrist, between the tendons of the palmaris longus and flexor carpi radialis, level with HT-7 Shenmen.
    Note: in the absence of the tendons of the palmaris longus, locate this point on the ulnar side of the tendon of the flexor carpi radialis.
  • In the depression in the middle of the transverse crease of the wrist between the tendons of the palmaris longus and flexor carpi radialis.
  • With the hand supine, this point can be found at the midpoint of the transverse crease of the wrist.

NEEDLING:

  • Perpendicular insertion 0.3 to 0.5 cun.
  • Oblique distal insertion along the carpal tunnel for carpal tunnel syndrome, 0.5 to 1 cun.
  • Caution: the median nerve lies directly beneath this point. Needling commonly produces a significant electric sensation. This is acceptable, but one elicited, further manipulation is inappropriate and may damage the nerve.
  • Perpendicular insertion 0.3 to 0.5 cun, causing local distention and soreness or a numb, electric sensation extending toward the finger tips.
  • Slanted insertion, needle pointed into the Blood vessels of the wrist, for vascular syndrome of the wrist, causing local distention and pain or possibly a numb, electric sensation extending toward the finger tips.
  • Moxibustion is applicable.

COMMAND FUNCTIONS:

  • Shu-Stream point
  • Yuan-Source point
  • Earth point
  • Sedation point
  • Sun-Si-miao Ghost point

ACTIONS:

  • Clears Heat from the Heart
  • Calms the Shen
  • Harmonizes the Stomach and Intestines
  • Unbinds the chest
  • Clears the Ying level
  • Cools the Blood
  • Expands the chest
  • Dispels fullness from the chest
