POINT: REN-13 (CONCEPTION VESSEL-13)  

NAME: SHANGWAN

English: Upper Cavity
Also Known As: Upper Controller Shangguan
Upper Stomach Cavity
Upper Epigastrium
Upper Duct

LOCATION:

  • On the Midline of the abdomen, 5 cun above the umbilicus and 3 cun below the sternocostal angle.

NEEDLING:

  • Perpendicular insertion 0.8 to 1.5 cun.
  • Caution: in thin patients deep needling may penetrate the peritoneal cavity.
  • Straight insertion 1.5 to 2 cun. Sensation: distention and a heavy feeling in the upper abdomen.
  • Puncture perpendicularly 0.5 to 1.2 cun.
  • Moxibustion is applicable.

COMMAND FUNCTIONS:

  • Meeting point with the Stomach channel
  • Meeting point with the Small Intestine channel

ACTIONS:

  • Harmonizes the Stomach
  • Regulates Qi
  • Descends Rebellious Qi
  • Stops vomiting
  • Regulates the Heart

NOTES:

  • This point sits at the top of the Stomach.
  • This is the best point to subdue Rebellious Stomach Qi.
  • It is used mostly in Stomach Excess patterns.
