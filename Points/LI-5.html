POINT: LI-5 (LARGE INTESTINE-5)

NAME: YANGXI

English: Yang Stream
Also Known As: Yang Crook
Valley of Yang

LOCATION:

  • With the thumb extended, this point can be found in the center of the hollow formed between the extensor policis longus and extensor policis brevis.
  • On the radial side of the wrist, in the center of the hollow formed by the extensor policis longus and extensor policis brevis (anatomical snuffbox).

NEEDLING:

  • Straight insertion 0.3 to 1 cun.
  • Perpendicular insertion 0.5 to 1 cun.
  • Puncture perpendicularly 0.3 to 0.5 cun.
  • Moxibustion is applicable.

COMMAND FUNCTIONS:

  • Jing-River point
  • Fire point

ACTIONS:

  • Clears Heat
  • Alleviates pain
  • Clears Yang Ming Fire
  • Calms the Shen
  • Benefits the wrist joint
  • Expels Wind
  • Releases the Exterior
  • Benefits the throat
