POINT: UB-36 (URINARY BLADDER-36)

NAME: CHENGFU

English: Hold and Support
Also Known As: Receive Support

LOCATION:

  • Just below the buttock, on the line directly superior to UB-40 Weizhong, in the center of the transverse gluteal crease, in a depression between the hamstring muscles.
  • On the posterior aspect of the thigh, at the midpoint of the transverse crease below the buttock.
  • On the posterior side of the thigh, at the midpoint of the inferior gluteal crease.
  • Located at the midpoint of the transverse gluteal fold.

NEEDLING:

  • Perpendicular insertion 1 to 2 cun.
  • Straight insertion, 2 to 3 cun, causing local distention and soreness or an electric numb sensation extending down the leg.
  • Puncture perpendicularly 1 to 1.5 cun.
  • Moxibustion is applicable.

COMMAND FUNCTIONS:

ACTIONS:

  • Activates the channel
  • Relaxes the sinews
  • Alleviates pain
  • Regulates the Lower Jiao
  • Treats hemorrhoids

INDICATIONS:

  • Lumbar pain
  • Pain of the sacrum and coccyx
  • Pain of the buttock
  • Sciatica
  • Bi and Wei syndromes of the lower limb
  • Chronic hemorrhoids
  • Bleeding hemorrhoids
  • Retention of urine
  • Constipation
  • Numbness and motor impairment of the lower extremities
  • Difficult defecation
  • Difficult urination
  • Genital pain
  • Seminal emission
  • Cold in the uterus
  • Swelling of the axilla
  • Low back pain
  • Paralysis of the lower extremity
  • Swelling of the coccyx
  • Anal, coccygeal, gluteal, lower back or genital pain and inflammation
