POINT: KI-3 (KIDNEY-3)  

NAME: TAIXI

English: Supreme Stream
Also Known As: Great River
Supreme Brook
Excessive Brook
Lushi
Great Creek
Greater Mountain Stream
Supreme Valley
Reflecting Sea
Bigger Sea
Greater Stream

LOCATION:

  • In the depression between the medial malleolus and the Achilles tendon, level with the prominence of the medial malleolus. Locate in the center of the depression, midway between the prominence of the medial malleolus and the posterior border of the Achilles tendon.
  • In the depression between the tip of the medial malleolus and the Achilles tendon.
  • Located in the depression between the medial malleolus and the tendocalcaneus, level with the vertex of the medial malleolus.

NEEDLING:

  • Perpendicular insertion 0.5 to 1 cun or join to UB-60 Kunlun. Sensation: distention and soreness, possibly a numb sensation extending to the sole of the foot.
  • For pain in the sole of the foot, point the needle slightly toward the medial malleolus, 0.5 to 1 cun. Sensation: an electric, numb sensation extending to the sole of the foot.
  • Puncture perpendicularly0.3 to 0.5 cun.
  • Moxibustion is applicable.

COMMAND FUNCTIONS:

  • Shu-Stream point of the Kidney channel
  • Yuan-Source point of the Kidney channel
  • Earth point of the Kidney channel

NOTES:

  • This is the most important point for treating Yin Deficiency.
  • This is an extremely important point used to tonify any Deficiency of the Kidneys.
  • This point is good for diseases of Nu, a type of alternating chills and fever.
  • This is also said to be a good point for dental pain.
  • This point is good to use if the Heart pulse is deep.
  • This point affects the pancreas.
