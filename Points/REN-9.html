POINT: REN-9 (CONCEPTION VESSEL-9)  

NAME: SHUIFEN

English: Water Separation
Also Known As: Water Divide
Water Part
Water Division
Water Balancer
Portion of Liquids
Water Distribution

LOCATION:

  • 1 cun superior to the umbilicus on the midline.
  • 1 cun directly above the navel.
  • On the midline of the abdomen, 2 cun above the umbilicus and 7 cun below the sternocostal angle.

NEEDLING:

  • Straight insertion 1 to 1.5 cun.
  • Perpendicular insertion 0.5 to 1 cun.
  • Moxibustion is applicable.
  • Perpendicular insertion 0.8 to 1.5 cun.
  • Caution: in thin patients deep needling may penetrate the peritonea cavity.

COMMAND FUNCTIONS:

  • Reunion point of the REN and Lung channels

ACTIONS:

  • Regulates the Spleen, Stomach and Small Intestines
  • Warms Cold
  • Regulates the water pathways
  • Resolves Dampness
  • Promotes the transformation of fluids
  • Treats edema
  • Harmonizes the Intestines
  • Dispels accumulation

INDICATIONS:

  • Nephritis
  • Ascites
  • Edema
  • Anorexia
  • Borborygmus
  • Abdominal pain
  • Coldness and swelling
  • Nausea
  • Vomiting
  • Sweating
  • Ring of pain around the umbilicus
  • Drum distention
  • Stomach and Intestines distended due to Cold or Deficiency
  • Umbilical pains which affect the Heart
  • Hernia
  • Enteritis
  • Stiffness and contraction of the renal region and vertebral column
  • Rectal prolapse
  • Epistaxis
  • Water diseases
  • Vomiting after eating
  • Abdomen swollen and hard
  • Failure of the fontanelle to close
  • Tetany
  • Periumbilical Cold
  • Periumbilical pain
  • Diarrhea
  • Urinary retention
  • Dysuria
  • Lower back pain
  • Palpitations
  • Edema in the lower extremities
  • Dilation of the Stomach
  • Abdomen distended and hard
  • Abdominal spasms
  • Tympanites
  • Cholera
  • Sudden epistaxis
  • Chronic gastric cancer
  • Chronic lesions of the Intestines
  • Sudden subsidence of the anterior fontanelle in children
  • Cramps
  • Depressed fontanelle
  • No pleasure in eating
  • Acid regurgitation
  • Sudden turmoil disorder with cramps
  • Periumbilical pain rushing up to the Heart causing difficulty breathing
  • Contracted sinews

CONTRAINDICATIONS:

  • Contraindicated to needle for those with ascites.
  • Contraindicated for moxibustion during pregnancy.
  • some say this point should not be needled just warmed with moxa.

NOTES:

  • This is a very important point to use whenever there is Dampness, Phlegm or edema.
  • Use this point when you can't harmonize the Bladder and Kidneys.
  • It is good for all Internal secretions.
  • It is good for the fluidity of the mind and of the limbs.
  • It balances the endocrine and lymphatic systems.
  • this is the main water control point in the body.
  • It is located just above where the Small Intestine separates the pure from the turbid.
