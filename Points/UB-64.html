POINT: UB-64 (URINARY BLADDER-64)  

NAME: JINGGU

English: Capital Bone
Also Known As: Central Bone
Citadel of the Bone

LOCATION:

  • On the lateral side of the foot, in the depression anterior and inferior to the tuberosity of the fifth metatarsal bone.
  • Posterior and inferior to the tuberosity of the fifth metatarsal bone on the junction of the red and white skin.
  • On the lateral side of the dorsum of the foot, below the tuberosity of the fifth metatarsal bone at the junction of the red and white skin.

NEEDLING:

  • Perpendicular insertion 0.3 to 0.5 cun.
  • Slanted insertion pointed downward and medially, 0.5 to 1 cun. Sensation: local distention and soreness.
  • Moxibustion is applicable.

COMMAND FUNCTIONS:

  • Yuan-Source point

ACTIONS:

  • Clears the head and eyes
  • Eliminates Wind
  • Calms the Shen
  • Relaxes the sinews
  • Activates the channel
  • Alleviates pain
  • Clears Heat
  • Clears the brain
  • Clears the head
  • Strengthens the back
  • Calms fright
