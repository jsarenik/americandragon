POINT: SJ-10 (SAN JIAO - 10)

NAME: TIANJING

English: Heavenly Well
Also Known As: Celestial Wells

LOCATION:

  • With the elbow flexed, the point is in a depression about 1 cun superior to the olecranon.

NEEDLING:

  • Puncture perpendicularly 0.3 to 0.5 cun.
  • Moxibustion is applicable.
  • Perpendicular insertion 0.5 to 1 cun. Sensation: local distention and soreness

COMMAND FUNCTIONS:

  • Earth point
  • Sedation point
  • He-Sea point

ACTIONS:

  • Relaxes tendons
  • Resolves Dampness and Phlegm
  • Dispels nodules
  • Clears Heat
  • Dispels Stagnation
  • Regulates the Ying and Wei
  • Regulates Qi
  • Descends rebellion
  • Calms the Shen
  • Alleviates pain

CONTRAINDICATIONS:

  • This point is forbidden to needle after the fourth month of pregnancy.

NOTES:

  • This is known as the "serene tranquility point".
  • This is the most neglected point in the whole body.
  • It keeps stability of mind, gives peace and serenity to sick patients.
  • It treats all types of acute pain.
  • It is a heavenly well of energy that we can reach into in times of drought. It can completely recharge the patient.
  • For disorders of the San Jiao, use the Lower He-Sea point of the San Jiao, UB-39 Weiyang.
  • This is a special point for scrofula.
