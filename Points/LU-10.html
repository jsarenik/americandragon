POINT: LU-10 (LUNG-10)  

NAME: YUJI

English: Fish Border
Also Known As: Fish Line
Fish Region

LOCATION:

  • On the radial side of the midpoint of the first metacarpal bone, on the junction of the red and white skin.
  • On the thenar eminence of the hand, in a depression between the midpoint of the shaft of the first metacarpal bone and the thenar muscles.
    Note: locate and needle close to the metacarpal bone.
  • With the palm facing up, this point can be found at the midpoint of the thenar surface of the first metacarpus, at the border separating the light colored skin of the palm from the darker colored skin of the back of the hand.

NEEDLING:

  • Perpendicular insertion 0.5 to 1 cun.
  • Puncture perpendicularly 0.5 to 0.8 cun.
  • Moxibustion is applicable.
  • Straight insertion, 0.5 to 1 cun, causing local distention and soreness.

COMMAND FUNCTIONS:

  • Ying-Spring point
  • Fire point

ACTIONS:

  • Cools Heat in the Lungs
  • Benefits the throat
  • Descends Rebellious Qi
  • Harmonizes the Stomach and Heart

CONTRAINDICATIONS:

  • Some sources say that this point is forbidden to moxa.

NOTES:

  • This is the main point to clear Lung Full Heat.
  • If the skin is cold, silvery and flaky, tonify.
  • If the skin is hot, ugly and red, reduce.
  • This point is used if the patient is very rigid in his outlook and needs warmth.
