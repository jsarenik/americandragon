POINT: SJ-22 (SAN JIAO-22)

NAME: ERHELIAO

English: Ear Harmony Crevice
Also Known As: Harmony's Seam

LOCATION:

  • Anterior and superior to SJ-21 Ermen, at the level of the root of the auricle, on the posterior border of the hairline of the temple where the superficial temporal artery passes.
  • Approximately 0.5 cun, anterior to the upper border of the root of the ear, in a slight depression on the posterior border of the hairline of the temple.
    Note: The point lies just posterior to where the superficial temporal artery can be palpated.
  • Above and superior to SJ-21 Ermen, at the natural hairline.

NEEDLING:

  • Puncture obliquely 0.1 to 0.3 cun. Avoid puncturing the artery.
  • Moxibustion is applicable.
  • Transverse insertion 0.3 to 0.5 cun.

COMMAND FUNCTIONS:

  • Meeting point with the Gall Bladder and Small Intestine channels

ACTIONS:

  • Expels Wind
  • Alleviates pain

INDICATIONS:

  • Migraine
  • Tinnitus
  • Headache
  • Head Wind
  • Swelling and pain of the tip of the nose
  • Clonic spasm
  • Facial paralysis
  • Lockjaw
  • Pain and heaviness of the head
  • Nasal discharge
  • Deviation of the mouth
  • Swelling of the submandibular region and neck
