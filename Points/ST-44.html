POINT: ST-44 (STOMACH-44)  

NAME: NEITING

English: Inner Courtyard
Also Known As: Inner Yard
Inner Court

LOCATION:

  • Proximal to the web margin between the second and third toes, in the depression distal and lateral to the second metatarsal joint.
  • At the proximal end of the web between the second and third toes.
  • On the dorsum of the foot, between the second and third toes, 0.5 cun proximal to the margin of the web.

NEEDLING:

  • Puncture perpendicularly 0.3 to 0.5 cun.
  • Moxibustion is applicable.
  • Slanted insertion toward the ankle, 0.3 to 0.8 cun, causing local distention and soreness.
  • Perpendicular insertion 0.5 cun,
  • Oblique insertion directed proximally, 0.5 to 1 cun.

COMMAND FUNCTIONS:

  • Ying-Spring point
  • Water point
  • Ma Dan Ying Heavenly Star point

ACTIONS:

  • Clears Heat
  • Eliminates fullness
  • Regulates Qi
  • Harmonizes the Intestines
  • Clears Damp-Heat
  • Calms the Shen
  • Stops pain
  • Promotes digestion
  • Eliminates Wind from the face
  • Cools and drains Heat from the Stomach
  • Clears the Stomach
  • Relieves fullness in the Stomach
  • Removes obstruction for the Intestines

CONTRAINDICATIONS:

NOTES:

  • This is a very important point on the Stomach channel, for clearing Heat.
  • It is mostly used for Excess patterns.
  • This is probably the best point on the Stomach channel for abdominal disorders, especially when accompanied by pain and fear.
  • This is also a special point for Food Retention in the Stomach.
  • This point is good for true Heat with false Cold (warm body with cold limbs).
