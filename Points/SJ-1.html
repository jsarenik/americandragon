POINT: SJ-1 (SAN JIAO-1)  

NAME:GUANCHONG

English: Rushing Pass
Also Known As: Gate Rushing
Barrier Rushing
Gate Thoroughfare
Rushing the Frontier Gate
Gate of Assault
Gate's Pouring
Gate Rush
Gate's Rushing

LOCATION:

  • On the ulnar side if the ring finger, about 0.1 cun posterior to the corner of the nail.
  • On the dorsal aspect of the ring finger, at the junction of the lines drawn along the ulnar border of the nail and the base of the nail, approximately 0.1 cun from the corner of the nail.

NEEDLING:

  • Perpendicular or oblique insertion directed proximally, 0.1 to 0.2 cun, or prick to bleed.
  • Straight insertion, 0.1 to 0.3 cun, or prick to bleed.
  • Puncture superficially o.1 cun or prick with a three-edged needle to cause bleeding.
  • Moxibustion is applicable.
  • Bleed to clear Heat from the eyes, head and throat.

COMMAND FUNCTIONS:

  • Jing-Well point
  • Metal point
  • Entry point

ACTIONS:

  • Clears Upper Jiao Heat
  • Benefits the ears and tongue
  • Activates the channel
  • Alleviates pain
  • Expels Wind
  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Restores consciousness
  • Stops convulsions

CONTRAINDICATIONS:

NOTES:

  • This point has a special effect on flesh over the eyes.
  • This point deals with regulation of Heat.
  • It is used mostly in Exterior patterns of extreme Wind-Heat.
  • It is a resuscitation point.
  • This is the gate of entry, if this gate is closed the San Jiao will fail and all of the organs will fail.
