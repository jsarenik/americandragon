POINT: LU-3 (LUNG-3)

NAME: TIANFU

English: Palace of Heaven
Also Known As: Heavenly Hall
Heavenly Palace
Heavenly Storehouse
Celestial Palace
Heavenly Spot
Heavenly Mansion
Heavenly Residence
Heaven's Residence

LOCATION:

  • On the medial aspect of the upper arm, 3 cun the end of the axillary fold on the radial side of the m. biceps brachii.
    Note: this point is located where the nose touches the arm.
  • On the anterio-lateral aspect of the upper arm, 3 cun inferior to the axillary fold and 6 cun superior to LU-5 Chize, in the depression between the lateral border of the biceps brachii muscle and the shaft of the humerus.
    Note: divide the distance between the axillary fold and the cubital crease of the elbow into thirds. This point is at the junction of the upper and middle thirds.

NEEDLING:

  • Puncture perpendicularly 0.5 to 1 cun.
  • Perpendicular insertion 0.5 to 1 cun.
  • Straight insertion 1 to 1.5 cun.

COMMAND FUNCTIONS:

  • Window of Heaven (Window of the Sky) point

ACTIONS:

  • Clears Lung Heat
  • Descends Lung Qi
  • Cools the Blood
  • Stops bleeding
  • Calms the corporeal Soul (Po)

INDICATIONS:

  • External invasion
  • Excessive euphoria
  • Eye problems
  • Claustrophobia
  • Agoraphobia
  • Forgetfulness

  • Sudden and severe thirst

CONTRAINDICATIONS:

  • According to several classical texts, moxibustion is contraindicated on this point as it can cause counterflow and disordered Qi.

NOTES:

  • This point opens the Spirit and ancestral energy.
  • It has a powerful psychological effect on all emotional problems related to the Lungs.
  • This point can be used as a first aid point for carbon monoxide poisoning.
  • This point is appropriate for Liver Fire Attacking the Lungs.
  • Floating-corpse ghost talk may refer to delirious speech seen in the terminal stages of pulmonary tuberculosis.
