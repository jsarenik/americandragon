POINT: SJ-16 (SAN JIAO-16)

NAME: TIANYOU

English: Window of Heaven
Also Known As: Heaven's Window

LOCATION:

  • On the lateral side of the neck,directly below the posterior border of the mastoid process on the level of the mandibular angle, and on the posterior border of the sternocleidomastoid muscle.
  • On the posterior border of the sternocleidomastoid muscle, level with the angle of the mandible, approximately 1 cun inferior to GB-12 Wangu.
  • Posterior and inferior to the mastoid process at the posterior at the posterior border of the sternocleidomastoid muscle, near the hairline.

NEEDLING:

  • Puncture perpendicularly 0.3 to 0.5 cun.
  • Moxibustion is applicable.
  • Perpendicular insertion 0.5 to 1 cun. Sensation: local distention and soreness.

COMMAND FUNCTIONS:

  • Window of Heaven point

ACTIONS:

  • Benefits the head and sense organs
  • Regulates and descends qi

INDICATIONS:

  • Headache
  • Neck rigidity
  • Facial swelling
  • Blurred vision
  • Sudden deafness
  • Loss of sense of smell
  • Stuffy nose
  • Confused dreaming
  • Stiffness and pain of the neck with inability to turn the head
  • Swelling of the supraclavicular fossa
  • Tinnitus
  • Deafness
  • Sore eyes
  • Excessive dreaming
  • Impaired hearing
  • Dimness of vision
  • Pain of the eyes with inability to see
  • Lacrimation
  • Rhinitis with nosebleed
  • Wind dizziness
  • Head Wind
  • Swelling of the submandibular region
  • Throat Bi
  • Scrofula
  • Breast swelling
  • Malaria
  • Sore throat
  • Constricted throat

NOTES:

  • This point, along with the other Window of Heaven points, is able to regulate and balance the Qi between the head and body.
  • It is used for inversion Qi (chaotic and rebellious Qi rushing to the head).
