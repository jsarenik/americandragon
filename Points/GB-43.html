POINT: GB-43 (GALL BLADDER-43)

NAME: XIAXI

English: Clamped Stream
Also Known As: Gallantry's Stream
Stream Insertion
Valiant Stream
Bold Ravine
Harmonious Stream
Reserve Creek
Brave Stream

LOCATION:

  • 0.5 cun posterior to the web margin between the fourth and fifth toes.
  • Between the fourth toe and the little toe, 0.5 cun proximal to the margin of the web.

NEEDLING:

  • Perpendicular insertion 0.3 to 0.5 cun.
  • Oblique insertion directed proximally, 0.5 to 1 cun.
  • On the dorsum of the foot, between the fourth and fifth toes, proximal to the margin of the web at the junction of the red and white skin.
  • Moxibustion is applicable.

COMMAND FUNCTIONS:

  • Ying-Spring point
  • Water point
  • Tonification point

ACTIONS:

  • Subdues Liver Yang
  • Benefits the ears
  • Resolves Damp-Heat
  • Reduces swelling
  • Clears Heat
  • Benefits the head, ears and eyes
