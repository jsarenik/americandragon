POINT: SI-16 (SMALL INTESTINE-16)

NAME: TIANCHUANG

English: Heavenly Window
Also Known As: Heaven's Window

LOCATION:

  • On the posterior border of the sternocleidomastoid muscle, level with the laryngeal prominence.
  • 3.5 cun lateral to the laryngeal prominence, at the posterior margin of the sternocleidomastoid muscle, 0.5 cun behind LI-18 Futu.

NEEDLING:

  • Perpendicular insertion 0.5 to 0.8 cun.
  • Straight insertion, 0.5 to 1 cun.
  • Puncture perpendicularly 0.3 to 0.7 cun.
  • Moxibustion is applicable.

COMMAND FUNCTIONS:

  • Window of Heaven (Window of the Sky) point

ACTIONS:

  • Benefits the throat and voice
  • Regulates Qi
  • Calms the Shen
  • Activates the channel
  • Alleviates pain
  • Clears Heat

INDICATIONS:

  • Deafness
  • Tinnitus
  • Ear pain
  • Throat pain
  • Swelling and pain of the cheek
  • Heat sensation of the skin of the face
  • Urticaria
  • Shoulder pain radiating to the neck
  • Shoulder pain leading to stiffness of the neck with inability to turn the head
  • Goiter
  • Sudden loss of voice
  • Sudden loss of voice following Windstroke
  • Hoarseness due to Cold in the throat
  • Lockjaw
  • Clenched teeth from Windstroke
  • Headache
  • Manic ghost talk
  • Mania-depression
  • Slowness and lethargy
  • Neck pain
