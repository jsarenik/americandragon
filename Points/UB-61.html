POINT: UB-61 (URINARY BLADDER-61)  

NAME: PUCAN

English: Servant's Respect
Also Known As: Serve and Consult
Pushen
 Servant's Partaking
Servant's Aide
Official's Aide

LOCATION:

  • 1.5 cun below UB-60 Kunlun.
  • Posterior and inferior to the external malleolus, directly below UB-60 Kunlun, lateral to the calcaneum at the junction of the red and white skin.
  • Located 1.5 cun inferior to UB-60 Kunlun, in the depression posterior and inferior to the lateral malleolus.
  • On the lateral side of the foot, 1.5 cun inferior to UB-60 Kunlun, in a tender depression on the calcaneum.
    Note: the distance between the lateral malleolus and the sole of the foot is 3 cun. Locate this point midway between UB-60 Kunlun and the sole.

NEEDLING:

  • Straight insertion 0.3 to 0.5 cun.
  • Puncture perpendicularly 0.3 to 0.5 cun.
  • Moxibustion is applicable.
  • Transverse insertion 0.3 to 0.5 cun.

COMMAND FUNCTIONS:

  • Meeting point of the Yang Qiao channel

ACTIONS:

  • Releases the sinews
  • Activates the channel
  • Alleviates pain

INDICATIONS:

  • Lower back and leg pain
  • Pain at the bottom of the foot
  • Paralysis of the lower limb
  • Beriberi
  • Knee inflammation
  • Atrophy of the foot and lower leg muscles
  • Drop foot
  • Headache
  • A heavy head
  • Heaviness of the head like a stone
  • Leg Qi
  • Sudden turmoil disorder with cramps
  • Swelling of the knee
  • All problems of the lower extremities
  • Cannot raise the knee
  • Muscular atrophy
  • Weakness of the extremities
  • Pain in the heel
  • Seizures
  • Muscular tetany
  • Convulsions
  • Muscle spasms due to severe vomiting and diarrhea
  • Mania
  • Manic raving
  • Seeing ghosts
  • Loss of consciousness
  • Childhood epilepsy
  • Vomiting
  • Turbid, painful urinary dysfunction
  • Fainting
