POINT: LU-9 (LUNG-9)  

NAME: TAIYUAN

English: Supreme Abyss
Also Known As: Great Abyss
Very Great Abyss
Extreme Abyss
Bigger Abyss
Maximum Whirlpool
Great Gulf

LOCATION:

  • At the wrist, in the depression between the radial artery and the tendon of the abductor policis longus, level with HT-7 Shenmen.
  • At the transverse crease of the wrist, in the depression on the radial side of the radial artery.
  • With the palm facing up, this point can be found in a slight depression at the radial end of the transverse crease of the wrist.

NEEDLING:

  • Perpendicular insertion, 0.3 to 0.5 cun, avoiding the radial artery.
  • Straight insertion, 0.3 to 0.5 cun. causing local distention and pain.
  • Puncture perpendicularly 0.2 to 0.3 cun. Avoid puncturing the radial artery.
  • Moxibustion is applicable.

COMMAND FUNCTIONS:

  • Shu-Stream point
  • Yuan-Source point
  • Earth point
  • Meeting point of the Vessels
  • Tonification point

ACTIONS:

  • Tonifies the Lungs
  • Tonifies the Lung Qi and Yin
  • Tonifies Gathering Qi
  • Transforms Phlegm
  • Promotes the descending function of the Lungs
  • Regulates and harmonizes the one hundred vessels
  • Activates the channels
  • Alleviates pain
  • Eliminates Wind
  • Resolves Lung Phlegm
  • Regulates the Lungs
  • Stops coughing
  • Promotes the circulation of Blood
  • Clears Lung and Liver Heat

NOTES:

  • This is the single most important point on the Lung channel to tonify Lung Qi and Yin.
  • This point is used for long-term problems.
  • This point is used for all disorders of Qi.
  • This is a first aid point for asphyxia, collapse, poisoning and concussion.
  • This point is good when the pulses are chaotic or not rhythmical or regular.
