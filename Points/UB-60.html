POINT: UB-60 (URINARY BLADDER-60)

NAME: KUNLUN

English: Kunlun Mountains
Also Known As: Older Brother
Storehouse

LOCATION:

  • In the depression between the tip of the external malleolus and the Achilles tendon.
    Note: locate in the center of the depression.

NEEDLING:

  • Puncture perpendicularly 0.5 to 1 cun.
  • Moxibustion is applicable.
  • Straight insertion, either joined to KI-3 Taixi or inclined slightly toward the lateral malleolus, 0.5 to 1 cun. Sensation: local distention and soreness extending toward the little toe.
  • Slanted insertion, needle directed upward or joined to UB-59 Fuyang for treating goiter. Sensation: local distention and soreness, possibly extending to the heel or toe.
  • Perpendicular insertion directed superiorly to join with KI-3 Taixi, 1.5 to 2 cun.

COMMAND FUNCTIONS:

  • Jing- River point
  • Fire point
  • Ma Dan-yang Heavenly Star point

ACTIONS:

  • Disperses Wind
  • Descends Excess
  • Clears the channel
  • Alleviates pain
  • Relaxes the sinews and muscles
  • Benefits the lower back
  • Clears Heat
  • Lowers Yang
  • Invigorates the Blood
  • Promotes labor

CONTRAINDICATIONS:

  • Contraindicated during pregnancy.

NOTES:

  • This is an important point to clear Excess Wind, Fire and Yang from the upper part of the body.
  • This is a key point for testing heroine and cocaine addiction, it will be tender.
  • Kunlun is a mountain range near tibet. It is also a name for Mount Everest and a name for Chinese Paradise - the source of enlightenment.
  • This point stimulates the pituitary gland and the testes.
  • When using this point, you are contacting the persons Spirit with the essence and quality of Fire and warming it.
  • This point is better for chronic rather than acute back pain and better for Deficiency than Excess.
  • One source says to use UB-60 Kunlun for the upper back and UB-40 Weizhong for the lower back.
