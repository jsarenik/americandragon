POINT: UB-67 (URINARY BLADDER-67)  

NAME: ZHIYIN

English: Reaching Yin
Also Known As: End of Yin
Extremity of Yin

LOCATION:

  • On the dorsal aspect of the little toe, at the junction of the lines drawn along the lateral border of the nail and the base of the nail, approximately 0.1 cun from the corner of the nail.
  • Located about 0.1 cun from the lateral corner of the fifth toenail.
  • On the little toe, 0.1 cun from the lower lateral corner of the cuticle of the toenail.
  • On the lateral side of the small toe, about 0.1 cun from the corner of the nail.

NEEDLING:

  • Perpendicular or oblique insertion directed proximally 0.1 to 0.2 cun, causing local pain or prick to bleed.
  • Puncture superficially 0.1 cun.
  • Moxibustion is applicable.

COMMAND FUNCTIONS:

  • Jing-Well point
  • Metal point
  • Tonification point
  • Exit point

ACTIONS:

  • Expels Wind
  • Clears the head and eyes
  • Clears the brain
  • Turns the fetus
  • Facilitates labor
  • Regulates pregnancy and childbirth
  • Removes obstructions from the channel
  • Invigorates the Blood

CONTRAINDICATIONS:

  • Needling is contraindicated during pregnancy.

NOTES:

  • This point clears fullness and Heat from the opposite end of the channel.
  • It is most effective for acute conditions.
  • It relieves pain anywhere in the body.
  • It is best known for its ability to reposition the fetus (used with moxa - once the fetus has turned, discontinue moxa).
  • This is a general equilibrium point.
  • This point affects all hormones in general, but especially the ovaries.
  • It rules all disorders of the head and face.
