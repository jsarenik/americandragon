POINT: ST-43 (STOMACH-43)  

NAME: XIANGU

English: Sunken Valley
Also Known As: Sinking Valley
Depth of the Valley
Deep Valley

LOCATION:

  • In the depression distal to the junction of the second and third metatarsal bones.
  • Between the second and third metatarsal bones, directly above the lateral side of the second toe.
  • On the dorsum of the foot, between the second and third metatarsal bones, in a depression 1 cun proximal to ST-44 Neiting.

NEEDLING:

  • Puncture perpendicularly 0.3 to 0.5 cun.
  • Moxibustion is applicable.
  • Slanted insertion, 0.1 to 0.3 cun.
  • Perpendicular or oblique insertion 0.5 to 1 cun.

COMMAND FUNCTIONS:

  • Shu-Stream point
  • Wood point

ACTIONS:

  • Eliminates Wind and Heat
  • Removes obstruction from the channel
  • Regulates the Spleen
  • Dispels Edema
  • Regulates and harmonizes the Stomach and Intestines

INDICATIONS:

  • Facial edema
  • General edema
  • Abdominal pain
  • Borborygmus
  • Swelling and pain of the dorsum of the foot
  • Choking
  • Ascites
  • Flatulence
  • Hernia
  • Nightsweats
  • Trigeminal neuralgia
  • Pain and swelling of the eyes
  • Abdominal distention and fullness
  • Fullness of the chest and lateral costal regions
  • Conjunctivitis
  • Hysteria
  • Suffocation
  • Chest disorders
  • Chronic coughing that doesn't stop
  • Ocular congestion
  • Wind-Heat Bi
  • Epigastric pain
  • Seizures
  • Tonsillitis
  • Generalized muscular aches
  • Heel pain
  • Fever without sweat
  • Cold shivering with malarial disorders
  • Difficulty flexing and extending the toes
  • Dropped foot
