POINT: KI-10 (KIDNEY-10)

NAME: YINGU

English: Yin Valley
Also Known As: Yin's Valley
Valley of Yin

LOCATION:

  • At the medial end of the popliteal crease, between the semitendinosus and semimembranosis tendons. Locate and needle with the knee slightly bent.
  • On the medial aspect of the back of the knee, between the semitendinosus muscle and the lower end of the semimembranosis muscle.
  • With the knee flexed, this point is located on the medial side of the popliteal fossa, level with UB-40 Weizhong, between the tendons of the semitendinosus and and semimembranosis muscles.

NEEDLING:

  • Perpendicular insertion 1 to 1.5 cun.
  • Straight insertion, 1 to 2 cun.
  • Puncture perpendicularly 0.8 to 1cun.
  • Moxibustion is applicable.

COMMAND FUNCTIONS:

  • He-Sea point
  • Water point

ACTIONS:

  • Clears Damp-Heat from the Lower Jiao
  • Benefits the Kidneys
  • Activates the channel
  • Alleviates pain
  • Dispels Heat
  • Regulates and soothes the Lower Jiao
  • Expels Dampness from the Lower jiao
  • Tonifies Kidney Yin
