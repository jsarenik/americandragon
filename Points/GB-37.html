POINT: GB-37 (GALL BLADDER-37)  

NAME: GUANGMING

English: Bright Light
Also Known As: Shiny and Bright
Bright and Clear
Brightness

LOCATION:

  • Five cun above the lateral malleolus of the ankle, at the anterior margin of the fibula.
  • 5 cun directly above the tip of the external malleolus on the anterior border of the fibula.

NEEDLING:

  • Straight insertion 1 to 1.5 cun. Sensation: soreness extending upward to the knee and downward to the top of the foot.
  • Puncture perpendicularly 0.7 to 1 cun.
  • Moxibustion is applicable.

COMMAND FUNCTIONS:

  • Luo-Connecting point

ACTIONS:

  • Regulates the Liver
  • Clears the vision
  • Benefits the eyes
  • Dispels Wind-Damp
  • Activates the channel
  • Alleviates pain
  • Clears Heat
  • Conducts Fire downward

INDICATIONS:

  • Night blindness
  • Optic nerve atrophy
  • Cataract
  • Migraine headache
  • Pain along the lateral aspect of the calf
  • Knee pain
  • Motor impairment and pain of the lower extremities
  • Blurred vision
  • Ophthalmalgia
  • Distending pain of the breast
  • Pain and lassitude of the legs
  • Difficulty staying upright
  • Agitated madness

  • Numbness of the whole body
