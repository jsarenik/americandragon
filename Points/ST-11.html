POINT: ST-11 (STOMACH-11)

NAME: QISHE

English: Abode of Qi
Also Known As: Cottage
Qi's Residence

LOCATION:

  • At the root of the neck, superior to the medial end of the clavicle, directly below, ST-9 Renying, in the depression between the sternal and clavicular heads of the sternocleidomastoid muscle.
  • At the superior border of the sternal extremity of the clavicle, between the sternal and clavicular heads of the sternocleidomastoid muscle.

NEEDLING:

  • Perpendicular insertion 0.3 to 0.5 cun.
  • Caution: deep insertion may puncture the Lung.
  • Moxibustion is applicable.

COMMAND FUNCTIONS:

ACTIONS:

  • Benefits the throat and neck
  • Descends Qi

INDICATIONS:

  • Swelling and pain of the throat
  • Scrofula
  • Goiter
  • Stiffness of the neck with inability to turn the head
  • A sense of poverty
  • The mind has gone barren
  • Dyspnea
  • Difficult ingestion
  • Hiccup
  • Sore throat
  • Pain and rigidity of the neck
  • Asthma
  • Pharyngitis
