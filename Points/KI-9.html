POINT: KI-9 (KIDNEY-9)

NAME: ZHUBIN

English: Guest House
Also Known As: House Guest
Guest Building
Building for the Guest
Building Guest
Building Bank
Construction on the Shore
Building a Great Chamber

LOCATION:

  • On the medial aspect of the lower leg, 5 cun superior to KI-3 Taixi, on the line drawn between KI-3 Taixi and KI-10 Yingu, about 1 cun posterior to the medial border of the tibia.
  • Locate at the junction of the lower third and upper two thirds of the distance between KI-3 Taixi and KI-10 Yingu, at the level of LIV-5 Ligou.
  • 5 cun directly above KI-3 Taixi, at the lower end of the belly of the gastrocnemius muscle, on the line drawn between KI-3 Taixi and KI-10 Yingu.
  • About 5 cun above KI-3 Taixi and 2 cun posterior to the medial margin of the tibia.

NEEDLING:

  • Perpendicular insertion 1 to 1.5 cun.
  • Puncture perpendicularly 0.5 to 0.7 cun.
  • Moxibustion is applicable.
  • Straight insertion 1 to 2 cun, causing soreness and numbness extending upward to the thigh and downward to the bottom of the foot.

COMMAND FUNCTIONS:

  • Xi -Cleft point of the Yin Wei channel
  • Point on the Yin Qiao channel

ACTIONS:

  • Clears the Heart
  • Transforms Phlegm
  • Regulates Qi
  • Alleviates pain
  • Calms the mind
  • Tonifies Kidney Yin
  • Opens the chest
  • Regulates the Yin Wei channel

INDICATIONS:

  • Madness
  • Mania
  • Mania-depression disorder
  • Raving fury, abuse, drooling and cursing
  • Vomiting of foamy or watery saliva
  • Tongue thrusting
  • Goiter
  • Hernia
  • Nephritis
  • Cystitis
  • Pelvic inflammatory disease
  • Pain along the medial aspect of the lower leg and knee
  • Colic
  • General weakness, especially of the legs
  • Depression
  • Schizophrenia
  • Drug poisoning
  • Shan disorder
  • Umbilical Shan disorder in infants
  • Pain of the medial aspect of the leg
  • Contraction of the calf muscle
  • Weakness of the legs
  • Mental disorders
  • Pain in the foot and lower leg
  • Seizures
  • Psychosis
  • Spasm of the gastrocnemius muscle
  • Insanity
  • Palpitations
  • Orchitis
  • Twitching and weakness of the legs at night in the elderly
  • Spasm pf the soleus muscle
  • Absence of milk
  • Swollen tongue
  • Decline of vitality

CONTRAINDICATIONS:

NOTES:

  • This point helps to clear Phlegm-Fire from the Heart.
  • It is an excellent to calm the mind.
  • It is also indicated for Disharmony between the Heart and Kidneys.
  • This point generates milk for nursing mothers.
  • This is an elimination point for releasing poisons associated with fluids (i.e. drugs). It is considered the main detoxification point for the body.
  • A gold needle at 3rd and sixth month of pregnancy will strengthen the fetus.
  • This point will be tender when venereal disease is present.
