POINT: DU-1 (GOVERNING VESSEL-1)  

NAME: CHANGQIANG

English: Long Strong
Also Known As: Long Strength
Grow Strong
Lasting strength

LOCATION:

  • This point is located midway between the coccyx and the anus.
  • This point is located on the midline, midway between the coccyx and the anus.

NEEDLING:

  • Perpendicular insertion 0.5 to 1 cun.
  • Moxibustion is applicable.
  • Straight insertion between the coccyx and rectum, 0.5 to 1cun. Sensation: local distention and soreness extending to the anus.

COMMAND FUNCTIONS:

  • Luo-connecting point
  • Meeting points of the Conception vessel, Gall Bladder channel and Kidney channel

ACTIONS:

  • Opens the DU channel and REN channel
  • Regulates the Intestines
  • Resolves Damp-Heat
  • Calms the Mind
  • Calms the Shen`
  • Treats hemorrhoids
  • Benefits the two lower Yin
  • Activates the channel
  • Alleviates pain
