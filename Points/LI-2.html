POINT: LI-2 (LARGE INTESTINE-2)

NAME: ERJIAN

English: Second Space
Also Known As: Between Two
Second Interval
Second Secretion
Second Instance

LOCATION:

  • With the finger slightly flexed, this point is located on the redial side of the second finger distal to the metacarpalphalangeal joint at the junction of the red and white skin.
  • In the depression anterior to the metacarpalphalangeal joint of the index finger on the lateral side.
  • On the radial border of the index finger, in a depression just distal to the metacarpalphalangeal joint.

NEEDLING:

  • Straight insertion, 0.3 cun.
  • Oblique proximal or distal insertion, 0.2 to 0.3 cun.
  • Perpendicular-oblique insertion towards the pain, 0.5 cun.
  • Puncture perpendicularly 0.2 to 0.3 cun.
  • Moxibustion is applicable.

COMMAND FUNCTIONS:

  • Ying-Spring point
  • Water point
  • Sedation point

ACTIONS:

  • Expels Wind
  • Clears Heat
  • Reduces swelling
  • Alleviates pain

INDICATIONS:

  • Hemiplegia
  • Facial paralysis
  • Trigeminal neuralgia
  • Fever
  • Somnolence
  • Toothache
  • Headache
  • Blurred vision
  • Epistaxis
  • Shoulder and back pain and stiffness
  • Cloudy vision
  • Eye diseases
  • Yellow eyes
  • Injury by Cold with water binding the chest and lateral costal region
  • Propensity to fright
  • Dry stools
  • Edema
  • Intestinal spasm
  • Tonsillitis
  • Dry mouth

  • Constipation
  • Abdominal pain
  • Diarrhea
  • Sweating
  • Inner canthus troubles

CONTRAINDICATIONS:

  • Some sources say that this point is forbidden during the ninth month of pregnancy.

NOTES:

  • This point calms the abdomen.
  • This point helps to control the balance between water and metal and is the best point for dry, hard constipation.
  • This point is good for spastic colon.
