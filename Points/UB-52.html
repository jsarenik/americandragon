POINT: UB-52 (URINARY BLADDER-52)  

NAME: ZHISHI

English: Residence of the Will
Also Known As: Will's Chamber
Will Power Room

LOCATION:

  • 3 cun lateral to the lower border of the spinous process of the second lumbar vertebra.
  • 3 cun lateral to the midline, level with the lower border of the spinous process of the second lumbar vertebra and level with UB-23 Shenshu.
  • Note: when the shoulder is relaxed, the 3 cun line corresponds to the medial border of the scapula.
  • 3 cun lateral to DU-4 Mingmen, at the level of the lower border of the spinous process of the second lumbar vertebra.

NEEDLING:

  • Straight insertion, 1.5 to 2 cun.
  • Transverse insertion, pointed toward UB-23 Shenshu, 2 to 3 cun for soft tissue injury to the low back or nephroptosis. Sensation: local distention and soreness, sometimes extending to the buttocks.
  • Caution: Care should be taken not to insert the needle too deeply to avoid puncturing the Kidneys.
  • Oblique insertion 0.5 to 1 cun.
  • Puncture perpendicularly 0.5 to 1 cun.
  • Moxibustion is applicable.

COMMAND FUNCTIONS:

ACTIONS:

  • Tonifies the Kidneys
  • Benefits the Jing
  • Regulates urination
  • Strengthens the lumbar region
  • Strengthens the back
  • Reinforces Kidney Qi
  • Reinforces the will power

INDICATIONS:

  • Painful urinary dysfunction
  • Infertility
  • Impotence
  • Prostatitis
  • Nephritis
  • Anemia
  • Edema
  • Nausea
  • Spasmodic pain in the hypochondrium
  • Spermatorrhea
  • Indigestion
  • Seminal emission with dreams
  • Fullness and pain of the lateral costal region
  • Hardness of the abdomen and hypogastrium
  • Vomiting
  • Epigastric fullness and pain
  • Lower back pain and stiffness
  • Swelling of the perineum
  • Swelling of the sex organs
  • Scrotal eczema
  • Nocturnal emissions
  • Urinary tract infection
  • Urinary incontinence
  • Dysuria
  • Diarrhea
  • Paralysis of the lower limb
  • Back pain
  • Dribbling urination
  • Sudden turmoil disorder
  • Difficult defecation
  • Pain in the back and knee
