POINT: DU-12 (GOVERNING VESSEL-12)  

NAME: SHENZHU

English: Body Pillar
Also Known As: Body's Pillar

LOCATION:

  • On the midline of the upper back, in the depression below the spinous process of the third thoracic vertebra.
  • Between the spinous processes of the third and fourth thoracic vertebra.
  • Inferior to the spinous process of the third thoracic vertebra on the posterior midline.

NEEDLING:

  • Perpendicular-oblique superior insertion 0.5 to 1 cun.
  • Caution: the spinal canal lies between 1.25 and 1.75 cun deep to the skin surface, varying according to body type.
  • Straight insertion pointed slightly upward, 0.7 to 1 cun. Sensation: distention and soreness or heavy, sinking feeling toward the lower back.
  • Moxibustion is applicable.

COMMAND FUNCTIONS:

ACTIONS:

  • Pacifies Wind in the DU channel (reducing method)
  • Eliminates Interior Wind (reducing method)
  • Calms spasms (reducing method)
  • Tonifies Lung Qi (reinforcing method)
  • Disperses Lung Qi
  • Strengthens the body (reinforcing method)
  • Expands and relaxes the chest
  • Calms the Shen
  • Clears Heat
  • Dispels Wind-Heat
