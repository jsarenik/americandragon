POINT: UB-30 (URINARY BLADDER-30) 

NAME: BAIHUANSHU

English: White Ring Shu
Also Known As: White Ring Hollow
White Ring Transporting Point
White Circle's Hollow

LOCATION:

  • 1.5 cun lateral to the midline at the level of the fourth posterior sacral foramen UB-34 Bailiao.

NEEDLING:

  • Perpendicular insertion 0.5 to 1 cun.
  • Straight insertion 1 to 2 cun. Sensation: local distention and numbness extending to the buttocks.
  • Puncture perpendicularly 0.8 to 1.2 cun.

COMMAND FUNCTIONS:

ACTIONS:

  • Benefits the lumbar region and legs
  • Regulates menstruation
  • Stops leukorrhea and seminal emission

  • Pain of the lumbar spine and sacrum
  • Lumbar pain due to Cold

CONTRAINDICATIONS:

  • Some classical sources forbid moxibustion on this point.

NOTES:

  • This is primarily a local point to activate Qi circulation and treat pain of the lumbar region, hip, coccyx and sacrum.
  • It is especially indicated to stop leakage of semen, leukorrhea and white, turbid urine.
