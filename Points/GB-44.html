POINT: GB-44 (GALL BLADDER-44)  

NAME: ZUQIAOYIN

English: Yin Portals of the Foot
Also Known As: Yin Cavity on the Foot
Foot's Yin Aperture
Foot Hole Yin
Cavity of Yin
Foot Extreme Yin
Foot Opening Yin
Foot Orifice Yin
Foot Cavity of Yin

LOCATION:

  • About 0.1 cun from the lateral corner of the fourth toenail.
  • On the dorsal aspect of the fourth toe, at the junction of the lines drawn along the lateral border of the nail and the base of the nail, approximately 0.1 cun from the corner of the nail.

NEEDLING:

  • Straight insertion, 0.1 to 0.2 cun.
  • Puncture superficially about 0.1 cun.
  • Moxibustion is applicable.
  • Perpendicular or oblique insertion directed proximally 0.1 to 0.2 cun or prick to bleed.

COMMAND FUNCTIONS:

  • Jing-Well point
  • Metal point

ACTIONS:

  • Clears and benefits the head
  • Benefits the chest and lateral costal region
  • Calms the Shen
  • Subdues Liver Yang
  • Clears Heat
  • Benefits the temporal area
