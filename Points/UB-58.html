POINT: UB-58 (URINARY BLADDER-58)

NAME: FEIYANG

English: Soaring Upwards
Also Known As: Flying Up
Flying Yang
High Position
Fly and Scatter
Flying Tight
Flying High
Soaring

LOCATION:

  • Located 7 cun directly above UB-60 Kunlun, on the posterior border of the fibula approximately 1 cun inferior and lateral to UB-57 Chengshan.

NEEDLING:

  • Puncture perpendicularly 0.7 to 1 cun.
  • Moxibustion is applicable.
  • Straight insertion 1.5 to 2.5 cun, causing local distention, numbness and soreness, possibly extending downward.
  • Perpendicular or oblique insertion, directed proximally or distally, 1 to 1.5 cun.

COMMAND FUNCTIONS:

  • Luo-Connecting point

ACTIONS:

  • Removes obstruction from the channel
  • Strengthens the Kidneys
  • Harmonizes the upper and lower
  • Expels Wind from the Tai Yang channel
  • Treats hemorrhoids
  • Alleviates pain

  • Sciatica
  • Hemorrhoids

NOTES:

  • This is used as a distal point for lower back pain and sciatica.
  • This is a great point for runners. They will run better, with stronger legs and more endurance.
  • This is called the "tirelessness" point.
  • It affects the parathyroids.
  • It brings about well-being, great vitality, and strengthens the body and mind.
