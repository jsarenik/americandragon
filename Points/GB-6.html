POINT: GB-6 (GALL BLADDER-6)

NAME: XUANLI

English: Suspended Hair
Also Known As: Suspended Millimeter
Suspended Regulator

LOCATION:

  • Below the temple, three quarters of the distance from ST-8 Touwei to GB-7 Qubin.
  • In the temporal region, within the hairline, three quarters of the distance from ST-8 Touwei to GB-7 Qubin.
  • Within the hairline, at the junction of the lower quarter and upper three-quarters of the distance between ST-8 Touwei to GB-7 Qubin.

NEEDLING:

  • Straight insertion 1 to 1.5 cun.
  • Transverse insertion 0.5 to 1.5 cun.
  • Puncture subcutaneously 0.3 to 0.5 cun.
  • Moxibustion is applicable.

COMMAND FUNCTIONS:

  • Meeting point of the Stomach, San Jiao and Large Intestine channels

ACTIONS:

  • Expels Wind
  • Clears Heat
  • Activates the channel
  • Alleviates pain

INDICATIONS:

  • Migraine headache
  • Toothache
  • Facial swelling
  • Neurasthenia
  • Tinnitus
  • Redness and swelling of the skin of the face
  • Agitation of the Heart with no desire to eat
  • One-sided headache
  • One-sided headache extending to the outer canthus
  • Pain on the outer canthus
  • Sneezing
  • Febrile disease without sweating
  • Heat in the Middle Jiao

COMBINATIONS:

REN-15
Jiuwei

    

Febrile disease with one-sided headache

   

CONTRAINDICATIONS:

  • According to several modern texts, moxibustion on this point is contraindicated.
