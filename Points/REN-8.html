POINT: REN-8  (CONCEPTION VESSEL-8)

NAME: SHENQUE

English: Spirit Gateway
Also Known As: Spirit's Palace Gate
Mind Palace
Qizhong
Middle of the Navel
Qishe
Abode of Qi
Qihe
Joining of Qi
Spirit Shrine
Sea of spirit
Palace of the Gods

LOCATION:

  • Located in the center of the umbilicus.

NEEDLING:

  • Use moxibustion only.
  • Use moxibustion on salt, sliced ginger, crushed garlic or aconite cake only for 20 to 30 minutes.
  • Caution: Do not needle this point.

COMMAND FUNCTIONS:

ACTIONS:

  • Tonifies, strengthens and regulates the Spleen (especially Qi and Yang) and Stomach
  • Strengthens the transporting function of the Spleen and Stomach
  • Regulates the Intestines
  • Warms the Interior
  • Reduces digestive Stagnation
  • Tonifies the Kidneys (especially Yang)
  • Warms and stabilizes the Yang
  • Regulates the water pathways
  • Restores collapsed Yang
  • Dries Dampness and Cold-Damp
  • Tonifies Yuan Qi
  • Warms and harmonizes the Intestines

INDICATIONS:

  • Gastrointestinal disorders due to Dampness or Cold
  • Headache due to Deficiency
  • Lip swelling
  • Abdominal Cold and pain
  • Periumbilical pain
  • Acute or chronic diarrhea due to Deficiency or Cold

CONTRAINDICATIONS:

  • Needling is contraindicated.

NOTES:

  • This point's main function is to strongly tonify Yang.
  • Use with indirect moxa after filling the navel with salt.
  • This point is especially good for people addicted to drugs.
  • This point deals with the nourishment of the spirit.
