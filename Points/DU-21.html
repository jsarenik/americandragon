POINT: DU-21 (GOVERNING VESSEL-21)

NAME: QIANDING

English: In Front of the Crown
Also Known As: Before Top

LOCATION:

  • 3.5 cun directly above the midpoint of the anterior hairline and 1.5 cun anterior to DU-20 Baihui.
  • At the top of the head on the midline, 1.5 cun directly anterior to DU-20 Baihui and 3.5 cun posterior to the anterior hairline.

NEEDLING:

  • Puncture subcutaneously 0.3 to 0.5 cun.
  • Moxibustion is applicable.
  • Slanted insertion, 0.5 to 1 cun.
  • Transverse insertion 0.5 to 1 cun.
  • Caution: this point should not be needled in infants whose fontanelle has not closed.

COMMAND FUNCTIONS:

ACTIONS:

  • Eliminates Wind
  • Treats convulsions
  • Benefits the head

INDICATIONS:

  • Epilepsy
  • Dizziness
  • Blurred vision
  • Vertical headache
  • Rhinorrhea
  • Head Wind
  • Visual dizziness
  • Wind epilepsy
  • Childhood fright epilepsy
  • Acute and chronic childhood fright Wind
  • Clonic spasm
  • Headache
  • Vertigo
  • Rhinitis
  • Rhinopolypus
  • Infantile convulsions
  • Pain and swelling of the vertex
  • Swelling and redness of the face
  • Edema
  • Aversion to Wind-Cold
  • Copious, clear nasal discharge
