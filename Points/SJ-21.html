POINT: SJ-21 (SAN JIAO-21)  

NAME: ERMEN

English: Ear Gate
Also Known As: Ear Door
Muliao
Ear's Gate

LOCATION:

  • In the depression anterior to the supratragic notch and behind the posterior border of the condyloid process of the mandible. The point is located with the mouth open.
  • With the mouth slightly open, locate in the depression anterior to the supratragic notch and slightly superior to the condyloid process of the mandible.
  • At the front of the tuberculum supratragicum, in the depression when the mouth is opened.

NEEDLING:

  • Puncture perpendicularly 0.3 to 0.5 cun.
  • Moxibustion is applicable.
  • With the mouth open, join to SI-19 Tinggong and GB-2 Tinghui. 1.5 to 2.5 cun. Sensation: local distention and soreness, sometimes extending throughout the side of the face.
  • Inferior oblique insertion, slightly posteriorly, 0.5 to 1 cun. The point is needled with the mouth open.

COMMAND FUNCTIONS:

  • Entry point

ACTIONS:

  • Benefits the ears
  • Clears Heat
  • Opens the ear

CONTRAINDICATIONS:

  • Some say that this point can cause blindness.
  • Ancient books prohibit the use of moxibustion when treating patients with pus in the ear.

NOTES:

  • This is only used as a local point for ear problems, especially if due to Liver Yang Rising.
  • It is better to disperse this point than tonify.
  • A local point for ear inflammation especially from Liver Yang.
  • The pulse here measures the Qi of the ears and eyes
