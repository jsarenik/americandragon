POINT: UB-11 (URINARY BLADDER-11) 

NAME: DAZHU

English: Great Shuttle
Also Known As: Big Shuttle
Big Reed

LOCATION:

  • 1.5 cun lateral to the lower border of the spinous process of the first thoracic vertebra.
  • I.5 cun lateral to DU-13 Taodao at the level of the lower border of the spinous process of the first thoracic vertebra.
    Note: locate at the visible highest point of the paraspinal muscles.

NEEDLING:

  • Slanted insertion toward the spine 0.7 to 1 cun. Sensation: local distention, soreness and numbness sometimes extending between the ribs.
  • Oblique insertion towards the spine 0.5 to 1 cun.
  • Transverse-oblique insertion 1 to 1.5 cun.
  • Caution: perpendicular needling carries a substantial risk of causing a pneumothorax.

COMMAND FUNCTIONS:

  • Influential point for bones
  • Meeting point of the Small Intestine channel, the Gall Bladder channel and the DU channel
  • Sea of Blood point

ACTIONS:

  • Nourishes Blood
  • Expels Wind
  • Strengthens bones
  • Benefits the bones and joints
  • Soothes the sinews
  • Releases the Exterior
  • Regulates Lung Qi
  • Alleviates cough
