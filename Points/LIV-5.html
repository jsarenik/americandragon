POINT: LIV-5 (LIVER-5)

NAME: LIGOU

English: Woodworm Canal
Also Known As: Gourd Ditch
Insect Ditch
Wood Border
Insect Gate
Drain of the Calf
Draining Shells
Worm-Eater's Groove

LOCATION:

  • 5 cun above the tip of the medial malleolus, on the midline of the medial surface of the tibia.
  • Located 5 cun superior to the vertex of the medial malleolus on the medial border of the tibia.
  • 5 cun above the medial malleolus of the ankle in the trough between the posterior margin of the tibia and the gastrocnemius muscle.
  • 5 cun above the prominence of the medial malleolus, immediately posterior to the medial crest of the tibia, in the depression between the medial crest of the tibia and the gastrocnemius muscle.
    Note: divide the distance between the tip of the medial malleolus and the popliteal crease into thirds; this point is located at the junction of the distal third and proximal two thirds.

NEEDLING:

  • Puncture subcutaneously 0.3 to 0.5 cun.
  • Moxibustion is applicable.
  • Straight insertion along the posterior margin of the tibia, 0.5 to 1 cun, causing local distention and soreness.
  • Slanted insertion pointed upward along the posterior margin of the tibia, 1.5 to 2 cun for diseases of the trunk. After Qi has been obtained, use strong stimulation for a short time, causing distention and soreness possibly reaching the genitals.
  • Perpendicular insertion 0.5 to 1 cun.
  • Oblique insertion directed proximally toward the abdomen 1 to 2 cun.

COMMAND FUNCTIONS:

  • Luo-Connecting point
  • Master point of Blood (menstrual)

ACTIONS:

  • Promotes the smooth flow of Liver Qi
  • Resolves Damp-Heat
  • Clears Damp-Heat form the Lower Jiao
  • Benefits Qi
  • Clears the channel
  • Regulates Qi
  • Benefits the genitals
  • Regulates menstruation
  • Treats Plumpit Qi Stagnation
